    • Description

    Secure Group is forming a team that will compete with some of the global cybersecurity giants to develop the first Android-based interoperable mobile OS. Join our team of champions and develop a multi-layered security solution, that will allow users to take full control of their privacy, security, and personal data.


    Tech Stack

    • Programming languages: Java, PHP
    • Toolkit: Android Studio, IntelliJ Idea
    • Test automation frameworks: Selenium, Appium
    • Testing framework: TestNG
    • Libraries: JUnit
    • Source control systems: Git
    • Project management: Jira, Confluence

    The Position


    Join our Secure OS team as a QA Automation Engineer, and produce a powerful set of test cases and internal testing tools to support the development of Secure OS.

    Ensure the quality of our solutions by identifying bugs, designing and executing tests, maintaining the technical documentation, and analyzing the systems to spot potential quality issues that could affect our solutions.

    We envision each of our employees as stakeholders, so you will be responsible for driving the collaboration with other members of the engineering team to find the best methods for solving problems and securing the quality and usability of our products.


    Requirements


    • Proficient understanding of programming languages, automation frameworks and ability to write logical automation test scripts
    • Experience with Agile software development and testing methodologies
    • Common knowledge and experience with Android integrated development environment
    • High expertise in test case design and execution
