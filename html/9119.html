Big Data/Data Engineer – Strategy & Innovation

Accenture Prague, CZ

Před 11 hodinami

Zažádat na webu společnosti
Our team is looking for talented, enthusiastic and passionate Big Data/Data Engineer with a bit of strategic and innovative mindset to help us to shape and deliver our Data Analytics vision.

Why is interesting working with us?

We’re a global strategy team part of a global CIO family that works with Big Data, Cloud and cutting-edge Data Science technologies for Accenture internal IT infrastructure in the connectivity, network and compute service area enabling our company to work across the globe.

Due to our scale, we ingest data streams from around the globe and process terabytes of data to perform advanced Analytics.

What will be your job?

You will design, develop, deploy and test a range of data pipelines end to end from different major cloud vendors that will enable our visualization and data scientists to perform predictive and prescriptive analytics at scale, so your data plumbing work will be key.

Basic Qualifications:
  • MSc. program or university degree (preferably in a quantitative discipline such as Mathematics, Physics, Statistics, Econometrics, Computer Science)
  • Hands-on Java, Python, C#, Spark, Hive, SQL, Linux
  • Write complex SQL queries to extract data from data lake
  • Create a Data Catalogue for different databases managed by tea
  • Experience in Building fault-tolerant, scalable batch and real-time distributed data processing systems an associated technology in the cloud
  • Experience in Building fault-tolerant, scalable batch and real-time distributed data processing systems an associated technology in the cloud
  • Experience in processing and transforming unstructured/structured data at scale in optimal ways (cost /performance)
  • Optimize performance and resource utilization on large big data cloud production environments
  • Experience with analyzing big data in magnitudes or Terabytes /petabytes preferably coming from ITSM logs, events tools at scale
  • Knowledge of ITIL
  • Building and working with RESTful APIs and / or data streaming
  • Knowledge of relational, nonrelational databases, data modeling and big data framework
  • Good English; written and verbal communication skills.

Nice to have skills:
  • Certified Google Cloud Data Engineer/Azure Data Engineer or willing to obtain them.
  • Hands-on either of major cloud vendors such as Google Cloud Platform (PubSub, Big Query, Dataflow, Data fusion...), MS Azure (Azure Data Factory, HDInsight, Databricks…), and Unix shell.Exposure of building architecture and strategic Big Data/Data Eng. road maps.
  • Exposure to any of the following ETL tools such as Informatica, Talend, Alteryx and/or Pentaho.
  • Enjoy discovering, testing and researching new Big Data technologies and being able to share results and best practices via whitepapers, blogs, townhalls...
  • General understanding of Knowledge about Data Mining, Statistics and Machine Learning
  • Experience of deploying microservices such as Kubernetes, docker...
  • Basic understanding of Agile and Design Thinking methodologies
  • Experience with infrastructure related Analytics would be a plus
  • General knowledge about Agile, DevOps delivery frameworks

What You Can Expect
  • You’ll have fun and be part of a pleasant and multicultural team with very good working life balance
  • You’ll be working with diverse and interesting projects at scale within a strategy & innovation team with direct exposure to our key strategic partners
  • There is an exciting career progression, providing great growth opportunities, learning & development possibilities
  • Working in a brand-new fancy office directly at the metro
  • You can expect all the great benefits and perks from a company like Accenture.
