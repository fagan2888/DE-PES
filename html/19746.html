Data Engineer (based in Vienna, Austria)

TourRadar Bucharest, Romania

Cu 6 zile în urmă

Depuneți-vă candidatura pe site-ul web al companiei

At TourRadar, we are a bunch of techy travel experts on a mission to enrich people’s lives through touring. We have built a marketplace platform that brings travellers together with a large number of global and local tour operators and we are committed to providing simple but awesome user experiences to them. By putting a bunch of cutting-edge technologies to good use, we are building an environment where engineers can grow and thrive while helping the company on its global mission.


We are looking for data engineers who enjoy solving hard problems, who think for on their feet and are obsessed with helping accomplish important missions while growing themselves in the process. At TourRadar, we take pride in building an environment of mutual trust and respect, where we have a ton of fun building a world-class tech platform.


You Will...

    • Work within our Foundation engineering team alongside other Data Engineers Design, in architecting and supporting new and existing data and ETL pipelines, and recommend improvements and modifications.
    • You will use your expertise in event driven architectures to build real time or near-real time data pipelines
    • Develop monitoring systems to ensure our data platform is behaving as expected, and alerting us when it is not.
    • Constantly look for, identify and analyze performance bottlenecks in our data layer (such as slow queries or process inefficiencies), helping other engineers debug issues with data pipelines.
    • Build the infrastructure required for optimal extraction, transformation, and loading of data from a wide variety of data sources.
    • Provide support for various engineering teams working on data synchronization with multiple microservices

You Have...

    • Good communication skills. We communicate all day long.
    • A growth mindset, and a deep curiosity to learn and improve yourself constantly.
    • A tendency to go above and beyond, always looking for ways to improve things around you. You thrive in fast-moving, sometimes ambiguous environments.
    • Experience building a scalable data platform within a microservice architecture.
    • Proven experience in implementation and maintenance of event driven architectures
    • Minimum of 3 years of experience with at least one of the following technologies in the context of event driven architectures: Apache Kafka, Rabbit MQ, Apache NiFi, Amazon Kinesis
    • Fluency in SQL and proven development experience (ideally Python or similar languages)
    • Hands-on experience with AWS and services like EC2, SQS, SNS, Redshift, RDS, Cache etc.A can-do attitude: no problem is too daunting for you to tackle.

Our Stack

    • Frontend: React, Redux, GraphQL, CSS-in-JS, Babel, Webpack, Typescript/ES6, Jest, Cypress, Netlify, Antd
    • Backend: PHP (Symfony and Laravel as frameworks), Golang, Python and NodeJS/TypeScript lambda-functions, and Java (for E2E testing)
    • Data Stores: MySQL, PostgreSQL, Redis, Elasticsearch, Redshift
    • Containers: Docker
    • Automation: Ansible, Terraform
    • Deployment: Jenkins, BitBucket pipelines
    • Hosting: AWS (EC2, ECS, IAM, S3, SQS, Redshift, Lambda, etc.)

Benefits & Perks

    • Progress: The excitement & stability of working for a rapidly scaling, Series C funded startup, including Silicon Valley investors.
    • Ownership & Impact: An environment where individual ownership of your career goals & projects means you can directly grow the TourRadar business. The opportunity to grow personally & professionally is limitless, for those willing to work hard.
    • Growth: A significant cash and time budget for making the most out of targeted training resources to help you develop your skill set
    • Free Travel: Generous travel credits are provided to experience multi-day tours around the world, updated annually to keep you travelling. International Office-Exchange is also a possibility, to work & learn from your colleagues in TourRadar offices in Brisbane or Toronto.
    • Awesome Office: Work in our expansive, casual office, right in the heart of Vienna’s 1st district. Including a private BBQ terrace, free snacks and drinks, office foosball, games and wellness activities. All in a dog-friendly environment that encourages collaboration and connection amongst teams.
    • Location: Vienna is a thriving startup hub, with ample opportunities to grow your network and enjoy the benefits of a city ranked #1 for Quality of Living for 10 years running. Affordable for families and young professionals, filled with nature parks, a lively restaurant foodie scene and plenty of street art, events and rolling vineyards to visit on your weekends.
    • Diversity: An international work environment with more than 40 nationalities, with English as the official working language, but many more overheard in the hallways!
    • Regular team outings : Team nights at least once a month, with different themes and activities to keep us connected. Team members often spontaneously organise events and trips together in- and outside of Austria, from skiing, to pasta making, rock climbing, wine tasting and more.


This role is based in our Vienna office.


Most of our current data pipelines are batch based, and we are looking for a specialist that can help use taking our data infrastructure to the next level and implement event driven patterns wherever possible.
