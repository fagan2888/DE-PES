Job Description

Capacity Analyst will be responsible for ensuring that sufficient capacity exists within all the infrastructures to meet Business Unit demands.

He/she will leverage the Refinitiv capacity management tool set along with other data analysis tools to monitor current and historical capacity, identify run out rates and forecast future capacity demand, initiating remediation actions or capacity expansion project when required and providing input into the software release planning process.

This role will also have responsibility to troubleshoot, investigate capacity or performance related issues in the infrastructure and liaise with Development team to resolve them.

  • Capacity Engineer will be responsible for ensuring that sufficient capacity exists within all the Time Series infrastructures to meet Business Unit demands.

Qualification
  • Bachelor degree in Business, Technology, or Operations Management.
  • Strong business analysis skills; ability to analyze data sets and develop technical solutions to complex business problems
  • Strong administrative and organizational skills required, as is a focused attention to details
  • Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Office
  • Strong data analysis and Microsoft Excel skills (preferably to advanced level)
  • Good background in programming or scripting languages. Java and Python knowledge is preferred
  • Good communication in English and strong interpersonal skills required
  • 2-3 years experience working in a technical environment or with a technical organization
