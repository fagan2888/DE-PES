Automation Engineer

Huxley Wolverhampton, GB

1 week ago

Apply on company website
A new opportunity is available for an Automation Engineer to join one of the largest privately-owned companies in the world at their site in Wolverhampton. Working here is an opportunity to thrive, within an environment where you can develop your career to the fullest whilst engaging in meaningful work that makes a positive impact around the globe.

They can offer a diverse, supportive environment where you will grow personally and professionally as you learn from some of the most talented people in your field. As the successful Automation Engineer you will get to be part of the site's engineering leadership group and so work in a role which is a mixture of hands-on work and developing the strategy of the process control & automation functions.

You will have an excellent opportunity to be involved in the future of the factories automation processes, to enhance the long-term reliability and performance of the plant. This will include developing and executing a plant network so that all assets can be centrally monitored, and preventing the obsolescence of controls/automation equipment by planning for lifecycle replacements.

In order to have your application considered you must have the following experience:

  • Proven experience in the design and execution of technical solutions for automation in industrial settings
  • Knowledge of programming languages for PLCs and SCADA/DCS systems
  • Ability to read and interpret electrical schematics

    • In return for your expertise and commitment, they will provide a fast-paced stimulating environment, which will stretch your abilities and channel your talents. As well as the outstanding career opportunities that are available in such a successful global business they can offer a starting salary up to £45,000 depending upon experience with flexibility possible for the ideal candidate, plus 5% annual performance bonus , 25 days holiday, company pension up to 10%, 8x life assurance , BUPA healthcare and more.

    If you would be interested in this fantastic opportunity for an interesting and engaging career with a truly world-leading business then please apply for more information and to be considered.

    To find out more about Huxley Engineering, please visit www.huxleyengineering.com
