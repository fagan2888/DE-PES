    Talent Acquisition Specialist at Quantum Surgical SAS

    • Quantum Surgical is looking for a Data Engineer to join a team of creative and energetic engineers who are building the next generation of smart tools for minimally invasive therapies.

    The Data Engineer will play a key role in providing clinically relevant, structured & actionable data for the development of technologies in a robotized platform with medical applications. He will be working closely with engineers to support the creation of real-life problem-solving solutions based on Artificial Intelligence techniques or advanced image-processing methods.

    The candidate ideally has a demonstrated experience with early stage product development, cross-functional teams and technology development in an industrial environment.

    Key Responsibilities

    • Recover, qualify and curate image data and clinical cases from clinical partners.
    • Design, implement and maintain an optimal data infrastructure capable of responding to rapidly increasing data needs.
    • Provide ready-to-use data to machine learning engineers for training and testing AI methods. Design the right datasets for each specific problem.
    • Work with company stakeholders to assist with data-related technical issues and support their data needs.
    • Develop and maintain tooling for data management and analysis.
    • Guarantee confidentiality of data and compliance to regulatory.
    • Manage DB-related information system.
    • Formalize data collection protocols.
    • Identify and interact with potential data providers.

    Experiences and Qualifications

    • MSc. in Computer Science or equivalent fields.
    • 3+ years of experience in data query, data mining, information retrieval, or statistical analysis.
    • Non-SQL databases.
    • Knowledge of Hadoop, MapReduce, Hive, Pig, Data streaming.
    • Experience in SQL, NoSQL, DashDB, MySQL, MongoDB, Cassandra
    • Python programming skills.
    • Excellent communication and documentation skills.
    • Self-motivated, curious and open-minded.
    • High interest in medical imaging required.

    Desired experiences

    • Experience or knowledge in DevOps is appreciated.
    • Experience in medical imaging appreciated.
