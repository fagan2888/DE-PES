    • Can you imagine if result of your work is immediately delivered to 7 million people? Join Group Customer Value Management (CVM) in our journey to future banking and make Swedbank more proactive, personal and customer centric. We are in transition from traditional bank to data driven fully digitalized business where data becomes as most important asset in the business. Join us to be part of the challenges to build-up Swedbank’s big data landscape and analytical service!

    About the team


    Right now we are consolidating our Data Development teams and setting up for the future journeys. Team which will be responsible for developing data products in Data Lake. In close cooperation with data scientists we will be doing development, maintenance, testing and deployment of big data solutions in Data Development. Our team will be located in Vilnius, but we will have very close cooperation with colleagues from Sweden and Estonia. We value cooperation & teamwork and believe that together we achieve more.

    About the role


    As a Big Data Engineer in Data Development team, we expect you to have experience in a wide range of technologies. You work on big data solutions which architect has designed while doing development, maintenance, testing and deployment of big data solutions. We expect you to have Data mindset, to be able to drive work forward by yourself and to bring in solutions. You will be in professional collaborative environment where opinions and discussions are valuable asset.

    What you need to succeed:


    • Hadoop is core platform so you need experience of Hadoop (Hortonworks), Spark, Ranger and DevOps automation;
    • Hands on experience with: ETL, HDFS, HBASE, Kafka/Confluent, Hive;
    • Good experience working in Agile;
    • General applied knowledge of relevant CI&CD and DevOps tools (Ansible, Jenkins, Docker etc.).

    As a person you are:


    • A strong driver, used to continuously prioritize, communicate and decide. Comprehensive understanding both long-term and short-term ‘big-picture’ perspectives;
    • Used to identify, plan and execute your own activities. I.e. work independently;
    • Enjoy to work in true multicultural environment.

    How does recruitment process look like?


    You‘ll meet Elena as a recruiter and Tomas as this team’s manager. This interview will be based more on your experience, soft skills and motivation. After that, hopefully both sides are happy with what they've heard and we'll invite you to join our team!

    Salary for this position ranges from 3000-4500 EUR gross i.e. before taxes.*

    *In Swedbank salary offer for the candidate is determined based on the predefined salary ranges for the position, and depends on the level of competence and experience of the candidate. Salary is one part of the overall remuneration including variety of other benefits and many opportunities provided by Swedbank.
