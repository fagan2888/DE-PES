Tech Lead Senior Software Engineer (Data Scientist)

Manhattan Associates Bangalore, IN

7 days ago

Apply on company website
Manhattan designs, builds and delivers market-leading supply chain technology solutions for leading companies around the world. We help drive the commerce revolution with unmatched insight and unrivaled technology, connecting front-end revenue and relationships with back-end execution and efficiency—optimized on a common technology platform. This platform-based approach is enabling leading companies across the globe to Push Possible® by getting closer to customers and achieving real-world results.

Responsibilities
  • Provide strong analytical, advanced Data Science and Machine Learning expertise as a key member of a specialized science-based team focused on the research, development, and deployment of Manhattan Associates’ next generation cloud-native Machine Learning platform
  • As a member of the data science R&D team you will work with our customers, product management, and application R&D teams to identify opportunities for ML enablement, then follow with further use case analysis, data extraction, prototyping, exploratory modeling, validation and benchmarking of proposed ML models/pipelines
  • As a member of the data science R&D team you will help to analyze, design, enhance, evolve, and support Manhattan Associates’ Machine Learning platform
  • As a member of the data science R&D team you will help with the deployment and lifecycle oversight, performance analysis, issue troubleshooting and associated data extraction, tuning and refreshing of production-level cloud-based Machine Learning models/pipelines
  • Work with large, complex data sets to extract, visualize, and infer meaningful insights
  • Stay current with state-of-the-art techniques and technologies in the field
Help to research, prototype/test, & benchmark new methods and technologies
