Who is Guardian Industries?

When you are looking at a city’s skyline or the grilles on the latest cars and trucks, chances are you’re seeing our products. Through Guardian Glass , a Guardian Industries company, we make high- performance, energy efficient glass for homes and buildings. SRG Global™, a Guardian Industries company, makes automotive trim products that define a vehicle’s DNA and help improve fuel efficiency.

And perhaps most of all, we make the ideas, formulas and processes that make these products possible. Guardian Industries is 18,000 people working to make products that improve people’s lives! To Learn more visit our website at: https://www.guardian.com

Guardian Industries is seeking you to become our next Process Engineer!

The focus of the Process Engineer is to reduce waste in the production of insulated glass window units. This position offers numerous opportunities for career advancement, with potential movement into engineering management, operations management, or plant management.

A Day In The Life Typically Includes
  • The employee must be awarded by Guardian health & safety policies
  • Employee must be fully inducted with safety inductions programs
  • Ensuring compliance with the health and safety regulations and quality standards
  • Participate in the preparation of annual plan for Utilities
  • Transfer the unit objective into individual objective based on manpower allocation
  • Align with shift manager the Shift productivity and report with any further activities related to shift
  • Ensure the communication within the unit and ensure that all unit tasks, activities is taking place with team
  • Ensure that the quality rate, percentage is matching standard
  • Organize the distribution of workload and tasks between units’ team
  • Organize the communication between Utilities and other co-related units within operations to ensure good level of coordination
  • Participate in the enhancement and development of unit process and procedures
  • Participate in the preparation of the annual maintenance plan
  • Ensure that the Maintenance plan implementation is taking place as planned
  • Communicate with Maintenance team with any maintenance issues and troubleshooting
  • Provide the needed support (technical, non- technical) to all unit team
  • Follow up production rates and ensure the efficiency of Utilities activities
  • Received the regular reports for the subordinates as agreed
  • Present the regular reports to direct manager includes all required approved data
  • The employee is responsible to achieve current assigned responsibilities and any further responsibilities assign in the future as well
  • Work on cost reduction percentage projects
