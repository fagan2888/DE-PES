Job Description

Workindenmark connects Danish companies and international jobseekers. Right now, Denmark is looking for highly skilled professionals such as:
  • Software Developers
  • Software architects
  • Engineers for Embedded Software Development
  • Software Engineers
  • Full Stack developers
  • Frontend and backend developers
  • SCADA engineers
  • IT project managers
  • Java Developers
  • Data Scientists
And many other IT-profiles…....

You are probably a computer scientist or an engineer, maybe with some work experience.

Which technologies you will be working with depends on the team and the business you will be part of.

As a person………
  • you are an independent teamplayer
  • you contribute positively to the team dynamics
  • you are ambitious
  • you work systematically
  • you always have focus on the detail
  • you have strong English skills
Interested?

Meet us at the TECH Job Fairs in London, Amsterdam, Munich, Dublin, Barcelona, Frankfurt. Stuttgard and Lisbon.

To prepare for the fair, find vacancies from Danish employers on our website www.workindenmark.dk and register your CV in our CV-database, and make it accessible to Danish employers and Workindenmark recruiters.

How To Apply

We look forward to meet you at our stand for further information. If you find a specific vacant position you are interested in, please apply directly with the employer as described in the vacancie.
