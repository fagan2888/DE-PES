BIG DATA ENGINEER

Technology is at the heart of YOOX NET-A-PORTER GROUP and is the driving force behind its success. Our in-house technology team allows The Group to give customers and brand partners the best possible experience across content and commerce, and to continually innovate and lead the online luxury industry.

Role Purpose

We are looking for Big Data Engineers / Developers to fill vacancies within our brand new BIG DATA Team. The Big Data Engineers will work on the collecting, storing, and processing of huge sets of data. Their primary focus will be on choosing optimal solutions to use for these purposes as well as maintaining, implementing, and monitoring them, but will also be responsible for integrating the solutions with the architecture used across the Company.

Key Responsibilities
  • Selecting and integrating any Big Data tools and frameworks required to provide requested capabilities Development of Reporting solutions and Multi-Dimensional Data Analysis
  • Implementing data ingestion pipelines from source systems to store row data
  • Working on Data integration through ETL/API
  • Implementing data transformations on row data to provide relevant business information
  • Implementing data delivery pipelines to custom consumers Applying Agile Methodologies
  • Monitoring performance and advising any necessary infrastructure changes (for the Senior Engineer)
  • Defining data retention policies (for the Senior Engineer)

Skills And Experience
  • Proficient understanding of distributed computing principles
  • Proficiency with Hadoop, MapReduce, HDFS
  • knowledge of Big Data querying tools, such as Pig, Hive, Impala
  • Experience with Spark
  • Knowledge of various ETL techniques and frameworks, such as Flume
  • Experience with messaging systems, such as Kafka or RabbitMQ
  • Basic experience on Dev-ops tasks
  • Engineering/Computer Science degree or equivalent working experience
  • Knowledge of AWS architecture and services (EMR, Lamda, SQS, API Gateway, S3)
  • Knowledge of NoSQL databases, such as HBase, Cassandra, MongoDB

The Following Will Be Considered As a Plus
  • Experience in building stream-processing systems, using solutions such as Storm or Spark-Streaming
  • Experience with integration of data from multiple data sources
  • Good understanding of Lambda Architecture, along with its advantages and drawbacks
Location: Zola Predosa, Bologna
