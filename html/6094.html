Data Engineer



About the role



Data engineer familiar with Microsoft Azure services, with projects of Cloud Data Engineering and with programming languages (Python, C#, SQL).

Essentials

  • Fluency in English is mandatory.



PhD is a plus



Statistical modeling (like R/Matlab/Mathematica) is a plus



Knowledge of Android and iOS app development is a plus
