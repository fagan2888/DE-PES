Company Description

Ubisoft is committed to enriching players’ lives with original & memorable entertainment experiences. We build worlds that are a playground for the imagination, offering moments of surprise, fun and adventure as well as opportunities for learning and self-discovery.
At Ubisoft, you’ll grow and collaborate with highly talented teams from around the world.

Located in Da Nang, Vietnam, a hotbed for tech and culture, and one of the best cities to live in Asia, the newest studio will focus on developing “Instant Games”, powered by HTML 5 technology, for several social platforms. This new playstyle has boomed significantly on social media on a worldwide scale boasting today millions of daily users. The teams at Ubisoft Da Nang will also collaborate with other studios on the development of mobile games.
By bringing the best creative and engineering talents from Vietnam, SEA and the World together, and allowing them to thrive in a dynamic and modern international work and living environment, Ubisoft Da Nang is the place to be to begin your journey in game development or take the big next step in your career.

Job Description

As a Data Analyst, your main responsibilities will be to:
  • Provide an in depth analytics and insights about our games
  • Develop new insights and analyses that inform decisions and help us continue to delight the people playing our games
  • Coordinate and analyze the efficiency of game events and of some specific game features
  • Produce Financial reports data on a daily / weekly and monthly basis
  • Create specific reports about new game content profitability using data from diverse sources
  • Be a key contact between the different stakeholders
Qualifications
  • Excellent SQL skills: extensive experience querying large, complex data sets
  • Advanced knowledge of Excel (macros and/or VBA is a plus) and MS Office suite is essential
  • A good knowledge in databases/BI
  • Good analytical and reporting skills
  • Ability to execute research projects, and generate practical results and recommendations
  • Good client management skills with a strong grasp of both technical and business perspectives
  • A good knowledge of Tableau software, R/Python is a plus
  • Proven ability to work in a fast-paced environment, and to meet changing deadlines and priorities on multiple simultaneous projects
  • 5+ years of experience in a similar role

Additional Information

We’ll come back to you as soon as possible with potential delays to expect due to the recent opening of the studio.
