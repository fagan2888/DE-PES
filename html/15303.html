Product Data Scientist

Vinted / Kleiderkreisel Vilnius, Vilniaus, Lithuania

2 weeks ago

Apply on company website

Vinted is the world’s biggest pre-loved fashion marketplace. Our mission is green: we want to make second-hand the first choice globally.


Currently, 22 million people use our platform, which is developed and supported by a team of more than 300 people from our offices in Vilnius, Berlin, Prague, and Warsaw. We have a unique work culture that’s based on trust, honesty, and attention to self-improvement.


As our new Data Scientist, you will become a member of a cross-functional product team. Teams like these have all the skills to ship new features to our members without any outside help.


In this position, you will:

  • Support the team with valuable data insights and help make decisions about product changes, new features and improvements
  • Help the team estimate and track opportunities and risks
  • Design and track the performance of A/B tests and the results of product changes and proactively provide data-driven insights
  • Select and track the most important KPIs
  • Join forces with your colleagues to update and improve our reporting system
  • Author data pipelines via Spark- and Scala-based ETL framework


You should be:

  • Highly business-result-oriented and comfortable with iterative processes
  • Have analytical/BI work experience
  • Able to extract and transform data with SQL queries
  • Keen on collecting and sharing business insights with your team/company
  • Able to understand the context of the domain and give timely insights
  • Have at least 1 year working experience with R, Python or another similar language
  • Excellent at communicating in English
  • Have a degree in statistics, mathematics, econometrics or similar field


Perks:

  • Opportunity to join stock options programme
  • Learning budget (13.2% of net salary)
  • 25 working days of holidays
  • All the tech you need to do your job
  • Daily meals at the Vinted Noise restaurant
  • Budgets for team buildings
  • In-house gym
  • Monthly Shop@Vinted budget


Please note that this position is based in Vilnius.


The salary range for this position is 2400-3300 Eur GROSS / month.


If this sounds interesting - you may well be just who we need. Apply by sending your CV as well as the cover letter and we'll be in touch.
