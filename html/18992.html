    Specialist Talent & Culture at district m

    • You have interest in learning and applying the latest technology trends? You’re passionate about the development and optimization of data processing? Here is the ideal job!


    district m is looking for a Data Engineer to join our amazing tech team in their 12,000 sq ft facility office in Montreal’s Mile-End district. The ideal candidate will be able to quickly pivot and change their design. We require someone who takes pride in their work, learns quickly, and adapts to ever changing standards.

    Your Responsibilities


    • Develop and maintain big data processing applications, both batch and streaming processes (Apache Spark);
    • Work within the development team on building data solutions, visualizations and dashboards;
    • Enable the product and business needs in data;
    • Assist in designing machine learning and predictive statistical models;
    • Convert Machine Learning and Predictive models to production-grade algorithms that scale;
    • Collaborate with internal teams for BI needs (reports, visualizations and ad hoc analysis);
    • Effectively communicate insights through reports and presentations skills like data storytelling;
    • Design new data models and data pipelines;
    • Manage support and maintain existing data pipelines;
    • Create dashboards and data exploration models (Looker);
    • Develop and maintain data extractions tools (REST, API and clients);
    • Occasionally provide ad hoc data.

    Technical skills

    • Good Scala and/or Python skills;
    • Good knowledge of SQL;
    • Overall knowledge of data transfer formats and tools like JSON, Protobuf, Kafka, AWS Kinesis etc.;
    • Overall knowledge of data storage formats (AVRO, Parquet, ORC);
    • Overall knowledge of basic data pipeline concepts;
    • Knowledge in statistical modeling and core AI and machine learning algorithms (an asset);
    • Knowledge of Apache Spark and Apache Flink (an asset);
    • Knowledge of Amazon Redshift and LookML (an asset);
    • Knowledge of Php (an asset);
    • Bilingual, French and English.

    What people say about you

    • Skilled at assessing business requirement;
    • Well organized;
    • Pay attention to details;
    • Are eager to challenge the status quo and willing to learn new programming languages.

    What's in it for you?

    • Competitive salary and benefits;
    • A generous group insurance plan;
    • A flexible schedule (and a summer schedule!);
    • 3 weeks of vacation;
    • Extra holidays between Christmas and New Year;
    • 4 extra weeks of vacation for your 5th year at district m;
    • A very active social committee keeping a strong corporate culture and fostering relations between districters;
    • Healthy snacks and coffee;
    • And more!

    About district m

    district m is a digital technology company that provides unique solutions for publishers and advertisers. For the supply side, we have developed proprietary products designed to increase revenue streams from digital ad inventory. On the demand side, we specialize in unique campaign management. Our exclusive and proprietary technology segments data and user profiles, which allows our clients the ability to reach diverse and specific audiences at the right time.


    Becoming a districter means you will join a team full of inspiring people who are always working to go above and beyond expectations. At district m, we pride ourselves on having a friendly and open environment where employees are encouraged to share new ideas and where all opinions are valued.


    If you've been looking to work for a company where you won't just be an employee amongst many, and where you would collaborate with other ambitious and success-oriented professionals, then we would like to meet you!
