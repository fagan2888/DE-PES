Position Summary

The employee will provide technical support and customer service to Mithril non-technical staff and Mithril clients. This involves approximately 50% remote & telephone support. The remaining 50% will be split between dispatches for on-site work at client locations and providing hands on technology services for equipment in the Mithril operations center. This individual is expected to be familiar with a wide variety of business environments, including specific experience supporting medical practices, and the ability to troubleshoot complex problems that include a combination of hardware, desktop, network, and end user elements. They must be able to determine when an issue needs to be escalated or handled as a priority case.

Essential Duties And Responsibilities

The essential functions include, but are not limited to the following:
  • Providing remote and onsite user/systems support & administration for Mithril and Mithril Clients
  • Engineering solutions and participating in technology projects for Mithril and Mithril Clients
  • Maintaining comprehensive documentation on all activities in Mithril designated systems
  • Ensuring proper documentation regarding all client encounters and support calls
  • Participating in on-call, late-shift, and night-shift rotations as needed
  • Dispatching to client locations for onsite support
  • Creating internal and client-facing documentation
  • Following up with issues to guarantee an excellent client experience
  • Taking on special projects as needed
  • Providing a daily status report with observations to help manage relationships
  • Performing other duties as assigned

Required Qualifications (Knowledge, Skills, And Abilities)
  • Medical practice support experience (understanding of basic process, regardless of application)
  • Self-starter with a willingness to learn and contribute to a high energy team
  • Excellent communication & time management skills; demonstrated attention to detail
  • Capability to represent the company on client sites, at trade shows, and events
  • 2-3 years hands-on Support & Administration experience with
    • Microsoft Windows and Office based work environments
      • Window 7/10
      • Office 2003 - 2016, Office365
    • Microsoft and Cisco networking basics including
      • wired/wireless connectivity configuration, connectivity troubleshooting
      • switching, routing, firewall, and name resolution
  • Working knowledge of
    • Microsoft SharePoint Basic Administration and Customization
    • Internal & Public DNS - registration and management
    • Apple and Android mobile devices
    • Malware cleanup and associated tools
    • Batch Files and Windows command line
    • MS Project and MS Visio

For Additional Consideration (Knowledge, Skills, And Abilities)
  • Microsoft Dynamics Use or Basic Administration
  • Vendor-side sales support
  • Power Shell scripting
  • Familiarity and comfort with at least one enterprise class service desk & ticket management solution (TrackIt, Altiris, Remedy, ServiceDesk, Dynamics, Etc.)
  • Current experience designing, implementing, and supporting
    • VMWare and or Microsoft Hyper-V
    • Office365 and Azure
    • Microsoft Intune
    • Windows 10 deployment automation, remote access and support
    • Solar Winds Orion implementation & Administration
    • Citrix or Microsoft Remote Desktop Services
    • Microsoft Exchange Server 2007/2010
  • Hands on with one or more of the following
    • Microsoft Intune
    • Webroot
    • CloudAlly
The company is an Equal Opportunity Employer, drug free workplace, and complies with ADA regulations as applicable.

Mithril employees feel valued and trusted Providing Managed Cloud Services, Infrastructure, and Concierge Client Service. You will have a unique opportunity for personal and professional growth which will enables you to deliver a positive user experience aligned with each customer's unique culture and business.

Our mission is to take care of IT so clients can focus on their core business by providing practical, cost effective, and secure technology solutions aligned with each client's unique business culture and strategy.

For some clients we provide strategic consulting on current and future technology use, for some we supplement existing staff, and for some we are their complete IT department from strategists on down all day every day.

Our High End White Glove IT Concierge Service is for those individuals who live in a high tech world and are used to having a team support them at home, abroad, and as needed.

Our clients include companies with basic support needs, multi-location organizations with complex needs, companies with or without existing technical staff, and high net worth individuals.
