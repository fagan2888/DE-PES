The Senior Data Analyst will be responsible for ensuring and improving the quality and efficiency of our ad-serving systems by analyzing terabytes of data. The advertising eco-system is a complicated landscape with many companies transacting on advertising space on websites and connected TVs. To detect invalid traffic and improve optimization, the Senior Data Analyst must be aware of the companies involved and what role they play.

The specific duties of the Senior Data Analyst will include:

Detect invalid ad traffic, such as bots-nets, click fraud, and "ad farms. An advertiser (like Living Social or eBay) buying ad space on a website wants to know that they are not showing ads to a bot or serving ads that cannot be seen. This is what is referred to as "invalid traffic." Invalid traffic is easily detected by knowing who the real owner of the inventory is.

If anyone other than that real owner is trying to sell the inventory, it can be a strong indication of invalid traffic. Using expertise in Python and Java, the Senior Data Analyst will analyze billions of rows of data to find patterns indicating invalid traffic. For instance, if the Senior Data Analyst detects 1000 ad requests in one minute from the same user, it is a very good indication of invalid traffic. By finding invalid traffic, the Senior Data Analyst will block it from our platform and prevent the advertisers we service from buying unwanted traffic. Help track down and identify complex bugs with in our ad serving system. A video ad serving system is comprised of many different sub-systems such as: client side javascript, ad serving application, data aggregation system and a UI/API.

When a bug or issue presents itself, it's often hard to pinpoint the root cause and which parts of the sub system are at fault. The Senior Data Analyst uses their knowledge of ad serving systems/sub-systems and their analytical skills to isolate the bug. Once isolated, the analyst works with the Product and Engineering teams to ensure all aspects of the root cause are resolved. In video ad serving, many requests go unsold (unfilled) because a publisher (site owners) cannot find an appropriate buyer.

We have developed a proprietary optimization algorithm that seeks to best match publishers with advertisers. The Senior Data Analyst will improve fill rate of video advertisements by searching for areas of improvement in our optimization algorithms.

Using his/her analytical skills and tools like Python and Excel, the Senior Data Analyst will search for new data points to incorporate into our optimization algorithm. For instance, the Senior Data Analyst might find through his/her analysis that certain countries have a higher fill rate than other.

S/he can then target those countries in our adserver to improve overall fill. Users of websites often are affected by "bad acting" creative content. Most video creative content just serves a video; however, some bad actors use creative content as a delivery mechanism for creating invalid traffic which slows down pages and monopolizes bandwidth.

This bad acting creative content can be detected in the following ways: 1) using Python and Excel to isolating patterns in our data sets that indicate malicious behavior (like many ad calls with the same request id); 2) using the browsers debug tools to analyze a creative's HTTP call chain to find the original source of the creative content so it can be shut off; 3) using a browser's Javascript and HTML debug console to search for hidden ads on a page that are monopolizing bandwidth without the knowledge of the page viewer. Requirements:Master's degree (or foreign equivalent) in Engineering, Math, Operations Research, Computer Science, or a closely related field.
