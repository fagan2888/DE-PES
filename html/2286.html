Description de l'emploi
Catalent Pharma Solutions is the leading global provider of advanced delivery technologies and development solutions for drugs, biologics and consumer health products. With over 80 years serving the industry, Catalent has proven expertise in providing development, production and packaging services for pharmaceutical and biotech companies.

For our sterile production site located in Belgium in Brussels (Neder-Over-Heembeek) which is currently expanding its capabilities, we are currently looking for a NPI Process Design Engineer .

The NPI Process Design Engineer reports to the Director New Product Introduction. What we offer with this role is a broad scope for creativity, as well as a crucial cross-functional aspect, with a lot of interactions with the shop floor, customers and other departments. As Process Design Engineer, you will be responsible for final product specification, and become the Subject Matter Expert for Catalent, gaining trust with our clients and bringing a solid expertise.

The Role
  • Design and implement the process to the detail for new products on the Brussels site, with a focus on Formulation and Manufacturing activities.
  • Perform process design for project transfer to production (focus on product characteristics, equipment and facility characteristics, operational efficiency and quality/risk approach)
  • Design, perform and evaluate accuracy studies on a scientific basis, e.g. pumps studies, kinetic studies, Visual Inspection studies, etc.
  • Be the process Expert for auto-injection devices
  • Lead and participate in risk assessment exercises
  • Build processes following Quality By Design : CQA, CPP, CIPC, etc.
  • Support the Process Validation steps
  • Define URS for specific equipment part of the process

The Candidate
  • Technical profile with scientific background
  • 5 years’ experience in the pharma industry with QBD proven experience. Junior profiles will only be considered if they possess great expertise in formulation.
  • Fluency in both French and English is mandatory
  • Demonstrated experience with auto-injector devices is a strong asset
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Ability to represent Catalent in front of customers and at seminars
  • Focus on risk analysis and customer satisfaction
  • Strong team player
  • Hands-on expert
  • Problem solving

Position Benefits
  • Defined career path and annual performance review and feedback process
  • Competitive contributory pension and benefits package all offered from day one of employment
  • 36 days’ holiday + bank holidays
