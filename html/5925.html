Job Responsibilities
  • Gather and analyze the current business data in details including usage pattern and relevant data that significant in the current processes efficiency and effectiveness
  • Explore industry trends, tools, and techniques in Open Source/Government Big Data
  • Design and implement data models to explain in solving the specified problems using various techniques such as data mining, statistical modeling, and machine learning
  • Provide insightful visualization and explanation of the results as well as recommend the next course of actions to decision makers and other functional groups
  • Assist in development of standards and policies for data management framework
  • Evaluate data management, data quality and data access processes for gaps, inefficiencies and opportunities; provide recommendations for improvement
  • Implement solutions and processes for management and governance across data quality metrics, metadata, lineage, data access rights and business definitions
  • Research, gather, analyze and share proven and innovative practice data management, apply or identify improvement opportunities to data modelling process
  • Possess strong knowledge of business process, data or information management, data flows, data relationships, data quality standards, processes
  • Able to perform data warehouse analysis and design, with full knowledge of data warehouse methodologies and data modeling
  • Able to think and communicate in both a business and technology context
  • Highly motivated and proactive with ability to work under pressure to meet demanding deadlines: analytical, well organized and able to work independently
Job Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s or Master‘s Degree specialized in a Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Technology, Computer Engineering, Computer Science
  • Minimum 2-3 years of data analysis, data management, data modeling or engineering
  • Minimum 1-2 years of working knowledge of the business use cases and emerging technologies used in the exploitation of “Big Data” including no-sql database management tools
  • Minimum 1-2 years of experience with understanding and exposure to change management, quality assurance/integrity measures, compliance and legal activities regarding data
  • 2-3 years of experience in data governance and analysis tools (i.e., Informatica, Vertica, TOAD, DB2, SAS, etc.) is preferable
  • Scripting experience in (Python, R, JavaScript, Shell Script or Scala preferred)
  • Strong desire and experience with data in various forms (data warehouses/SQL, unstructured data environments/PIG, HIVE, Impala, Spark)
  • Strong in communication both written and spoken and presentation skills
  • Ability to clearly articulate messages to a variety of audiences.
  • Forward looking with a holistic approach.
  • Problem solving and root cause identification skills.
  • Able to work effectively at all levels in an organization.
  • Must be a team player and able to work collaboratively with and through others.
