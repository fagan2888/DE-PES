About Us

Verizon Media (formerly Yahoo!, now a subsidiary of Verizon), is a values-led company committed to building brands people love. We reach over one billion people around the world with a dynamic house of 50+ media and technology brands. As a global leader in digital and mobile, Verizon Media is shaping the future of media. Our mission: build brands consumers love, build platforms customers love, and build a company talent loves. Yahoo! Gemini is a first-class global Native advertising platform serves Native advertisement on Yahoo! properties such as Yahoo! Homepage, Sports, Finance, and external publishers sites such as Microsoft and Samsung. Gemini team is building next-generation technologies to enrich our advertiser and user experience through complex scalable ad platforms, with ever growing and interesting challenges. We work on all Yahoo user data -- building the data pipelines and statistical data models to process billions of events, and making machine learning work. We are looking for world-class, fun-loving Big Data engineers to join our team where you will have the opportunity to help develop low latency and large scale data systems. You will analyze requirements; investigate optimal software solutions; architect, design, implement and test those solutions.

Primary Responsibilities
  • Perform all phases of software engineering including requirements analysis, application design, and code development & testing
  • Apply architecture design patterns to develop technical solutions
  • Design, develop and test reusable frameworks, libraries and Java components
  • Use Big data technologies to enrich and transform data and data analytics, machine learning
  • Lead technology decisions, drive product implementation and delivery
  • Complete code reviews and provide feedback relative to best practices, improving design and performance
  • Demonstrate substantial depth of knowledge and experience in a specific area of Big Data and Java development
  • Collaborate with peers across engineering teams to deliver project deliverables per defined timelines

Minimum Job Qualifications
  • BS/MS degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or Electrical Engineering
  • 5+ years hands-on expertise with Big Data technologies
  • 5+ years experience is Data Warehouse design and implementation
  • Solid understanding of data structures and algorithms
  • Hands-on experience in developing scalable data solutions
  • Hands-on experience in real-time query engines (Druid, Presto) preferred
  • Proficiency in Hadoop, Spark, Hive, HBase, and Oozie technologies
  • Highly energetic in learning, research, analytical and quantitative skills with proven experience of large sets of data, and solid programming experience in Java, Pig, Scala or Python.
  • Good skills and experience in Linux, XML, JSON, REST
  • Experience with fault-tolerant system design and high-performance engineering
  • Experience with machine learning algorithms and/or statistical methods is preferred
  • Able to focus and deliver results in a fast paced and entrepreneurial environment
  • Proactive, good interpersonal, strong analytical and problem solving skills; Team player and able to work independently.
  • The ability to English communicate in both written/verbal forms

Preferred Qualifications
  • Ads Analytics
