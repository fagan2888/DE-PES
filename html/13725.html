    • Fantastic opportunity for Senior Manager IBP (Integrated Business Planning) to support a centre of excellence for Integrated planning and Inventory Management in Cork


    This is a  world class supply chain through building core capabilities in the integrated business planning (IBP) / sales and operations planning process (S&OP)


    The company is one of the Worlds leading Medical Devices companies passionate about improving peoples lives through excellence in Healthcare.


    Apply Today for more details.

    What You will Do:


    • Responsible for design development and implementation of standardized Sales & Operations planning and integrated business planning processes
    • Develop and lead programs to build core planning capabilities & transformation projects needed in the sales and operations planning / integrated business planning process
    • Design and deploy financial reconciliation process between operations and commercial finance as part of IBP process
    • Develop and implement learning & development programs for planning leaders’ and stakeholders on how to orchestrate IBP process
    • Develop a maturity framework and assess IBP process  to develop strategic roadmap to improve S&OP / IBP maturity


    What You Need:

    • Degree or master’s in supply chain, Business, Production Management, Engineering or similar discipline
    • 7 years of experience of in end-to-end supply chain, S&OP, demand and/or supply planning.
    • 5 years of experience running in S&OP / IBP process across different business units, products and geographies
    • In-depth understanding of portfolio planning, demand, supply planning and executive S&OP steps of the process.
    • Experience in process design, SCM management and financial planning
    • Deep understanding of technology enablement and current technology tools for planning
