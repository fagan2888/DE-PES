What You'll Do



Cisco Systems Engineering Team in Moscow is looking for Partner System Engineer to drive enablement for our Partner Ecosystem. The focus will be on driving development of technical competencies of the Partners in key architectures Cisco offers Networking, Data Center, Collaboration and Security.

The Partner Systems Engineer will support all technical aspects of the partner enablement around the products and solutions Cisco offers. You will analyze the portfolio in close collaboration with Architecture team and you will propose programs and activities to drive the awareness and technical knowledge within our Partners. The Partner SE will build and maintain a sound knowledge of all applicable Cisco technologies in order to support partner colleagues and help them in addressing business deals. You will assist in the development of our partners’ knowledge on Cisco solutions to maximize their capabilities on the market.

In this role you will
  • Manage and help to develop partner technical enablement plans as part of the overall Architecture practice.
  • Attend partner events and enable & deliver technical trainings for the Partners.
  • Become trusted Partners’ Technical Advisor on Cisco’s portfolio and emerging technologies.
  • Be part of and develop the community of Systems Engineers at the Partner.



Who You'll Work With



The Partner Organization in Moscow aims to develop the most relevant partner ecosystem, valued by partners, customers and Cisco Employees. Growing partner technical capabilities in Cisco architectures and solutions is going to ensure profitable growth for our ecosystem to deliver business value to our customers. Join us for this exciting journey and help us to bring this vision to life!



Who You Are


  • Ideally, if you come with 3-5 years of technical experience in one (or in a few) of the following architectures Enterprise Networking, Data Centre, Collaboration or Security.
  • Strong interpersonal skills combined with good time-management and presentation skills are essential for this role.
  • Past experience in partnering with System Integration companies would be welcome, as we expect you to be comfortable in evangelizing the value of Cisco solutions at the Partners
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, IT, Computer Science or equivalent.
  • Cisco or industry certificates would be welcome.
