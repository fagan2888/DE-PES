Our Partner is one of the largest Public Relations agencies in the world. Polish office is one of over 120 branches operating in 81 countries and currently looking to hire a full-time Senior Data Engineer to build and maintain its analytics engine and support core capabilities.

As a Senior Data Engineer , you would be directly involved with the product team including software engineers and data scientists, to build systems that elicit information from multiple data platforms in order to help identify relevant and actionable trends and/or concepts.

You Will Be
  • Developing for and maintaining an internal ETL systems and big data processing applications
  • Building scalable data management solutions
  • Supporting large-scale data loading and analysis efforts
  • Managing deployment of internal systems
  • Streamlining and improving existing processes
  • Enforcing coding best practices & code reviews.

We Expect You To Have
  • 3+ years hands-on experience in Python programming
  • Solid skills within Apache Spark or Dask
  • An expertise within Linux systems
  • Proven track of experience within deployment, covering Ansible, Puppet or Chef
  • Advanced English skills (min. B2).
We would also not mind if your you are familiar with Kubernetes ecosystem!

Once Getting On Board, You Will Get
  • Opportunities to grow your expertise in a multinational environment where collaborative model of work is fundamental
  • Possibility to have a real influence on the technological landscape of the project
  • Benefits package (in process of forming), for now including a full medical coverage, dental and vision insurance, along with possibility to work from home
  • A unique chance to work for a global communications network, awarded for their creativeness, quality of work (Age’s Best Places to Work in 2019) and ability to make impossible - possible :)
Do not hesitate to apply if you like to do things that matter. If you do not see yourself in a such position, spread the word among other professionals who might be a fit!
