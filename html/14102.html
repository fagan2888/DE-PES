At QuintoAndar you will:

  • Work with a top-notch team that uses the best practices and best tools.
  • Learn a lot.
  • Be part of a high-impact project that will affect many people's lives.
  • Have autonomy to make decisions with no endless meetings or bikeshedding.
  • Work in an informal environment and horizontal hierarchy.


Every Data Engineer responsibilities at QuintoAndar

  • Develop and execute data warehouse and ETL/ELT solutions.
  • Know the data schema by heart and all there is to know about the company's databases to provide support for end users and developers.
  • Ensure that all reporting, KPIs and ETL/ELT processes are running correctly and smoothly in real-time.
  • Be the go-to person for querying the database.
  • Contribute with architecture optimizations of ETL, ELT, BI, and the life-cycle of an EDW solution.
  • Fetch data from RESTful APIs and provide APIs for data to be fetched.


Lead Data Engineer responsibilities at QuintoAndar:

  • Be a reference for your team
  • Discuss business requirements with the Data Product Manager on a deep level and anticipate risks
  • Help the career development of your team
  • Build a great team by both recruiting great talent and helping them grow


Please only apply if you have:

  • Experience leading engineering teams - a plus if it was in a high profile startup during high-growth stages.
  • Strong command of SQL, data architecture and relational data models and databases.
  • Strong coding skills in Python (or other scripting languages).
  • Amazing analytical and problem solving skills.
  • At least some experience in Columnar Storage Databases (like Amazon Redshift).
  • Data- or science-related background. Examples: Engineering, Computer Science, Mathematics or Statistics; alternatively, you have demonstrated experience in process management and scientific methodology.
  • Consider yourself BS-free: we dislike people who pretend to know stuff. Be honest.


We will like you even better if you:

  • Have previous experience in building data marts, data lake and structuring a data warehouse.
  • Have experience with analytics implementation.
  • Are up to date with cloud solutions (preferably AWS).
  • Have knowledge in PowerBI, Tableau, QlikView/QlikSense, Alteryx or other similar tool.
