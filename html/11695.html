PRINCIPAL SOFTWARE DEVELOPER

United States - Pennsylvania – Pittsburgh

We are looking for a Principal Software Developer in Pittsburgh, PA.


A successful candidate will have experience with ESB/SOA design and development, deployment, automation, and preferably development on Linux/UNIX/Windows platforms and integration experience. You will develop complex application software for system enhancements and assess and determine design considerations including application programs.



Skillset:


  • Preferred Experience with application development on WebSphere Message Broker or a similar ESB
  • Experience with IBM WebSphere Transformation Extender.
  • Experience as a Software Engineer/Systems Integrator working on Java/JEE application development and web services development.
  • Experience with WEB API management
  • Experience developing Restful web services
  • Possess an expert and thorough understanding of IT concepts including software/application design and development, database concepts & structures, and testing techniques
  • Experience with large complex systems, influencing technology solutions that match the goals and objectives of business.
  • Preferred Experience with IBM FTM.
  • Experience with the Agile Methodology.

What you will do:

  • Aligns business strategy with software solutions.
  • Proposes, designs & codes software solutions to address complex business needs. Oversees technical and procedural documentation required.
  • Leads complex problem solving.
  • Provides technical guidance and support to colleagues and solution development.
  • Displays an innovative approach to apply modern principles, methodologies and tools to advance business initiatives and capabilities.

Requirements:

  • University / college degree, with 5-7 years of industry-relevant experience.
  • Specific certifications are often required.
  • In lieu of a degree, a comparable combination of education and experience (including military service) may be considered.

ONLY CITIZENS OR GREEN CARD HOLDERS.

Relocation Assistance Available for ideal candidate.
