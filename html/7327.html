With a significant portfolio of projects to deliver, our client are looking for a seasoned PM to join their Agile team working across high profile projects such as a Data warehouse implementation, Reporting database which is a cloud solution, Master Data Mgmt and an IoT implementation and data capture.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Manage and coordinate 3-4 medium-sized projects and see them through to successful delivery.
  • Produce project documentations such as business plan, project plan etc.
  • Produce monthly reports such as financial reports, forecasts and estimates for executive managers.
  • Manage project risks and internal and external resources and schedules.
  • Build effective stakeholder relationships and collaboratively work with SME’s, existing teams and the PMO.
  • Manage project costs and budgets and business readiness activities
  • Production of tender documents, statement of works, RFI’s, RFP’s & RFQ’s


Requirements:

  • 10+ years of experience in project management in similar environments.
  • Agile certification and or relevant Agile/ Scrum exposure is essential.
  • Bachelor’s degree in a relevant discipline such as ICT, Commerce or Engineering.
  • Project management certification in PRINCE2, PMBOK or equivalent.
  • Proven ability to work with a mix of stakeholders including executives, users, suppliers, external commercial advisors and internal support teams.
  • Demonstrated experience in developing and maintaining project business case and relevant plans (project plan, stage plan, procurement plan, change management plan and benefits realisation plan).
  • Pragmatic application of project life cycle processes, methodologies and technologies.
  • Project scheduling using proprietary tools.
  • Financial management of projects across the entire financial lifecycle.
