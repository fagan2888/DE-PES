    • The company’s origins date back to 2010 when its founders identified a niche in the global finance market and decided to pursue their dream of creating a service dedicated to lives of millions of people in the developing World. Today, it is a stable fin-tech business, with nearly 1000 employees working across the globe, their technology is used in over 150 countries and they continue to grow.


    You could become part of that journey, working as a Senior Data Engineer within the data engineering team and helping to develop the data lake and data warehouse. You will be using AWS, Python and SQL to interpret data from a wide range of sources and deliver it to all parts of the business.


    What’s on offer?

    Salary from £90k-120k per year, private medical and dental care plans, pension scheme offering 8% matched contributions, free breakfast and fruit every day and Friday ‘afternoon tea’ drinks and nibbles, life assurance of 3 times your salary, should the worst happen.


    You will enjoy working here if:

    You write great code, You are happy to work closely with product owners, designers and other engineers to design and refine our work, You own delivery and work beyond just a pull request, You care about bugs, scalability, uptime and other non-functional requirements and You are happy to review other’s work and happily seek feedback on yours.


    What it takes to work here?

    You are experienced in building complex ETL pipelines using Python, You like working in a fast-changing environment, You not only interact with a SQL database but can design new schema as well, You have some previous experience of working with the AWS toolset, You have some knowledge of Big Data related tools and techniques (data lakes, distributed processing platforms, streaming technologies), You’ll be comfortable working with a system hosted in Azure and AWS, You see a problem; you fix a problem, You get buy in for your solutions and keep tickets moving, You are willing to use your skills and experience to mentor less-experienced engineers. A desire to learn from others and make yourself better every day.


    PLEASE NOTE: Unfortunately, we won't be able to shortlist candidates who are not eligible to work in the UK or the EU, or who require a Tier 2 General Visa sponsorship.


    FreshtechIT Limited acts as an Employment Business for contract and temporary recruitment as well as an Employment Agency in relation to permanent vacancies. We always aim to respond to all applications immediately, however, from time to time this may not always be possible, and we thank you in advance for your patience. ​​
