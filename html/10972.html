What is the opportunity?

The Manager of Data Engineering leads and develops a team of Data Engineers providing superior client experience in fulfilling the data needs of our business partners. Acting as the point of contact for our business partners, this role is considered a Data Specialist for the lines of business it is aligned to and is able to curate and combine complex data dashboard requests for those areas. This role ensures a high level of employee capability and engagement through goal setting and coaching routines, empowering their team to reach their full potential.

What will do you?
  • Responsible for the design, development and management of data dashboards requests of medium to high complexity, ensuring timely and accurate responses to each request within acceptable time service standards by the business. (e.g. combining multiple source systems, writing unique or new code, discovering creative ways to deliver on business requirements).
  • Advise stakeholders and business partners on the use and interpretation of custom built reports to highlight business intelligence.
  • Lead, coach, develop and mentor a team of Data Engineers, building a high-performing, diverse team that leverages individual capabilities & strengths
  • Work with the Senior Manager, Practice Excellence and the Senior Manager, Data Engineering and Operations to prioritize service requests and assign to team members, as appropriate.
What will you need to be successful?

Must have
  • Minimum undergraduate degree in Statistics/Mathematics, Finance, Computer Science, or a discipline that develops capability in data analytics and reporting
  • Minimum 5 years’ experience working with financial/insurance products/services and corresponding data
  • Solid working experience with very large relational databases and large volumes of data of various formats
  • Advanced SAS, SQL programming demonstrating the use of efficient querying/datamining techniques
Nice to have
  • Solid working experience with MS Office Professional Suite with emphasis on Visual Basic or Visual Basic for Applications
  • Knowledge of statistical procedures and applied data validation mechanisms
  • Strong knowledge of Dashboard Reporting methodology
  • Solid knowledge of unix/AIX v5, Teradata
  • Experience with Tableau, Netezza and Hadoop
What’s in it for you?
  • A Total Rewards program that includes flexible benefits, work/life balance and career development programs and investment and retirement savings plans
  • Competitive pay and high-earning potential
  • All the tools, training, and team support you need to grow your career
  • Flexible work/life balance options
  • Sophisticated RBCI software tools to boost your productivity
