We Offer

Are you ready to become a part of our Team within our dynamic state-of-the-art organisation that values diversity and offers flexibility? By joining us you will have a chance to build up your career by performing a variety of meaningful tasks
  • The opportunity to become part of our highly motivated data engineering team in Credit Suisse focusing on building and driving new and state of the art technologies in various fields
  • A challenging role as Data Engineer in a dynamic, international and fast paced environment using the latest innovations in Big Data
  • You will work closely with Data Owners, Business and Big Data Project team to clarify requirements
  • You will take care of data discovery and understanding in source systems, collecting metadata, mapping data elements to business data requirements and do data profiling
  • You will involve in preparing technical connectivity specifications for the development team and implementation of data quality checks
  • You will serve as data subject matter expert during user acceptance testing and in discussions with IT partners
  • You will be part of an open-minded team with a strong team spirit that acts in a versatile, dynamic, and flexible working environment
  • A chance to work in a fun team with strong technical mentoring in a new greenfield project where you will be stretched daily and will learn all about technology in finance

Open to discussing flexible/agile working.

  • Knowledge of Agile tools such as JIRA and Confluence

  • Knowledge or familiarity with Unix environment
