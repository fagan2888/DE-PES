Glovo is in a super-fast expansion spiral, rapidly extending into new markets and opening new cities every week. The business model is also evolving while we develop new revenue streams and we need highly talented people to help us improving our data management and analysis.

If you relate to that, then this is a role for you. We’re looking for a data wizard to help us unleashing our growth in Eastern Europe, with a deep understanding of the key drivers of a digital on-demand business like ours and good strategic abilities to proactively provide with game changing insights.

As the position evolves, it is also expected from this person to support forecasting, identify the next big growth levers in the market and lead a team of growth analysts for the region.


You will:

  • Be the go-to person for all things related to data for the Growth team in Eastern Europe

  • Identify growth opportunities for the region to increase our users base, step change retention rates and maximize our profitability

  • Liaise with the data science team - you will play a central, pivotal role in building reliable sources for data and insights

  • Propose strategic changes to the way we track, maintain and use data


Requirements:

  • 4-5  years of data/analytics experience in a fast-paced startup environment, preferably in the on-demand/e-commerce industry

  • Bachelor’s degree in Math, Engineer, Stats or an equivalent quantitative background

  • Knowledge of visualization tools like Looker, Tableau, QlikView, etc..

  • SQL, EXCEL ninja  expertise | Coding experience is a bonus

  • Travel on a need based basis

  • Our core values are very important to us:

    Gas: We think big and take calculated risks to go for all business opportunities, no matter how challenging they are! We work hard as a team and execute fast

    Everyone Wins: We are here for the long run. Glovers, Partners, and Users are in the very center of everything we do. If they win, Glovo wins. So for every decision, we make sure we are positively impacting them

    Good vibes: Our teams are built of people that radiate positive energy and managers that are honest and fair. No assholes allowed! We stay optimistic no matter what, infecting each other with positivity, joy, and the desire to spend time together

    Stay Humble: Always act humble: Everybody must roll up their sleeves and get their hands dirty to make our big plans a reality. We are tiny! We are conscious of how big the opportunity is and how much we’ve got left to do

    Glownership: Act as one company—one team: Care first about Glovo, not your role or your department. Own your stuff, no question, but go beyond your role; help before you are asked. Everything you need to make things happen, you do it. Glow it!

    If you believe you match these values, we look forward to meeting you!

  • Young, international and talented team, used to work in a fast paced and vibrant way - currently, we are over 1000 employees from all over the world!
