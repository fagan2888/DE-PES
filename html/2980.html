    • About eShopWorld

    Founded in 2010, eShopWorld has a mission to build a better online international checkout. Working with some of the world’s leading brands, we provide online services to manage local payment, logistics and online checkout. We have recently been selected by a number of large US based online retailers to support their international expansion plans, and this means we must grow our platform development and client delivery teams.


    Role Description

    This is exciting times for the Data team in eShopWorld, as we are currently in the early stages of designing a sustainable data environment, through Google Cloud, that will provide the foundations for fast, data-driven management decisions, and discover new insights in a growing market.

    This opportunity is a perfect fit for a motivated, curious and talented developer who is interested in building and influencing this design from an architectural direction, performance, security, etc. perspective.


    Key Responsibilities:

    • You should be committed to delivering fast, delivering quality and possess a passion for data and the value it can bring.
    • Working with the rest of the data team on GCP to build a dedicated data architecture to support the needs of eShopWorld and its partner retailers.
    • Concept building, case building and interacting with product teams and business units;
    • Analysis of operational problems and requests for change;
    • Production of technical specifications and estimations of work;
    • Development of solutions and delivery into production;
    • Generation of documentation to support solutions delivered.
    • Peer reviews of work with other team members.


    Desired Experience:

    • 5+ years of relevant experience
    • Strong Java, Python, C# (desirable) Go (desirable) JavaScript (desirable)
    • Google Cloud Platform (BigQuery, Google Storage, DataFlow, Pub/Sub, Cloud Functions, etc.)
    • Apache Beam, Airflow, Kafka desirable
    • Experience with Microsoft Azure is desirable
    • DevOps release management
    • Knowledge of eCommerce would be also an advantage


    Desired Behaviours

    • You should be eager to work in a fast-paced, eCommerce environment.
    • Excellent personal organisation & work-management skills – ability to prioritise multiple tasks.
    • Strong problem-solving skills.
    • Excellent debugging and problem-solving skills
    • High level of dedication to see things through to completion, with high quality.
    • Strong level of versatility & willingness to contribute to a company with phenomenal growth.
    • Willingness to introduce change and challenge existing processes and procedures
