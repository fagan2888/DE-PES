    Budapest:

    At Oracle we are building something great in Budapest. Oracle Applications Labs as part of Oracle Product Development extends its presence in Budapest with a new global technology and innovation competency center, where we want to shape a culture in which challenge, development and innovation happen every day.

    We are a diverse bunch of people who like to analyze, reinvent, and enhance how we work, and all in the pursuit of a better experience for our clients and colleagues. There are limitless problems to solve and opportunities for learning and development, alongside a team of clever and caring colleagues.


    Our culture is one of respect, challenge, stretch and teamwork and we expect and value feedback. We believe that people reach their best potential when the different facets of their home, work and community are all cared about and are in balance.

    We will have a fun, fast paced work environment located in cutting edge facilities, with competitive and innovative benefits, and the ability to define your role, pursue your aspirations and shape your future within Hungary and beyond.


    Business Unit Overview:

    Oracle Applications Labs (OAL) implements, maintains, upgrades and extends the applications that Oracle needs to meet for the rapidly changing business requirements and provides a showcase for Oracle's products.


    Oracle Applications Labs influences the direction and strategy for Oracle's products through collaborative and often pioneering involvement in business applications.

    The OAL New Initiatives Customer Relationship Management Systems (OAL NI CRMS) team collaborates with Business partners and Product Development teams to provide solutions that help drive changes and business transformation, and also help Oracle to be successful in the Cloud.

    The OAL Business Intelligence team designs, develops, and implements Oracle’s business intelligence systems and corporate information warehouses. OAL BI makes Oracle more competitive and profitable by putting actionable intelligence into the hands of executives, managers and staff throughout the company. It is at the forefront of implementing advanced business intelligence technologies. OAL NI CRMS BI in the intersection of OAL NI CRMS and OAL BI is responsible for creating and deploying sales and marketing related BI solutions for Oracle’s sales and marketing organizations all over the world using Oracle and open source technologies.


    Job Description:

    We are looking for a self-driven, creative, agile and experienced Data Warehouse and Business Intelligence specialist to join the global OAL NI CRMS BI team.

    • Are you passionate about implementing complex, cross functions BI solutions and projects?
    • Are you someone with a strong technical background who enjoys working with developers and with business teams?
    • Are you someone who would jump at the opportunity to learn new technologies?

    If so, come join the team of developers to solve complex business problems and implement solutions for Oracle’s internal business team.

    As a BI technical specialist in Oracle Applications Labs’ Business Intelligence team, you will have the opportunity to drive business success by deploying some of the newest and most exciting BI technologies.


    Market leading Oracle products like Exalytics, Exadata, ODI, Business Intelligence Enterprise Edition, BI Applications, Golden Gate, and Endeca extended with selected open source and Big Data technologies give us the capability to offer unique and outstanding solutions to our business users.


    We follow best practices in developing and implementing large scale, best-in-class business intelligence applications for Sales, Marketing, Alliances, Order Management, Financials, Supply Chain, and so on.

    Though we are looking for candidates with strong technical skills in Business Intelligence, Data Warehousing, ETL tools, and Data Integration, the best candidates must demonstrate ability to learn quickly, adapt to change easily and see it as an opportunity, able to see requirements and solutions globally and act in implementation quickly aligned with the team.

    Team members will design, develop, test, and deploy business intelligence applications to help meeting the needs of Oracle’s business organizations.

    Job duties are varied, complex, and challenging.

    The pace of work is quick and demanding.

    Strong technical skills and good judgment are highly valued.

    Successful candidates must be able to work effectively in a collaborative team environment. The ability to communicate effectively with both business users and technical team members is crucial.


    Job duties:

    • Participate in the preparation of project proposals, develop system and user documentation and train end-users.
    • Analyse business requirements, propose design solutions and technology selections
    • Develop DW and BI solution including technology selections, data modelling, ETL, business reports and analyses, data visualizations and beyond.
    • Conduct ad-hoc analyses based on available DW data as needed.
    • Promote and demonstrate team work and best industry practices.
    • Continuously learn emerging new technologies and seek for the possibilities of their adaptation in order to improve continuously the technology stack we use.


    Required skills/experience:

    • Knowledge of the foundations and principles of relational technologies
    • Knowledge of and experience in data modelling foundations (e.g. ER, Relational, Multidimensional, UML)
    • 2 years of experience delivering BI solutions in a medium to large corporate IT setting
    • Experience developing data warehouse solutions and business intelligence systems.
    • Experience with an industry leading DW/ETL/BI infrastructure.
      Skills in OBI EE, Oracle Data Integrator, Oracle Database, Informatica, Endeca, Golden Gate, Big Data or open source streaming technologies are strong plus.
    • Ability to optimize performance of complex reports and SQL statements.
    • Strong written and oral communication skills in English
    • Excellent analytical and problem solving skills
    • Ability to prioritize and multi-task
    • Ability to work in a matrix environment
    • Bachelor’s degree in Statistics, Economics, Computer Science, Engineering, Math, Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Social Sciences or other related technical field.
    • Experience with project management including scope management, risk management, and project delivery with focus on Business Intelligence projects is a plus
    • Experience in agile software development methodologies and tools are a plus.
    • Knowledge of and experience in continuous integration and delivery is a plus.
    • Knowledge of some of the following programming languages and technologies is highly desirable: PL/SQL, Java, Java Sript, Groovy, Python, R, Ruby, Linux, Shell Scripting, Scala, Kafka, Spark, Machine Learning, Data Visualization


    Position is based in Budapest, Hungary, working with a global organization within a local team.

    Amount of Travel: Occasionally, less than 5%
