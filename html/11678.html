Data Engineer

Cpl South Dublin, IE

4 weeks ago

Apply on company website
Responsibilities & Accountabilities
  • Our data sits in multiple client sites all over the world. We want to centralize this, bringing it together to garner real value. You’ll be central to this.
  • Implementation of ETL applications with SSIS / Informatica / Talend, Data warehousing principles (Kimball Methodology), architecture and its implementation in event driven message environments
  • Once we have the data we want to mine, create new models and visualizations telling more complete and better stories. You’ll be able to contribute in big way to how this happens.
  • Business as usual needs to happen, so we’re looking for someone with an inquisitive mindset who just wants to get the job done.
  • Legacy processes are there to be followed, sometimes we must, sometimes they can be improved, you have a suggestion let’s talk about it and get to the right answer.
  • Generating metrics and reports and presenting them periodically
  • Combining analytics methods with advanced data visualizations produce dashboards using third party tools
  • Working within an Agile Environment, therefore the nature of our work can pivot.

Skills & Experience Required
  • Database and data warehouse development, to include creating complex functions, stored procedures, materialized views, and partition schemes
  • Previous experience with RDBS (SQL Server, Oracle, Sybase, PostgresSQL) or MPP (Teradata, Netezza) or NoSql (Azure HDInsight, Azure Data warehadateouse, Cloudera Impala)
  • Exposure to NoSql database tools (Azure HDInsight , Data Lake, Spark)
  • Understanding of columnar oriented data format (Parquet / ORC) for supporting the advanced data science Production experience in Microsoft / .Net development
  • Working knowledge of OLAP and data visualization (Power BI, Tableau)
  • Experience with basic DBA tasks (DR, indexing, performance tuning, partitioning)
  • Experience with data blending tools (e.g. SAS, SPSS, Dataiku, Alteryx)
  • Experience with OLAP technology on HDInsight Experience with Azure event / Queues
  • Understand concepts of metrics, SLAs and KPIs in context of software engineering
  • Good communication skills (ability to present reports and findings) is a must
  • Experience in data cleansing; identifying gaps and making these gaps historical.
  • Degree in Computer Science or a similar related discipline
