Who is it for? The client is a  predictive analytics company run by world-class quantitative marketing scientists and industry-experienced data team.


In this role, you will implement and deploy data analysis pipelines at the various client's locations and also support and overlook the work of other data engineers. This includes participating in and supervising the activities below:


  • Understand deeply the business problem we are trying to solve by our analytical solution

  • Through continuous consultations with employees of our client, discover the client's existing data sources that are relevant to the problem we try to solve.

  • Working together with the IT teams of the client, define the technical architecture for the analytical solution that we are to deploy for the client.

  • Implement the data ingestion subsystem: this is the system responsible for moving all the necessary data sources to a single location where the actual analysis will happen. This is a challenging task as all clients use different database and data storage technologies and hence the ingestion system is always very different at different clients

  • Implement the data analysis pipelines. This needs close collaboration with data scientists who oversee the data analysis and modeling techniques to solve the business challenge.

  • Integrate the results into business UIs developed by the team or pre-existing client software systems.


What the client is looking for

    • Relevant tertiary qualification, preferably at Masters level or above, in Engineering or another relevant discipline with strong academic results

    • Experience in data engineering or data science, preferably in analytics or machine-learning environment (or relevant organization) and with a B2B focus

    • Strong programming skills, including thorough knowledge of Python (and ideally Scala)

    • Good understanding of the Linux OS including basic sysadmin and shell scripting abilities

    • Good understanding of databases and related technologies, e.g. SQ

    • Experience in project delivery

    • Ability to be client-facing, with strong interpersonal skill. Analytical thinking and very strong problem-solving skills

    • Strong understanding of various Big data technologies.

    • Willingness to travel (up to 50%) throughout the region
