Main Activities:

  • Document and follow up with hardware and software issues, Windows & MAC OS, MS Office.
  • Troubleshoot technology problems according to escalation protocol.
  • Maintain hardware and software inventory.
  • Assist in testing new hardware and software prior to implementation.
  • Document software license compliance.
  • Install/upgrade operating system and standard workstation software.
  • Cooperation with IT teams of CEMEX and IBM within proper investigation and dealing with incidents.
  • Administration, taking care of invoices, obtaining new HW based on requests, assignment of HW.
  • Management of HW suppliers, negotiate prices and new contracts.
  • Training of newcomers, end-users and suggest process improvements.

Key Requirements:

  • Excellent English and Czech communication skills both verbal and written, Spanish is plus
  • 2+ years of experience in administrative operations, troubleshooting hardware and software issue
  • Advanced knowledge of Windows 10, MAC OS
  • Knowledge of MS Office products, MS Office 2013, MS Office 365
  • Experience with basic administration of Active Directory and MS Exchange 2010
  • Experience with network monitoring and troubleshooting, knowledge Ethernet, TCP/IP, DHCP, DNS
  • Understanding of cybernetic security VPN, Firewall
  • Basic knowledge of conference systems as Cisco Videoconference, Zoom Room etc.
  • Experience working with different cultures (preferred), open-minded person, patient and tolerant, to do attitude, prioritization skills

We offer

  • Possibility to work in friendly multicultural start-up environment, using English on daily basis and growing professionally within worldwide NEORIS;
  • We offer guaranteed professional growth through the technology trainings and technology communities inside NEORIS & CEMEX Group;
  • Large space for self-fulfilment and participation in new projects;
  • Benefit program (5 weeks of vacation, 3 sick days, meal vouchers, flexi pass, up to 1000 CZK of Pension insurance, free of charge multisport card, language courses, fruits & vegetables...);
  • Comfortable and modern offices in Prague 5 – next to metro station Anděl;
  • Start ASAP / Negotiable (ideally start in middle of November).
