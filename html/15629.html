Systems Business Analyst

Brown Brothers Harriman Kraków, PL

5 dni temu

Aplikuj w witrynie firmy
Brown Brothers Harriman is looking for a Systems Business Analyst to join Security Master File Systems Team. In this role you will be a part of a global product-oriented team having real influence on the project. You will work as liaison between technical and business user groups. You will interpret what the user’s needs are and translate those requirements into detailed specifications; take part in solution design, development, testing and production deployment.

Key Responsibilities
  • Facilitate communication between business users and technical teams
  • Analyze business requirements and creating functional specifications for application developers which may include data modelling, dataflow diagramming, screen design, workflow analysis
  • Act as systems project coordinator for assigned projects, including tracking to project milestones, ensuring proper SDLC documentation exists, creating formal requests for software changes, coordinating implementation playlists with technical lead/developers
  • Act as the subject matter expert for assigned business applications and business lines
  • Involvement in design, development and deployment process
  • Work closely with business users, developers, infrastructure teams and external vendors to provide efficient solutions which meet business needs

Requirements
  • Master’s or bachelor’s degree (from area of Accounting, Banking, Economics, Finance, Computer Science or Management Information Systems preferred)
  • 2+ years of experience in a similar role
  • Security Reference Management experience would be an asset
  • Expert analytical skills
  • Great communication and organizational skills
  • Financial services experience and related business knowledge
  • Understanding of Systems Development Life Cycle
  • Fluent English
  • Technical knowledge (e.g. programming skills, knowledge of SQL, database design, infrastructure, system integration methods) is preferred

What We Offer
  • A collaborative environment that enables you to step outside your role to add value wherever you can
  • Direct access to end users, information and experts across all business areas around the world
  • Opportunities to grow your expertise, take on new challenges, and reinvent yourself — without leaving the firm
  • A culture of inclusion that values each employee’s unique perspective
  • High-quality benefits program emphasizing good health, financial security, and peace of mind
  • Rewarding work with the flexibility to enjoy personal and family experiences at every career stage
  • Volunteer opportunities to give back to your community and help transform the lives of other
