Aplikuj w witrynie firmy
The Akamai Carrier Business Unit provide advanced applications for major ISPs around the world that enforce Security at the DNS layer, deployments of DNS, and especially for Security Applications (Akamai SPS) that provide ISPs innovative network-based security products (security, content filtering, messaging). The business has four major data science tasks-
  • Analyze petabytes of DNS/Proxy data that are shared from our ISP customers and constitute the equivalent of a massive honey pot of network-based data to find attacks (on the network (DDOS), phishing, malware).
  • Choose and analyze security feeds and/or content feeds from over 100 partners.
  • Work with other teams at Akamai to incorporate information from our security products into the Carrier products.
  • Work with product management and customers to demonstrate that our products are effective, show value from our 1st party data, find new threats in the data.
In order to deliver on these tasks, we need to develop and deploy an innovative data ingest and data analytics capabilities. Today this consists of using Hadoop/Spark/streaming analysis of the DNS data above, other sources of security information (e.g. VirusTotal data, information from web-crawling suspect domains, etc.), and implementing special purpose Machine Learning algorithms to discover new and evolving threats. Handling the scale of this data and building out these components is a big challenge. This role bridges the gap between our data science team and the real-world application of these algorithms to real production data.

This role is for a great software engineer who also have experience improving algorithms for Machine learning and developing/deploying these algorithms in a cloud environment.We are looking for someone who can start the group and understand what we have today and then can build out and manage a team that builds out these components and works with an existing devops team to evolve these services.

About The Team

Akamai’s Carrier Division is a dynamic “start-in“group that defines, develops, and sells Akamai’s carrier products to fixed and mobile carriers. The group is responsible for Akamai’s Aura Network Solutions and incorporates the strength of Akamai’s globally distributed computing system packaged into products and sold to network service providers (Telcos, ISPs, MSOs). The division collaborates closely with the other product and engineering groups within Akamai as well many external partners and customers.

Required Education And Experience

Applicants must meet one of the following education and experience requirements-
  • 10 years of relevant experience and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or its equivalent or its equivalent
  • 6 years of relevant experience and a Master’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or its equivalent
  • 3 years of relevant experience and a PhD in Data Science, Computer Science or its equivalent
  • Ability to mentor and leader other team members
  • Experience with implementation around Big Data systems, such as Hadoop, Spark, etc.
  • Experience translating high-level algorithms from data researchers into scalable, production, code.
  • Experience with cloud-based deployment techniques (e.g. Docker, Kubernetes, etc.)
