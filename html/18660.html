  • ASAP start
  • 6-12 months contract
  • Melbourne CBD location
My client, a large and reputable organisation is currently looking for an experienced Data Scientist to join their diverse set of researchers, analysts and machine learning engineers.

Responsibilities

Successful candidtae will be responsible for:
  • Mine and analyze data from all available data sources to support product development efforts
  • Track product usage, build churn models and present actionable insights to leadership teams
  • Design, monitor and analyze controlled experiments
  • Conduct a review and prepare a recommendations paper to build a data base that will cover off ongoing review of fees, complaint process and remediation process.


To be considered, you will have the following experiences
  • Extensive experience in a digital or technology role with data science
  • Previous banking experience would be beneficial
  • Proficiency with Postgres SQL (or any form of SQL) is required
  • Expertise in the use of data science tools such as R, Python, Julia,SQL, MapReduce, Hadoop, Hive and Big Data technologies
  • Significant experience extracting, transforming and working with large sets of data
  • Experience working with large amounts of structured and unstructured data
  • Strong analytical, strategic and creative problem solver
  • Ability to remain calm under pressure and strict deadlines
