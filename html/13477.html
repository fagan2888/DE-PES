Unstructured Data Solutions (UDS) Systems Engineer – Inside Sales

General Summary

Candidate will provide technical expertise in support of Pre-Sales activities and maintaining specialist level technical acumen. Candidate will assist in the analysis, design and development of fully integrated technology solutions. Technical emphasis is focused on EMC’s Unstructured Storage Solutions, Isilon and ECS, as well as integrating EMC solutions with non-EMC solutions. Candidate will assist Sales in developing opportunities, delivering product demonstrations, customer presentations, and responding to RFPs.

Principal Duties And Responsibilities
  • Qualifies complex sales opportunities in the terms of customer technical requirements, competition, decision making process and funding.
  • Responsible for designing complex solutions to protect customer’s data using EMC’s suite of products
  • Presents the design and value of proposed solution and business case to customer or prospects remotely, via webex.
  • Delivers technical training to peers, product specialists, and sales teams
  • Able to vocalized complex ideas in a way that can be easily understandable to individuals with varying levels of product knowledge
  • Able to pick up new concepts quickly and be able to relay them to others in a concise, succinct manner via PowerPoint or whiteboard trainings, or in one on one discussions
  • Possess the ability to be the SME on EMC’s UDS portfolio in order to facilitate support for channel partners and internal sales and presales teams
  • Leverages cross-functional resources to achieve results/meet customer needs
  • Understands , analyzes and applies industry and market knowledge to position value of the solution
  • Understands and articulates strengths and vulnerabilities of the competition
  • Must be able to multitask and deal with requests for information from multiple avenues in a timely fashion
  • Communication skill
  • Problem solving skill

Desired Skills
  • Deep expertise or knowledge of next generation storage architectures: NAS and Object
  • Understanding of basic Data Center concepts, like rack sizing, power requirements
  • Knowledge of the multiple potential use cases for ECS and Isilon: Archiving, Backup, Media and Entertainment, Health Sciences, AI, IoT, etc.
  • Knowledge of networking concepts
  • Understanding and/or knowledge of Windows, LINUX/UNIX, Hyper-V, and VMware
  • Experience with any/or of the following: SMB, NFS, HDFS
  • Understand Cloud concepts
  • Time management skills
  • EMC Proven Professional Certification desired

Education Required: Bachelors (Tech) or equivalent

Experience Required: Minimum of 3 years of Presales experience

Business Travel Required: As required
