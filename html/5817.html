Job Summary / Introduction

ANDRITZ stands for passion, partnership, perspectives and versatility - core values to which the company is committed. The listed Group is headquartered in Graz, Austria. With almost 170 years of experience, 29,000 employees, and more than 280 locations in over 40 countries worldwide, ANDRITZ is a reliable and competent partner and helps its customers to achieve their corporate and sustainability goals.

Detailed Job Description

ANDRITZ Pulp and Paper has a Full-Time opportunity for a Hydrocyclone Process Engineer . Training will be held in our Montoursville, PA office and out in the field.

As Hydrocyclone Process Engineer, you will serve as a technical specialist supporting the Hydrocyclone product line. You will provide support services for customers utilizing Hydrocyclone parts and systems that are supplied by ANDRITZ. You will conduct mill audits in order to help identify and make recommendations on effective modifications or upgrades needed to help promote system efficiencies of mill operation. You must be able to identify problems; research proper solutions and help customers implement these changes successfully. This position requires at least 75% of travel time . Air travel is required. International travel may be required.

  • Must have a mechanical aptitude
  • Must have strong interpersonal skills with the ability to effectively communicate verbally and build on customer relationship.
  • Must be able to manage a territory and must be self-motivated to complete all responsibilities of the position.
