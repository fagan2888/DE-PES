Senior UI Engineer
We’re Cricket Australia. Our purpose is to unite and inspire communities through Cricket. As a truly national function, Technology plays a key role in enabling this. We build and operate cutting edge solutions to help each member of the Australian Cricket workforce perform at their best in the service of our volunteers and participants across the nation.
An exciting new opportunity is now available for an experienced and motivated Senior UI Engineer to join our Technology team.
In this role, you will be responsible for delivering engaging user experience across our suite of digital products, from websites to mobile apps. You will be part of a team that is responsible for nurturing a set of products that include iterating on ideas based on insights and supporting it in production. As a Senior UI Engineer you will work closely with product managers and designers so that the design and technology considerations create an extraordinary experience.
At Cricket Australia, we aspire to have the #1 cricket digital network in the world and this role is key to realising this aspiration.
In This Role You Will
Technical leadership and direction in ensuring our experiences not only meet our users expectations but also our technology direction
  • Contribute to the UI development practice
  • Work with our team to contribute towards our strategy and goal of continuous delivery
  • Work collaboratively within the team
  • Support the delivery of outstanding solutions that address Australian Cricket’s needs
To Be Successful In Your Application, You Will Have
This is a fantastic opportunity to join the highly successful Technology team.
  • 5+ years experience in software development
  • Strong experience in HTML5, CSS3, ES6 & JS framworks (React is a must), Node/ Express and related tooling system
  • Good understanding of responsive design, browser behaviour and compatibility
  • Good understanding of optimisation techniques to improve client-side performance, good practices and industry standard
  • Good experience in developing with common open APIs such as Facebook, YouTube, Twitter.
  • Ability to help set up and configure local development environments including exposure to build systems such as Gulp, Grunt or Webpack.
  • Good knowledge of SMACSS, OOCSS or BEM concepts.
  • Good experience with source control (trunk based development preferable) and branch strategies
  • Skills in modern software development techniques such as continuous delivery, test driven development, pair programming, etc
  • Cloud technology experience (Azure preferable)
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
The work we do at Cricket Australia stretches across the sporting and entertainment industries - which means there are all kinds of opportunities for bright-minded, passionate and friendly people.
Cricket’s a sport for all Australians and Cricket Australia’s a workplace that reflects that. Your age, gender, sexual orientation, disability or religion won’t impact whether or not you’re part of the team. It’s your drive to do great work that’ll help you shine.
At Cricket Australia we work flexibly, offer a competitive salary package and extensive employee benefits including, salary packaging, gym access and discounts with our corporate partners.
Ready to see what you can do? Then apply now - we are looking forward to meeting you!
Applications close 30 October 2019, 12pm.
