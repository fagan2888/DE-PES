Senior Developer (Big Data) - Alcobendas- Madrid, Spain



Who We Are


Solera is a global leader in data and software services that strives to transform every touchpoint of the vehicle lifecycle into a connected digital experience. In addition, we provide products and services to protect life’s other most important assets: our homes and digital identities. Today, Solera processes over 300 million digital transactions annually for approximately 235,000 partners and customers in more than 90 countries. Our 6,500 team members foster an uncommon, innovative culture and are dedicated to successfully bringing the future to bear today through cognitive answers, insights, algorithms and automation. For more information, please visit solera.com.

The Role We are looking for a Product Manager that will drive the development of our state-of-the-art Software for the Automotive Claim Industry



The Role


We are looking for a Talented Software Engineer & Big Data Expert with extensive experience working with Hadoop ecosystems and related Big Data technologies (Hadoop, Spark, HBase).


What You’ll Do


  • Write new and reusable code to support our in-house applications in an agile, test driven development environment. We use Scala and Java across big data clusters and microservice cloud architectures.
  • Develop scalable batch and streaming applications using Hadoop, being responsible for their end-to-end lifecycle (including design, development, test and deployment)
  • Support in ETL, Data analysis and modelling tasks. Integrating massive amounts of data from different sources.
  • Support continuous improvement by investigating possible alternatives and new technologies.
  • Follow the agreed quality standards and testing coverage best practices of the organization.
  • Develop, execute and maintain manual and automated test scripts
  • Provide technical guidance and coaching to less experienced developers
  • Showcase agile methodologies and prove its potential to achieve ambitious objectives quickly and in a structured manner

What You’ll Bring


Must Have:


  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or numerate discipline
  • 5+ years of recent experience being a hands-on developer with Java and/or Scala
  • 2 years of practical experience with Hadoop 2.0 and related big data technologies (Spark, Kafka, Spark SQL, Hive).
  • Practical working knowledge in relational databases (e.g. Oracle or Postgres), as well as modern persistency mechanisms (e.g. HBase, Mongo)
  • Hands-on experience with Enterprise search engines (Solr, Elasticsearch).


Highly Desirable :


  • Knowledge of streaming analytics (Spark Streaming, Storm..)
  • Exposure to microservice architecture and understanding of the core principles.
  • Experience working in an agile environment


You don't fit a cookie-cutter job description – your can-do attitude and wide experience of software development best practices allow you to contribute to the team by any means necessary.
