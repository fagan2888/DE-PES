Data Scientist/Machine Learning Engineer - Kendall Square

An NLP company in Kendall Square is looking to bring on experienced Data Scientists and Machine Learning Engineers to help expand their natural language processing platform. This company is at the forefront of machine learning and AI, and their platform analyzes customer service conversations from enterprise companies to improve communication and product. Experienced Data Scientists and Machine Learning Engineers are encouraged to apply. Software Engineers with a focus in Big Data should apply as well.

Required Skills & Experience
  • 5+ years of Python development experience
  • A background in Machine Learning or Natural Language Processing
  • Either a Master’s, PhD, or significant experience with Data Science or Big Data


Desired Skills & Experience
  • A background in analyzing massive amounts of data
  • The ability to be client-facing
  • Experience in algorithm development


What You Will Be Doing

Daily Responsibilities
  • Collaborating with a master team of Engineers
  • Consulting clients on algorithmic and data analysis
  • Working at the forefront of Machine Learning engineering

The Offer
  • Up to 160K base salary


You Will Receive The Following Benefits
  • Medical Insurance & Health Savings Account (HSA)
  • 401(k)
  • Paid Sick Time Leave
  • Pre-tax Commuter Benefit


Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the United States on a full-time basis now and in the future.

This position does not offer sponsorship.

Workbridge Associates, part of the Motion Recruitment network, provides IT Staffing Solutions (Contract, Contract-to-Hire, and Direct Hire) across 11 major North American markets. Our unique expertise in today’s highest demand tech skill sets, paired with our deep networks and knowledge of our local technology markets, results in an exemplary track record with candidates and clients.

Peter Hammel is leading the search for this position.

Compensation: $130K-160K DOE
