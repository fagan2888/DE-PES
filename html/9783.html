HENKEL IS FOR THOSE WHO STEP UP. DO YOU?

At Henkel, you can make a difference and craft your career. That’s why you own your projects and take full responsibility from an early stage. Our unique brands in markets around the world open up countless opportunities to follow your convictions and explore new paths. If you have an entrepreneurial mindset that allows you to always think out of the box - take the chance and shape the digital future together with us.

YOUR ROLE
  • Develop, implement, and maintain new and existing data pipelines within our data lake
  • Create use-case specific data assets in collaboration with our data scientists and business users
  • Develop understanding of Henkel’s rich data landscape
  • Monitor and debug ETL pipelines
  • Maintain/enrich our automated data catalog
  • Ensure proper data governance including GDPR and our internal rules for data processing

Your Skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Business Informatics, Computer Science, or any relevant study
  • Minimum two years of experience as data engineer
  • Fluent English speaker
  • Knowledge about one or more general programming languages: practical experience with Python and others like Scala
  • Experience with SQL and non-SQL data bases, as well with ETL processes
  • Experience with Hadoop Map-Reduce, Spark, Hive, Databricks or similar technologies
  • Knowledge about cloud platform data tools, preferred Azure, AWS or others
  • Basic knowledge about SAP connectors and data structures is a plus
  • Experience in front-end development; JavaScript is a plus
