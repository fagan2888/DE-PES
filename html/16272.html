    Your mission You lead the analysis, development and implementation of new solutions or make evolve existing ones (i.e. based on business, operational or regulatory requirements,) on the Subscriber Data Management and Diameter Routing Function technical domains. You have the end-to-end responsibility for taking all the appropriate actions from the requirements to the move in production.

    As a Senior Solutions Engineer – Subscriber Data Management & Diameter Routing Function, you:
    • Analyze and assess technical solutions and evolution to fulfil business, regulatory and operational (functional and capacity) requirements
    • Lead medium to large size implementation projects and coordinate (and coach if necessary) a core team for the solution or evolution implementation
    • Write functional and technical specifications and requests for proposal.
    • Evaluate and make recommendations for the future considering the technical evolutions on the market
    • Define in collaboration with the supplier, the technical part of the contract (Scope of Work, Project Management Plan, Professional Services). Supports purchasing (i.e. cost of services) and analyze the requests for proposals (supplier selection).
    • Generate methods, procedure and technical documents regarding technical solution and evolution (architecture, impact analysis).
    • Conduct the implementation of the technical solutions (in collaboration with the integration engineers if needed)
    • Act as a technical interface with Orange Group, suppliers, other operators, regulatory institutions, to gather requirements and technical solutions, challenge Orange Belgium (OBE) or Group solutions
    • Analyze performance statistics/indicators, network behavior, operational processes, tools, and service architectures; identify gaps, problems and risks and make recommendations on improvement opportunities

    Your specific expertise: Subscriber Data Management :
    • Experience with:

    Nokia HLR, Nokia HSS, Nokia One-NDS or Registers, Nokia AAA, Nokia EIR, Nokia ETL, NetAct, Archive Cloud, Ultimaco HSM

    Provisioning flows and customer profiles

    Experience with 5G features would be a plus

    Diameter Routing Function
    • Experience with:

    Oracle DSR

    Diameter interfaces for mobile networks: S6a, Gx, Rx, S13, SWm, VoLTE…

    Interconnections including with MVNOs

    • Deep knowledge of:

    -trends in networking industry and servers products (Dell)

    -network Operations Support Systems and corresponding protocols (SNMP, Syslog, fault management, performance management, inventory, etc.)

    - Good knowledge of mobile telecommunication services and technologies

    In general :
    • Strong analytical skills including data collection and analyzing, reporting, problem handling etc.
    • Capable of finding and processing technical documentation and data
    • Experience with remote overseas operation teams (Europe)
    • Extensive knowledge and experience with project management

    Your profile
    • Bachelor degree in a telecommunications/IT technology or equivalent level of experience
    • Min. 5 years of experience in the technical domain Subscriber Data Management and Diameter Routing Function
    • Knowledge of Networks, Telecommunications, IT environment and IP protocols.
    • Experience with remote overseas operation teams (Europe)
    • Communication skills as well as listening skills
    • Customer orientated and service minded
    • Team player
    • Bilingual : English and Dutch or French

    Discover our offer!

    You will make the difference in this highly innovative vibe. As a result, you will develop your full potential by working with highly skilled colleagues and through continuous training. Moreover, you can count on a highly competitive and versatile compensation and benefits package.

    Oh, and one more thing : working here is fun. Ask our people, during one of our many team events and company get togethers.

    Inspired? Please send us your resume and we will rapidly get back to you to invite you for further interviews.
