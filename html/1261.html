  • Senior Frontend Developer | Leisure Product Company | West End | £75-80 k


A Product Company located in the West End are expanding their Engineering team and have a requirement for a new Senior Frontend Developer.


The business started over 5 years ago and has gone from strength to strength year on year since. Their product/ service is simple, in demand and unique - They now operate in over 150 countries and have customers all over the globe. Their success and annual growth means they need to constantly improve and enhance their offering to keep their place at the forefront of the market.


They are always striving to improve and looking for new ways to innovate and use technology to provide their customers with the best experience/ service possible.

As a Frontend Developer you will help spearhead and drive their frontend strategy to create a market leading platform. You’ll use JavaScript to create a beautiful, engaging and eye grabbing UI that sets their products apart whilst keeping things simple and easy to use.


They are consumer focused, passionate about technology and data driven. They understand the importance of technology and have created an environment whereby Engineers can flourish, learn and have fun whilst doing what they do best.


The culture is everything you’d expect from a market leading tech company – Collaborative, ambitious, team-orientated and fun. I can provide more information on the project roadmap and what your role as a Senior Frontend Engineer will be if you are interested..


Skills required:

  • JavaScript/ ES6
  • OO Programming
  • Webpack
  • React/ Redux
  • BDD/ TDD
  • Agile


The role:

  • Frontend Developer (mid-senior)
  • Product Company – Leisure/ Tech
  • Based in the West End
  • Up to £70,000
  • Excellent culture/ Start-up
  • Backed by large company
