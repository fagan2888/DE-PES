Using neuroscience-based assessments and machine learning algorithms, pymetrics is reinventing the recruiting industry by matching candidates to jobs and companies where they are most likely to succeed. We are leading the charge in an evolving industry, and growing our amazing team to support the mission of using data to unleash one's full potential.


We’re looking for a talented Quality Engineer to join our agile Engineering team, which consists of developers who value shipping quality code, owning their products, learning from one another, and having a blast doing it. The ideal candidate will create comprehensive test strategies while contributing to our test automation frameworks. This is a hands-on role. The candidate will participate in internal testing events, collaborate with product feature scrum teams as new features are developed, and ensure release timelines are met.


Responsibilities include:

  • Plan, create, and execute both manual and automated tests
  • Develop test plans for functional and end-to-end regression by collaborating with the cross-functional team and identify tests for automation
  • Improve existing test automation frameworks and support engineers in writing additional UI tests
  • Perform exploratory testing and assist in the design of test automation
  • Maintain existing UI tests, monitor failures and report bugs
  • Design and apply test strategy to improve efficiency in manual testing


Current Technologies/Tools:

  • Python/Django
  • Angular/React/Javascript
  • Amazon Web Services
  • Docker/Packer/Terraform/Travis CI
  • Github/Jira/Testrail
  • Selenium


Requirements

  • Can-do engineer with demonstrated ability to thrive in a startup environment, get work done, ship product, and move us forward
  • Expert in developing and documenting test plans, test cases and reporting bugs
  • Experience in building automation frameworks and writing reliable automated tests
  • Solid experience working with other teams such as engineering and product teams
  • Displays a passion for learning, especially with implementing new processes and tools that benefit the engineering teams
