The Opportunity

We’re looking for a Software Engineer to help us shape the future of education. With nearly 20,000 organizational customers and millions of student users, Blackboard is the world's leading education technology company with the industry's most comprehensive student-success solutions. Our mission is to partner with the global education community to enable student and institutional success by leveraging innovative technologies and services.

In this role you'll work on Blackboard SafeAssign, a truly SaaS product that offers industry-leading security, encryption, and data residency requirements. It integrates with learning management systems to compare submitted assignments against a set of academic papers to identify areas of overlap between the submitted assignment and existing works. Both instructors and students can use SafeAssign originality reports to review assignment submissions for originality and create opportunities for learners to understand how to properly write and express themselves.

Specific Responsibilities Will Include

As Software Engineer, you will work in a team environment, collaborating on the design, implementation, and support of Blackboard’s products.
  • Collaborating with teammates and others to design solutions to complex problems
  • Implementing new features within SafeAssign product
  • Contributing to the ongoing support and improvement of SafeAssign product
  • Working with a variety of software development technologies, tools, and techniques (e.g. Java, Linux, Agile/Test-Driven Development, etc.)
  • Developing a deep expertise in certain areas of the product to serve the primary point of contact for support and consultation

Required Skills

The Candidate:
  • 4+ years of proven hands-on software development experience
  • Exceptional abstract thinking and ability to understand concepts of OO programming and design
  • Profound knowledge of programming in Java
  • Knowledge of technologies for creating Java-based web applications
  • Knowledge of SQL and good orientation in database programming in Java
  • Command line scripting knowledge in Linux-like environment
  • Very good command of written and spoken English
  • Good team player
  • Eligible to travel to the USA

Preferred Skills
  • Knowledge of AWS
  • Experience using IntelliJ Idea or Eclipse IDE, Sublime, VIM(m)
  • Experience with a version control system (Git, Perforce, etc.)
  • Experience with a bug tracking system (Bugzilla, JIRA)
  • Build tools experience (Ant, Maven, etc.)
  • Knowledge of JavaScript, Struts, Spring, Hibernate
  • Experience with web application server technologies (Tomcat or similar)
  • Experience with databases (Oracle, SQL Server, and PostgreSQL)

The Team & Office

Blackboard has an office in the beautiful and vibrant city of Brno.
  • Great office location in city center near Luzanky
  • Accessible by public transportation as well as bike, with bike stands and showers in the office
  • Cozy personal office spaces where you can shut your door to really focus or leave it open to interact with others
  • Flexible work hours with no expectations of overtime
  • Great benefits including health & life insurance and English lessons
  • Countless walkable places to eat including Stará Pekárna, U Bílého beránka, and U Dřeváka Beer & Grill
