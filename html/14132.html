SENIOR DATA ENGINEER

My client – a top tier-1 Asset Manager - is currently looking for an experienced Data Engineer to join their tech team. In this role, you will be supporting the quants team providing them with a solid data platform.

They are looking for someone with a solid ETL and DW background as well as a good knowledge of cloud technologies. Ideally, you will have a good understanding of the wealth management industry (equities, fixed income, etc.).

Are you keen to know more about it? Send me your CV and I will reach out to you!

Job Responsibilities – Senior Data Engineer
  • Understand business requirements, design and develop ETL solutions
  • Develop an innovative and business-oriented data framework and improve your skills working on the latest technologies such as AWS
  • Develop scripts to automate the processing of ETL execution
  • relationships with different stakeholders


Requirements – Senior Data Engineer
  • Relevant experience with AWS and SQL

  • Business acumen and good communication skills
