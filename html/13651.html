Data Engineer

PharmiWeb.Jobs Oxford, GB

1 month ago

Duration: Permanent

Salary: £40,000-55,000 d.o.e

A Big Data Engineer is required to join a market leading organisation that are at the forefront of using Data Analytics. You will be joining the company's already well-established Analytics and Data Science team who are using cutting-edge technology with major clients. You will be helping to deliver the data solutions to clients, so you must understand what the client needs and deliver solutions to fulfill the needs of the client.

Your position as a Big Data Engineer focuses upon using the firm's existing cutting-edge technologies to undertake tasks such as creating Data Lakes and Data Platforms. You will be working in a number of industries and working with all types of data. You will gain experience in Data Science as you will be working alongside people who are specialists in the field.

Requirements

Below is not a list of requirements but technologies that you will have exposure to in the position

In this role, it is crucial that you have experience as handling the dataflows between algorithms.
  • Hadoop eco-system (Scoop, Hive, Kafka)
  • AWS
  • Python
  • Machine Learning
Please note that these are not a list of requirements, but a list of the technologies you will have exposure to.

This is a brand-new requirement and my client are looking to fill the position as soon as possible. They are looking to line up interviews very quickly so if this role is of interest please send your CV to a.desmit@x4group.co.uk, or call Adam on 0207 812 7700 for more information.
