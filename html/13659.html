Description

Are you a Developer, Sr Systems Engineer, or Sr Systems Administrator with a deep understanding of Amazon Web Services, Active Directory, security, and Windows system management? Can you build and automate solutions that empower customers and reduce support costs? If the answer to these questions is YES, then the Security Innovation, Data, and Engineering (SIDE) team at Amazon.com wants to talk to you.

The SIDE team's mission is to deliver robust, highly available, and easy-to-use services to our internal customers across the global enterprise. We are looking for an engineer who is an expert in IT infrastructure technologies with a focus on Microsoft Windows Server, Linux, Java, Active Directory, Microsoft SQL Server, Amazon Web Services, and assorted Third-Party applications. As a Sr Systems Development Engineer you will help design and build the future of physical security systems infrastructure at Amazon while maintaining the current global infrastructure. To be successful in this position you must have a strong desire to automate, lead, and be comfortable taking initiative.

You will be responsible for implementation, monitoring and maintaining the critical systems that all Amazonians depend on. This also includes technical service ownership, system design, capacity planning, and handling on-call technical support. As one of the few teams running enterprise Windows systems at Amazon there will be initiatives to create novel solutions to issues that no other company has solved yet.


Basic Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or related field, or 5+ years relevant work experience.
  • 5+ years experience in at least one modern Object Oriented programming language such as Java, C#, or C++
  • 2+ years operating enterprise applications in a cloud environment
  • Experience with cloud design patterns and service-oriented architecture
  • Demonstrated application of information security best practices
  • Demonstrated ability to program to solve problems and automate repetitive tasks in a common scripting or programming language such as Perl, PowerShell, and/or VBScript
  • Leadership experience with track record of driving projects to completion
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience administrating Linux-based systems
  • Experience with Linux based LDAP solutions and/or Microsoft Active Directory
  • 3+ years building web-based applications using HTML, jQuery, Ajax, CSS (without Flash, Shockwave, Silverlight)
  • Flexibility, thinking on your feet, and strong problem solving skills
  • Proven record of driving vendor adoption of best practices for service-oriented architecture, ITIL operations framework, and information security
  • Experience building highly-scalable globally-distributed systems
  • Familiarity with Amazon builder tools, monitoring systems, and infrastructure management tools.
  • Demonstrable track record dealing well with ambiguity, prioritizing needs, and delivering measurable results in an agile environment
  • Clear, professional communication skills able to contribute to team discussions, knowing when to contribute, when to listen, and when to ask questions
