About the Company


SocialTalent - tell me more


We are a SaaS company that offers a complete learning solution to hiring teams all over the world. But we’re much more than that, we’re leading a recruitment revolution and we’re on a mission to make people work smarter, teams happier and companies more successful. Through our platform, we focus on the key aspects of changing and sustaining productive behaviour. We combine analytics, expert content and reward software, to measure, change and reward teams' learning and work behaviours. Think on demand learning!


At SocialTalent, we believe that our people and our culture are the backbone of our success and we try to live and breathe our values every day. We’re looking for pretty special people to join our team and help spread our mission globally. The good news is that we have a great solution and our customers love us! We already have more than 1,000 enterprise customers and have learners in more than 90 countries.


Job Description


Seeking: Unicorn or other mythical creature, bringing your experience in software engineering, your side hustle in data science, and current life experience as a Business Analyst, to a job you’ll love.


We know there are only 14 of you in the world, we know your names, and we’re delighted that you’re reading this. Because we want need you.


Here’s the challenge :


  • Get the data sources talking to one another: build a data warehouse, implement and maintain multiple data sources and crunch the numbers to bring real and compelling insights to the forefront of the business’s decision making.
  • Design and deliver on a reporting roadmap, enhancing the data science capability of the whole company.
  • Automate your way out of the weeds, driving investment in data analytics maturity and automation of (some) efforts to get to effective working practices quickly.
  • Become an asset for the business, driving data analysis and insights for not just our sales organisation, but how our product works too, and even people analytics - you know they’re all connected.


Reporting to Rob, our CFO, you’ll be responsible for all business performance reporting and advanced data analytics. As the Unicorn-slash-mythical human with this weird combo background, you’ll have the data science know-how, advanced analytical skills, the experience and acumen to bring to the role that expands all of our understanding and drives real progress.

You’ll likely be working as a Business Analyst today but have meandered through loads of different projects that’s taken you out of your Computer Science background, teased you with data and finance, and here you are. A combo human unicorn with so much to offer.

We want to hear from you, as one of 14, you’re a pretty elite find.


The bit you skipped at the beginning, in a nutshell :


  • Start-up come scale-up Irish business based in Kilmainham where the majority of our team members work from (we also have a development team in Poland, and some team members work from home)
  • Going into 2020 with dreams (and plans) for exciting growth (we’ll tell you more when you meet with us)
  • About 60 colleagues in Dublin that are all lovely (a couple of incredible combo-humans, some brilliant specialists, and lots of excellent generalists too - you’ll learn lots, and we’re eager to learn lots from you too)


The To-Do List:


  • Liaise between the Finance and Commercial team, translating colleagues’ needs into objectives, real solutions and define project requirements for development and implementation
  • Evaluate technologies that bridge our gaps, fill our needs and drive the business forward
  • Determine optimal solutions for integrating data from a range of sources into a common data warehouse and analyse the data to bring real insights
  • Partner with the Product Development team and external consultants to design, develop and drive implementation of the business vision and systems/tools required to do your best work (support reporting, data analytics and information delivery), as well as provide insights to customers and end-users
  • QA: Coordinate UAT to ensure the end-to-end To Be process is fully tested before going live


Qualifications, Knowledge, Skills & Experience. The shopping list!


Need to have

  • BSc in Computer Science / Mathematics / Statistics with at least 5 years of experience in a data company working across multiple platforms, software and languages.
  • 5+ years of professional software development experience (Java, Python +/or efficient SQL statements) and adapting the right method/software for the task.
  • Extensive hands-on experience in web based integrations,advanced analytics, including predictive modelling, machine learning using Java/Python/R.
  • Hands-on cloud experience, Data Warehouse and ETL design, implementation and maintenance,.
  • Excellent communication, project management skills with the ability to work with a wide variety of departments.
  • Proven ability to analyse software requirements, model business processes/workflows, and align software requirements to business strategy.
  • Able to lead project teams, collaborate with business partners, vendor/ consulting organizations and peer level professionals.
  • Experience utilizing BI Tools, Google Sheets, G Suite etc. to create high quality presentations and data representations.


Nice to have

  • Strong delivery experience leveraging major capabilities within the Salesforce Platform e.g. Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, etc.
  • Development & Integration of data analytics products into CRM (Salesforce) platform with minimum 5 years’ experience


About You


You will be incredibly ambitious, and this role doesn’t feel daunting to you. We’re looking for exceptional, driven, strategically focused people who have a want and desire to impact the Company at a senior level. You will be deeply invested in the Company as a whole (we expect this as a minimum) and it’s growth.


What’s in it for you?

﻿

We’ll offer you a competitive base salary based on experience, but we’re talking up to €80K based on your capability.


Your benefits will include life, health and travel insurance, a company MacBook and mobile phone, unlimited holidays, lunch on Mondays, yoga and wellness initiatives throughout the year (We like doing fun stuff!).


You will be based in our offices at Kilmainham Square, Dublin 08, D08 ET1W

The start date: ASAP would be ideal
