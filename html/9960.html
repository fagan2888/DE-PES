Tagit Pte Ltd Johannesburg, ZA

4 days ago

Apply on company website
The Enterprise Information Management Division (EIM) in the Bank's Business Systems and Technology Department requires the services of Data Modellers. These resources will form part of the EIM Centre of Excellence (CoE) that will provide the required support to the business data specialists with standards, methodologies and potentially capacity and capabilities as the needs from the business areas may evolve in enabling the Bank to fulfil its mandate. The Data Modellers will be responsible for analysing, capturing data requirements, data definitions, business rules, data quality requirements by producing logical and physical data models to support the Bank in expediting the creation of data repositories as well as creating and populating the bank wide data inventory. Implementing new legislative requirements such as the FSR Act will rely on embedding best practice data modelling techniques and standards ensuring effectiveness and efficiency. Supplementing the above they will also be responsible for the planning, implementation and control activities to ensure proper creation, storage, integration, and control of organisational data and support associated usage of meta-data. They will be responsible for the planning, designing and implementation of reference and master data management policies, standards and tools that consistently define and manage the critical reference and master data of the Bank to provide a single/common point of reference across all the Bank’s Strategic Focus Areas (SFA’s). Managing meta data and master data efficiently will support and improve information sharing across all SFA’s as well as support the cross-functional research agenda by having an organisational view of the Bank’s terms and definitions used (Bank wide information inventory). Key deliverables: ? To conduct an assessment on data modelling and data development maturity levels within the Bank against best practices ? To develop standards, guidelines, processes, procedures for data modelling and data development within the Bank ? To develop and promote data modelling awareness across the organisation to ensure appropriate translation and specification of business information requirements ? To specify and translate business information requirements to data definitions ? To define data modelling and data development measurement and metrics to support reliable decision making ? To monitor, measure and report the data modelling and data development quality levels to ensure the correct information is captured on business requirements ? To enable the improvement of data modelling capability by developing relevant stakeholders in all aspects of data modelling (e.g. definition of data business rules, analysis and assessment of data models) ? To create, maintain and integrate a meta-data environment that enables sharing and standardisation of business information ? To enable the improvement of meta-data capability by developing relevant stakeholders in all aspects of meta-data (e.g. the maintenance of the business glossary) ? To monitor, measure and report the meta-data quality levels to ensure the correct information is captured about business information ? To play an oversight role in the remediation of data modelling and data development issues ? To develop and manage stakeholder relations effectively to promote data modelling awareness across the organisation and support reliable decision making Formal qualifications/Education: ?
