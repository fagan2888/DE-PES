Do you have experience with writing code to ingest data? Do you like data wrangling, digging into data sources, and process data to a readable and usable state? Do you love that feeling of accomplishment when data is flowing seamlessly into a data lake, day in day out, hour after hour, based on code that you have carefully crafted? Then this job is just what you are looking for! What will you do For multiple enterprise customers we create data connectors to make data flow from various sources to the data lake of choice: that can be Splunk, Databricks, Hadoop or any other technical implementation. What they have in common, is that the data will be used in a Production environment, so it has to flow seamlessly and needs to be monitored for disruption. Also, data validation and data quality are important. Activities
  • Create data connectors, using Python or other coding languages
  • Define data validation tests to run in the data pipeline
  • Define monitoring and alerting to ensure visibility when the data flow is interrupted or corrupted
  • If incidents occur, you take the lead in getting to the root cause as soon as possible in order to solve the incident with as little impact on the end users as possible
  • You and the team are responsible for building, deploying, maintaining and optimizing the data ingestion and data connectors to have data flow to the data lake This is Itility Itility is an IT Company working for innovative Enterprises. We are involved in all stages: strategy, design, execution and the run. With our I(o)T knowledge we help our customers with their digital transformation. We deal with private and public cloud projects, IT automation (infrastructure as code), application development, Big Data and IoT. We partner with our customers and learn from each other every day. Based on our vision, we start with building blocks to then use IT in a fully flexible manner. And we do it well, because our customer portfolio and team continue to grow. We are committed to the use of new technologies and innovative ideas. This is what we offer You will be given the opportunity to develop in the best way possible, under the personal guidance of fellow data engineers and architects of Itility. If you do not have the required expertise for the job but do have the passion for data engineering, we offer a substantial trainee program to get you up to speed for the job in a planned manner. In addition to a competitive salary, you will receive extras such as:
  • Training at the 2-year Itility Academy and external customized courses
  • Additional performance-based variable payment
  • 26 holidays per year
  • Expense allowance
  • Car, laptop and telephone Who are you? You believe in scrum/agile way-of-working and in software practices to enable a professional data flow. Further requirements:
  • You have experience with creating data ingestion scripts
  • Experience with Linux is a pre
  • You have a good understanding of SQL and Python
  • You are a team player and you have good communication skills
  • Ideally you have worked with data platforms and data lakes within an enterprise environment Location Randstad area or Eindhoven area (based on where you live)
