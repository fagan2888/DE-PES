    • Description

    Studyportals is looking for an intern to support the Analytics and Consulting Team, based in Eindhoven (the Netherlands).


    Studyportals is a fast-growing online platform that helps students to find and compare their education options internationally. In ten years of professional operation it has grown into a popular platform amongst students worldwide, and into a leading position in the recruitment for universities around the world. We have a strong growth vision and we are now shifting the company to the next phase. With initial success in the European market, our ambition is now to quickly penetrate the global market as well.


    The Analytics and Consulting Team (ACT) at Studyportals advises and assists universities and other organisations in the higher education sector to realise their ambitions in the field of international marketing and international student recruitment. Our team of consultants cover everything from strategy, branding and marketing to customer relationship management, student recruitment, market insight, competitor analysis, Google Analytics, training as well as custom services. Our areas of expertise are Analytics, Market Insights & ROI and Strategic Marketing Consulting.

    For this we are looking for a data analytics intern to support our data analyses.


    The Challenge

    We aim to provide actionable advice to higher education institutions using the huge amount of data we possess. You will experience hands-on consulting by assisting us in the execution of projects from beginning to end.


    The Activities

    An internship with the chance to carry out some research and work on several tasks and projects, to provide support to the Analytics and Consulting Team (ACT) and you will be involved in following activities:

    • Big data analysis and data mining and data validation against external data (most likely statistically validating our own proprietary dataset with Australian enrollment data), with a focus on investigating how to make the best use of the data available from millions of website visitors for reliable consulting to universities across the world. For an example of previous work done by one of our interns, click here (https://www.studyportals.com/blog/online-study-search-behaviour-and-student-enrolments-how-strong-is-the-link/).

    • Data analysis and consultancy for clients. You will be involved in practical, hands-on projects that help the recruitment of higher education institutions.

    • Further crystallizing the mission, operating procedures and client base of ACT.

    Requirements


    The Person You Are

    • Currently pursuing your Bachelor’s or Master’s degree, preferably in Data Science, (Applied) Statistics, Econometrics, Industrial Engineering or Quantitative Marketing

    • Good knowledge of databases, data processing, data visualisation and scripting languages (e.g. SQL, Python, Excel, Tableau)

    • Know your way around Microsoft Suite Office, especially Excel and Word

    • Preferably have experience with Google Analytics

    • Be an analytical thinker that pays great attention to details

    • Have strong proficiency in English (no Dutch required)

    • Open and enjoy an environment of direct constructive feedback

    • Proactive with a can-do attitude

    • A good team player and fun to work with, but also able to work independently

    • Able to thrive in an open and fun, but also sometimes noisy office

    • Last but not least, our mission and core values should match your own and give you energy


    The offer

    • Monthly allowance of EUR 400

    • A full-time internship position starting a.s.a.p. for a period of 5 months or more

    • A chance to gain knowledge in Analytics & Consulting for higher education

    • An informal, entrepreneurial and flexible atmosphere where self-initiative and innovation is highly appreciated

    • A company environment that is serious about having fun with almost everything we do, including regular memorable team events and a wide range of games and activities during lunchtime or after work

    • The opportunity to make our world a little better and be at the core of a study choice giant that the world education market is desperately waiting for

    • If you want more information, don’t hesitate to contact us. If you want to meet us in person, we are always available for a cup of coffee at our offices
