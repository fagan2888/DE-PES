Junior Data Engineer

EyeTech Solutions Brussels Area, Belgium

1 week ago 70 applicants

One of the leaders in the media and digital industry in Belgium is now looking for a Junior Data Engineer .


You will join a multicultural environment allowing you to gain strong experience in the data & analytics fields and become a tech lead . The evolution of their employees and their satisfaction at work is one of their most important values. Indeed, they focus their philosophy on human values.


Our employees have a say, their testimonials serve as a basis for improving our communication.

Working within the Data & Analytics team on designing , implementing and maintaining optimal data platforms will allow you to be involved in end to end projects and gain expertise on each project’s stage.

Your tasks :


  • You will have functional analysis responsibilities
  • You will have the responsibility of implementing data models, ETL jobs and data integration flows
  • With the help of the business, you will develop test sets and participate in the validation of deliverables


Your Profile :


  • You have a degree in computer science or other quantitative fields
  • You have knowledge of ETL tools and data transformation. Knowledge in Talend is a plus.
  • You are fluent in French or Dutch with command in English

Your Benefits :


  • You will earn a salary up to 50.000 euros a year depending on your experience
  • You will benefit from a very competitive package:
    • Insurance package
    • Mobile phone + Laptop
    • Days of home working
    • Meal vouchers
  • You will join a family-minded company with horizontal management which will allow to be close to your colleagues and have a clear view of the business
  • You will join a well-known company in the media and digital industry giving you the opportunity to stay up-to-date with the newest IT technologies
  • You will start a long-term career within an ambitious team which will help to grow and become a tech lead
  • You will have the chance to join a unique work environment that encourages the development of young talents

- CONFIDENTIALITY ASSURED -


Please note that by applying your CV is sent directly to Amine, in charge of this opportunity.

Exceptional candidates who do not meet these criteria may be considered for other roles provided if they have the necessary skills and experience.
