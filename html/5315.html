    • FREELANCE DATA ENGINEER - GHENT REGION

    For our client, a dedicated TV player in the world of visual digital entertainment, Hays IT Contracting is currently looking for a FREELANCE DATA ENGINEER .


    · Data Warehouse design, SQL, Python

    · Work with the newest technologies

    · Research and Development


    As a Data Engineer you will work with Big Data and Data Analytics systems to support the execution of end-to-end Data Science Projects. The starting point will often be Data Exploration and Validation of new data sources. Besides that, you'll collect, clean, prepare, validate and load the necessary data so that the Data Scientists and BI analysts can use it. Also, you will develop integration tests for the new data processing flows.


    About you:


    • You have a strong knowledge of the following technical skills: DWH design, SQL, Python.
    • You have a Master's degree in computer science or a related quantitative field.
    • You have a DevOps mindset and are used to having end-to-end responsibility in a project.
    • You have the ability to understand business context and specificities.
    • You have excellent communication, visualization, reporting and presentation skills.
    • You speak fluent English.


    The team you'll be working is a close group where good communication and having a laugh now and then is very important. You'll work in an international environment with various facilities like a company restaurant. Lastly you’ll get the chance to develop yourself and work with the newest technologies.


    Interested? Contact Jules Steevens (jules.steevens@hays.com) or apply online!
