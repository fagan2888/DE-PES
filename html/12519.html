Job Description

Senior Infrastructure Engineer

12 Month FTC - Immediate Start

Our client is a large Housing Association based in central London.

Role

This is a purely hands on and technical role.

Maintaining, Developing and documenting client's IT infrastructure aligned with industry best practice standards to meet needs of the business requirements in line with the IT strategy.

Provide third line operational support for client's network, server and client infrastructure.

Manage the organisation's storage infrastructure, switches and servers.

Supported by the Infrastructure Manager act as an infrastructure solutions architect, design authority and project manager for specific infrastructure projects

Deliver business improvements by using new technology applications, working with user and suppliers to develop the full potential of key systems.

Experience

Administering and managing Microsoft Active Directory and/or Azure AD

Administering and managing a Windows Network

Office 365 and Azure Cloud Technologies

Virtual environments including VMware ESX and vCenter and Hyper-V

Citrix environments

Knowledge of SAN technologies

Knowledge of IP telephony

Using a service desk application

Working in public sector environment

Benefits

Salary £42-45k + Flexi working

Keywords: Housing, Housing Association, IT Infrastructure Engineer, Infrastructure Engineer, Citrix, Windows, Office 365, Azure, VMware ESX, vCenter, Hyper-V
