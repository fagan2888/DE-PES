    The Business Information and Technology Solutions (BITS) department is seeking an experienced Database expert to join our newly restructured department. The newly formed BITS department works collaboratively with the business to provide strategic direction on technology services across the organisation.


    LET'S EXPLORE THE JOB


    The Database Engineer role is a key member of the IT Infrastructure and Systems team and is responsible for administering, supporting and maintaining Councils SQL servers and Enterprise Systems databases. Working closely with all members of the IaS team you will ensure optimal performance of the databases by monitoring and tuning where necessary, provide troubleshooting of technical issues related to the backend of our enterprise applications and help ensure the security of our data is maintained at all times.


    • Administration of all enterprise databases, including monitoring of database performance and tuning of databases to ensure optimal database operating conditions are maintained
    • Troubleshooting database related issues to identify the root cause and implement appropriate measures and changes to rectify the problem
    • Provide advice on cyber vulnerabilities and threats in conjunction with analysis from industry and vendor sources
    • Analyse the security needs and implement appropriate security measures and controls to meet audit requirements and ensure that the risk of the organisations data being compromised is minimised, ensure that data privacy and integrity is maintained
    • Produce or assist with the production of enterprise wide reporting needs, using reporting tools such as SSRS, Tableau, Crystal and Telerik
    • Establish strong relationships within the BITS team, including key Development and Data management technical staff
    • Design and build security frameworks, policies and controlled procedures for Councils systems


    Please note: A full list of accountabilities is located within the attached Position Statement.


    LET'S DESCRIBE YOU


    • Extensive experience administering, maintaining and supporting Microsoft SQL Server platforms
    • Extensive experience working with database reporting tools (SSRS, PowerBI, Tableau, Crystal, Telerik)
    • Proven ability to analyse database performance issues and implement measures to improve performance
    • Proven ability to write and review complex T-SQL scripting
    • Experience working with integration platforms, middleware or iPaas, working with application teams to integrate data between systems


    LET'S REVEAL THE PERKS


    • Salary: $96,332 to $103,711 per annum plus 12% superannuation
    • 35-hour working week
    • flexible working roster
    • long service leave after 5 years
    • education assistance
    • training and development opportunities
    • free parking within walking distance of work site
    • corporate fitness program
