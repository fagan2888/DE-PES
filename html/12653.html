Job Description

We believe that clothes — and how you make them — can make a difference. A
company doesn’t last 160 years by standing still. It endures by reinventing
itself, striving to delight its consumers, winning in the marketplace, and
by remaining true to its values.
We are looking for a Data Scientist based in our European HQ in Diegem,
Belgium. You will report to the Director of Data Science. You will help
accelerate the effectiveness of Levi’s and Dockers global businesses. You
will partner with merchandising, planning, and content teams to tackle
challenges of strategic importance to the company. You will also play an
important role to help mentor team members, manage projects, collaborate
with outside vendors and consultants, and evolve processes to scale the
work of the team. You will present recommendations to leadership and
implement recommendations throughout the organization.

Responsibilities

Lead and support assortment optimization projects:
  • Partner with various teams to determine problem statements and hypotheses.
  • Identify relevant data sources, collect, clean, and transform data to use
in analyses.
  • Explore data to increase understanding of customer behaviors, attitudes,
and preferences.
  • Develop state-of-the-art statistical procedures, algorithms, and models in
Python to solve a range of problems in diverse domains.
  • Design and deploy algorithmic solutions to better meet the needs of
customers.
  • Conduct experiments to test the effectiveness of models and sequentially
iterate upon and improve deployed algorithms.
  • Communicate project status accurately and on time. Ensure that projects
follow established technical standards and methodology.
Use analytical models to reduce uncertainty and guide important decisions

Across The Organization
  • Understand the customer lifecycle; produce relevant insights and
recommendations to ultimately increase revenue.
  • Estimate the effect of micro and macro-economic trends and customer’s
behavioral patterns on the business.
  • Create compelling presentations for both internal and external customers
using (D3.js, R Shiny, Looker, Tableau, or similar tools)

Collaborate Cross Functionally And In Teams
  • Maintain a collaborative working relationship with various teams across the
organization. Be the go-to person for questions about site personalization
and user behavior.
  • Develop an in-depth knowledge of data sources relevant to each function.
  • Lead by example and provide on-going mentorship and guidance to our team of
data scientists and analysts.
  • Be part of our culture of continuous learning and improvement by providing
solutions and accomplishing goals.
  • Ensure the accuracy and relevance of the team's deliverables through
frequent peer-reviews and pair-programming sessions.

Requirements
  • Master's/PhD Degree in Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science, Electrical
Engineering, Statistical Computing, or an equivalent discipline.
  • 2+ years of prior experience in a business-focused Data Scientist role
  • 2+ years of hands-on experience analyzing and interpreting data, drawing
conclusions, defining recommended actions, building predictive models, and
visualizing results.
  • 2+ years of SQL development skills writing complex queries.
  • 3+ years of experience with Python, R, Java, C++, Julia, or another widely
used open-source programming language.
  • 2+ years of experience deploying algorithms in a production environment.
  • 2+ years’ experience with big data processing tools such as MapReduce, PIG,
and HIVE, with considerable experience working with Spark.
  • 2+ years of experience with data visualization tools (D3.js, R Shiny,
Looker, Tableau or similar).
  • Experience working with AWS ecosystem and Linux/UNIX shell.
  • Experience using data access tools and building dashboards using large
datasets and multiple data sources.
  • Project management experience desirable.
  • Experience in applying data-driven models to create impactful business
decisions.
  • Well- organized and detail oriented, with a strong ability to
simultaneously coordinate and track multiple deliverables, tasks and
dependencies.
Travel: 5-10%
What We Offer
  • An opportunity to build a career with a world-renowned, historic fashion
brand that lives by its values
  • A chance to guide real change in an organization undergoing growth and
transformation
  • A diverse, international environment of smart, inspiring people
  • A fun, creative, modern casual dress culture
