Be Bright.

We’re disruptors, innovators and dreamers who aren’t afraid to challenge what’s possible.

At Bright Machines we bring intelligence to the factory, enabling manufacturers to effortlessly and autonomously build physical products. Our intelligent, Software-Defined Manufacturing brings automation, greater clarity, flexibility and speed to the manufacturing process.

Our global team of manufacturing and software veterans combine the best in robotics and software with machine learning, computer vision and data science to deliver solutions that will transform an industry. Come join us!

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are looking for an Automation Engineer who will join the Field Ops team in EMEA.

WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING
  • Automation service engineer (supporting all automation system aspects, mechanical, SW, Vision, and Communication)
  • Supporting customer BM install base and other customer automation machines in case its required
  • Perform trouble shooting process, repair process and Failure analysis.
  • GUI & HMI Support and implementation
  • API’s & Integration with ERP, shop-floor management systems support (PLC’s, Drivers, Robots controllers, Industrial communications protocols)
  • Support and supervise integration
  • Data gathering, statistics, analytics and representation through reports
  • Perform troubleshooting, and failure analysis
  • Work with customer to find automation improvement solutions
  • Join customer meetings to support from business development aspects

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR
  • At least 5 years of experience as an automation engineer with experience in multi-disciplinary system, multi software platforms, industrial automation & industrial equipment controls, and manufacturing/process
  • BSc in automation, computer science, electrical engineering or mechanical engineering
  • Working experience in an automation company is a must
  • Working experience with robot field such as, PLC/IPC logic & programming, troubleshooting, and configuration with industry leading vendors (Beckhoff, Siemens, Omron, Mitsubishi, Toshiba, etc…)
  • Experience with robotics programming: Fanuc, Epson, Kuka, Denso, Nachi, others a big advantage
  • Knowledge in Serial & Bus Communication protocols: Ethernet/Profinet, RS232/422/485, Modbus, CAN Open, EtherCAT, Profibus, TCP/IP and others – an advantage
  • Knowledge in vision systems - an advantage
  • Technical savvy, system oriented, great attitude to machines, processes and controls
  • Passionate and creative, hands-on, love to code
  • Loves to work in a dynamic environment
  • Well organized, pays attention to details, methodical
  • Capable of analyzing, de-bugging and solving complex problems
  • Quick learning, team player, working under pressure & multi-tasking
  • Very good English skills (oral and written)

  • You believe in the power of the team, diversity of thought and unity of action
  • You always act with integrity, keep your commitments and practice transparency
  • You think boldly, lead with courage and demand the highest standards
  • You drive for results and act like an owner!
