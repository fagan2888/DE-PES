Data center Engineer

Green Light PS Pty Ltd Melbourne, AU

3 weeks ago

Apply on company website
Responsibilities

Green Light Worldwide currently has a contract opportunity for an experienced Data Centre Infrastructure Engineer to join a team of highly skilled technology experts, supporting critical Wintel and VMware data centre infrastructure. The Data Centre Infrastructure Engineer will be involved in design, implementation and other technical solutions and this organization is looking for an IT professional who is keen to advance their career with additional training and a desire to progress professionally towards Architecture. The Data Centre Infrastructure Engineer will manage and maintain hardware, virtualization platforms, messaging and Windows server environments and provide proactive and reactive support services to maintain the availability and reliability of the client environments.

Uses requirement specification produced by the Migration Architect, Migration Components from the Migration Services Factory library and other foundational materials to produce a detailed migration plan in a technical specialty for single client engagement.
Maximizes use of existing migration strategy components developed by the Migration Services Factory and ensures capture of all improvement opportunities.
Develop new migration strategies as required by the Migration Architect within their technical specialty where no current standard exists.
Works with Design Engineers in other specialties to ensure end-to-end design interlock.
Ensures detailed designs clearly articulate assumptions, risk and client impact.
Ensures designs are costed according to budget principals from Migration Architect.
Accountable to Migration Architect for fit-for-purpose design.
Lead technical escalations within a technical subject matter area.
May lead technical resources during migration.
Works with the delivery organization to facilitate Production Handover Process.
Participates in post-implementation technical review

Requirements

Experience developing detailed technical solutions within a specific discipline for a heterogeneous data centre infrastructure within a quality management framework.
Experience with infrastructure and/or application relocation projects.
Experience with data centre enterprise infrastructure technologies including one or more of Vmware, Recpver Pint, Dell EMC VNX , Unity and Isilon
Experience in the production of technical solution design documentation.
5 years of experience with Wintel technologies and experience in Dell EMC Networker, Data Domain and Protect point is a MUST!
Senior technical lead Design/Build/Test/Deploy experience with Windows Server, Active Directory, Windows Management (MOM/SMS), SQL Infrastructure, Microsoft Exchange Migration & Upgrades, and VMware virtualization.
Ability to influence peers, cross-functional partners, and management.
Solid technical aptitude, mentoring, and customer-facing skills.
Apply technology best practices and enterprise standards.
Demonstrated ability in solving complex technical problems.
