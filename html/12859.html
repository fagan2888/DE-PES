Can you handle some of the newest technologies within software development? Are you an expert in .NET and #C? Are you passionate about software development in close collaboration with the customer? Do you want to be a part of an international organization in growth?

Become a part of our Analytics team in Kamstrup

Kamstrup is a Danish technology company, and one of the world’s leading developers of intelligent measuring solutions. We are in growth, and we are investing massively in the development of intelligent measuring equipment and systems for wireless reading of consumption meters for electricity, water and heating. We are experiencing great interest for our solutions, and therefore we are seeking a passionate Software Developer to our team.

The Analytics team is an agile development department in Kamstrup, who works with the newest technologies and processes. The department is build with a start-up mindset, where we aim to have a flat structure, where everyone takes part in the tasks. The speed from idea to production is key, and therefore the code must be highly testable and developed iteratively.

Software Developer with focus on creating environmental-friendly solutions

As a Software Developer, you will become responsible for developing solutions, which will improve the utility companies’ ability, to optimize the production and distribution of energy and water. Our services is developed in close collaboration with selected innovative utility companies, and our software is built through a mircroservice architecture, which unrolls in Service Fabric on the Azure Platform. In your daily work, you will work with the following technologies:

  • C#
  • Event Hubs
  • Cosmos DB
  • Databricks
  • Git
  • Jenkins
  • Octopus deploy


You must expect 5-10 travel days per year, as part of the team is located in Malaga.

Personality matters

We are looking for an engaged and ambitious person with a high professionalism. You have a solid foundation as IKT-engineer, data-engineer or similar. You have 3-5 years of experience from a similar position, with high knowledge in .NET, #C and web applications. Moreover, you work with an agile mindset with continuous delivery. We also expect that you are outgoing, responsible and strive to make a difference.

As we are an international organization, it is a demand that you speak Danish and English both orally and verbally.

We offer more than a job

We offer a job in a international company in growth, with a flat hierarchy, short chain of command, open doors and dialogue. We emphasize development, education and offer great possibilities for strengthening your professional competencies.

We offer flexible working hours and numerous employee benefits, counting a healthy canteen, free fruit, fitness facilities, in-house dental clinic, hairdresser and a free health clinic. Moreover, we have an active employee association.

Do you want to be a part of Kamstrup?

Please submit your application by using the link below. Applications are reviewed on a continuous basis, but you can expect a response within six weeks.
