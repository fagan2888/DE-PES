BorgWarner Oroszlány PowerDrive division is searching for talented candidates for the following position

Project (Production Development) Engineer

(Electric motor assembly)

Key Roles & Responsibilities
  • Key role in introduction of new programs
  • Create risk assessments
  • Create manufacturing quotations for new business opportunities
  • Develop scenarios manufacturing concept for industrialization projects
  • Create specifications for assembly line purchasing
  • Negotiation with equipment suppliers
  • Cooperate with global engineering for learning new technologies
  • Design manufacturing process
  • Ability to calculate tolerance stacks
  • Understands drawing references
  • Review and approve line designs
  • Create preliminary PFMEA for production
  • Support FAT and SAT activities
  • Participate in continuous improvement projects
  • Support BorgWarner Global Engineering Organization within PDS (ITC, PTC, ETC)

Requirements
  • Bachelor degree in Mechatronics, Mechanical OR Electrical Engineering
  • Experience in Electric motor assembly
  • min. 5 years relevant professional experience
  • Fluent in English and Hungarian language
  • Experience in control systems, assembly systems, and risk analysis
  • High level of technical competence, problem solving, organization and communication skills
  • Decision Quality, dealing with ambiguity, Priority Setting

What We Offer
  • Stable background, steady job
  • Learning and developing opportunities
  • Outstanding salary, benefits
  • Bonus scheme
Development is our lifestyle.
