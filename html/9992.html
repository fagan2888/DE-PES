In order to pursue our growth in Luxembourg, Synechron is recruiting:


Senior IT Security Linux Engineer


Your Mission



The right candidate will participate in creation and implementation of security solutions in accordance with the relevant security procedures. They will also have the opportunity to work in a dynamic and collaborative environment with continuous learning opportunities.


Your responsibilities:


  • Testing, implementation and deployment of security solutions based on Linux
  • Suggesting improvements to existing environments
  • Participation or guidance of coordination meetings and report the progress
  • Procedural documentation


Your profile:


  • University diploma or equivalent
  • Proven experience with Linux (RedHat)
  • Strong experience with scripting languages such as Ksh, Bash or Perl
  • Basic project management skills would be a strong advantage
  • Fluency in both written and spoken English; German would be a plus
  • Good interpersonal skills and detail-oriented personality


Why join Synechron?


We are always looking for new talents, ready to immerse themselves in their work to the satisfaction of our customers.
We are offering you:
- A specialization in Finance: we can assist you in planning your career and offer all the necessary training and opportunities for development. (IT, Finance, coaching, personal development)
- An international presence: we encourage your mobility projects within our subsidiaries.
- Careful selection and support of our consultants: we engage in duration.
- A true spirit of conviviality, punctuated by regular events.
- An attractive salary package including a fixed and variable compensation as well as extra-legal advantages.
