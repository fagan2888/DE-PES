    Ranjan 3ilogics Private Limited is Established in 2007. It is involved in activities of providing services and maintenance of websites of various firms across the world and the creation of multimedia presentations. Headquartered in the San Francisco Bay Area, USA, 3iLogics is a leading IT company with a huge client base in the USA, UK, Australia, Canada, Singapore, and India delivering best-in-class services in Web development, Web designing, Mobile Application, and Digital Marketing.

    With our talented and skilled team, we have acquired 500+ Satisfied Clients World Wide.

    24*7 availability for all time-zones and with over 11+ Years of experience in the IT Sector. Have great expertise in handling large complex projects.


    Job Description:


    Job Summary

    The incumbent is responsible for combining marketing and social media management to enhance the company’s social media presence, interaction with target audiences, promoting the brand’s engaging content, and expanding the opportunities for increasing the revenue. The incumbent should have the understanding and knowledge of each social media platform, social media marketing strategies, and how to optimize content to make it engaging on the social media channels.


    Responsibilities and Duties:

    • Thorough research on the market to attract new technologies specifically to write topics for Blogs and postings.
    • Should be able to create branding for the company through Digital Marketing.
    • Responsible to Collaborate and create a social media strategy for the company.
    • Engage in all promotional activities of the company to enhance the traffic & promotion.
    • Engage in digital marketing research for the company
    • Promotional activities for the company through social media platforms.
    • Promote the brand’s services on various social media channels.
    • Should be able to create high impact social media posts to build a positive relationship with the target customers
    • Should manage marketing collaterals and other digital assets, ensure their availability at marketing portal and website
    • Should monitor and follow digital trends, best practices, and emerging technologies
    • Achieve audience engagement, website traffic, and revenue by exploiting all digital marketing aspects of social media marketing roadmap.
    • Build and manage rich content like blog posts, E-workshops, E-books, webinars, whitepapers, infographics, reports, etc. which attracts qualified traffic.
    • Support link building strategies for the website.
    • Support social media and PR requirements to meet organizational goals.
    • Must be responsible for the overall development and enhancement content as required for other emerging formats.
    • Understand the company's SEO strategy and suggest relevant content to drive traffic to the website.
    • Must be responsible to engage in affiliate marketing activities.
    • Should have knowledge of Brand & Product management.


    Required Experience and Qualifications

    • Experience in marketing, branding is preferred. Understanding of marketing principles is a must.
    • In-depth knowledge and understanding of social media platforms, online advertising, content marketing, etc.
    • Communication Skills: Good communication skills, both written and verbal is a must.
    • Being able to articulate campaign ideas to clients.
    • Analytical Skills: A command on numbers, marketing effectiveness measurement is essential.


    Experience: 5+ years
