Which company it is?
  • My client is one of the most influent company in his sector.
  • They are working in 14 different countries crossing four continents.
  • They are more or less 80 000 people in the world.

They are looking for a NETWORK SYSTEM ENGINEER to join their Luxemburgish office, thanks to this position you will be involve in Worldwide projects and work with the IT European Team.

Position: Network System Engineer

Your Mission

As Network System Engineer, you will be in charge of the maintenance, implementation & creation of security based network equipment inside the European IT Team.
  • In collaboration with the Head of IT Europe, you will manage & maintain all domain name related issues concerning Europe Team
  • You will be responsible to manage & have appropriate maintenance contracts with suppliers of the running Security Infrastructure
  • In collaboration with Group IT Operation you will implement all requested tactical security infrastructure & monitor, operate & manage properly
  • You will bring your security knowledge

Technical Environment: IronPort, Checkpoint, VPN & Authentication, DNS, Riverbed WAN, F5 BIG IP, McAfee WebGateways, CISCO Routers & Switches.

Daily Responsibilities
  • Management of IronPort Cluster in Europe
  • Management of the Checkpoint cluster which connects other subsidiaries
  • Management of DNS
  • User support on VPN & Authentication
  • Management of Riverbed WAN Optimizers in all subsidiaries connected to their network
  • Management of F5 BIG-IP web portals in their European Datacenter
  • Management of McAfee WebGateways everywhere in Europe
  • Management of CISCO routers & Switches in Europe


Hard & Soft Skills We Are Looking For
  • You have a Bachelor in IT
  • Minimum 8 years of experience in IT Network Management
  • You have technical certifications in Checkpoint and/or CISCO Products
  • You have a strong knowledge on LAN WAN & Wireless
  • You are Fluent in English
  • Strong organisational & interpersonal skills
  • Team player in a global team
