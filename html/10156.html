About Us.

We are Dataprovider.com – a fast growing business with over 40 people and a mission to collect and structure the web. Each month we index 450 million domains from over 50 countries around the globe. We gather more than 200 variables per website and present all this data to our clients in a clean, smart and searchable database with endless possibilities for in-depth research.

This job requires you to have a broad knowledge of development and data engineering, as well as a strong motivation to keep developing your skills. The job and the data science team offer ample opportunity for growth.
  • You have a HBO or University degree related to the work you will be doing here;
  • you already have some years of work experience, but you want to keep learning new things;
  • you are a real team player; and
  • you already know different programming languages.

We Also Offer

You will initially receive a one-year contract and we plan to offer you a permanent position in the near future.
  • Salary based on your skills and experience;
  • hardware of your choice (Linux or Mac);
  • free lunch and a full refrigerator;
  • informal and friendly atmosphere;
  • additional benefits such as pension provisions and leave days; and
  • a company bike.
