Who are you?

If you are not afraid of technology - you are able to independently take care of the test environment, you are familiar with the Docker and Kubernetes. If you can ask accurate questions about the product and therefore improve its quality. If you are finds yourself perfectly in exploratory and functional testing we are looking forward to meeting you!

What do you bring to the table?
  • At least 2+ years of experience
  • Exploratory & functional testing
  • Analytical skills
  • Problem solving
  • Technical skills
  • Strong knowledge of software tests methodologies, tools and processes
  • Experience in clear, concise and comprehensive test plans and test scenarios
  • Experience working in Agile/Scrum development process
  • Fluent English (writing and speaking)
  • Very good communication skills

Responsibilities
  • Analyzing our development and release process to identify what should or could be improved for higher quality and development speed
  • Reviewing quality specifications and technical design documents to provide timely and meaningful feedback
  • Creating detailed, comprehensive and well-structured test plans and test scenarios
  • Identify, record, document thoroughly and track bugs
  • Perform thorough regression testing when bugs are reselved
  • Stay up-to-date with new testing tools and test strategies

Nice To Have
  • Knowledge in Docker, Java, Gira, Git, Kubernetes
  • Experience in Selenium
  • Coding experience
  • Performance testing
  • Security mindset with testing
  • Writing automated tests in e.g. Selenium
  • Continuous integration
  • Own initiative
  • Independent decision - making

Our Ideal Candidate
  • Is motivated, a good communicator and self-driven
  • Can interact with both business level people (consultants, project managers) and technical people (architects, developers and system administrators)
  • Thrives under pressure
  • Is an independent and flexible self-starter, and upholds the highest standards in the team’s communication and documentation
  • Deliver tasks on time following our Agile process
  • A great team player
  • Based in Tricity or willing to relocate
  • Is preferably fluent in English
What we have to offer?

You’ll be working with a talented team of inspired young people in an international environment. As well as a challenging job with plenty of opportunities for personal development, we offer all the things you need to make your contribution to Onegini the best it can be! As a scaleup, Onegini has plenty of room for personal growth and will support your career development as we like our people to grow as we do.

And yes, we have some nice extras too…
  • Lunch catered on-site
  • A flat organization and politics are at a minimum
  • A fantastic work hard/play hard startup environment
  • Yearly company trips, for wintersports- and summer fanatics
  • Many in-house competitions to join: weekly squash, daily table soccer etc.
  • And then some....
What do we do?

Do you want to work with a strong team on creating innovative software products? Do you want your software to be used by millions of consumers? Do you want your software to be used by strong brands in the financial industry? Do you enjoy working with the latest technology? Do you enjoy to work with highly skilled software engineers and architects? Join Onegini!

The world of IAM is changing. New fast-growing software companies focus on consumers, mobile apps and portals. Onegini is one of those scale-ups, with 250% growth numbers and customers such as Aegon, Univé, Proximus, CFG Bank, Van Lanschot, NS and many more.

The challenge many banks and insurance companies face is making sure their mobile apps and portals are user-friendly and secure. As a product company, we offer them an end-to-end solution as well as high end (C)IAM consultancy services.

You will work with the latest technologies and focus on an international market. @Onegini you‘ll work in teams in which new software versions are developed using the Scrum methodology. Developers, architects and operational engineers all work together in one team.
