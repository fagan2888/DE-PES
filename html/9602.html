    Consultant - Technology Recruitment at Endeavour Search Limited

    • Our Client, a leading IT software provider with a strong business pipeline in Hong Kong and China, is looking to hire a Data Science Lead to lead the new R&D project for their Data Analytics and Machine Learning modules.

    Job Description:

    • Take lead in the designing and integration of the company’s upcoming Data Analysis and Machine Learning module for their Enterprise Software products
    • Design and develop advanced algorithms and predictive models to achieve optimal data and insight generation
    • Formulate and test hypotheses, analyze and interpret the results and re-optimize the model if deemed necessary
    • Work closely with client team to translate prototypes into new products, services, and features and provide guidelines for large-scale implementations
    • Provide knowledge and insights in algorithm design, data modeling, machine learning and quantitative analysis for the development team as the domain expertise
    • Identify cutting-edge data tools and techniques and lead the overall adaptation into the product

    Qualifications

    • Degree holder in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science or related disciplines
    • Over 4 years of demonstrable experience in developing Data Science solutions with at least 2 full development cycle experience
    • Highly proficient in computer languages such as Python and R
    • Knowledge of predictive modeling algorithms and frameworks
    • Knowledge of machine learning trade-offs and model evaluation
    • Experience with open source tools for deep learning and machine learning technology such as Keras, Tensorflow, Pytorch, Scikit-learn, Pandas, etc.
    • Good command of written and spoken Chinese and English
