Data Scientist

OLX Group Buenos Aires, AR

Hace 2 semanas

Solicitar en el sitio web de la empresa
We're looking for a Senior Data Scientist. You will have a unique opportunity to use terabytes of real-world data together with OLX's infrastructure to develop state-of-the-art machine learning techniques that improves the experience of millions of OLX's users and help the growth of OLX's businesses.

We're a team of 5,000 ambitious, curious people building marketplace platforms that make it super easy for anyone to buy and sell almost anything, boosting local economies.

What You Will Be Doing
  • Leverage terabytes of OLX's data on a global scale to identify opportunities, evaluate business impact and propose data-driven solutions for various product initiatives.
  • Design, implement, evaluate, and iterate on machine learning models all the way from prototypes to production systems. Potential projects include advancing of machine learning models that power personalized recommendations, automation of posting flow and search experience at OLX platform.
  • Collaborate with a global team of engineers, data scientists and project stakeholders in utilizing machine learning to push the boundaries in classifieds marketplaces.
  • Work in an agile environment to deliver high quality product while meeting aggressive schedules


Who We're Looking For
  • Degree in Computer Science or related fields.
  • Deep understanding of machine learning techniques and hands-on experience in applying them on solving real-world problems.
  • Strong programming skills (Python, Scala or Java are preferred) and ability to write clean code while embracing best engineering practices (unit tests, code reviews).
  • Deep Learning and Deep Learning frameworks experience (Tensorflow or equivalent) will be a plus.
  • Knowledge in NLP is a plus.
  • Having worked in productionizing machine learning models in e-commerce industry is preferable but not excluding.
  • Experience with distributed computing systems (Spark or equivalent) is desirable.
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills. Strong English both oral and written skills are a must have.


What We'll Offer You
  • International career and travel opportunities.
  • A budget for conference and education.
  • Competitive salary, family health plan OSDE 310 and great benefits.
  • High-end computer-gear.
  • Free coffee, snacks and drinks!


What You Need To Know About Us
  • OLX is the world's leading classifieds platform in high-growth markets. It's available in more than 35 countries and in over 50 languages. The platform makes it so easy to connect people to buy, sell or exchange used goods and services.
  • OLX is part of the OLX Group, a global product and tech company with 17 brands, +40 countries, +5000 people and one mindset.
  • Our mission is to make it super easy for people to buy and sell almost anything, boosting local economies.
  • We are proud to be different, and we work differently too. We combine the spirit and agility of a start-up with the maturity that comes from being part of a 100 year-old company.
  • We are curious, ambitious and allergic to corporate interference. We improvise, experiment and push each other further, embracing uncertainty and driving change.

If you are as excited as us about this position and our company, we hope you join us! If you'd like to learn more about the OLX Group take a look at the other parts of our website. And remember, we are an equal opportunity employer.
