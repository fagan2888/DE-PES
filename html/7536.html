Assists both external and internal clients in the implementation of operational software and hardware solutions. Analyses, evaluates, and clearly defines client needs, develops configurations and interfaces that support clients’ business processes. Outlines and executes on delivery and implementation plans. Tests and troubleshoots final system setups. Provides training and end-user support during and after the implementation process. Actively and continuously participates in pre-sales product presentations and provides assistance in scoping projects and developing proposals. A high level of professional demeanor and conduct is expected.

Duties and Responsibilities:

    • Obtain a detailed picture of what the clients wants and needs are
    • Collect customer data, define objectives, scope, and timelines
    • Educate and train customers on the software capabilities and features
    • Collaborate with the sales department in presenting the company’s products to customers
    • Assess customer’s IT hardware and software infrastructure and make sure it meets the minimal requirements for a successful implementation and operation of the proposed solution
    • Assist the sales department and business partners with proposal formulation
    • Consistently and efficiently conduct software installation, software interfacing, and software development required for the optimal operation and communication of the offered systems with customers’ enterprise suite
    • Document every software installation, interface, or any other type of intervention conducted with all customers
    • Travels to customers’ sites both domestically and internationally to perform previously planned and outlined activities
    • Team work with every member of the company to ensure customer satisfaction

Qualifications:

    • In-depth knowledge of programming software such as C++, C#, Java, etc.
    • Web service development (REST & SOAP)
    • Extensive experience in database administration (MS SQL Server Mandatory )
    • Highly skilled in network management and most common computer operating systems (MS Windows Particularly)
    • Experience managing or operating an ERP suite (Highly Desired)
    • Strong software and hardware troubleshooting skills
    • Customer care, account management experience in a professional or corporate setting (Mandatory)
    • 2 year software implementation, software development, and project management experience
    • Effective and professional communication skills (Both Verbal & Written)
    • 100% Bilingual (English – Spanish) (Italian Fluency a Major Plus)
    • Availability to travel domestically and internationally
