    • In this role, you will be joining an amazing team located in Medellín or Bogotá, Colombia and participating in a challenging real estate project for an USA client,  using  React.js in the Front -End and Node in the Back-End.



    Position requirements:


    • 3+ years of experience with JavaScript.
    • Loving the front-end and having a strong background using React.js.
    • Experience working with Node  in the Back -End or other JavaScript frameworks/libraries.
    • Having a good conversational  English level (B2 or higher) that allows you to communicate with native clients.
    • Already living in Colombia and the possibility to relocate to Medellin or Bogotá (we are not considering international relocations for the moment).



    About You:


    • You like challenges and are used to translate the client’s most crazy ideas into a piece of good quality code.
    • You don't give up. Instead, you ask for help when needed, and also offer your shoulder if someone else is stuck.
    • You are willing to learn new programming frameworks because you are not afraid of trying new tools and thinking outside the box.
    • You feel sad for humanity when you read a bad piece of code, and you understand that automation and continuous integration makes your life easier.
    • You like to travel and work with distributed teams, not only in Latin America but also in the US.



    UruIT's benefits:


    • Prepaid Medical Insurance (Sura)
    • Exequial insurance for you, your family and pets (Prever)
    • Flexible working hours.
    • Work from home 2 days per week.
    • In-house English classes on Thursdays.
    • Possibility to travel to the US and Uruguay. UruIT will cover all your VISA expenses.
    • Social events and team building. Breakfast and Beer Fridays.
    • Access to paid online courses and videos. We also buy printed books if you prefer to take a break on a screen device.
    • Financial support to get certifications and participate in conferences.
    • Be part of the Medellín team in a horizontal, collaborative and positive work environment. We are defining everything in the company together.
