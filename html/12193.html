Manhattan designs, builds and delivers market-leading supply chain technology solutions for leading companies around the world. We help drive the commerce revolution with unmatched insight and unrivaled technology, connecting front-end revenue and relationships with back-end execution and efficiency—optimized on a common technology platform. This platform-based approach is enabling leading companies across the globe to Push Possible® by getting closer to customers and achieving real-world results.

We are looking for a Senior Analyst who enjoys working in a friendly & collaborative team environment, where plenty of excellent learning & development opportunities will be provided to support his/her career growth!

Senior Analysts provide technical support throughout a customer implementation. They have developed technical expertise and excellent troubleshooting skills for a single MA product.

Key Responsibilities
  • Develops custom modifications.
  • Mentors other analysts.
  • Proactively troubleshoots and resolves technical issues that arise during an implementation.
  • Provides onsite support as needed throughout the project.
  • Performs the initial install of the application and subsequently trains the customer on the install process.
  • Works with appropriate customer team members to implement interfaces with external systems.
  • Works with appropriate MA personnel to develop and test interfaces between multiple Manhattan products.
  • Works with MA PDO to resolve product issues or identify solutions to implement client requirements.
  • Participates in training and provides training to new employees.
  • Minimum of 50% travel required.

Minimum Requirements
  • 2 years of post-bachelor’s experience in software related technical field.
  • 1 year of experience developing SQL Queries.
  • Adheres to departmental processes and procedures.
  • Bachelor's or foreign equivalent degree in Computer Science, Supply Chain Management, Engineering, Business or related field or related work experience.
  • Customer oriented, self-motivated and team player.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills in both Japanese and English.
  • Excellent time management and prioritising skills.
  • Strong problem-solving skills.
  • Hands-on experience in CORE JAVA.
  • Sound knowledge of Object Oriented Analysis, Design and some familiarity with standard J2EE Design Patterns (hands-on working experience will be a plus).
  • Good Knowledge and hands on development experience on different databases especially Oracle.
  • Proficient in Writing/Understanding PL/SQL objects like stored procedures, triggers.
  • Knowledge on various Application servers including WAS, WLS and JBoss..
  • Exposure to AJAX, JSF, Struts, Springs, Hibernate is nice to have (Hands-on working experience would definitely be a plus).
  • Exposure to version control tool- GIT is preferable.
  • Quick learner with strong interest in learning new products and solutions.
