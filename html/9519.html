    • About this Job:
    • Become part of a highly dynamic team working with cutting edge scientists to enable the next generation of predictive and prescriptive genetics. As a data engineer you will utilize your engineering background to support data scientists in their data pipeline needs. In this critical role you will work on fast paced teams to build a scalable data platform.

    Essential Duties & Responsibilities:

    • Architecting, creating and maintaining reusable data pipelines will be your primary responsibility
    • Develop tools to locate, manipulate, QC and ensure interoperability of large diverse datasets
    • Collaborate with engineers, scientists, and stakeholders to define and deliver technical solutions
    • Design and own solutions to maintain high quality standards
    • Interface directly with data customers to continuously improve data processes
    • Enable policies for proper use of sensitive datasets
    • Collaborate with team members across the company to integrate with existing data sources
    • Proactively communicate plans and updates to colleagues and stakeholders
    • Provides input into peer reviews, coaches, and mentors junior team members

    Qualifications

    Education & Experience:
    • Degree in Computer Science, Engineering or other quantitative field, or an equivalent combination of education and experience.
    • 2-3 Years’ experience as a Data Engineer
    • Demonstrated experience in Big Data technologies (such as: Spark, NoSQL, EMR, Presto, Hive, HBase, Cassandra, MongoDB)
    • Java or Python Development experience
    • Knowledge of Perl/Python and Unix scripting
    • NoSQL and RDBMS databases
    • Experience with data transformation ELT or ETL
    • Familiar with Cloud computing concepts in AWS
    • Demonstrates ability to work in a geographically and culturally diverse team environment
    • Demonstrates strong analytical and problem-solving skills
    • Ability to communicate complex issues in a non-technical manner to a diverse audience

    Additional Knowledge, Skills, Traits & Abilities:
    • Knowledge of toolsets and capabilities utilized by expert data scientists and modelers
    • Data analytics and visualization
    • Data Modeling

    What We Offer:
    • Full Benefit Package (Medical, Dental & Vision) that begins your first day
    • 401k plan with company match, Profit Sharing & Retirement Savings Contribution
    • Paid Vacation, 12 Paid Holidays, Maternity and Paternity Leave, Education Assistance, Wellness Programs, Corporate Discounts among others
    • A culture that promotes work/life balance, celebrates diversity and offers numerous family oriented events throughout the year
