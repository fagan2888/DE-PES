Job description Marketing Autimation


We are looking for a Marketing Automation Specialist, with at least 2+ years of relevant experience as automation specialist, to join our team in Medellin.

Marketing automation is a fast-growing area of marketing with enormous potential for career growth for Systems Engineer or IT. The Marketing Automation Specialist will have the opportunity to work with the very best and brightest and be responsible for the design, testing, execution, maintenance and reporting of automated online marketing campaigns for our client base. This role will be a power user of marketing automation and CRM systems.


Responsibilities:

·         Support client operations by designing, executing and reporting on B2B demand generation campaigns.

·         Work with account teams to translate marketing requirements into functional campaigns within marketing automation and CRM systems.

·         Work closely with operations team to define and implement new functionality and product offerings.

·         Continuously analyze operational process to find and implement new efficiencies.

·         Manage outbound e-mail campaigns.

·         Provide day-to-day system maintenance and configuration of the systems, as well as act as the first line of defense for system support.

·         Work with technology partners to understand system functionality and roadmaps.

·         Provide documentation and training to end users.

·         Routinely audit user practices to ensure compliance.

·         Support account teams in quarterly performance reports, requiring the ability to consolidate data from various systems, analyze, and provide unique insight.

·         Integrate different Marketing Automations tools with CRMs or other platforms.


Minimum Qualifications:

·         Experience with any of the following (or similar) applications: Eloqua, Marketo, Silverpop, Pardot is a plus .

· Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Systems Engineer or IT.

·         Must have a good understanding of the Web and Web technology (HTML, basic CSS) and the ability and desire to expand current technical skill-set.

·         Previous experience in or working closely with Information Systems and/or marketing groups.

·         Good understanding of relational data structure.

·         Experience establishing and maintaining system documentation and training materials.

·         Interpersonal skills: exceptional verbal/written communication, listening, coaching and presentation skills with attention to detail.

·         Proven track record of obtaining results without direct report authority.

·         Highly self-motivated contributor who works well as an individual and within a team environment.

·         Excellent organizational skills; capacity to manage multiple priorities and tasks simultaneously, cross-functional teams and communicate with all levels of management.

·         Strong business, marketing and information systems acumen.
