App Annie delivers data and insights to succeed in the app economy.

What can you tell your friends when they ask you what you do?

As a Staff Data Analyst I help app professionals to have the most comprehensive and high quality App market data, this is the key role of App ecosystem in various business practises.

How will you be doing this?

As a Staff Data Analyst, You Will Work Closely With Various Cross-functional Teams Such As Product, Engineering And Data Scientist To Develop And Deliver Analyses, Solutions, And Insights, With Actionable Recommendations, This Includes
  • Collect requirements from product managers, work with them to refine product concepts, design and implement data model that can help engineer team to make it into reality
  • Analyze large-scale data by leveraging BIG Data Techs; develop deep-dive analysis and machine learning models to drive product/project success, and be good at doing presentations to explain the models to various teams (PM, Data Scientists and Engineers)
  • Working on the front line for data problems analysis, find the root causes by using statistics methodology and design model to resolve the problems, provide detail design document for engineering team to implement

What will you bring to the team?

You should be an experienced Data Analyst in data operation domain.
  • Strong working knowledge of machine learning algorithms including Regression, Classification, Clustering algorithms, etc.
  • Excellent data mining skills, including a working knowledge of several of the following statistics packages and languages: Python including scikit-learn, Jupyter, “R”, SPSS, SAS, Matlab, Mathematica. Analytics databases: Vertica, Sybase IQ, Hadoop, SQL, Hive
  • Strong presentation skills. Feel comfortable to explain (written and verbal) data pattern findings from data/problem to other stakeholders, e.g. product managers, senior management, etc.
  • Good communication skills and feel comfortable to work in English working environment.
  • Strong willing to drive complex issues moving forward, coordinating with all the stakeholders globally.
  • Self motivated and strong ownership. Be sensitive about any potential issues inside the production data set.
  • Familiarity with visualization tools such as Tableau is a big plus.
  • Deep Learning experience is a plus.
  • Ph.D. required in relevant technical field such as Applied Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering, Physics, or Economics.
  • 5 working years experience in data quality
