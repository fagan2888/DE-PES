工作內容

CloudMile is a young start-up team which enables clients solving most mission-critical challenges by leveraging top-notch artificial intelligence, cloud and security technologies with leading vendors in the markets.
As a “CloudMiler”, we thrive to be the leading Enterprise-grade Artificial Intelligence, cloud and security solutions providers in Asia. We are the First Google Premier Partner to acquire Infrastructure and machine learning Specializations in North Asia with strong investment in R&D and technologies.

Role And Responsibilities

Collaborate with data scientist, cloud architect, frontend engineer, backend engineer, UI/UX designer and product manager to develop the system.
Development of jobs & pipelines from multiple data sources.
Design, develop, build, test and maintain highly scalable data pipelines with monitoring and logging.
Works with Google cloud service (Big Query, Dataproc, Pub/Sub, etc.) to deliver best-in-class cloud based data solutions
Engineer & model curated and keyed Data Warehouse solutions that meet business objectives that perform efficiently and effectively
Build and optimize performance of ElasticSearch cluster and relevance
Select and integrate data tools and frameworks required to provide requested capabilities
Recommend ways to improve data reliability, efficiency and quality

Basic Requirements

BS degree in Computer Science, similar technical field of study or equivalent practical experience.
2 year of software development experience in one or more programming languages, preferred Python or golang.
Experience for RDBMS and NoSQL.
Experience for BigQuery.
Great at resolving problems, debugging, troubleshooting, designing and implementing solutions on technical issues.
Experience for Git.

Preferred Requirements

Familiar with Google Cloud Platform, such as Compute Engine, Kubernetes Engine, BigQuery, Dataproc, Pub/Sub and Stackdriver.
Experience with BI and data visualization tools.
hands-on experience with Elasticsearch.
Hands-on experience with service monitoring.
