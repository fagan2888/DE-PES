Senior Solution Consultant

Aspen Technology Algeria area

قبل 2 أسبوع

Responsibilities

      • Apply consultative skills in customer-facing situations to identify customer problems and understand their business and operational issues. Facilitate customer understanding of AspenOne Engineering solution technical functionality, value creation and business process improvement.
      • Articulate the overall solution and provide the vision with an understanding of the customer's business and technology needs, AspenTech's previous successes and the competitive landscape.
      • Support the account team to develop the account strategy based on the industry and client business environment. Refine the solution and vision messages for the project overall and work with other team members to develop presentation materials that effectively communicate these messages, leading clients to action.
      • Participate and/or lead customer consulting engagements to facilitate sales, including demonstrations, workshops, value assessments, and seminars. Proactively support pipeline development (e.g. account planning, awareness sessions, etc) and sales execution for assigned accounts and opportunities.
      • Develop commercial proposals for assigned opportunities. Respond to RFI/RFQ/RFP questionnaires as required.
      • Accountable for achieving personal targets such as productivity, bookings and other Business Consulting related targets.

    • Additional Job Description


      • B.S. in Engineering with a 5-8 years practical experience in consulting, technology, and/or industry.
      • M.S Degree is an added advantage.
      • Process Modeling/Simulation expertise required
      • Exposure to Middle East/North Africa Oil and Gas market
      • Self-motivated & creative problem solver
      • Strong communication skills both written and verbal
      • Experience in the process or manufacturing industries
      • Strong business acumen. Adept at performing customer interviews, identifying improvement opportunities, and overcoming resistance to change. Ability to assess and articulate customer value creation.
      • Flexibility to travel 30-50% of time.

  • Specific Product Knowledge:

    • Chemical and/or refining industry experience strongly preferred.
    • Strong knowledge of Chemical production and engineering business processes.
    • Understanding of one or more of the following functions in the processing industry.
      • Knowledge of the functional AspenONE Engineering Suite of Products viz. Aspen Hysys, Aspen Plus and Energy Management Applications.
      • Knowledge of industry standard process modeling tools in the Oil and Gas domain
