Zažádat na webu společnosti
Zebra Technologies with more than 15 years of expertise in Brno and over the past five decades globally drive attractive and sustainable profitable growth. Excellent execution on our strategy brought 13 percent sales growth in 2018. We are encouraged by our strong momentum and therefore we understand the need for continuous employee development, education and their growth which is supported also by our awarded career development.

The primary responsibility in this role is to drive the success of the Circular Economy and remarketed Devices program through providing accurate, innovative, and timely reporting and analytics while allowing management to make sound, fact-based decisions. This Analyst will work closely with the Program leads, in developing analytics related to Customers and Verticals that help identify opportunities within the program scope that maximize revenue and margin results.
  • Develop/Create reporting models utilizing historical data mining to drive predictive analytics
  • Work directly with business stakeholders in designing moderately complex reports using one or multiple platforms
  • Develop management dashboard reporting
  • Continually drive innovative analytics to drive sales forecasting and demand planning
  • Proactive analytics to identify key program and sales opportunities
  • Engage with other teams to ensure all available resources are utilized effectively
  • Performs other Analytic functions as required
  • BS Degree in finance, business or technical field or related field or equivalent related experience
  • Minimum 3 years of experience related to Business analytics experience
  • Strong analytical and data management skills, including Power BI
  • Detail-oriented and able to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to manage tasks in a time-conscious manner to achieve goals
  • Strong critical thinking skills
  • SFDC experience a plus

About Zebra

Zebra is a US business that has been established for 50 years and sells innovative technology solutions within barcode and product identification. That means RFID, barcode scanners, printers, data analytics and software. We employ over 7,000 people across 100 locations in 50 countries. Our solutions are used by BMW, Amazon, DHL, Tesco’s and numerous hospitals around the world and even the NFL! Zebra in Brno was awarded as a TOP employer of the year 2018 in Jihomoravsky kraj and also ABSL Diamonds Awards 2019 for innovations.
