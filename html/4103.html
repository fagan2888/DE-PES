Position Overview

For our IT Delivery Center in Sofia, we are looking for a Senior Data Engineer to contribute to the development and management of BI and analytics solutions that turn data into knowledge. This role includes developing well designed, testable, efficient solutions by using best software development practices and leading and mentoring less experienced team developers. You should have an analytical thinking, background in data and business analysis and excellent communication skills. If you also have a business acumen and problem-solving aptitude, we’d like to meet you. Ultimately, you will enhance our business intelligence systems to help us make better decisions.

Key Responsibilities
  • Lead developers with expert knowledge.
  • Translate business needs to technical specifications.
  • Design, build and deploy BI solutions.
  • Carry out database modeling and system performance tuning.
  • Maintain and support data analytics platforms (e.g. Microsoft Azure BI Platforms & Tools).
  • Conduct testing and troubleshooting.
  • Evaluate and improve existing BI systems.
  • Collaborate with teams to integrate systems.
  • Create visualizations and reports for requested projects.
  • Develop and update technical documentation.

Education Requirements
  • Bachelor’s Degree Required, BSc/BA in Computer Science, Engineering or relevant field.

Related Work Experience
  • 3+ Years proven experience as a developer.
  • Background in data engineering (data science experience is considered a plus).
  • Experience with DBMS, ETL development and OLAP principles, NoSQL and Big Data technologies, preferably with Spark, Azure Databricks, Azure Cosmos DB.
  • Proficiency in development with SQL, DAX, Python (C#, .NET, Power Query is considered a plus).
  • Knowledge and experience with Azure data processing and computation services is considered a plus (e.g. Azure Storage, Azure Functions, Azure SQL DB, Azure App Service).
  • Proven abilities to take initiative and be innovative.
  • Analytical mind with a problem-solving aptitude.

Our Growth Culture

One of the reasons our company continues to thrive after 130+ years is having a company culture that supports and rewards behaviors that lead to growth. Our “Growth Behaviors,” as we call them, are ways of being and working that help to make us successful. Think about how you can bring this to life in your next role at Coca-Cola.

Curious

Empowered
Make it happen . True empowerment is the result of taking responsibility. This means giving yourself permission to see it, say it and do it, and owning the outcomes. Because we move forward faster when we all take action.

Version 1.0, 2.0, 3.0
Push for progress, not perfection. There are very few overnight successes. Greatness is borne of many little victories (and failures). Share v 1.0, test it, and make it better. Then create the next version. Because the moment we think something is perfect, it will be obsolete.

Inclusive
Include, value and trust each other. We are smart alone but together we are genius. This means being inclusive, giving the benefit of the doubt and being responsible for each other. Because, for our company to thrive for the next 100+ years, smart isn’t enough. We need genius.
