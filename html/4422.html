Digitise Labs Bulgaria is part of GoGroopie which was established in 2010 in London, UK. The company operates in the field of online marketing, advertising as well as product and service sale.


GoGroopie is rapidly growing Tech-and-Data-driven company that has emerged as one of the leading online discount marketplaces, connecting millions of buyers with thousands of sellers throughout the UK, giving customers an endless range of unique and inspiring items and experiences.


We are looking for an experienced Senior Automation QA Engineer to join our team in Sofia.


As a Senior Automation QA Engineer, you will be part of a dedicated team responsible for delivering the highest quality of our products. On this journey, you will become a member of the Software Development Department focusing on automation testing. You should monitor all stages of software development life-cycle and be able to identify system malfunctions. The role will help facilitating the quality of our products using a combination of automated and manual testing each iteration.


Required skills:

  • Excellent spoken and written English
  • Ability to carry out exploratory testing
  • 4+ years of professional experience in the software testing field with a focus on creating automation tests
  • Proven track of experience with Selenium


Main Responsibilities:

  • Creating, executing, analysing & improving our existing automated tests
  • Improving/extending the existing testing framework using C# & Selenium
  • Identifying, logging and tracking product issues
  • Defining test plan and test case specifications
  • Mentoring, tutoring and helping grow team members (both automation and manual QA tracks)
  • Defining and making Quality Assurance Reports based on major KPIs
  • Following and improving established company testing standards, procedures, and guidelines to ensure consistent product quality
  • Contribute to the definition and execution of the overall testing strategy of the company


Will be considered an advantage to have:

  • Experience with C# or other object-oriented language
  • Experience with web "recording" tools (Ranorex, Progress Test Studio, etc.)
  • Knowledge of software quality and testing methodologies as well as test automation tools
  • Experience designing and implementing testing tools, testing frameworks and systems is an asset
  • Experience with Appium
  • Experience with Mobile testing
  • Very good understanding of Agile software development life cycle and QA processes
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills


Please note: we will process only those applications that have an attached (or linked) sources of real automation tests/frameworks.
