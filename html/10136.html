    Hearsay Systems is looking for talented Data Engineer to join our team.


    Who is Hearsay?

    Hearsay Systems is an enterprise fintech company transforming how bankers, advisors, and insurance agents acquire and service customers in an increasingly digital and regulated environment. Founded in 2009 and helmed by Clara Shih, we are a tightly knit and dedicated group that passionately believes in our products, our people, and our culture. Our products engage customers across Facebook , LinkedIn , Twitter , Instagram , email, text, and voice. Every Hearsay product syncs beautifully with Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, and other CRM systems.


    Hearsay is used by more than 170,000 financial advisors and insurance agents. Our customers include Morgan Stanley, Goldman Sachs, Wells Fargo, JP Morgan Chase, Prudential, and many of the largest financial services and insurance companies in the world. We enable them to get a real-time pulse of field conversations and to trigger more effective targeted advisor-client outreach with AI-optimized calls, text messages, email follow-ups, and social media interactions.


    About the role:

    • Partner with Product Managers, Data Analysts, Customer Success and business domain experts to build & manage reliable data sets in the data warehouse / data lake to empower our BI products and support operational and exploratory analysis
    • Design and develop new ETL / Data pipelines
    • Enhance and refactor the existing in-house developed ETL Framework
    • Support and manage the existing Data warehouse
    • Work with the SRE (Site Reliability Engineers) team to optimize processes and resolve any issues that may arise
    • Identify, document and promote best practices throughout the SDLC along with data team operations
    • Help drive the team’s ML and data science efforts, application and capacity


    About you:

    • 3+ years of experience in ETL / Data Pipeline development
    • 3+ years of experience in Python / Scala / Java development
    • 3+ years of experience in SQL Development
    • You have a good understanding of Data Warehousing concepts
    • You are experienced in dimensional data modeling, big data technologies (Hadoop, Hive, Spark etc), distributed columnar databases like Redshift, Snowflake, Google BigQuery etc
    • You are genuinely curious about exploring new technologies
    • You aren’t afraid to try out new ideas, accepting failure as a key part of learning


    We love working here, and we hope that you will too!
