Project Description

Travel industry leader is looking for Oracle Data Integrator to work with their big data storages across company. You would support many state of art solutions with Oracle as database.

Responsibilities

  • Build, manage and maintain data integration processes in travel industry solutions.
  • Designing and developing database structures based on the requirements.
  • Designing and developing the most optimal data flows among systems.
    • Assuring architectural consistency of the data models and interfaces across the systems
  • Support the reporting team in aligning data models and interfaces to the team's needs, support reports optimization.
  • Support testing, test automation and data quality improvement process.
  • Support existing and new queries optimization.
  • Support database performance improvement.

    Must Experience in:

  • Oracle Data Integrator
  • SQL and PL/SQL (minimum 2 years of experience in Oracle development)
  • requirements engineering (good to have business/system analysis experience)
  • data governance, data management, data structuring

    • Nice to have Experience with big data solutions and Oracle certificates are a big plus.
