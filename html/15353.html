IQVIA™ is the leading human data science company focused on helping healthcare clients find unparalleled insights and better solutions for patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness the power of healthcare data, domain expertise, transformative technology, and advanced analytics to drive healthcare forward.


PURPOSE OF JOB


IQVIA is developing its next generation Global Data Warehouse platform to support near real time analytics and insights against hundreds of Terabytes and Petabytes of health care data.

We are currently seeking resources with experience in building and taking to production low latency, Massive Parallel Processing (MPP) data and analytic systems, using technologies such as Hadoop and Spark.

PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES
  • Should be able to understand application requirements
  • Responsible for application design and development
  • Handle complete unit testing of the component or application
  • Support testing and UAT phases and resolve the identified issues
  • Implement the application in production and complete post-production checks
  • Work in a fast-paced agile development environment along with DevOps and ProdOps concepts/ teams.
  • Partner with architects to develop effective solutions that solve business challenges
  • Work with data architects on the logical data models and physical database designs optimized for performance, availability and reliability
  • Proactively help resolve difficult technical issues
  • Participate in projects through various phases
  • Scripting and automation to support development, QA and production database environments and deployments to production
  • Keep management informed of work activities and schedules


KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE
  • Computer Science Bachelor’s Degree
  • Experience in Big Data technologies such as Hadoop/HDFS, Hive, Impala, HBase, Cloudera etc.
  • Experience in Spark using Scala
  • Experience in database and data warehousing concepts and techniques and Core Java
  • Understand relational and dimensional modeling, star/snowflake schema design, BI, Data Warehouse operating environments and related technologies, and ETL, MDM, and data governance practices
  • Experience with low latency (near real time) systems and working with Tb data sets, loading and processing billions of records per day
  • Ability to work with end-users to gather requirements and convert them to working documents
  • Ability to complete the full lifecycle of software development and deliver on time
  • Must be able to interface with various solution/business areas to understand the requirements and prepare documentation to support development
  • Healthcare experience is a plus
