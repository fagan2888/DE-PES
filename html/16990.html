Data Infrastructure Engineer RTL is looking for a Data Infrastructure Engineer to join the team that provides the foundation for all our intelligence activities. We’re experiencing strong growth and need your help making our vision of infrastructure-at-scale a reality. RTL collects a wide range of data (for example, video views, navigation clicks, social media data, sub titles, weather data et cetera), and there are many different end users of the data. Your role The data are stored in our cloud-based data platform. We process high volumes of data in both streaming and batch fashion. The Data Engineer at RTL is responsible for creating, maintaining and innovating the underlying infrastructure of the data platform as well as implementing pipelines that will handle all the information that needs to be processed. They troubleshoot and identify. Even more importantly they write software to detect and even mitigate the same problem early the next time around. Every day you work close with data analysts, data scientists and collaborate and advice how to use the data in the most optimal way. As an example, together with the data science team you bring all kinds of algorithms, like recommendations for RTL Nieuws or Videoland, into production. Responsibilities Data has a bright future at RTL. Together with your team you develop a truly scalable platform for any type of data that is used within RTL. To do this, your stack includes Kafka, Java, Kubernetes, Terraform, Docker and AWS. We prefer to write in Python and PySpark, but if you want to convince us with a great alternative, we’re open to it! Within the team you are responsible for optimizing the infrastructure of our data platforms.
  • Working with internal stakeholders to ensure that we have the right infrastructure to address our current needs and future wants; capacity planning for both short and long-term growth
  • Diving deep into hardware issues to determine major contributing factors and apply fixes
  • Solving routine problems and frequent processes with software
  • Implement and optimize data pipelines to ingest data from the large variety of data-sources Together with other data engineers and data scientists you initiate and implement data-driven innovations. Minimum Qualifications
  • Thorough understanding of Data Ops and troubleshooting
  • Thorough understanding of data infrastructure
  • Experience developing software / applications with Python or Java
  • Experience with Git and Code Review process
  • Experience with data and engineering in cloud environments
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills and the ability to thrive in a diverse, fast-changing environment
  • Agile mindset Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience setting up and testing CI/CD pipelines
  • Experience in using Terraform, Kubernetes and Docker
  • Experience developing API’s This is where you’ll be working You will be part of the Data Intelligence team within the Technology business unit of RTL Nederland - reporting to the Head of Data Intelligence. The media industry is changing rapidly. People watch content more and more on demand - what, when and where they like it. The use of online video services such as Netflix and Videoland are growing in popularity. At RTL Nederland, the largest commercial media and entertainment company in the Netherlands, the role of intelligence increases. There is a wide variety of use cases like customer segmentation, churn prediction, recommendations, forecasting, price optimization, video analyses and many more. The Data Intelligence team consists of data engineers and data scientists with top talents from all around the world. The Data Intelligence team builds data science products that are creating business value for RTL and explores new data-driven innovations. Interested? At RTL there is plenty of room for individual initiative and personal responsibility in a challenging and changing environment. In addition, RTL has extensive secondary benefits such as retirement, career development and profit sharing elements. Acquisitie naar aanleiding van deze vacature is niet gewenst.
