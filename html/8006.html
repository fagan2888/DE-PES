    Digital Recruitment Consultant at RattleBerry Talent Solutions

    • We’re looking for a smart, savvy and highly-motivated Digital Project Manager to work on creative, innovative, multifaceted projects on behalf of our global education technology client.


    The Digital Project Manager leads multiple projects through all phases including initiation, planning, execution, monitoring, reporting and closure. We will need you to work with marketing, sales, product, developers, external agencies and the senior leadership team on a wide range of projects, such as the creation of new courses, building new ecommerce capabilities, and launching marketing campaigns.


    Responsibilities:


    • Support the design and development of new projects and initiatives

    • Provide end to end creative project management including brief intake, resourcing, scheduling, budgeting and tracking.

    • Establish and manage detailed and realistic project plans, ensuring on time and accurate delivery for high-quality implementation.

    • Manages multiple projects and schedules simultaneously, shifting priorities and timelines as needed.

    • Acts as primary contact between creative and cross-functional teams to follow up on deliverables and ensure objectives are clear and actionable.

    • Identify opportunities for increased collaboration and operational improvement

    • Audit current tooling and processes; streamline for efficiency

    • Facilitate stakeholder creative reviews and navigate towards the best and most balanced solution that satisfies a select number of stakeholders


    About you:


    • You have 5+ years of experience as a digital-focused project manager.

    • You are a master of detail, always taking responsibility for tying up the loose ends

    • You have experience working in a multidisciplinary environment: design, development, content, marketing, sales

    • Ideally you have experience delivering digital marketing projects, including email, social, video, print + website assets.

    • You have experience defining plans, timelines and budgets

    • You’re a people person with excellent communication, collaboration + organizational skills.

    • You are comfortable working in a start-up environment: small, fast-paced and constantly changing

    • You have a passion for improving and refining processes.


    About us:


    We are a talent solutions business focused on helping our clients in the digital product, marketing and solutions space hire the best talent for their team.


    We are welcoming conversations with suitable people for this open role and we look forward to engaging with you should you fit the bill. Get in contact by emailing hello [at] rattleberry [dot] co or apply where you are reading this.
