Job Description

Are you a dedicated and bright mind that would like to join the One ERP Program and support Danfoss’ digital transformation?

Your Main Tasks And Responsibilities Will Include

One ERP is the foundation of Danfoss’ digital transformation. It will give Danfoss speed and flexibility enabling us to give a better digital customer experience. An ambitious plan is in place and will cover all Danfoss entities across the world. The One ERP team is working out of a hub in Flensburg, Germany where joined workshop are conducted.
  • Provides business segment and data expertise (data objects structure knowledge)
  • Provide business segment oversight
  • Knowledge of Data Cleansing rules and standards specific to the individual business segments
  • Design Data Cleansing solutions and initiatives
  • Setup of the BODS and SAP IS system landscape supporting the Data Cleansing activities
  • Take care about fine tuning in SQL code improving performance and accuracy
  • Raise issues and risks for specific release to Key Stakeholders

Background And Skills
  • 2-5 years of experience with Business background
  • Good communication skills in both Chinese and English
  • Inter-cultural competencies
  • Understanding of principles of Data Management
  • Knowledge of data systems and processes
  • Data Management skills
  • SAP IS / BODS
  • SAP Business process background
  • Basic SAP technical skill
  • Office location: Flexible, Tianjin is highly preferred

We build energy-efficient solutions that reduce food loss and make cities cleaner – using digital technology to make systems smarter and more connected – and we engineer innovative new ways to save energy and transition to clean energy for the good of our climate.
