ICIS is part of Reed Business Information (RBI), a division of our parent company, RELX Group, which had revenues of $10 billion in 2017. Serving global supply chains both up and downstream, ICIS provides data services, information and marketing solutions that help businesses in the energy, petrochemical and fertilizer industries make strategic decisions , mitigate risk , improve productivity and capitalise on new opportunities . We make this possible through a global presence that delivers the targeted , local insights customers need to achieve growth in this new world of meaningful data.

ICIS is seeking a Key Account Manager to join its experienced petrochemical sales team in South Korea; Primarily focused on generating business from existing clients. You will be responsible for contacting and qualifying prospects, explaining our products and services, proposing solutions that meet customer needs, and closing new business. An effective Key Account Manager must be an excellent communicator, have superior people skills and possess a strong work ethic. They must be comfortable presenting products or services over the phone and face to face as well as dealing with sales objections.

Experience & Accountabilities

  • You will have a proven track record at managing, retaining and growing the company’s top tier accounts as well as for maintaining accountability for both personal performance and results.
  • You will be able to establish strong, long-term customer relationships across the organisations you will manage at all levels of the organisation.
  • You will have the credibility to speak with CEOs and senior business managers as well as being able to maintain relationships with decision makers, stakeholders and users across several departments.
  • You will be curious, and you will be keen to continuously learn in order to lead your customer conversations with insight.
  • You should be able to develop a deep understanding of customer workflows and customer value whilst maintaining high volume and high-quality customer engagement through phone, email, social media and onsite visits and should be travelling to targeted clients each month.

Behaviours

  • You are curious about everything, willing to ask questions and challenge the status quo in order to provide better customer outcomes.
  • You have passion & belief in yourself and the company and you are driven by proving a world-class customer experience.
  • You have courage, you are prepared to innovate and you will take risks as you strive for excellence.
  • You work hard, go the extra mile and are committed to fulfilling your potential.
  • Be prepared for occasional foreign travels

Qualifications

  • Previous experience in a consultative sales position preferably license based subscriptions or data services.
  • Focus on solution sales and selling on value



RBI is an equal opportunity employer and all qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, colour, religion, sex, national origin, disability status, protected veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law.
