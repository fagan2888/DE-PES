    Our Data Engineers design and build complex real time streaming data ingestion and processing pipelines that power our Applied Intelligence applications on the Vulcan-ai Platform to help enterprises do more with less.


    Innovation.Applied.At Scale.Now.



    Vulcan-AI is an enterprise AI solution provider that helps enterprises do more with less. Better outcomes, with lesser resources and carbon footprint. We build AI to better manage the Internet of Things (IoT) and power responsible enterprises.


    We build intelligent applications that incorporate industry, functional processes and engineering know-how into AI applications that can be deployed >10X faster than typical consulting engagements.


    Our products are elegantly and beautifully designed not just on the outside, but also on the inside, creating a User Experience that both Users and Enterprises love.


    Our goal is to democratize AI and make it easily accessible to our clients at a fraction of the cost and a faster time to market compared to current approaches. Our team works on cutting edge tools and technologies related to artificial intelligence and IoT. We offer a fun environment where you are an inventor at our AI & IOT garage where the digital and physical world come together to create new solutions / products.


    At Vulcan AI you’ll work with a team of people who are passionate about AI, IoT, Big Data and Design and love their respective crafts. The founding members are leaders from Accenture and EY where they incubated and scaled AI practices. The Management Team has a successful track record of delivering successful AI and IoT projects across APAC and building analytics business that were very successful commercially. Our AI team members have several research papers and patents in the field. You will be working with, and learning from the best.


    Some of our areas of focus include

    -       Vision AI for Alpha generation by tapping on data like Satellite Imagery, Weather, etc

    -       Sensor Fusion and Operational Intelligence

    -       AI powered Robotics for Manufacturing Quality Control

    -       Deep Learning & Reinforcement Learning for Supply Chain Synchronization & Trading etc.

    -       Smart Factory solutions for Asset Health, Energy Management and Yield Optimization

    -       Intelligent Process Optimization


    As a Data Engineer/Data Modeller you will work alongside our talented data engineering team and data scientist team to build and deploy AI application for our customers on cloud and on-premise data ecosystems. You will use your expertise to solve complex challenges in data engineering and deployment for Vulcan AI platform


    ﻿ Your Responsibilities:

    • Design, develop, and maintain performant and scalable full-stack applications.Build and improve data engineering application development for data science that enable data scientist to build an end-to-end AI application quickly.
    • Work very closely with, and incorporate feedback from Product Management, Designers, and data scientist.
    • Design and build data management platform, data landscape and data models
    • Implement Data Architecture at scale on various cloud platforms with clustered databases
    • Engage in cross-team technical design discussions on application architecture, UI components, UX, back-end and third-party integration, and testing.
    • Rapidly fix bugs, solve problems, and proactively strive to improve our products and technologies.
    • Manage individual project deliverables and mentor junior team members on industry coding standards and design techniques.
    • Must have can do attitude and evolve yourself by learning new techniques, workarounds and tools everyday
    • Help build a team and cultivate innovation.


    Requirements:

    ·       Preference for Bachelor’s/Master’s or Doctoral degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related engineering field.

    • Ability to communicate the logic of design changes in a way that product stakeholders can understand and relate to.
    • Ability to iterate on designs and solutions efficiently and intelligently.
    • A willingness to help and share knowledge with other team members.
    • A willingness to learn and should be go getter.
    • Open to feedback and constructive criticism.
    • The ability to anticipate needs and take required actions.
    • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
    • Strong work ethic and the utmost attention to detail.

    ·       7+ years of experience with design and development of distributed, real-time, scalable data engineering and ETL/ELT applications.

    ·       3+ years of experience with using and developing technologies (minimum two if not all) such as Big Data(Hadoop all flavours), MySQL, MongoDB, InfluxDB, Redis, TimescaleDB, Apache Cassandra, CouchBase/CouchDB, Spark(Scala/Python), Relational Databases, PostgresSQL, RedShift, Azure CosmosDB, Amazon DynamoDB, Google Datastore ElasticSearch, Docker, AWS EMR, Azure Databricks, HDInsight etc

    ·       3+ years of experience developing data engineering applications using Spark/Flink(Scala/Java/Python), Azure Databricks stack, Azure Data Factory, AWS Glue

    ·       2+ years of experience in developing Cloud Stack such as AWS, Azure, Google etc

    ·       2+ years of experience in shell scripting(Bash/KSh/CSh), Scala, Python, Jscript, Ruby, Go etc.

    ·       2+ years of experience in designing data models using tools such as ERWin, ER Studio, Toad Data Modeller etc. Must have understanding of Data Lake Data Models such as Data Vault,

    ·       Experience or knowledge on Cloud IoT platforms such as Azure IoT Hub, AWS IoT Services/Solution, Google Cloud IoT etc will be added advantage

    ·       Strong knowledge in any commercial ETL tool such Talend/Informatica with Big Data with real-time streaming stack will be added advantage

    ·       Experience in maintaining code version using Git, GitLab, Bitbucket etc.

    ·       Good to have knowledge on Open Source projects for IoT such as Apache Brooklyn, Apache Camel, Edgent, Apache Mynewt, and Apache NiFi

    ·       Good knowledge in CI/CD tools such as Jenkins, Bamboo, GitLab Puppet, Chef, Bamboo, Ansible etc. will be added advantage

    • Knowledge on container orchestration tools such K8S, Docker Swarm, AWS ECS, Azure Container Service, Google Container Engine etc. will be added advantage


    ·       Good experience in building containers using Docker(Compose/Services/Stack/Secure).

    ·       Rigor in high code quality, automated testing, and other engineering best practices.

    Preferred

    • Good knowledge of big data, analytics, and cloud technologies.
    • Understanding of machine learning concepts, examples, and benefits.
    • Ability to juggle multiple priorities and effectively deliver in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.

    ·       Knowledge of SBC(Single Board Computers) such as Nvidia Jetsen Nano, Raspeberry Pi, PCDuino, BeagleBone(Black/Blue), Arduino etc

    ·       Knowledge of Apache Flink with Scala/Java API knowledge

    ·       Singapore Citizen/Permanent Resident

    ·       For the right candidate Employment Pass will be sponsored (>8 years of experience)
