Principal Software Engineer

Parsons Corporation Annapolis, MD, US

1 month ago

Apply on company website
Parsons, a global company delivering cyber and intelligence solutions, has immediate openings in some of the most sought-after work in the development and engineering community.

Our Principal Software Engineers (SWE) work as part of an integrated team to formulate and define system scope and objectives for programs. The Principal SWE has full technical knowledge and responsibility of all phases of applications, systems analysis, and programming. The Principal SWE must understand the mission function for which applications are designed and review and monitor the work of team members to ensure adherence to quality assurance.

Required Qualifications
  • TS/SCI Clearance with Polygraph.
  • 8+ years of experience performing systems analysis, development, and implementation of software systems, to include:
    • the development and design of application software for prototyping, production, and architecture products.
    • the design, development, flow analysis, detailed design data flow diagrams, structure charts, and PDL’s for each module of the software system.
  • Experience with full stack development: Javascript, JQuery, HTML, CSS, LESS, Java, Spring, Junit, REST

Desired Qualifications
  • 8-10 years of experience working within the DoD/IC mission space is preferred.
  • 5+ years of experience in Agile or Software Prototype development
  • 4+ years of leadership experience (e.g. directing/mentoring team members)
  • Experience solving software related problems, generating and reviewing software designs for accuracy, completeness, and appropriateness in light of objectives.
  • Experience with Apache, NiFi-MiniFi
  • Experience working in a Cyber Operations environment.

Education

A Bachelor’s Degree and Master’s Degree in an Information Technology related field such as Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Math, or Physics from an accredited college or university is desired.

As an employee-centric organization, Parsons provides our engineers and developers opportunities to thrive within an entrepreneurial culture with the backing and stability of a large company. Our benefits reflect our commitment as a people first organization. Apply today to learn more.
Must be eligible to obtain and maintain, or currently possess Top Secret SCI Poly clearance.
Ready for action? We’re looking for the kind of people who see this opportunity and don’t hesitate to act. Parsons is a leader in the world of Technical Services and Engineering. We hire people with a broad set of technical skills who have proven experience tackling some of the greatest challenges. Take your next step and apply today.
