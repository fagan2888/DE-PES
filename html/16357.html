    • About The Firm


    Zoe is an NYC based start-up set to reinvent wealth management. The firm has a strong leadership team with over 15 years of industry experience at firms like Morgan Stanley, JP Morgan Stanley and Learnvest. Zoe has raised $5 million in seed funding from THE top executives at the largest investment firms in the world (JP Morgan and Blackrock.)



    About the Role

    Zoe is looking for a Software Engineer to join the engineering team in our Bogota (Zona Chico) office.

    As an engineer on our team, you will solve, with code, many challenging technical problems. Your work will require creative thinking and problem-solving. You will have the opportunity to work at a company that is redefining personal finance through technological innovation.

    Responsibilities:

    • Write, maintaining and improving existing codebases and peer review code changes
    • Diagnose, and troubleshoot problems & develop solutions to help Zoe grow
    • Liaising with colleagues to implement technical designs
    • Improving system quality by identifying issues and common software patterns



    Requirements:

    • Expertise in PHP framework (Laravel)
    • 2+ years of experience with relational database design, programming, and integration experience in MySQL and/or PostgreSQL
    • 1+ year experience with REST API’s Development
    • Experience with software engineering best practices (e.g. unit testing, code reviews, deployment process, design documentation)
    • BS in Computer Science and 3+ years of experience


    Qualities we are looking for in our Engineers:

    • Attention to detail. Personal pride in data correctness and cleanliness
    • Comfortable transforming messy incoming data into easily maintainable databases
    • Belief in complete honesty and respect for your colleagues and team
    • The desire to continuously grow professionally, intellectually & personally
    • Love to experiment and use data to drive decision-making
    • Clear communication
    • Passion for building industry transforming APIs and the tools and documentation to help the industry adopt them
    • Interest in learning about finance, investing and/or economics
    • A love of both delightful user experience and correct back-end implementation
    • Experience working as an engineer in at least one other company

    We’ve got a competitive salary and stock options package (as a member of the team you own a piece of the company we get to build together)
