Data Warehouse Specialist

Accenture Greece Athens, Attiki, Greece

5 days ago

Apply on company website

The Data Warehouse specialist will be part of Accenture Applied Intelligence as an engineer focused on implementing data models and ETLs for Data Warehouses. He/she will be responsible in delivering parallel DWH solutions using new and innovative technologies on the Cloud or on premise. He/she will be part of Accenture’s Advanced Analytics Innovation Center located in Athens, Greece and will be working on exciting projects form clients across the globe.


Responsibilities:

  • Translate business requirements into specifications that will be used to drive the Data Warehouse design and configuration
  • Design and implement technology best practices, guidelines and repeatable processes for Data Warehouses, including sourcing, loading, transformation, and extraction of data
  • Map data between source systems, data warehouses, and data marts and design relational and dimensional data models
  • Use ETL tools to implement data extraction procedures from other systems and load them into the datamarts


WHO WE´RE LOOKING FOR?


  • B. Sc. from Computer Science, Engineering or related fields
  • M.Sc. or Ph.D. from Computer Science, Engineering or related fields will be considered as a plus
  • Proficient verbal and written communication skills in English
  • At least 2 years of hands-on experience in parallel Data Warehouse implementation using platforms such as Exadata, Netezza, MS SQL Server etc. and ETL tools such as Informatica, MS SSIS, Oracle Data Integrator etc.
  • Experience with defining dimensional data models (star schema)
  • Experience with BI/Visualization tools such as Qlik, Power BI and Tableau is a plus

    Mid-Senior level

    Full-time
