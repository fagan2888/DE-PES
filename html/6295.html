Job Description

IQVIA™ is the leading human data science company focused on helping healthcare clients find unparalleled insights and better solutions for patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness the power of healthcare data, domain expertise, transformative technology, and advanced analytics to drive healthcare forward.

Role Overview

Data services offering development and maintenance, sub-team management and development
Patient Surveys offering development and maintenance (data production part)
PMR systems suport and development.

Responsibilities
  • Ownership of main production areas - data input, data outputs, outputs delivery
  • Ongoing analysis and evaluation of existing processes with the objective of providing continuous improvements in cooperation with both business, production and functional teams in IQVIA
  • Development of production systems and processes, migration of legacy (tools: SAS, MSSQL, Oracle, Python, .NET, C#, VFP)
  • Ensuring deadlines are met for deliverables and we are going toward target data architecture
  • Automate of production processes, integrate local production and international production
  • Supporting team members, serving as subject matter expert, Supporting implementation of new initiatives of the company.

What We Are Looking For
  • A data analyst/ programmer, responsible for processing data from the pharmaceutical sector
  • Data transformation programming knowledge, data cleaning and integration, analytics / analytical outputs, process automation
  • Ownership of the area and its development according to requirements, work in individual and projects, space for own initiative
  • Advantage of knowledge of some of the following technologies - Oracle, MSSQL, SAS, Power BI / Power Pivot, Python, C #, OLAP (MDX / DAX), VFP
  • Location can be Prague or Bratislava

Join Us

Making a positive impact on human health takes insight, curiosity, and intellectual courage. It takes brave minds, pushing the boundaries to transform healthcare. Regardless of your role, you will have the opportunity to play an important part in helping our clients drive healthcare forward and ultimately improve outcomes for patients.

Forge a career with greater purpose, make an impact, and never stop learning.
