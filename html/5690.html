    Joel Selzer

    Co-Founder & CEO of ArcheMedX

    • ArcheMedX is looking for an experienced Data Engineer to join our team. We are a well-funded, revenue-producing, early stage startup with great product vision and a growing user base. Every team member has a meaningful stake in the success of the business and the direction of the product. If you are interested in taking on an impactful role building solutions that will transform the healthcare and life science industries and truly make people’s lives better, let's talk!


    Please include links to any work you can share, descriptions of solutions you have built that you are particularly proud of, Github account, and any blogs or other relevant communities you contribute to, along with your resume.


    ﻿The Role

    Work directly with the company leadership and engineering team to grow and maintain our robust Data Warehouse and ETL pipeline, providing internal and customer-facing Insights and Analytics in support of our award-winning online learning and behavioral insights platform. Insights and data are at the core of our value proposition, and the team member in this role will be central to helping create the future vision for our product.


    We are already changing the lives of thousands of healthcare professionals and millions of patients. We have the vision to revolutionize how people use digital technology to learn, and a wealth of huge opportunities to have impact in the market. Join us!


    What You Bring

    ﻿

    • You know relational databases, SQL, and are excited by the kind of meaningful insights a well-instrumented product can generate.
    • A background architecting and building elegant, extensible, and flexible data-driven software solutions.
    • Hands on work creating clear, actionable dashboards and reports in a tool like Looker.
    • An understanding that data is only as valuable as the clear story it tells through focused insights and beautiful reports.
    • Real world experience with Data Warehouses, ETL pipelines, and reporting and insights solutions.
    • Belief in flow, low-latency delivery of value, and a tight build/measure/learn dev cycle.
    • You thrive in a fluid environment where we constantly reassess priorities but deliver product value without chaos and interruption.
    • (Bonus) Experience with AWS or other cloud SaaS platforms.
    • (More bonus) You are interested in functional programming languages like Clojure.


    What We Believe In


    • The magic of the two pizza team, and the ability of a small group of dedicated craftspeople to change the world.
    • Software Development is a creative problem-solving process.
    • Everyone should wear a lot of hats.
    • Everyone has good ideas.
    • We are all on the product team.


    What We Offer


    • The time and tools you need, and the freedom to advance in your craft.
    • A collaborative, whiteboards-everywhere, fun workplace.
    • A team of smart, like-minded, creative professionals who will constantly support and challenge you to build better solutions every day.
    • A safe environment where every idea is heard, where mistakes are the way we learn, and where experimentation is the rhythm of our day.
    • Trust that you know best how, when, and where you should work to achieve great results.
    • The opportunity to ship code constantly.


    Perks


    • Live and work in beautiful Charlottesville, VA
    • Cool office space on the downtown pedestrian mall
    • Employee stock options
    • Retirement plan
    • Unlimited paid time off
    • Generous employee health, dental, and vision benefits
    • New Apple equipment
    • Continuous opportunities for professional development, including a learning stipend to be spent at your discretion
    • Bi-annual company wide outings
