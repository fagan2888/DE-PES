    • The company:

    Our client, based in Brussels, really invests in their people. When you start you’ll get a 6 months training program to get integrated in the company to really understand it’s business. They’re pretty advanced in data and digitalization and invest a lot in innovation, so be ready to learn a lot! There’s a pleasant and dynamic atmosphere which can be noticed for example when you enter the renovated building. The combination of green, open spaces and a lot of natural light makes the office the modern environment of today. You end up in cohere organization where hard work is absolutely rewarded by a good work-life balance.

    The function:

    As a Senior Big Data Engineer you will be single point of contact between the data team and the architect. Your tasks will be the following:

    • Manage the full data pipeline in AWS
    • Review codes of Data Scientists an Analysts
    • Build datamarts (starting from DataVault)
    • Take care of the automation
    • Build the data dictionary in AWS
    • Optimize data modeling & perform algorithms on the data model
    • Coaching a junior data engineer and managing consultants.

    Nice to know: One of the big advantages of this position is that you will be able to obtain certificates in DataVault and other technologies.

    Your profile:

    • You have 5+ years’ experience in Data Engineering
    • You have experience in setting up Cloud environments (Azure, AWS, Google Cloud, …)
    • You have excellent SQL and Python coding skills
    • It is a big plus if you have experience in DataVault
    • Your English is perfect and you speak French or you’re willing to learn it
