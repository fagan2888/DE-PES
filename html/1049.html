About SCOR

SCOR is the 5th largest Reinsurance Company worldwide, following a profit-driven underwriting policy and practising selectively across all branches of reinsurance. Our specialist teams operate in over 120 countries, developing value added and innovative products and services and making long-term commitments to their clients, namely insurers and large corporations.
SCOR's aim, as an independent global reinsurance company, is to develop its Life and P&C business lines, to provide its clients with a broad range of innovative reinsurance solutions and to pursue an underwriting policy founded on profitability, supported by effective risk management and a prudent investment policy, in order to offer its clients an optimum level of security, to create value for its shareholders, and to contribute to the welfare and resilience of Society by helping to protect insureds against the risks they face.

  • Job Summary

    The Innovation team is in charge of managing capability of innovation like Machine Learning and AI (Artificial intelligence), Big data, RPA (Robotic Process Automation), Blockchain… and providing ad hoc technical environment.

    The Data Engineer Innovation is responsible for support Business on their needs on new technologies, challenge solutions, implement Proof of Concepts, provide, manage and promote Innovation services to business and functional IT, and train users for the best utilization. He could build quick prototype construction using business data to develop, test, elaborate and maintain analytics infrastructure to improve the data use capability, manage and support Machine Learning, RPA and AI implementation on POC or business projects.

    This position is an opportunity to be in a transversal division, supporting innovation in both IT functional, IT technical and business.

  • Key duties and responsibilities
    • Perform research and analysis as needed to understand, create and/or improve maintenance of sandboxes (data sciences, big data…) and give advices on architecture of big data, data transfer & dataflow to optimize performance results.
    • Manage RPA, machine learning and AI sandboxes, automate dataflow processes, data reconciliation issues.
    • Analyze deep impact of improvement by learning of robots/data sciences algorithms/IA on IT environment, processes and organization;
    • Keep up-to-date with latest technology trends in data analytics, machine learning and robot process automation, including installation method and managing infrastructure.
    • Support business & IT integration efforts by assisting in the development and understanding of business applications, rules, mapping and scoring, up to software engineering/code development.
    • Participate in the creation of rules, studies and systems designed to incorporate business value.
    • Foster and participate in a culture of continuous improvement.
    • Create and maintain process documentation as required.
    • Adhere to all Information Security policies and best practices, including security awareness training and other information protection initiatives.
    • Communicate on several subject, with right level of popularization/expertise, both formally and informally, in network with capability to animate communities, and provide large hall (& audience) presentation
    • Other duties as required for the position.

  • Required experience & competencies
    • Experience:
    • 3+ years in innovation area, implementing key technologies in IT environment.
    • Experienced in implementing & managing IT infrastructure evolution needs by new technologies: analytics infrastructure including profiling, reporting & data visualization, Hadoop installation…
    • Experienced in Software engineering (Java, Python, R) on several environments or frameworks (Spark, Jupyter, RStudio…) with capability on writing parallel execution code in Hadoop environment (Java, Python)
    • Experienced on Machine Learning and/or Artificial Intelligence implementation,
    • Experience on Robotic Process Automation & robotics developments greatly appreciated: rule based and automated systems implementation, including development and enhancement of rules, process, preferred.
    • Basic knowledge on mathematics, statistics and algorithms
    • Capability to support business statisticians & data scientists with software engineering appreciated
    • Experience in functional design creation, financial transactions, troubleshooting, dealing with (re)insurance issues appreciated

      Personal Competencies

    • Problem Solving – Identifies and analyses issues, proposes and rolls out relevant solutions after appropriate approvals.
    • Communication – Knows how and when to develop/sustain a message and/or an idea (speaking and writing), uses the right tools to present information. Ability to work within, cross functional & technical teams.
    • Resilience – Demonstrates an ability to step back when facing difficult situations and to bounce back after a failure. Learn on failure.
    • Decision Making – Makes decisions and/or rolls out the decisions taking into account all available information.
    • Accountability – Alerts, raises the critical points when needed, shoulders and corrects his/her decisions and shows responsibility regarding cost management in autonomy.
    • Commitment – Takes initiatives and makes effort to bring his/her skills and uses his/her expertise to contribute to the success of the Group.
    • Curiosity & Open-minded – Demonstrates an appetite for learning and developing his/her-self in his/her field of expertise, can develop new practices and methods in relation with his/her day-to-day work.
    • Creativity – Capacity to merge different ideas, to capture and integrate tendencies, and to capitalize to purpose new ways of works and functionalities.

      Digital Competencies

    • Ability to employ information: identify, recognize, record and store digital information to facilitate retrieval and use.
    • Ability to access information: locate, select and retrieve digital information.
    • Ability to determine data's quality, value, and relevance in different contexts.
    • Apply security measures in digital environments: protect hardware, software applications, data and personal information, data encryption.
    • Ability to create information: generate new digital content and knowledge by organizing, integrating, adapting and applying digital information.
    • Ability to identify and assess accurate sources of information, and understanding architecture. Required Education
    • Bachelor’s degree in mathematics, applied science, computer science or equivalent work experience.
