    • About Our Client
    Our client is a well-funded innovative tech start-up backed by international investment funds. They apply Machine Learning and AI to create the top-class products to mass users. They just relocated their R&D hub to Vietnam with the crystal clear strategy of making this is one of their best across the other hubs in Asia.

    Job Description
    • Create standardized data engineering frameworks for data integration - ELT/ETL programs , design, plan and handle platform upgrade activities for the Enterprise Data Lake platform
    • Perform POCs and generate valuable results together with new big data technologies based on team's strategies.
    • Participate in Agile sprints and ceremonies; supports rapid iteration and development
    • Develop, maintain, and test data pipelines, application framework, infrastructure for data generation; works closely with information architects and Data Scientists
    • Experience initializing and managing cloud platforms: AWS, Azure etc
    • Design and implement data architectures in production environments
    • Implement data orchestration pipelines, data sourcing, cleansing, augmentation and quality control processes
    • Deploy machine learning models in production
    The Successful Applicant
    • BSc in Computer Science, Statistics, Informatics, Information System, Mathematics or equivalent quantitative field preferred
    • Understanding of software development lifecycle
    • Able to achieve most tasks using own judgement
    • Understanding of how technology supports company's business strategy
    • Familiar with modern software architectures and API-driven development
    • Familiar with traditional database technologies such as: Oracle, MySQL, Postgres, Hadoop, Spark
    • Understanding of relational and warehousing database technology working with at least one of the major databases platforms (e.g., Oracle, SQLServer, Teradata, MySQL, or Postgres)
    • Solid working knowledge of data processing tools using SQL, Spark, Python or similar open source and commercial technologies
    • Knowledge of Spark, Java/Scala especially in relation to big data open source software preferred; knowledge of NiFi
    • Knowledge of NoSQL (HBase, MongoDB) databases preferred
    • Knowledge and familiarity with machine learning models application and production pipelines

    What's On Offer
    • International environment with transparency and support from management team
    • Flat Agile Organization
    • Top-tier salary and other benefits
