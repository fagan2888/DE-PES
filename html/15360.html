Mobility and Energy industries are currently facing some of the biggest challenges of their history, with the noble and simple idea to optimise the mobility of goods and people, as well as the resource-efficiency that intends to reduce the cost and the environmental impact of mobility.
Whether it is providing a highly safe and performing European safety system, designing a new type of offshore wind turbine or developing IT and mobile services dedicated to civil aviation, Koneos is driven by innovation and an entrepreneurship vision.

More than your technical skills, we strongly believe that your personality, your values and your passion will have more positive impact to overcome the technological challenges of our customers.

About The Job

As Signalling Design and Data Preparation Engineer, your main technical responsibility will be to successfully lead ETCS / IL Design, manage the data preparation and ensure the realisation of ETCS L2 technical subsystem. We expect from you a strong expertise in ETCS / IL sub-system analysis, in order to define global architecture and implement ETCS / IL products, to tackle the engineering phase and to define the interfaces between sub-systems or systems related to mobility projects :
  • Analyse, review, evaluate and engineer all ETCS / IL design and data subsystem requirements specifications
  • Produce design plan and data engineering setup
  • Produce technical ETCS / IL sub-system documentation (design, data files, interfaces,concept, ...)
  • Review ETCS / IL sub-system related documents
  • Produce schematic Cable Distribution Plan, Power supply distribution, Room Layout, etc...
  • Establish the URS of the sub-system (when applicable) as basis for R&D work andprocurement
  • Engineer the ETCS / IL sub-system (or lead external engineer teams), as well as data test
  • Assist the manufacturing and ordering process
  • Work with the ETCS / IL integration and test team
  • Support the system site integration and commissioning works
  • Support the validation and approval works

Reporting And Controlling

Along with your technical skills, we expect from you to ensure a regular and seamless communication and support the interfaces between the Project, the Technical and the Program Management teams, as well as with the miscellaneous key-players on the project. This includes :
  • Plan and control on quality, time & cost basis the project execution of the ETCS / IL relevant work packages
  • Analyse and supervise the fulfilment of the plans with regard to functional, technical and time-related goals
  • Early recognise deviations in his/her work packages early, identify problems and solve them (in coordination with the technical project management, when necessary)
  • Report the outputs of the controls to the Project and Program Management teams
    Interface, partner and risk management :
  • Ensure and manage the interoperability of the system
  • Manage the Interfaces, the Integration and the Testing & Commissioning
  • Involve and interface timely the R&D, the internal and external subsystem partners
  • In case of shortcomings escalate problems regarding quality, deadline shifts and collaborationwithin the given timeline



    • Improvements

  • Drive ideas and activities as part of the project related tasks with the aim to improve the project results
  • Use any possibility to improve the products and systems, as well as the system standardisation and system related processes

About You

You hold a Master degree in Electrical / Electronics engineering or related.
You are fluent in Dutch, English and French languages. Knowledge of German language is a plus.
You are passionate about the railways sector.
You are autonomous, have an analytical mind, some good communication and time management skills and develop a natural team spirit, as well as a practical sense of priorities.

Your Experience
  • You have a minimum of 5 years experience in Sub-system Engineering for ETCS / IL implementation in similar railways contracts
  • You already worked in an international environment
  • You already worked and lived experience abroad (e.g. within the scope of several businesstrips)



    • Your Qualifications

  • Railways signalling
  • ETCS level 1 / level 2 knowledge
  • Interface management and coordination
  • Sub-systems Engineering
  • Process Rules and Standards (CENELEC, European Norms, ...)
  • Requirements Engineering, Quality Management, Configuration management,Documentation management
  • System documentation, interface management and system integration
  • Experience in Engineering, Installation, Integration, Testing and Commissioning



    • Your Computer Skills

  • Expertise in Tools (MS-Office, AutoCAD, SAP, technical specific engineering tools, ...)
  • Extensive experience dealing with engineering teams, customers and suppliers
