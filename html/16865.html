    • We currently have a vacancy for a Data Expert with experience in Machine Learning fluent in English, to offer his/her services as an expert who will be based in Brussels, Belgium . The work will be carried out either in the company's premises or on site at customer premises. In the context of the first assignment, the successful candidate will be integrated in the team that will closely cooperate with a major client's IT team on site.

    Your tasks:

    • Analyse, assess and maintain a catalogue of AI use cases;
    • Implement AI use cases as end-to-end machine learning system, from proof of concept to production;
    • Participate in analysing and drafting strategy and action plans documents related to data and artificial intelligence;
    • Interact with stakeholders to define data governance rules;
    • Design strategic data architecture and coordinate its implementation;
    • Analyse data architecture for consistency, completeness and accuracy;
    • Participate in data management and data engineering tasks;
    • Participate in business intelligence implementation.

    Requirements:

  • Minimum 16 years of combined relevant university studies and experience in IΤ;

  • Background in data science or data engineering or artificial intelligence;

  • Proven experience with IT implementation of AI solutions in production;

  • Proven experience with IT implementation of data strategy, data architecture and data management tools;

  • Excellent understanding of machine learning techniques and algorithms;

  • Experience with common data science toolkits, such as R, Weka, NumPy, MatLab;

  • Experience with data visualization tools, such as D3.js, GGplot;

  • Experience with scripting in SQL and NoSQL databases (MongoDB, Cassandra, HBase);

  • Experience with Business Intelligence tools (SAP BI or similar);

  • Knowledge of Agile methodology;

  • Proven professional experience in coaching and training and excellent interpersonal and communication skills.

    • Benefits:

    If you are seeking a career in an exciting and dynamic company, where you will offer your services as part of a team of a major European Institution, operating in an international, multilingual and multicultural environment where you can expect real chances to make a difference, please send us your detailed CV in English, quoting reference: (DE/10/19) .


    We offer a competitive remuneration (either on contract basis or remuneration with full benefits package), based on qualifications and experience. All applications will be treated as confidential.

The company employs over 600 engineers, IT experts and consultants (around 3% PhD, 36% MSc and 53% BSc or equivalent). We design and develop software applications using state-of-the-art technology. The group generates annual revenues in the range of EURO 40 million, with an EBITDA in the range of 20%. The value of our contract portfolio exceeds EURO 250 million.
