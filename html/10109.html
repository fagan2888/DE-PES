Data Scientist

Unlimit Recruitment Bratislava, SK

6 months ago

Apply on company website
Náplň práce
Your key focus area on this position will be the migration/ update and on-going development and support for a cloud based, web-scraping & price modelling tool that provides insights on policy pricing.

Your Role Will Be To
  • Design, develop, test and maintain architecture and systems for modelling & advanced analytics processes.
  • Employ a variety of languages & tools to combine systems together, ensuring that the architecture & systems will support the needs of Business.
  • Convert data codes from R to Python.
  • Support actively Swiss Business Unit.
Požiadavky
  • University level education, e.g Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or Engineering Discipline
  • At least 3 years relevant experience as a Software Engineer or Software Developer in an advanced analytics environment
  • Experience with commercial software development or contribution to open-source software development projects
  • Advanced knowledge & high degree of competence with Python
  • Knowledge / competence in a range of programming languages / technologies combined with a desire and aptitude to broaden that experience (VBA, R, SQL, MySQL, Matlab, CI/CD toolsets)
  • Ability to work effectively in a global organization, under time pressure and to manage ambiguity.
  • Fluent in English (spoken and written).
O spoločnosti
Our client is Global insurance company delivering services in more than 170 countries, successfully running a Service Center in Bratislava since 2006 with 180 employees currently. The company provides actuarial and financial services including actuarial modeling, reporting, software development & maintenance of financial data calculation, change management processes, support in Finance planning and performance management, IT enterprise application services, GI Claims analytics.
