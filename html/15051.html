Ansøg på virksomhedens websted
At Danfoss Cooling, within the Service Innovation team, we are currently looking for an IoT Technical Lead (m/f/d) in Hamburg, Germany . Alternatively, you may be located in Nordborg or Copenhagen, Denmark.

The Service Innovation team is part of Danfoss Cooling - the leading supplier to the refrigeration and air-conditioning industries. Within a high-paced and dynamic environment we have high ambitions to deliver even better digital services to both existing and new customers. To realize our ambitions, we are strengthening our Center of Excellence for IoT Cloud solutions to take our existing service business to the next level.

As our new IoT Technical Lead, you will be responsible for the design and successful delivery of new digital services on an innovative cloud platform. You will collaborate with development teams, build the strategic roadmaps and manage the integration points. Additionally, you will keep track of requirements such as costs and timeline, consult internal customers and represent the team in communication with other departments and external stakeholders.

Background And Skills

For this role, you must have a university degree in computer science with substantial experience in a modern IoT solution environment. As a person, you are proactive and an effective communicator with a collaborative and open-minded attitude. Additionally, we are looking for the following skills:
  • Fundamental understanding of developing digital services in a cloud-based environment (Microsoft Azure)
  • Experience in leading a team in an agile environment
  • Proven record of leading projects in a technical or data processing environment
  • Fluent in English - German language skills are a plus

For further information about the position please contact Eva Isselstein, Senior Manager Talent Acquisition, at +49 461 4301 4623.

We are Engineering Tomorrow
The world faces growing populations, booming urbanization and rapid climate change. At Danfoss, we engineer ready-to-use solutions to these challenges. We build energy-efficient solutions that reduce food loss and make cities cleaner – using digital technology to make systems smarter and more connected – and we engineer innovative new ways to save energy and transition to clean energy for the good of our climate.
