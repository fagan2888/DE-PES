Job Title: Store Keeper

Location of Position: ALGERIA DZA

Posting Number: req3747

Description

Key Accountabilities:

Receives and inspects all incoming materials and reconciles with purchase orders;
processes and distributes documentation with purchase orders; reports,
documents and tracks damages and discrepancies on orders received.
Ships cancelled and damaged items back to vendors as appropriate.
Handles and documents storage and transportation of hazardous materials.
Maintains the warehouse, records area and stores area in a neat and orderly manner.
Answers questions regarding procedures and resolves discrepancies regarding receipts, deliveries, warranties, repairs and surplus property.
Uses forklift during material/ goods uploading and unloading
Determines inventory levels for all items purchased and stored; prepares orders sheets including item specifications and desired quantities.

Unload goods and check them against order forms count and record the number of items in store during stockades

Keeps a tight control on stock movements and to have all items inventoried and issued correctly at all times.

Be aware and follow security/fire/safety procedures according to Ship’s rules and regulations.

Necessary Knowledge, Skills And Abilities

Knowledge of inventory processes and procedures.
Ability to prepare, document and maintain inventory and supply records and reports.
Ability to perform routine mathematical calculations.
Excellent knowledge of MS-Office software suite
Ability to solve problems, take initiative and plan the tasks
Flexibility with time to work extra hours when the project requires.

  • Maximo knowledge and English are an advantage
