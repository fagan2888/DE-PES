Your Role and Responsibilities

Do you have good analytical skills and do you like moving in the field of data processing? Would you like to become an expert in access control and start your career in security environment? Do you want to apply in a company where they prefer career growth? Do not hesitate to write to us! We are looking for a suitable candidate for the position of: SECURITY DATA SPECIALIST

The Identity & Access Management (IAM) Security Specialist is responsible to ensure the timely and complete execution of UserID validation on customer systems and to detect users without required business need. This job role is an excellent entry into security and risk management. It will provide you with increased knowledge of the IBM internal security processes and provide a platform for future growth into roles such as, team leader, security manager, compliance coordinator, process leader and many others.

Responsibilities
  • Execution of the access control re-validation based on agreed schedule.
  • Interlock with the system administrator teams to gather the necessary input.
  • Process the received data and open service tickets to remove the unnecessary UserIDs from customer server systems.
  • Track the tickets until resolution. Document the process steps end-to-end and present it to IT Security auditors if requested.
  • Documentation and processes following and regular updating.
  • Support other Service Delivery functions/roles when needed.
  • Support all IAM project implementation.
  • Provide new ideas to further support and improve the service.
