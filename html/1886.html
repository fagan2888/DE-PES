    • Etex is a leading global manufacturer of building materials, with a turnover of €3 bn and 14,500 employees. For the division Etex Exteriors to support our marketing team, we are looking for a Digital Specialist LatAm.

    Reporting to the Regional Marketing Manager, you will work alongside locals Brand/Product Managers of the different categories/brands & Digital local teams to help to manage the customer journey and maximize customer value through digital means.

    Responsibilities

    The Digital Specialist will be responsible for deliver targeted marketing campaigns which create awareness and generate demand (valuable leads/enquiries) from the key DMU by implementing Etex Exterior digital marketing strategy, channels (selected & new) and tools.

    As a regional function, you are responsible to enable the local digital marketing organizations in your region to perform to their full potential. You will support local organization to develop and coordinate digital content & efficient use of channels & budget to targets completion. With your expertise and your personality, you can drive growth through efficient coordination and engagement.


    Requirements

    · Bachelor´s degree

    · You have clear experience in digital marketing with strong IT literacy ( graphic design is a plus ).

    · You have a strong understanding of digital key metrics (SEO, Analytics, Social media managers, etc).

    · Preferred background in a commercial organization focused on value creation .

    · High level of awareness, and pro-active approach towards safety , with a soundtrack record of achieving high standards of safety at all times for self, team members, all colleagues and third parties as appropriate.

    · You have strong conceptual and analytical skills : can back up opinions with facts, can identify what is important and what not.

    · You have excellent communication, strong presentation and persuasion skills.

    · You are critical, customer centric and action oriented.

    · Exceptional proofreading skills and an eye for detail and creativity .

    · Able to manage workload to meet deadlines and resilient, patient to stick to strategies until results follow in a structured and systematic way.

    · You feel comfortable with handling and enhancing change in an ever-ambitious and growing organization.

    · Ready to work in a multi-national matrix organization with feeling for local cultural aspects and specificities: Able to work as part of a team and independently.
