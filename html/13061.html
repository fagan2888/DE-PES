應徵

Job description:

  1. Extensive cross-functional communication including but not limit to interview with associate BU, define project scope and data schema
  2. Define Project Scope, schedule and evaluate algorithm/ tools to be used
  3. Define and build prediction models using ML and Python with incomplete or uncertain information
  4. Define advanced visualizations for story-telling
  5. Create presentations, management reporting and white papers as required
  6. Provide guidance to junior/ intermediate data scientists and data analysts


Qualifications:

  1. Master or PhD in Computer Science, Electronic Engineering, Statistics or related field.
  2. Overall 5-9 years of IT experience.
  3. More than 3 years of practical experience of data scientists
  4. Hands on experience with model development and software development
  5. Machine learning algorithm development, Deep learning-AAN, CNN, SVM, SGD, Logistic regression, Random forest, CNN, RNN (LSTM), GAN, and Reinforcement Learning.
  6. Experience of analyzing complex, high-volume, high dimensionality data from varying sources with unbalanced data, missing data, non-label data
  7. Familiar with version control, Jira, SW management methods and processes
  8. Familiar with Python or R and SQL
  9. Experience in implementing large-scale data projects and working with other data scientists
  10. Familiar with any of the following is Plus (AWS or GCP or Azure, IBM)
  11. Have a neural network experience like Tensor flow, Pytorch is Plus
