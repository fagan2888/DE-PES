Senior Data Analyst
Bradford
6 months
425- 455 per day

A Senior Data Analyst is required for our client based in Bradford.
The ideal candidate will have-
Experience across advanced analytics, data / business analysis, requirement definition and logical data design.
Business-facing approach and extensive experience working with end users across the business to document business/data requirements and understanding how the data is used.
Coaching business teams/leaders to help shape the problem/solution thinking, we are dealing with new concepts and techniques so there is ambiguity to overcome.
Experience with telemetry devices and data and/or IoT concepts.
Experience with data profiling and analysis of data with both technical and business stakeholders.
Conducting and supporting root cause analysis of data.
Ability to query and analyse a variety of datasets.
Strong Business Analyst experience
Strong data analyst experience
Some data architecture experience
Business intelligence experience
Advanced analytics including predictive and prescriptive models and design techniques
Experience with real-time / operational analytics
Data mapping
Data modelling experience
Clear understanding of data concepts
Strong stakeholder management.
Strong communication skills.
