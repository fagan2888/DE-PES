The Network Engineer is proactive in identifying, investigating and resolving technical incidents and problems and restoring service to clients by managing incidents to resolution. His/her primary objective is to ensure zero missed service level agreement conditions.

  • Ensure that assigned IT Infrastructure at the client site is configured, installed, tested and operational. Perform necessary checks, apply monitoring tools and respond to alerts.
  • Identify problems and errors prior to or when they occur and log all such incidents in a timely manner with the required level of detail.
  • Be a part of IT Network projects and operating on infrastructures: leveraging tools and processes to better resolve any IT incidents.
  • Produce breach and other reports to identify failures and short comings.
  • Focus on second line support for low complexity incidents.
  • Proactively offer improvements of operations in order to better meet the SLA standards


EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE, SKILLS

  • Higher degree in IT or Computer Science.
  • A first relevant experience in the field


  • Knowledge or experience of IT Networks:
  • Installing, Configuration, Routing and Switching
  • A keen interest in Infrastructure
  • Knowledge and certifications in Cisco SDA and Cisco ASA are considered an asset
  • Knowledge of Cloud systems (AWS, Azure) is also an asset


  • You demonstrate:
    • problem analysis and solution formulation skills.
    • learning and service orientation.
    • excellent attention to detail.
    • ability to adapt, to change and to think innovatively.
    • good verbal and written communication ability.
    • good planning and time management
  • Languages:
    • English is mandatory.
    • French and German are an asset
  • Any other certifications (CCNA, CCNP) are considered an asset
