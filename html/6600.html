Backend Software Engineer (Data Platform)

Vinted / Kleiderkreisel Vilnius, Vilniaus, Lithuania

2 weeks ago

Vinted is the world’s biggest second-hand style marketplace. Our aim is to make second-hand first choice worldwide. More than a year ago Vinted has reached a major milestone - we’ve become self-sustainable while still growing really fast. More than ~35 million people use our platform, which is developed and supported by ~240 Vinties. Vinted’s Data Platform Team makes sure everyone in our company has fresh data to make good decisions.


At the moment Data Platform Team consists of four engineers with real hacker mindset. You'll be working on improving the data platform to meet company needs: enable analysts to analyse ever-growing amounts of data (125+ TB of data at the moment, +16B new events per week) and help product teams with heavy data lifting to make our core product even greater.


We are looking for someone who likes to solve problems related to data. As there are many unsolved problems in the domain, we are always on lookout for new techniques and technologies, we experiment a lot and use unconventional ways to solve problems. Knowledge on how database systems work is a big plus. Experience with Apache Spark is not mandatory, though very useful. We value pragmatism, big picture thinking, curiosity and problem solving skills.


We expect you to be familiar with most, and have deep knowledge in at least a few of the following disciplines: Database Systems, Algorithms, Software Engineering, Systems Architecture, Big Data, Systems Scaling, Systems Performance Tuning, Computer Science.


Proficient programming skills are required as we need to engineer and maintain our tools daily.


This is a mid / senior level position.


In this role you will:

  • Help design a data platform on new hardware and move it there
  • Ensure that the company has new metrics ready for analysis every day (nightly, hourly and streaming job pipelines)
  • Optimise data collection, storing and analysis on a large scale
  • Identify opportunities for improvement in our data platform and build solutions to implement them


Perks:

  • Learning budget (10% from gross salary)
  • Ability to work remotely up to 50% of the time
  • 25 working days of holidays
  • We buy all the tech you need to do your job
  • Daily breakfast, lunch and an afternoon snack at Vinted Noise restaurant
  • Budgets for team-buildings
  • Inhouse gym
  • Shop @ Vinted budget


We’re looking for an engineer to join Data Platform Team in their work. Depending on your experience, the salary range is €2000 - €4000 net.


Here are some of the technologies we use: Hadoop HDFS, Apache Spark (for ETL, Streaming, ML and ad-hoc analysis), Apache Kafka (message bus for tracking events and integration with our core product), Cloudera Impala (for run-time metric aggregation, quick ad-hoc querying). For most of the solutions we use Scala and Ruby.
