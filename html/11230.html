Healthcare Information Solutions Resident Field Engineer

Philips Empoli, Tuscany, Italy

5 giorni fa 39 candidati

Candidati sul sito Web dell’azienda

In this role, you have the opportunity to

Be the main point of contact for the Customer for what concerns their installed solutions and work on, but not limited to, installations, maintenance, repair, customer training and other associated activities at customer’s premises on ESTAR-EMPOLI Hospitals (Empoli – Fucecchio - San Miniato - Castelfiorentino)

You are responsible to

  • Provide support to the customer solution(s)

  • Provide technical assistance for other support specialists and/or sales specialists

  • Provide vital link in the escalation process to Incident Support, maintaining problem ownership

  • Update technical documentation concerning site

  • Work with Project Managers to implement customer bought solutions

  • A commitment to a remote 24x7 support on-rota is expected

  • Set the highest standards for customer communication and documentation/paperwork

  • Actively adhere to Company’s Working Practices and Health & Safety guidelines

  • Other duties as assigned to support the general purpose of the position’s function.

You are a part of

the HCIS Incident Support team, supporting ESTAR-EMPOLI Hospitals (Empoli – Fucecchio - San Miniato - Castelfiorentino)

To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience

  • Qualified IT/Electronics engineer or equivalent.

  • Comprehensive knowledge and experience of databases, servers, storage, computers, networks.

  • At least 1 year Field service experience in a customer facing role

  • Experience of Windows Server based operating systems

  • Working knowledge of network architecture and IT products/solutions

  • Experience of remote support software

  • Self-motivated and able to manage own workload.

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to establish relationships..

In return, we offer you

A path towards your most rewarding career. Philips is growing its healthcare information solutions capability enterprise wide. Succeeding in this role in a complex environment will open many doors for your long term career, in other areas in Philips or otherwise. We also believe that we are at our best as a company when you are at yours as a person. Thus, we offer competitive benefits, a flexible work schedule and access to local well-being focused activities.

Why should you join Philips?

Working at Philips is more than a job. It’s a calling to create a healthier society through meaningful work, focused on improving 3 billion lives a year by delivering innovative solutions across the health continuum . Our people experience a variety of unexpected moments when their lives and careers come together in meaningful ways.
