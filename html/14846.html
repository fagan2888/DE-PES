We are currently working with a leading technology company who are looking for researchers to join their team. This will give you the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research on Machine Learning and Artificial theories and technologies.

As a Machine Learning Research Engineer your responsibilities will include;

·         Applying machine learning and big data analysis to enable the auto-drive optical/access network; e.g. predictive maintenance, auto service provision, self-healing and self-control.

·         Researching intelligent OAM of transmission and access networks with problems; e.g. root cause analysis, failure prediction, anomaly detection.

As a Machine Learning Research Engineer your skills will include:

·         Masters or PhD degree in Computer Science, Telecommunication or Automation, Applied Mathematics

·         Robust theoretical and applied knowledge, and high interest in solving ML/DL tasks; e.g. multivariable time series analysis, anomaly detection, online learning, ML on graphs.

·         Extensive experience in standard Python frameworks and libraries for data science; e.g. PyTorch/Tensorflow/Keras, numpy, scikit-learn, XGBoost)

If this sounds like it could be an amazing opportunity, please send a CV attached to your application or email me at jet@eu-recruit.com .

Feel free to also refer someone you may know who could be good for the role. If they are successfully placed, we offer a great referral scheme!

Key words - Machine Learning / Artificial Intelligence / Engineers / Deep Learning / Python / Programming / C / C++ / Java / Automotive
