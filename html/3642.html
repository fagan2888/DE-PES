Group Company: Deutsche Börse AG

Data Warehouse Engineer (f/m/d)

Full-time | Permanent

Field of activity

StatistiX® is the Data Warehouse of Deutsche Börse Group. StatistiX® provides external and internal users worldwide with statistical information on financial markets, offers Data Store Services and acts as Business Intelligence and Risk Analytics platform of Deutsche Börse Group.

Tasks/responsibilities


  • Functions as a primary contributor in designing, coding, testing, debugging, documenting and supporting all types of DW / OLTP applications consistent with established specifications and business requirements to deliver business value.
  • Gather business requirements, elicit technical requirements, and prepare specifications together with business area.
  • Design, Develop & Test components across development, test, production environments and implementation support as necessary.
  • Devises data verification and quality checks and system standard procedures that will be used to support Enterprise DW/BI platforms.
  • Develops complete descriptions of all specifications and solutions as required.
  • The Data Engineer also plays a key role in technological decision making for the future data, analysis, and reporting needs. He supports the business’s daily operations inclusive of troubleshooting and identify delivery mechanisms for acquiring and reporting such information as well as addressing the actual needs.


Qualifications/required Skills


  • University degree in Computer Science or similar, technical subject.
  • At least 2-5 years relevant work experience.
  • Knowledge with Informatica 10, Oracle RDBMS (Exa) & DWH concepts, SQL/PL-SQL, Java, Unix, Shell scripting and
  • Control-M.
  • ETL fine tuning skills and data modelling skills are absolute necessary.
  • Extreme fluency in SQL, SQL/PL-SQL, Oracle Exadata is needed.
  • Knowledge on Financial Markets, systems and process will be an added advantage.
  • Experience with Real-time technologies (wire protocol like AMQP and TCP/IP) will be an added advantage.
  • Know-How on Java, Spark & BI reporting will be an added advantage.
  • Good understanding of Data Lake, Operational Data store and DWH concepts is needed.
  • Exposure to Bigdata platforms e.g. Cloudera / Data bricks etc will be added advantage.
  • Experience in CI/CD pipeline and exposure to DevOps methodologies will be considered as added advantage.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in German and English with the ability to interact with business and technical IT management teams.


Dedication, team and communication skills, flexibility as well as competent handling of MS Office applications round out your profile. There are numerous good reasons to work for us: responsibility at an early stage, attractive social benefits, an international working environment and a broad variety of career opportunities. Applications from disabled persons are welcome.

Are you interested in working with a pleasant and very dedicated team? Convince us with an appealing application. Please use our online application portal.
