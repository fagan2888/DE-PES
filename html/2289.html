    Corporate IT Recruiter at JSC "Lithuanian Railways"

    • We are looking for a junior ETL Developer / Data Warehouse Developer / Data Engineer to join our Analytics group within LG Digital, our IT centre. The Analytics group is at the core of JSC „Lithuanian Railways“ digital transformation journey. You’ll be given an early responsibility to shape and drive the design and development of our business intelligence systems and landscape. You’ll be at the front of truly complex projects, which will impact and add value to multiple parts of our business while utilising the newest technologies and best practices.


    We are in the process of modernising our data management and analytics capabilities (cloud architecture, big data and IoT capabilities, advanced analytics) and most of the project work will be in these areas. If you join early – you’ll play an important part in the design and delivery of the new solutions.


    If you have experience in business intelligence, love data and data warehousing and feel comfortable with designing and building new solutions – we want to hear from you!


    Key responsibilities will include


    • Supporting and improving the existing ETL processes and Data Warehouses, modelling and building new data flows to meet business information needs
    • Participating in all phases of business intelligence projects and supporting the existing business as usual activities
    • Ensuring quality assurance and data security best practices across business intelligence systems


    Requirements


    • ETL development and data warehousing experience. Advantage, if you have experience working with different database / DWH types and data integration tools
    • Preferred experience with MS SQL Server and SSIS and / or SAP BO DS
    • Good SQL knowledge and good understanding of data structures, relations, data flows and database modelling and design
    • Experience in working and contributing to project work and tasks
    • Professional approach to using project management and issue tracking tools
    • Good understating of dev/test/prod lifecycle, testing and data quality assurance practices


    We offer


    • Join an inspiring and competent team in one of the biggest companies in Lithuania
    • Exciting and challenging work that leads to further professional and career development
    • A dynamic work environment during a period of big organizational change
    • A chance to work on truly complex projects that will enhance your technical business intelligence skills
    • Working on new solutions that add value to the company and you
    • Participation on various projects with opportunities for self-expression
    • A strong leadership where showing initiative and creativity is highly encouraged and valued
    • An opportunity to travel with company’s trains free of charge in the territory of Lithuania
    • Additional health insurance
    • Gross salary starting at 1895 EUR/month (salary offer depends on candidate‘s professional experience, knowledge and competencies)
