    • This position is the critical link between Operations, the R&D and project teams

    You will be based in the client's office in Luxembourg and will use sound chemical and process engineering principles and techniques

    This position will offer you an autonomous job of high versatility which might requires travelling


    Your Responsibilities

    * Manage product troubleshooting, standardisation, improvement
    * Implement and maintain product standards for existing products, using standardised operating procedures and statistical process control
    * Assist in new process integration and standardisation
    * Work with R&D and Engineering to integrate new processes and technologies
    * Interact with other team members and department to share best practices on process and equipment upgrades and modifications
    * Some travel required


    Your Profile

    * University degree in Chemical Engineering (Verfahrenstechnik, Génie chimique)
    * Minimum 5 years' experience in process engineering or R&D in the chemical manufacturing industries are preferred
    * Exposure to CO2 capture processes
    * English and German and/or French fluency mandatory
    * Ready to adapt rapidly in an international and multicultural team
    * Ability to travel up to 30%
    * Knowledge of Aspen
    * Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
