Position Highlights


The Data Science team is part of ICBC’s Strategic Analytics department which has responsibility for predictive analytics, program evaluation and measurement, as well as contributing to continuous improvement in data driven decision making. We currently have an opportunity for an experienced Data Scientist. As a member of the team, you will leverage your skills to improve strategic and tactical decision-making and to support the achievement of corporate priorities.


The data science team partners with its internal customers to develop data solutions tailored to their needs and objectives. As a Senior Data Scientist, you will be responsible for:

  • Engaging with business areas to identify challenges and opportunities
  • Gaining deep understanding of relevant business processes as to define clear objectives
  • Obtaining buy-in around findings and proposed solutions
  • Designing and deploying high quality, peer reviewed and reproducible data products
  • Supervising and coaching junior members of the team
  • Contributing to the team’s continuous improvement efforts.


A key focus of the data science team is helping the Claims division handle insurance claims as efficiently and as fairly as possible. This process being complex, it creates a very challenging environment for data scientists. However, it also means that being successful in this role contributes to making ICBC the best it can be for its customers and for the population on British Columbia.

Position Requirements


You have demonstrated leadership and creativity in defining, tackling and deploying solutions to complex business problems. You are known for being a team player and your excellent analytical skills and critical thinking is complemented by effective communication skills.


You have obtained your Master’s Degree or PhD in a relevant field that may include computer science, economics, or statistics and have many years of related professional experience. Strong knowledge of data extraction, exploration and analysis tools such as Python, Spark, R and SQL is also required.

Only candidates legally entitled to work in Canada at present will be considered for this position.
