Senior Software Development Engineer (SSDE) - Fullstack JavaScript, up to £85,000 +benefits (including £5k guaranteed cash allowance), Central London


A well-known and well-loved UK retailer; my client has been clothing and feeding the nation for the past century. They’re looking for a Senior Software Development Engineer - Fullstack Node.


You’ll be working within a newly formed team with exciting objectives ahead of them.  This role would focus on Fullstack Node - strong vanilla Javascript skills and ability to render NodeJS on the server. To give you a feel for the wider tech ecosystem, existing assets have been built using; Java, Spring, Mongo, JavaScript, HTML, Node and Express. CI/CD is performed via Concourse to a Docker/Kubernetes/Azure environment. The team are pushing for a DevOps culture, investing in automated testing, agile methodologies, and continuous improvement.


What do you consider important when executing and delivering code?


  • The team are passionate about code quality, reusability, performance, testability, and how the applications they’re helping to construct are built, packaged and deployed.


What traits would set you apart as a Senior Software Development Engineer within the team?


  • Real technical and backlog ownership without the need of significant feedback from your peers.
  • Pragmatism. Maintaining a balance between perfection and business needs. A strong desire to produce quality software that's product led, built for the long term, enterprise ready and capable of scaling.
  • A willingness to interact with team members and stakeholders. To get in front of people to work out kinks, champion your ideas and promote cross team collaboration.
  • You’ll be at the point in your career journey where you can invest in progressing others. Upholding clean, quality code through strong engagement in code reviews and pair programming.
  • Strong software design. You’ll be expected to know a wide array of software design patterns in depth, display a strong understanding of algorithms and data structures, and able to show multiple real-life applications of this.


Essential skills needed for the Senior Software Development Engineer:

  • Expert level JavaScript, HTML & CSS skills
  • Strong understanding in responsive design
  • Node, Express
  • Experience with testing frameworks and libraries (Mocha, Chai, Webdriver.io)
  • Working within Agile & CI/CD environments. Proficiency with TDD & BDD
  • Ability to mentor and provide constructive feedback to members of the team. Experience with pair programming
  • Git
  • Understanding of Accessibility best practices
  • Experience with using templating engines (Handlebars)
  • Experience of cross browser support and supporting older devices

Desirable skills:

  • Experience with PWA
  • Prior experience within Azure environment
  • Good SEO knowledge

For more information on the Senior Software Development Engineer role contact: pearl@digitalgurus.co.uk
