Major Investment Bank. Join a global Data Management team and help implement business critical data projects.

Job Title: Data Engineer
Salary: 10M- 13M + Bonus
Location: Tokyo, Chyoda-ku
Ref: 1071197

Job Description.
The Data Management Technology team is responsible for supporting the Data Management Office by providing technology services enhancing the firm's capabilities across the data management framework including discovery data, discovery, governance, cleanup, and access. Based in Tokyo, this position will be part of a globally distributed team and is responsible for the development, integration and support of third party data management applications.

Responsibilities:
Work with the global development team to implement business critical projects.
'Hands-On' coding and development of solutions to meet the goals of the Data Management Office.
Partner with the Data Management Office members to understand and meet their application needs.
Work closely with other teams across IT to implement the strategic Data Management Technology platform.

Required Skills & Experience.
Business level Japanese & English.
Exp of working in a global team environment.
Exp of data discovery - creating processes to automatically extract, transform, and load data from key data sources.
Exp of designing and optimizing the Audit Data Warehouse and data pipelines.
A team player with strong Japanese and English communication skills, verbal and written.
Proven track record technical solutions in a global environment
2yrs experience of Installation, Setup & Configuration of 3rd party vendor applications.
Fluent in writing complex SQL (SQL Server, Oracle or similar).
Exp of creating advanced scripts for automating various operating System / Software Functions (Unix, Windows)

Desirable Skills:
Data Modeling Principles / Methods including Conceptual, Logical & Physical Data Models desirable.
ETL, Python development, BI tools (Tableau, Power BI etc.) Java, SQL Tuning, Restful API.
Experience with DevOps tools (Git, Jenkins, Docker, Splunk) is desirable.
Experience working in a Financial Services Organization desirable Understanding of financial products and capital markets.

For full job details please reply attaching your CV in word format to nicholas.klimcke@hays.co.jp or call me on 03 3560 2624
