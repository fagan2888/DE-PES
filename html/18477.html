    • Executive Placements has an immediate need for a Lead Software Engineer for a new hands-on development role with the responsibility to lead the work of others in Walnut Creek, California . This is an incredible Direct Hire opportunity with a fantastic company and the ability to work remote.

    Ideal Lead Software Engineer Experience:

    • Designs and develops new software or makes modifications to existing complex software applications in accordance with written specifications.
    • Develops software using disciplined processes, adhering to industry standards and best practices, including complete ease with Agile development methods throughout the SDLC.
    • Participates in Agile sprint planning sessions, working closely with business analysts and team members to analyze requirements, and provides design recommendations for complex systems.
    • Leads and mentors Junior developers.
    • Takes part in reviews of work, (e.g. design and code reviews)
    • Works with technical staff to analyze and understand problems with complex software systems and resolves them.
    • 10+ years of experience in a Software Engineering environment with a demonstrated technical knowledge. One year supervisory or leadership experience in Software Engineering required. Bilingual skills preferred.

    Lead Software Engineer Required Skills, Knowledge and/or Abilities:

    • Expertise with Source code control systems and multiple simultaneous version/release.
    • Knowledge of web services (ASMX and WCF.)
    • Knowledge of Test-Driven Development and Strong Knowledge and Experience of/with C#, .Net Framework, asp.net and MVC.
    • Strong knowledge of OOAD and multi-tier architectures; including MVC / MVP / MVVM design patterns.
    • Strong knowledge of web services, WCF, REST and SOA Architecture.
    • Knowledge of MS Entity Framework.
    • Expertise with tools such as MS SQL Studio, PL/SQL Developer / Toad, etc.
    • Strong knowledge of MVG GUI design including the ability to incorporate jQuery, JavaScript, CSS, plus expertise with AJAX/SPA.
    • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills. Strong analytical problem solving and planning skills. Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment. Team Play with an Enthusiastic attitude and the drive to continually learn and improve.
