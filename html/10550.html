    General Manager at The M Agency

    • The M Agency and Live Nation Australia are seeking an experienced CRM & Data Specialist to join their growing team, based in Melbourne. This is an exciting new position for a talented individual with a passion for both music and data!


    The CRM & Data Specialist is responsible for leading and implementing the CRM strategy for Live Nation Australia. They will oversee the day-to-day running of the account and ensure a high-quality product and service is delivered at all times.


    Role Overview

    • Ownership & execution of our CRM strategy.
    • Strong ability to collect, structure, maintain and analyse data to identify areas of opportunity.
    • Audience segmentation based on ticket buyer data – such as artist affinity, genre, demographic, geography & customer purchase behaviour.
    • Managing content & creative, and utilising optimisation tactics to make recommendations on output.
    • Ability to learn and understand the flow of data within the Live Nation ecosystem.
    • Managing relationships with internal stakeholders and business partners - such as Ticketmaster Product Management team.
    • Understanding of journey mapping, campaign workflow, personalization, and the delivery of content to maximize conversation and engagement.
    • Digital innovation and new ideas as applied to the nuances of the role.
    • Integration of CRM marketing tactics with other owned and operated channels.
    • Understanding of A/B testing and reporting.
