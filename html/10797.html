Developer Technical Software

Do you have a passion for the high-tech industry? We do as well. A more dynamic field does not exist. Technological innovation is the future! If it would be up to us: your future! Being employed with ENTER you can work on challenging Research & Development projects with a variety of high-tech companies in Brainport Eindhoven.

Job info

As Developer Technical Software, you are responsible for development of software that is applied in complex and technically advanced products. You are typically part of a scrum team that has a lot of autonomy in organizing its development activities. It also offers a great environment for juniors for growth.

Activities Include
  • Work with other developers on designing solid solutios
  • Produce (and test) clean, efficient code compliant with applicable quality standards
  • Integrate software components and third-party software
  • Setup and/or improve automated build, test, and deployment pipelines
  • Verify and deploy programs and systems
  • Troubleshoot, debug, improve, and upgrade existing software
  • Create technical documentation for reference and reporting
  • Participating in the scrum ceremonies: sprint planning, review, retrospective, and daily standups.

Job Requirements

We are looking for a Developer Technical Software with a passion for technology and who is not afraid to analyse complex problems and build innovative solutions.

What We Expect From You
  • Driven by quality and teamwork.
  • Agile mindset and someone who values software craftsmanship
  • Analytical, result-oriented, creative and a good communicator
  • Experience in implementing technical software in an object oriented and/or functional language.
  • Active in technical communities (e.g. stack overflow, contributor to open source projects, blogger, etc)
  • 2 - 5 years experience in development of technical software.
  • Bachelor or Master degree in computer science, electronics, or comparable technical field.
  • Good understanding of English language is required as well as Dutch or willingness to learn Dutch.

Organisation

BU Embedded provides software craftsmanship in product development. We apply our knowlege and experience in software engineering to develop high tech products and innovations. Our customers areproduct development companies that operate in high tech machine building, medical systems, consumer electronics, automotive, transport & handling systems, oil & gas, and maritime systems.

ENTER employs experienced software architects as well as junior software engineers. All of them with a passion for technology, innovation,and software engineering!. Our employees have a Bachelor, Master, or PhD degree and work in a variety of roles, such as as developer, tester, architect, scrum master, integrator, and build & configuration manager.

We are looking for talents in the field of software engineering. It is essential that our employees have passion for their profession and possess the right technical, process, social knowledge and skills. Do you recognize yourself in this profile? Then you’re possible our new colleague!

Offer

ENTER offers an inspiring international work environment in which you can develop yourself by doing challenging projects and following dedicated training courses.
