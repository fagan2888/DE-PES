    • Why YOU want this position


    Enverus delivers business-critical insights to the global energy industry through a state-of-the-art SaaS platform built on industry-leading data and energy analytics. Our solutions deliver value across the entire energy value chain, empowering customers to be more agile, efficient and competitive. The range of energy industry participants we serve includes exploration and production (E&P) companies and related businesses such as oilfield services, midstream, capital markets, power generators and utilities, energy traders, and downstream commercial & industrial energy consumers.

    We are currently seeking a highly driven Data Engineer to join our team in Austin, TX. You will have the opportunity to shape the future of Enverus by building and maintaining business-critical customer-facing applications and helping deliver the next generation of Oil and Gas information retrieval and analytics.

    Performance Objectives:

    • Responsible for creating and maintaining the pipelines to assemble different datasets, all this following an architecture defined by the company.
    • Write maintainable code, and work in a team driven environment (source control, shortened release cycles, continuous integration/deployment, documentation, agile development).

    Competitive Candidate Profile

    • 5+ years of experience building, maintaining and delivering data-based products
    • Advanced Level with Programing Languages: GO, Python
    • Strong familiarity with database languages, SQL Server, SSIS
    • Strong familiarity with version control systems: Git, SVN
    • Strong familiarity with agile development
    • Excellent analytical and critical thinking skills, should show initiative and a positive attitude.
    • Experience with developing object-oriented, enterprise-sized systems
    • Desirable experience with Programing Languages: JAVA, Python
    • Desirable experience with: Airflow, AWS, Kafka, Docker
    • Desirable experience with e very layer of an Enterprise system (from Database to UX).
    • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or similar
