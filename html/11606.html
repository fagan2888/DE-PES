Technical Solutions Engineer

Yieldify London, GB

1 week ago

Apply on company website
Yieldify is a customer journey optimization company that brings personalization to the full customer journey. It combines award-winning software with a proven CJO methodology to deliver measurable results quickly and easily. To date, Yieldify has influenced over 100 million sales through more than 200,000 journeys. It currently delivers customer journey optimization for over 1,000 leading e-commerce websites, including Domino's Pizza, L'Oreal and Megabus.

The Solutions Engineering team executes various development projects, technical problem solving and consultative duties for internal departments and clients. The main focus of the Technical Solutions team is to meet the requirements of our clients and execute their requests with the Yieldify platform. This allows us to work with some of the largest brands and advertisers in the world, solving some interesting technical and business problems.

Our Solutions Engineers supplement our internal teams in driving new innovation. Whether it's optimising existing setups through rapid iterations and providing a holistic and in depth view of the product or creating alpha/beta versions of a new product idea. This combination allows Yieldify to offer a unique portfolio of products through the Technical Solutions team that will ultimately be a key driver for business change.

You'll Love This Role If
  • You're adaptable and thrive in fast-paced environments.
  • You love variety and enjoy solving technical challenges.
  • You're as cool as a cucumber when dealing with deadlines.
  • You like working with different types of people - from fellow techies to clients.
  • You want to see the difference you make to the end user, firsthand.


In This Role You Will
  • Help design supportable, scalable technology solutions (with TypeScript) based on your evaluation of technical requirements supplied by our clients.
  • Driving innovation by developing bespoke new campaign formats with React.js.
  • Providing in depth knowledge and guidance of the Yieldify platform to inform technical decision making and project planning.
  • Bug spotting, bug fixing and effectively coordinating between Engineering and commercial teams.
  • Diving into the technical setup of a campaign that is not functioning correctly. Providing a scalable fix where necessary.
  • Support local and external teams in their day to day technical troubleshooting and requests. This could be via email, client calls or in person.
  • Interact with a variety of stakeholders such as; Project Managers, Account Managers, Clients, Product and Engineering.
  • Contribute to improving internal tools and business processes, improving productivity and building infrastructure for on-boarding and ongoing support.


We're Looking For Someone With
  • Some familiarity with web languages such as JavaScript, HTML and/or CSS.
  • Bachelor degree of 2:1 or higher (technical speciality preferable) or another form of programming training.
  • Must be organised, detail-oriented, enthusiastic, resourceful and capable of envisioning innovative solutions in a complex environment.
  • Ability to convey technical concepts in a digestible manner.


Bonus
  • Knowledge of TypeScript, NodeJS, React.js, Regex, Browser Dev Tools, Web APIs and JS Frameworks.
  • Strong knowledge of JavaScript.
  • 1-2 years of commercial experience would be ideal but not necessary.


What will you get from us?

Guidance - we'll give you all the training and tools you'll need in order to become successful here from your first day with us.

Autonomy - after you've got to grips with how things work here, we'll encourage you to become a trusted member of the team flying solo on lots of smaller projects and we'll support you as you build yourself up to take on projects for our prestigious larger customers.

Education - we champion self-learners who are dedicated to their own growth and development. We'll encourage you to share your knowledge with the team and support you as you expand your mind and skills; we provide regular tech talks and learning opportunities, as well as tuition credit and discounts on General Assembly courses and Sunlight - our personal development portal.

Diversity - we're proud to work with such a great mix of people. Everyone applying for a role at Yieldify will be considered regardless of race, colour, religion, sex, national origin, sexual orientation, age, disability, marital status or gender identity.

Lifestyle - competitive salaries and a great work-life balance - we enjoy 25 days holiday per year, flexible hours and can dedicate time to charities or community projects.

Perks - include Cyclescheme access and bike storage in our brand new company headquarters! We're based in Aldgate - a stone's throw from Shoreditch and the City - and regularly host events and parties! We have fresh fruit, a great tea & coffee selection and a beer fridge. You can enjoy a beverage or take a meeting up on our rooftop with sprawling views of London and even enjoy some mini golf up there too!
