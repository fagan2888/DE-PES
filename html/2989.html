The Data Product Engineering team is a subdivision of Strategy and Biz Ops, TW International Consumer Growth Marketing Org., which powers various businesses at Verizon Media through big data technologies and AI solutions. Our mission is to create value out of audience, transaction and advertising data and build data products that deliver the most valuable information user need.

A Lot About You

As an AI Backend / Data Engineer, you have tracked records building large scale services that deliver AI/ML intelligence to improve user’s digital experience.You’ve developed data pipelines that handle vast amounts of data which have high throughput and guaranteed message delivery semantics. You process data in an efficient way by knowing how distributed system works. You’re self-driven and passionate about building software that is highly efficient and reliable. You’re excited to face large amount of traffic and tackle the challenges through elegant solutions. You are able to provide insightful thinking when designing systems and doing code reviews. Last but not least, you’re a team player who can fit into an open and transparent culture we have.

Your Day

・Participate in the development of data products which consist of system design, coding, testing, code review and continuous delivery.

・Be able to dive deep into implementation details and provide solutions with good design and code quality.

・Build tools and systems that enable efficient AI/ML development and processing.

・Handle large amount of data by leveraging suitable technologies and tools.

・Solve various technical problems with your problem solving skills.

・Keep learning emerging technologies and share the knowledge you have to the team to improve team skills.

・Mentor/coach junior engineers and make them become you.

You Must Have

・At least 3 years of experience as backend / data engineer.

・Proficient in one of the programming languages: Java, Scala.

・Experience in either scripting/untyped languages: Shell, Python.

・Knowledge of software engineering and system/data architecture.

・Experience in working with Big Data related technologies, such as Hadoop, Hive, Pig, HBase, Spark, Flink, Kafka, Elasticsearch, Solr, MongoDB, Presto, etc.

・Experience in working with ML/DL related technologies, such as Tensorflow, Keras, PyTorch is a plus

・Open Source Contribution Experience Is a Plus.
