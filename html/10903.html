Description

Are you customer obsessed, flexible, smart and analytical, strategic yet execution focused, hungry and passionate about e-commerce, experienced, and entrepreneurial leader with a strong work ethic? If yes, this opportunity will appeal to you.

Amazon.com strives to be Earth's most customer-centric company where people can find and discover virtually anything they want to buy online. By giving customers more of what they want - low prices, vast selection, and convenience - Amazon.com continues to grow and evolve as a world-class supply chain and logistics operation. Global Specialty Fulfillment (GSF) is an organization that focuses in ultra-fast delivery, we deliver in one or two hours to customers in France, Germany, Italy, Spain and the UK. We operate in large metropolitan complex areas such as London, Paris, Madrid, Berlin and Milan.

You should thrive and have demonstrated success in an environment which offers ambiguously defined problems, big challenges, and quick changes. You will be expected to balance detailed execution with speed and possess solid collaborative skills. You will be working in a fast-paced environment where every day brings new challenges and new opportunities to innovate. You should have excellent business and communication skills and be able to work with business owners to develop and define goals and metrics. The top candidate naturally dives deep to drive business through data insights and is passionate about socializing business impacts to guide the broader organization.

Skills

You will also have the opportunity to display your skills in the following areas

The Sr. Data Scientist will help us create and extract meaning from large volumes of data to develop delivery models that allows us to scale and make the right business decisions while balancing accuracy and risk. The candidate will have to set the right vision, strategy and roadmap and work alongside with several stakeholders in the organization to make it happen. The candidate knows and loves working with business intelligence tools, can model multidimensional datasets, and can partner with business leaders to answer key business questions. You work with high-end technology that Amazon constantly develops with modern BI world (AWS, Redshift, S3). You are analytical and creative, and you don’t quit. You are true visionary in BI world that want to have meaningful impact on ultra-fast delivery and create direct impact on millions of customers.
  • Participate in strategic & tactical planning discussions.
  • Optimize last mile delivery across cities like London, Paris, Berlin with data models that you will design and create from processing millions rows of data.
  • Define the long term delivery strategy by contrasting different models and simulations with large datasets.
  • Build solutions and the strategy that will support GSF that deliver products to customers within 2 hours across 5 countries.
  • Define proxy information, heuristics and algorithms for data that it is unknown today.
  • Simplify and reduce the manual work by automating analysis through scripts and tools such as python, R and SQL.
  • Gather and analyze information from live operations to identify opportunities to shape the delivery strategy and leverage those opportunities in the short term.
  • Define parameters and success criteria for live tests to confirm/deny hypothesis in models.
  • Design, implement, and support platforms that provide ad-hoc access to large datasets.
  • You will focus on wide range of aspects of modern Business Intelligence from Data Engineering, Data Visualizations to Advanced Data Science.
  • Be connected and influential within the Amazon scientists’ community.
Produce research and papers that will be discussed with Directors and VPs.


Basic Qualifications
  • Masters in Data Science, Economics, Finance, Engineering, Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics or other business/analytical discipline.
  • 5+ years of relevant work experience in a business analyst/data analyst/statistical analysis role.
  • Data modelling and definition of algorithms with software, scripts and/or techniques (Excel, Python, R, Machine Learning, regression, linear programming, Design of Experiments, etc).
  • Advanced Microsoft Office skills, particularly Excel and analytical programs.
  • Advanced SQL skills (Redshift preferred).
  • Familiarity with BI tools such as QuickSight, Tableau.
Preferred Qualifications
  • PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, Industrial Engineering, Mathematics, Economics.
  • Demonstrable experience in dimensional modelling and Data Warehouse tuning.
  • Demonstrable knowledge of statistical methodologies and algorithmic design.
  • Experience working with datasets related to geospatial information (postcodes, area, density).
Experience in supply chain, network modelling and/or operations research is strongly preferred.
