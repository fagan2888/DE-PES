Data Scientist

Wize Solutions Sofia, Sofia City Province, Bulgaria

4 weeks ago 31 applicants

Apply on company website

Are you passionate about data? Would you like the opportunity to work for an international organisation driving the Data Science area? If so Wize Solutions are currently recruiting for a Data Scientist to analyse our data and provide predictive insights both internally and to our clients.


Duties and responsibilities

  • Set the data science vision and strategy and drive this in the organisation together with the management team
  • Lead and inspire all data science initiatives throughout the company
  • Perform hands on data analysis and modelling with huge data sets
  • Apply data mining, NLP, and machine learning to understand correlations and hence improve the performance of the business operations
  • Work with and alongside stakeholders to model and frame business scenarios that impact critical business processes and/or decisions
  • Design and develop innovative and effective approaches to solve analytic problems
  • Discover data sources, get access to them, import them, clean them up, and make them “machine learning ready”
  • Create and refine features from the underlying data. You’ll enjoy and have intuition about what features might make your model perform better
  • Collaborate and communicate with management, internal, and external partners regarding analysis results, methodologies, project progress and issue resolution
  • Explore new design or technology shifts in order to determine how they might connect with the customer benefits we wish to deliver.

Skills and competencies

  • MS, or PhD in an appropriate field (Computer Science, Statistics, Physics, Applied Math, Operations Research, etc.)
  • Extensive background in data science with proven experience in delivering real commercial value, through the use of data science techniques
  • Expertise in modern advanced analytical tools and programming languages such as Python, Scala, Java and/or R, SQL, Hive, Spark, Hadoop etc.
  • Expertise in data mining algorithms and statistical modelling techniques such as clustering, classification, regression, decision trees, control charts, forecasting, ANOVA etc.
  • Solid communication skills -- demonstrated ability to explain complex technical issues to both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Ability to create build and lead a talented technical team to push a data-driven culture within the company
