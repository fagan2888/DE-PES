QA Engineer

RFG Holding Limassol, Limassol (Lemesos), Cyprus

2 weeks ago

Apply on company website

A european Forex broker is looking to add an employee to its Team to work as a QA Engineer in Limassol (Cyprus).


Job description:

  • Manual QA of web/ios/android, autotest support.
  • Working with bug tracking system (JIRA), creation and prioritization of bugs.
  • Working with testRail.
  • Creation and maintenance of documentation on iteration, regression, and computer-aided testing (test cases, checklists, etc.).
  • Creation and population of acceptance checklists.
  • Performance and update of autotests.
  • Analysis of application log files.
  • Analysis of user data from database application.


Requirements:

  • Previous employment as a QA Engineer 1-3 years.
  • Knowledge of QA pipeline, understanding of the way features make from design to release and testing.
  • Experience in JIRA, testRail, or similar.
  • Understanding of SQL queries, creating elementary scripts for collecting information.
  • Knowledge of Unix console at the level of application log files analysis.
  • Understanding of programming fundamentals, experience in using JS.

The following knowledge is welcome:

  • Experience in software development for financial industry.
  • Experience in computer-aided testing.


Conditions:

  • Work schedule: 40 hours a week, from 9.00 AM to 6.00 PM.
  • Comfortable workplace in the city center (Limassol).
  • Official employment.
  • Employment visa processing, if necessary.
  • Due payment of salary.
  • Salary: according to the interview results, probation period.
