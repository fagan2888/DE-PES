    Our client is a global private markets investment group with more than 10 offices worldwide. A strong advocate of long term and responsible practices and ownership, they seek to hire a Data Engineer Professional to be based in Singapore.


    Your responsibilities-


    - Development and enhancement of analytics-related services using Python, SQL, Java and other technologies.

    - Developing leading POCs to create breakthrough solutions, performing exploratory and targeted data analyses and support dashboard development and maintenance using data visualization software.

    - Implement new business requirements in the core systems.

    - Fulfill ad-hoc data reporting requests.


    A good university degree with an emphasis in IT with at least 7 years of experience in Quantitative Engineering/Development capacity preferably in the Private Equity Domain. Strong programming skills (C++, Java, Python and etc). Good working experience in Agile Development Methodologies, particularly in TDD.


    Above all, you must have excellent communication and interpersonal skills. Ability to engage at all levels, multi-task and to work under pressure. You are highly motivated, energetic and enthusiastic with the gravitas and confidence to work within a fast-moving environment and deal with your internal and external stakeholders.


    If you fit the above requirements, I would love to hear from you. I'm afraid only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.


    Please email your cv to sivam.kumar@talentinsight.com.sg
