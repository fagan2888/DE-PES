Description

We are member of French ALTEN Group present in 25 countries around the world and employing over 34,000 engineers and IT specialists. Since 1988 we deliver advanced IT systems for well-known brands, develop medicine and the renewable energy industry. ALTEN innovates aero and cosmonautics, trains, electric and autonomous vehicles, and even space rockets. We are currently looking for a Big Data Engineer who will join our team.

Main tasks

Engagement with SMEs (Subject Matter Experts) and efficient communication with stakeholders

Facilitate and co-ordinate all responses to requests from clients, ensuring responses are complete and are consistent globally

Analyze and propose solutions based on business requirements

Working closely with Chief Data Officers, IT Architects, Big Data engineers and Subject Matter Experts to maintain and improve the existing data structure and build the next generation data models/warehouses

Requirements

University graduate (preferable in IT) followed by at least 5+ years of professional experience

Good knowledge of Hadoop environment
(Data engineering, data architecture, data
transformation, automation, HDFS, YARN jobs, Spark/Tez/MapReduce, etc.)

Good knowledge of Big Data querying tools, such as Pig, Hive, and Impala

Experience with integration of data from multiple data sources

Experience with NoSQL databases, such as HBase, MongoDB, CouchBase, etc.

Experience with Python, Java, or Scala

Experience with BI Tools

Experience with automation tools in Big Data

Knowledge of various ETL techniques and frameworks

Experience with various messaging systems, such as Kafka

Experience with data analysis (data lineage, metadata, data quality and governance) and Data Modeling activities (conceptual, logical and physical)

Proficiency in English

Optional Skills

Prior working experience in a bank or a financial institution

We offer

Stable and long-term cooperation

Certification and training opportunities

Possibility to choose type of contract (B2B also)

Benefit package (MultiSport card, Medicover, MyBenefit)

Unlimited growth and development opportunity

Relocation support & relocation package

Do not hesitate and join our team!
