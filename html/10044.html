    Platform Engineer

    AMD is looking for Platform Engineer to develop our long-term platform vision, which will have significant impact for AMD, and the future of our products.

    In this role, you will lead the definition of forward-looking projects and novel system concepts to significantly impact AMD’s future product portfolio. You will be responsible for driving the development of key technologies in different operating systems including, but not limited to, Linux, QNX, and custom OS. Your team will also help define AMD’s hardware and software architectures with insights gained from the development of advanced technologies.



    AMD Advanced Technology Group is an entrepreneurial research and development team to build AMD's future advanced platforms & products. Our teams work closely with outside customers and internal teams to develop hardware, software and systems solutions into next generation computing platforms. As part of AMD Advanced Technology Group, you will have the opportunity develop a winning team that will collaborate with internal teams & customers, explore new platform technologies, and lead the development of best-in-class hardware, software and systems technologies that our customers will use for real-world problems.



    You Will Have The Opportunity To
    • Lead AMD's display technologies for current and future generations products
    • Develop software and platforms solutions that address the evolving needs of AMD’s customers
    • Drive research and development, proofs of concept, technology transfer, and customer interactions that lead to the development of AMD best-in-class machine intelligence platforms
    • Collaborate with research, engineering, and other AMD teams to drive platforms solutions for machine intelligence applications.



    The Best Applicant Will Have
    • 15+ industry experiences in working with display technologies
    • Experience developing for Android
    • Expertise with RTOS systems design & architectures
    • Expertise with various display systems hardware & software research & development
    • Experience with machine learning frameworks, for example, TensorFlow or Theano
    • Large-scale machine learning software experience
    • Expertise with boot loaders, BIOS, BSP for various HW platforms
    • Master or equivalent experience in a field associated with real time operating system development as applied mathematics, statistics, or computer science.
