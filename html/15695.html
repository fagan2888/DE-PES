Data Engineer

Engineer.ai - What would you Build? London, GB

1 week ago

Job DescriptionWere looking for intellectually curious, humble, ambitious and razor-sharp data engineers with varying levels of industry experience. You are someone who is passionate about technology generally, but more specifically, you are experienced at building machine learning data pipelines that ingest data, run and productionise models in a cloud based setting at scale. You are someone who has a keen interest in machine learning and artificial intelligence technologies more generally and have cut your teeth at powering products build upon such technologies. We embrace those who see things differently, arent afraid to experiment, and who have a healthy disregard for constraints. The ideal candidate is someone who is a master of their domain and is skilled at taking ambiguous business or product requirements, translating them into hypotheses, finding the right solution, prototyping ideas and productionising work. You are fundamentally motivated by wanting to drive significant business impact through the application of their knowledge and skills. Furthermore, you are able to inspire your colleagues and champion your skills through influence and effective communication. Why We Need This Role You will be a part of the Intelligent Systems based in London but will work closely and collaborate with global product and engineering teams across 3 locations: London, New Delhi and Los Angeles. The Intelligent Systems team will drive all of the innovation powered by data science, machine learning and AI (decision making). It is likely to witness significant growth over the course of the next year and beyond. The key responsibilities of our data engineer will be:
  • Build, maintain and manage data pipelines that support the modelling initiatives of data scientists
  • Work with wider engineering teams to make the right data available for model building and analysis
  • Choose and use the right analytical libraries, programming languages, and frameworks
  • Leverage best practices and open source frameworks to build cloud based development environments such as JupyterLab
  • Set the standards for software engineering practices within the team
  • Actively support Product Owners and fill their backlog
  • Engineer at scale using service-oriented architecture, containerized applications and functions as a service, especially in cloud service environments Why You Should Join This is a challenging and diverse role that will require you to be a part of the growth of the Intelligent Systems department from the ground up. The problems we face are unique, with some of them still in early stages of sustained academic research. Furthermore, this is an opportunity to help grow our suite of products that in conjunction are aiming to automate the entire software development lifecycle. Requirements Essential skills
  • Post-graduate in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Physics or a related subject
  • Talented programmer with expert knowledge of developing ETL pipelines and production ML solutions
  • Knowledge of at least one scripting language i.e. Python
  • Experience working in a distributed processing environment such as Spark, Dask
  • Significant experience in software development practices such as; testing, paired programming
  • Experience in cloud computing methodologies such as orchestrating, managing and scaling data pipelines and building and deploying containerised applications
  • Significant experience in database manipulation using SQL across multiple databases and data warehouses Desired skills
  • Ability to communicate with diverse stakeholders
  • An entrepreneurial and a can do attitude
  • Previous experience in a consumer, product or an eCommerce business would be beneficial
  • Industry recognition
  • Leadership and team management Benefits
  • Performance-based bonuses and stock options
  • Generous vacation and time off benefits including paid holidays
  • Generally flexible working hours
  • Working in a beautiful office space in London
  • A collaborative culture where unconventional thinkers come together to solve interesting problems and having fun while doing it
  • Mission-driven company that is making the world a better place
