What you will do?

You will be working mostly on automated and manual web testing which includes but not limited to:
  • Work with Google Analytics data and internal tools, looking at real Customer Cases.
  • Development and troubleshooting of Automation Tests.
  • Create test scenarious based on Customers data.
  • Support of integration and other QES organization projects.

About The Team

As an SAP Concur iXP Intern - QA Engineer you will to join our Quality Engineering Services organization, especially the Suite Integration team who is working with solutions to provide a high quality product to our Customers.

Who You Are
  • Pursuing Bachelor or Master Degree in Computer Science or a related field.
  • Basic understanding of software testing principles and methodology.
  • Analytical Skills
  • Knowledge of Java, or other OOP language.
  • Interests for software testing and especially in test automation.
  • Energetic and flexible team player willing to work in dynamic environment
  • Fluent business English in written and verbal.
