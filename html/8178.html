Huntress

IT Infrastructure Engineer
Location Ashford
Salary to £35,000

As the IT Infrastructure Engineer, you will work as part of a team, providing largely 2nd/3rd line IT support to the company's end-users, predominantly on a face-to-face basis.

You and your colleagues will report into a highly innovative IT Manager, and also get the chance to overlap on key projects. It's not just a BAU support role; you'll also get the project exposure. The role will be varied in scope, as you will cover everything from server and desktop support, which is mainly Windows-based, through to networking and database responsibilities.

The Successful Applicant

As the successful candidate for the IT Infrastructure Engineer vacancy, you will be experienced in hands-on, end-user support of:

Windows 7, 8 and 10
Windows Server 2016
Office365
Active Directory - DNS, DHCP and Active Directory
Networking - TCP/IP and LAN/WANIt will also be highly beneficial if you have experience in:

Cloud technologies
NAS/SAN Storage systems
