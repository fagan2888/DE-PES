Security Operations Specialist (Web Proxy)

NNIT Prague, CZ

Před 2 dny

Zažádat na webu společnosti
We are looking for a Security Operations Specialist who will join our Security team in Prague, which is a part of our global Infrastructure & Application Services, where various infrastructure and outsourcing services are being delivered. You will be working Monday to Friday within regular business hours. You will be taking over the shift from other NNIT locations (China, Philippines) and working together with approximately 10 security colleagues in the central European time zone.

Your Responsibilities

You will be responsible for keeping service quality, helping with investigation of security incidents, troubleshooting, participating in project and continuous improvements of the NNIT Managed Security Services portfolio (MSS).
  • Daily maintenance of Proxy/Secure Web Gateway platforms
  • Troubleshooting
  • Working on customer service requests
  • Looking for improvements, optimization and implementing changes
  • Helping with investigation of security breaches and malicious activities
  • Possibility to learn and work with other technologies


Your Qualification And Experience
  • You have experience with:
    • Proxies/Secure Web Gateways (Cisco Ironport, Cisco Umbrella, Zscaler Internet Access)
    • Intrusion detection and prevention systems (IDS/IPS)
    • Networking technolo gies (Cisco Switches, Routers & Firewalls)
    • Operations system (Unix/Windows)
    • Vulnerability scanning
  • You are fluent in English both spoken and written
  • You have passion for learning new things


It Is Your Big Advantage
  • If you have experience with:
    • Log management/SIEM (LogPoint, ArcSight, McAfee, Splunk)
    • Antivirus (McAfee, Palo Alto Traps, Symantec)
    • Endpoint encryption (Symantec, PGP)
  • And if you have one of these certificates: CCNA, CCNA – Security, CompTIA Security+, ITIL standards, GDPR, and knowledge of ISO27000 series
