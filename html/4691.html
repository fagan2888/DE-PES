Data Warehouse Engineer

Abbott Ho Chi Minh City, VN

1 month ago

Apply on company website
Working place: 18 Luy Ban Bich, Tan Thoi Hoa Ward, Tan Phu District, HCMC

Working time: Mon - Sat (2 Sat/month)

Key Responsibilities

Build, Deploy and Manage Centralized Data Warehouse and BI Tools
  • Understand business data requirements from various groups of decision makers
  • Map relevant internal and external data sources (DMS, Compiere, Nemo, CRM, AnForce, MI, Talentnet, Acacy, People Link, EC vendors) that can address those business requirements
  • Collaborate with relevant Data Warehouse (DWH) vendors to design and enhance DWH architecture, data models and BI application concepts based on business requirements for all stakeholders to sign off a blueprint
  • Collaborate with DBMS vendors to implement the development of the DWH-BI system blueprint in hardware and software
  • Conduct UAT for the DWH-BI system and get users to sign off
  • Deploy DWH-BI production system to relevant business users
  • Maintain and enhance DWH-BI production system based on changing business requirements and new technology evolutions
Manage and execute ETL Tool and Processes for Data Integration into DWH
  • Leverage ETL tools to extract data from multiple sources, transform it in line with data requirements and load it into the centralized DWH
  • Ensure the stability and continuity of data ETL operation
  • Develop and update data definition handbook and act as a custodian of the data (e.g. dimension and measures) definition updating process for reference by stakeholders to provide clarity and alignment between DWH and various data sources from transaction/operation systems
  • Ensure data accuracy and integrity in DWH as “a single source of truth”
Leverage BI tools and Other Applications to Conduct Data Analytics and Visualization in Dashboards and Reports
  • Understand and make sense of business requirements for information from stakeholders for decision making
  • Leverage BI tools and other applications to design, develop and automate rich and interactive business intelligence dashboards and reports to support and accelerate data-driven decision making
  • Write and conduct data quires in DWH and develop relevant data models in BI applications to meet all requirements for standard and exceptional reports and dashboards
  • Develop business insights, conclusions and recommendations as well as highlight risks, outliers, abnormalities, inconsistencies and inaccuracies based on data analytics to share with relevant stakeholders
  • Act as a subject-matter expert, i.e. the "go-to person" for all issues related to data and BI tools
Develop Advanced Data Analytics Methodologies and Predictive/Simulation Models to Validate Trends
  • Develop and apply data mining, advanced data analytics and predictive/simulation models to identify, validate and forecast trends, correlations as well as cause-effect relationships and make conclusions to stakeholders
Act as a subject-matter expert, i.e. the "go-to person" for all things related to data and BI tool
  • Develop data definition handbook for reference by stakeholders to provide clarity and alignment
  • Provide BI tool training to users to enable them to use these tools effectively
  • Continue research and developments to stay abreast of technological evolutions and trends in business intelligence
Support in Business Intelligence Project Management, Vendor Management and Budget Tracking
  • Support BI Manager in engaging with stakeholders to identify BI initiatives and form BI projects accordingly (with clear scope, project team, budget and timeline for securing stakeholder alignment and management)
Managing execution of BI initiatives and BI projects Work DWH and BI tool vendors to design the most suitable support programs to support the deployment and operation of DWH-BI system Track above vendor performance vs. the level of service commitment as mutually agreed in service contracts via specific KPIs Track the budget allocated for operating and enhancing DWH and BI tool to ensure forecast accuracy

Key Requirement
  • University degree
  • At least 5 to 7-year relevant experience in executive role in FMCG/nutrition/pharma/consulting industry
  • At least 3 to 5-year experience in business intelligence, data analytics and visualization or developing, managing and operating DWH (Oracle, Azure) BI tools (Powder BI and Qlikview)
  • Proficient in data extraction, aggregation, transformation and loading (ETL)
  • Advanced SQL skill at working with relational databases to write complex queries and stored procedures
  • Competent in BI technologies (Power BI and Qlikview)
  • Logical thinking
  • Advanced skill at complex data mining
  • Advanced data analytics
  • Strong business acumen
  • Planning, organizing and alignment and change management
  • Presentation, communication, facilitation and influencing skills
  • Analytical, problem solving & decision-making skills
  • Project management
