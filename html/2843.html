Acoustic Application Development Engineer

Eastman Chemical Company Gent Area, Belgium

4 days ago

Apply on company website

The Acoustic Application Development Engineer role is focused on improving and co-creating acoustical foundation that can be applied for current and future applications. You will be participating and leading the improvement and development efforts of our acoustics capabilities to enable next generation acoustic products, as well as validation of product performance. The Team Leader part of this role is your chance to develop your initial coaching skills.

We are seeking a highly motivated candidate who wants to leverage existing knowledge and experience to quickly pick up new skills and knowledge. This individual should have experience working cross-functionally with an ability to quickly build relationships. This individual should also have a demonstrated history of success leading or participating in complex projects, able to be self-guided and proactive to take initiative.

As the connection between the market and our customers, you will be regularly visiting our key global customers, which will require mainly travelling within EU to 25% of your time.

Responsibilities

  • Actively contributing and/or leading acoustical performance improvement projects

  • Working closely with (new) industry connection to upkeep and acquire new tools as required

  • Development of the acoustical product performance evaluation, working with internal and external partners

  • Understanding and tracking industry unmet needs and requirements and actively helping to prioritize potential programs

  • Representing Eastman during acoustical product collaboration programs in Europe

  • Conducting in field product performance validation testing

  • Working closely with AMI Acoustic Segment Manager, Technical Key Account Representatives, customers and OEM Specification Representatives

  • Creating technical papers related to acoustical product knowledge

  • Representing Eastman during industry events and fairs

Required background & qualifications

  • Master’s in (polymer) engineering or other advanced degree in the field of science

  • Minimum of 3 years’ experience in a similar industry setup and complex technology projects

  • Initial leadership skills or clear will to develop a creative coaching leadership style

  • Understanding of production processes preferably in extrusion industry

  • Ability to quickly learn the job (with mentoring from others)

  • Ability to clearly and concisely communicate and interact with others, both oral and written

  • Ability to interface with many activities and champion decision taking and resolution

  • Good understanding and application of technology project management

  • Collaborative mind-set: proactively establish and maintain effective working relationships

  • English, Dutch and/or any West-European language (business fluent)


As an Eastman Team Member, you will be evaluated against the behaviors below that we feel are critical to meeting our business strategy.

  • Market Insight: Leverage market insights to guide decisions and determine what is right

  • Courage: Make courageous choices to innovate and accelerate value creation

  • Optimism: Optimistically focus on continual growth over a longer-term horizon

  • Bias for Action: Have a bias for action, prioritizing issues and making sound decisions, despite uncertainty or risk

  • Adaptability: Adaptable to market and customer opportunities quickly


Our offer

It’s about growth and your long-term success. At Eastman, your career path can take many routes—each offering growth opportunities, competitive salary and benefits, and a globally growing work environment. Eastman fosters a culture of diversity steeped in teamwork and employee involvement, a place where dedicated individuals turn great ideas into materials that make a difference in everyday life. We support and encourage one another and believe that leveraging diversity in our thoughts and experiences allows us to excel in business.
