Senior Data Engineer

Dynamo, LLC. Greater Omaha Area

1 day ago

Apply

So here's the deal.


We're looking for a Senior Data Engineer with experience in designing, implementing, and maintaining large-scale data systems and a passion for learning modern AWS and big data technologies.


You'll be working with a growing technology company that provides millions with unique data-driven insight and have an opportunity to build modern solutions that scale systems and make data analysis more efficient across the company including:

  • Redesigning data systems from a warehouse-centric to a lake-centric architecture to be more cost-effective as our data volume grows.
  • Rolling out a structured events pipeline for analytics and productization of high-volume event streams.
  • Building a centralized, discoverable data catalog to enable users across the company to efficiently find and use data.


What you will be expected to know or be committed to quickly learn:

  • Experience with multiple different big data stacks and/or architectures, working directly on designing, building and maintaining them
  • Broad knowledge of industry patterns, frameworks, platforms, and tools, and how they’re commonly applied to achieve specific business outcomes; needs to have a good feel for modern stacks (e.g. Spark, Hadoop, Fivetran, Stitch, Luigi, Airflow, Parquet, Kafka, etc.)
  • Experience designing or working within a lake-centric architecture
  • Understands the differences between a data lake and a data warehouse; the strengths and weaknesses of each pattern
  • Experience with commonly-used data processing platforms like Spark/Hadoop
  • Preferred hands-on experience with AWS big data tools like Redshift, Glue, EMR, etc.
