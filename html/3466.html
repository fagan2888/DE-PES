Company Overview

ConocoPhillips is the world’s largest independent E&P company based on production and proved reserves. Headquartered in Houston, Texas, ConocoPhillips had operations and activities in 17 countries, $71 billion of total assets, and approximately 10,900 employees as of June 30, 2019. Production excluding Libya averaged 1,303 MBOED for the six months ended June 30, 2019, and proved reserves were 5.3 BBOE as of Dec. 31, 2018.

Employees across the globe focus on fulfilling our core SPIRIT Values of safety, people, integrity, responsibility, innovation and teamwork. And we apply the characteristics that define leadership excellence in how we engage each other, collaborate with our teams, and drive the business.

The Position Is Accountable For

Perform all engineering planning and implementation of Well Integrity Management System (WIMS) and well integrity operations in conjunction with multi-disciplined technical team involved in Well Integrity Operations. Accountable for the Well Operations’ Well Integrity Budget including LRP and reporting. Accountable for compliance to maintenance schedule, well maintenance data management, interpretation and follow-up of deficiencies. Accountable for the HSE and performance of all operations and personnel at the wellsite.

Role And Scope
  • Actively participate in promoting safety and environmental awareness with all company / contract staff such that well intervention operations are carried out in a safe and environmentally sound manner.
  • Implement Well Integrity Management System (WIMS) for all life cycle of wells.
  • Support the development and implementation of a consistent well integrity process for the ConocoPhillips Indonesia.
  • Responsible for developing and implementing a consistent well integrity program and procedures for the ConocoPhillips Indonesia including well integrity compliance and assurance for all ConocoPhillips Indonesia assets. This includes government, BU and Corporate requirements such as the Well Integrity Recommended Practice.
  • Responsible for Well Integrity related contracts and administer contracts with outside suppliers of equipment and services. Assist in the preparation, evaluation and contract awards for support services and materials.
  • Work directly with the production engineers, field technicians and well intervention personnel in development of diagnostics procedures and checklists, etc. Interfacing with internal work groups such as Production Engineering, Well Intervention, Production Operations, A&OI and occasionally with government agencies.
  • Represent ConocoPhillips Indonesia well integrity by participating in global efforts on well integrity and close collaboration with other business units and corporate ConocoPhillips.
  • Responsible for Well Integrity assurance and compliance for all COPI assets.
  • Develop and implement a consistent well integrity process for the BU. This will include identification of wells producing outside of the well operating guidelines, well integrity diagnostics, waiver and mitigation recommendations, wellhead and tree valve maintenance programs, tubing and casing integrity programs and development and management of a well integrity database.
  • Institute program for investigating well integrity anomalies and causes, develop contingency plans for mitigation and prevention.
  • Provide guidance to other ConocoPhillips Indonesia work groups regarding well integrity, well repair and regulatory issues.
  • Monitor Well Operations’ Well Integrity Budget and spending on a-monthly basis.
  • Ensure Well Integrity Operations are properly captured in the ConocoPhillips reporting system (WellView®) and perform quality control of the report and revised as required.
  • Represent ConocoPhillips Indonesia well integrity by participating in global efforts on well integrity and close collaboration with other business units and corporate ConocoPhillips.
  • Support the development and implementation of consistent well integrity process for the ConocoPhillips Indonesia:
  • Well barrier polices and Well Integrity diagnostics.
  • Waiver and mitigations recommendations.
  • Wellhead and tree valve maintenance programs.
  • Tubing and casing integrity programs and development.
  • Management and continued development of a well integrity database.
  • Lead cross functional risk assessment process complete with recommendation and mitigation solutions.
  • Conduct monthly contract meeting with contractor to monitor the ongoing activities, HSE and any improvement needed for contractor.
  • Participate on Process Safety Council to identify any issues related with process safety and follow up action.
  • Monitor annulus pressure trending using SafeWells, identify for anomalies based on annulus pressure trending.
  • Sharing knowledge with other BU through NoE teleconference and Engineering meeting.
  • Provide training for young engineer, field personnel to increase the knowledge regarding the well integrity.
  • Review and regularly update well integrity procedure and guideline every four (4) years.
  • Involve in well intervention and integrity daily meeting and monitoring the execution of well integrity work and to capture any findings.
  • Create the job program or procedure related to well integrity work.
  • Provide documentation for Well Integrity work programs, such as justifications and plans.
  • Collaboratively works with other functions to raise integrity issues and drives fit for-purpose mitigation solutions to reduce risk.

Qualifications
  • Minimum BSc. Degree in Engineering.
  • Minimum 8+ years industry experience in well operations or production engineering.
  • Uncompromising adherence and commitment to our SPIRIT Values (Safety, People, Integrity, Responsibility, Innovation and Teamwork).
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills and will be interfacing with internal work groups such as Production Engineering, Well Intervention, and Operations, and occasionally with government agencies.
  • Collaborative individual who can objectively assess well integrity risk.
  • Ability to communicate recommendations based on assessment of various options against defined decision criteria.
  • Proven innovative thinker in order to provide solutions to complex well problems.
  • Commitment to promoting safety and protecting the environment.
  • Familiar with SafeWells and WellView software application.
  • Problem solving capability and seek new technology for solution.

Preferred Qualifications
  • Well Integrity experience, well service experience
  • WellCat and/or other stress analysis calculation methods.
  • Experience objectively leading risk assessments.
