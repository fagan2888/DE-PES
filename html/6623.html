    • Job Description

    Datalogic is seeking a Mechanical Engineer able to autonomously design, develop and test mechanical and optical components for complex optomechatronic devices such as Scanners, Sensors and Safety products.

    She/he will design parts and products together with optical and hardware designers, using Siemens NX CAD and will simulate/anticipate possible issues by means of FEM tools and rapid prototyping.

    Main Responsibilities
    • Carry out detailed mechanical design activities, leveraging 3D modelling, simulation tools (e.g. thermal & stress), tolerances analysis, prototyping and experiments.
    • Responsible for preparing and presenting mechanical designs at design reviews, sustaining deep and thorough technical debates with knowledgeable counterparts;
    • Ensures design files and technical documentation are kept up to date;
    • Works closely with operations, manufacturing and production engineers to ensure products are designed for being manufactured
    • Create accurate Bill of Materials;
    • Conduct product cost, design effort and delivery time estimation.


    Requirements

    Education: Degree in Mechanical Engineer (MA Degree)

    Languages: Fluent English.

    Experiences: 5+ years of experience in Design of mechanical parts for mechanical / electronic / optical appliances, instruments, complex products.

    Experience and knowledge with plastic/metal materials and all production processes (injection molding, die casting, cnc, laser cutting etc.) including pre-qualification tests.
    • Knowledge in using 3D CAD systems and Microsoft office tools (Siemens NX PREFERRED);
    • Use of FEM SW (MSC Nastran SW PREFERRED).
