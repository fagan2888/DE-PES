    We’re looking for Software Engineer - SRE (Service Reliability Engineering) and Distributed System to make our distributed database product and its infrastructure reliable, scalable and easily operatable.


    Responsibilities :

    • Make and keep our production systems reliable, scalable and easily operatable
    • Automate, codify, and standardize operations, improving productivity for both developers and ops
    • Promote and establish conventions among different services for monitoring, provisioning, deployments, and development flows
    • Respond to incidents and supporting postmortems
    • Enhance Scalar DLT from various perspectives


    Requirements :

    • A BS or MS in Computer Science or equivalent experience with a solid understanding of computer science (algorithms, data structures etc.), acquired either in school or at work
    • Strong experience in infrastructure and networking
    • Strong experience in distributed system
    • Strong experience in orchestration tools such as ansible and terraform
    • Strong experience in public clouds (such as AWS, Azure and Oracle Cloud)
    • Strong experience in Docker and Kubernates
    • Strong experience in application development and its lifecycle
    • Ability to initiate and drive projects to completion with minimal guidance
    • You love to “engineer” the development cycle (e.g., writing, reviewing, testing), making it faster and more reliable


    Nice-to-Haves :


    * Programming experience in Java

    * Strong experience in database system

    * Strong experience in performance analysis and/or performance optimizations
