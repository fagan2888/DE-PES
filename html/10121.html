About The Team

Recruiter Name, Email Monika Chojnowska , monika.chojnowska@zalando.pl

As Electrician Engineer for building service engineering you’ll support the building facility management team in our logistic center. You will provide support in relation to all maintenance tasks and projects being undertaken, including day to day breakdown, equipment updates, new installations, and building/infrastructure upgrades. With a strong team you’ll help thousands of customers each day.

WHERE YOUR EXPERTISE IS NEEDED

  • Overall responsibility of monitoring, testing, troubleshooting and carrying out all necessary maintenance works and reactive task associated with building
  • Performing assembly/installation including electrical systems and connection of electrical loads, contribution to preparation of revision documentation
  • Responsible for creation and maintenance of testing and measuring records including fixed and mobile electrical appliances and devices
  • Perform error diagnostics, faults corrections and contribution to the safety of systems and sites by sending information on detected faults/hazards to the building management

WHAT WE’RE LOOKING FOR

  • You have successfully completed a professional education in the field of industrial electrics/automation/control engineering or comparable professional technical education (preferably with an additional qualification as master craftsman or certified technician) and have at least 2 years’ experience
  • Knowledge in the automation levels of building control systems and their systematic networking, operation saving systems and operational knowledge of electrical appliances.
  • Experience with Environmental Monitoring System (EMS) / Building Management System (BMS)
  • Fluent in Polish and English (German skills are a plus), you are highly motivated
  • Strong working knowledge of MS office package with emphasis on excel

PERKS AT WORK

  • A motivated and multinational team of experts to inspire and support you. There’s no time for boredom at Zalando!
  • A detailed, fully compensated training. Furthermore, you can look forward to comprehensive on the job training and personal development opportunities in a rapidly growing company
  • Great salary as well as benefits. How about 40% discount on our online shop, free fruit and drinks every day, parties and team events to name just a few
  • A corporate culture where your skills are judged only by your results-our motto every day is casual Friday


We celebrate diversity and are committed to building teams that represent a variety of backgrounds, perspectives and skills. All employment is decided on the basis of qualifications, merit and business need.

About Zalando

Zalando Lounge is an online shopping club for fashion and lifestyle products in 13 European countries. We offer our members daily, time-limited sale campaigns with discounts of up to 75% off the recommended retail price. However, Zalando Lounge is so much more than discounts. Our strength lies in our focus on fashion from sought-after labels to niche brands, from famous international names to trendy luxury brands, we make sure to meet the fashion taste of all our customers, who are always on the hunt for the best products at the best prices. Working with our brand partners, we're pioneering innovative supply-chain and production processes and offer them an impact and smart solution, creating a new way to shop for fashion - and a new way to sell.
