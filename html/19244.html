Aplikuj w witrynie firmy
PM Polska SA is looking to hire a moitivated self-starter to join Project Team as an
PROJECT ENGINEER
The main purpose of the role is to ensure comprehensive execution of assigned engineering projects and contribute with the technical expertise in order to ensure that all projects are properly implemented, adhering to all cost, quality, time and safety requirements in full compliance to applicable standards and procedures, contributing to continuous improvement of the production processes. Prepare reports for factory management about the status of all CAPEX projects.

Your responsibilities will include
CAPEX PROJECTS MANAGEMENT
You will be preparing local project with detailed project scope, budget, objectives, benefits, deadline, methods, layouts, and deliverables or provide EU Engineering with data necessary to complete regional Business Case with respect to local requirements.
PLANNING & FINANCIALS
You will be creating budget of Capex projects in Operation Department, Coordinating and controlling expenses, ensuring finishing and closing of the project within planned deadline and budget.
EQUIPMENT QUALIFICATION
Project engineer will coordinate local qualification of machines and installations, contribute to establishment of acceptance criteria and control if they are fulfilled by the supplier. You will as well provide local report, which includes information and expertise needed for taking decision if the qualification can be accepted or not, ensure accessibility of the results to EU Engineering and other Philip Morris affiliates.
PERFORMANCE and CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT
You will lead and manage activities in the department in order to ensure that relevant KPI's objectives are met or exceeded. Drive continuous performance improvement by initiating the development and improvement of technological / business processes in order to identify opportunities to improve performance and support achievement of short/long term performance objectives with regards to environmental and safety legislation and technology.
Requirements
• Engineering Degree ( Mechanical background or equivalend would be an asset)
• 2 years of experience in The manufacturing department, preferably in the project engineering
• PC skills (MS office package)
• Proven track of experience in project management
• Excellent knowledge of Auto Cad or similar tools would be an advantage.
• Fluent English and Polish




Please apply until 31.10.2019. Please be advised that only online applications will be taken under consideration. We will contact only selected candidates.
