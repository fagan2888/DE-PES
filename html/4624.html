Senior Data Scientist

Oura Oulu, Northern Finland, Finland

4 weeks ago

Apply on company website

Your primary task is to turn data into customer insights or product features of the Oura ring. The data includes biosignals, long-term user metrics and everything in between. You will be part of the multi-talented Oura team and your focus will be on machine learning and big data analytics. As a part of the Science team you can be on the leading edge of technology-driven wellbeing and develop features in both short-term and long-term research projects.


The actual position will be tailored to the direction that makes your eyes sparkle!

REQUIREMENTS

  • Background in real world machine learning
  • Understanding of human modelling and generalisation of models
  • Matlab and Python experience
  • 5+ years of experience from a relevant field or tasks
  • Self-driven and forward-looking attitude

BENEFITS

At Oura, we care about you and your wellbeing. Everyone here at Oura has a ring of their own and everyone learns something about themselves and how could they improve their health and wellbeing. We also learn from each other.

We use the latest tools and we're always ready to hear what has worked for you in the past.


We’ll hire someone for this role when we find the right person. Please apply as soon as possible.
