    Senior Recruitment Consultant at ITbility

    • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central & AU Payroll
    • Sydney
    • 6 Months + (Long Term)


    My client is urgently looking for a SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central & AU Payroll to join their team in Sydney for an 6 Months +(Long term) contract. For more details please apply or email your resume to anil.more@itbility.com.au


    Required Skills:


    • Data models modifications and upload –corporate data model , succession data models etc .
    • Workbook modifications based on customer  requirement and system modifications
    • Provisioning instance configuration , Super Admin creation , Upgrade Center handling in SF .
    • Master data Migration from Legacy system/SAP HCM In-premises to SAP Success factor
    • Success factor Solution Designing , Workshops with Customer , Integration of various SF modules
    • Like EC to Compensation/ONB etc
    • Configuring data models in SuccessFactors EC & Job scheduling
    • Time-off – Configuring Time type, Time Account type . Holiday Calendar , Accrual rules ,
    • Leave policy rules for maternity , paternity and Time-off data load activities
    • Configuring Success factor from provision console and integration with SAP.
    • Setting up jobs and required reports for real time data synchronization between EC & other modules
    • Role Based Permission (RBP) configuration , job synchronization , HR users role group creation etc.
    • Data synchronization with HRIS sync job (Employee Central).
    • Configuring Metadata Framework, generic object definition, rules, workflow.
    • Configure position management – defining positioning generic object, permission checks, job code rules, approval workflows, creation of positions.
    • Play back with client for system testing , UAT Points closure & client signoff
    • Production Instance Synchronization , Production data migration , cutover activities like job scheduling etc.
    • ORD Reports (Online Report Designing) –Extensively worked on SAP Adhoc reports , Standard reports,
    • Standard Reports bundle upload , ORD reports creation using calculative fields , LMS details reporting , Golden gateway synchronization etc .
    • Expertise in the configuration of Organizational Management, Personnel Administration, Time Management, ESS and MSS and Payroll AU.

    All applicants should have full working rights in Australia.

    To apply, please submit your resume ASAP for immediate consideration or email anil.more@itbility.com.au

    Only shortlisted applicants will be contacted for this role.
