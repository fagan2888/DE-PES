SENIOR DATA ENGINEER

Omina Technologies Antwerpen, BE

1 week ago

Apply on company website
Are you a Big Data Engineer and do you know of the state-of-the-art big data technologies that enable companies to get their data under control? Are you familiar with SCALA, SPARK, HADOOP and NOSQL? We have the right opportunity for you!

Main Activities and Responsibilities Develop and apply image processing and machine learning methods in projects across Omina Technologies

The suitable candidate will bring active experience in big data engineering on the projects that require large data processing

Using the available data, the big date engineer will actively promote new ideas to enhance our competitive offerings

The Machine Learning Engineer will actively contribute to product and operational content strategies by identifying and ingesting new technical capabilities to forward Omina Technologies mission of leading the way in advancing science and technology

Pro-actively make knowledge available for operational use within Omina Technologies

Analyze extracted information to drive such processes as automated and manual data cleansing

Use data analytics to drive decisions for our content acquisition strategy

Use visualization tools to present findings to stakeholders and other relevant parties

Serve as internal and external specialist on big data engineering techniques

The Machine Learning Engineer will also be able to act as a liaison between IT developers and (content) subject matters experts

Qualifications

Master or PhD degree in Computer Science, Engineering

A creative problem solver with a strong knowledge of statistics, image processing and machine learning methods and strategies

Industry Experience Is Strongly Preferred

Ability to drive new developments and implement process changes and disruptive technologies in the organization

Good communication and documentation skills with the ability to convey complex technical concepts to non-technical professionals

Experience working with a variety of stakeholders at the mid and senior management level

Technical Skills

Design and deploy a set of data applications

Experience in cloud and on-premise solutions

Experience with SCALA, SPARK

Knowledge of ETL for large data sets on distributed systems

Knowledge of Hadoop

Knowledge of aggregations, deduplication and linking to identities

Extensive knowledge in NOSQL, SQL, MongoDB

Experience in Python

Experience in data jobs / streams and pipeline

Experience in workflow management and job shedding of data loads

Experience in real time data streaming and processing

Experience with big data technologies (such as Azure Data Factory, EventHub, DataLakes, HD Insights, …)

Knowledge of the various cloud platforms and on premise.

Knowledge of data science and machine learning algorithms so that you can ensure that your data deliveries are ideal for data scientists.

Knowledge of distributed systems, load balancing and networking, massive data storage, and security.

Capable to coach junior profiles and provide technical advice and direction.

Knowledge of Dutch and/or French and English

What We Offer

We welcome you to a truly dynamic and challenging environment with great opportunities for personal development. Omina Technologies’ benefits are very competitive.
