    Náplň (druh) práce
    Join our growing Consulting Team and be our Data Engineer Rockstar who will do your best to constantly improve data reliability and quality.

    Your responsibilities

  • Create and maintain optimal data pipeline architecture for Minit process intelligence
  • Assemble large, complex data sets that meet functional / non-functional business requirements
  • Build the infrastructure required for optimal extraction, transformation, and loading of data from a wide variety of data sources
  • Create data tools for analytics and data scientist team members that assist them in building and optimizing our product into an innovative industry leader
  • Work with data and analytics experts to strive for greater functionality in our product
  • Create custom software components and analytics applications for our product
  • Recommend different ways to constantly improve data reliability and quality

    • Your profile

  • Overall 4+ years of experience in IT industry with 1+ years working on products built to handle big data in both cloud and on-prem settings.
  • Experience of working on both SQL & No-SQL databases.
  • Extensive practical knowledge of T-SQL.
  • Experience with object-oriented design, coding and testing patterns as well as experience in engineering (commercial or open source) software platforms and large-scale data infrastructures.
  • Experience in Python, data processing and parsing.
  • Experience of building platforms/solutions in cloud using AWS, Azure or GCP.
  • Experience in offline batch processing and/or online real-time stream processing systems.
  • Experience with extraction of data from SAP, Oracle, Salesforce and ServiceNow is an advantage.
  • Interested in working across our entire data science stack including model building, data pipelining, and performance/scale analysis.

    • Salary: From 3000 € + depending on experience
