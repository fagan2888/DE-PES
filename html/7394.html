    • Onapsis is looking for a QA Engineer and also, an Automation Engineer , here are the differences between the two roles:


    QA Engineer:

    • User-facing test role
    • In-depth product & business knowledge
    • Functional analysis skills
    • Perfectionist at test cases design
    • Obsessed with "edge cases"
    • Automation oriented thinking
    • Meticulous documentation writer


    Automation Engineer:

    • Developer-facing test role
    • Full coding skills
    • Testability keeper and consultant
    • Testing frameworks writer/maintainer
    • Testing tools developer for developers
    • "Want-it-all automated" advocate
    • Mock or fake environments


    Knowledge/Qualifications:

    • Knowledge of Python *or some other programming language*
    • Knowledge of testing automation, including creation and management of testing environments.

    Experience:

    • 3+ years of experience in Testing/ Quality Assurance teams
    • Experience with agile software development - Scrum
    • 3+ years experience in developing or collaborating with automated testing/QA solutions
