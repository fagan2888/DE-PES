CONTEXT


We are looking for a Web and Mobile Developer to cover the following tasks:

  • Architecture design review
  • Software development, upgrades and maintenance
  • Publication and export data

Skills & Technical Knowledge


Knowledge and skills

  • .NET and Mobile application development
  • Open Street Map/Google mapping design
  • Analysis of the crowd sourcing applications architecture (asset)
  • Net neutrality compliant internet software design (asset)

Specific expertise

  • Minimum 11 years of relevant studies and experience after the secondary school
  • Web and smartphone (iOS and/or Android) interface and application design
  • Minimum 6 years of relevant professional experience with application architecture design

General skills

  • Capacity to work efficiently in a multi-disciplinary environment.
  • The working languages will be English.
  • To integrate in a multicultural environment and strong teamwork attitude would be a strong asset.
