As a Data Engineer, you´ll work within a cross-functional team, delivering customer value at pace and insisting on high quality and simplicity.

What You´ll Do
  • Integrate additional internal and third party resources and handle ETL for data ingestion.
  • Delivering and promoting technical excellence based on fundamental principles and standards that you contribute to.
  • Working with the Chief Data Officer, Data Architect, fellow data engineers and other teams to implement and deliver the Data Analytics platform.
  • Maintaing robust and performant data pipelines.
  • Sharing your technical expertise within the team.
  • Supporting and enforcing controls on the data platform to contain risk and align with regulatory policies.
The Skills You´ll Need
  • Programming languages, including Python, SQL, Scala or Java.
  • ETL processes, such as Apache Airflow.
  • Messaging, event and streaming technology, including Apache Kafta.
  • Big databases, such as AWS Redshift, Postgres, Snowflake.
  • Infrastructure such as code, including Terraform.
  • Containerization, such as Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Cloud technologies, including Amazon Web Services and Google Cloud Platform.
  • Large-scale data processing frameworks, such as Spark or Hadoop.
  • Passionate about data, technologies and techniques.
  • Experience in engineering for Data Science, Big Data or related software industry areas.
  • Ability to explain data pipelines in the forms of diagrams and other documentation.
  • Experience of building data processing pipelines for use in production hands-off environments.
  • Having a deep understanding of data structures and schema design.
