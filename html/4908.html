Job Ref: BBBH 17486


This is a Senior AI / ML Data Science Engineering role with our client, based in Dublin city centre, a vibrant young software product company, with a unique software product, developing a SaaS based AI-powered, NLP-content rich service portal and mobile app focused on IoT smart devices and the application of machine learning for autonomous IoT product support.


Company Selling Points:


Working with the CTO, product & technical architects and senior developers you will be responsible for product feature design, development and release management.

  • Vibrant start-up, great technologies
  • Dublin City Centre based location.
  • Excellent, competitive salary

Ideal experience to succeed in this role:


3+ years in commercial software product design and development delivering mission critical web, messaging/voice and mobile apps integrated with scaled data analytical capability leveraging a combination of expert Machine Learning, NLP, algorithmic, predictive consumer focused insights and content working on product design (architecture and vision), research and development.


Technical Expertise :

  • Data Science expertise (predictive algorithms)
  • NP technologies, platforms & tools
  • Java, Python
  • Cloud
