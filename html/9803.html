Role Title: Senior Data Engineer

Location: Düsseldorf

Are you bold? Innovative? Inquisitive? Great, then we can help you thrive…

The V by Vodafone team was created just 2 years ago to pioneer a new way of connecting our 500 million customers globally to the world of Internet of Things.

We’re a team of fearless, restless visionaries in a completely new technology, on a mission to create world-changing products that connect people to their homes, their smart devices, and the things they love. Using our smart V SIM card, innate objects can now be controlled and monitored from anywhere at anytime, giving people the opportunity to do things they have never done before, offering freedom, security and control on a new level.

We have already launched 15 products in 7 markets internationally …and we just can’t stop!

This is where you come in…




We are seeking like-minded intrepid explorers to join us. People that aren’t afraid to take risks. The daring ones, the trailblazers, the pioneers!

The oil of the future for the IT industry and especially IoT is data. This role will ensure that the rigs and fountains are well scoped out, integrated and the machinery is running at maximum throughput with high level of security. The Data Engineer for the Performance & Analytics team will responsible for the infrastructure, integration and security for Consumer IoT for the space of Performance & Analytics. We are looking for a person that can define efficient, scalable and reusable solutions on native cloud services as well as on premise solutions that help us to enrich our data lake continuously. You have an understanding of the meaning of data and acknowledge its importance, you are experienced in building data models and basic visualisations. Data visualisation as well as data discovery will be part of your day to day business.

Being a data engineer requires strong communication skills as you will interface with people with different backgrounds and needs across multiple locations around the globe. You will work with these teams to understand their data, integrate new data sources and evolve the technology portfolio for data and analytics. You will work and share your experience with a dedicated team of data scientists and data engineers across multiple locations. Together with other technology teams you will define the future of analytics by introducing and applying capabilities like machine learning, artificial intelligence as well as moving Consumer IoT towards cloud native analytics solutions.



With us you will:



  • Build the infrastructure for data pipelines for Consumer IoT
  • Ensure quality and security of data stored and transferred
  • Design, built, adopt data models and pipelines
  • Share your knowledge with colleagues, clients and communities.
  • Analyse usage of data products incl. correlation patterns and define potential technical enhancements


Apply if you have:



  • Strong communication skills to manage local and remote stakeholders in a distributed team
  • >= 5 years professional work experience with data, analytics, BI
  • Deep experience of Big Data Analytics technologies (infrastructure, pipelines, data storage)
  • Deep experience and expertise across data integration and data management
  • Experienced in data quality and security management
  • Hands-on experience with Apache Hadoop stack: HDFS, Oozie, Hue, Spark, Hive, Impala
  • Experience with cloud native services like AWS, Kinesis, Lambda, S3 and others
  • Programming experience in e.g. Python, JavaScript, Java
  • First experience with Apache Tez, Datameer, Tableau, Splunk is an optional plus
  • Applied Scrum / Agile methodologies
  • Professional experience and knowledge of Internet and/or Telco industries

What you can expect from us:



  • Dedicated support and mentoring
  • Opportunity to develop and progress – plus a solid dedication to work-life balance. It all means you’ll have everything you need to advance your career and achieve your full potential.
  • Annual company bonus
  • 30 day’s holiday
  • Contributory company pension scheme
