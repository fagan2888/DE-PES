    • Brief job description

    We don't necessarily agree that DevOps is a job title. We are looking for a great Site Reliability Engineer who will join our teams to help them in Infrastructure as Code , security automation and feels comfortable in DevOps model .


    We spend 1/4 of our lives at work so why not to make it a place you co-create, where your opinion matters, you feel safe and you do meaningful things


    Basics

    • Location: Łódź, Poland
    • Work start date: ASAP
    • Contract length: permanent


    Compensation

    • 11000 - 18000 PLN + VAT (B2B) per month
    • 9000 - 15000 PLN gross (UoP) per month


    Must have

    • Linux
    • Docker
    • Python
    • Shell
    • AWS
    • Git
    • Terraform
    • Cultural fit
    • English


    Nice-to-have

    • Terragrunt
    • Networking


    Cultural fit explained

    • growth mindset
    • curiosity and courage
    • egoless
    • focus on end-user
    • end-to-end responsibility
    • willingness to experiment


    What we offer

    • you will have impact on architecture and technology stack and, therefore, opportunity to master it,
    • we will help you understand why and how we do things, but also invite you to challenge our ways of working,
    • we will trust you to know best how to organize your time and do things within the boundaries defined by being responsible for yourself, your colleagues, the team and company,
    • you will work in an environment in which agility and proper organizational culture are considered one of the key aspects of business success,
    • you will be invited to co-create a great company and workplace with us.


    Work methodology

    • Agile management: Scrum, Kanban
    • Issue tracking tool: Jira, Github, GitLab
    • Knowledge repository: Confluence, Github, GitLab
    • Virtualization: Docker
    • Network virtualization: AWS VPC
    • Provisioning: Terraform
    • Cloud infrastructure: AWS
    • Operating system: Linux
    • Application stack: JVM, Node.js, Golang, Kafka
    • Version control system: git
    • Databases: MariaDB, PostgreSQL, MongoDB


    Equipment supplied

    • Operating System: you choose (MacOS, Linux, Windows)
    • Computer: notebook + up to 2 external displays


    Specs

    • Job profile: Mainly new features
    • Paid holiday: Yes
    • Part time work: No
    • Remote possible: Yes (1 day a week)
    • Flexible hours: Yes
    • Travel involved: Some
    • Freedom to choose tools: Yes


    Perks in the office

    Bike parking, In-house trainings, In-house hack days, Startup atmosphere, No dress code, Free coffee, Swedish fika, Company events


    Perks

    Flat structure, Small teams, International projects, Training budget, Conference budget


    About us


    " Everything we do we believe smart way is the only way to solve problems together. "

    We are proud that thanks to our work we helped among others:

    • neurodivergents (people with autism spectrum) to grow as human beings regardless of their ability to function,
    • people to have more convenient access to blood tests which leads to faster diagnosis of their health state,
    • students to become financially independent by making gig economy more accessible to them,
    • people to save money by making it a fun activity associated with positive emotions.

    We are not just a software house, nor are we an outsourcing or body leasing agency. We are the home of business growth. Our goal is not write lines of code but to solve real-life problems with code among others.

    We build products we identify ourselves with and software is usually a crucial ingredient.

    What members of our gang appreciate most in our company is:

    • autonomy and end-to-end responsibility for a product resulting from flat structure of our company - a team takes ownership of architecture, quality, technology and way of working (there are no architects, team leads, junior or senior developers - let’s focus on cool work to be done instead of titles and hierarchies etc.),
    • opportunity for mastery at various technologies - we focus on a problem first and then find the best tool for that instead of being tied to one technology only,
    • serving greater purpose by building meaningful products and co-creating a company where psychological safety and fun matter.

    If it is also important to you, join our gang.

    DNA Technology is a business unit of Digital New Agency responsible for product and software development. We’re an experienced technology partner working with companies of different sizes - from developing startups to engaging in digital transformations of large enterprises.

    The company has offices in Stockholm, Sweden and Lodz, Poland, with Lodz being our primary development site.
