Job Purpose

Project Engineer (Automation) Responsibilities

Leads and takes ownership for the implementation of high technology equipment. Product and/or processes for the production, finishing and inspection of IOLs and delivery system products.

Support the integration of new products/process and suppliers into the existing manufacturing processes and/or product portfolio at Alcon Ireland.

Manage equipment and process validations under the Quality system change control process

To manage projects in which there is a significant automation / software component. Major Accountabilities
  • Initiate change controls, validation and risk documentation within the quality system as required for new product design transfer equipment, materials, process improvements and new product implementation.
  • Responsible for equipment / CS documentation development and approvals e.g. URS, FDS, FAT, SAT, IQOQPQ.
  • Support cross functional design transfer project teams to ensure successful implementation of assigned project deliverables
  • To develop and manage desing transfer projects in which there is a major software component. This includes management and development of all change control deleverables.
  • Responsible for ensuring all projects are completed to agreed timelines and within budget.
  • Generate safety risk assessments for any new equipment and /or processes introduced to the manufacturing facility.
  • Ensure seemless transition of new equipment. processes and products to the MTO and QA functions in Alcon Ireland.
  • Assigned work adheres to H&S and environmental requirements.Specific Position knowledge/skills/abilities: Knowledge and experience of process engingeering, equipment and automation specification Knowledge and experience in process engineering, equipment and automation validation A good understanding of GAMP 5 and 21 CFR Part 11 requiements Strong background in Operating Systems, SCADA, PLC, SQL Server. Analytical skills in problem solving Well developed Project Management skills Ability to work on own initiative. Highly developed people skills Knowledge of GMP, Validation and Change Controls Good problem solving skills and PC skills such as Word, Excel and operating systems, good time management.

    Knowledge of ISO, FDA, GMP standards. Excellent organizational skills with attention to detail

    Preferred Position knowledge/skills/abilities: Knowledge and experience of optics and optical metrology Knowledge of vision systems, robotics systems is beneficial

    Ideal Background

    Education

    Minimum: Degree in Engineering or related discipline

    Preferred: Masters in Engineering related discipline

    Experience

    Minimum: 3-5 years experience in Automation within a manufacturing environment working in a GAMP / regulated industry environment

    Preferred: 5-10 years experience in an engineering / automation function executing product and process technology transfer in a regulated industry environment.
