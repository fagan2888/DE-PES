    • Are you a data engineer who wants to work for a fast-growing food tech brand?

    Does being the 1st data engineer at the company in the US excite you?

    Do you want to work alongside some of the smartest data scientists in the industry?

    This company has over 2.48 million active customers worldwide. They have offices globally and you will be joining their fast-growing data team in New York. As the data engineer, you will be designing and building a world-class self-service data platform.

    You will be working on two amazing projects from day one:

    1. Optimization of audiences and targeting across the companies food tech platforms
    2. A data-driven and ROI based, campaign testing machine

    You'll have…

    • 2-3 years related experience
    • A relevant degree in computer science, software engineering or related field
    • Advanced Python skills (object orientated programming)
    • Experience with Spark, AWS and SQL (PostgreSQL or MySQL)

    This is an opportunity to join a world-class data science function as their first data engineer. If this sounds interesting to you, please contact us today with your CV.
