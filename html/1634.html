    • Working with Service Operation teams for business change management and releasing management
    • Managing 3rd party vendors to implement projects

    • University degree in Computer Science or related Engineering discipline
    • Specialized ITSM education and certification

    • 3-5 years’ experience in project management
    • Significant experience in process re-design, re-engineering using various tools and technologies
    • Business acumen and effective communication skills
    • Ability to establish strong relationships with the corresponding technical community
    • Ability to serve as a visionary concerning future technological capabilities and operational scenarios; ability to create new business models and technologie
    • Ability to lead activities and teams within a global organisation.
    • Strong English language skills both verbal and written
