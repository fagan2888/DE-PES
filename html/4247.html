About This Role

Gartner Digital Markets is looking for a passionate Full Stack Sr. Software Engineer for our Vendor Portal team located in Gurgaon, India.

This position works closely with our product owners, leads and engineers across teams to working web API, database, web portal using RDBMS, Node.js using REST API or Graph API and client UI using React or other JS frameworks.

What You’ll Do
  • Deliver high quality, innovative, flexible, cost optimized and scalable technical solutions that meet business requirements
  • Develop, construct, test and maintain architectures with databases, large scale data processing, APIs and intuitive user interfaces
  • Support the production web applications that your team will build
  • Perform hands-on development and provide thoughtful code reviews
  • Collaborate with team members to continuously improve quality and productivity to achieve business results
  • Design, develop, test and support applications and services using Node.js, React, RDBMS, Elasticsearch and Serverless Framework
  • Partner with the business stakeholders, peers, IT groups and communicate effectively
  • Conduct Proof of Concepts for testing business ideas
  • Recommend ways to improve operational excellence, reliability, performance and security of applications
  • Follow SCRUM Agile Development methodology

What You’ll Need
  • Bachelors or Masters Degree in Computer Science or Computer Engineering
  • 5+ years of relevant experience of which at least 2 years needs to be as Senior Engineer
  • Advanced knowledge of software development methodology, techniques & standards.
  • Strong understanding of software design concepts, patterns, tools and best practices.
  • Must have full stack experience using Node.js, React, Oracle RDBMS, using Serverless framework and Elasticsearch.
  • Experience supporting and debugging production web application environments
  • Ability and willingness to work with remote teams
  • Should have built and used a REST API
  • Any experience using Ruby on Rails, GraphQL and D3 JS library is a plus
  • Experience working with Unix based platform is a must

What We Offer

A collaborative, positive culture. You’ll work with people who are as enthusiastic, smart and driven as you are. You’ll be managed by the best too.

Limitless growth and learning opportunities. We offer the excitement of a fast-paced entrepreneurial workplace and the professional growth opportunities of an established global organization.

Outstanding compensation. We offer a competitive salary and generous benefits; including a robust time off allowance and great perks.
