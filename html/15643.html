We are the Data Analytics Center of Excellence of TMB . We are looking for a data scientist with expertise on big data and advanced machine learning to work on practical high - impact projects to improve the Bank’s performance.

  • You will have direct engagement with C-suites and senior management and work cross-functionally with business units, i.e. Corporate, Retail, Trade Finance & FX, and Risks.
  • This is a unique opportunity to work with multidisciplinary team of creative individuals, focusing on TMB’s core business agendas.
  • The team leverages on our massive dataset of customers to provide insights on strategies and tactical actions to take TMB forward in a digital journey to improve customer life and transform TMB toward data-driven organization.


Qualification and Knowledge :

  • A degree in Data Science, Engineering, Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research or equivalent with at least 1 years of work experience
  • Advanced knowledge of machine learning and big data analytics.
  • Familiarity with unstructured data, NLP, Text Mining, Graph Theory, Deep Learning
  • Technical Skills : Python, R, Tableau, SQL.
  • Experiences with Hadoop, PySpark, Hive, Scala, Kafka, Impala etc.
  • Strong passion and ability to ask relevant business questions to leverage the Bank’s customer and external data
  • Proactive personality with initiative, resourceful, enthusiasm, and able to think on his own idea
  • Strong capabilities to communicate clearly both in writing and presentations in Thai and English
