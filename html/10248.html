Engineer

Manpower UK Thatcham, GB

1 week ago

Apply on company website
The Engineer will report directly to the Senior Engineer/Engineering Manager on site. They will be accountable for the completion of work as set by the Senior Engineer/Engineering Manager, including ensuring that the work is completed in a quality and timely manner. They will provide support in relation to all maintenance tasks and projects being undertaken, including day to day breakdown, PPM, equipment updates, new installations, and building/infrastructure upgrades. The position requires flexibility on hours as the pattern being plant specific and on-call work to provide cover for emergency breakdowns and shutdown periods.

Key Requirements
  • Ability to develop effective working relationships across management and other staff
  • Flexible and able to react quickly to the needs of the business
  • Problem solving/analytical ability
  • Must possess a recognised qualification relevant to the role (e.g. mechanical / electrical C&G)
  • Skilled in both Electrical and Mechanical trades: Apprenticeship with indentures preferred.

Key Responsibilities
  • Ensure the company assets are maintained to achieve maximum efficiency.
  • Excellent communication between Production staff and co-workers.
  • Undertake planned maintenance tasks as instructed to the relevant engineering and safety standards.
  • Undertake remedial engineering works to equipment or site as instructed by the Engineering Manager.
  • To ensure any risks of safety or failure of equipment or site are communicated to the Manager.
  • All activities are carried out in accordance with the Site Health and Safety instructions and the SSG Health and Safety manual, as well as in accordance with relevant engineering standards.
