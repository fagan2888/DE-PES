    • WHAT WE LOOK FOR


    We are looking for talented cloud data engineer with a deep understanding of Python/Java to join our young and international team based in Barcelona.


    As part of our team you will be responsible for providing outstanding technical support for Google Cloud Platform big data products (such as BigQuery, Dataflow, Pub/Sub, Dataproc, Composer...) to worldwide clients.



    Minimum requirements:

    • 100% fluent in English (minimum C1)

    • European citizenship or work permit in Spain

    • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, software/computer engineering, applied mathematics, physics, statistics, or applicable work experience.

    • Experience with Linux/UNIX

    • Understanding of basic web technologies (e.g. TCP/IP, DNS, HTTP, etc.).

    • In-depth programming skills in Python or Java

    • Experience with relational SQL and NoSQL databases

    • Understanding of streaming data architectures and technologies for real-time data processing


    Desirable skills:

    • Data warehousing and ETL Tools

    • Experience with big data tools (e.g. Hadoop, Spark, Kafka, HBase, etc.)

    • Experience in Machine Learning

    • Experience with code repositories

    • Experience with cloud providers (Google Cloud Platform, Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure)



    WHAT YOU WILL GET

    • Permanent contract

    • Full time position (39 hours / week)

    • Salary package: 30.115€ gross/year (25.000 as base salary + 3.000 as bonus + 1.540 ticket restaurant + 575 as private health insurance with Adeslas)

    • Total salary package will go up to 30.615€ gross/year after 6 month in the project. From that moment your salary will be reviewed every 6 month based on you performance

    • Two months of paid training

    • Continuous training and certification within Google products

    • Ticket restaurant

    • 23 days of vacation and day off on your birthday

    • Free fresh fruit and coffee

    • Ping pong, arcade machine, pool table and punching bag

    • Teamwork in a multicultural environment, social activities and personal development

    • Relocation package


    You will be working from Monday to Friday from 9am to 6pm.

    You will also be working one weekend every two month being extra paid in case that you do so. The weekend working shift could either be from 9am to 6 pm or from 6am to 3pm (according to necessity).
