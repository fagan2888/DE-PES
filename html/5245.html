Data Scientist

Easybrain Cyprus

3 weeks ago 172 applicants

We are looking for a specialist who will become one of the key members in Business Intelligence team of Easybrain. You will work on BI tools used to analyze monetization, user acquisition and mobile applications development.

Responsibilities:

- Development of machine learning prototypes to be used in the daily operations of BI and company’s decision making process;

- Working with large amounts of data from multiple sources to predict users’ LTV and other key metrics;

- Search and proposal of new approaches to increase the accuracy of forecast of key business indicators;

- Proposal of new data sources and optimization of the existing ones to improve the quality of analytics and model creation.

Requirements:

- Experience in analyzing large amounts of data and predictive modelling;

- Structural approach to data analysis;

- Profound understanding of statistics and machine learning technologies;

- English language proficiency (intermediate level or higher);

- Russian language proficiency is a must;

- Experience in advertising technologies and mobile games will be a plus.

Conditions:

In addition to the engaging tasks, support from experienced colleagues, the opportunity to realise your own ideas, challenges and drive, we offer:

- High salary + KPI bonuses;

- Best office in Limassol (The Oval), modern equipment;

- Snacks and drinks, gym in the office building, sea view, beach bar 100 metres from the workplace, attendance of key industry events;

- Medical insurance;

- Sports reimbursement;

- English lessons;

- Relocation package to the country with 300 sunny days a year;

- Fast-paced and easy-going environment.
