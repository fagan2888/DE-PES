Organization: Plan International Canada

Reports to: Director, Business Solutions and Infrastructure

Location: Toronto (Mount Pleasant & Eglinton)

Status: Full-time, Permanent

Contact Level: B


What can you expect from joining a leading organization in the global movement to advance children’s rights and equality for girls?


An inspiring team of intelligent disruptors that challenges themselves to Defy Normal – to be bold, insightful, focused and innovative.


We are one of Canada’s Most Admired Cultures and here’s why: together, we have created a climate of trust and inclusivity – where diversity in people, ideas and approaches is not only respected, it’s encouraged. We are empowered to command change and build powerful partnerships, with support from passionate leaders to unleash our full potential and learn, lead, decide and thrive. Further information can be found at: plancanada.ca


Be Part of Plan International Canada’s Digital Journey

We are about to begin a period of change with a new emphasis on digital which is centred around 4 key elements:


• People : The right people with the right skills focused on creating value for all stakeholders by leveraging the tools available to them.

• Processes : Efficient internal processes to improve service delivery and external processes focused on creating high-calibre constituent experiences across all channels

• Data : Leverage data-driven insights to drive faster decision-making to be agile in delivery

• Technology : Leverage systems and tools that support innovation and impactful experiences for our constituents and enable our staff to operate as efficiently as possible


Supporting both our Digital Journey initiative and existing IT operations, Plan International Canada is looking for a highly motivated and committed team member capable of working in a dynamic environment with time-sensitive requests and multiple work streams running in parallel.


The Position

  • Data Architecture

oDevelop and own detailed data models for logical and conceptual database designs in support of an enterprise data governance model aligned to business requirements

oDesign and implement database solutions and models to store and retrieve data

oExamine and identify database structural necessities by evaluating client operations, applications, and programming


  • Solution & Integration Architecture

oDevelop solution integration architecture for current and future applications to facilitate integration of SaaS or in-house development projects

o  Analyze existing or proposed enhancement requests to determine their feasibility and time estimates required

o  Responsibility for solving highly technical and complex problems while maintaining a high level of influence on projects and internal customers


  • Technical Leadership, Standards, Best-Practices

oSupport the definition of an architecture framework and standards based on Plan International Canada’s digital roadmap, business strategy and current business solution needs

oBuild consensus, facilitate collaboration and decision-making within and across business units and technology teams

oEnsure projects comply with technology domain architecture, design and operational processes against technology architecture and development standards.

oCoaches, mentors, and provides technical leadership to developers, vendors, business analysts and data analysts to facilitate their work and foster high performing technical teams


  • Solution Evaluation / Project Delivery Support

oSupport project delivery teams to answer questions and remove any design roadblocks to enable a successful implementation

oIdentify and research relevant technologies, perform Proof of Concepts, and recommend applications of such technologies to future product architectures.

oSupport design and migration of applications into cloud-based offerings.


As our ideal candidate you will have:

  • Excellent interpersonal, written and verbal communication skills
  • Computer Science or Engineering degree or equivalent professional experience
  • Prior experience as a data architect, integration architect, senior software engineer, or senior systems designer
  • Successfully developed and documented an integration architecture focused on CRM, financial systems and digital tools (Marketing automation, CMS, eCommerce)
  • Experience with data migration or digital transformation projects
  • Experience leveraging integration platforms similar to MuleSoft, Talend, Snaplogic, etc.
  • Proven ability to create innovative solutions to solve complex business requirements leveraging internal capabilities or SaaS solutions
  • Experience with Azure-based cloud storage and warehouse solutions is a strong asset
  • Understanding of Blackbaud CRM and Salesforce applications would be desirable
  • Experience in designing and developing solutions for mobile, web and cloud-based applications, i.e. cloud computing, service models


How to apply:

To express interest in this exciting opportunity to join a dynamic organization and a high performing team please forward your resume and cover letter to gethired@plancanada.ca by November 3, 2019 . Please reference Data and Integration Architect in the subject line .


Plan International Canada is an inclusive workplace, and is committed to championing accessibility, diversity and equal opportunity. Requests for accommodation can be made at any stage of the recruitment process providing the applicant has met the bona-fide requirements for the open position. Applicants need to make their requirements known when contacted.

Consistent with our Safeguarding Children and Young People Policy, the successful candidate must receive clearance by a police background check, including the vulnerable sector screen.

Applicants must be eligible to work in Canada for the duration of the work term. Proof of eligibility will be required.

Plan International Canada sincerely thanks all applicants for their expressed interest in this opportunity however only those selected for an interview will be contacted. No phone calls please.
