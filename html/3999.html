Job Description

We are seeking a dynamic ETL Developer / Data Engineer to join our growing SaaS organization. The ETL Developer / Data Engineer will work closely with our software developers and client on-boarding and delivery teams to build tools and write scripts focused on importing and exporting data from different systems into and out of our property tax software, itamlink.

The successful candidate will need to have the ability to design and create their own tools, use existing ETL tools as well as model and extract data via SQL scripts.

Main Responsibilities
  • Write conversion scripts based on data mapping documentation to import new customer data.
  • Develop tools to assist with data conversion between external systems and our product, itamlink.
  • Develop tools within itamlink to assist with large data manipulations.
  • Assist the Customer Success Team with data mapping and onboarding of new customers.
  • Insert, update and remove database data on request.
  • Analyze sets fo data using statistical methods to generate useful queries and reports.
  • Develop data analytics to gather insight into operations and customer behavior.
  • Assist with the design and development of API’s to facilitate data exchange.
  • Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent.
  • Strong knowledge and experience with PostgresSQL RDBMS.
  • Strong knowledge and experience with Java.
  • Experience with Microsoft Excel including VBA.
  • Ability to analyze key performance indicators/metrics.
  • Ability to perform critical thinking in the area of data analysis.
  • Flexible, self-motivated and able to meet deadlines.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Strong attention to detail.
Desired Skills & Abilities
  • Statistical skills and knowledge of a variety of analytical approaches to apply to different problems.
  • Prior experience working with and/or designing APIs.
  • Experience with python based data science libraries such as anaconda, scikit-learn or tensorflow, etc.
Who We Are

We are the industry leading developer and vendor of web based, real estate asset management software focused on property tax and assessment. We are re-imagining how companies tackle one of the largest expense line items on their financials. Our clients, including many in the Fortune 500, trust us to provide them with the latest technology and superior customer service to help them reach their property tax and financial goals.

We would like to thank all applicants for their interest. However, only suitable candidates will be contacted further. **
