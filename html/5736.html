System Engineer

Otto International Kowloon, Hong Kong SAR

4 days ago

Apply
  • Provide broad technical support
  • Maintain global infrastructure
  • Design the future system with automation solutions


As a System Engineer, you will provide technical support across a broad range of technologies from managing servers, operating systems, applications, network that support the infrastructure development (both on-premises and cloud) to different IT applications. You will have a strong desire to automate, lead, and be comfortable to take initiatives in the development process.

How will you make a difference?


  • Contribute the infrastructure from system implementation, administration, maintenance and support by 90% focus on Linux platform and 10% focus on Windows
  • Support the system performance tuning and technical infrastructures such as servers, network and security systems
  • Design the future system with automation solutions while maintaining the current global infrastructure
  • Make recommendations for improvement to increase productivity on IT operations
  • Outline and document system operation procedures
  • Collaborate with other teams in support of the project goal and provide support during non-office hours occasionally


What are we looking for?


  • Degree holder in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or related discipline
  • 3 years of experience in administrating and supporting infrastructure/server/cloud environment e.g. Linux, CentOS, Ubuntu
  • Familiarity with AWS/ MS O365/ MS Azure AD Administration
  • Good concept of technical infrastructure environment on-premises and cloud
  • Experience with DevOps, SysOps, Hybrid cloud environment preferred but not mandatory
  • Strong analytical and excellent problem-solving skills in system administration and documentation
  • Self-motivated, dynamic, flexible, result-oriented
  • Ability to work with a wide range of stakeholders, take initiatives and run projects under minimal supervision
  • Good communication skills in English (written and spoken)


Why work for us?


  • Collaborative, fun and challenging work environment
  • Open, transparent and diverse culture with 24 offices worldwide
  • Double-pay, performance bonus, flexible working hours
  • Full healthcare insurance



At Otto International, it’s not just a job, it’s a mindset.
