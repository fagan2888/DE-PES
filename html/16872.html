Rakuten Mobile, Inc. is an entity established for the launch of its mobile carrier business as a MNO (Mobile Network Operator.)

We aim to provide the most attractive and convenient service that meets customers’ needs and demands by its own innovative technology and spirit.

In order to produce future world-standard innovations, we challenge the best of our own possibilities and address to build a workplace with an environment and system full of opportunities.

We're looking for talented individuals who can take on and enjoy this challenge with us.

Rakuten Mobile is now preparing brand new mobile network as a fourth mobile operator in Japan. For enhancing and improving business process for this mobile network service, we are currently looking for a skilled project manager of tool development.

Responsibilities

Looking for the architect who can develop the future infrastructure on Rakuten Mobile platform.

  • Ability to design and configure the IT system infrastructure base on business requirement.
  • Ability to make the research and run the PoC to new technology.
  • Ability to create the system monitoring scheme.
  • Good response to urgent or trouble happens in responsible system.
  • Ability to learn and catch up to existing system.
  • Eager to commonize the new technology and automate the unneeded operation.

    • Required Qualifications
    • Knowledge of TCP/IP, HTTP, DNS, etc Network protocol and designing.
    • Basic knowledge of web security
    • Experience of development of infrastructure based on Linux OS
    • Experience of designing and development of system monitoring.
    • Experience of operational automation using Shell, Python or others.
    • Experience of deploy automation using Chef, Ansible, Terraform or others.
    • Experience of developing infrastructure with deep consideration of reliability, availability.
    • Experience of trouble shooting operation.O31

    Preferred Qualifications
    • Experience of Web Application development
    • Experience of operating Database(RDB, NOSQL)
    • Experience creating CI/CD process
    • Experience of Scrum or Agile development
    • Knowledge of Docker, Kubernates
