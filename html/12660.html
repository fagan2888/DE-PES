Seeking a DevOps engineer with strong production support background to build automation into Big Data delivery

Your New Company

This industry-leading bank is looking for a passionate DevOps Engineer candidate to join them in building a new cloud data lake.

Your new role
  • Support and Incident Management. Diagnosing production issues and performing root cause analysis. Follow the Incident process, track and report on the incident status as it occurs.
  • Participate in out of hours on-call support (rostered).
  • Creation and maintenance of delivery pipeline tooling. Including build/deploy/CI/infrastructure scripts, monitoring, logging
  • Building automated provisioning that can setup secure cloud environments
  • Continually learning and researching best practices and tooling for Big Data delivery
  • Automation is built-in into every aspect of delivery as a core component, not an afterthought.
What you'll need to succeed
  • Strong infrastructure production support experience. L1 and L2.
  • Experience with monitoring tools (e.g. Cloudwatch, Splunk)
  • AWS experience
  • Deploying and managing Big Data solutions (Hadoop clusters, ETL processing, etc)
  • Infrastructure provisioning automation (Terraform, Ansible)
  • Jenkins/Hudson experience (or similar CI tools)
  • Multiple roles available, both senior and junior.
What you'll get in return
Your expertise will allow you to contribute to an exciting team within a market leader as well as obtaining a competitive market salary package. You will be entitled to all full-time permanent position benefits and be working in a happy workplace where all employees are rewarded and recognized.
