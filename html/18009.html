    • We're ushering in the era of Data Intelligence.

    We’re shaping the way companies manage data by helping customers connect the right data, insights, and algorithms for all Data Citizens. When everyone across the organization is enabled with data, true transformation can take place. We are building a team of exceptional people to help us deliver on that promise. If you are interested in a career at the leading edge of technology, we look forward to hearing from you.

    How You'll Impact Collibra

    In order to support our current and future growth, we are looking for a top-notch QA Engineer. You will be part of our QA team in an environment focused on quality and a sane combination of automated and manual testing. You will take on many responsibilities.

    A day in the life of a QA Engineer at Collibra:
    • Review requirement specifications and user stories to provide timely and meaningful feedback
    • Help in creation of Acceptance Criteria and improvement of product documentation
    • Create a detailed, comprehensive and well-structured test plans and test cases
    • Ensure quality on every level of software life-cycle
    • Participate in test automation process (Selenium, API tests) - optional
    • Identify, document and track bugs
    • Perform thorough regression testing when bugs are resolved
    • Follow the team on the way towards continuous testing


    Why We Need You
    • You have a strong knowledge of software QA methodologies, tools (JIRA) and processes (ISTQB is a nice to have)
    • You have strong Business Analytical skills
    • You're able to write clear, concise and comprehensive test plans and test cases
    • You have hands-on experience with black box testing, acceptance testing, requirements analysis
    • You're experienced in working in an Agile/Scrum development processes
    • You have knowledge about continuous delivery/continuous testing environment
    • You have a general knowledge about GDPR and/or other Data Privacy regulations - optional
    • You have 1+ year experience in automated testing tools (Selenium), API testing, Java
    • You're eager to learn relevant business knowledge
    • You are a team player and know how to contribute to the process
    • You are flexible and proactive
    • You have good communication skills and are able to collaborate across teams
    • You're proficient in English


    Why You Want Us

    We offer...
    • The opportunity to be part of a successful, fast-growing, acknowledged market-leading, unicorn company that has been named the "Best Data Driven SaaS Product" at the 2018 SaaS Awards and acknowledged on the Forbes 2018 Cloud 100 List
    • The chance to have a big impact on the company in its most robust period by building and managing a strong team and participating in the creation of a great enterprise platform
    • Working in a startup-minded environment with highly skilled professionals using cutting edge tech (Java 11, Spring, Hibernate, Apache Kafka, Elastic Search, PostgreSQL, ReactJS, Apache Spark, Kubernetes, Docker and many other)
    • A friendly, agile environment where your input is valued and growth is fostered
    • Ongoing opportunities to collaborate with fellow Collibrians globally
    • Flexible hours = quality > quantity
    • Bi-weekly catered lunches. Never go hungry again!
    • Did we mention happy hour?
    • A spacious office easily accessible the centre of Wrocław
    • A MacBook pro
    • And of course, a competitive salary with a strong benefits package
