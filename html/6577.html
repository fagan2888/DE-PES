Job Summary

This position will ensure that purchased parts and assemblies are processed and tooled to meet optimum quality, quantity, and cost requirements. This position will develop and implement supplier quality work assignments. Help provide leadership in developing strategies to enhance the quality of incoming product while meeting corporate metrics.

Job Responsibilities
  • Demonstrates in depth conceptual and practical knowledge in Supplier Quality and basic knowledge of related areas such as Advanced Product Quality Planning (APQP) and sign off of the Production Part Approval Process (PPAP).
  • Impacts quality within suppliers by identifying, initiating and completing necessary corrective/preventive actions to achieve desired performance targets.
  • Solves complex problems related to Supplier Quality and enacts a new perspective on existing solutions. Works independently and receives minimal guidance.
  • Acts as a resource for Supplier Quality Analysts with less experience. Responsible for ongoing activities, objectives and may lead projects or steps within a broader project.
  • Communicates difficult or sensitive information and builds relationships with external suppliers and internal stakeholders.
