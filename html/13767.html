**This role is located in Brisbane. We're looking for candidates already in Brisbane or willing to relocate for an exciting Permanent Opportunity**


As a Senior Data Engineer you will shape the Data Solutions in our Gaming Technology team

  • Develop a strong Data Engineering culture
  • Fantastic & fun team
  • Permanent opportunity

You will help shape the design and development of data solutions whilst also supporting existing business intelligence solutions. Coaching, motivating and mentoring other engineers, you’ll make getting great solutions to our customers smoother (and faster) than ever. You’ll improve every element of delivery and build, pinpointing opportunities to improve how we work together across technology.

Our tech teams deliver gaming moments that pack a punch – for industry partners and their customers. Think intuitive, joined-up, real-time digital experiences that make it easy for customers to choose the play that’s right for them. Not forgetting the support we provide to thousands of venues across Australia. That means maintaining, updating and creating leading-edge gaming software. Life here is about using technology to make great moments better, as well as developing all-new digital experiences. It’s about shaping an industry. Let’s get started.

To be successful in this role you will need the following experience:

  • Design, implement, and maintain cloud-based data platforms providing access to large datasets
  • Resolve critical and complex technical issues within AWS and on premises data warehouses and pipelines
  • Build robust and scalable data integration (ETL) pipelines using SQL and AWS data pipelines
  • Collaborate with business customers, gathering requirements and delivering complete data asset solutions
  • Continually improve ongoing reporting and analysis processes, automating or simplifying self-service support for customers and technology teams
  • Identifying and evaluating industry innovations, trends and offerings, act as a SME and be at the forefront of data engineering practices
  • Manage and co-ordinate between internal business partners, acknowledging Security, Network, Architecture and Service Management requirements.
  • Ensure solution quality
  • Operationalise Machine Learning Models

What you’ll bring:

  • Expert in cloud-based data platform and data warehouse solutions (AWS Redshift, Azure SQL
  • DWH, GCP BigQuery, Snowflake)
  • Expert in cloud-based data integration tools (Azure Data Factory, AWS Glue, GCP DataFlow)
  • Expert in using business intelligence reporting tools
  • Strong experience building/operating highly available, distributed systems of data extraction, ingestion, and processing of large data sets
  • Strong experience in software development, data engineering, business intelligence, data science, or related field with a track record of manipulating, processing, and extracting value from large complex datasets
  • Experience in providing technical leadership and mentoring to other engineers for best practices on data engineering
  • Experience in communicating with users, other technical teams, and senior management on data engineering strategy and data management best practice
  • Experience in designing for security and compliance
  • Experience in ensuring fidelity and reliability
  • Experience in designing and implementing for flexibility and portability

If this sounds like you, please send through your CV in word format by clicking on APPLY NOW quoting Ref No: 2004588
