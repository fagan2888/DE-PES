    • At FLYR , we're changing the way airline industries use data to ease the traveler’s experience — from dynamic pricing, elastic demand forecasting, to bundling the best offers for end users. We’re taking off on a mission to deliver a real-time prediction and tracking platform to major travel industry partners, each with millions of consumers hungry for a new, first-rate travel shopping experience. To achieve this vision, we’re looking for a talented & diverse Software Engineer who is willing to specialize in data engineering to join our rapidly growing startup in San Francisco & Kraków, Poland.


    Our Data Engineering team is responsible for all things data - architecting and implementing mission-critical data pipelines, engineering improvements to our real-time models, and maintaining high quality code standards to improve the accuracy of our algorithms. As an Engineering team, we strongly believe in fostering an environment for intellectually interesting work that advances your career progression on this journey. Let’s get you on board soon.



    Roles & Responsibilities:

    • Development of data processing platform, primarily writing code in Python
    • Development of scalable and low-latency data pipelines to integrate clients data warehouses with our data warehouse
    • Cooperate with our Business Data Analysts and Data Engineers



    About you:

    • 3 years of hands-on experience in backend software development
    • 1 year of experience with data pipeline development (hands-on writing code to processing the data)
    • Experience with cloud platforms and their solutions
    • Experience with CI/CD development
    • Experience with the version control system
    • Good knowledge of software design principles
    • Good knowledge of a popular object-oriented or functional programming language
    • Good knowledge of SQL databases and their query languages
    • Good written and oral communication skills in English



    Desirable skills:

    • Deep knowledge of Python
    • Experience with following Apache projects: Apache Airflow, Apache Spark, Apache Kafka
    • Experience with Google Cloud Platform
    • Experience with Docker and Kubernetes
    • Airlines domain knowledge



    What we can offer you?

    • Regular employment contract (or B2B)
    • Flexible working hours
    • Working from home
    • Work equipment to choose (laptop /macOS or Linux/, two monitors, keyboard and mouse)
    • Medical care
    • Sport card
    • And more!


    About FLYR product

    FusionRM, our dynamic revenue management platform, used by major commercial airlines both internationally and domestically. FusionRM unlocks unique opportunities by leveraging a forecasting platform that was built from the ground up to use machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques throughout an airline's commerce funnel. Our platform is growing with strong customer demand for this game-changing tool.
