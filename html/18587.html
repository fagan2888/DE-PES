Roles & Responsibilities

YOUR ROLE

Since 2014, Group Finance has embarked on a major transformation program (Group Finance Architecture Program, (GFAP) to uplift our end-to-end finance capability, upgrade our Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) platform, automate our regulatory reporting and build up our analytics capability.

In 2017, under GFAP, a new stream of work has commenced to review and improve our cost allocation methodology and processes across the bank. The objective of this program is to develop the framework, processes and associated management tools for deeper, actionable insights on our costs to with a significant level of granularity and transparency.

The delivery of this complex program requires simulating the results using a prototype model. This work requires a very capable and highly motivated Business Analyst to own, develop and enhance the prototype model, as well as customise it as required for rollout of the new cost allocation design to other countries.

Principle Responsibilities
  • Own, develop and enhance the prototype cost allocation model
    • Taking over the existing model and making further modifications / enhancements as required
    • Ability to ‘join the dots’ take a business view on data by leveraging knowledge of the team, understanding methodology and creating linkages across diverse data sets
  • Customise the prototype as required for rolling out the new cost allocation design to other countries
Implementing modifications / enhancements to the prototype as required for each country

Key deliverables: Develop and Document a fit-for-purpose working prototype model to simulate the impact of the new Cost Allocation methodology

Requirements

We are looking for a candidate with 3-5+ years of experience as a Business Analyst; candidates with degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Information Systems will be preferred.

The candidate should also have experience using the following software/tools:
  • Experience with object-oriented function scripting languages: Python , C++
  • Experience with common Python libraries toolkits, such as NumPy, Pandas etc
  • Experience with statistical computer languages (e.g. Python, R) to manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets.
  • Experience building and optimizing 'big data' pipelines, data architectures and data sets.
  • Advanced working SQL knowledge and experience working with relational databases, query authoring (SQL) as well as working familiarity with a variety of databases (Optional)

The Candidate Should Also Demonstrate The Following Qualities
  • Strong business acumen
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills: able to communicate ideas, conclusions and recommendations clearly and concisely
  • Structured approach to problem-solving: able to break down a problem in a logical, structured manner and develop appropriate solutions / options
  • Strong drive: self-starter with high level of energy, commitment and focus to drive results
