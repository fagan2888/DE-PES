    Join Eaton and help us provide energy-efficient solutions that have a real impact on the environment. We make what matters work. Find out more on https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=baa_aiJ4L7E

    Do you want to work for a global player, where we care about ethics, inclusion and diversity and our people?Whether it is planes, hospitals, factories, data centres, vehicles or even the electrical grid, our products and solutions are there to make it work and you will too!

    As a Senior Data Scientist you will be directly involved in the application of machine learning, statistics, and analytic approaches to answer challenging business questions. This work will help businesses across the enterprise solve critical business problems, uncover new business opportunities, and turn their large volumes of data into valuable, actionable insights. You will be part of a global world class innovation team and will interact with many different business units in EMEA.

    We’re now actively recruiting Data Scientists at all levels for the start date 2019/2020.

    Your Key Deliverables
    • Derive, interpret and effectively deliver the results of data analysis
    • Understand traditional and new data analysis methods
    • Utilize a broad range of techniques in machine learning, data mining, statistics, and big data interrogation methods
    • Work directly with business stakeholders, engineering, and test to create high quality solutions that solve end-user problems
    • Evaluate business requirements to determine potential solutions
    • Assist other engineering teams with their data science initiatives

    Qualifications

    Do you have? :
    • At least Master’s degree in computer science, software engineering, statistics, applied mathematics, engineering or physics. PhD is highly desirable.
    • Previous experience in developing and designing technology solutions
    • Practical applications or machine learning or statistics to diagnostics, prognostics, pattern detection, data mining, etc.
    • Familiarity with Python, R, and/or other programming languages
    • Excellent verbal and written communication skills and ability to quickly learn new domains
    Then we want to hear about you!
