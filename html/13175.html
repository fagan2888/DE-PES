    About Grid Edge

    Grid Edge is changing the way that people use energy by putting intelligent, data-driven control into the hands of building operators. Based in Birmingham, we are an award-winning AI start-up company developing machine-learning-based software services that empower building operators to predict, optimise and control their energy and carbon profile. We are working with some of the world’s leading companies who share our vision for putting empowered consumers at the heart of the 21st century energy system.


    Job Description:

    You will be responsible for managing a diverse and complex pipeline of inward and outward data from our data science core and to our consumer service. We gather data from some of the most modern and secure IoT and SCADA process technologies all the way through to building systems that have not been updated since the moon landings. We aim to deliver a robust service that helps our customers reduce their carbon footprint and accelerate the decarbonisation of our global energy systems. You will work closely with all the technical parts of the business: you will facilitate and improve the smooth capture and ingress of data into our systems, you will contribute to the cleaning and conditioning of that data, you will work with our data scientists to ensure they have robust and reliable data for experimentation, you will work with our software team to ensure they are working most efficiently with the correct, reliable data sets and services and you will work with our management function to report on the performance of the product.


    Key responsibilities will include:


    • Ingress of data: Deployment and commissioning of new data coming in from site hardware; Provisioning of new third party and customer specific API data; Handling of new versions of internal API services


    • Data management: Design and upkeep of a data pipeline using cloud technologies


    • Egress of data: Test and prepare data for the front end that will be displayed to the customer; Create contracts for our APIs


    • Service maintenance: Maintaining and deploying Kubernetes and serverless technologies for processing functions; maintenance and management of our cloud databases.



    Must haves:

    The candidate will need to have experience in:

    • Python
    • Linux
    • ORM
    • Kubernetes
    • Docker or similar
    • Cloud computing
    • Testing and test suites
    • Redis
    • REST APIs


    Desirables:

    • Projects that you have led where you have been responsible for data engineering
    • Experience of working in agile working environments (we use Atlassian/JIRA)
    • Experience of building and maintaining testing environments


    Salary & Benefits:

    Competitive Salary, plus: Performance Bonus, Employer Pension, 25 Days Annual Leave (plus your birthday off), Personal Development Budget for training and development


    If you are creative, curious and looking for an exciting and dynamic place to work, we’d love to hear from you.
