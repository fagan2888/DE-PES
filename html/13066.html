Lead Data Analyst & Data Analyst

Equifax Melbourne, AU

5 days ago

Apply on company website

Were currently recruiting for a Data Analyst , as well as a Lead Data Analyst .

We are looking for people with hands-on experience, who would like to broaden their skill-sets to sell and deliver Equifax services to external clients.

The successful applicants will work with external clients to evaluate collection and recovery opportunities, determine how much value Equifax can add and then assist with the planning on how to achieve desired outcomes.

This role will be mostly internal, although there will be some customer contact (with various banks, telcos, government departments and utilities). Whilst there are no direct reports into the Lead Data Analyst role the Lead will be responsible for driving the evaluation and implementation of solutions. You will be experienced, confident/free thinking, responsible, creative, solution focused and have an ability to coordinate people who can deliver change.

Both positions should see the successful growth of the Equifax Debt Services business, i.e.:

  • Drive portfolio liquidation performance on existing collections and recoveries portfolios using our platforms and data
  • Support the Equifax Debt Services sales process to acquire new clients including processing data for proposals and pricing

So, what will impress us for the Data Analyst roles?

  • Science, mathematics, engineering or numerical degree level qualification
  • Demonstrable attention to detail and understanding of statistics
  • Proficient in database environments and data set manipulation
  • Firm understanding of SQL & Excel VBA
  • Problem solver, MI (Management Information) creation & analytical skills
  • Experience delivering high quality analytical outputs

What would really impress us in addition to the above:

  • Competent using visualisation tools such as Tableau
  • Analytical experience: collections, recoveries, operational call centres or vintage analysis
  • Analytical tools such as R or Python
  • Six sigma style improvement techniques

What's in it for you?

This is a great chance for you to join a fun, high-energy, adaptable and professional team who love what we do. We also provide you with:

  • We provide an opportunity that transitions people from analysts into industry specialists. Our aim is to ensure that our analysts are experts in their field, and we encourage individuals to work across business lines to broaden their capabilities.
  • Work/life balance & flexibility, employees consistently rate this category as five stars
  • Curated learning opportunities available through Equifax's online training portal. We will also fully support further educational opportunities on a case by case basis
  • Income Protection Insurance - benefit payment for up to 75% of salary up to two years
  • You are eligible for a day off for your birthday, paid floating holiday and a paid volunteer day
  • Final Friday of every month we facilitate a company-wide gathering/celebration (drinks/canapes)
  • Stunning offices in central locations
  • BUPA Corporate Healthcare Plan
  • Southern Cross Healthcare Plan
  • Multiple local and international career opportunities given our 10,000+ employees in 24 countries. Offices in London, New York, Paris, Atlanta & Dublin to name just a few
  • Several recognition programs
  • Referral reward - successfully refer a new employee and receive monetary compensation
  • Employee Assistance Program free counselling services
  • Novated lease options available

Finally, a little about us:

Founded over 100 years ago, Equifax employs 10,000+ employees across 24 countries and is regarded as a global leader within information solutions. Equifax uses data, analytics and technology to provide first-class products for its customers. One billion consumers and over ninety million business customers worldwide rely on Equifax to organise, assimilate and analyse data. Equifax is also recognised as one of the three largest credit agencies in the world and has grown from a consumer credit company into a leading provider of insights that helps its customers make more informed decisions.

Put simply, Equifax helps to power the financial future of individuals and organisations around the world.
