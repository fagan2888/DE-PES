Senior Network Engineer at Cisco

Soitron Košice, SK

2 months ago

Apply on company website
Engineer will spend the work time at customers Network Operations Center (NOC) in Kosice (Slovakia). In that role the engineer is involved in high-touch technical consulting roles with the customer, demonstrates deep technical capability, together with the ability to fully understand all aspects of a customer’s technical and business environment.

Information About The Position
  • Analysis of complex network designs and diagnosis of networking problems.
  • Act as the technical specialist and lead for complex customer environments.
  • Strong business acumen, including the ability to fully understand customer's overall technical and business needs.
  • Ability to communicate with different customer instances including also different management levels.
  • Good collaboration with the service delivery team for customer T-Systems.
Job Requirements:
  • Typically requires BS, CS, University degree or equivalent plus 5+ years experience in network engineering or telecommunications support environment.
  • Good knowledge of Cisco product lines, supported technology and networking solutions.
  • Broad technical knowledge of IP networking, data center solutions and virtualization concepts.
  • Engineer must participate in 24 by 7 on-call duty every 3 or 4 weeks alternating with other Cisco engineers, including the ability to reach customer’s site within two hours after being alerted.
Desired Skills:
  • Senior client-facing skills to include cross-functional influence, external and internal consulting / partnering.
  • Excellent written and verbal communications skills; ability to communicate technology strategy and architecture approach to engineers, executives and senior management at the customer and inside Cisco.
  • Cross-functional contribution and impact with development engineering on product development and serviceability design.
  • Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert (CCIE) certification in at least one track relevant for the unified infrastructure area is a plus
Benefits:
  • Access to Cisco’s library and knowledge portals
  • Internal trainings
  • Possibility to work directly for Cisco after 18months
  • Work for modern and flexible company with open and friendly atmosphere
  • Interesting salary evaluation depending on seniority, skills and performance
  • Continuous improvement as a life style choice
  • Strong opportunity of career development (90% of careers rises and promotions come from internal sources)
  • Huge benefits program with possibility to personalize it according to your preferences
Information about the company: We are currently one of the largest IT service providers in Central and Eastern Europe with offices in Slovakia, Czech Republic, Bulgaria, Romania, Turkey and Poland. We employ over 800 qualified and experienced specialists from our state of the art delivery centers and technical training facilities. We are strategic partners with leading technology companies including: Cisco, Microsoft, Oracle, Avaya, IBM and HP to name a few. Soitron is also one of the very few Cisco Master Unified Communications accredited companies in Europe. More about us at www.soitron.com.
