Senior DevOps/Middleware Engineer


Responsibilities:

  • The position involves installation of various Middleware Products, creating, configuring, deploying and troubleshooting monitoring solutions; and providing deployment implementation best practices.
  • Will be responsible for product upgrades and installations.
  • Provides subject matter expertise for modeling, simulation and analysis efforts.
  • Develops new documentation, departmental technical procedures and user guides. Participates in projects and assures quality, security and compliance requirements are met for supported area and oversees testing as well as creation of updates to business continuation plan.
  • Application code deployment.
  • Application monitoring and systems health checks and response times including responding to pages and alerts.
  • Lead production problem resolution including troubleshooting, documentation and communication as related to the product installation.
  • Experience in Performance Tuning, Capacity Planning, Cluster Design/implementation and troubleshooting.
  • Excellent skills on JVM and its memory management, diagnostics and configuration.
  • Middleware Certificate Management expertise.
  • Effectively manage multiple, competing, high-priority projects with varying deadlines
  • Able to fix the issues meeting the SLA.
  • Hands-on development of DevOps and automation tools
  • Define repeatable patterns/tasks and automate them
  • Stay current, research and recommend new tools needed
  • Collaborate with multiple teams to ensure automation adheres to best practices and establish standards where necessary
  • Work with development and operations teams to configure and maintain alerts
  • Assist internal team members with tooling application training
  • Develop and update documentation on tools usage
  • Lead the effort for tools (homegrown tools) migration to Cloud Service Provider



Requirements:

  • Proficient in CI/CD practices and tools - Perforce/Git, Jenkins, Maven/Gradle, Nexus and Artifactory.
  • Extensive experience building CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins
  • Experience in automation via shell scripting and using Configuration Management Tools, Perforce, Ansible.
  • Proficient scripting in Python, Bash, or similar.
  • Configuration management experience in Ansible
  • Hands-on development of DevOps and automation tools
  • Experience with Log Analysis and Monitoring tools such as ELK
  • Experience in monitoring tools such as Graphite, Grafana, Prometheus , NewRelic
  • Experience with Azure or similar cloud provider
  • Proficient English skills(90% Level)

Bonus to have:

  • Knowledge of MySQL and non-SQL data stores.
  • Experience with Java or any other programming language.
  • Administration and implementation experience in a high usage, high profile platform environment.
  • Experience in product administration, specifically with Oracle Weblogic, Tomcat, Nginx, Apache


About us:

Bydrec, Inc. is a nearshore software development outsourcing company that provides highly-skilled, bilingual software engineers to clients that have full-time technical outsourcing needs. Founded in 2016 by tech pros with more than 17 years of industry experience, we differentiate ourselves by prioritizing productivity.


We are headquartered in Northern California and operate out of Colombia, South America. Unlike our Asian and European counterparts, we share time zones with U.S. clients, allowing our team members to engage with clients in a more collaborative endeavor. This reduces delays and ensures we deliver on the goals and requirements of projects seamlessly.


By working with Colombian developers, we’re also able to offer high-quality teams for half the cost of U.S.-based talent. Our nearshore outsourcing rates are competitive with Eastern Europe and Asia. We provide dedicated programmers that work full-time as members of your team for a flat, affordable monthly rate.


As industry experts, we know the benefits of companies outsourcing their technical needs for business applications. That’s why we are dedicated to helping give companies a competitive advantage by optimizing efficiency, focusing on excellent customer service and delivering customizable solutions.
