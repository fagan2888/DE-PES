Data Analyst

FROSSELL PARTNERS LIMITED Milton Keynes, GB

1 week ago

Apply on company website
Due to expansion our Prestigious and award:winning company is recruiting for a Data Analyst, proficient in MS Excel to join their highly successful team.

The successful candidate will possess a real passion for data analytics, have a keen eye for detail and ultimately support continuous business improvements.

Main Responsibilities
Scrutinise information using company data analysis tools Pulling meaningful results from raw data and help colleagues and clients make important decisions by identifying various facts and trends Using advanced computerised models to extract the data needed Removing corrupted data Performing initial analysis to assess the quality of the data Performing further analysis to determine the meaning of data Preparing reports based on analysis and supporting the Commercial Data Analyst in presenting to management
Key Competencies

Customer Focus

Dedicated to meeting the expectations and requirements of internal and external customers; acts with customers in mind; establishes and maintains effective relationships with customers and gains their trust and respect.

Action Oriented

Enjoys working hard; is action oriented and full of energy for the things he/she sees as challenging; not fearful of acting with minimal planning; seizes more opportunities than others.

Learning abilities

Learns quickly when facing new problems and is open to change. Analyses successes and failures to identify areas for improvement, and experiments to find new solutions. Enjoys the challenge of unfamiliar tasks and can quickly grasp the essence and underlying structure of anything.

Interpersonal skills

Relates well to all kinds of people, both inside and outside the organisation. Builds appropriate rapport and effective relationships with those they work with. Uses diplomacy and tact when required and can defuse high tension situations comfortably.

Essential Skills

Highly proficient in Excel, filtering, VLOOKUPs, Pivot tables, graphs and understating formulas A high level of mathematical ability The ability to analyse, model and interpret data Problem solving skills A methodical and logical approach and the ability to plan work and meet deadlines Excellent Interpersonal skills A great team player Good written and verbal communication
