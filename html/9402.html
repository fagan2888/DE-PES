Job Description

Our client is a leading MNC focused on International Travel with headquarters in Australia. Due to their expansion they are seeking a Senior Data Engineer to join them based in Bangkok. Outstanding work environment focused on utilizing the latest technologies and delivering cutting edge projects.

In this role, you will be working closely with the Data Warehouse Leader and the team of data experts globally on various projects and iniatitives important for the business. Fantastic opportunity to explore state of the art technologies on world leading cloud platform as well as managing and analyzing a huge stack of data to make a massive impact for the business.

To be considered for this role you MUST have the following experience;

  • Azure Databricks
  • Data Factory Expert
  • Data Engineer experience using Python, SQL, Parquet file format.
  • Large scale data management experience

    • Successful candidate will receive a very competitive remuneration package, work in central Bangkok with easy access to BTS and many more benefits!
