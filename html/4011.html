Senior QA Engineer (Data)

Nasdaq Vilnius, LT

2 weeks ago

Apply on company website

This is an extraordinary opportunity to join a constantly growing and innovative FinTech company where finance and technology meets, producing some of the most low-latency, high-performance and data-intensive systems in the world.


If you are someone who enjoys working in a results-driven and high-performing international culture, and thrive in a creative and dynamic environment, this is the role for you.


Nasdaq Technology is now looking for Senior QA Test Engineer in data area who will join our growing Software Testing team in Vilnius. The individual will be integrally involved in meeting our goal to develop robust high quality software, with ever increasing performance requirements and simultaneously contribute to improve agility and time to market in a changing business and regulatory environment.


Depending on the experience you will be:

  • Reviewing & analyzing requirements, providing continues feedback to a project team
  • Testing and analyzing data stores in cloud
  • Automating data tests
  • Running automated test batches and evaluating the results
  • Designing, implementing and improving data models
  • Giving continuous feedback on improvements to the product and the testing process


Requirements:

  • Quality-oriented mindset
  • Understand data modeling principles
  • Understand data testing methods
  • Strong SQL skills
  • Experience working with Linux, Python
  • Experience working with big data, NoSQL in cloud would be a big plus
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal English
  • Relevant College / University degree (e.g. Mathematics, Physics, Statistics, Engineering or Computer Science)


This is a permanent full time position located in Vilnius. As the selection and interview process is ongoing, please submit your application in English as soon as possible.


A valid working permit for the EU is mandatory.


Monthly salary (Gross): 3000 - 4000 Eur
