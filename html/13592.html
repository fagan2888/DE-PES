About The Data Engineer Role

We are dedicated engineers building a better platform for local government. We believe in creating tools that are powerfully customizable but seamless to use. As a member of our engineering team you will use various technologies to develop applications and systems to put that data to work.

As a Data Engineer, we'll trust you to develop strategies and solutions to manipulate data using SQL and NoSQL-based solutions. You'll quickly and accurately translate concepts and specifications into workable data solutions, build reliable and effective pipelines, ensure a consistent and coherent approach to database development, and perform general data platform engineering, maintenance, and bug fixing.

Responsibilities
  • Participate in design and development of SQL and NoSQL-based solutions
  • Design and maintain data pipelines
  • Perform data migration, integration, and other ETL processes
  • Work as a team to develop data products where all components communicate effectively
  • Optimize performance across the data architecture
  • Develop reporting architectures
  • Develop best practices across the engineering team
  • Participate in retrospective reviews
  • Develop work estimates for client projects
  • Bring new perspectives to problems
Requirements
  • Background in Computer Science, Management Information Systems, Mathematics, Business Analytics or related discipline
  • 2+ years of proven professional data development experience
  • Understanding of ETL concepts and tools
  • Understanding of database design concepts
  • Proficient with relational data modeling
  • Years developing with SQL (MS SQL preferred)
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience with shell scripting
  • Experience with NoSQL systems
  • Exposure to data warehousing concepts
