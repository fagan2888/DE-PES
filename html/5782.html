  • Proven experience in building data science models and products.

  • Strong experience working with languages such as R, Python or Matlab.
  • Good understanding or experience working in Agile environments.
  • Experience in working with cloud-based data science platforms.
  • Effective communication skills and experience working with senior-level internal stakeholders.
