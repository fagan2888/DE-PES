Machine Learning Engineer

3 months ago 35 applicants

Description

We currently have a vacancy for an ambitious and highly motivated Machine Learning Engineer to join our expanding development team in Athens and lead in materializing the next generation of AI tools targeting real time decisions by combining disparate data sources. A strong background in large scale data processing and analytics is required while firm knowledge of stream processing tools will be considered a plus.

Main Responsibilities And Tasks
  • Design and implementation of machine Learning models for data quality, risk analysis and fraud detection;
  • Design and implementation of machine Learning solutions;
  • Solution implementation using ML libraries (e.g Apache MLlib).


Requirements
  • MSc or BSc degree in Computer Science and/or Mathematics and/or Statistics or PhD level research in a topic closely related to AI/DL/ML;
  • A minimum of three years of proven experience as a Software Engineer building machine learning systems;
  • Deep understanding of statistical and predictive modeling concepts, clustering and classification techniques, recommendation and optimization algorithms;
  • Solid and demonstrable knowledge of machine learning principles;
  • Excellent programming skills in a language such as Python or Java;
  • Experience with common data science toolkits, such as Pandas, Scikit, Statsmodels, Weka, NumPy;
  • Experience with Apache Spark/ Mesos clusters;
  • Experience with Big Data technologies and NoSQL databases;
  • Problem solving aptitude;
  • Fluency in verbal and written English.


Benefits

As a Machine Learning Engineer you will have the opportunity to explore and apply the latest development tools/technologies. As part of a friendly team you will work closely with a highly professional team of full-stack software developers / engineers. As part of an international company you will contribute to challenging IT software projects for major international public organizations and institutions. As part of a dynamic company you will get the chance to develop your skills and your career.

We offer a competitive remuneration (with full benefits package) including:
  • Competitive full-time salary;
  • Private Health Coverage on the Company’s group program;
  • Flexible working hours, top - of - the Line tools, language courses, specialized trainings;
  • A great opportunity to grow and work with innovative and exiting specialists in the industry;
  • Being part of an environment that offers challenging goals, autonomy and mentoring, which creates development opportunities, both for you and the company.

If you want an exciting challenge, work with some of the coolest technologies and enjoy your time doing it, then join us! Send us your detailed CV in English, quoting reference: (MLE/11/18)
