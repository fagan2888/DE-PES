What We Are Looking For

We are looking for a Backend Engineer who likes working with large amount of datato work on a team thatbuilds real-time data pipelines, capable of handling over 1 billion events daily and help to scale our machine learning infrastructure.

If you are a strong developer with some knowledge in Python, Go or Scala and you would like to master those skills in a fast-paced environment full of technological challenges, you might be the new Jampper we are looking for.

This is an on-site position in Buenos Aires, Argentina or Córdoba, Argentina.

What You'll Do
  • Contribute to the development of Jampp's data infrastructure by building high-quality components for distributed data processing systems.
  • Design and implement ETL (extract, transform, and load) processes from a wide variety of sources.
  • Benchmark systems, analyze system bottlenecks and track data quality and consistency proposing solutions to improve them
  • Work creatively and analytically in a problem-solving environment.

Requirements
  • 2+ years of programming experience in Python, Go or Java/Scala.
  • 1+ years experience with databases and SQL.
  • Rock-solid foundations in Computer Science (data structures, algorithms, optimization) and Software Engineering (OOP design, microservices architectures, etc).
  • Extreme attention to detail.
  • Aptitude to learn a lot about state-of-the-art data processing technologies.
NICE TO HAVE
  • Experience using the AWS Big Data stack (Kinesis, Firehose, EMR, Redshift, Lambda, etc).
  • Experience using an orchestration tool like Airflow, Luigi, and Oozie.
  • Experience with Hadoop, Hive, and Presto.
  • Experience with stream processing technologies like Kafka, Kinesis, Flume, Flink, and Spark Streaming.
  • Experience using Docker.

Who We Are

Jampp is the programmatic platform of choice for the fastest growing on-demand companies worldwide.

In 2013, we noticed the chaos surrounding mobile performance advertising and wanted to change it, somehow. We built a suite of growth products that unlock programmatic advertising for some of the biggest names in mobile (like Uber, Rappi, iFood, & Postmates to name a few).

We solve the most complex and large-scale technological challenges on mobile advertising today. Jampp handles over 50 billion ad requests and process 100 terabytes a day, across three global data centers. We rely on real-time machine learning models (with billions of features) that give us predictions in less than 100ms.

We’re a team of builders - engineers, data architects, designers, and entrepreneurs - who love what we do.

We serve a global customer base from our offices in San Francisco, Berlin, São Paulo, Singapore, and Buenos Aires.
