Lead Data Scientist

Digital Workforce Services Ltd Helsinki, Southern Finland, Finland

4 weeks ago 26 applicants

Apply on company website

Interested in being part of creating something radically new? Digital workforce is on the mission to disrupt how business processes are executed within companies by developing smart and cognitively intelligent software robots – an area well known as Intelligent Automation. We are now looking for a Lead Data Scientist with expertise in NLP and ML to join our existing team of Data Scientists.

Digital Workforce is the highest growing Intelligent Automation (IA) service company operating in Europe, UK and US. Overall, we are now a team of 250 and growing fast!

The rapidly growing IA business, internationalisation and product development means that we operate in fast paced work environment and value involvement from all team members. At the core of everything we do is a learning mindset which means that at Digital Workforce we want to constantly grow both as a company and on an individual level.

And now a bit more about the position we are currently hoping to fill at our office in Helsinki. If needed you may be located in our Stockholm or Copenhagen office as well.


Lead Data Scientist

As Lead Data Scientist, you will be a key part of Digital Workforce’s Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning practice. Our vision is to make processes run autonomously using cutting edge technologies like Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Image Processing and Analytics, Computer Vision etc. To execute on this vision, we are building an Intelligent Automation SaaS platform which can extract information from various unstructured data formats like text, images, etc. You will get an opportunity to not only lead the core team that is developing the platform but also solve real-life business problems for customers.

In this role you get to be a pioneer of your field and have a chance to shape the company’s take on AI. We already have some projects ongoing around the subject and have competent people who will be eager to help you in driving things forward. We want to do things in an agile way and our fast-paced growth offers an exciting ride. Sounds interesting? We hope so! This is a great opportunity to join a growing, fast paced IA company operating in Europe, UK and US.

To summarise, see the specs below and how to apply. We are filling the position as soon as we find the right person so apply as soon as possible!


What we look for:

  • Innovative problem-solving mindset
  • Prior experience in Machine Learning with a focus on using Natural Language Processing (NLP) for Information Retrieval from Unstructured Data.
  • MSc or PhD degree in Machine Learning, Mathematics, Statistics or other closely related field
  • Should have worked on libraries like keras, tensor-flow, scikit-learn etc.
  • Thorough understanding of statistical machine learning or deep learning
  • Experience of building scalable solutions in the cloud will be an advantage
  • A good balance between theory and practice and a strong desire to learn and keep up with the latest technologies
  • Ability to use English as your working language. Finnish or Swedish is also an advantage but not required.


What we provide:

  • Super interesting working environment
  • Great, fun team who are true professionals in what they do
  • Growth company committed to learning and involving all team members
  • International company, ability to work with multicultural team
  • Great benefits (e.g. Health insurance that also covers leisure time, lunch benefit , Travel insurance for leisure time travel etc.)
  • Great company culture: value-driven company with nice get-togethers, for example Friday afternoon beers, sporting events etc.
  • Encouraging attitude towards learning mindset and self-development: taking part in seminars, events, courses, knowledge sharing and for example blogging on AI topics on company’s blog
  • Possibility to work from home if needed

For more information, please contact our Head of Intelligent Automation Teemu Vieruaho tel. +358 50 520 3853, e-mail. teemu.vieruaho@digitalworkforce.com

Read this blog to find out more about our thoughts on value adding use cases for combining RPA and AI/ML technologies and check this video out and get to know our team!
