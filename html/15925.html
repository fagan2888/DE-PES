    Process Engineer with Raw material expertise (Dry/Liquids) in the Food sector sought for international Food organisation in Belgium


    Type: Freelance

    Location: Flanders + Eastern Europe (frequent international travel)

    Contract length: 24 months

    Start date: Early November 2019

    Hourly rate: €65-€68


    The objective of this project is to support de upgrade of the raw material process/production lines of a site in Belgium and the implementation of a new raw material production line in one of the sites in Eastern Europe.


    This means that frequent travel within Europe will be necessary depending of phase of project.


    Profile requirements:

    ·        Proven experience as a process engineer with raw materials in the Food sector

    ·        Fluent in English

    ·        BSc. in Process/Mechanical Engineering or other relevant area

    ·        Strong equipment knowledge (tanks, utilities, silo’s, powder distribution, piping etc.)

    ·        Experience with equipment vendors/supplier

    ·        Good understanding of engineering systems (design reviews, vendor acceptance tests etc.)

    ·        Willingness to travel and work internationally


    This concerns an urgent position with a fast interview process. Interviews can be set up on the 24th and 25th of October.


    Interested in this project? Get in touch for more information!
