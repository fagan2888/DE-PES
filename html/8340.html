    Corporate Recruiter France chez NXP Semiconductors

    • The Safety and Power Management (SPM) group from NXP semiconductor develops mixed signal Power Management IC for the automotive market.


    In the context of developing the present business through our Multi PMIC solution, SPM is looking for a system and application solutions engineer, to define, develop and support products with Multi PMIC attach purpose.


    This multi PMIC solution is all about scalability in power and safety. This solution will allow customer to supply the whole ECU which could include several SoC from NXP or non-NXP plus the additional peripherals. Our solution will bring also added value on the safety aspect.


    The role consists of defining, developing and validating all our multi PMIC solutions (Supply & Safety) proposed. This consists in defining attach architectures and developing products that support this Multi PMIC Attach strategy, then to validate in collaboration with System Engineering team the proof concept : Validation at lab, creating eco system on our solution, creating apps notes and user guide, creating demos to teach customer how to use and develop with our solutions.


    This team will work in close collaboration with Business development team and System Engineering teams to facilitate IC adoption at customer level, and attach with multiple MCU and use cases, to be used in a complete system power solution.


    Role and Responsibility:

    • Definition & Development of Next Generation Safety PMIC Attach solutions for MosaIC
      • Understand System and MCU requirements to drive the roadmap of FSBC products and support MosaIC concept
      • Define and support New Product Development of next generation SPM products, to accelerate revenue growth
      • Develop collateral around our Power management system solutions : Apps notes, User guide and Safety Documentation
      • Define & develop ref design for NXP processor system supply
      • Develop IC ecosystem and tools to help customer designing their system and support system power solution from the power tree to the thermal system behavior.
      • Work with technical publication team to ensure the product launch on the web with all the related documentation and tools
    • Support
      • Customer in his system implementation (Supply & safety)
      • Business development team on customer technical support
      • Provide market/customer input to segment team
      • Test different uses cases to validate the system behavior down to the IC level
    • System
      • Map the system needs on each targeted application
      • understand system and applications constraint to drive value creation and innovation
      • Build test plan to validate the system solution behavior down to the IC level.


    Behavior and skills set

    • Master Degree or equivalent in Electrical Engineering
    • Minimum 5 years experience on Automotive Power management application role
    • Good knowledge in analog measurement.
    • ISO26262 safety knowledge
    • System knowledge
    • Hardware schematic development
    • Customer & business oriented is key
    • Autonomous & creative
    • Good problem solving capabilities
    • Good communication skills
    • Ready to travel world wide
