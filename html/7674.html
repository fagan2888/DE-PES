Data Engineer

Telia Carrier Warsaw, PL

3 tygodnie temu 29 kandydatów

Aplikuj w witrynie firmy
If you already have a few years of experience and are confident in your skills but are the kind of person that like to develop data patterns that really show how great results give BIG Data analysis can be and at the same time become even better at development of Machine Learning - then we have the perfect job for you! We offer the right candidate the chance to work closely with our Network Engineering department in the daily analysis of data of telecommunication network.

You will be part of a Data Analysis team with likeminded experts, where the Network Engineering lead will support you, whilst you and the team are responsible for designing, developing and maintaining new automated, reliable and intuitive solutions using the latest technologies in data analysis. You will, in this role, not just enjoy work but also keep learning more.

The Network Engineering team is modern in the sense that they support each other to ensure the company more efficiently and profitably develop to our engineers and our customer the optimal solutions. The role is in the Warsaw Network Engineering department at Telia Carrier and the location is Warsaw. The Data Engineer reports to the local Team Leader in the Warsaw Telia Carrier office.

The business language across the company is English but the local team speak Polish.

The Main Responsibilities Are

  • Assist in Data Engineering projects or tracks and participate in large data centred projects re quiring in-depth knowledge of e.g. data modelling, data engineering and data governance.
  • Create and modify data flows for analytical and reporting purposes
  • Select, validate and productionize machine learning models, including live trials
  • Demo prototypes to business stakeholders
  • Coach other team members in areas you are expert in

    • The Main Tasks Are

  • Identify and solve problems of a complex nature through advanced analytical methods
  • Assist to progress within overall defined deliverables of a projects
  • Apply statistics, modelling, and machine learning to improve the efficiency of systems and relevance algorithms across our network suite
  • Lead proofs-of-concepts and then effectively transition and scale those concepts into production at scale through, engineering, deployment and commercialization.
  • Learn new things every day

    • We Believe That You Have
    • A degree in data engineering or other relevant background
  • High level of drive with an independent approach and high genuine affinity with problem solving
    • Few years of work experience in Data Engineering or BIG Data
    • Few years of experience in system development
  • Knowledge of statistics and experimental design
    • Good skills in:
  • Development of solutions using Python, R, SQL
  • Designing and implementing data warehouse and pipelines
  • Set up and use of time series databases like ElasticSearch or InfluxDB
  • Set up and use of monitoring tools like Prometheus, Grafana or Kibana
  • Meaningful experience in Distributed Processing (Spark, Hadoop, EMR, etc)
    • Hands-on experience of:
  • Data Network Operations or monitoring of telco networks
  • Ability to deal with structured, semi-structured, unstructured and streaming data.
  • Set up log collection using Logstash or similar
  • Using Linux and other open source software
  • Set up full-stack elastic search environments
  • You are willing to develop in continuous delivery and DevOps way of working
  • Greater knowledge or interest in one or more of the following areas:
  • Layer 1 to 3 telecom networks
  • Mechanics around monitoring/logging, primarily through Netflow, SNMP, Telemetry, CLI and BGP-LS
  • The AWS framework for application deployment, would be nice
  • Container technologies, would be nice
  • Modern IT security
    • Ability to work independently when needed but care about the team
    • A passion to explore and learn more
  • Build relationships and convey complex information to a technical and non-technical audience
    • We also expect you to be fluent in English, both written and oral

    Your personal characteristics

    You have very good knowledge of network and Data system capabilities and drawbacks. You also have a strong interest in the business and how modern IT solutions will enable the business to excel. You are at the same time open for collaboration and sharing knowledge with colleagues that not have the same level of skills and you are inspired by being a mentor to others.

    The aim of the Network Engineering department at Telia Carrier is to deliver amazing network solutions. Amazing, because the business was not aware it was possible and because it helps the company become even better at what we do. We make this happen through close cooperation with the business departments.

    Location : Warsaw

    Application : Please, apply no later than October 31, 2019. Should you have any questions, please contact Andrzej Milkowski at andrzej.milkowski@teliacompany.com
