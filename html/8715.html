Lead Data Engineer - Edinburgh

One of the UK's finest Digital Marketing organisation are seeking an experienced Data Engineer to join their fast growing team. You will work to support the delivery of numerous data projects for your clients and provide hands-on data engineering with appropriate testing. This is an exciting opportunity to work for a company who invests heavily in data and technology.

Responsibilities
  • To support the distribution of various data projects
  • Support the project delivery process by communicating regular updates to major stakeholders
  • Ensure the smooth running of projects sticking to strict time frames
  • Develop and maintain ETL\ELT processes
  • Hold responsible for BAU work commitments
  • Strategic Planning & Decision Making
  • Working with the senior technology team to highlight that the right technology is being used for the current project, likewise you will offer your expert opinion if you believe you have a better suggestion

Experience Required
  • Experienced in data development, with a good level of SQL Server and or AWS knowledge
  • Past experience reporting with SQL Server
  • Experience using the BI Stack consisting of - SSIS, SSAS, SSRS
  • Experience with cloud based platforms (AWS and Azure)
  • A background of leading on high-value projects in data provision
  • Experience working in a Data Warehouse environment

This is a permanent role offering a competitive salary and benefits package. You will also be provided with a tailored development plan that will help you to continue progressing throughout your career, you will never be at a standstill.

If you're interested in finding out more about the role please contact Megan Southern on 0191 814 7804. Interviews are taking place over the next two weeks, do not hesitate to apply as deadlines will soon be closing.
