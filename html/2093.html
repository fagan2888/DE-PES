Software Development Manager

BlueCat Toronto, Ontario, Canada

24 hours ago

Apply on company website

The BlueCat Product team prides itself on being a diverse team with innovation at our core. The delivery of quality products is our mission. We strive to deliver by using agile methodologies; our team works to make an impact and are ready to tackle some of technology's greatest challenges. At BlueCat our core values are the foundation of our culture of fun, we win as a team and value employees who have the courage to act to make an impact every day.


BlueCat seeks a driven, results-oriented Software Development Manager who, along with being a phenomenal leader, enjoys collaborating across teams to build outstanding products.

BlueCat’s DNS Edge product is shaking up how large-scale companies implement their networks. Breaking DNS from physical hardware, DNS Edge allows companies to manage their entire global infrastructure from a single application. By moving quickly and listening to our customers, BlueCat is able to build and deliver features that are completely changing the DNS landscape.


How We Work:

  • In tightly-knit agile teams made of developers, architects, scrum masters, and product owners
  • Using strong automated test coverage to allow us to ship our product continuously with the quality our customers expect
  • Intelligently evolving our codebase to meet the demands of customers and leverage the latest technology the Internet has to offer


What you’ll do:

  • Coach, lead and manage a team of software developers with varying experience levels
  • Engage in design/architecture discussions with Product leaders and other stakeholders
  • Hands-on with our development process and deeply involved in SDLC
  • Work with our cutting Edge technology stack, which includes: AWS, Docker containers, Java, Python, React, Redux, Jenkins, Kafka, GO and more
  • Ability to define a process with regards to customer escalation for our mission-critical product
  • Leverage our people & their talent to take our product to the next level
  • Help our development teams disrupt the DNS space

Our Vision of an Ideal Candidate:

  • Encouraging areas for growth and training
  • Drives BlueCat culture within the teams, cultivating an environment of high morale and engaged employees
  • Drives full-cycle recruitment and role definition within Engineering
  • Experienced with goal setting and continuously improving the performance of individuals and teams
  • Passionate about building high performing, cohesive teams
  • Experience leading a software development team in an Agile environment
  • Real passion for people, performance improvement and team building
  • Strong leadership skills
  • Significant development experience with large enterprise solutions or cloud-based applications


BlueCat’s core values are:

Curiosity - We care and inquire about the challenges and needs of our customers.

Clarity - We keep communication crisp, clear, and responsive.

Collaboration - We win with each other, never alone.

Transparency - We ensure all relevant information is available – no questions are off-limits.

Ownership - We are responsible and accountable for getting things done.

What you’ll get from us:

BlueCat is a place to grow! Our clients are some of the world’s largest and hottest logos. We are a well-established business with a culture that supports development and growth through exciting challenges. Our experienced leaders provide the foundation and support you need while allowing the space for you to create and be innovative.

While we’re growing together, we’ll have your back with solid compensation/bonuses, education budgets, record-breaking sales quarters, and a company culture that values curiosity, clarity, collaboration, transparency, and ownership.

If you’re as excited about the future of our company as we are, we would love to hear from you!

Working at BlueCat allows you to challenge yourself every day. Our culture advocates innovation, continuous learning, and work/life balance. Feed your passion, be bold, get inspired and grow with us!

Accommodations will be provided throughout the recruitment, hiring and employment process in accordance with the AODA and the Ontario Human Rights Code.
