    • At Rome2rio we’re busy helping people get from A to B around the globe. With millions of unique monthly visitors, our multi-modal travel search and booking service is one of the top online travel resources used around the world.


    We are looking for a Data Engineer to join our Data Science team in Melbourne. We have a range of challenging projects which you may tackle depending on your skills and interests.


    For example you might:

    • Implement and manage an Extract-Transform-Load (ETL) system
    • Manage and process data using Google Cloud Platform and BigQuery
    • Manage engineering challenges around the moving and processing of significant amounts of data
    • Work with Data Scientists to improve toolchains and infrastructure
    • Interact with AWS and BigQuery using our proprietary purpose-built infrastructure


    Check out our company blog for an idea of what we are busy working on.


    Who we're looking for


    We are looking for somebody with a Computer Science or Software Engineering bachelor degree (or higher). Experience with a breadth of programming languages, a broad toolkit, the ability to thrive independently and a "get stuff done" attitude are important to us. Our backend codebase is C#, however you do not need to be a C# expert; you just need to be the type of engineer that wants to quickly learn new languages and can work within an existing codebase.


    Skills and Requirements:

    • Experience in an object-oriented or general purpose programming language such as Java, C/C++, Objective C, Python, JavaScript, or Go
    • Experience with version control systems like Git
    • Experience with SQL
    • An understanding of Agile methods and concepts
    • Excellent problem-solving, analytical and collaboration skills
    • Experience in or willingness to mentor team members, review code and contribute to implementing good engineering practices


    Bonus points:

    • Familiarity with marketing attribution pipelines and concepts
    • Familiarity with GA 360 BigQuery exports
    • Experience with AWS S3 and EC2
    • Experience with C#
    • Experience conceiving, developing and testing hypotheses in A/B testing environments


    Most importantly the team at Rome2rio are a collaborative bunch and we welcome people with a passion to get things done who are comfortable delivering across strategy and tactical execution with the ability and enthusiasm to work as a member of a multidisciplinary team.


    Rome2rio proudly succeeds through diversity and inclusion. We encourage all suitable candidates for this role to apply without hesitation.
