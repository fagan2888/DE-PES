Our client is an investment holding company. They are looking looking for a Data Engineer whose primary focus of this role is to design and implement domain-specific Logical Data Models using analytics built on kdb+ and/or Hadoop, and make the data accessible via Tableau and Delta Dashboards

JOB SCOPE (responsibilities, environment, etc)

  • Design and build analytics on a kdb+ enterprise data warehouse to produce business domain-specific data sets
  • Integrate data from various sources and develop data reconciliation and integrity checks
  • Develop solutions to ingest and store a variety of unstructured/semistructured data
  • Work with Data Management to define Data Dictionaries, Business Glossaries and metadata that defines the regulatory data sets
  • Build connectivity between kdb+ and other big data platforms (Hadoop, S/4HANA)

MANDATORY JOB REQUIREMENTS (must have skills and experience)

  • Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or related fields
  • 2-4 years of working experience in a data-centric role
  • Experience in q/kdb+ is preferred
  • Proficiency in SQL and at least one programming language (Java, Python, C++ preferred)
  • Familiar with Unix/Linux environments
  • Highly driven, pro-active and a strong team player
  • Excellent interpersonal skills and written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Ability to multitask effectively and handle large amounts of data
