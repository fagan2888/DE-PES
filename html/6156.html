Modelling & Tools Software Development Engineer

Red Bull Racing & Red Bull Technology Milton Keynes, United Kingdom

1 week ago

Apply on company website

We require a passionate engineering software developer, ideally educated to at least degree level in Applied Mathematics or Computer Science.


You should have excellent C++ software development skills with experience of Linux and Windows. The ideal candidate will have experience developing GUI driven data visualisation tools using Qt and OpenGL. You will have done data analysis and modelling to extract insights from large data sets and will have experience of interfacing to databases and web services.

You should be self-motivated and proactive, with excellent communication skills and the ability to work to very tight deadlines within a dynamic team environment, often with minimal supervision.


Working from our Milton Keynes factory you will be part of a small team and provide key software tools to the aerodynamics department and the wider company. This role is a Fixed Term Contract position until the end of 2020.


The closing date to submit applications for this role is 27th October 2019.
