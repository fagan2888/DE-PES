Descriptif du poste
The leading Care Platform in Europe is looking for a data engineer.
Yoopies.com, present in 14 countries, has over 3 million members and its
reinventing the future of work for the home care services of childcare,
housekeeping, tutoring, eldercare, and pet care. We are looking for a
passionate data engineer to help us grow.

Missions
  • You will maintain and improve an existing data stack composed of loosely
coupled but complementary batch (Airflow, Redshift) and streaming solutions
(Snowplow, Kinesis, Lambda).
  • You will ensure the quality and continuity of the flow of data from and
towards high-end marketing and analytics tools.
  • You will implement technical solutions to practical problems in order to
collect, move, transform, enrich and deliver data. You are often on the
receiver end of data requests.
  • You will assist in building visualizations and running analysis on the data
Profil recherché

Skills
  • You have strong Python and SQL skills and strive to implement best
practices as well as proven design patterns
  • You can design, implement and maintain scalable and robust services,
especially for data transfers and manipulations (both streaming and batch).
  • You cultivate deep and broad knowledge of data architecture technologies,
from relational and non-relational databases to ETL frameworks and general
tech stacks
  • Youve had exposure to DAG orchestrators like Airflow, Dataform, dbt
  • You naturally document your code and projects, and can easily communicate
your decisions with less technical stakeholders

Bonus
  • You know your way around AWS tools and the Serverless framework
  • You have knowledge of devops practices (deployment automation & tools,
dockerization, networking, security)
  • You have experience with data science projects, frameworks and libraries
(pandas, scikit-learn, tensorflow, pytorch)
  • You speak french and english ;)
Behavior
  • You are curious and willing to learn in every aspect of the job if not more
  • You understand that data engineering practices are enablers and you strive
to provide practical, real-world solutions.
  • You cultivate versatility and are willing to work on various projects
beyond the sole scope of data engineering
  • You are not afraid to leave your comfort zone and challenge yourself

  • Something original or interesting about you!
