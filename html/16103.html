Description

Amazon Web Services (AWS) provides a highly reliable, scalable, low-cost infrastructure platform in the cloud that powers hundreds of thousands of businesses in 190 countries around the world.
We are hiring a Senior Data Scientist for our Solutions Architecture Innovation Lab. The AWS Solutions Architecture function facilitates customer awareness and adoption of AWS services in support of their business objectives. Within the Innovation Lab, we explore new ideas to serve our customers better at scale, and we run experiments to validate their viability.
As a Senior Data Scientist, you will be responsible for designing and executing data science experiments in short and agile cycles. You will use some of the latest AWS technologies in Machine Learning, Data Science, and Business Intelligence. You will be part of a community of builders and data scientists in our Solutions Architecture organization and will also be responsible for sharing your experience within that community.
The ideal candidate will be curious and able to learn new technologies quickly. You will have hands-on data science experience working with large scale data sets. You are passionate about sharing your knowledge to enable others. You empathize with the needs of our customers and align your work accordingly.
This position is based in the vibrant city of Luxembourg.


Basic Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Economics, Physics, or other quantitative field
  • Experience with a relevant programming language (Python, R, Scala, etc.) and SQL
  • Track record in gaining insights from large datasets
  • Ability to adapt to new technologies and learn quickly
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
  • Fluency in English
Preferred Qualifications
  • PhD preferred
  • Experience with Machine Learning and advanced data analytics on AWS
  • Experience in building end to end data science pipelines
