We are now looking for a Data Engineer to join our tech team. This is a newly created role with the opportunity to make it your own!


Objectives of this role include:

  • Design, develop and maintain enterprise data warehouse/data lake and data engineering solution on cloud at an enterprise scale including data marts, metadata, data warehouse models
  • Develop data engineering patterns, framework and best practices for metadata-driven data engineering processes for both data pipelines and data warehouse ETL processes
  • Manage and configure data engineering workflows
  • Conceive analytics and business intelligence solutions for internal stakeholders to power business analytics by applying dimension modelling.
  • Work with internal stakeholders to assist with data-related technical issues and support their data needs to solve and find business problems & opportunities.


You have


  • 3-5 years of experience as a data engineer within data and analytics domain. Must have experience in ingesting and processing data from various types of source systems.
  • Strong experience in building and managing the cloud infrastructure required for a data warehouse in AWS. This includes S3, EMR, Athena, Redshift, Glue
  • Working knowledge of data platform concepts - data lake, data warehouse, ETL, big data processing (designing and supporting variety/velocity/volume), real time event streaming and stream processing architecture for data platforms
  • Advanced working SQL and programming (preferably Python) knowledge and experience
  • Strong experience in designing and implementing the dimensional modelling
  • Knowledge of best practice in optimising columnar and distributed data processing system and infrastructure
  • Strong attention to detail skills in data quality, data cleansing, data validation
  • Experience in engaging with both technical and non-technical stakeholders
  • Experience with a modern enterprise-grade BI tool e.g. Tableau, PowerBI, Microstrategy, Qlikview
  • Experience in working in a fast-paced Agile environment
