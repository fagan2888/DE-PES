    Patrick Donaldson

    Talent Acquisition Manager - Data Integration, Cloud and Big Data

    • Datasource Consulting, an EXL company, is looking to build a new nearshore team in our EXL office in Bogota Columbia to help deliver enterprise data management solutions to our clients across the US in the areas of Data Integration, Data Architecture, Cloud and Big Data Management, Data Analysis, Data Quality, and Business Intelligence. We are targeting data management professionals with solid hands on experience in data integration/engineering and data architecture as building blocks for this team. Communication skills in written and spoken English are a priority, as you will be supporting projects in the United States.


    RESPONSIBILITIES:

    • Works with the Data Warehouse Architect to perform source system analysis, identification of key data issues, data profiling and development of normalized and star-snowflake physical schemas
    • ETL design, development, optimization and support, creates and implements scheduling strategy, and develops error handling processes
    • Ability to drive a project as a lead consultant and have high level conversations with internal and external stakeholders.
    • Coordinates with Project Managers and help drive project planning
    • Works with business partners to review prototypes and develop iterative revisions
    • Oversees and performs troubleshooting and provides resolutions to reporting issues.


    QUALIFICATIONS:

    • 5 years minimum Informatica PowerCenter or SSIS ETL design and development experience
    • Solid understanding of Business Intelligence concepts & Data Warehousing methodologies
    • English language proficiency in written and spoken English
    • Strong understanding of Data Integration
    • Experience in Analysis, Architecture, Design and Development of DW/BI solutions
    • Ability to work in a team environment
    • Solid background in Oracle database systems along with strong knowledge of PL/SQL and SQL
    • Experience in designing and writing code with ability to write high-performing complex queries
    • Experience working with multi-divisional business communities to leverage information across the enterprise to improve business effectiveness
    • Experience in decision support, business intelligence environments utilizing tools such as Tableau, Business Objects, Cognos, TIBCO, MSBI, Oracle BI, etc
    • Experience designing metadata used in developing BI projects
    • Strong team building, interpersonal, analytical, problem identification and resolution skills
