Data Scientist / Data Engineer Hybrid

This leading analytics company is looking for a Hybrid Data Engineer and Data Scientist to come work on building pipelines and deploying models for their core analytics platform. You’d be working end-to-end with multiple teams using Python, Spark, SciKitLearn, GCP, and SQL. Any CPG or marketing Data experience is a big plus.

Required Skills & Experience
  • Experience with large datasets
  • Strong in Python or SAS
  • Strong Statistical/Modeling experience
  • CS Fundamentals/Ability to Code
  • BS+ in CS/STEM/Related


Desired Skills & Experience
  • SQL Experience
  • Strong with Spark
  • Deep Learning with Tensorflow/Keras/etc.
  • 4+ Years of professional experience

What You Will Be Doing

Tech Breakdown
  • 50% Data Science/Predictive Analytics
  • 50% Data Engineering/Pipelining

Daily Responsibilities
  • 70% Hands On
  • 30% Team Collaboration

  • Contract Duration: 6 Month Contract-To-Hire
