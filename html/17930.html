    Senior Recruiter at PIMCO

    • PIMCO researches and trades one of the widest complexes/universes of financial products. As a result we understand the importance of high quality, timely reference data to underpin trading and research activities.


    The Reference Data team provides the platform which enables the business to acquire, master, steward and publish reference data to the rest of the firm and support evolving business needs and transforming technology landscape.


    We are seeking an experienced Data Engineer join our team and help us to deliver the next generation of our reference data platform.


    RESPONSIBILITIES

    • Work in close collaboration with the business and evolve the reference data platform to meet changing business needs
    • Design and build PIMCO’s reference data platform
    • Provide technical leadership in the area of Master Data Management (MDM) best practices
    • Work with external data and product vendors to expand the data universe/MDM product capabilities
    • Implement industry leading solutions and best practices surrounding data governance


    BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

    • Bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, mathematics, or a related technical discipline
    • 8+ years of industry experience in data engineering, data modeling, data science or related discipline
    • 8+ years of experience working on MDM platforms (Informatica, Markit-EDM etc.)
    • Proven expertise of Master Data Management concepts
    • Track record of enabling business outcomes through delivery of MDM solutions
    • Excellent analytical, problem solving and logical reasoning skills
    • Understanding of relational database concepts and expertise in SQL

    PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

    • Previous experience working in Asset Management industry and familiarity with the application ecosystem of such firms.
    • Familiarity with the financial instruments (equities, fixed income and derivative instruments), their core data structure and valuations.
    • Thought leadership in the field of Master Data Management
    • Track record of leadership/management/mentoring of a team of engineers
