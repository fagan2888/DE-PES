    Talent Acquisition Specialist bei scoutbee GmbH

    • We are looking for a Data Engineer to join our growing team of analytics experts. The ideal candidate will be responsible for expanding and optimizing our data and data pipeline architecture. You should be an experienced data pipeline builder and data wrangler who enjoys optimizing data systems and building them from the ground up. The Data Engineer will support our software developers, database architects, data analysts and data scientists on data initiatives and will ensure optimal data delivery architecture is consistent throughout ongoing projects.


    Responsibilities

    • Empower our customers to discover the best suppliers in the world and provide critical information to key decision makers in industry-leading companies like Audi or Bosch
    • Write and run data transformation scripts on big scale using PySpark
    • Extend and maintain our data scraping & processing pipeline that is running on Airflow as well as our Elasticsearch databases
    • Work in a cross-functional scrum team together with other Data Engineers, Data Scientists and Backend Developers


    Requirements

    • Graduate degree in computer science, statistics, informatics, information systems or another quantitative field, related vocational training (e.g. "Fachinformatiker") or have at least 2 years of experience in a data engineer role
    • Strong analytical skills to working with structured and unstructured data
    • Excellent knowledge of Python
    • Solid knowledge in Elasticsearch and PySpark, good knowledge of Airflow or aiohttp is a plus
    • Proficient command of English
    • Appreciation of working in an agile environment and optimally have experience with Scrum
    • Excellent team player



    Are you super driven in Data Engineering, but your level of experience doesn't meet our requirements? Nevertheless, this job ad sounds super interesting for you - Please don't hesitate! For us, personality and cultural fit are more important than qualifications. We are aware that underestimation of one's own abilities and the imposter syndrome can be reasons why someone doesn't dare to apply.



    scoutbee is an equal opportunity employer and all qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to ethnic background, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability status, protected veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law.



    We'd love to hear from you!



    About scoutbee


    We are scoutbee, specialists in AI and big data backed supplier discovery. scoutbee offers its clients an extensive data-driven supplier discovery suite, where appropriate suppliers can be found and evaluated effortlessly serving customers such as Audi, Bosch, Continental, etc. at a global scale. We are backed by the prestigious Holtzbrinck Ventures (investors of Zalando, Flixbus, Westwing, etc.) as well as by Toba Capital, a +1 billion USD fund from Los Angeles. We are the leaders of our industry and we are scaling fast across the globe.
