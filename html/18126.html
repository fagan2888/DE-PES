Data Scientist

SmarTone Kwun Tong, Hong Kong

2 weeks ago 40 applicants

Apply

Responsibilities:

  • Apply data mining and statistics (i.e. retention models, customer segmentation models, predictive forecast)
  • Apply machine learning to surface valuable insights
  • Deliver big data analytics: think end-to-end, from analysis to strategy through to solution exploration to implementation
  • Explore and identify realistic business opportunities for application and implementation of various big data initiatives; collaborate closely with business stakeholders from ideas crystallization/ requirements gathering through to subsequent business implementation, tracking and fine-tune
  • Identify data sources to support use case hypothesis and analyze the resulting data effectively using statistical methodologies and insights in order to harness the multitudes of unstructured data
  • Work closely with technical teams and/or vendors to explore solution shape design and architecture
  • Identify and execute on high impact projects; manage teams or vendors on data analytics relation projects implementation
  • Prepare reports based on analysis and present to management with strategic actionable insights
  • Review and validate models effectiveness and performances


Requirements:

  • Master in Statistics. Advanced degrees preferred
  • Minimum of 8 years’ experience with Analytics, Big Data and/or Data Warehousing data toolsets, e.g. SQL, Python, R, Hadoop, Spark, Hive, Pig, Kafka, SAS etc.
  • Minimum of 3 years’ solid statistical models development experiences
  • Strong knowledge on languages such as Python, R, Java and java script for data processing and visualization
  • Solid knowledge of Big Data processing tools and/or Data Warehousing data toolsets, e.g. SQL, Hadoop BigSQL,
  • Solid implementation experiences of statistics, decision tree models, k-means clustering, logistic regression, Neural, random forest, xgboost etc.
  • Ability to translate open ended business problems in analytical hypothesis and test the hypothesis leveraging various data sources
  • Highly effective communication with the ability to translate complex and technical analytical approaches into business language and outcomes
  • Great communication skills and fluent in English, both written and spoken
  • Great sense of ownership and servicing mindset to ensure efficient and effective customer service processes
