Data driven Manufacturing Engineer

Agilent Technologies Glostrup, DK

5 dage siden

Ansøg på virksomhedens websted
Do you want to ensure effective support to Agilent's production of reagents for cancer diagnostic? We are looking for a analytical and detail oriented Manufacturing Engineer to Production Support.

You will join the Finished Production Support team of 15 academics, which is responsible for providing support to daily operations in the Finished Production site in Glostrup. The support-group also ensures that the equipment and production processes are validated and meet ISO and GMP requirements. The support area covers raw materials, packaging materials, buffer preparation, antibody dilution, filling and packaging. Additionally, you will work closely with our Master Data Analyst, always ensuring that our Master Data are correct and updated accordingly.

About The Job

Your primary responsibility and duties:
  • Handling changes in masterdata, documents and formulas
  • Support to the daily operation incl. troubleshooting, NCR and CAPA handling
  • Optimization and Continuous improvements
  • Support and maintenance of master data for the area
  • Gathering and analysis of production data
  • Update of master data incl. relevant procedures
  • Ad hoc participant in projects.


You will interact with many people within Agilent -- The production areas, IT-projects, QA, regulatory affairs, R&D, Warehouse, Procurement and Logistics. Therefore, strong communication skill is mandatory in this position.

Your
  • Engineer, Pharmacist, MSc or similar education within chemistry or biotechnology
  • Experience managing GMP and regulated production environment
  • Experience with Oracel
  • Experience with Agile and SAP is an advantage
  • Fluent in English is a must, Danish is an advantage.


Your Personal Competences
  • Drive and ability to complete task to agreed deadline
  • Structured and quality-minded, with a great attention to detail
  • Enjoy working both independently and in teams
  • Proactive, positive and supporting personality.


We Offer
  • A job among highly qualified and committed colleagues in an international growing organization developing and producing diagnostic products
