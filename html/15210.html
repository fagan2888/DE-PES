Data Scientist - Paiania

Intracom Telecom Dimos Paiania, GR

5 months ago 95 applicants

Apply on company website
Responsibilities

The Data Scientist will join a team which designs intelligent products and solutions for all business verticals of INTRACOM TELECOM.

He/she Will Be Involved In The Following Activities
  • Understand customer’s business problems and identify opportunities for leveraging data to drive business solutions
  • Mine and analyze data from various data sources to drive optimization and improvement of product development, marketing techniques and business strategies.
  • Assess the effectiveness and accuracy of new data sources and data gathering techniques.
  • Identify candidate models and algorithms matching the business problem
  • Coordinate with different functional teams to design models and monitor implementation and outcomes.
  • Evaluate model quality & tune candidate algorithms/models
  • Develop processes and tools to monitor and analyze model performance and data accuracy.
  • Prepare Technical proposals
  • Technical Presentations & Demos to customers
Requirements
  • University degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science or other related discipline
  • MSc in Data Science, Business Analytics or other similar major will be considered a strong plus
  • At minimum 5 years of experience manipulating data sets and building statistical models, as well as being familiar with the following software/tools: Knowledge and experience in statistical and data mining techniques Experience querying databases and using statistical computer languages Experience creating and using advanced machine learning algorithms and statistics Experience with distributed data/computing tools: Map/Reduce, Hadoop, Hive, Spark, kafka etc. Experience with text analytics Experience visualizing/presenting data for stakeholders
  • Experience using statistical computer languages (R, Python, SLQ, etc.) to manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets.
  • Knowledge of a variety of AI and machine learning techniques and their real-world advantages/drawbacks will be considered an advantage.
  • Experience working with and creating data architectures.
  • Knowledge of advanced statistical techniques and concepts and experience with applications.
  • Strong problem solving skills with an emphasis on product development.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills for coordinating across teams.
  • A drive to learn and master new technologies and techniques.
  • Strong command in English language
  • Excellent communication, presentation and negotiation skills
  • Availability for short travels
Nice To Have
  • Familiarity with IT transformation analysis, design and/or projects
  • Knowledge of Telco or Banking markets
Personal Characteristics
  • Team-oriented
  • Results-oriented
  • Self-motivated
  • Strategically thinking
