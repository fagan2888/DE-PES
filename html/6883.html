W're looking for an experienced PM Engineer to join our reliability team at the plant, below is a brief of the job responsibility and expected qualifications

  • Job Summary
    • Sett up and optimize detailed inspections of defined areas of plant. The assessment of all trend data, generation of work requests. Provide plant history and cost information for all activities undertaken.
  • Duties and responsibilities
    • Ensure the permit to work system and any other safety system (Hot Work, Confined Space, Work at Height, lifting, slinging and rigging etc.) are strictly adhered to.
    • Assess risks, carryout Risk Analysis and plan corrective actions as and when required.
    • Responsible for a designated area of plant to provide detailed inspection program, carryout inspections, log data, analyze data and report to Reliability Engineer
    • Utilize inspection techniques such as, NDT, dynamic balancing, thermography, laser alignment, Oil Debris analysis etc.
    • Raise work requests from inspection and liaise with planning engineers to ensure risk assessments.
    • Analyze data prepare detailed technical report and histories.
    • Advise Reliability Engineer of conclusion and assist in recommending plant improvements.
    • Supervise contractors at maintenance shut down periods
    • Quality assure installation and repair work with designated area of responsibility.
    • Assist in route cause analysis of plant incidents or failures.
    • Liaise with other departments to understand and assess the production, optimization and process impacts and influences on equipment reliability.
    3: Qualification

    Level of education
    • B.sc of Engineering - Mechanical Engineering

    Knowledge & Specific Experience
    • 3-5 years of Experience in the cement industry
    • Specific knowledge of Cement plant machinery.
    • Good knowledge of Cement manufacturing process.
    • Thorough knowledge of general mechanical maintenance
    • Knowledge and experience of inspection techniques
    • Experience of computerized maintenance systems.

    Technical Skills
    • NTD, Oil Debris analysis vibrational Measurement and analysis, laser alignment, dynamic balancing
    Personal Competencies
    • IT literate particularly
    • Commitment to maintaining and improving Health & Safety standards
    • Strong problem solving and trouble shooting skills
    • Ability to work well within a small team.
    • Self-Motivated with a proven ability to motivate others
    • Flexibility in working hours
