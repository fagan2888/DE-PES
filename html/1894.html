포지션: Data Analytics & Visualization Analyst



ADW (Autonomous Database Warehouse) , Oracle Analytic cloud

Summary

Designs analytics for strategic use across the line of business. Design/Create reports and analytics that provide key business insights used for data driven decision making.

Possesses a strong business insight coupled with technical ability to know how to develop, explore, analyze, and present data and solutions to a line of business audience.

Responsibility

• Need a proactive, consultative approach to analytics. Asks the right questions of the pain point and makes proactive recommendations aligned to overall business strategy

• Analyze data from multiple data sources and design and create reports

• Communicates and presents analysis and insights to business customers and executive management.

• Evaluates business needs and improves efficiencies and capabilities through the use of technology.

• Need train personnel in scope and functionality of processes, systems, and solutions.


Related Experience Period : 3 ~ 6 yrs.

Knowledge, Skill & Abilities

• Basic understanding of fundamental data concepts (table joins, data deduplication, Boolean logic, if/then logic and machine learning ).

  • Has experiences with analytic programming, Data Management, SQL, data cleansing(Oracle, SAS and etc)
  • Has experiences with BI Tools(MSTR, Tableau, Oracle BI Tools and etc)
  • Has DW Project experiences

• Ability to relate technical concepts and data analysis to a business audience

• Strong communication skills

Education

• 통계학과 또는 관련 학과 출신

• 경영학과, 마케팅, 산업 공학과 출신
