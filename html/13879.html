The next big thing in data is you!

As the Mechanical Staff Engineer, you will provide engineering support for Shock and Vibration HDD related activities by perform Characterization test, summary, failure analysis, in servo mechanical aspect. To serve as a technical resource for new product transfer by way of acquiring job knowledge on failure analysis techniques for specific components or function. Work with Suppliers and Factory Supplier Quality Engineers on issue pertaining to mechanical dimension. Work with Design Engineer on issue pertaining to mechanical design improvement. Develop or bring in new functions, test setups, abilities to improve department efficiency and capability.

Essential Duties And Responsibilities
  • Shock and Vibration Characterization setup, test, maintenance and summary report.
  • Provide in-depth analysis for critical failures related to servo-mechanical issue.
  • Provide Hardware/Software trouble shooting, setup of existing or new capability supporting the organization.
  • Facilitate Engineering Evaluations on design changes, component revision rolls, process verifications or any deviations in materials or components to ascertain effects or benefits on production line yield, scrap, cycle time, OQA and reliability performance.
  • Develop, maintain and update a working knowledge on component technology, manufacturing methods and parts application, providing technical guidance for junior engineers.
  • Participate in product and technology transfers between sites. Work with Design Engineering from San Jose and Fujisawa on Component Design Enhancements, ECOs, Evaluations and coordinate implementation in the Factories.
  • Provide team leadership in taskforce / action teams in driving critical issue/failure.
  • In depth expertise in major function of the department supporting the organization on critical studies and issues.
  • Derive and develop CA for future improvement on products by performing design change based on lesson learnt.
  • Ensure commonality/standardization among same process/system within related manufacturing sites.
Qualifications

Required
  • Degree in Engineering (Mechanical, Mechatronic, Electrical, Industrial Engineering) or Physics.
  • Minimum 5-7 years of working experience preferably in the HDD or consumer electronics industries.
PREFERRED:
  • Knowledge in disk drive will be an advantage.
  • Knowledge in disk drive shock and vibration will be an added advantage.
  • Be able to work under pressure and possess a strong sense of urgency, commitment and responsibility towards the work especially in a crisis.
SKILLS:
  • Good English communication skills (written/verbal).
  • MS office, SPC, Minitab, JMP, MATLAB, GD&T, DOE, Mechanical Drawing Interpretation
  • Excellent SolidWorks skill.
  • Capable of performing mechanical stack up tolerance analysis.
