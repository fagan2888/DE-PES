Senior Network Engineer

Verizon Prague, CZ

Před 1 týdnem

Zažádat na webu společnosti
Job # 531211

What You’ll Be Doing...

You will be designing, implementing and managing the deliverables for our customer networks (WAN, LAN, data center, IPT, etc.) and creating documentation for the implementation of the customer's network. As a Senior team member also support and coach other engineers in the team at a technical level to provide professional engineering support to enable VzB’s organization to provide high quality services/solutions to our customers.

As a Senior Engineer, your technical skills are top notch, and you are known for pro-actively driving the development of the engineering role together with the other Design Engineers, Implementation Engineers and digging into the details to find the best solutions. You know how to get to the bottom of your customers’ needs, and how to manage your stakeholders’ expectations. Talking to people comes very naturally to you and you have an ability to communicate complex information in a way that people understand.

You’ll Need To Have

What we’re looking for...
  • Bachelor’s degree or relevant work experience.
  • Experience in designing and implemented large scale customer networks.
  • Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) certification or relevant experience.
  • Fluency in English.

Even Better If You Have
  • A degree in IT and experience in large complex customer environment.
  • Cisco Certified Design Expert (CCDE) or Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert (CCIE) certification.
  • Experience with Cisco hardware (routers, switches), WAAS, Juniper routing and internal wireless products.
  • Project management experience.
  • Experience with contemporary technologies including MPLS, TCP/IP, IPSec, BGP, QoS, IP Telephony, Multicast, and/or other similar tools.
  • Software Defined Networking (SDN) knowledge and Virtualization Technologies.
  • Willingness to travel - 25% or less.
When you join Verizon...

You’ll have the power to go beyond - doing the work that’s transforming how people, businesses and things connect with each other. Not only do we provide the fastest and most reliable network for our customers, but we were first to 5G - a quantum leap in connectivity. Our connected solutions are making communities stronger and enabling energy efficiency. Here, you’ll have the ability to make an impact and create positive change. Whether you think in code, words, pictures or numbers, join our team of the best and brightest. We offer great pay, amazing benefits and opportunity to learn and grow in every role. Together we’ll go far.
