The G Band Senior Specialist: Engineering and Technical Building Solutions role is based within Facilities Services




The role of the Senior Specialist: Engineering and Technical Building Solutions is e

To be involved in both strategic planning and day-to-day operations, particularly in relation to buildings and premises. Provide a key support function to all relevant business units within Vodacom operations and to be responsible for the management of technical services, maintenance and real estate processes that supports the core business of Vodacom. Ensuring effective and efficient innovative ways of working and building equipment assets,plant and machinery.

Take ownership and ongoing management and maintenance of utilities related to water, power, energy, rates and taxes specifically in sustainability reporting, implementing smart building solutions in enhancing facilities through IOT platforms.

Direct and support all activities pertaining to maintenance, build and construction in an agile manner in ensuring project deadlines are kept, within budget, meet site supervision standards and ongoing coordination of contractors participating in all built environment operations and activities to maintain not only efficient delivery but best in class turnaround times, highest quality and 100% compliance.

Effective integration and coordination with the Vodacom HSE team. Full management of Health and Safety and Environmental Management within the Vodacom portfolio. Ensuring awareness and compliance with industry standards and applicable Property Legislation, safety and security guidelines and municipal bylaws.

Your responsibilities will include:

  • Prepare tender documentation for technical projects;
  • Project management, supervising and coordinating of contractors;
  • Investigating technical suitability of new building premises;
  • Environmental Champion driving GREEN & ENERGY building initiatives
  • Benchmarking industry cost of services to achieve maximum value for money;
  • Planning and organizing future developments in line with strategic business objectives;
  • Managing and leading change to ensure minimum disruption to core activities;
  • Liaising with tenants of commercial properties;
  • Directing and planning essential central services related to building maintenance
  • Ensure building compliancy to municipal regulations and Health & Safety
  • Contractor management and SLA benchmarking and following up on any deficiencies
  • Responding appropriately to emergencies and urgent projects as they arise.
  • Business case compilation and business case modelling
  • Research, benchmarking and analysis with the aim of developing and delivering leading and competitive facilities management propositions, strategies and execution tactics.
  • Overall budget management of in terms of Capex and Opex expenditure.
  • Contractual and commercial application within the built environment.

  • Sound knowledge of Facilities / Building Management
  • Relevant Facilities Health, Safety and Quality Legislation
  • ISO standards

  • Project Management
  • Building Management Processes and Control system
  • Applying expertise and technology
