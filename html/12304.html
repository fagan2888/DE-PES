Description

We are currently recruiting for a Data Analyst to join a fantastic client in Cirencester to help them manage marketing data for their clients. This is an exciting opportunity to join a growing and exciting Cotswold based company with an office in London, working in the data driven marketing space.

The Role
  • Format, process and transform client's data files to load to their hosted database solution
  • Use SQL within Kognitio (primarily), MySQL, MS SQL Server and other SQL compliant databases to deliver to action business processes
  • Use of SQL database and Apteco FastStats Suite to produce counts, extracts and reports from data files and managed databases.
  • Support the set-up and execution of outbound digital marketing campaigns within the Apteco FastStats Suite and supported ESPs, primarily Adestra and MAPP.
  • Maintenance and updates client databases and document all processes.
  • Support client facing teams with the best technical solution to achieve client objectives.
  • Communication of technical issues / solutions with clients and suppliers
  • Quality checking of work produced by others within the team.

The Skill Set Required Of Data Analyst
  • Use of databases preferably in a Direct Marketing environment
  • Previously knowledge of MySQL or MS SQL server or Oracle
  • Excel
  • Other programming language experience

Salary And Benefits Of The Data Analyst

This Data Analyst role offers a salary of between £23,000-£28,000 per annum dependant on experience. You will also receive attractive benefits including 25 days holiday, company's life assurance and travel insurance schemes and will automatically be joined to the auto enrolment pension scheme with an option to opt out.

There is also parking, free fruit, employee of the month award, Christmas and Summer parties usually pizza once a month.

Suitable candidates with equivalent qualifications and more or less experience can apply.
