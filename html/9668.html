About The Role



Our Big Data & Advanced Analytics team is a key enabler to transforming Vodafone into a data-driven, customer centric, digital company. The Data Engineer is a key role in our Big Data team, who owns the development, tests and maintenance of large scale processing systems based on Big Data (Hadoop related technologies) and aimed to provide insights to business critical decisions. This is a very hands-on role, shifting data, data modelling and data transformation.

Key contributions
  • Oversee and manage the creation, maintenance and deployment of the re-usable assets in the appropriate Big Data technology
  • Explore opportunities for data acquisition
  • Develop reliable data ingestion and preparation processes
  • Employ a variety of languages and tools to properly integrate heterogeneous systems
  • Recommend ways to improve data reliability, efficiency and quality

Key Competencies, Knowledge & Experience
  • Hands-on expertise with multiple Big Data technologies (mastery level with at least one tool)
  • Recognized expert in the appropriate Big Data technology with a significant track record of delivery solutions in that technology
  • Languages and tools: Hadoop / Cloudera / Scala / Python / Spark / Kafka
  • Computer Science/Informatics degree
  • Able to communicate and influence effectively across a range of stakeholders
  • Fluent in Italian and English
