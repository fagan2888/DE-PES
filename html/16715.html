    • About Maxima

    Maxima Europe is looking to hire experienced Senior Java Developer to join our growing team in Brussels, Belgium. This is a new and unique opportunity to join a newly formed team for one of the largest and most recognizable brands in the financial sector in Europe.


    Position Description

    You will be part of a dynamic development Java engineer team in order to build, maintain, train, coach to the current framework for the entire IT organization.

    This team centrally develops and supports any foundation of the banking applications. Foundations mean to develop frameworks (Java, .Net), content management systems (Open text, Adobe), API gateway in order to display banking information outside the bank, exchange layer between the front end application and the legacy systems in an Agile way of working


    Main Responsibilities

    • Provide support & advice in client or back-end -side and best practices.
    • Analyse & Develop or integrate new commons components within internal development framework.
    • Discuss with Application Lead developer, Application consultant/integrator to handle/challenge project feedback and new components candidate.
    • Write user guide for framework users.
    • Maintain existing framework components.
    • Provide support & advice regarding framework aspects.
    • Participate to framework community (organized as a guild) to share technical evolution of the framework.


    Key Skills

    • At least 6 years of experience as a JAVA engineer in an agile XP-environment.
    • Broad knowledge and experience with Java, HTML5, ReactJS, NodeJS, Spring, OOP, REST, SOA, XML/XSD/WSDL, JSON.
    • Experience with frameworks or tools as Zuul, Kubernetes, GridGain, Springboot, Nginx, Docker.
    • Strong knowledge of Javascript/HTML5/CSS. Also in frontend frameworks as React, Angular, Node JS.


    Must Have

    • Strong analytical skills.
    • Experience with microservices in banking sector.
    • Learning continuously and taking full ownership of your personal development.
    • Curious and understanding/following latest developments in the technical area.
    • Teaching, coaching and training our colleagues.
    • Sharing knowledge and experience.
    • Good understanding of banking in general.
    • Able to discuss with your stakeholders and product owner about features and stories.
    • Translating these into software with real business value.
    • Fluent in Dutch and/or French.
    • Fluent in written and spoken English.


    Education

    University degree or Master preferably in IT, or equivalent by experience.


    Company Benefits

    We offer competitive salaries, a great working environment with room to grow and Occasional business travel .


    Benefits include:


    • Private Health Care
    • Pension Scheme
    • Holiday Allowance / Vakantiegeld
    • 24 days PTO
