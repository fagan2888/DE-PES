The BlueCat Product team prides ourselves on being a diverse team with innovation at our core. The delivery of quality products is our mission. We strive to deliver by using agile methodologies; our team works to make an impact and are ready to tackle some of technology's greatest challenges. At BlueCat our core values are the foundation of our culture of fun, we win as a team and value employees who have the courage to act and to make an impact every day


BlueCat seeks a driven, results-oriented Software Engineer who, along with being a great Developer in their own right, enjoys collaborating with team members in building great products.


Responsibilities:

  • Contribute to the development and design of BlueCat’s solutions in an agile, team environment
  • Collaborate with your peers, customer stakeholders and partners for technology/solution advancement and product delivery
  • Participate in product discussions, influence the development roadmap and take ownership and responsibility over projects to make them happen


Skills and Experiences Required:

  • In-depth software design experience
  • Versatile development mindset with the ability and desire to pick up different languages, frameworks, and techniques with ease
  • Comfortable working with large, complex codebases and understands the challenges around moving them forward
  • Fluent in at least one language & happy working in one or all of Java, C, and C++
  • Experience with DNS, DHCP, and networking is an asset
  • Experience deploying cloud applications and/or in containers is a plus
  • Experience with any of the following is a strong asset: Node.js, Docker, AWS, vCloud, OpenStack, Postgres
  • Experience managing a long-term personal project? Contributed to an open-source project? Tell us about it!


BlueCat’s core values are:

  • Curiosity - We care and inquire about the challenges and needs of our customers.
  • Clarity - We keep communication crisp, clear, and responsive.
  • Collaboration - We win with each other, never alone.
  • Transparency - We ensure all relevant information is available – no questions are off-limits.
  • Ownership - We are responsible and accountable for getting things done.


What you’ll get from us:

BlueCat is a place to grow! Our clients are some of the world’s largest and hottest logos. We are a well-established business with a culture that supports development and growth through exciting challenges. Our experienced leaders provide the foundation and support you need while allowing the space for you to create and be innovative.


  • Competitive salary and Quarterly bonuses
  • Career Development; we believe in mentorship and supporting you to achieve your goals
  • Hackathons
  • Team and company socials
  • Daily discounted breakfasts and lunches catered from all over the city
  • Choice of hardware, flexible hours
  • Corporate discount for gym memberships for you and your family
  • A beautiful office in Toronto, with easy access to transit (on Yonge line)

And a whole lot more!


If you’re as excited about the future of our company as we are, we would love to hear from you!


Accommodations will be provided throughout the recruitment, hiring and employment process in accordance with the AODA and the Ontario Human Rights Code.
