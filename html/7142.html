Data Engineer, Chicago, IL

Optomi in partnership with a real-estate focused organization is seeking a contract Data Engineer to drive the requirement gathering process, working direct with technology as well as clients. Experience being hands-on with data visualization tools, the ETL process, SQL, and data warehousing is a must. Cloud (Azure)/Python Experience are nice to have as well.


Unfortunately we can not offer visa sponsorship for this role. Unable to work on a corp-to-corp basis.


Apply today if your background includes:

·         5+ Years Data Engineering Experience

·         Hands-on ETL knowledge (Informatica preferred)

·         Ability to write SQL Queries (T-SQL experience nice to have)

·         Data Visualization & Data Warehousing

·         Experience with Spark, Wherescape, Data Vault Modeling

What the right person will enjoy:

·         Chance to thrive in a friendly and collaborative environment

·         Join a growing team with lots of career opportunity

·         Both west-suburban and downtown locations

·         Expanding on-shore team being built out

Key Responsibilities:

·          Step-in to this complex environment and make in immediate impact

·          Understand and communicate data-architecture decisions

·          Own data components and relay data structure and flow information

·          Excel in a fast paced environment with a focus on speed-to-market
