Air Brussels is an advertising agency located in Brussels. We have more than 25 years of experience in the communication business. We have a combination of national and international clients for whom we create communication campaigns, marketing programs and sales support.

For one of our international clients - ExxonMobil - we are looking for a meticulous Label Data Analyst . The labels are built with input of data out of a central database. It’s an important role since the label needs to meet specific legislation and safety requirements. Next to this the labels data analyst will ensure regular reporting of label releases to ExxonMobil plants and keep the digital library up to date.


What are your responsibilities?

Label development

  • Analyze new client briefings to ensure all data to build the label is correct
  • Build labels with the help of the Prysm software program
  • Manage database MS Access for release of labels
  • Direct daily contact with client
  • Regular reporting on number of label releases
  • Link between commercial and IT profiles


Online asset library

  • Manage and keep the digital asset library up to date
  • Provide overviews of the activity on the digital asset library to clients
  • Define workflows and roadmaps for asset approval and download
  • Liaise with the IT manager to answer to client queries for new sections in the library


What are the required competencies?

  • Good knowledge of MS office (Excel in particular, Access (B.K.))
  • Feeling comfortable to process large amounts of data and reports
  • Good English communication skills, Dutch or French is a plus
  • An analytical and logic mind
  • Client oriented, able to set priorities


What profile are we looking for?

  • You have a 1st experience (1 to 2 years minimum) in data management and processing (Excel, Access)
  • Preferably a first international client
  • Experience in an international environment is a plus
  • You’re able to work autonomously and as part of a team (commercial and IT colleagues)
  • Eager to learn new software
  • You are willing to go the extra mile


What do we offer?

A salary in line with your experience and competencies + benefits.
