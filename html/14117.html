Research Scientist - Pricing Team

Agoda Ōsaka-shi, JP

1日前

企業のWebサイトから応募する

Working Location: Bangkok, Thailand

We provide full relocation package and Visa assistance for all successful expatriates

Agoda is a global online travel agency for hotels, vacation rentals, flights and airport transfer. And as a Booking Holdings company, we are part of the largest online travel company in the world.

Customers care a lot about hotel prices and determining the best price is one of the most impactful and challenging projects in Agoda. Your task is to drive profit and growth by improving existing machine learning algorithms, creating new algorithms altogether, and designing new features. Success is highly measurable. You will be working together with some of the best talents from top universities such as Cambridge, Harvard, and Stanford.

What are we looking for?

  • Post-graduate degree in a machine learning related discipline, commercial experience in pricing optimization, or being exceptionally talented in math or physics
  • An enthusiastic and humble team player
  • Participating in math Olympiads, or having a proven record in data science competitions (e.g , Kaggle) are a big plus

Why should you apply?

  • Competitive compensation plus annual discretionary bonus
  • Good work/life balance
  • You'll be doing your research at big scale with the cutting-edge versions of Spark, TF (or any other framework that you need), on our GPU clusters or our massive compute clusters.
  • Having a real impact on the company bottom line
