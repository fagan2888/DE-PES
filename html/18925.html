WEB DEVELOPER TECHNICAL LEAD (E-U10)

Tech People Group Budapest, Budapest, Hungary

1 day ago

Apply on company website

The role: This is a high-profile leadership role with the opportunity to make a significant impact across the business as we continue to grow. A client facing role, you will act a partner to the wider business, while taking on responsibility for managing multiple projects through use of best practices. The role line manages a number of the development team and requires an outwardly positive person who will hit ground running, and build collaborative relationships to support the dynamic environment we operate.


Responsibilities

  • Solution design both in sales and production including writing collateral
  • Leadership on the technical direction, engineering quality and execution of product development
  • Architectural coherence in multiple projects, ensuring truly effective deliveries
  • Reliability, availability and support of components in production
  • Fostering a strong team of cross functional members including performance and personal development
  • Generate enthusiasm and a sense of pride by elevating projects
  • Support to the management team as and when requiredd


Required Skills

  • Proven experience of delivering multiple digital projects including but not limited to building performant, scalable and secured web applications, back-end API integration, database development
  • Proven team leadership and line management showing team growth
  • Experience using Git (BitBucket, Github, Gitlab, etc…)
  • HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Bootstrap
  • Angular.js / React.js experience
  • Extensive experience in writing scalable web services using node.js
  • Hands on knowledge of designing and developing web services using the AWS stack (CloudFront, API Gateway, Lambda Functions, S3, EC2, Elasticache, etc…)
  • Knowledge of OOP & Design Patterns
  • Experience in designing and optimising relational database schemas (MySQL, MS SQL, Postgres SQL, etc..)
  • Knowledge of one or more NoSQL database engines (DynamoDB, MongoDB, etc…)
  • Working knowledge of different caching strategies (Edge, server, key-value stores, etc…)
  • Experience with the LAMP stack
  • Strong organizational skills to juggle multiple projects and meet deadlines
  • Excellent communicator and collaborator
  • Ability to translate extremely technical concepts to non-technical audiences


Quality


  • A natural and commercially astute leader with a positive and friendly attitude
  • Great written and verbal communication skill
  • Able to discuss highly technical aspects in a coherent way having gauged the audience



What does the company offer?

  • A fast-paced vibrant office in Budapest
  • The chance to work with some truly world-class brands and entities from media to sport
  • Free tea, coffee and soft drinks
  • Monthly “All Company” meetings and project demos, enabling teams to share their work
  • A passion for all things digital that will enable you to learn and immerse yourself in new technologies


Start: ASAP

Salary: competitive

Job opportunity: full time, employee or contractor position

Location: Budapest / Hungary (1 week travelling to London or to NY)
