Data Engineer

talpasolutions GmbH Essen, DE

Vor 3 Tagen

Auf Firmenwebseite bewerben
You...
  • are looking for a motivating atmosphere in a fast-growing start-up company
  • are interested in IoT time series data, SaaS and data analysis in a young interdisciplinary team
  • are keen to work in a data-driven company where data science is our core product
  • you are interested in big machines

You should bring...
  • at least two years of experience in building data pipelines in a production environment
  • extensive knowledge in data architecture and database concepts
  • proficiency in Python and experience in professional software development
  • a graduate degree in a quantitative field
  • a keen desire to learn, a self-starter attitude and the ability to thrive in an interdisciplinary and diverse environment


Advantages (but Not Required) Would Be...
  • experience with big data and distributed systems
  • familiarity with machine learning, time series and deep learning algorithms for machine sensor data
  • experience in microservice architecture, agile methods, DevOps tools (GitLab), container technology (Docker), cloud computing (GCP)

We want you to feel comfortable...
  • attractive working atmosphere with all-round service
  • free choice of working environment (Linux, Windows or Mac)
  • individual training, conferences, coding sessions
  • flexible working hours
  • cool cutting-edge projects with leading international industry partners
  • industry oriented salary

A unique opportunity is waiting for you...

We are building the next generation of data analysis for mining machinery. Whether above or below ground: our products analyze and control the largest machines in the world. Develop the future of mining together with engineers, data scientists and IT specialists.

Next steps...

Please send us your CV, cover letter and, if available, your portfolio.

We are looking forward to your application!
