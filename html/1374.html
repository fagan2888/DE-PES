The expertise we’re looking for

  • 8+ years of hands-on experience in architecture, design and developing highly distributed data processing systems to scale
  • 5+ years of hands-on experience in implementing batch and real-time Big Data integration frameworks and/or applications,
  • 2+ years of hands-on experience in one or more modern Object Oriented Programming languages (Java, Scala, Python)
  • 3+ years of experience with DevOps, Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (Jenkins, Stash).
  • Experience and comfort executing projects in Agile environments (Kanban and Scrum).

Education and Experience

Required Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of experience designing and developing large-scale Big Data engineering applications,
  • In-depth understanding of Hadoop Echo System & HDFS File System
  • Experience with Hive & Impala (must have minimum three years hands on experience)
  • 5+ years of experience working with Oracle databases, PLSQL Programming
  • Experience with data ingestion tools SQOOP
  • Working experience with ETL Informatica.
  • Experience with Spark - Understanding of Spark architecture and internals.
  • Experience with ETL using Spark SQL &
  • Experience with cloudera distributed version of Hadoop
  • Exposure/Knowledge of streaming apps like Kafka and/or Flume is an advantage
  • Experience with shell scripting, control M

Behavioral Attributes

  • Extremely good interpersonal skills with ability to work with cross functional teams located across geographies
  • Good interpersonal and client-handling skills with the ability to manage expectations and explain technical detail.
  • Ability to multitask, prioritizes tasks, and quickly adjusts in a rapidly changing environment.
  • Ability to work effectively with geographically dispersed team.
  • Ability to handle complex information with accuracy and attention to detail.
  • A methodical, investigative and inquisitive mind; together with creative abilities.
  • Excellent conflict management and negotiation skills
  • Eager to learn and continuously develop personal and technical capabilities.
  • High level of commitment, initiative, vision and enthusiasm
  • Professional approach to time, costs and deadlines.
