Our Culture

Our culture is important to us. We are a diverse bunch; the pigeons who flock here are curious, creative and determined to go beyond the brief for our hosts, guests and for each other. We aim to create the best environment possible for them to let their talent shine through.

What will you be doing?

We are looking for a Big Data Engineer or Spark developer with a strong interest in data analytics and data loading systems, data warehouses/lakes, ETL pipelines.

  • Develop/Maintain our ETL pipelines from different sources like GA, Airdna, Zendesk and many more into our data warehouse (redshift)
  • ETL pipelines to maintain data in different data-structures, ex. From redshift to elastic-search.
  • Support our transition to target architecture of moving towards event streams running on Kafka or something similar.
  • Support the data-analytics team to ensure they have access to data in an optimised format.

What can you bring?

  • 2-6 years of experience as a Data Engineer or in a data engineering focussed role
  • Experience in data modelling and data structures
  • Experience with Big data tools like Spark, Storm, AWS EMR
  • Experience with Python and Node.js
  • Experience in data loading systems, data warehouses/lakes, ETL pipelines.
