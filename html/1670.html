About

Heuritech is a fashion technology company that offers brands predictive
analytics on trends, sales and stock.
Using advanced artificial intelligence (AI) to translate real-world images
shared on social media into meaningful insights, The Heuritech market
intelligence platform empowers brands to forecast demand and trends more
accurately, produce more sustainably, and achieve unprecedented competitive
advantage.
Heuritech collaborates with leading brands worldwide, such as Louis
Vuitton, Dior, Adidas, etc.
The team is composed of 40 people, among which a quarter has a PhD in
Artificial Intelligence.
Heuritech is backed by French VCs Elaia and Serena, as well as private
investors. With 4M€ fundraising in 2019, Heuritech’s next objectives are to
open the Beauty industry and international development in New York and
Singapore.
Joining Heuritech is a unique opportunity to participate to the growth and
construction of a leading and international fashion-tech startup.

Job Description

The Core team is responsible of Heuritech's data processing pipeline, which
retrieves millions of Instagram posts every day and process them with our
Deep Learning prediction models. The annotated data are used by the Product
team to provide Heuritech's clients with detailed statistics and insights
on their products.
As a Data Engineer in the Core team, you will work to improve the design of
our data processing pipeline to make it more scalable, robust and easily
monitorable.
SPECIFIC MISSIONS
  • Dive into our data processing pipeline to propose optimizations and
enhancements
  • Review our data architecture to help us create a roadmap for scalability
  • Develop back-office tools to improve the internal processes (e.g.,
dataset creation, training, ...)

Preferred Experience

We are looking for a data engineer with the following profile:
  • 2+ years experience in Python for backend development
  • Extensive knownledge on MongoDB internals
  • Worked on ETL / data processing projects with significant volume of data
  • Architect mindset, source of proposal, autonomous
  • Always monitoring what’s happening in the dev community
  • Comfortable working in an English-speaking environment
Nice To Have
  • Experience with other SQL and NoSQL databases
  • Experience with task queue systems (e.g., Celery) and message brokers
