Job Description

Solutions: Data Analytic and AI

Vacancy: 5
  • Developing advanced analytics solution
  • Collaborating across IT team and business team to understand complex business questions
  • Leading meeting directly with business users and IT users, including requirements, design review, model validation and result progress.
  • Identifying machine learning opportunities and deliver end-to-end model development including research, explore data, prototyping, model validation, implement into production
  • Guiding the suitable applications of data science and machine learning with the quality
  • Creating deliverable documents, including Business requirement document, EDA document and Model Manual
Requirements
  • Minimum 1-2 years of experience in Data Scientist or similar role
  • Experience applying Data Science to business problems
  • Good knowledge and applying of machine learning / mathmetical models
  • Strong skills in Python, R, Spark, SQL, Statistics
  • Able to communication; can clearly explain advanced technical concepts in simpler terms
  • BSc in Computer Science, Engineering or relevant field
