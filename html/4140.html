As a DevOps/API Engineer at Sertis you will be in the Machine Learning/Data Engineering team collaborating with Software Engineers and Data Engineers to manage code releases and merging the barriers that exist between software development, testing and operations teams and keep existing networks in mind as they design, plan and test.

As Our DevOps Engineer You Will Handle
  • Deployment - Handling the Continuous Development, Integration, Testing and Deployment of Cloud-based systems.
  • Development - Creating scalable, customer ready APIs for our teams and products.
  • Collaborating - Working with Dev, Infra, and QA to drive productivity and service reliability.
  • Communicating - Speaking to team members to define DevOps best practises.


What You Will Need
  • Experience with cloud technologies and infrastructure .
  • Experience with automation and monitoring tools and scripting languages.
  • Some experience in API Development a plus!
  • Sharp analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to think creatively.
  • Great attention to detail!
  • English speaking skills a plus!


What You Are Like
  • A leader! Absolutely love challenges!
  • Very open minded (eager to ask for comments/suggestions.)
  • Confident with sharing and suggesting ideas.
  • Have a can do and will do attitude!
  • Able to go shoe-less (we are a shoe-free environment!)
  • Nice and outgoing; we have some pretty cool social events at work!

Why Sertis?
  • We run on a flat hierarchy.
  • Ownership/responsibility from day 1!
  • Open/Honest relationships with colleagues (our CEO is super approachable!)
  • Western Tech start up vibe
  • Lawn, instruments, beanbags AND games... need I say more?
