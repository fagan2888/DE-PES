Job Description:


Reporting to the the DevOps Lead, the DevOps Support Engineer is responsible for supporting our cloud and on premise infrastructure. This position will also work closely with our IT teams across the company to deploy, and maintain a highly available environment. The DevOps Support Engineer will work in a complex, fast-paced environment with a focus on ensuring a high level of system stability.

The DevOps Support Engineer will work directly with development team members to provide highly available and high performing services. The qualified candidate will work with agile teams of software engineers and must thrive in a high-energy environment with complex multi-disciplinary projects.

The DevOps Support Engineer will develop a strong understanding of Opta internal processes, business needs and strategic direction. This will include building strong working relationships with internal and external team members. Strong communication and interpersonal skills are essential.


Key Responsibilities:


* Maintain the health of Cloud & Micro Service based environments

* Incident support (identification, notification, remediation, reporting).

* Proactively monitor all aspects of the platform

* Strive automation in all aspects of the infrastructure

* Execute application deployments and upgrades

* Execute infrastructure software upgrades

* Participate in the on-call rotation

* Identify defects in new and existing applications

* Assist development teams diagnose infrastructure related issues

Competencies required:


* Ability to quickly learn and apply new technologies

* Strong problem solving skills

* Ability to manage parallel work streams

* Good verbal and written communication skills

* Thrives in a dynamic team environment as well as focused individual efforts

* Ability to participate both onsite and virtual support teams

* Ability to create and maintain technical documentation

* Experience managing a cloud based environment

* Solid understanding of AWS and its key features

* Familiarity with monitoring tools such as New Relic, Nagios, Incinga, Grafana, Cloud Watch, Zabbix, Datadog, etc.

* Experience using CI/CD automation servers like Jenkins

* Practical experience with source/revision control tools like GIT, SVN

* Solid understanding of cluster based architecture utilizing container based technologies

* Experience managing database environments (MSSQL, MongoDB, MySQL etc)

* Experience with Kubernetes or Docker preferred.

* Strong ability to clearly communicate technical concepts to both technical and non-technical people in written and spoken English

Education, knowledge and experience required:


* Formalized education in Computer Science or another Information Technology based program

* Experience supporting large external facing environments

* Strong practical Linux and Windows based administration skills.

* Automation experience using current tools such as Terraform, Chef, Puppet, Ansible

* Very strong scripting skills (Python, Bash, Powershell, etc.)

* Prior success with automated build scripts used for multi environment release management

* Prior experience in establishing code deployment and promotion processes

* Experience in managing highly automated infrastructures

* At least 3 year of experience working in a 24/7, multi-tier, service-provider technical support team


OPTA and affiliates welcome and encourage applications from people with disabilities. Accommodations are available on request for candidates throughout the recruitment and assessment process
