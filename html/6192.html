Test Environment Engineer
Base Monthly Rate (DOE): 14,000 - 16,000 AED per / month (£3,000 - £3,600).
Package: Medical Insurance + Gratuity Bonus + Paid Holiday
Global Travel Organisation - Dubai, UAE
Long Term Rolling Contract (12+ Months)
As a successful and preferred recruitment partner, we have been instructed by one of the most prestigious and well-known global organisations in the world, operating in over 60 countries, to assist in the hire of a Test Environment Engineer to join the QA test team. You will facilitate test environment setup and develop on-demand test data generation capabilities. The successful Test Environment Engineer will also work with key stakeholders and will play a major role in supporting and enabling teams to improve time to market and ensuring better quality, by ensuring the provision of test environments to support on-going changes.
Our client's global HQ is based in the vibrant and extravagant multicultural city of Dubai. Looking to increase diversity within the group, this organisation is keen to receive applications from talented individuals who may be looking to relocate and work in Dubai. In return they are offering a competitive tax-free salary, free visa arrangement and sponsorship, 22 days paid holiday (plus 10 public holidays), medical insurance and an end of service gratuity bonus (worth an extra one month's pay). Long term, rolling contract of 12 months initially, with likely extensions.

Experience / Skills Required
  • Circa 5+ years in managing the setup, build, upgrade and maintenance of test environments for multiple products/solutions, both on-premises and on cloud as well as experience in test data management
  • Ideally 5+ years in working with large IT Organisations with a large suite of applications, building test environments from the ground-up.
  • Hands-on experience working with Test data management tools and setting up test data generation capabilities
  • Knowledge of Java, Python, JS, Ruby, Perl, .NET etc
  • Relational and No-SQL Databases
  • Knowledge of Architecture, including Microservices Architecture, SOA and cloud hosted large scale systems
  • Good understanding of DevOps practices, including Continuous Integration, Continuous Deployment and Continuous Delivery
  • Degree or Honours in Computer Science ideally
If you are a good fit for the above requirements and are interested in this exciting opportunity, we would be very keen to discuss this further with you. Apply now to receive a full job description, client name, client fact sheet, a guide to relocating to and working in Dubai, and further information on the application process.
Relevant Job Titles: Environment Engineer, Test Engineer, Test Environment Coordinator
