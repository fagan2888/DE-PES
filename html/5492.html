    For our team based in Vienna (1070) we are currently looking for a:


    Senior Data Scientist (m/f/d)

    (38.5 hours/week)



    Your tasks:

    • working as part of an international team on existing and new development projects and processes focusing on anomaly detection in time series data
    • developing automation strategies for analysis and prediction of renewable energy processes
    • setting up test infrastructures and streamlining automated workflows



    Our requirements:

    • technical degree in Computer Science, Data Science or Mathematics
    • knowledgeable inAdvanced Machine Learning methods (meta-learning, concept learning, explainable AI)
    • advanced visualization and storytelling skills, preferably in Python (Bokeh, plotly)
    • high level experience in designing, implementing and evaluating Machine Learning algorithms and solutions(bayesian networks, clustering, NNs)
    • expertise in software development (C++ / Phyton)
    • expert knowledge with Deep Learning frameworks (Keras, Tensorflow)
    • at least three years of experience working with Python as main language
    • knowledge in automated testing with pytest and hypothesis libraries beneficial
    • loves learning and working with cutting-edge technologies



    Your personal skills:

    • initiative and good communication skills
    • eager to learn new things
    • open to new ideas as well as suggesting own ideas
    • reliability
    • flexibility
    • eager to share knowledge with peers
    • structured and accurate operating principles
    • you have good knowledge of English (spoken and written), German is a plus



    In consideration, we are offering:

    • working with cutting edge technologies
    • participation in the development of innovations in the area of renewable energy sources
    • an exciting and interesting field of activities
    • ongoing work with new open source technologies
    • an exceptionally friendly working atmosphere with a flat hierarchy
    • flexible working hours
    • collaboration in a young and motivated team
    • pleasant working environment with very good career and training opportunities
