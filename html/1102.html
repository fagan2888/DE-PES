    International Recruiter at Fujitsu - Western Europe 🌈 Join the Fujitsu Force! 💪

    • Are you a motivated professional, passionate about new technologies with a desire to transform the way people do business? Do you have a strong background in building Big Data applications using open source software? Are you business minded, pro-active, curious and independent? If you enjoy working with statistical models and complex algorithms on diverse business environments then you are the one we are looking for to reinforce our Digital Business Solutions (DBS) team as a Data Engineer !


    Fujitsu DBS is powered by 4 practices – Robotic Process Automation, Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics and Blockchain. As part of our Data Analytics practice in Belgium, you will be at the forefront of digital, helping our customers make faster, smarter decisions and solve business issues by developing new data driven solutions. Your responsibilities include the design, development, testing and implementation of analytically-informed and issue-based solutions, more precisely by:


    • Collecting and formulating the business requirements & analyzing the customer’s needs;
    • Designing, implementing and developing applications with integration of Big Data technologies (spark, elasticsearch, hbase, graph db, etc.);
    • Developing unit tests for the verification of the developed code;
    • Developing data products and predictive models in collaboration with the data scientists;
    • Implementing advanced analytical solutions via Big Data Platforms;
    • Identifying and resolving bugs of the application;
    • End-to-end developing the functionalities included in specifications;
    • Supporting the customer throughout the entire implementation schedule;
    • Working in close collaboration with the product owner and the customer’s project team;

    Your profile

    You have a degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, or Engineering, hands-on experience within the banking, IT and/or consultancy industry and working with data mining techniques, statistics, machine learning and deep learning. You have already worked with Big Data Platforms (such as AWS or Azure) and with Data Visualization tools. You are also familiar with languages such as R, Python, SQL, Matlab and tools such as SAS or NLP.

    As a person you are curious and independent by nature, you like to combine your technological background to a business environment and enjoy working and interacting on daily basis with clients. On top of that, you have excellent communication skills and focus all efforts on ensuring the customer satisfaction. Next to this you have excellent French/Dutch and English language skills.


    What we offer

    When you join our team we offer you an attractive salary and secondary benefits, including a variable bonus scheme, group insurance and meal vouchers. You are ensured a minimum of 30 holidays based on a fulltime contract, together with the freedom to do staff purchases. Thanks to Fujitsu’s presence all around the globe, you will have the opportunity to grow your career internationally. Furthermore, you will get the opportunity to work in a flexible & dynamic environment with the possibility of remote working and the access to an extensive choice of trainings.
