Associate Data Center Engineer – Data Center Operations

IHS Markit Coulsdon, GB

1 day ago 31 applicants

Apply on company website
Position summary.
The Associate Data Centre Engineer is responsible for the day to day operations of multiple data centres locally. This position will also be available to support global data centres as part of the Global Data Centre Operations team. This person should have a general knowledge of IT Infrastructure to be able to communicate with other teams in IT.

Operations And Support
  • Perform day to day tasks in a data centre. This includes installing and removing servers/storage/network equipment, running and documenting cables and possible ladder work. Must be able to work with others to install/remove equipment.
  • Provide 24/7 cover based on a 1 in 8 rotating shift – ensuring 15-minute response times are achieved.
  • Take responsibility for approving and signoff of all DC Installations activity (receipt of hardware to successful commissioning) within the Data Centres, Server and Communications Rooms.
  • Provide guidance and direction to both projects and support teams wishing to install, maintain, modify or remove equipment.
  • Coordinate and plan the installation of all servers and network hardware plus the Data Centre, Server and Communications room ‘fabric’ infrastructure (cabling / racking etc.).
  • Cataloguing and auditing of equipment.
  • Support the decommissioning of equipment.
  • Be knowledgeable to understand differences between different cabling topologies (ethernet, Fiber).
  • Work with other teams to coordinate work in the data centre. This can include scheduling work during the work day, after hours or weekends.
  • Be able to use Visio and Excel to document environments in the data centre.
  • Follow existing documentation to support and troubleshoot hardware and design issues.
  • Willing to learn and follow Senior Engineers on processes and be able to implement those processes with minimal oversight.
  • Able to follow directions and complete tasks unsupervised.
  • Having a general knowledge of how to troubleshoot hardware and work with 3rd party vendors to replace parts.

Requirements
  • Bachelor degree in Information Technology, Management Information Systems, Information Science, Computer Networking and Telecommunications, Engineering, Computer Science or relevant practical experience.
  • Ideal candidate will have experience in Linux and Windows for helping to troubleshoot.
  • Knowledge of Server, Storage and Network equipment and what their functions are.
  • Experience installing Cisco UCS servers, NetApp and SAN storage, Cisco network equipment a plus.
  • Demonstration of good listening, oral and written communication required.
  • Ability to prioritise and plan work effectively.
  • Rigorous compliance with all working practices.
  • Experience using a ticketing system like ServiceNow.
  • Network patching experience (copper and fibre) install and break/ fix
  • Understanding of criticality of support
  • An ability to assimilate technical details required for project delivery in a short time frame and in a pressurised environment
  • Troubleshooting, ability to diagnose potential issues impacting programme and offer resolution
  • Self-motivated problem solver

Day To Day Duties
  • Patching for server installs
  • Patching for server migration and relocation projects
  • SAN-attached network patching
  • Distribution/ core layer patching
  • Management of requests and tasks to SLA
  • Infrastructure design and implementation, space allocation for kit.
  • Hardware break-fix for Cisco systems (Hotswap chassis modules, GBICs & PSUs)
  • Rack and stack of servers and kit to cabinets
  • Change control implementation and change management experience
  • Day-to-day running of the environment and issues, environment health checks and escalations
  • Receiving and management of stock and equipment for DC and its teams

Working Conditions/Physical Effort
  • Responsibilities will include evenings, weekends and on-call duties
  • Local travel is required with some possible International travel
  • Lifting equipment that can weigh 25Kg or more with lifts and other engineers
