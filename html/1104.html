Field test engineer - senior

The Lubrizol Corporation Shanghai, CN

1 天前

Purpose

Offer test services to the business segments to deliver competitive advantages of products to support business growth

Responsibilities / Accountabilities
  • Responsible for planning and execution of day-to-day field trial projects, including components evaluation, photography, data logging and data analysis;
  • Develop specific test solutions to fulfill the needs from business units;
  • Locate and establish relationship with test sites to match testing needs;
  • Preserve good relationships with fleets, suppliers and all stakeholders;
  • Maintain the Field Test Management System (FTMS) with all relevant data;
  • Coordinate the collection and analysis of used oil samples and operational data, and resolve problems with data as it occurs;
  • Leverage trends in data to shape solutions and generate ideas;
  • Suggest and implement improvement actions with a proactive and creative approach;
  • Develop fleet documentation required to start and run field trials;
  • Review project progress on a regular basis;
  • Provide clear, concise and timely reports reviewing the pertinent details, status and results of test activities;
  • Offer vehicle hardware and engineering consultancy to business units for problem solving and faults diagnosis;
  • Partner with colleagues at other global Testing Division locations, striving for consistency in processes and resource sharing when practical;

Requirements - Education, Experience, Skills
  • Bachelor’s Degree or above in Automotive or Mechanical/Electronic Engineering is preferred;
  • 3 to 5 years of vehicle engineering related working experience is preferred;
  • Strong hands-on capability
  • Fluent written and spoken English and Chinese Mandarin;
  • Proficiency with MS Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word and Outlook), Matlab or R; STAT and data analysis capability is preferred
  • Detail-oriented ang good personal communication skills to effectively interact with all levels of field test personnel
