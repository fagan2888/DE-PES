Junior Monitoring Engineer

Ramboll Copenhagen, DK

2 dage siden

We invite you to bring your enthusiasm, experience, and curiosity in monitoring and we look forward to seeing your solutions both the more traditional but also the ones which demonstrate out of the box thinking. We hope to see you bring these skills into play as you will become part of our monitoring team, which will span wide across many departments and therefore require some drive to try new things and an ability to look holistically on a challenge. To succeed in this role, you must have knowledge within monitoring such as which sensor technologies exist and understanding where they can be used. For this you have the capabilities within both statistic and dynamic analysis tools. You will also work with more traditional tasks within bridge design and reinforcement of existing bridges why a good knowledge of concrete/steel is a plus. Are you our new junior engineer with experience in monitoring and construction? Click the apply-button to send your application.

Inviting bright minds

Do you want to push the boundaries of your profession and develop your excellence in an open, collaborative, and empowering culture? We work to create a sustainable future, and our inspiring projects and innovative solutions aim to set the standard among our peers. You will join a global company that has been growing successfully since its founding in 1945. Together, we lead and leave a positive impact on societies, companies, and people around the world.

You will join our Existing Bridges and Monitoring department

As our new junior engineer with experience in monitoring and construction you will be part of a dynamic group that works with monitoring, that being everything from bridges to high-rise buildings. Our latest assignment consisted of setting up measuring equipment on a bridge in Australia and being part of the tender for a new high-tech high-rise building in Hong Kong. In addition, our department has been responsible for the design of the monitoring system installed at Queensferry Crossing in Scotland. Between the monitoring tasks there is also ample opportunity to work with the design of new smaller bridges in both steel and concrete.

Your key tasks and responsibilities will be
  • Design and planning of monitoring systems
  • Participate in on-site setup of smaller sensor monitoring systems
  • Data analysis from sensor data
  • Contribute to department with latest knowledge in your field
  • Help with calculating load capacity on existing bridges
  • Participate in design teams on new smaller bridges

Your starting point for constant growth

From the moment you join Ramboll, we will support your personal and professional development for you to grow within the company. For this role, we believe your starting point is
  • Engineer with specialties within the civil or mechanical engineering
  • Experience with Labview for setting up measuring programs and sensors
  • Experience with data analysis using Labview, Matlab, and Python
  • Are curious about Big Data and have experience here with
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Knowledge of FEM software such as LUSAS
  • Has knowledge of structural dynamics within concrete and/or steel
  • Can be part of the design team working with strengthening of existing bridges but also help in design of new bridges
Personal qualities that will help you succeed in this role include You have control of the details and you thrive by working independently as you eventually become responsible for your own smaller measuring tasks. That you thrive by sitting in a position that interfaces with the entire Ramboll organisation and therefore you have an ingrained desire and are not afraid to work with different professional groups.

Welcome to our Transport division

Ramboll is a global transportation consultancy and we work on some of the biggest and most innovative infrastructure projects in the world. We are close to 3,000 bright minds working within Transport worldwide, creating practical, sustainable, and economic solutions for national transport authorities, private contractors, and municipalities alike.

Ramboll in Denmark

Ramboll is the leading engineering, design and consultancy company in Denmark and has more than 3,500 experts working across 13 offices applying their passion to deliver innovative solutions in markets as diverse as Buildings, Transport, Planning and Urban Design, Water, Environment and Health, Energy and Management Consulting. We invite you to contribute to a sustainable future working in an open, collaborative, and empowering culture.

How To Apply

Apply online. Attach your CV, cover letter, and relevant documents showcasing why you are the right fit for the role, and when you are available to start. We look forward to receiving your application.
