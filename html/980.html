Data Platform Engineer

Belgie Vacature Groep Brussels, BE

4 weeks ago

Apply on company website
Vias institute’s Data Solutions team works towards scalable data applications that can be integrated as cloud services within our customers’ operational flows. We integrate different kinds of data sources, from our own applications, open portals or our partners and we process them to new insights using modern algorithms.

As a data platform engineer, you are responsible for developing and maintaining the Vias big data cloud platform. You develop high performance pipelines that integrate the big data stack with different analytical services, both for internal and external customers. Together with data scientists you implement machine learning use cases.

  • You have a degree in computer science, a master degree in industrial engineering or equivalent based on experience.
  • You are an experienced developer in Java, Javascript, Python or another general-purpose object-oriented / functional programming language.
  • You are experienced in building and managing containers, databases, and Linux servers.
  • You are experienced in serverless framework.
  • You are experienced in developing API.
  • Experience in one of the following technologies is a plus: Apache Spark or Kafka.
  • Experience in Cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure or GCP is a plus
  • You have general knowledge of data pipelines, ETL processes, SQL/no-SQL database systems, big-data storage/processing technologies and agile software development.
  • Knowledge of web services and REST is a plus
  • You are ready to dive into the above mentioned new technologies through internal training and self-study.
  • You are customer oriented and a real team player.
  • You love learning about the latest developments in data methodologies and technologies.

Vias offers a varied work as part of a professional, forward-looking organisation. The salary package we are offering is in line with the market and includes numerous of extra-legal benefits (hospitalization insurance, group insurance, meal vouchers, eco cheques,..)

For more information you always can contact Debora Vanden Broucke via Debora.vandenbroucke@vias.be .

For more information, visit our website (click on our logo).
