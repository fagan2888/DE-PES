YouVisit is looking for...

YouVisit is looking for a Senior Data Scientist, reporting to the Director of Data & Analytics, responsible for using data to help building product and support business decision-making and fostering a data-driven culture across the entire organization. This position is a remote.

In This Role, You Will
  • Conduct exploratory data analysis on large datasets across multiple data sources.
  • Develop and test hypotheses and provide insights based on the results of statistical analyses.
  • Determine the best algorithms to apply to the data, including machine learning tools as appropriate.
  • Work in teams to develop machine learning models to support and enhance our products by adopting best practices in processes and techniques for statistical modeling.
  • Prototype and build machine learning pipelines that train the models
  • Build tools and metrics to monitor and analyze model performance and data accuracy.
  • Collaborate with the product and engineering teams to deliver data products and incorporate your models/pipelines/architectures into deployed products.
  • Effectively communicating model results and limitation considerations to internal stakeholders.
  • The Senior Data Scientist also provides mentorship and guidance to our junior data analysts and data scientists in the Data and Analytics team.

To Be Successful In This Role, You Must Have
  • A Master's or Ph.D. degree in Statistics, Computer Science, Physical Sciences or a related technical field
  • A strong track record of performing data analysis using SQL, Python (numpy, scipy, pandas, scikit-learn, tensorflow/keras/pytorch, etc.), R or other equivalent language
  • At least 3 years experience using statistics and analytics to solve complex problems including machine learning applications
  • Versatility and willingness to learn new technologies on the job
  • Ability to write production-quality code utilizing knowledge of data structures and algorithms with Python or related programming languages
Plus
  • Understanding of common software development practices around design, coding, and testing a large codebase
  • Experience in the marketing technology space or with marketing technology products (interactive web content, web browsing tracking, etc…) and related analytics (users' web browsing behavior, web page bounce rate, etc)
