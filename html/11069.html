Working at the cutting edge, we design and develop software for platforms, peripherals, applications and diagnostics — all with the most advanced technologies, tools, software engineering methodologies and the collaboration of internal and external partners.

Key Responsibilities
  • Support the Corp IT Innovation Team on developing new capabilities to support production stability
  • Interface with stakeholders within Business and IT Teams
  • Collaborate with team members within Innovation Team
  • Help innovation team to drive adoption of Machine Learning and AI

Essential Requirements
  • Experience with Python
  • Experience with Python Frameworks/Toolkits like Flask, SqlAlchemy, Alembic and Marshmallow
  • Experience with Microservices and Cloud Development like AWS, Azure, Google Cloud
  • Experience with Git (Gitlab, Github)
  • Strong communication and relationship skills

Desirable Requirements
  • Knowledge on Java development
  • Knowledge on TDD and BDD disciplines
  • Experience with User acceptance testing and change control
  • Keen interest in keeping up to date on the latest trends and technologies
  • Adaptatively mindset to apply the correct technology (programing language, design patterns) to different business needs.
  • Knowledge on machine learning is a plus
