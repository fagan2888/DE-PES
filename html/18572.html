Jack’s Labs is looking for a data engineer to join our established team in Jakarta. The Data Team in Jakarta manages big data projects - Hadoop, Spark and HBase.


Attention:  Only apply if you have at least three years of hadoop and big data experience.

You are;

  • A problem solver who likes complex technical challenges and variety;
  • Willing to travel to and work in Perth, Australia for up to four weeks at Jack's Labs, Australia office, and with our clients;
  • Able to work in an agile and international environment.

You will;

  • Have sound technical knowledge of the Hadoop ecosystem;
  • Collaborate with project stakeholders in all locations and with other component developers, making sure that Hadoop, Spark, HBase and other cluster components operate in the platform;
  • Troubleshoot customer and production issues, providing help and fixes when necessary;
  • Be open and transparent, think of better ways of doing things.

Your skills and experience

  • Bachelors or master’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or related technical field;
  • 4+ years of software development experience;
  • Experience with Hadoop, HBase, Spark, Python, Scala, Cloudera and /or Hortonworks toolset, Kafka;
  • Exposure to Scala code with update Spark version, and development experience;
  • Exposure to Linux based operating systems and shell script;
  • Experience in Git or Bitbucket Source Code Version control tools;
  • Experience with databases like SQL Server / PostGres / PostGIS;
  • Solid interpersonal and English skills;
  • Self-driven & motivated personality, with a passion for problem solving.

Bonus points:

  • Any experience with geoscience data (seismic, well logs, grids);
  • Any experience or interest in GIS data and related distributed databases (Geomesa);
  • Interest or experience with Machine Learning.

We are seeking enthusiastic and motivated team-members who are looking to fast-track their experience with exposure to clients in South East Asia and Australia.

This position is ideal if you have at least three years of experience and want more exposure to real world and offshore projects with high-end and prestigious clients to add to your CV.


We have high expectations and our pay rates and excellent employment conditions match these expectations.

A willingness to learn and challenge yourself is important to us, and you.
