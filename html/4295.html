Tenable is looking for a talented and experienced Big Data Engineer to join Data Science team. As a Big Data Engineer you will be involved in designing, developing and maintaining distributed systems for data analysis, transformation, modelling and visualisation.

Can you join Team Tenable? Yes you can - if you’re excited by the thought of working with over 800 colleagues, 23,000 customers and a range of revolutionary products that are shaping cybersecurity in 150 countries. Being valued for who you are as well as what you do, you can embrace the pace (we’re recognized as one of the fastest growing security product companies by SC Magazine, Red Herring, Info Securities, Deloitte and E&Y) as well as enjoy a superb rewards package (we’re seen as a Best Places to Work by Glassdoor, The Washington Post, The Baltimore Sun, The Baltimore Business Journal and Expert Marketplace). The key question is: Is This You?

Your Role

Tenable is looking for a talented and experienced Big Data Engineer to join Data Science team. As a Big Data Engineer you will be involved in designing, developing and maintaining distributed systems for data analysis, transformation, modelling and visualisation.

Your Opportunity
  • Work in a start-up environment building a state of the art data science
    platform from scratch
  • Develop and automate innovative algorithms for data analytics, machine learning, artificial intelligence
  • Explore and work with the cutting edge data processing technologies
  • Work with distributed, scalable cloud based technologies
  • Collaborate with a talented team of Data Scientists and Engineers
  • Make an impact on the future of Data Processing/Science and Cyber Security


What You’ll Need
  • 5+ years experience in Python development
  • Strong experience in designing and engineering data processing workflows
  • Hands on experience implementing Machine Learning/Artificial Intelligence or statistical algorithms
  • Experience with Hadoop ecosystem: Spark/PySpark/SparkSQL, Flink, Storm, Hive, Impala, Sqoop, etc.
  • Deep understanding of data storage technologies for structured and unstructured data
  • Familiarity with cloud services (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
  • Experience using Linux as a primary development environment
  • Strong communication and analytical skills


And Ideally
  • BSc or MSc in Computer Science, Data Science or directly related field
  • Experience working in Agile environments, Scrum/Kanban
  • Background in Java, Scala or Groovy
  • Experience in Cyber Threat or Vulnerability related area
If you’ve reached this point in the job description and feel you’re still not sure if you should apply…Just do it! We know there are no perfect applicants. You may not have 100% of all those bullets listed above - and that’s okay. If you’re feeling like you’re not going to fit in with our teams - that’s not ok. We're One Tenable which means however you identify and whatever background you bring with you, we encourage you to submit an application if it’s a role you can be passionate about doing every day.
