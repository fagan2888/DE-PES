For our employees as well as for our clients we pursue one goal: Growth from Knowledge!

    Job Description

    Mission of the role

    As a Senior Cloud Engineer, you will be a key part of a Global Infrastructure team and focused on cloud infrastructure and services. We run both private and public clouds, with a focus on VMware, AWS and Google Cloud. Our purpose: Implementation of all necessary operative tasks to guarantee maintenance of operation. Support running applications, services, and platforms and network to deliver promised service portfolio according to SLAs. Help ensure global IT support based on a follow-the-sun organization as well as 24/7 on-call-duty.

    You will…
    • Help manage and push our move to a fully integrated hybrid cloud infrastructure
    • Help manage and support in planning and implementation of other projects / new designs
    • Assist in incident resolution, Incident management
    • Help managing and implementing changes, Change management
    • Manage and support installation of new versions, patches
    • Have close customer contact during operative work
    • Help manage interaction with suppliers
    • Write documentation of all executed tasks in a ticket tool and CMDB
    • Support team in daily cloud and infrastructure related tasks

    You have…
    • High level knowledge and understanding of VMware enterprise products
    • Good working knowledge of NSX
    • Good working knowledge of vRealize Application Suite
    • Administration of Cloud and private Cloud datacenter infrastructure
    • Good working knowledge of public cloud services including AWS and Google Cloud would be an advantage
    • Sound understanding of network general knowledge (routing, firewalling, load balancing concepts)

    Be welcome to send us your up to date CV in English and we will offer in return...
    • Exciting work environment that brings people together
    • Use of the latest digital technologies
    • Initial and ongoing trainings to support your development
    • Opportunities for personal and professional growth
    • Competitive remuneration and bonus scheme linked to individual performance and company results
    • 3 additional non-working days annually
    • Food vouchers
    • Sodexo Sport Pass plan B-Active preferences
    • Health insurance
    • Discount program with external vendors
    • Eco friendly travellers are welcome to the office – parking places for bikers and free card for public transportation are available to all employees
    • Variety of sport activities such as football and traditional Bulgarian dances
    • Last but not least – GfK Sofia office is located close to the city centre and easily accessible from any point by public transportation – 47A Tsarisgradsko Shose Blvd
