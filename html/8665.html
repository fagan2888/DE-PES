A Day in the Life

Responsibilities may include the following and other duties may be assigned.
  • Develops, inspects, mines, transforms, and models data to raise productivity, improve decision making, and gain competitive advantage.

  • Conducts statistical analysis on data and information to ensure correct predictive forecasting or classification
  • Manages all aspects of end-to-end data processing utilizing customized report building functions of systems.
  • Maintains analytical systems, verifies the accuracy of the data, its integrity and normalization, and acts as a liaison to the business.
  • Instructs sales, marketing managers, field area managers, and support staff in accessing sales and marketing information.
  • Supports sales incentive plan design and methodology
  • Align work between Sales Incentive team and the needs of the Sales Force
  • Leads projects to support Business strategies to deliver growth
  • Provides consulting and project management to the Business Unit
  • Be the go-to person for any ad-hoc data-related questions

    • Must Have: Minimum Requirements
    • Requires practical knowledge in leading and managing the execution of processes (project management skills), projects and tactics within one job area.
    • Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, Qlik View and Power BI
    • High level of written and verbal communication skills.
    • Requires a Bachelor degree and minimum of 7 years relevant experience, or advanced degree with a minimum of 5 years prior relevant experience.
    • Portuguese language
    Nice to Have

    Previous knowledge of relational databases and query language as SQL is a plus
