Job Description

Location: Krakow/ Poland

The position is to manage technical data and documents of assigned upstream operating units, are free of data defects, in accordance to Controls and Standards.

Accountabilities
  • Support the Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS) activities for various Business Units,
  • Create/Develop Standard Work Instructions ways of working for PM data management for assigned processes,
  • Support Operating unit Run & Maintain Process to meet their Plant maintenance data request by bringing process improvements through automation & continously improving the process,
  • Managing business partners/ representatives of various operating units through effective communication and identifying & resolving the bottlenecks for data analysis, maintaining issue logs and use the same in implementing changes in the processes.

Requirements
  • University Degree in Mechanical Engineering/Electrical/instrument or related university degree
  • Minimum 5 years of overall working experience in in Oil & Gas/ Petrochemicals /Energy/ Maintenance
  • Strong proficiency in using English for both spoken and written communication
  • Hands on Experience in Plant Maintenance area within oil & gas and Petro-chemical complex as an engineering/end user of SAP PM Module/ Computerized Maintenance Management Systems (CMMS) .
  • Experience in Technical/Maintenance Data and Documents management including processes like tools - SAP PM module/ MAXIMO /PACER
  • Understand the Function of Equipments in the Site and has indepth understanding of P&ID's and Process Engineering Flow schemes.
  • Experience in processing the master data change requests (Z6) in SAP PM and Safety Critical Elements is and added value
  • Virtual working experience/ International Project management
