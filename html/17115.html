Who We Are

Incorta delivers the industry’s first hyper-converged analytics platform. We are the fastest way to analyze complex data which powers business decisions in seconds not weeks. Incorta is on a mission to bring quicker, better insights and data-driven decisions to every business. Everything we do is aimed at making sure our customers love every aspect of Incorta, from our great products and technologies to our ease of doing business and support.

DevOps Cloud Engineer focuses on operating the Cloud Environments internal , POC and customer related environments . The main goals are to deliver a managed service, to continuously increase the level of automation of our work, to ensure high availability of the service and make sure our infrastructure is secure . He/She will be working closely with different type of cloud platforms including (AWS,GCP and Microsoft Azure )

  • Support cloud deployments .
  • Develop and implement Dev/Test environments including (Preparing VMs with proper testing data ) .
  • Manage different types of SQL and NoSQL databases.
  • Manage cloud resources patching and upgrade activities .
  • Support benchmark testing environments .
  • Support with new technologies configuration and testing activities .
  • Troubleshooting cloud/environment incidents and provide root cause .
  • Suggesting effective and automated enhancements for routine tasks .
  • Take ownership and drive resolution for environmental issues .
  • Implement highly available and fault tolerant cloud environments .
  • Proactive monitoring for cloud resources .
  • Cloud resources security .
  • Cloud infrastructure and configuration management automation .
  • Support current CI/CD pipelines .
