Job DescriptionWho we are Vanquis helps people who are excluded by mainstream banks take control of their financial situation. Whether they're new to credit or need a hand rebuilding their credit file to manage life better, we make sure our services are straightforward and suitable for them to succeed. We're here to put people on a path to a better everyday life, not just our 1.7 million customers but our colleagues too. And we're proud of the difference we make. You and your team Data is such an important focus at Vanquis where we use it every day to better utilise data as whole improving our customer's outcomes and satisfaction! As a Data Engineer, you will have interactions with all areas of the business as well as collaborating with other technology Teams. The Data Engineering team are accountable for building data pipelines keeping in mind that the processes are reliable and adhere to best practice. In your day to day role you will: Translate raw data into usable datasets to support business requirements. Deliver robust, high-performing and scalable solutions for loading, transforming and processing company data. Develop adhering to the current development best practices whilst contributing to the continual improvement of these practices. Maintain formal data and metadata structures (lineage) to support integrity and searchability of information via a data dictionary. Provide handover documentation for delivered solutions. Working collaboratively with: The Data Engineering lead to understanding policy and strategies Data engineers for guidance and review of tasks Our Data Architect to define and build data marts Our Data Platform team to ensure high level of performance and provide support for delivered solutions What you'll bring to the team As a Data Engineer you will be looking at the way we can transform our data sets while staying ahead of the curve with the technology stack we are using. As a team, Data work on all areas of the business and is crucial to how we move forward not only within technology but in the commercial world. Exposure to engineering tools and processes with one or more languages (Python, Scala, java, JavaScript (node.js), .net/C#, Go, R, etc). Adopting a creative & thoughtful approach to design and solving problems. Ability to communicate clearly on all levels of the business. Demonstrated ability to analyse and interpret complex problems or processes that span multiple business areas, identify and understand requirements, and develop alternate solutions. Why Vanquis? Our colleagues are the most important part of delivering our purpose to put customers on a path to a better everyday life. If it wasn't for their amazing efforts, ideas and energy we wouldn't be the successful business that we are today. So we offer an attractive, competitive salary and benefits package for everyone including: Competitive base salary and rewards Discretionary bonus scheme 25 days holiday entitlement - with one day accrued for each year of service (to max 30 days) Defined contribution pension scheme Private Medical Insurance for you and your family (partner and dependant children) Extensive opportunities for personal and career development Flexible and dynamic working policies A range of initiatives offered through our Sports & Social and Social Impact Programmes Perks at Work - A perks platform offering over 30,000 unique discounts Free tea, coffee and fruit Discounted gym membership Subsidised on-site restaurants Find out more Check out our social media channels find out more about everyday life at Vanquis - and don't forget to follow us while you're there!
