Position


1. Contribute to business management by effectively solving business-related problems through data modeling/analysis/prediction.
2. Analyze and process a large volume of data in order to guarantee high quality data for AI algorithms


Reponsibilities

- Contribute to data organization, synchronization and cleaning from a set of different devices
- Analyze data in order to understand behavior of the devices used (data mining, visualization, etc.)
- Organize data in order to guarantee high quality data for AI algorithms


Main Requirements:


1. Solid Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or similar formal education;
2. Solid knowledge and skills in: temporal-series data analysis, statistics and modeling, machine learning algorithms and libraries;
3. Experience in Data Mining, Data Analytics, and Data Visualization;
4. Experience in data modeling and SQL/NoSQL DBMS;
5. Experience with data analysis for a high volume of data;
6. Solid programming skills including (a) programming languages Python/R, Java/Scala, or C/C++, (b) programming paradigms, and (c) programming tools;
7. Proven 3+ years of experience applying required skills in advanced and meaningful set of projects.


Education Degree


1. Bs.C. in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or other similar titles .
2. Ms.C. or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or similar area (nice to have).
