Sr. Solution Architect - Big Data, Cloud, Analytics

Michael Page Central, HK

1 day ago

Apply on company website
  • Experienced Solution Architect (Analytics) / Data Architect
  • Knowledgeable in big data, cloud or analytics solutions
  • Needs to be agile and dynamic
  • Support servicing industries


Client Details


Our client is a fast-growing technology-focus management consultancy firm which is part of a listed company. Their key clientele lies across the servicing industry.


Description

  • Work closely with both Business (Sales) and IT Delivery team to identify customer requirements and opportunities to adopt innovative technologies, and provide comprehensive technical solution to help solve the customers' business needs.
  • Perform hands-on solution architecture (data architecture) design work and actively participate in solution design for clients to ensure both delivery of desired business outcomes and alignment are up to enterprise standards.
  • Define the scope of work of the projects and ensure requirements are listed clearly to make the execution happen. Conduct extra research on a client's existing systems and configurations, work with internal development team and external vendor to determine approaches, deliverable, timeline, required resources and cost if needed
  • Interface with clients from the initial conversations to determine project scope and specifications to the final presentation of the software solutions
  • Provide client feedback and updates on the development projects directly
  • Provide architectural governance and review projects to ensure alignment to the architectural and technical strategy




Profile

  • 10+ years' experience working in IT, with recent years in data architect /solution architect implementing solutions in a regulated environment.
  • With expert knowledge in supporting applications and infrastructure
  • Ability to explain complex technical solutions to audiences at different technical knowledge
  • Ability to articulate business case for IT investments to business
  • Solid experience of Big Data architect, Cloud or Analytics Solution projects
  • Sound knowledge of innovative technologies, design patterns and keep abreast of latest technology trends
  • Proficient experience in designing integrations between different systems, working with different stakeholders and driving an agreement on the technologies and methods to use.
  • Practitioner of agile methodologies
  • Establish trusted advisor at senior customer on technology visions and strategy
  • Experience of Service Agreements / Statement of work documentation.
  • Be able to communicate effectively with excellent presentation skills, strong written and oral skills, customer relationship skills including the ability to quickly create productive collaborations among key stakeholders






Job Offer


Outstanding remuneration package will be offered to the right candidate.
