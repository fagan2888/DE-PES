    The Specifics:

    The Team: Design, Delivery & Service Operations

    Contract Type: Permanent

    Work Schedule: Normal Working Hours

    Home office opportunity: Yes, depending on further agreement

    What you'll be doing:

    As Senior Data Engineer, you will be in charge of:

    • Being responsible for understanding business needs to design and build new databases to satisfy business requirements.
    • Being an expert at turning complex data and large data sets into easy to understand visuals which can be presented and articulated to senior audiences (up to MD level).
    • Exploring and discovering opportunities for data acquisition within the company and identify areas which can be developed into data science/analysis opportunities.
    • Identifying deficiencies in data and recommend measures to improve data reliability and quality. Collaborating with stakeholders dealing with database management and data in general.
    • Developing robust pipelines for data modelling, mining and usage in production.
    • Using popular data tools and scripting languages (Python, R) to facilitate seamless integration into existing systems.
    • Using your skills and knowledge to support the work of the Data Scientists and Data Analysts in the team.


    We'll also need to see these on your CV:

    Soft skills:

    • Ability to meet the customers’ needs in line with the business requirements is an advantage
    • Focusing on the objectives and the required outcomes of the processes during delivering a service
    • Working with a cooperative and positive attitude in a group settings to achieve common goals
    • The ability to convey information to someone effectively and efficiently while creating a good first impression and engaging the audience
    • Ability to comprehend and to systematically organize the various aspects of a problem or situation
    • Taking the responsibility and takes care of the consequences of making a decision
    • Acting in advance of a future situation, e.g. making things happen, putting energy to solve the situation is an advantage
    • Academic - MSc is an advantage


    Professional skills:

    • Oracle SQL/MySQL/PostgreSQL
    • Hadoop plus Big Data tools such as Apache Spark and MapReduce
    • Big Data query tools such as Hive/Impala
    • Knowledge of Python/R/Scala, non-relational databases such as NoSQL, MongoDB is an advantage
    • Experience with ETL is an advantage


    Language skills:

    • English on a fluent level
