As a member of KPMG's IT Enablement and Transformation service line, you will contribute as an IT advisor for top clients in engagements and also internal projects. As a manager you will be responsible to directly engage with clients for IT advisory delivery and expected to deliver quality high work with supervision from project managers.



Qualifications / Requirements:

  • Min 5 - 7 years SAP SD/ FICO/ MM professional in analyzing, designing, building, implementing, and supporting SAP SD/ FICO/ MM solutions to achieve defined client business goals – and must possess a minimum 2 full cycles SAP SD/ FICO/ MM implementation experience.

  • General SAP skill qualifications:

- Developing functional specification, SAP SD/ FICO/ MM end-to-end configuration,                               prepare & execute testing and training, support data migration and post go-live.

Having SAP ABAP Debugging experience is a plus

-  Analyze & Design: map all gathered information and requirements into integrated SAP                   SD/ FICO/ MM solution by developing business blueprint, business requirement                                   document, to-be process flow, gap analysis, SAP authorization, roles matrix et cetera

-  Lead workshops with client’s business and IT stakeholders to develop business

requirements, build prototype, suggest best practice solutions


  • Experience in managing a team as an  SAP SD/ FICO/ MM module lead

  • Good knowledge of integration points among SAP SD/ FICO/ MM  and other SAP module

    Good understanding of underlying technology and integration with other IT systems

  • Result oriented, and must be able to demonstrate a “can-do” attitude (adaptability, flexibility and resourcefulness)


To apply please attach your CV and not just with your LinkedIn profile only.


*) Due to number of applications, regretfully only shortlisted candidate will be contacted.
