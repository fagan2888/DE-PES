    Location

    UK, Germany, France, Netherlands, Czech Republic, Italy or Romania – Applications from candidates based in these countries will be considered.


    With the proliferation of data protection vendors, Veeam’s Competitive Intelligence team is expanding to ensure our Veeam teams and Partners have the necessary edge to keep Veeam the #1 data protection provider.


    You will be tasked with covering major and emerging competitors that Veeam sales teams and Partners will encounter, gathering the intelligence to empower the business (Sales, Systems Engineers, Marketing, Channels, Executives) and our Partners to differentiate Veeam from a specific set of competitors in order to gain market share.


    Job Function and Key Responsibilities

    • Market intelligence gathering – Understand the competitive landscape and trends, including current and emerging vendors, their sales motions, as well as marketing, product, go-to-market, and organizational strategies. It includes mining considerable amounts of data and data sources to extract information
    • Competitive differentiation – Support development of differentiating products, messaging, processes, and services
    • Competitive positioning – Create internal and external competitive messaging that explains Veeam’s unique benefits. It includes translating technical differences into business benefits (translate technical/tactical insight into business/strategic intelligence)
    • Sales enablement – Train Sales and Channel teams on how to differentiate Veeam from the competition at both business and technical levels
    • Sales support – Develop sales tools to help Sales teams and Partners in competitive opportunities as well as personally support Sales teams in competitive opportunities
    • Marketing support – Help drive quick adoption of new products and capabilities by working closely with Marketing and Product Marketing to educate organizations on Veeam’s unique business values and capabilities
    • Win/Loss analysis – Determine win/loss reasons and make tactical and strategic recommendations to increase win rate
    • Competitive Intelligence consumers support – Assess the needs of Sales, Systems Engineers, Marketing, Channels, and Executives related to the competitors you cover and ensure your resources and services fulfil these needs, and are easily accessible and consumable. It includes promoting Competitive Intelligence resources, guiding people, delivering presentations and trainings


    Required Skills and Experience


    Technical

    • Backup software experience: First-hand experience with data protection software, disaster recovery tools, copy data management software, and software-defined storage or similar.
    • Mastery of data protection concepts and functionalities
    • Virtualization hypervisors experience: First-hand experience of VMware, Hyper-V, Nutanix AHV, or similar. Mastery of virtualization concepts


    Non-Technical

    • Competitive Intelligence (ideal, not mandatory) : : Completion of competitive analyses, contribution to or creation of Competitive Intelligence processes and resources
    • Technical Sales (Sales Engineer/Presales) : Experience differentiating a solution technically from competitors and working with technical decision makers.
    • Collaboration: Experience working in a matrixed organization
    • Presentation: Experience presenting to large groups, to technical audiences, to business audiences, to Executives
    • Speed: Experience working in agile organizations with a start-up mentality
    • Product evaluation: Experience deploying, managing, or testing data protection software
    • Communication : English fluency (written, oral, presentation)
    • Autonomy : Ability to work with little supervision, act proactively and meet deadlines
