Who we are

An ambitious team turning big dreams into reality

The $100 billion gaming industry is on the verge of revolution. For decades, gamers have suffered under the pretense of ownership: purchasing digital assets to which they hold but negligible rights. Now, with the advent of blockchain technology, comes the opportunity to empower gamers with true ownership. At Immutable, our mission is to lead this revolution and transform the space as social and mobile platforms did years ago.

With a small but exceptional team of artists, designers and engineers we are building Gods Unchained: a AAA trading card game where players have complete control over their assets. As we approach launch, we need the help of other talented professionals to help bring our game to the world.

That's where you come in...

Job Description

Immutable doesn't just pay lip service to data, we live it. One of our values is 'We are scientists: We hypothesis, we test and we iterate'. We are looking for a passionate data engineer to design and build scalable data infrastructure to support this goal.

Design the data infrastructure for a scaling company

This is a great opportunity to design a modern-day data infrastructure from the ground up. We currently cloud based, storing our events on a postgres server hosted on AWS. You will be able to pick the tools to create a data lake and multi-speed processing architecture that can handle our growing user base and functional requirements.

Stay abreast of best practices within data

You will be heavily hands on implementing the architecture you design as the first data engineer hire into the company. The company will support your continued education and development to ensure our data infrastructure remains best in class.

Grow soft skills

This role will involves a high degree of collaboration with other engineering teams and business stakeholders. You will have opportunities to cover everything from requirements gathering to project management to presentations.

Preferred Tech Stack
  • Experience with AWS technologies strongly preferred (i.e. S3, EC2, Kinesis, Redshift, Athena)
  • Golang (but experience with any statically typed language is fine)
  • PostgreSQL
  • python (not a requirement)
Job description
  • Builds systems to collect, process, store and deliver data across a wide range of use cases from reporting to near real time analytics
  • Manage and enhance the data warehousing environment, taking it through future iterations.
  • Define and implement technical requirements and technical data architectures for the data warehouse
  • Partner with VP-Data and functional leads (marketing, product, game design) to deliver analysis ready databases
  • Maintains and improves data quality and provides regular reports to management
  • Manage data security and privacy, including GDPR compliance
No remote working: Whilst we want to support remote working in the medium-term, we are only hiring for onsite roles to promote collaboration and grow our culture.
