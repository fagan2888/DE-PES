What We're Looking For

Title: Platform Software Engineer (Principal)

We are looking to hire a Principal Platform Software Engineer to lead the development of software components and features in our world-class Analytics Services in the Cloud.

You will join a crack team of experienced and talented software engineers based in Patras Greece, a Citrix Site with years of history delivering high-quality carrier-grade products and global cloud services, in a fast-paced business environment.

This position requires passion for building cutting-edge software in the cloud, with scalability and performance, and offers the opportunity to increase your skills, competencies and innovation to new levels.

Our market leadership and large installed Customer base also present a unique opportunity to experience real-life deployments and problem-solving techniques that will become an invaluable individual career asset.

Role Responsibilities
  • Design and build scalable, high-performance distributed systems processing billions of transactions per day, globally
  • Lead the design, development, maintenance of high-performing, highly-scalable Platform components/features, for our Analytics Cloud Service
  • Enhance troubleshooting and diagnostics capabilities of our Platform
  • Collaborate with colleagues in other teams (frontend, backend) to introduce e2e diagnostics/troubleshooting telemetry/tooling, scenario-based e2e testing automation
  • Own the whole life-cycle of your work; from design, to development, to fully automated testing, to performance and scaling, to working directly with Ops

Essential Requirements
  • You will need 5+ years professional software development in C++ (preferable), Java or C# (expert level)
  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field; equivalent experience may be substituted for formal education
  • Commercial experience designing and developing scalable, high-performance, high-availability, multi-threaded, distributed systems software
  • Ideally you have an understanding of and interest in asynchronous event-based systems
  • Strong quality-oriented mentality through thoughtful architecting, design, prudent testing
  • Strong foundation and discipline in software engineering processes
  • Knowledge of source-control management/versioning concepts and tools (such as Git)
  • Proficiency in one or more scripting languages (e.g. Python, shell programming)
  • Knowledge of the Linux O/S

Preferred Skills/Qualifications
  • Cloud development experience (AWS, GCP)
  • Experience with CI/CD pipelines
  • Cloud security concepts
  • Experience collaborating efficiently with Customers and Field personnel, with a passion to diagnose, troubleshoot and help address real Customer situations in an effective manner
  • Professional experience with Boost, Folly, Proxygen libraries
  • Knowledge and experience with Agile methodologies (e.g. Scrum)
  • Solid understanding of networking and data communications - TCP/IP, DNS, Routing
  • Experience with Big Data and large-scale Databases
  • Experience with Kafka, BigQuery
