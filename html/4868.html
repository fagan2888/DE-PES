About The Project

We help to develop a modern payment platform for medium and large businesses in the US and Canada that is heading further into the world, for example, management of virtual payment cards or corporate payments. Your task will be to help connect the platform with several internal and external systems.
  • you’ll be part of a small team of 7-8 people – just 1 girl, but we’re looking for more!
  • the team consists of five Data Engineers, one Data Architect, and one Team Leader.
  • Eddie, Team Leader in Utah, will be the closest person you will work with.
  • even though you will work in Ostrava, in spirit you will be with colleagues in Utah.
  • our developers and QA will help you and keep you company at our office.
What you will do
  • write pipelines and connectors to connect internal (on-premise database/cloud) and external systems (3rd party APIs, e.g. from banks) to SnowFlake.
  • write microservices in Python that process data from Snowflake.
  • deploy what you write to AWS.

What You Should Know/be Able To Do
  • Python is the foundation of everything.
  • a degree is not important, but you need to have an idea of how frameworks, protocols, and data management systems work.
  • you have experience with the operation of cloud storage (AWS, Azure or other).
  • you will use English daily to communicate with your closest colleagues from the USA (e.g. via Slack).
What will please you
  • quarterly bonuses: 25 percent of company profit is shared with our employees.
  • ability to travel on business trips to the USA.
  • home office (2 days a week), flexible working hours.
  • 5 weeks of vacation plus 2 sick days.
  • free English language lessons with a native speaker in the office (2 hours a week).
  • informal modern offices with a refresh zone and common breakfast every Friday.
  • support of sporting events (starting fee, jersey…) plus shared Multisport card.
  • meal tickets – 100 Kc a day.
  • opportunity to expand your career on a professional and financial level. You can become a software and technology expert that will lecture at conferences or profile yourself as a top IT manager.
  • company and spontaneous events.
