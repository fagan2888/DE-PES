Exiger is the global leader in providing technology-enabled compliance solutions to the financial and corporate marketplace. Its combination of subject matter expertise and disruptive purpose-built technology is changing the way companies tackle their biggest compliance challenges. As Exiger professionals, we create value for our clients and stakeholders by living our guiding principles: Expertise, Trust, Innovation, Excellence, Teamwork, Integrity & Courage.


Research Associates are creative and inquisitive. They use a wide range of research tools to gather risk and compliance intelligence on individuals and companies. The research is then analyzed and developed into a narrative report, which is used by clients to understand the general background of the subject, assess the subject’s legitimacy and reputation, and inform strategic decision-making. Research Associates work both independently and collaboratively on research and reports for a variety of domestic and international clients.


Please submit a English version of your resume to be considered for our Research Associate role!


Key Responsibilities

    • Work with a growing team to research and investigate cases using a combination of transactional information, customer due diligence, case management system information, web-based compliance tools, and open source searches.
    • Apply diligence in reviewing files and data to identify deficiencies, discrepancies, and omissions.
    • Review negative news, sanctions, and/or Politically Exposed Persons (PEP) alerts.
    • Investigate and/or escalate any identified risk factors.
    • Exercise independence in recommending cases for escalation or closure.
    • Develop and maintain professional working relationships with colleagues and clients.


Knowledge & Skills

    • Strong analytical and research skills.
    • Solid communication (written and verbal) and organizational skills.
    • Ability to deliver high quality work in a fast-paced, high volume environment with strict deadlines.
    • Strong commitment to continuous improvement and learning, with an openness to receiving feedback and training.
    • Team player with high level of initiative and a proactive approach to work.
    • Ability to work effectively in a team as well as independently; takes ownership of tasks and follow up to ensure that they are successfully brought to completion.
    • Ability to utilize technology to solve problems.
    • Keen interest in the banking and financial services sector.


Professional Experience Required

    • Proficient written and verbal English language skills.
    • Bachelor's Degree preferred.
    • 0-2 years of professional experience.
    • Reading proficiency in Portuguese
