THE ROLE

The Network Services Operations Level 2 position is a key role within the Command Center organisation to support the ever growing Network, Wireless, Security and Unified Communications products and services provided by SITA globally.

Within the Command Center organization this position is the Subject Matter Expert for the Network Services and Unified Communications. The role of the Command Center is to be the provider of end-to-end support to our customer base worldwide, thus the high level of expertise required to ensure the Command Center is fully self sufficient.

The primary focus is to ensure timely resolution of incidents brought to Level 2 and apply approved changes where applicable. You’re expected to provide knowledge sharing and transfer to the rest of the team.

Experience, Knowledge & Skills
  • Diploma/Degree in Computer Science, Data Communications, Engineering (or equivalent)
  • At least 4 years of experience in an operational role in a 24-7 organisation
  • Industry Technical Certifications are required Cisco, Fortinet, Juniper Palo Alto etc
  • Good knowledge on one or more of the following areas
  • LAN/WAN/Wifi (Cisco Nexus, Juniper EX, Aruba, Huawei etc)
  • Protocols (BGP, OSPF, EIGRP, MPLS, Multicast, QoS, SIP, VoIP etc)
  • Security Management (Cisco, Juniper, Fortinet, Palo Alto, VMWare NSX etc)
  • Unified Communication (Cisco UC Manager, Cisco Voice Gateways, Microsoft Lync, PureCloud, Call Accounting, Contact Center etc)
  • Tools and Automation for LAN/WAN/Wifi/Firewall etc
  • Application compression, optimization and load balancer (Riverbed, F5 BigIP, Netscaler etc)
  • Virtualization (VMWare) and Cloud Operations (IaaS, DaaS, SaaS etc)
  • Experience in project delivery
  • Knowledge of ATI Operations would be a strong benefit.
