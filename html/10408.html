Join us today and make a difference in people's lives!

We are looking across Europe, Talented Embedded Software Engine ers who wants to join us in our Munich facility, to provide hope for patients and their families through innovative medical technologies , delivering life-changing improvements for both the Head and Heart.

We want to hear from Embedded Software Engineers who want to help define, architect, design and develop our next-generation Products. We look for people passionate about technology and love to collaborate! Using your excellent communication skills, we will be looking to you to not only be technically strong but to be able to present and create technical documentation to back up your work.

Job Summary

The Embedded Software Engineer will work within the Cardiac Surgery Business Franchise and will support the HW/FW Team on the development of new products. He/she will work specifically on life-sustaining devices as Heart-Lung Machines on a wide spectrum of tasks during the concept, design and verification phase. The incumbent customizes the Software platform (e.g. Linux) and develops Embedded Software applications that meet all predefined requirements; establishes product requirement specifications in close collaboration with the other stakeholders; defines the Software Architecture considering all constraints coming from legacy products, develops the Software and creates the necessary detailed documentation; defines the Test cases and verifies the SW through up-to-date static/dynamic code analysis tools; contributes to the improvement of SW Development processes.

Essential Job Functions
  • The Embedded SW Engineer will be responsible for the development of Embedded Software for medical products.
  • Will work mainly on Heart-Lung Machines comprised of electrical, mechanical, electromagnetic, user interfaces and software from both a total system as well as a component perspective.
  • Will work on up-to-date High-safety HMIs interfaced with sensor probes and sensor interfaces as well as actuators.
  • Will be responsible, for the specific sub-system assigned, for the software development and release activities, including design, test, documentation, build management, configuration control.
  • Will define the Software Architecture, considering all constraints coming from legacy products or other sub-systems, and develop necessary libraries, source code, compiled binary code and GUI software elements.
  • Will report/resolve software bugs/issues.
  • Will contribute to the continuous improvement of SW Development processes.


Skills
  • Able to work in a fast-paced multicultural environment.
  • Open-minded.
  • Hands-on attitude.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Management of 3rd party contract designers, manufacturers, suppliers.
  • Good command of English language. Fluency in German language is appreciated.

Knowledge/Abilities
  • Strong programming skills in C, C++
  • Strong knowledge of microprocessor based embedded systems, mainly ARM processors.
  • Solid experience with Qt Graphic Framework and developing complex Graphic User Interfaces.
  • Solid experience of Software platform development, such as Embedded Linux and custom builds.
  • Experience with GUI test tools (e.g. Squish) as well as static/dynamic code analysis for SW Unit and SW Integration testing (e.g. VectorCAST and Polyspace).
  • Knowledge of SCM tools as SVN, GIT, JIRA, Eclipse IDE, ClearCase etc.
  • Knowledge and previous experience working with Agile methodologies.
  • Knowledge of Application Lifecycle Management tools as Polarion.
  • Knowledge of EU, US and international medical quality, safety standards (IEC 62304, ISO 14971, ISO 13485).
  • Operation and use of standard test and measuring instruments.
  • Candidates with Bachelors/Masters Degree in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or equivalent are preferred.
