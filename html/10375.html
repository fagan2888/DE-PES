The Challenge

There’s never been a better time to join Adobe. We are looking for outstanding talent ready to make an impact, an engineer passioned about Linux Services, automation, CI/CD, cloud and monitoring technologies. The successful candidate will work as part of the Cloud Services Team, performing system and software duties for our large-scale cloud based platforms and services.

The Role

Areas of focus for this role include but are not limited to:
  • IT Service Design & Support – The candidate will be responsible for working closely with our Architecture and Product teams to identify service gaps, design new services, update existing technical services and then implement these services throughout our complex worldwide infrastructure.
  • DevOps and Automation – A key part of the teams services are related to scaling our infrastructure using software and industry best practices such as Infrastructure as Code and CI/CD.
  • Operational Support – We receive numerous requests daily to update, expand, and support our supported IT services. We need someone to be able to understand the customer request and provide them with responsive and friendly service.
  • Performance & Optimization – Work with product teams to determine correct workloads and provide optimization guidelines regarding supported platforms. We need someone who can ensure that our multi-thousand node marketing cloud service is running at peak performance and throughput at all times.

What You Need To Succeed

The successful candidate will demonstrate experience or capability in the following areas:
  • Experience with programming/scripting languages such as Python, Ruby, Bash, Perl.
  • Experience with Unix/Linux system administration. CentOS preferred, but not required.
  • Experience supporting Linux services such as: DNS, FTP, DHCP, LDAP, SMTP, RabbitMQ, SysLog, Kickstart, Apache, MySQL, Memcache, Kafka.
  • Recent large-scale experience with configuration management tools such as Puppet, Ansible, Salt, Chef, or equivalent.
  • Experience with platform monitoring and alerting tools such as Nagios, Grafana, New Relic, CollectD, Prometheus, RRD.
  • Strong comprehension of continuous integration and continuous deployment methodologies and tools such as Jenkins, Terraform, Spinnaker.
  • Developing, running, and/or consuming cloud technologies such as AWS, Azure, OpenStack, Google Cloud Platform.
  • Experience with complex IP networking capabilities in large production environments.
  • Experience support high availability services in global computing environment (Pacemaker, Corosync, HA Proxy, Zookeper).
  • Experience supporting security policies, standards and frameworks (including SOC2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI, etc.)
  • Knowledge of software version control systems (e.g. Git) and release management process.
  • Experience running software container solutions desired, such as Docker and Kubernetes

About Our Ideal Candidate
  • Passionate about infrastructure operations. We want someone who does not have a preference for one tool but rather looks for the right tool for the job.
  • Analytical with the ability to fix complex system problems, to assess/resolve system performance problems, and can troubleshoot situations looking for all the options and turning over every rock to find the best solution.
  • Eager to learn, to take guidance, and understanding that there is always more to learn.
  • Ability to work hard as a member of a small team that is asked to do a lot while always remaining customer focused.
  • Can work well as part of a multi-functional team of developers and operations.
  • Is able to manage time properly such that commitments are communicated and then met.
  • Able to think holistically and excel at systems thinking.
