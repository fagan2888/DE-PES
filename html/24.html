    Head of Workforce Analytics and Business Development at Houston Analytics Ltd

    • Houston Analytics is looking for a new member to join our growing team of data scientists, engineers, analysts and information management experts who are dedicated to generating data-driven insights and solutions for our demanding global clients and our own products.


    The team works in collaboration with domain experts, product managers, sales team and client representatives. Together, we strive for creating practical and effective means to deliver world class data solutions for our clients needs.

    The Lead Data Engineer role and Your impact

    We are looking for Lead Data Engineer who is passionate about both data and building infrastructure to support teams who use it.


    In the Lead Data Engineer role your job is to independently use your skills and vision to support the development and deployment of technical data infrastructure supporting Integrations, Cloud-based data pipelines and Data warehousing.


    As a Lead Data Engineer you'll be tasked to lead and manage the team and ensure quality. You'll also be occasionally asked to support our pre-sales team and if you like to - contributing in developing our working methods.

    What you will do

    • Design, build and scale solutions and services from technical proof of concepts to industrialized, fully automated and scalable services and solutions
    • Mentor and collaborate with data scientists in building scalable analyses and data sets.
    • Collaborate with other data engineers, data scientists and stakeholders, taking learning and leadership opportunities that will arise every single day
    • Work in multi-functional teams to continuously experiment, iterate and deliver on new projects and product objectives
    • Evolve our data platform capabilities and tooling with hands-on collaboration to technology research and prototyping


    Who you are

    To succeed in this role, you'll have experience from projects where you've been charged to manage extensive data set processing and how these data sets are being described at their different life stages.


    We require that you have the capabilities what it takes to learn and adopt the new features of the technologies being used and train and implement those changes to the organization.


    Below, some of the qualifications for this position:

    • Databases we work with: Oracle, SQL Server, DB2, Netezza, Teradata, Postgres
    • Expert level SQL scripting and query optimization
    • ETL, Big Data Processing, Data Warehouse design
    • Version management, access control, data security
    • You are a communicative person who values building strong relationships with colleagues and partners, enjoys mentoring and teaching others and you have the ability to explain complex topics in simple terms
    • Good communication skills in English (Finnish is a plus)
    • You are pragmatic and understand the trade-offs between the perfect solution and a working solution
    • Experience in working at analytics and reporting projects is considered as an advantage
    • You have a few years of relevant experience, or much more; we welcome diverse applicants with various seniority levels.
    • You know how to work with high volume heterogeneous data, preferably with distributed systems such as Hadoop, BigTable, and Cassandra
    • You are knowledgeable about data modeling, data access, and data storage techniques
    • You appreciate agile software processes, data-driven development, reliability, and responsible experimentation
    • You understand the value of partnership within teams


    You will work from our awesome headquarter office located in near Helsinki city center.

    What you can expect from us?

    We offer you an innovative and challenging environment to further develop your skills and apply them across a variety of projects from practical business focused data problems to strategic digitization initiatives related to Digital Twin, AI, Machine Learning etc.


    Data Engineering will be a core capability to enable our next generation solutions for industrializing data science and you will play a key role in building it.


    We provide industry standard compensation and benefits with several learning and professional development opportunities

    Got interested? Let’s chat!

    Please tell us a bit about yourself, your background and future interests. We want to get to know you and together figure out what would work for both of us.


    Send your application by email to careers@houston-analytics.com with title "lead data engineer position"


    For more information about the role and the work at Houston Analytics, please contact country manager Tommi Havukainen either via email tommi.havukainen@houston-analytics.com or calling +358 50 340 6340 on Tuesday 8.10 between 12-16:00 Wednesday 16.10 between 13-16:00 EET.


    We will arrange two interview rounds and you'll have an opportunity to meet with your potential new colleagues and team members. You will also be given the opportunity to prove your technical skills with a small exercise, and we will provide constructive feedback on the results.

    About Houston Analytics


    Houston Analytics is a leading European provider of advanced analytics skills and solutions. We are partners with global technology vendors and niche solution providers and are often called in to develop high value, novel analytics solutions at major projects.


    We at Houston are focused on the industrialization of data science and advanced analytics. In most data science today, one-off analyses by mathematical/statistical experts working at the coding level go no further than research or prototyping. Our approach is focused on delivering operational solutions: we are experts in creating, packaging and automating analytical processes which run continually with minimal human intervention. We have delivered systems of this kind for some of Europe’s largest industrial players, continuously monitoring complex, critical equipment and processes.
