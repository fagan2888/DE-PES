    HR Generalist en Stayforlong.com

    • Stayforlong is the first booking website specializing in long stays. We offer bookings from three nights onwards with excellent discounts depending on the total nights booked. Currently, we work together with 200.000 hotels worldwide and with the main metasearchers such as Trivago, Tripadvisor, Kayak and Google Hotel Ads.


    We’re looking for a highly analytic Data Scientist to join our talented team in Barcelona.


    Job Purpose


    You’ll help us to further understand and model our business, metrics, pricing strategies, and predict, segment and retain our clients. As a Data Scientist, you’ll get the role of creating and optimize product and reporting projects. You’ll be in the team helping us to understand all areas of the business.


    Main job responsibilities are


    • Apply statistical methods (and some machine learning) to improve our metrics, reporting, pricing strategies and increase sales through those improvements.
    • Be the master for Data Analysis at the company.
    • Monitor daily results and improve KPIs performance.
    • Explore our data to find growth opportunities.
    • You’ll help draw out the product roadmap, design experiments to estimate the impact of future releases.
    • Build fast data visualizations.
    • Build ETLs and all the infrastructure and engineering behind.
    • Help us on the migration to real-time architecture.


    Desired skills and experience:


    • You’ve at least 2-3 year of experience as a data scientist, data analyst, or business analyst.
    • You’re comfortable talking about mathematics, statistics, or computer science.
    • You can show us how you applied your knowledge to resolve problems.
    • You’re pragmatic. You value simple and effective solutions over complicated brain-melters.
    • You can take a complex concept and make it sound simple, using clear visual references to help.
    • You’ve got experience building queries with SQL or other query languages.
    • You can code Python, R, or similar languages.


    What we offer:

    • Flexible schedule. You will be able to work remotely when you want! :)
    • Competitive salary. We offer flexible remuneration system. You will be able to include restaurant card, transport card, kindergarten check, health insurance and training.
    • Tons of coffee for everybody!
    • Excellent work environment.


    If you would like to be a part of our project and join our multicultural and talent team, we would like to meet you!
