Manufacturing Engineer

Real Staffing Sligo, IE

Apply on company website
A Manufacturing Engineer is urgently needed to join a team of highly talented professionals in a growing medical device company in Sligo.

Company/Culture

The Company are well recognised in their field and are located in the heart of Sligo. They are dedicated to ensuring their team culture is as inclusive as possible, often organising team-building events for staff including team day and nights out.. They are well-known for encouraging upskilling and training internally so this is a really good company to be a part of in order to build up your skillset and gain invaluable experience. This is a full-time, permanent position working to a 40 hour week. You will be compensated with an extremely competitive salary (DOE) plus a yearly bonus.

What will I be doing as Manufacturing Engineer?

As Manufacturing Engineer, you will gain exposure to all areas of the business, supporting the Quality Management System with a focus on driving Continuous Improvement within existing systems & processes, and supporting the integration of such improvements into the Quality Management System. You will also support Project teams to ensure effective execution of project/product implementation.

Your background?

To become a Manufacturing Engineer, will be a self-starter, highly motivated, enthusiastic individual, educated to degree level within a discipline pertinent to Engineering. You should have a proven track record of at least three years' experience working in Engineering & Manufacturing based roles, with Medical Device exposure preferred.

Meet the above requirements? Think this is the job for you? If so, please send me an updated CV for immediate consideration.

*EU passport/Stamp 4 Visa required.*
