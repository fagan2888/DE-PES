    • Role:

    We are looking for a Software Engineer to fit in our Agile team, whose main function will be developing and maintaining network security products running on a Linux platform.

    Main Duties:


    • Design and write code in line with specified project timescales, quality and functional requirements.

    • Conduct Unit tests on own code and participate in System and Integration testing of other code.

    • Participate in peer reviews of code and designs as requested, making suggestions to increase quality.

    • Participate in implementing test automation working together with QA engineers.

    • Provide estimates of time required for allocated tasks and clear updates on progress.

    • Schedule own time to deliver against priorities assigned, in line with agreed project timescales.

    • Escalate any problems that need to be addressed in a timely manner, with suggested solutions or workarounds.

    • Build and maintain effective relationships with team members to develop production code.

    • Exchange information with other engineers in the team to identify issues and recommend solutions.

    • Suggest improvements to departmental standards and procedures.

    Essential skills:


    • University/college degree in computer science

    • At least 2 years of experience in using C language on Linux/Unix

    • Comprehensive knowledge of Linux/Unix platforms

    • Knowledge of HTTP/HTTPS protocols

    • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills

    • Experience with unit testing systems

    • Team player

    • Fluent English

    Desirable skills:


    • Experience with Apache web server

    • Experience with (or willingness to learn) Perl or Python

    • Knowledge of SSL/TLS protocols and encryption systems

    • Security software development experience

    • Experience of working in Agile (Scrum, Kanban) environment

    Benefits:


    • Professional advancement and training potential

    • Modern development methodologies and cutting-edge technologies

    • Excellent work environment with a young and agile team

    • Language courses

    • Secure background provided by a stable international company

    • Competitive salary

    Place of work: Budapest XI.

    Working hours: 40 per week
