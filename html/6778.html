    • Data Engineer, EY Transaction Advisory Services (TAS) - Nicosia

    At EY we support you in achieving your unique potential both personally and professionally. We give you stretching and rewarding experiences that keep you motivated, working in an atmosphere of integrity and teaming with some of the world's most successful companies.

    And while we encourage you to take personal responsibility for your career, we support you in your professional development in every way we can. You enjoy the flexibility to devote time to what matters to you, in your business and personal lives. At EY you can be who you are and express your point of view, energy and enthusiasm, wherever you are in the world. It's how you make a difference.

    Job opportunity

    We are currently seeking to recruit a highly motivated Data Engineer for our Transaction Advisory Services practice. Being part of the broader TAS team, you will be responsible for supporting data scientists and analysts, developing required infrastructure and tools to aid in building end-to-end solutions to challenging clients’ business problems.

    You will use your knowledge and experience to shape solutions that go beyond standard ETL pipelines. Your creativity and innovativeness will significantly impact our velocity in delivering cutting edge solutions to our clients, efficiently dealing with data quality issues and pipeline bottlenecks, in multi-faceted engagements.

    To qualify for the role, you must have
    • Strong academic background, including at a minimum a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or any other related field.
    • Fluency in data processing frameworks, preferably in Python.
    • Solid knowledge of writing and tuning SQL.
    • Strong analytical mind-set, ability and experience to analyse complex data, including transactional, behavioural, web and research data.


    Ideally you will also have
    • Any relevant work experience will be considered as an advantage.
    • Good knowledge of web scraping tools and techniques.
    • A master’s degree in the data science field will also be considered as an advantage.
    • Familiarity with the mathematical theory and practice behind common machine learning algorithms.


    What Working At EY Offers

    EY offers a competitive remuneration package where you’ll be rewarded for your personal and team performance. We are committed to being an inclusive employer and are happy to consider flexible working arrangements.

    Plus, we offer
    • Support, coaching and feedback from some of the most engaging colleagues around
    • Opportunities to develop new skills and progress your career
    • The freedom and flexibility to handle your role in a way that’s right for you

    If you can confidently demonstrate that you meet the criteria above, please contact us as soon as possible.

    Join us in building a better working world.
