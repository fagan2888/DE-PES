Presales IP Engineer

We are searching for a Presales IP Engineer to our IP/Optical division .

We offer you an exciting position in a cross functional European team. You will be working with the latest technology and you will be NEC’s key interface for the northern Europe region for all IP design and technical related questions or concerns. In this role, you will work for opportunities initially for the Nordic countries as well as being a centralized resource for Europe/Global business.

The position is based in Budapest, but will require international travel based upon client obligations. You will be part of a small team, in a globally large organization. Your colleagues are based in different countries in Europe and your reporting manager is located at the European headquarter in London. At NEC you work as a team towards common targets.

What We Offer
  • A flexible role, which you can influence how your further development will progress, depending on previous experience and your interests
  • Working with large customers and very latest technology within IP and 5G
  • The opportunity to work remotely and in an international environment


Key Responsibilities
  • Being NEC’s key interface for Nordic region for all IP design and technical related questions or concerns
  • To engage with Sales Account Managers from the early stage of the opportunity cycle.
  • Design and develop innovative and competitive solutions, working individually or leading a wider virtual team for the qualified opportunity
  • Support the Sales Account Manager with the commercials of the opportunity
  • Qualify opportunities and leads based on technical and commercial criteria
  • Adopt a Lead role in major bids and technical responses
  • Actively engage with the market, through key partners, suppliers & vendors and leverage relationships where ever possible


Skills & Experience Required
  • This role requires a few years’ experience from IP related Projects
  • A certification in Cisco or Juniper is required
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills. It is important that you have the ability to articulate technical issues in a meaningful way to both team and executive level management
  • The ability to present and converse in a professional, confidant manner
  • Fluent in English (verbal and written)
  • It is an advantage if you have experience from presales and design environment within Service provider arena. As well as experience from an international work environment.

If you are a candidate who meets the above set of qualifications and looking to make an impact - we would like to hear from you as soon as possible. For any questions regarding the position please feel free to contact Mike Rumis, Head of pre-sales, +44 (0) 7736 890910 or Erica Lander, HR Business Partner + 46 72 162 8631
