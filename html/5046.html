Are you looking to work for one of Sunderland's best and most innovative companies, who really valued their staff? Are you a Data Analyst with solid SQL scripting skills? If so, CV Screen has the role for you!

Role Overview

An experienced SQL Data Analyst, who will have strong SQL & TSQL skills, knowledge of Postrgres and, ideally, an understanding of SSIS and ETL, is required by a leading tech company based in Sunderland. A salary of up to 36,000 plus benefits (inc profit share and healthcare) is offered depending on experience.

Working within the Software and Data Teams, you will take responsibility for publishing and maintaining the SQL script repositories, improving existing scripts, resolving QA problems and all documentation processes.

Required Skills

The SQL Data Analyst will ideally have the following:
  • Excellent knowledge of SQL and T-SQL (SQL Server 2012+)
  • Educated to degree level in IT or Maths
  • Strong Postgres knowledge
  • Any knowledge of SSIS and ETL
Salary
Basic salary to 36,000 + Benefits

Sunderland
