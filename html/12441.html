Zažádat na webu společnosti
You want a dynamic role to challenge you to grow and make an impact? Reinvent with our global award-winning Supply Chain team!

Recognized by Gartner Inc in the 2018 Supply Chain Top 25 companies worldwide, HP continuously pushes boundaries of what’s possible at a magnitude it’s hard to believe.

As a member of the Office Printing Hardware Supply Chain Operations team, you will drive alignment and lead in-region planning & fulfillment activities for a set of given printers and accessories in order to achieve and optimize shipment, revenue, inventory & market share goals while delivering on customer premises. You'll be joining an international team with great team spirit - your colleagues are based in Barcelona, Boeblingen, and Prague.

As a Junior Supply Chain Business Operations Analyst you will be responsible for:
  • Demand planning, order fulfillment, inventory activities for a given list of products;
  • Assessing and understanding product demand, aligning with stakeholders and perform adjustments in the forecast;
  • Short term shipment optimization to support business objectives (e.g. drives inbound prioritization and backlog management);
  • Collaborates with regional and global business unit planning teams;
  • Drives actions for shipments to meet customer expectation (ship on time);
  • Reviews demand planning metrics on a regular basis, identifying opportunities for improvement.

Qualification
  • Graduate or up to 12-month experience – Business or Engineering degree;
  • Working experience during studies preferred;
  • Business process Management skills welcome.

Competencies And Skill Set
  • Very good knowledge of English;
  • Self-driven person, taking ownership and able to drive end to end initiatives;
  • Analytical skills and the capacity to interpret data;
  • Knowledge of Excel;
  • Very good communication skills, internal and towards external partners;
  • Capability to work across different IT environments.

You will be part of a group of people closely interacting and creating a strong team. Learning is ensured through cross-functional interactions and training. Personal skills are supported through trainings or a Mentoring program.
