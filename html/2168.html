Your Role and Responsibilities
  • Design, implement, testing and operating distributed data processing pipelines using tools and languages prevalent in the big data ecosystem
  • Build the ETL pipelines required for optimal extraction, transformation, and loading of data from a wide variety of data sources using NiFi, SQL and other tools.
  • Responsible for implementing methods to improve data reliability, efficiency and quality

    • Required Technical and Professional Expertise
  • Bachelor’s degree or relevant work experience in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Electrical Engineering or related technical discipline.
  • 2+ years of experience developing software with proficiency in programming in Java/Scala
  • Strong understanding of Enterprise architecture patterns, Object Oriented & Service Oriented principles, design patterns.
  • Foundational knowledge of data structures, algorithms, and designing for performance
  • Experience in database technology like DB2 / Oralcle / MySQL
  • Enjoys working with algorithms and data structures (e.g. trees, hash maps, queues)
  • Hadoop/Spark/Flink etc Big Data knowledge will be a plus
  • Self-starter who can work independently and proactively
  • Fluent written and spoken English and Mandarin

    • Preferred Technical And Professional Expertise
    • English fluent
