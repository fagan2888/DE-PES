    General Manager at Friendly Fire Esports

    • We are looking for a Senior Frontend Developer who is a gaming enthusiast and eager to grow and share knowledge.


    The team has 10 mmbrs including a Head of Development.


    Requirements for this position are:


    Development is divided into five components :

    (the brackets are the technologies we use):

    Backend (PHP / Laravel, MySQL / MariaDB, Redis, Linux, C ++, ...)

    Frontend (HTML, CSS, Javascript, Vue.js, ...)

    Desktop app (C ++, Qt, WinAPI, ...) - Mobile app (Qt, Flutter, ...)

    Sysadmin (C #, .NET, Windows Domain, Linux Servers, ...)

    The responsibilities of the Senior Frontend Developer position are:

    Designing and programming key parts of Frontend - responsibility for the development and maintenance of Frontend (functionality, code quality, documentation, maintenance)


    Responsibility for the development and maintenance of our websites
    intensive cooperation with the development manager

    Responsibility for the budget and timetable for the development of Frontend
    selecting and managing internal and external associates working on Frontend development - revision of the associate code and its implementation in the test and production application conditions


    Starting offer is from will be comunicated at first contact.

    You can expect 13k net and higher, dependeble on experinece.

    Company Car is also an option.

    Education and certification also.


    Join us and we will learn from you.


    Best regards
