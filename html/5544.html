التقدم

Description

Incorta is a Silicon Valley based company with two subsidiaries in Cairo and Alexandria. Our Incortian Quality Control Engineers, enable the world to take educated decisions, by simplifying data complexity through the most efficient tools and techniques.

An Incortian “Quality Control Engineer” will be working in a highly creative, motivating, learning and collaborative environment. An environment that is guided with Trust, Respect, Innovation and Passion as our 4 core values and the main drivers at Incorta for the company’s decisions and behaviors.

The intended Quality Control Engineer, will estimate, plan, and coordinate testing activities. While also ensuring that quality issues and defects are appropriately identified, documented, tracked and resolved in our defect tracking system.


Responsibilities


  • Create/review/maintain manual test scripts.
  • Create/review/maintain automated test scripts.
  • Create/resolve Jira issues.
  • Manage test data and environments needed.
  • Deploying a tenant.
  • Running a tenant after various customization/ modifications.
  • Importing / replacing content periodically.


Requirements


  • B.S. degree in CS, software engineering or or any related discipline.
  • 2+ years of software quality control.
  • Strong communication skills, including the ability to explain and teach methodologies.
  • Good knowledge of SDLC & STLC.
  • Good knowledge in Linux & SQL.
  • Familiar with web applications (eg. Tomcat).
  • Familiar with BI & Data analytics basics.
  • Can lead the testing activities of small to medium projects & contribute to planning and estimates.
  • Familiar with automation tools, Good to have: automation experience.
  • Familiar with bug tracking tools (eg. Jira) and test repositories (eg. TestRail, Zephyr).


Benefits


Alongside our competitive financial packages, Incorta offers:

  • Knowledgeable environment (talks and sessions for knowledge sharing - company support for events and meetups - time space for learning and discovering the cutting edge technologies whereas the company's mission).
  • Healthy environment (company support for sport activities - fruits day - sports equipment in the workplace).
  • Friendly environment.
  • Competitive salary package.




We could tell you that we’re building something that truly optimizes the way business intelligence is done. Or, that we’re backed by marquee VC's such as Google Ventures and Kleiner Perkins. All true, but the real reason you will want to come work for Incorta is that you’ll be part of an early team that is building a game-changing analytics product that will allow companies to answer complex questions in seconds...not days or weeks like the current solutions.
