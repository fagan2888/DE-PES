Ansøg

Would you like to develop a data warehousing solution for the no. 1 white label takeaway system provider in Europe? Then this is a unique opportunity for you to use your strong development skills and passion for data. You will be a part of BI team, responsible for gathering, storing, providing access and analysis of the firm’s data to aid stakeholders in decision making. As a data engineer you will take full ownership of ETL process to gather data from a variety of data sources and load it into a data warehouse, providing single source of truth for business reporting.


Background

OrderYOYO is a technology company, transforming the way people order food online. We are focused on enabling restaurants to make the most of the modern ordering culture. By providing takeaway restaurants with personalised, branded websites and mobile applications, we are ensuring that our customers are not paying predatory commissions to online food portals, but instead take control of their own businesses and win back their own customers from the portals.


Culture

OrderYOYO is headquartered from a state-of-the-art office in Østerbro, Copenhagen. Our dog-friendly, young and ambitious team is pushing the limits when it comes to delivering top results. We highly value work/personal life balance and care for our employee’s happiness. Occasionally working from home or a beach bar is not uncommon here. If there is a need to bond with your colleagues – we have various leisure activities, i.e. Friday bar, bingo nights, board game events, quiz nights out etc. Our sales and operational office in Manchester are serving our customers in our main market of UK/Ireland.


Key responsibilities for the Data Engineer include

• Full ownership of building and running a data warehousing solution • Data from external sources integration into data warehouse

• Building and maintaining ETL pipelines

• Ensure data quality and consistency for BI reporting

• Building a data platform to support assisted decision making and advanced analytics


Formal requirements

• Bachelor’s degree in computer science, engineering or similar

• Minimum 3+ years of DW development experience with data warehouse solutions

• Hands on experience with Google BigQuery or Azure SQL Data Warehouse

• Mastery of C# and/or T-SQL and/or Python

• Experienced in using REST API’s and JSON

• Knowledge within statistical modelling, predictive models and machine learning is a plus


Personal qualities expected

• Structured, analytical and detail oriented

• Self-motivating and proactive

• Professional and service minded

• Strong English skills, both verbal and written


You will be part of an informal and international organization, in a fast-growing company. Job interviews will be held on ongoing basis and we are looking to fill the position beginning of August/September 2019.
