    • Who we are


    Airplane Solutions is one of the more ambitious Technologic Aeronautical Companies in Spain. The company is headquartered in Barcelona, since it was founded in 2015.

    We deploy through disruptive technologies (Cloud, Blockchain, SaaS, IoT, Big Data, AI and VR) solutions to manage airlines' operating costs and improve operations efficiency.

    We are excited about innovation, the creation and development of new ideas, products and ventures within the business, always thinking about innovative and functional solutions.


    ABOUT the role: DevOps Engineer

    ﻿

    AIRPLANE SOLUTIONS is looking for DevOps engineer with a strong interest building and maintaining a complex infrastructure full of interesting challenges. As we grow rapidly, we need our cloud-based infrastructure to keep up.



    Key Requirements


    -Build and maintain our 'infrastructure as code'. Build and maintain Cl/CD pipelines with Jenkins. Administrate and configure Linux servers for security, connectivity, and reliability. Learn and adapt quick­ly to work with new systems across our stack.

    -Experience with AZURE, Jenkins or similar, Docker, Kubernetes.

    -Familiarity with code versioning tools such as Git.



    General Skills & Esperience:

    ﻿

    -Proficiency in English at Upper-Intermediate level.

    -Best Practices: SOLiD, Design Patterns, Clean Code, Testing, Dependency Injection.

    -Methodology: ALM, Continuous Integration, Continuous Delivery, Agile, SCRUM (GITLAB/GITHUB experience).

    -Application Architecture: Monolitic vs Microservices, Kubernetes, SOA.

    -Cloud: Google Cloud Platform, Azure, AWS, etc.
