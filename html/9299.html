Dematic is a leading supplier of integrated automated technology, software and services to optimize the supply chain. Dematic employs over 6,000 skilled logistics professionals to serve its customers globally, with engineering centers and manufacturing facilities located around the world. Dematic is one brand under the KION Group of companies and has implemented more than 6,000 integrated systems for a customer base that includes small, medium and large companies doing business in a variety of market sectors.

Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, Dematic is a member of KION Group, a global leader in industrial trucks, related services and supply chain solutions. Across more than 100 countries worldwide, the KION Group designs, builds and supports logistics solutions that optimize material and information flow within factories, warehouses and distribution centers. The company is the largest manufacturer of industrial trucks in Europe, the second-largest producer of forklifts globally and a leading provider of warehouse automation.

Dematic is looking for a Junior Proposal Engineer , to be hired with a permanent contract , for its Sorter Center of Excellence in Milan . He/she will be working as a part of an international team to design the solution in Material Handling Automation as Sub System expert. Click here!

Your Challenge
  • Develop and design the technical solution, the layout and the related proposal to internal customer, in conjunction with the technical and business staff;
  • Develop a tailored solution and product concepts based on the customer requirements
  • Technical support to sales department during the customers meeting
  • Organize in collaboration with Product Management and R&D of the test and simulation
  • Develop internal tools (like cost list, application tools, spreadsheet) in collaboration with other department
  • Collaborate and attend at worldwide trade shows
  • Collaborate with: Customers, suppliers and all major internal Center of Excellence departments (e.g.: operation, purchasing, service, engineering, manufacturing)

Your Challenge In The Medium Term
  • Has the opportunity to collaborate in international environment

Who are we looking for?

A Candidate With The Following Features
  • At least 6 months of experience in automation or material handling as Application Engineer, Proposal, Project Engineer or Technical sales
  • Bachelor's / Three Years Master's Degree in Mechanic, Automation, Industrial, Civil or others engineering
  • Good Knowledge in AutoCAD, 3D Cad Software, MS Office (Excel, Word, Power Point, etc.)
  • Knowledge in programming software will be a plus (C, C++, Visual Basic or Java)
  • Excellent knowledge of English and Italian, both written and spoken
  • Strong orientation to reach the targets, initiative, autonomy and propensity to work in team
What can we further offer?
  • An international environment where curiosity and proactivity is highly appreciated
  • Fast growing company
  • Meal totally paid by the company
  • Personal Computer and Smartphone
  • A permanent contract
Further details

Dematic pays much attention to Diversity and guarantees equal opportunities. We believe strongly in both taking responsibility and being transparent with your data. For the new GDPR we manage only online applications and we’ll not accept CVs by mail.
