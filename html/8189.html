    • BI/Data/Analytics Developers - London

    Associated British Ports (ABP) is a major port operator with 21 port locations across the UK. We handle around 100 million tonnes of cargo across a range of different industries and contribute £7.5bn to the UK economy every year.

    We have a new CIO with vision who is looking to build a team of talented and experienced IT professionals who will drive a level of technology change that will set the pace and direction not only for ABP, but for our industry.

    Based in the London head office, the BI/Data/Analytics Developers will be responsible for delivering the reports across SAP Business Objects, BW and HANA-ECC. Working closely with the Finance department, working knowledge of the SAP Simple Finance solutions is a must.

    Reporting to the BI/Data/Analytics Lead, these roles will form an important part of the IT Solution Delivery team. Main responsibilities will include:

    • Work with the functional consultants to technically review the reporting functional specifications
    • Translate and document the functional requirements into HANA, BW and Business Objects designs and solutions
    • Build and test the HANA, BW, Business Objects solutions
    • Support the functional teams and the business in the system, integration and user acceptance testing of the BI reporting solutions

    Essential skills:-

    • SAP business analysis experience
    • Expert knowledge of SAP Business Warehouse 7.5 BW on HANA, (version BW4HANA desirable)
    • Solid experience of BW level ABAP development and Business Objects 7.4 experience
    • Strong data science background
    • Ideally come from the shipping and cardo handling, oil & gas or trade contract handling industries

    Great benefits at ABP:-

    As well as a competitive salary, you’ll get access to our generous pension scheme, life assurance, private healthcare for you and your family, and 26 days of annual leave, with the opportunity to purchase more.
