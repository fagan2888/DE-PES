    • About the Company:
    My client is an exciting and dynamic consulting firm based in Melbourne. They work with 25 tier-one enterprise clients within the ASX100. They are a business built on broad experience and an ability to execute and help their customers reach their goals. One of their clients who is located in Abbotsford is in need of a customer-driven, level 2 engineer who would be willing to travel to remote sites as well as service headquarters.

    Culture:
    The people who work for my client enjoy being there because they feel as though they are a part of a team but there is also a lot of variety across different customer environments. This business hires those who have broad experience in their domain but never compromise on the right values that fit their culture. Even though a large portion of their business is of a contract nature, my client works hard to make sure contracts are renewed or others are found within their portfolio of clients so that contractors are never without work. The founder is very supportive and understanding. He takes a genuine interest in people and staff always walk away from conversations with him feeling as though they are heard.

    About the Role:
    This is a six-month contract Desktop Engineer role which would suit someone who is customer focussed and can help users at varying levels of technical knowledge. You will be working across typical Microsoft technologies like AD, Office 365, Windows 10, Windows Server as well as switch and route networking, printing, video conferencing and mobile devices etc. You love troubleshooting and investigating things to their root cause and have the patience to collaborate with users to successful outcomes. There will be after-hours work and this company has many remote sites so travel would be expected too when required.

    Skills & Experience:
    To be considered for the Desktop Engineer role we are looking for the following skills and experience:

    • Minimum 3 years’ experience working in helpdesk support
    • Minimum 5 years’ experience working in customer service type environments
    • Hands-on experience troubleshooting and solving problems involving Micro
    • Understanding of ITIL frameworks – certification would be a bonus
    • Experience working with ITSM and ticketing systems
    • Strong communications skill over the phone and in-person
    • You can demonstrate a clear and effective troubleshooting process
    • You naturally work well in a team and enjoy working around people
