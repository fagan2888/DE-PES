Senior Software Engineer - Big Data Engineer

(Milan/Italy)

  • Company: Tech Start-Up
  • Experience: 4+
  • Skills: Java, JVM language,Git, SVN, Mercurial


As a Big Data Engineer, you will leverage on your past Software Development skills to design, implement and maintain processes and algorithms necessary to ingest, analyze and serve large volumes of geolocation data. If you’re not based in Milan Cuebiq offer a generous relocation package. See below for details.


Responsibilities

  • Build and maintain pipelines and architectures for stream and batch processes;
  • Collaborate with data architects and data scientists to design and build analytic systems that are correct, scalable, reliable, and accurate;
  • Analyze large datasets to allow for data driven design;
  • Understand product and target use cases;


Required skills

  • 4+ years of experience developing products in enterprise software development
  • Experience with any JVM language (Kotlin, Golang or Python is a plus). We value the experience and not the specific language: show us you know REALLY how to use it, then we'll know you can learn any of our stack!
  • Deep understanding of data structures, algorithms, and distributed systems
  • Machine learning, and statistical methods are a plus
  • Experience with version control systems such as Git, SVN, Mercurial. We use Git.
  • At ease with industry standard software development tools (IDEs, etc.)

We’re also looking for:

  • A hunger for coding
  • The ability to say no — and to explain why
  • Excellent English (we work a lot with the New York office)
  • Experience working in a distributed organization (again, New York!)
  • Flexibility and people skills, with a positive and collaborative approach
  • Results driven mentality, delivery-oriented approach


The perks we offer:

  • If you’re like us, we know you’ll enjoy having a ​generous learning budget ​for conferences or certifications. Moreover, we have a ​learning time​ every Friday in the office just before the ​aperitivo​!
  • We value work - life balance and flexibility: ​smart working​; a super casual office environment with a f​ully stocked kitchen, games, lounge area
  • If you're not from Milan... no worries, we'll make you feel at home! We offer a generous relocation package ​as well in order to help you start getting accustomed to the city
  • You’ll also be excited by our international annual company retreats - last year we were at the ​Bahamas​. Also, you’ll get to travel with us: around Milan, with our ​free yearly ATM pass ​and, maybe, back and forth from ​our New York office
  • Plus the serious stuff your parents and your family will ask about: ​health insurance​ for yourself and next of kin
