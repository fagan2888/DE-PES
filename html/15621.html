    • About Us

    Vision Critical builds customer trust and creates new business value through the collection and application of consent data. With its unique, relationship-based approach, Vision Critical’s Sparq platform lets brands directly interact with insight communities of opted-in customers. Unlike traditional voice of customer methods, Vision Critical unlocks the real-time feedback that companies need to build better products, deliver better services, and achieve better business outcomes hand in hand with their own highly engaged, well-profiled customers.

    Hundreds of high-performing organizations across multiple industries benefit from Vision Critical’s technology and guidance, including BuzzFeed, LinkedIn, GoDaddy, VMWare, and more . To learn more about Vision Critical, follow us on LinkedIn and Twitter , or visit us at www.visioncritical.com .

    Equally important to the success of our business are our people. We couldn’t do what we do without our awesome VC’ers and look forward to welcoming a new VC’er, you to our VC family. Come be great with us, #TheVCWay.

    The Team

    We have opportunities in multiple teams that own the Sparq platform.

    The activities team own the platform’s primary data collection tools and is responsible for building engaging user experiences that encourage sharing of opinions, thoughts, and ideas - the fuel to our customers’ decision-making engine.

    The analysis and insights team owns the tools that our customers use to analyze data, harvest insights, and generate reports. Their mission is to make insights discovery a pleasurable and rewarding experience while also championing the value of data and our user’s role in the digital enterprise.

    All our teams work in an agile and collaborative environment. We ship code to production weekly and believe in getting early feedback from customers as we iterate on features.

    The Job

    We are looking for an experienced Software Engineer who is passionate about building products that deliver the value of data to the customer. You will play an active role in building our next generation data collection and analysis tools, while also transforming our platform into an ecosystem of scalable microservices. To be successful in this role, you need to be able to champion the customer while finding the right balance between time-to-market, architecture, usability, and cost.

    About You
      • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or equivalent preferred
      • Minimum 3 years’ working experience - full stack experience building SaaS applications
      • Strong in one or more development languages such as C#, JavaScript/TypeScript, or Java
      • Experienced in building web services in ASP.NET and/or Node.js
      • Experienced in front-end technologies such as React and jQuery as well as in HTML5 and CSS
      • Understanding of various storage technologies such as RDBMS (SQL Server, PostgreSQL, Oracle), S3, NoSQL (MongoDB, DynamoDB, ElasticSearch), etc
      • Working knowledge of building and deploying AWS infrastructure as code is a plus
      • Demonstrated experience with Docker containerization of apps hosted in ECS or Kubernetes clusters is a plus
      • Have test first mentality and understand when a unit, contract, or integration test is required
      • Thrive in a team environment, enjoy sharing ideas, and curious about how things work
      • Passionate about software development and stay informed about the latest developments in the industry
      • Aspire to take a high degree of code, process, and delivery responsibility
      • Enthused to work in a high-paced environment where code backed by automated tests is shipped into production in as fast as a week
