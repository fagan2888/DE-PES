Based Start-up, Building The Next Generation HR Technology Solution To Speed The Decision And Hiring Process For Hiring Managers, Recruiters And Human Resource Organizations, We Are Looking For

For U.S.

Data Engineer

Location: Krakow

Responsibilities
  • designing and building cloud based data infrastructure
  • integrating data from various resources and managing data storage and use
  • implementing ML production tools
  • participating in peer reviews
  • collaborating with local and offshore co-workers
  • debugging and solving complex problems either individually or within a team

We Expect
  • hands-on experience in the data warehouse space, custom ETL design, implementation and maintenance
  • 2+ years professional experience in SQL or similar languages and development experience in at least one scripting language (Python preferred)
  • experience architecting, developing software in cloud environments such as Amazon Web Services, Azure or Google Cloud Platform
  • proven experience in leading data driven projects from definition through interpretation and execution
  • outstanding problem solving and organizational skills
  • experience working in Scrum teams
  • continuously looks for ways to improve and sets a very high bar in terms of quality
  • BS/MS degree in Computer Science or related IT / technology field or equivalent experience
  • excellent English communication skills, both oral and written

Nice To Have
  • experience in machine learning
  • experience with large data sets, data processing software: Spark, Hadoop, Hive, data processing algorithms: MapReduce, Flume and data visualization tools
  • experience working in a continuous software delivery model is a plus
  • hands-on engineering and implementation knowledge with container technologies like Docker & Kubernetes
  • experience working with geographically distributed engineering teams

We Offer
  • ability to choose the equipment you work with (MacBook or Windows equivalent)
  • options to travel to the U.S. to company headquarter
  • professional training budget per each employee
  • a standing desk and ergonomic chair
  • spacious and cozy office space in the heart of Kraków
  • competitive benefits package, including health care and gym pass (MultiSport)
  • healthy food, snacks and drinks in the office

We kindly inform you that we will contact only chosen candidates.
