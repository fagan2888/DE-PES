    ﻿ AccelOne is looking for a talented Senior Full-Stack Lead Developer with an advanced English level to be part of an important international project.


    Company Background

    AccelOne provides Outsourced IT resources to companies in the US and Latin America from their HQ in Kirkland, WA with offices in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Our company was created and built by seasoned technology professionals and entrepreneurs from a foundation of respect, transparency, fluid communication, and accountability.


    Project Overview

    AccelOne is looking for an experienced, talented, passionate and hard-working Full-Stack Lead Developer in order to be part of a challenging project. Our client is an important American Marketing Digital Company that helps clients analyze, develop and execute strategies to capture value.


    Summary Job Description

    -Be a full-stack engineer for innovation projects on AI/ML, AR/VR, custom machine learning, and natural language interfaces

    -Lead the architecture of leading-edge, cloud-based solutions for our enterprise customers

    -Provide guidance and mentoring to other full-stack engineers

    -Implement modern, cloud-based strategy for process automation, enterprise data management, and design a system that is scalable

    -Design datastore/management, APIs, and processing services using the public cloud

    -Make strategic technical decisions related to new products and functionalities

    -You can translate designs into working functionality with flare

    -You will work in Agile/Scrum/Kanban/Waterfall environments and feel comfortable with any

    -We expect you to take smart risks and champion new ideas


    ﻿ Job Requirements

    -You have a deep interest in knowing the latest trends and techniques in the industry.

    -7+ years of full-stack development experience using modern languages such as Python, Java or C#

    -2+ years working in a lead capacity, driving the architecture of solutions and providing support to other developers

    -Experience developing enterprise solutions using public cloud platforms, AWS, Azure or Google Cloud

    -Experience working with one or more data storage technologies (SQL, NoSQL, etc.)

    -Working knowledge of Dev Op's tool sets

    -Proficient in front end web client technologies (HTML5, CSS, Javascript, Angular) ideal. --Experience with Unity is a plus.

    -Exposure to machine learning and/or computer vision a plus

    -Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related technical discipline

    -Advanced English Level (verbal and written) is a must.

    -An opportunity to join a company that is growing rapidly and improving the way that software products are developed.

    -A creative, flexible, fun and challenging environment with international clients.

    -A Competitive Salary.

    -Remote position

    -English classes

    -The opportunity to be exposed to the newest technologies (Blockchain &Cryptocurrency, Machine Learning, AR/VR/MR and others).

    -Corporate Benefits with electrical and electronic stores.
