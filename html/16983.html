    • Senior Data Engineer

    LONDON

    £90K + bonus + benefits

    Trust in Soda are looking to attract a Senior Data Engineer for one of the most innovative Fin-Tech organizations based in Central London.


    This is an exciting opportunity to work with cutting edge technologies in one of the most forward-thinking companies in the Fin-Tech industry.


    Requirements

    ·         Previous experience with any of the following programming languages: Python, Java, SQL, Scala

    ·        Solid experience working with cloud technologies such as AWS or Google Cloud Platform

    ·         Comfortable with large scale data processing frameworks such as Spark/Hadoop etc…

    ·         Previous experience working with big data databases such as Snowflake, Postgres, Redshift etc…

    ·         Strong knowledge of ETL processes

    ·        Ability to engage and work closely with senior stakeholders and cross functional teams

    Responsibilities


    ·         Play a key role in a diverse, fast-moving & successful data team who are completely innovation driven

    ·       Continuously improve the brand’s data engineering and analytics practices, processes and tools

    ·       Play a key role in the architecture, development and maintenance of modern data infrastructure and data processing frameworks

    ·       You will work closely with the Chief Data Officer, Data Engineers & other cross functional teams to prioritise the feature backlog and define the technical direction of the company.

    ·       Work collaboratively with other teams to ensure delivery of the best solution to end users

    ·       Work closely with junior members on the team and share your technical expertise


    Bonus


    ·         Experience within the FinTech industry

    If interested in the opportunity, please apply with your CV and we will be in touch straight away.
