Operations Engineer

GoodData Prague, The Capital, Czech Republic

Před 3 týdny

Zažádat na webu společnosti

In GoodData, we are expanding our team dedicated to the back-end of our unique analytical platform, designed for creating analytical data products for our customers. We are seeking an experienced operations engineer to join our Applications Back-end DevOps team.



Your job will be to


  • take care of the back-end part of our SaaS application (web servers, database servers, load balancers, proxy servers, messaging servers, etc.)
  • design, develop and maintain configuration and tooling
  • deploy new software versions (includes development of Puppet recipes)
  • ensure integration with underlying cloud platform and with logging, monitoring and alerting systems
  • meet high availability and performance goals according to our SLA
  • maintain and contribute to improvement of GoodData security measures



You should have


  • BSc. degree in Computer Science or relevant field
  • 4 years work experience as operations engineer
  • good knowledge of Linux operating system administration, troubleshooting and fine-tuning
  • good knowledge of shell scripting
  • knowledge of TCP/IP based computer networks and common internet protocols
  • ability to manage and monitor SQL database servers
  • advanced written and spoken English
  • no problem to participate in 24x7 on-call support (a week once in five weeks)



It is nice if you have


  • knowledge of JVM and some Java web server / servlet container (e.g. Apache Tomcat
  • experience with Docker and Kubernetes
  • experience with Puppet, Python, Perl and Git
  • experience with cloud computing
  • experience with messaging systems (RabbitMQ, Apache Kafka)
  • experience with DB design and tuning
  • experience with scrum/agile environment
  • fluent English
