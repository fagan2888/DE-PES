Job description


About The Company You Are Applying For


The startup company was established in January 2018. They created an application that makes exercises and workouts more fun and creative by having a virtual fitness trainer combined with popular music that you can access anywhere and anytime. The company tries to make staying active more fun and personalized by having an app with different features such as having a wide range of exercise levels from beginner to advanced level, various music genres, exercise duration types, and location usage (whether you are working out at the gym, home or outside). The various classes are updated weekly to keep the workouts fun. The fitness voice trainers are selected carefully to match the different types of exercise classes available ranging from running, cycling, yoga and many more.

The company’s philosophy revolves around promoting an active and healthy lifestyle for people, young and old. The CEO became interested with healthcare and fitness when he started joining the gym and working out when he was staying in New York while taking his masters in business. He was inspired and motivated to find a solution to improve an active lifestyle through technology. Together with the team, they created BeatFit not only because of the potential in the healthcare and fitness industry but because they all share the belief in trying to promote health and wellness through an active lifestyle.


Roles and Responsibilities


The company is looking for a data scientist who will analyze many kinds of data and propose what function they need to add on this product.If you are someone who is driven to create a functional and sensational experience for customers then this is the job for you. Keep in mind that the company's main service is a mobile fitness app and they would like their team members to be also passionate about promoting health and wellness not only externally but also within the company. They want people who are motivated to promote wellness and contribute to change in the society.


Key Responsibilities:
-Analyze the user data and propose a good way to develop their products
-Read the scientific articles and implement the algorithm

Development Environment:
-Server side: Ruby (Rubyon Rails), Firebase, Node.js
-Front end: ReactNative, Riot.js
-DB: MySQL, BigQuery
-Infrastructure: AWS, heroku
-Project management: Backlog, GitHubProject
-Communication tool: Slack
