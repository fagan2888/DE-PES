    • It's an exciting time to be part of a dynamic, passionate team working on some of the most innovative telematics products and services in the market today.


    We are looking for an Associate Data Engineer reporting to a Lead Data Engineer.


    Who you are…


    You are a passionate individual with sound knowledge in Data Engineering and Software Development Lifecycle. You have basic knowledge of or experience working in Cloud environments (preferably AWS) and good technical understanding of how to interact with databases and other data stores. You will excel in a team environment, collaborating with members of different skills and experience.


    Work with us to take it to the next level…


    The Associate Data Engineer is responsible for integrating multiple data sources into production data pipelines and customer-facing data products. You will contribute to the automation and streamlining of our operational activities and processes, including the management of data assets throughout their lifecycle, to deliver quality data.


    You will be part of our Data and Content team, responsible for providing the largest traffic data product across Australia and New Zealand, that allows deep insights into how people move around our cities. We are building a world-class cloud infrastructure capable of handling terabytes of real-time transit data every day. You will be part of an extremely collaborative team that fosters an environment of continuous improvement, rapid prototyping and innovation.


    • Work with various data formats (JSON, XML, CSV, etc.) to assess their value in building re-usable data assets
    • Analyse data from various sources such as APIs, flat file feeds and data warehouses
    • Contribute to the development and maintenance of datasets integration in processing and delivery pipelines and ensure continuous flow of data
    • Contribute towards data processing test, stage and QA environments for integrating a variety of data sources, formats and types into the product
    • Update data asset inventory and gather feedback to ensure accuracy and optimal performance
    • Contribute to ensure datasets are structured in a way that is usable for BI and data science workloads
    • Contribute to the testing, deployment, release management, monitoring and documentation
    • Contribute to the continuous improvement of our automation capabilities and operational efficiencies
    • Proactively engage in learning, knowledge sharing and cross skilling


    Bring with you…


    • Experience working with and/or knowledge of cloud environments (AWS preferred), including basic storage, compute and security
    • Sound knowledge of data lifecycle and fair understanding of software development lifecycle
    • Technical understanding interacting with databases, data warehouses and their underlying technologies, limits and capabilities
    • Knowledge of best practices in developing sound business logic and APIs that make data accessible in a safe, scalable and logical way to the users
    • Experience in formalising data models and communicating data definitions via specifications such as Swagger/OpenAPI, jsonschema, Database ERD, XSD
    • Experience working with and/or knowledge of different types of compute infrastructure including VMs (eg. EC2), containers (eg. docker) and serverless functions (eg. Lambda), basic connectivity and messaging mechanisms (eg. pub/sub, API)
    • Experience working with and/or solid understanding of production and non-production environments, and the promotion of changes through different environments
    • Intermediate SQL skills (T-SQL or PostgreSQL preferred)
    • Basic skills in Python, Git, unit testing, etc.
    • Basic experience with NoSQL, DynamoDB, MangoDB, Athena, Redshift, Hadoop/Spark, Glue, etc. desired
    • Experience with and/or exposure to implementing internal and public APIs, Infrastructure as a Code, data sensitivity, security and privacy
    • Collaborative, curiosity and commitment to learning
    • AWS Associate or higher-level certification, preferably Solution Architect Associate and/or other equivalent accredited Cloud certification highly desired

    Benefits and Perks!


    • In return for your hard work we offer a range of great benefits from RACV and our Partners products and services. We take diversity and health and wellbeing of our employees as a priority by offering flexible working conditions, arrangements and encouraging wellbeing at all levels of life.
    • We have dedicated investment of paid professional training and development with a culture of ongoing learning.
