Software Engineering_Big Data

J.P. Morgan Bangalore, IN

6 days ago

Apply on company website

Senior Big Data Java Development role based out of Bangalore, the role will require the candidate to have in depth understanding of Big Data Stack including Ingestion, storage and distribution technologies.

The role is a full hands-on technical development role where the candidate will be involved in establishing the building blocks for the post trade reservoir (PTR) platform; interacting with multiple teams, learning about the exiting solutions already in place and aligning them with solutions built centrally by PTR team while adhering to standards provided by CIB Data Engineering organization.

The role will also require the candidate to work with global teams, performing code reviews; doing peer programming; interviewing other candidates, establishing software engineering processes and adhering to firm wide software engineering processes and standards and  influencing various stakeholders.

The job will also require having in depth understanding of technical project planning, backlog management, participating and driving global scrum and sprint calls and taking role of a scrum master from time to time.

Required Technical Skills:

· Development (Coding) using Hadoop, Spark and Java - 8+ years of development experience required.

· Strong Java and Python skills.

· Understanding of Cloudera Management Console.

· Good understanding of RDBMS and columnar databases(Oracle/Cassandra/Vertica)

· Understanding of development frameworks e.g. Spring Boot

· Knowledge of Business Intelligence tools including Cognos/Qlikview/Business objects

· Good hold on scripting including Shell/Perl and Python.

· Good understanding of Testing frameworks

Other Important Skills/Attributes.

· Process Orientation: Establishing right SDLC practices including code reviews, pair programming and adhering to firm wide development and release standards

· Relationship Building : Working closely with plan teams and business aligned technology teams.

· Business Orientation : Translate complex functional and technical requirements into detailed technical designs and performing POC’s.

· DevOps: Working closely with operate teams, understanding JPMorgan DevOps practices and aligning releases accordingly

· Documentation : Documenting technical flows in MS Visio and Confluence.

· Communication : Good communication and interpersonal skills.

· Domain : Understanding of financial products and their flows (Equities, Fixed Income)

· Good hold on MS office (power point, Excel, Word and Visio)


Our Corporate & Investment Bank relies on innovators like you to build and maintain the technology that helps us safely service the world’s important corporations, governments and institutions. You’ll develop solutions for a bank entrusted with holding $18 trillion of assets and $393 billion in deposits.  CIB provides strategic advice, raises capital, manages risk, and extends liquidity in markets spanning over 100 countries around the world.

When you work at JPMorgan Chase & Co., you’re not just working at a global financial institution. You’re an integral part of one of the world’s biggest tech companies. In 14 technology hubs worldwide, our team of 40,000+ technologists design, build and deploy everything from enterprise technology initiatives to big data and mobile solutions, as well as innovations in electronic payments, cybersecurity, machine learning, and cloud development. Our $9.5B+ annual investment in technology enables us to hire people to create innovative solutions that will not only transform the financial services industry, but also change the world.


At JPMorgan Chase & Co. we value the unique skills of every employee, and we’re building a technology organization that thrives on diversity.  We encourage professional growth and career development, and offer competitive benefits and compensation.  If you’re looking to build your career as part of a global technology team tackling big challenges that impact the lives of people and companies all around the world, we want to meet you.
