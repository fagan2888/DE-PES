PDS Tech, Inc. is seeking a Systems Design & Integration Specialist in Kent, WA.98032. Contact Elisabeth Laspe for details. 314-669-8020 elaspe@pdstech.com
12 month contract with possible extensions. Must be a US Citizen and have an Active Secret Clearance to qualify.


Have multiple positions with various skill and experience levels 2,3, and 4.


Summary:
• Build strong relationships with key business stakeholders across multiple business units and be their trusted advisor
• Work with the business, service managers, and the IT delivery teams to ensure that business needs are prioritized, understood, and supported in a timely matter
• Collaborate with key business stakeholders to translate business strategies and objectives into capabilities and present solution concepts for input, buy-in, and sign off
• Ability to work independently to determine approaches to solutions
• Work with key organizations (e.g. Networking, Information Security)

Primary responsibilities:
• Assist with creating, managing, and supporting all administrative functions associated with multiple DevOps tools (e.g. Atlassian application stack, JFrog-Artifactory, SmartBear, etc)
• Assist in the creation and management of Atlassian plug-in components
• Gather requirements from users, propose solutions that meet business needs, and deliver agreed upon solutions
• Assist with the development, testing and deployment of integrations between DevOps tools and other internal systems
• Maintain an up-to-date knowledge of DevOps standard tools, administration best practices, and agile methodology best practices
• Ensure systems are stable and available and that user issues are addressed in a timely manner
• Ability to work a rotational on-call schedule

Basic qualifications:
• Technical Bachelor's degree or higher
• This position requires a US Security Clearance for which the US Government requires US Citizenship
• Experience with Linux and/or Unix and Windows operating systems including system administration experience
• Scripting experience (Shell, PERL, Python, or similar)
• Working knowledge of web application structure (app servers, DBs, SQL, etc.)
• Microsoft Active Directory Administration and Group Policies implementation and development

Desired experience, skills & qualifications:
• Experience with server admin tools like (e.g. Putty)
• Experience managing virtualized infrastructure
• Experience with System Configuration Management
• Experience working with Government customers and Government Classified systems
• Strong customer focus, with the ability to work across cross functional, diverse teams
• Experience with general programming (C, C++, Java)
• Experience working with DevOps applications stack (Atlassian - Bamboo, Bitbucket, Confluence, JIRA; Jfrog, Jenkins, Smartbear)
• Experience with automation / working knowledge of Ansible
• Strong understanding of Application Lifecycle Management
• Experience with Scaled Agile Framework or Agile Methodologies
• Experience with network technology, storage systems
• Experience with System Configuration Management
• Experience with Cloud technologies
• Experience with Load Balancing (Preferably F5)
• VMware vShphere implementation and experien ce
• Experience with Security requirements
