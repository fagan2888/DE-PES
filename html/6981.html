Data Import Engineer

Chrono24 Karlsruhe, DE

Vor 2 Monaten

Auf Firmenwebseite bewerben
As data import engineer, you develop and maintain web scrapers for over 400 dealers. Together with your team of working students, you ensure that the offers in our dealers’ online shops are automatically published on Chrono24. You continuously develop our framework to ensure that it will also satisfy our evolving needs.
What you can expect

  • As a part of the import engineering team, you're responsible for automatically publishing offers from our watch dealers' online shops on Chrono24.
  • Using our Java-based import framework, you develop and maintain web scrapers for over 400 dealers.
  • You further develop our framework to ensure that it will meet our evolving needs.
  • You're the main contact person for colleagues from the account management and sales teams when they have questions about dealer imports.
  • You coordinate a team of working students and maintain an overview of existing and new import tickets in our issue tracker JIRA, in order to ensure that import development runs smoothly.
What sets you apart

  • You're very confident with HTML and JavaScript and know how to access data on a website.
  • You have knowledge of XSLT, XPath, and Regexes.
  • Experience in web development with Java is a plus.
  • You have a degree in IT or comparable training.
  • Don't know much about luxury watches? Don't worry, we'll teach you everything you need to know.
What we offer

We’re wired a bit differently, not just in reference to our cutting-edge technology. At Chrono24, we pursue ambitious goals in an intriguing environment. We love a dynamic corporate culture with flat hierarchies. We offer a start-up atmosphere with excellent development opportunities. Are you enthusiastic about tackling new challenges that suit your personality and abilities? Perfect! Because as a Data Import Engineer in full-time, you’re able to actively shape our future success.
