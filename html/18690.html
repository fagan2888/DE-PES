Desired Skills And Experience

Industry experience with deployment of EDA tools (CAD, design, and/or development) in at least one of the following domains: STA, custom design, standard cell characterization, circuit simulation. Experience with Unix or Linux environment, Tcl/Perl/shell scripting, openness for learning new concepts and techniques. Work experience with external customers is a plus. Experience with quality and software processes is desired. Proficiency in analyzing transistor or gate level schematics. The preferred candidate is expected to have a Masters or PhD in CS/EE/CE with 8+ years of relevant experience
