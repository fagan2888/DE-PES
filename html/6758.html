Database Engineer

ProtonMail Taipei, Taiwan

前往公司網站應徵

ProtonMail is a fast-growing technology company with more than 140 employees in offices in Skopje, San Francisco, Geneva, Zurich, Prague and Vilnius. We provide secure email services to millions of customers in over 150 countries. Our customers range from journalists and activists, to small and medium businesses, large government entities and people who care about they own privacy. In addition, we operate ProtonVPN, one of the best free VPN services, and we are rapidly expanding into other Internet services.


We are growing fast and we adding a new office location in Taipei. You will be joining a highly talented and fast-paced Engineering team.


Databases form the backbone of internet services and must scale with our growth while being highly reliable. You will join a growing database team to improve and scale our relational databases and plan for disaster recovery. You will collaborate with various backend teams to ensure the efficient use of data storage.

Your work will directly improve the user experience of millions of people around the world. You should have extensive experience developing and operating distributed and highly-available systems.

What you will do:

  • Support backend team's database needs (schema changes, indexes)
  • Create, migrate and split shards
  • Improve alerting, failovers and backups (hot and cold)
  • Automate, deploy, monitor, and troubleshoot critical database systems
  • Participate in rotating on-call duties in a global, 24x7x365, team


Requirements

  • Expert on relational databases (MySQL, Oracle DB)
  • Software engineering experience with production systems (Python, PHP)
  • Experienced with Linux and automation (Ansible, Puppet)
  • Bonus: worked with cold storage and tape backups


Do something meaningful. We are challenging giants such as Google, Yahoo, and Microsoft and transforming the way private data is treated online. Our products are used on a daily basis to protect the lives of journalists, dissidents, and activists around the world. We are a company with a social impact.
