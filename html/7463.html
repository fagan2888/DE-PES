About this role

Blackrock is currently seeking a software engineer to join a development team embedded within Global Investment Operations (GIO). You will be developing exception-based, business-process-centric technology solutions that provide scale to operations teams globally. You will work closely with business users in Budapest to develop service orientated workflows across GIO’s functional areas: Trading & Market Documentation, Transaction Management, Collateral Management, Payments, Corporate Actions, Cash & Asset Operations, and Securities Lending Operations. While this role offers a great opportunity to further develop your IT skills, you will also gain insight into the asset management business processes, understand and learn how the various business functions work.

BlackRock’s technology teams are:
  • Passionate about technology, and love to solve complex problems.
  • Building solutions for both BlackRock and over 100 client organizations who also use the Aladdin ecosystem.
  • Working with leading technologies such as Java, Maven, Camunda, Docker, Kubernetes, Kafka, Cassandra, and a host of other languages and open source technologies.
  • Operating in an agile environment.
  • Committed to open source, and love contributing back to the community.
  • Writing testable software every day, with a focus on incremental innovation.

You like to:
  • Work in a culture of openness, inclusion and proactive engagement at all levels.
  • Work closely with users, designers, developers and product managers to translate imaginative designs into working prototypes.
  • Understand the business you support, and work closely with users to design, develop & create innovative solutions.
  • Solve complex problems in an ever-changing environment, with a focus on process optimisation, data, & technology.
  • Foster strong relationships with your colleagues and clients based on a culture of collaboration and service.
  • Operate in a fast-paced, high-intensity environment and work as part of a global team.

You have:
  • 1-3+ years hands-on experience developing complex enterprise software systems.
  • BA/BS in Computer Science from an accredited university
  • Good analytical, problem-solving and communication skills
  • An ability to translate real business problems into technical solutions
  • A track record of forging strong relationships and building trusted partnerships through open dialogue and continuous delivery
  • Knowledge and experience with software development best-practices including test driven development (TDD), unit testing discipline, agile development and CI/CD strategies.
  • Strong OO skills and design patterns knowledge and implementation experience using one or more object-oriented languages (e.g. Java, C/C++, Python)
  • Solid knowledge and experience developing and working with relational databases (e.g. MySQL, Oracle, Sybase)
  • Experience building cloud-native applications is a plus
  • Experience developing using an open-source, lightweight & modern BPM solution such as Camunda is a plus.

Want to learn more?

For 30 years, to drive innovation, BlackRock has bought great minds together. From the very beginning technology was harnessed for insight and efficiency to help our businesses better understand their client’s objectives. Again, BlackRock is accelerating innovation and technology to invest even more in their Aladdin platform and looking for people with a passion for technology and a love of solving complex problems to join their teams.
