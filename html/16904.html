Discover ING Tech

ING Tech is an international hub for technology & innovation, developing IT solutions across ING in areas such as Core Banking, Big Data, Financial Markets, Data Management, Touch Point Architecture and many more. By using the latest methodologies in software development, our fast growing team gathers more than 750 tech enthusiasts who work for international projects that go beyond traditional banking.


ING Shopping platform

ING will develop a new Shopping platform, empowering consumers and merchants to bring a new shopping experience, using data from the entire ecosystem: mobile-bank-merchant, curated by the Platform through machine learning algorithms.


Mission

We are looking for an enthusiastic data scientist who inspires colleagues to change on the basis of insights from transactions, profile and other public data, to strengthen the Shopping platform in realizing fantastic shopping experience. You are razor-sharp in an analytical sense and always make decisions based on facts and data. You understand consumer behavior, triggers and motivators and your are interested in improving customer life!

Your day-to-day

  • You combine marketing and data management expertise. are responsible for carrying out data analysis and research in order to strengthen and improve offer relevancy, the customer journeys and to gain valuable insights for the rest of team. To this end generate, measure, monitor, present and analyze your internal and external data
  • You are responsible for the data analyzes end-to-end. You give  colleagues (personal) insight into their  shopping needs and relevant developments with the help of data. The aim here is to apply data smartly and innovatively and to continuously respond to changes
  • You devise and develop data-driven applications. You are able to interpret the data, draw conclusions and make appropriate recommendations with the aim of further extend the relevancy of the offers, merchant insights
  • You participate in relevant discussion on business challenges and you formulate hypothesis


What you bring to the team

  • Education level: higher education (eg Econometrics, (technical) business, Mathematics) Psychology and Marketing (Intelligence)
  • You are an innovator, a strong communicator and energetic
  • You dare to take risks and have the courage to question the status quo.
  • You design models using different methodologies and algorithms
  • You are analytically very strong and make decisions based on data and facts.
  • You have extensive hands-on experience with tools and techniques for data preparation and analysis (eg R, Python, BI tooling)
  • You have experience with drafting reports including conclusions and the translation of data into (management) advice;
