What We Propose

At the crossroads of management consulting and digital transformation consulting, Wavestone accompanies large companies and organizations in their most critical transformations with a conviction: there can be no successful transformation without a shared culture of enthusiasm.
This positive state of mind is nourished by the pleasure of the challenge, the pleasure of imagining new ways and the pleasure of succeeding as a group, which every day drives our 3100 consultants in 14 offices on 4 continents and allows them to support our clients in the end of their most beautiful ideas.

As a member of the Luxembourg Office with a staff of nearly 70 consultants, you will be expected to support our clients on the following main topics: assist CIOs, CISOs and CDOs in the management of their projects, be involved in major digital transformation, security and data programmes as well as in the operational project management strategy (IS strategy, review of IS architectures, data valuation, cybersecurity...)

As a Consultant, You
  • Analyze the existing situation and the different ways of improving it
  • Produce market orientation and benchmarking
  • Produce high quality deliverables, and be autonomous and proactive in carrying out your assignments,
  • Develop a relationship of trust with your internal and external contacts and ensure their satisfaction,
  • Quickly appropriate new topics and identify the information to be capitalized and shared as part of knowledge management,
  • Explore and then master in depth the technologies behind the digital transformation movements in companies,
  • Address all aspects of their implementation: framing and strategy, governance organization, sourcing, business model, business plan, financing, change management...
  • Develop skills related to the consulting business (formalization, taking a step back, feedback, supervision, conviction sharing, business development...)
Your Profile

Your strengths :

Graduated from an engineering school or university, you are attracted by consulting and business transformation issues. Your qualities make you a future consultant with many talents. E

Specially You Have Developed
  • An intellectual curiosity, a sharpened critical and analytical mind allowing you to quickly understand the specificities of our customers and produce quality deliverables
  • A very good interpersonal skills, a strong sense of service and a certain pro-activity to build a relationship of trust with your customers and guide them to excellence in their major transformation projects
  • A taste for entrepreneurship and innovation.
You are looking for a stimulating work environment that is conducive to personal development within a recognized and growing consulting firm?

Would you like to to be an actor in an ambitious corporate project and to put your talent and enthusiasm to good use in a company that will offer you a dynamic career path and rapid responsibility?

Then give yourself the opportunity to join our teams!
