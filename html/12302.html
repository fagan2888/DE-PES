Data Engineer - Store Pick

Ocado Technology Sofia, BG

3 weeks ago

Apply on company website
Ocado Technology is the biggest online only supermarket. We excel in delivering groceries from our highly automated warehouses to our customers’ homes. Using our expertise we are building OSP (Ocado Smart Platform) in order to enable other retailers across the globe to do grocery deliveries as efficiently as possible. More than 80 people work in our Sofia office and we continue to grow.

About The Product

StorePick is a system that supports the in-store fulfilment of online orders, a physical process we continuously strive to improve on with our software. In order to do that, we capture tons of data, stream it and analyse it into a Data Lake. Your job will be to help us organise, prepare and gain ever deeper insight by tracking even more information, harvesting new data sources and devising clever ways of identifying bottlenecks and areas for improvement. To facilitate all this, a robust and flexible architecture is cornerstone: data flows, APIs, SQL jobs, dashboards all working in lock-step to achieve our challenging goals. Which of course are measurable.

We are now looking for Data Engineers to join our team and do:

Responsibilities Within The Team
  • Deep understanding of the business processes
  • Identifying and collecting all key data anticipating current and future needs
  • Maintaining high quality of the stored data
  • Organizing the data in a way that is easy to query and analyse


Essential

Knowledge and skills we are looking for:
  • SQL, Transact-SQL (T-SQL)
  • Data modeling and query optimisation
  • Window functions, aggregation, partition tables, Big data
  • Data processing, ETL
  • Data driven / knows the importance of data to run a successful business

Desirable
  • BigQuery, Redshift, Oracle, PostgreSQL
  • Hadoop, Spark
  • Ability to chart and visualise data
  • Interest or experience with ML
  • Experience with Java or Scala


In Return We Can Offer You
  • Flexible working hours
  • 25 Days annual leave
  • Compliments card
  • Additional health care
  • Developer Exchange program: a 2 - 3 week exchange giving you the opportunity to work at one of our overseas development centres
  • A choice of equipment (iOS / linux / windows operational system)
  • Both in house and external training programs
  • A selection of snacks, tea and coffee and fresh fruit
  • Barista coffee machine
  • Monthly drinks events to find out what’s happening in the rest of the business, and to meet and relax with your colleagues
  • Team building activities
  • Relaxed dress code


There’s a lot going on at Ocado Technology!

Come and find out more about us at one of our Tech events.
