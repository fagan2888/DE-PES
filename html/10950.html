GK) IT - Data Scientist

Asurion Tokyo, JP

4日前

The Data Scientist with practical hands-on experience is expected to deal with data analytics for Japanese customers, communicating with our global data team.

  • Asurion Japan is seeking for an exceptional candidate for our fast growing data science team,

    newly established in Japan in September 2016.

    The Data Scientist with practical hands-on experience is expected to deal with data analytics for

    Japanese customers, communicating with our global data team.

    【 Specific Responsibilities 】
  • Image processing, general ML related projects.
  • Data collection with required quality and cleansing by collaborating with related departments across the company.
  • Build statistical/ML models to analyze data according to product requirements.
  • Rapidly develop proof-of-concept prototypes to prove out hypotheses.
  • Take ownership of the deliverables and technical document writings.
  • 【 Essential Skills and Experience 】
  • Experiences of using recent up-to-date machine learning technologies especially strong background from image processing fields.
  • Computer/Mathematical Science background and 4+ years working experience in practical data science related projects.
  • Ability to work independently, prioritize projects and meet client deadlines.
  • Ability to interpret results.
  • Willing to learn new technologies.
  • Can-do attitude.
  • 【 Preferred Skills 】
  • Working experience of Natural Language Processing such as topic extraction, answering system, summary-like generation.
  • Working experience of theoretical/practical algorithm development in those fields such as machine learning, mathematical optimization.
  • Experience of using Apache Spark or other platforms used in distributed computing
  • 【 Language 】
  • Business-level: either Japanese or English
  • Fluent: either Japanese or English
  • 【Others】
  • Fast growing, multi-cultural, international team
  • Exposure to the latest data analytics and machine-learning technologies.
  • Work in a dynamic, collaborative, transparent, non-hierarchal, and ego-free culture where your talent is valued over a role title.
  • Work/Life Balance: we honor great work and producing great deliverables. Not sitting in the office all day and night.
  • Opportunities: variety, innovation and unique challenges, where you can learn something new every day.
