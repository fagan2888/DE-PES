Falcon.io Copenhagen, DK

Ansøg på virksomhedens websted
As a Falcon Frontend Software Engineer, you will help build, scale and improve the frontend of a fast growing Customer Experience platform. You will aid us in building a real-time, scalable, stable platform serving thousands of clients in a reliable, consistent and performant manner.

You will be using new technologies, to solve hard problems in a friendly and encouraging work environment. You will also be encouraged to share your experience and knowledge, sparring with your colleagues to grow together.

Your Colleagues

You will be part of the Platform Experience team and the the larger Frontend group at Falcon. Set in an informal, positive and respectful working environment you’ll interact, learn from and brainstorm with experienced Frontend Engineers and Frontend Designers – all with a clear focus on delivering high-quality solutions and following modern best practices.

The R&D team, in general, are a bunch of geeks who like having fun and are good at celebrating achievements. The team enjoy bonding through outings and activities and occasionally also invite guest speakers from different fields of expertise as we believe in gaining knowledge beyond software and IT.

Who You Are
    • Hands-on and proactive, with a wealth of experience handling cross-department stakeholders
    • Enjoy sparring with team members, while at the same time making decisions that fit the overarching goals and deadlines

What You'll Do
    • You’ll be working as a key member on our Platform Experience team.
The team consist of cross competencies, ranging from other Frontend Engineers to Product Designers. The focus of the team is to support the developer experience of our product teams and ensuring that the work translates into world-class user experiences. This is done by owning the development of our design system, build pipelines and general tooling and documentation of our frontend stack.

How You'll Do
    • Help build the rich user interface that hundreds of global client teams work with every day
    • Enable the product teams to focus on feature development
    • Deliver high-quality solutions
    • Act as a sparring partner to various members of the team
    • Proactively share and debate best practices with your peers

What You Have
    • Expert knowledge of JavaScript.
    • Knowledge of front end frameworks such as AngularJS. Angular2+ is a plus.
    • Familiarity with the whole web stack.
    • Practical experience with Typescript and Redux would be desirable, though not required.
    • Experience in building and debugging complex systems in a team environment.
    • Knowledge of unit testing frameworks (Mocha) and end-to-end testing is desirable.
    • Excellent skills in communication & teamwork.
    • Adaptable, resourceful and able to share your knowledge with the rest of the team.

What We Offer
    • You will join a multinational, open and informal work environment.
    • Work at a central location in Copenhagen (the home of the best craft beer and bicycle lanes in Europe, also the most liveable city on the planet!)
    • Get a high level of responsibility, work with the newest technologies, and have room for experimentation
    • Work life balance, flexible work hours and work from home options
    • Choose your own gear (laptop & phone)
    • Pension and health insurance
    • Healthy lunch and drinks daily
    • Healthy, and not so healthy, snacks. We take our candy seriously!
    • Friday bars and other social events

About Falcon.io

Falcon.io provides a unified SaaS platform for social media listening, engaging, publishing and managing customer data. We enable our clients to explore the full potential of digital marketing by managing multiple customer touch points from one platform. Our platform has already achieved great international traction in the market, with its technology endorsed by partnerships with Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.

Founded as Falcon Social in Copenhagen in 2010, we offices in 6 different locations with 3 main offices (Copenhagen, Budapest & Sofia) dedicated to the R&D development. We’re a highly diverse team, and we’re always looking for passionate and curious people who want to be part of a fast-paced, high-energy environment.

For further queries and information please reach out to: jobs@falcon.io
