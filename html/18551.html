About Us

Job Description

Infosys is a global leader in technology services and consulting. We enable clients in more than 50 countries to create and execute strategies for their digital transformation. From engineering to application development, knowledge management and business process management, we help our clients find the right problems to solve, and to solve these effectively. Our team of 190,000+ innovators, across the globe, is differentiated by the imagination, knowledge and experience, across industries and technologies that we bring to every project we undertake

Responsibilities
  • Primary responsibilities include full time work in Project Management (Proposal, Contracting & Agreement, Project scoping, Estimation, resource planning, scheduling, Quality planning, Risk planning, Project Plan review, Transition, Production Support, Project tracking, control & Report, People management, Client management, Knowledge management.
  • Contribute to unit-level and organizational initiatives with the objective of providing high quality and value-adding consulting solutions to the customer WITHIN the guidelines, policies and norms of Infosys Limited.
  • Work with offshore, client, onsite teams as a part of the project.
  • Analyzing the problem faced by the client and designs the solution for the problem with the documentation.
Requirements
  • Worked in software development projects for construction companies (Good to have
  • Understands the information needs that arise in the Construction business (Good to have)
  • Has worked on IoT projects before and understands the architectural landscape in such projects
  • Total 12+ years’ professional experience and at least 5+ years’ in Project Management
  • Proven track record in IT Consulting/Solution delivery.
  • Excellent communication / presentation / verbal and written communication skills.
  • Should be able to drive customer discussions independently and act as trusted advisor.
  • Should have good adaptability for an engagement and work in an onsite-offshore team environment - sharing ideas and working
collaboratively.
  • Strong organizational and analytical skills
  • Strong ability to take bottom line responsibility.
  • Self-starter.
  • Tertiary Qualification in IT / Computer Science / related discipline
