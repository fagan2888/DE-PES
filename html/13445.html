    • Software/Data Engineer - Job Brief

    Neurocom is presently looking for a Software/Data Engineer to join our team in Luxembourg.

    As a Software/Data Engineer you will participate in European research projects, collaborate closely with partners and will be responsible for designing developing, implementing and supporting big data applications.


    Main Responsibilities

    • Participates in European research projects (FP Horizon 2020) and collaborates closely with university partners and engineers in a multidisciplinary work environment
    • Participates in detailed technical design, development, implementation, and support of big data streaming applications
    • Keeps abreast of the latest developments in the field by continuous learning and proactively propose new methods relevant to the problems at hand
    • Proposes innovative solutions to problems, perform experiments to show their feasibility and refine them into a real-world context
    • Researches, designs, and prototypes robust and scalable models based on machine learning, data mining, and statistical modeling to answer key business problems.


    Job Requirements

    • PhD in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Informatics, or MSc with 2 years of experience in a related field
    • Strong software development skills using Java or Python
    • Knowledge of various open-source machine learning and analytics packages
    • Knowledge of machine learning, data mining and statistical predictive modeling
    • Strong verbal and written communication skills in English
    • Ability to work independently
    • Ready to travel


    Desirable Knowledge & Experience

    • Delivering analytics models and solutions
    • Open source big data and streaming technologies such as Kafka, Flink, etc.
    • Kubernetes, Docker, …


    Workplace

    Luxembourg

    • Candidates must be domiciled either in Luxembourg or in Germany, France, Belgium (close to the Luxembourgian border)
    • Foreign candidates will have to move in one of the above areas
