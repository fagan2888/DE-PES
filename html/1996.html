    Talent Acquisition Specialist at SessionM

    • As the Senior Integration Engineer, you will have the unique opportunity to join a successful and rapidly growing company in a leading client facing role. We have some of the world’s most interesting people working on highly advanced Big Data solutions that power the rewards & loyalty programs of some of world’s biggest and best brands!


    The Senior Integration Engineer is the key technical professional responsible for leading a team of Integration Engineers helping our enterprise clients leverage the power of SessionM’s customer engagement platform. This will involve the design and implementation of sophisticated API and data integration solutions - connecting disparate systems and assuring the smooth flow of massive amounts of datasets (we process about 4 billion transactions per day - in near real time!).


    Ideal candidates will be creative, and critical thinkers, who can drive execution and handle multiple tasks simultaneously with efficiency and effectiveness. Additionally, they need to be solution oriented, process driven, fast learners, strong communicators and passionate about technology!


    Responsibilities


    • Be a team player with the energy, drive, and excitement to help grow a fast moving company.
    • Managing a team of integration engineers for client delivery tasks for a SaaS platform in the loyalty / CRM industry
    • Interface directly with enterprise clients to integrate their mobile, web, e-commerce, and point of sale applications with our APIs/SDKs and custom features.
    • Strong understanding on integration technologies, data flows and strong data management knowledge
    • Manage the technical customer relationship for all facets of the integration including, but not limited to, requirements clarification, integration testing, load testing, giving demos, upgrading software, and responding to Customer needs for technical information.
    • Work closely with product and engineering in delivering enterprise-level solutions to customers through problem solving, testing, and code delivery.
    • Maximize efficiency in a constantly evolving environment where the process is fluid and creative solutions are necessary.
    • Work with development engineers, product managers, account managers and other departments to understand the technology resolve problems
    • Position may require some travel and some availability for off-hours support required.


    Requirements


    • 5+ years experience in solution integration/delivering, testing, and interfacing with customers.
    • Exceptional organizational skills, meticulous attention to detail, and the ability to maintain focus in fast paced environment.
    • Ability to thoroughly document the custom solution down to API calls.
    • Formulate and execute integration tests, load tests, and performance tests.
    • Strong knowledge of APIs, SDKs, scripting, and ETL.
    • Understanding of Web Services, Internet architecture, SQL, and software development.
    • Self-motivated and self-managed with a high degree of dependability.
    • Excellent communication skills.
    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or other supportive program, or commensurate technology industry experience preferred.


    What we are offering

    • Competitive pay, including equity
    • Opportunity to get in early to build a world-class organization, shape the future of our team, our product, and our company.
    • Excitement of working on our high-volume - 4 billion transactions/day - and high-performance - 50 ms service response times - platform for some of the most exciting brands – Coca-Cola, Air Canada, Chipotle, L’Oreal to name a few.
