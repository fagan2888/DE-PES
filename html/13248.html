Data Scientist

Morgan McKinley London, GB

1 week ago

Apply on company website
An American private equity firm based in London are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join their data science team and work with a portfolio of clients and help them in deploying analytical solutions.

As most of the team are based in the US, this client is looking for someone who is a team player, can take the lead on projects, has great communication skills and has a wide breadth of experience.

Salary is flexible based on experience.

Essential
  • Commercial use of designing and building machine learning models
  • An excellent coder using either Python or R
  • The ability to work with structured data as well as develop complex algorithms
  • Consultative experience and the ability to present findings to clients
  • Great communication skills

If you or someone you may know is interested in learning more about this opportunity, please email Sadie on sharlow@morganmckinley.co.uk or call +44 2070920229

Thank you

Morgan McKinley is acting as an Employment Agency in relation to this vacancy.
