Barcelona


A bright Spanish service startup is currently welcoming a brilliant Senior Data Scientist to join their highly-trained team.


The candidate will be responsible for:
  • Building, deploying and maintaining predictive models for marketing & customer activities (including conversion/response/churn predictions/customer segmentation) using advanced statistical analysis;
  • Manage and conduct regular reporting and post campaign analysis;
  • Collaborate and challenge product teams to improve effectiveness against Marketing ROI;
  • Develop and innovate analysis of customer behaviour, attitudes and experience;
  • Provide actionable insights to support the development, implementation and tracking of the Customer Acquisition/retention.

Your Profile:

  • Significant experience applying analytics to answer business questions;
  • Degree in a quantitative subject area (Statistics, Math, or Computer Science);
  • Very strong SQL, Python, R OR Matlab;
  • Fluent in english;
  • Strong Analytic skills;
  • Knowledge of MS Office and Tableau is beneficial
