Supplier Quality Engineer

Terex Corporation Omagh, GB

1 week ago

Apply on company website
Omagh, NIR, GB

Terex are delighted to announce, that due to recent expansion we are now recruiting for a Supplier Quality Engineer to join our quality team at our Omagh site. This is an excellent opportunity to become part of a global engineering organisation with excellent job security and one that offers long term career development.

This is a key role, working closely with the all business key stakeholders and supplier development and innovation group to achieve performance targets to enhance the overall effectiveness of the organization.

Responsibilities
  • Continuous improvement of supplier quality control and manufacturing capabilities for serial production
  • Co-ordination and implementation of engineering changes for new product development
  • Corrective Actions process with suppliers
  • Manage process audits of existing bought-out parts, where the parts are the subject of a problem solving/action/deviations
  • Conduct supplier audits and provide support for supplier quality systems improvements
  • Develop and maintain the supplier first article submission program
  • Manage supplier change notifications to ensure that they are appropriately assessed, communicated and all follow-up activities are tracked through timely completion.
  • Supplier Quality Assessments and formal notification to suppliers of their evaluation status
  • Manage suppliers’ action and progress plans (serial production)
  • Initiate and validate corrective and preventive actions with the suppliers for immediate containment actions
  • Work with suppliers in implementing necessary actions to resolve supplier corrective actions through the SCAR process
  • Consolidate required supplier quality KPIs for the site and communicate on site and to Supplier Quality director
  • Accept Production Part Approval Process (PPAP) when serial production starts
  • Ensure good coordination and communication with engineering, sourcing/purchasing and quality


Qualifications

Engineering Degree or equivalent experience in an Engineering/Manufacturing environment with quality/supplier quality experience.

Skills And Experience
  • 2 years’ experience in Manufacturing, New Product Development, Engineering Changes Order, Lean Manufacturing, Total Quality Management, Supplier Quality
  • Experience in auditing and assuring implementation of corrective action at suppliers
  • Technical knowledge characteristics of a wide range of component parts, assembly, tooling and manufacturing process
  • Demonstrated success in problem solving, as well as product and process analysis
  • Excellent analytical skills and trained to work with relational databases
  • Results driven, action oriented
  • Process discipline
  • Communication and collaboration skills with the ability to develop relationships across levels, geographies and segments

The role will involve a level of travel both locally and internationally.

The successful candidate will be offered an exciting opportunity within a global engineering organization that can offer excellent long term career prospects plus an attractive salary and benefits package that includes pension, life assurance, healthcare plus others. In addition to that you will also get exposure to global engineering best practices and the opportunity to learn from an international team of engineers.

To apply for this role please click the link below or visit the following website http://terex-uk.jobs/ and select the relevant role from our website.

Closing date for Applications is Friday 6th September at 9am.
