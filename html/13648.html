  • Support the Movember campaign and help make a positive difference to men’s health

**Re-advertised position**


Currently operating in over 20 countries, Movember raises funds that deliver innovative, breakthrough research and support programs that allow men to live happier, healthier and longer lives. Since 2003, millions have joined the men's health movement, raising more than AUS$800 million and funding over 1,200 global projects. We’re addressing some of the biggest health issues faced by men: prostate cancer, testicular cancer, mental health and suicide prevention. Our goal is to reduce the number of men dying prematurely all over the world. You can help us get there.


This position works within the Insights Team to manage the data pipeline including implementing, testing, and maintaining the infrastructure. This role will have an in-depth understanding of the various technologies and frameworks, and how to combine them to create and iterate solutions that will enable Movember’s data pipeline processes. To find the best candidate we are open to offering a part time or full time work opportunity.


Responsibilities:

  • Use your programming and systems creation skills to create, iterate, and maintain data pipelines
  • Ensure data capture, and reporting methodologies are valid, consistent, and accurate
  • Some basic to intermediate level analytics
  • Work with prominent internal stakeholders to understand and determine data and information needs, making recommendations of required changes to provide enhanced analysis and insights


Expertise required in:

  • Knowledge and experience with AWS, GCP, Azure
  • Knowledge and experience with a variety of tools, and languages including Python, SQL, Microsoft Office, JavaScript , HTML, and CSS
  • Highly organised with an ability to manage own time, and work on concurrent projects in a demanding and rewarding environment
  • Keen attention to detail
  • Project Management skills


If you feel you have the talent and personality to join the Movember team, please provide a cover letter with your CV addressing the following questions:

1.   Why do your skills and expertise make you a good match for this role?

2.   Which Movember value resonates most closely with you and why?


Please apply for the role by October 24, 2019.
