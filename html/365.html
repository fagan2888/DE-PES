    • Job Description

    In this role you have the opportunity to:

    assess, analyze, monitor and improve the critical capabilities and processes within the factories of Philips and Philips’ suppliers. This, in a very hands-on manner, where you speak the language of the factory and its management. Important is that you bring along deep understanding of manufacturing processes, (supply chain and/or quality preferred). The main goal is to find real improvement opportunities serving the efficiency targets of each site.

    Philips aims to improve the lives of 3 billion by the year 2030. This is done through meaningful innovation. As Operational Excellence Director you have the chance to continuously improve the supply chain for both Philips as well as Philips’ suppliers. You’ll be acting in a leading position in finding concrete improvement proposals at a Philips- or suppliers-site and drafting an improvement plan over time including implementation support on demand.


    You are responsible for

    • You will be overseeing the potentials of the complete value chain. Next to that you will set priorities which are connected to our business specific strategic goals for/with your stakeholders;
    • Structurally support the different Plant-MT’s to improve factory KPI’s by regular OPEX assessments and identify operational improvement opportunities in close cooperation with the site MT (outside- in view); eg line balancing / OEE/ Workspeed analysis / factory load / etc
    • Cost calculation on integral TCO-basis: (Product pre-Calculation bottom-up, Target Calculation top-down Post Calculation)
    • Act as a change agent within the organization to drive standardization;
    • Work in an international context with multiple stakeholders and cultural diversity.

    You are a part of

    the global Operational Excellence team. You will be a spider in the web and will work in a challenging, multi-cultural, innovative environment with great visibility and opportunities for you to explore.


    To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience

    • Bachelor/master degree in a relevant technical discipline Industrial Engineering is a must.
    • 5 years of relevant working experience
    • Experience with process improvements and cost optimization.
    • Experience with modern engineering methods, tools, processes and statistical data analyses
    • In-depth understanding of manufacturing and product development processes
    • Thorough knowledge about the business areas supported including their strategies, goals, competitive environment, functional processes, and organizational dynamics
    • Knowledge of lean methods and change management
    • Able to work in an international organization: virtual teams, international setting, global organization. Familiar with cultural aspects of region(s). working across teams from different culture and in different time zones. Experience in working within a global context (preference). Travel up to 40% is required.


    In return, we offer you

    We welcome you to a challenging, innovative environment with great opportunities for you to explore. You’ll be working in a global team of Supply Chain/Operational Excellence specialists who will focus on continuous optimize the processes within factories from both Philips and Philips’ suppliers.


    Why should you join Philips?

    Working at Philips is more than a job. It’s a calling to create a healthier society through meaningful work, focused on improving 3 billion lives a year by delivering innovative solutions across the health continuum. Our people experience a variety of unexpected moments when their lives and careers come together in meaningful ways. Learn more by watching this video.
