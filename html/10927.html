    Lead High Impact Recruiter | Talent Acquisition

    • UST Global is looking to hire a Data Scientist for our busy MDM team

    This position can be worked REMOTELY from anywhere in the US.

    Your responsibilities in the team:

    • Design, develop and implement a data architecture which is scalable and adheres to the enterprise architecture and overall strategy

    • Design, develop and enforce project level policies in regards to aligning data management with the organizations business strategy

    • Design a flexible data architecture that will evolve in response to future business and technology changes as well that meets corporate data integration and sharing needs

    • Provide subject matter expertise and independently develop policies and procedures governing corporate data in areas such as data deployment decision making, subject area definition accountability, data management responsibility, data quality


    Job requirements:

    • Should have strong healthcare domain knowledge with an excellent membership domain background.
    • Should be an SME of Membership systems, need to understand the various membership system and data models.
    • Should be proficient in data profiling and interpret to mapping documents
    • Should be an expert in Master data management.
    • Strong SQL skills to write Adhoc queries for data analysis data profiling.
    • Experience in tools like Relito would be an added advantage
    • Have experience in conducting requirements gathering sessions meetings by facilitating a group of Subject Matter Experts to elicit business requirements.
    • Have experience in conducting business requirement walk throughs, facilitate technical requirements walk through with Dev, SIT and UAT teams.
    • Have experience in requirements to ReqPro JIRA , trace back to stakeholder requests.
    • Have experience in UAT test cases preparation and test strategy plan by identifying all possible business scenarios from Business owners.
    • Have experience in user validation in UAT environment.
    • Fluent in system analysis, impact gap analysis
    • Determine and document technical responses, source target mapping docs
    • Good communication skills
    • Potential to learn the system quickly
    • Past Healthcare domain experience would be an added advantage
