Are you our new data solutions engineer?
  • Do you love to solve problems within the domains of data analysis and business intelligence?
  • Do you get motivated by working with large amounts of data and the potential it has for providing key stakeholders with crucial, value-generating insights?
  • Do you have an eye for finding structured, functional and elegant solutions to seemingly complex data analysis problems?
  • Do you love to solve hard, time-sensitive challenges in collaboration with dedicated colleagues - and do you enjoy celebrating your shared success?
If you can answer yes to these questions, then this position might be for you!

As a Candidate, You Should
  • Routinely work within the area of data science given experience from your studies (M.Sc. level proficiency within e.g., software or IT technology), and/or previous work (3 years or more)
  • Be passionate about solving hard, technical data related problems in a structured manner
  • Be familiar working with varying data input/output and standards from data sources in the form of databases, APIs and files
  • Be excited about technology and the impact it can create for others
  • Take pride in the quality of your work via thorough and structured development, testing, deployment and documentation of the solutions you create
  • Have strong verbal and written English skills
  • Collaborate well within a team setting, composed of experts within respective domains, both technical and business related in nature

The Ideal Candidate Furthermore Has
  • A drive towards learning other technical skills, such as high-level programming languages like R or python
  • Knowledge of writing optimized database queries within e.g., SQL

You Will Be Tasked With
  • Designing, implementing, and testing complex workflows in Alteryx
  • Integrating these workflows with numerous data sources and internal as well as external party systems (files, databases and APIs)
  • Executing and delivering working Alteryx workflow solutions based on a well-defined set of requirements from several stakeholders within both business and technical domains
  • Maintaining and operating our Alteryx workflows, and documenting them as well as their interaction points with internal and external IT systems
  • Standardizing and modularizing an ever-growing number of existing and to-be-developed Alteryx workflows
  • Ensuring and improving data processing speed within our Alteryx solutions

To apply, please send us your motivational letter, resumé and transcripts via The Hub
