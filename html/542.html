About GRESB

GRESB was launched ten years ago by a group of large pension funds who wanted to have a better engagement with their managers on sustainability issues. In the intervening years, we have grown to become the leading Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) benchmark for real estate and infrastructure investments across the world.

Our ESG data and benchmarks now cover over USD 4 trillion in real estate and infrastructure value and are used by more than 100 institutional investors to make decisions that lead to a more sustainable world.

We have an engaged community of asset managers who are proud to participate in our annual benchmarking process and a group of inspirational industry partners and ESG service providers who amplify our reach.

The GRESB team comes from diverse backgrounds with experience spanning sustainability, data science, software development, real estate, infrastructure, and the capital markets. We’re passionate about tackling the sustainability challenge by providing the insights that will power the markets of the future and enable investors to allocate the world’s capital in a more responsible way.

We are an energetic group of 25+ people, covering dozens of different nationalities, located in Amsterdam, with satellite offices in Vancouver, Washington DC and Sydney. We care deeply about creating a positive and inclusive working environment where everyone feels comfortable contributing their ideas.

We have shared lunches, regular happy hours and team retreats in fun locations.

The Position

As a Senior Data Scientist/Engineer you uncover insights in data sets, which can be used for public output by leveraging your expertise and skills in computer science, applications, modelling, statistics, analytics, and math. The Senior Data Scientist/Engineer links the meaning of the data of the assessments to Marketing, so all metrics, visualizations and other data insights are potentially marketable to GRESB’s user base. The Senior Data Scientist/Engineer works in a team with other Senior Data Scientists/Engineers, and reports to the Product Manager. What will you do

  • Scoring model
    • Managing the scoring model - replicating of all core scoring models for testing purposes, outlier validation system, aggregation of performance indicators, etc. Scoring model development and analysis is all done in R; sometimes using internal R packages that were internally developed for GRESB specific use-cases
    • Adding third party data sources in scoring model - spatial data as flood maps, external metrics mined from websites as GRI or UN PRI

  • Analytics, visualizations and insights
    • Developing asset level data analysis tool and output, in collaboration with GRESB team
    • Cleaning and verifying the integrity of data
    • Enhancing data collection procedures
    • Creating data visualization graphs
    • Delivering helpdesk support on data issues
    • Getting data into the right format, convenient for analysis/interpretation

  • Support and advise
    • Acting as a consultant to other GRESB teams as a Subject Matter Expert
    • Advice on assessment design and rules on correct reporting
    • Supporting GRESB partners with GRESB’s API or loading asset level spreadsheets
    • Formulating the questions that will help GRESB in relations and business development and then proceed in solving them
    • Converting Data into Marketable Business story


    Requirements
    • A minimum of 4 years experience working in R
    • A minimum of 2 years experience with developing and deploying R Shiny applications
    • Experience developing production-level code is a plus
    • Experience working with Git is a plus
    • Experience using SQL database is a plus
