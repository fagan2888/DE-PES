    RESINS PROCESS ENGINEER


    - Specialized in Manufacturing/Process/Design Engineering, this role manages various plant processes and systems, technologies. Interfaces with various plant stakeholders. This role can be based in France, Italy, Poland or other locations. The person will report to the Director Resin Operations & SC EMEA


    - Ensure industrialization of new products through the assessment of the business needs; process & safety requirements; existing facility design and/or need for adjustment in order to deliver consistent quality at best conversion cost and capacity utilization


    The incumbent is responsible for taking leadership over the entire industrialization process collecting all necessary information to perform adequate pilot batch trial; PFMEA; risk assessment; implementation of facility upgrade/MOC practices; identification of training needs at the receiving plants.


    Acountabilities:


    1. Coordinate ACE/Auto/IC/PAC/PMC/REF SBU resin development / ensuring that the process is safe, feasible an reliable and that the prodcuts will meet business requirements

    2. Manage Technology transfer to achieve optimization of capacity utilization by recommending the most suitable production plants (quality and cost), reviewing equipment design and following the first production batches if required

    3. Resolve differences in processing and quality to ensure solid supply chain process including the development of back-up plan.


    Requirements:


    • Expertise in polimer chemisty, processing and process control, manufacturing and knowledge in EH&S and resin process safety;
    • Familiarity with computerized database utilization, problem soving and statistical analysis technics;
    • Skills in project management and decision making processes;
    • Fluent in English and excellent interpersonal skills in working with technical and operational personnel, inside and outside PPG, in order to be the central coordination point for any technological change in resins manufacturing across Europe
    • Aptitude to work autonomously, to correctly estimate priorities, to travel easy
    • Available to travel: 50% of the time

    Success Factors ( Prime Success Factors (focus on results, drive change, promote teamwork, build trust & respect, understand market & customer perspective) plus the following:

    • Take responsabilities;
    • Mobilize resources;
    • Manage complexity;
    • Plan and organize (project management)
