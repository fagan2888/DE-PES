Executive recruitment company Monroe Consulting Group Indonesia is recruiting on behalf of a leading Industrial Estate. As expansion continues, our client is seeking a professional with at least 15-20 years’ experience in industrial estate for the job of General Manager Technical and Safety. The job is based in Gresik, Surabaya, Indonesia.

Key job responsibilities include:

  • Report directly to the President Director
  • Manage five division: Master Planning & Estate Management, Engineering, Project Management, Infrastructure Maintenance and SHE
  • Ensure site development and sale of land are according to the agreed master plan.
  • Provide inputs to sales for the lands ready to sell
  • Oversee detailed site development plan
  • Set estate regulations and adjust as needed
  • Propose adjustment to master plan based on changes in the business environment
  • Act as technical support for operational enablement
  • Map out and ensure key processes are supported by standards, policies or SOP
  • Provide technical support to Operations for design of new construction and/ or extension of utility plants
  • Manage construction of plants and infrastructure
  • Compliance to regulations, budget and spec
  • Manage end-to-end construction within expected quality, on time and within budget
  • For utilities construction, ensure good coordination with Operations Division to make sure fulfilment of operational requirements
  • Ensure all infrastructure and landscape are well maintained
  • Set infrastructure maintenance guidelines
  • Monitor progress of maintenance execution
  • Set and ensure safety standards, guidelines and processes are implemented. Perform any other assignment given by superior
  • Conduct effective risk management process and promote risk awareness



Job Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in engineering, or discipline from reputable university
  • Minimum 15 years’ experience in Industrial Estate
  • Proven track record of developing and maintaining infrastructure and landscape
  • Knowledge of estate regulations
  • Analytical skills and understanding of the construction of plants and infrastructure
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, ability to work with teams and networks and influence outcomes at many levels in an organization
  • Excellent project management skills on Master Planning & Estate Management, Engineering, Project Management, Infrastructure Maintenance and SHE
  • Strong oral and written communication skills are mandatory
  • Influencing and networking skills
  • Strong strategic to handle end-to-end construction within expected quality, on time and within budget
  • Solid leadership skills, mentoring and coaching
  • Strong problem-solving ability and effective employee management experience
  • Willing to base in Gresik, Surabaya
