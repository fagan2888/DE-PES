Technical Master Data Management Engineer (m/f)

TGW Logistics Group is an international, fast-growing company and one of the global players in the intralogistics industry. In the course of the introduction of a new ERP system, we are extending our standardisation team at the Wels office. In this responsible position, you will make a significant contribution to the constant extension and improvement of our material master data management processes – with the goal of sustainably improving data quality. With your technical understanding, your interest in programming and your customer-oriented way of working, you will play a major role in the successful implementation and constant optimisation of our new ERP system (SAP S/4Hana). Depending on your previous experience, you will receive extensive training. Additionally, we will support your individual development.

Tasks

TASKS
  • Programming and maintenance of rules and workflows
  • Definition of and adherence to programming guidelines (naming convention, structure,
    consistency)
  • Continuous coordination of requirements within the team
  • Performance of tests and documentation of new rules
  • Support in case of questions and problems concerning our SAP 3rd party application

Requirements

REQUIREMENTS
  • Completed technical or economic education
  • Interest in programming; ideally basic knowledge and first professional experience
  • Very good command of MS Office (especially Excel)
  • SAP knowledge ("material master" module) is an advantage
  • Ability to define and establish structures (analytical and logical thinking)
  • Good command of English

WE OFFER

WE OFFER

The minimum annual gross pay for this position is 31,990 Euros according to the collective agreement. A possible overpay depends on your individual job experience and qualification.

Furthermore, we offer the safety of a privately owned company, flexible working hours and a cooperative working environment with attractive benefits.
Use your know-how and skills for an exciting job and start your career in a diversified field of responsibility in an international network for automated logistics technology.
Become part of our dynamic team!
