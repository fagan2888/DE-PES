    Corporate Recruiter at funda

    • Funda's data is a real Valhalla for econometrists and statisticians. As an applicant, you recognize the tremendous opportunities which a combination of the data received from several millions of website visitors per month, highly intelligent colleagues (from product development and UX to marketing and sales) and your own statistical models presents.


    Based on facts you ensure that we can go that extra mile, deploy our people and means more effectively, and, as a company, remain at the top of our game. As a Data Scientist (or Data Analyst, if you prefer), you know that R isn't some hot new nightclub. In addition, you are looking for a job in which you can choose your own strategies and priorities, and enjoy the freedom of deciding how to execute them.


    Your job

    You will be analyzing funda's data, using it to create insight into the behavior and needs of customers and users. To do so, you will carry out complex analyses with the aid of statistical models. You will ensure that our data becomes even better and – as a result – our business as well. For this you will of course be validating the results from our data sources and identifying erroneous data.


    You identify subjects and derive customer insights, determine the segmentation and forecasting models and their development, and you will advise colleagues on the use of all these opportunities. You will therefore need to know how to build a strong relationship with teams within Product, Engineering and Commerce, among others.


    Your team

    At funda, you won’t be locked away in a cubicle on a business park. As a Data Scientist, you'll work in the heart of Amsterdam, as part of a diverse 6-person data team. This team includes two Back-End Engineers (Joao and Liewe), two Data Scientists (Edwin and yourself), a Data Infrastructure Engineer (Marco) and your Head of Data.


    Your profile

    • You have a higher vocational quantitative degree (Science, Econometrics, Engineering Management, Statistics);
    • You have 3-5 years of experience in a similar role (e.g. as Data Scientist, technical analyst);
    • You have work experience with digital analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics etc.) and TMS;
    • You have extensive experience in data analysis and visualization tools (such as PowerBI, Tableau, QlikView etc.);
    • You have extensive experience with or knowledge of SQL or another query language;
    • You have experience in programming (Javascript, Python, R);
    • Having experience with REST API's is an advantage;
    • You have experience with Agile (Kanban, Scrum).

    We prefer applicants from EU/EEA citizens, not in need of a working permit for the Netherlands.


    This is what we offer

    Funda offers you the most challenging job in your field. In a great office in the heart of Amsterdam, with our own ‘funda square’ and an unobstructed view of the IJ river, or a cruise ship on it – that just depends on the day (and yes, the latter makes for an instant holiday feeling). You are within two tram stops of Amsterdam Central, or 5 minutes by bicycle or 10 minutes by foot. Just what suits you.

    You work with 120 great (international) minds who make you feel right at home. It’s only natural that we take good care of our talents and therefore of you. If you come work with us, we offer you:


    • Excellent salary with annual bonus scheme and 25 days off (you can buy more days if you need them);
    • Flexible working hours;
    • Reimbursements for your telephone, home internet, travel expenses, gym and a part of your pension;
    • Heavenly chair massage 6 times a year (during working hours);
    • Chef-prepared fresh lunch every day, ranging from soups to salads, fresh bread rolls from the baker’s and a snack each Friday;
    • Dinners, team outings, drinks and a birthday present;
    • Personal training budget for courses, congresses and training courses, as we believe that you've never learned enough. You can also let your ideas run free during our bi-annual hackathon;
    • Especially for our foreign co-workers we offer Dutch lessons.

    Interested?

    At funda we like to act swiftly. That's why you may expect the following procedure from us:

    You apply:

    You can do this by sending in your resume including a cover letter (or video) via the application button.

    Hooray!

    Going to the next round? Then our Corporate Recruiters will invite you for a telephone screening.

    And then...

    If you have passed the telephone screening, we will schedule that first job interview with you as soon as possible. You may expect a maximum of 2 rounds of interviews.

    To case or not to case

    For this vacancy a case will be part of the last interview round.
