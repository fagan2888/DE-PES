Description

Mondelēz International , Inc. empowers people to snack right in over 160 countries around the world. We’re leading the future of snacking with iconic brands such as Oreo, belVita and LU biscuits; Cadbury Dairy Milk, Milka and Toblerone chocolate; Sour Patch Kids candy and Trident gum. Our 90,000+ colleagues around the world are key to the success of our business. Great people and great brands . That’s who we are.

Join us on our mission to continue leading the future of snacking around the world by offering the right snack , for the right moment , made the right way .

Purpose of the role:
  • The role will have the overall responsibility to develop, deploy and maintain high quality, reliable and sustainable statistics and analytics providing business insights. Key starting point is providing most accurate and reliable sales forecast and build trust and confident with business partners such as market Demand Planning, Sales, Marketing and Finance
  • This requires work closely with stakeholders to understand their needs/pain points, scope the problem and develop business case on how to turn data into critical information and knowledge that are actionable, to be used for policy making, streamlining operations or developing solutions
  • The role reports directly to Data Analytics and Modeling Manager within the MEU CS&L organization

Responsibilities:
  • Lead SAS tool implementation according to market roll-out plan and adjust the complex solution where required to market specifics
  • Build statistical modeling and predictive analytics solutions to improve supply chain performance especially more accurate sales forecast and faster than today
  • Guide market Demand Planning teams to get to the most efficient forecasting process by evaluating results and ensure segmentation and automation is in place
  • Build strong relationships with Supply Chain and related business areas to understand and develop Statistical analytics solutions for key business needs
  • Researches and introduces new analytics technology and business trends within the supply chain with a clear continous improvement mindset Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in a quantitative field such as Statistics, Applied Mathematics, Engineering, Computer Science, Economics or similar
  • Strong applied knowledge of analytical techniques in statistical modeling and machine learning, minimum Time Series model experience
  • Experience with programming software SAS, R, Python and similar
  • Excellent analytical and presentation skills; especially finds simple ways and business language to expain stats results to supply chain/sales/marketing/finance people
  • Strong influential skills and advocating data driven analytics across the organisation
  • Data Visualization experience utilizing tools like Tableau, Power BI.
  • High level of Microsoft office package (Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Work experience in CPG companies is an advantage
  • Demand Planning experience is an advantage
  • Proactive, self-motivated, collaborative and driven to perform at pace
  • Fluent in English
  • Analytical and Problem Solving
  • Systems, Tools and Processes e.g APO, SAS tool, R3
  • High Learning Agility
  • Able to translate stats results into business language

Use this opportunity to join our team. We love what we do and we’ll reward you for what you do. We offer an exciting environment and plenty of fun along the way. Let the joy begin!
