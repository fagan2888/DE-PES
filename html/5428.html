    Talent Acquisition & Workplace Culture Enthusiast

    • Welcome to Client Engineering at the company responsible for one of the most popular Music and Sound Search applications in the iTunes Store, and the ground-breaking voice technology powering the Hound personal assistant. In this position, you'll play a key role in the development of our next generation Audio Recognition apps, taking SoundHound's highly-sophisticated backend technology and creating a polished UI/UX to delight our hundreds of millions of users and maintain SoundHound's place as THE go-to app for Music Search and Exploration. To achieve this, you're an experienced Mobile Engineer with deep knowledge of the iOS platform, who enjoys working as part of a tight-knit team comprised of passionate, innovative and highly-collaborative Developers. You believe things like SoundHound's unique 'LiveLyrics' feature and Hound's ability to interpret complex queries are awesome, and would relish the opportunity to sign your name to even cooler functionality within future products. Sound good? Read on!


    Responsibilities:

    • Work on iPhone/iPad application architecture and drive development using the latest mobile standards in music and social gaming.
    • Participate in all phases of our software development life cycle to convert concepts and specifications into released iPhone/iPad applications and features.
    • Interact daily with the systems and processes used in professional software development, including source control, bug tracking, testing, etc.


    Requirements:

    • Fluency in and a deep knowledge of Swift and key technologies for the iOS platform like UIKit, CoreAnimation, CoreLocation etc.
    • Experience working on applications consuming JSON from RESTful services.
    • Experience building custom UI and a strong intuitive sense for good user experience.
    • Familiarity with good design patterns and best engineering practices within iOS.
    • Strong experience detecting and correcting memory usage issues, and optimizing code for application performance.
    • Experience managing provisioning profiles, developer certificates, distribution certificates.
    • Experience distributing apps to the Apple App Store, as well as navigating the Apple review process.
    • BS/MS in Computer Science or equivalent


    Nice to Haves:

    • Experience with one or more of the following: low latency audio, low latency networking, performance analysis and Social APIs (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
    • Scalability


    How to Apply:

    • Download and use our iPhone/iPad app. We'll talk to you about the experience as a part of the interview process.
    • Apply through LinkedIn or our Careers Page. Please be sure to include an appropriate cover letter.
    • If you have shipped any iPhone/iPad apps that you are proud of, point us in their direction.
