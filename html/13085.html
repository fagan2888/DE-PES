Corporate Overview

QuinStreet makes the digital media channel affordable at scale for marketing clients. We match high-intent consumers in one of the nation’s largest media networks to the right solution and the right client.

Our industry leading segmentation & matching technology platform allows marketing clients to target and reach “research & compare” customer prospects, segment by segment. Our databases and optimization algorithms are built from almost 20 years and billions of dollars of online media experience.

We believe in the direct measurability of digital media. We believe in performance marketing. (We pioneered it.) And we believe in the advantages of technology. We bring all of this together to deliver truly great results for our clients in the world’s biggest channel.

Job Category

For our office in São Paulo, Brazil, QuinStreet is looking for an exceptionally talented and enthusiastic Data Engineer - Pleno to join our highly visible, start-up like team. You will collaborate with business teams in São Paulo as well as engineers in the U.S. headquarters, and contribute to build BI for our major products and sites, catered for the Brazilian market. This will include but is not limited to building the data access layer, converging data from different sources within QuinStreet´s complex platform and third-party applications into a big data environment. You will love this position if you are passionate about data and want a challenging role that is critical to the growth of our business in Brazil!

If you pride yourself on being an active problem solver and a team player, then this is an opportunity to launch a fast-paced and rewarding career in a cutting-edge field.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with business users in requirement gathering and design of analysis, reports and dashboards.
  • Develop SQL queries to extract/organize data from different sources (Oracle, Data Warehouse, Hadoop, cloud applications) and make it available for data analysts.
  • Be the Data Expert in Brazil office, interfacing DWH and Big Data teams in the US office.

    • Requirements
    • Must be fluent in Portuguese and English.
    • BS/BA in Engineering, CS or equivalent, with a minimum of 1-3 years of professional Data Engineer experience.
    • Proficiency in SQL (advanced SQL is required!) and ETL.
    • Strong presentation, communication, and collaboration skills, enabling effective interaction and knowledge transfer to teammates and other departments within the company.
    • A high task load is expected. You should be able to work within tight deadlines while exhibiting exemplary time management and task prioritization.
    • Positive attitude and professionalism in the workplace as well as great sense of humor is always appreciated.

    Desired (but Not Required)
    • Experience with:
      • Big Data (Hadoop / Hive)
      • Python / PHP
      • Shell scripting
      • Tableau
