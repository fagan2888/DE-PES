Our client, a sizable conglomerate is now looking for high caliber candidates as follows:


Big Data Engineer / Senior Big Data Engineer



Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain the core Big Data system for the company;
  • Design and maintain the Big Data architecture according to the requirements of business function;
  • Ensure the backup and recovery procedures are adequate and enforce information security standards and procedures

Requirements:

  • Degree or above in Computer Science, Information Technology or related disciplines (Data Mining, Machine Learning);
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in Big Data architectural design;
  • Familiarity with Hadoop, Map/Reduce and NoSQL databases;
  • Comfortable in a programming language like Python, PHP, R and SHINY;
  • Experience with machine learning packages;
  • Hands-on experience on Oracle, MySQL Server, Greenplum with GIS database administration and optimization;
  • Knowledge in Business Intelligence Techniques, ETL processing and methodologies is a definite advantage;
  • Good command of spoken and written English, Cantonese and Mandarin;
  • Solid interpersonal, communication and organizational skill.


Please send in your full resume with last drawn, expected salary and availability to jobs@bondwest.com.hk

For any query, please feel free to contact Mr. Eric Wong at 852 2957 8079.

For more job opportunity, please visit our website: www.bondwest.com
