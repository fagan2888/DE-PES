Descriptive Job Title: Software Engineer – Equipment Development Engineering

Areas of Interest : Software Development, Equipment Development, Equipment Performance, Automation Solutions

Who We Are
  • Seagate is an innovative company that delivers advanced digital storage solutions to meet the needs of today’s consumers and tomorrow’s applications. Through technology, leadership and innovation, Seagate continues to help individuals and businesses maximize the potential of their digital content in an ever-evolving, on-demand world.

About Our Group
  • Equipment Development Engineering (EDE) team provide total engineering solution and support to Operation and R&D division in the organization through prototyping, equipment efficiency optimization and mass production activities.
  • Our team develop the automation machine for the HDD assembly process

About The Role/A Day In The Life
  • Develop software features for new equipment, New Process capabilities and /or Equipment Performance Improvements
  • Develop software for equipment enterprise management for overall status control and data analytics
  • Involved in Equipment Development for Substrate, Media Test and Media Servo Write Automation line with Scara robot and linear actuator automation

What Success Means/Who Is a Top Performer
  • Enjoys working on projects with a team and taking on more independent/personal responsibilities for the project
  • Takes initiative to seek new ways to get involved in more projects
  • Hardworking and able to meet deadlines for project milestones
  • Able to communicate in a clear, precise and actionable manner with stakeholders.

About You
  • Bachelor’s / Master’s degree and/or equivalent experience
  • Fresh graduate are encouraged to apply

You’re Good At
  • Both technical skills and soft skills
    • VB6 ,C# ,C
    • Adept V+, BnR/Beckhoff controller PLC Structured Text, ABB Rapid language
    • SQL database query language
    • JSON , XML data exchange format
    • WinForms, WPF XAML GUI programming
    • Python, Panda

Your Experience Includes
  • Software development
  • Working with people from around the world, flexibility with meeting times and deadlines

Company Culture
  • Our culture is focused on our Corporate Values --People, Customer Success, Excellence, Innovation, Teamwork, Openness, Social Responsibility and Sustained Profitability.
  • Join a worldwide team of talented, collaborative, innovative people who thrive in a work environment where people can share knowledge and learn from one another. You won’t find a more exciting place to build your career and challenge yourself. Accept the challenge and Go where innovation leads
Location:

Our Woodlands site is one of the largest electronics manufacturing sites in Singapore, housing our recording media operations. Spread over three sites, it is easily accessible via bus or from the MRT Station, with many employees taking mass-transportation to work. Ample free on-site parking is also available. Here at work, you can enjoy breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks at our on-site canteen and coffee shop. We offer a range of facilities including an in-house gym and dance studio, as well as after-work badminton and table tennis competitions. On-site celebrations and community volunteer opportunities also abound. Don’t miss our Lunar New Year Lion Dance performance or our Seagate’s Got Talent event.
