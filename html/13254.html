    Job description

    Do you enjoy cracking data, integrating and creating new products and working with Big Data tools? We are looking for a talented Data Engineer to join our exciting and dynamic team in Barcelona.

    At DerbySoft, we leverage the huge amount of data of 95+ top travel brands to generate hundreds of millions/year of hotel revenue on Google, TripAdvisor, Trivago and other large Internet platforms. As a Data Engineer, you will be challenged to design, build and deploy the data infrastructure that supports Click, our digital marketing and metasearch manager tool.

    Key responsibilities:

    • Design, implement and maintain a data warehouse model to provide the Data Science and Product teams with a uniform data interface.
    • Design, implement and maintain custom Big Data solutions for specific Product and Data Science requirements. Use the right tool for the right job.
    • Monitor data quality and enforce the Big Data team to implement data validation rules both at system and business level.
    • Introduce new technology tools to increase development speed and reduce intervention times to fix data inconsistencies.
    • Coach team members with model design technical skills and domain knowledge.

    Desired skills and experience:

    • Experience working with bash scripting and Linux environments on a daily basis
    • Knowledge of Chinese: You will be working with the teams in Europe and China.
    • Experience with Cloud Services and Big Data tools.
    • Experience in design, implementation and maintainingKnowledge of with various database solutions (RDBMS SQL, NoSQL, Data Warehousing…)
    • Experience in implementation and maintenance of Big Data ETLs
    • Ability to work effectively in both independent and team environments
    • Good oral and written English communication skills

    Other skills:

    • Master or bachelor degree in computer science, engineering or other related fields
    • Experience Amazon Web Services is a big plus
    • Good software engineering skills
    • Experience with data logging tools and APIs



    About DerbySoft

    Founded in 2002, DerbySoft is a Shanghai and Dallas-based technology company serving the global hospitality industry. The mission of the company is to facilitate a global distribution network utilizing direct connections between hospitality suppliers and their demand-side counterparts. The company’s infrastructure is globally distributed and its partners include all of the world’s largest hotel companies, online travel agencies, metasearch engines, and other industry participants.
