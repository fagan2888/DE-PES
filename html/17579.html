Description

Company Description:

Ampere is designing the future of hyperscale cloud and edge computing with its 64-bit Arm server processor architecture. Born in and built for the cloud with a modern architecture, Ampere gives customers the freedom to accelerate the delivery of the most memory-intensive applications such as artificial intelligence, big data, machine learning and databases in the cloud. The Ampere eMAG family of products delivers the highest memory throughput and lowest TCO tailored for the emerging growth of cloud computing and next-generation data centers.

Our Story

Like the scientist behind its name, Ampere employees are innovators. We understand the needs of cloud computing and different software requirements. We are inventing what comes next and looking at everything from the structure of memory and how efficient the system is, to considerations on speed, cost of electricity and ability to cool. Power, size, weight and cost are driving the technology requirements and the innovation to come.

Our world class team of engineers, with depth and expertise in the cloud and semiconductor industries, is not only focused on the development of new semiconductor designs but also building out the first software ecosystem for Arm®-based server processors. Through the Ampere approach to the cloud and edge, we give our customers the freedom to challenge the status quo and accelerate next-generation data centers for the most memory-intensive applications. Given the challenge we have outlined, we are building a culture of entrepreneurs that ensure customers come first, proactively approaching industry challenges in the areas of security, power and performance, delivering results that matter most.

Job Description

Ampere Computing is seeking talented Software Development Engineer in Test to join the platform engineering team. This position will be working closely with internal and external design teams to develop tests that validate motherboards, systems, and other server components. Software Development in Test includes, but not limited to, interface design, analysis and prototyping, product maintenance, updates as features change, working through to resolution of bugs with the validation tests, automation, user interface, reporting dashboards, etc.

Responsibilities
  • Create new automated test reporting, analysis, and dashboarding of the test data
  • Maintain and improve validation test plans and test instructions
  • Development of test utilities, scripts and python tests within the automation environment
  • Ensure robust, sustainable and scalable design approaches that meet design intent
  • Update existing platform tests for scale testing and robustness in automation environment
  • Recommend development, design, and quality improvement to products and procedures
  • Report progress and status of assigned projects utilizing

    Incidental Duties: The above statements describe the general nature and level of work performed in this job. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all duties, and indeed additional responsibilities may be assigned, as required, by the management.

Qualifications
  • Minimum 2+ years of working experience in platform validation/testing
  • Strong knowledge of computer architecture, Kernel, UEFI, Linux
  • Excellent problem-solving skills
  • Operational experience with Linux, Windows, networking
  • Strong experience level with Python and Bash
  • Well versed in unit test creation and working with continuous integration environments
  • Solid hands-on experience in enterprise motherboards and its components with strong testing knowledge
  • Strong sense of teamwork and a good team player, strong communication skills in both speaking and writing, communication with remote team will be required
  • Knowledge and experience of server performance benchmarks and tuning is a plus
  • Working knowledge of a test automation environment
Education
  • Bachelor or Master's degree in Electrical/Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or equivalent
