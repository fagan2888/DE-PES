Miba is one of the leading strategic partners to the international engine and automotive industry.
IT - Data Analytics Team
In close cooperation with the business leaders and experts, digitalization champions and industrial engineers, data scientists and IT colleagues, we create the technological and organizational basis for digital transformation of the company.
Our mission is to support the technology leadership and global growth of Miba by creating and developing a framework allowing the company to leverage data from various sources across the enterprise and beyond to promote better decision making, business improvement, innovation and creation of new data-driven business models, products and services.

Responsibilities
  • Design, maintain and improve the data architecture and processes to support the requirements of business stakeholders, data scientists and IT
  • Design, build and maintain data integration, storage, processing and analytics solutions based on the data architecture
  • Design and build data solutions and processes that support new digital platforms and Industrial IoT solutions
  • Support business initiatives in discovering opportunities for data acquisition, leveraging and monetization, realization of data-driven business models and data-enabled products and services
  • Define and implement approaches to improve data reliability, efficiency and quality, reduce data integration, storage, processing and analytics costs


Requirements
  • Bachelor’s or Master Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Mathematics, Statistics or related field
  • At least 3 years of working experience with data ETL tools, storage, processing and analytics solutions (e.g. Microsoft, Oracle, Informatica, AWS…)
  • At least 3 years of working experience with integrating data from different systems and data sources (e.g. SAP, Cronetwork, Siemens, QMS…)
  • Experience with cloud platforms and technologies (Microsoft Azure, Amazon AWS)
  • Knowledge and practical experience with SAP HANA, MS SQL and Oracle DB, Matillion ETL and MS SSIS, AWS S3 and Redshift, Tableau and MS Power BI are preferable
  • Experience with advanced analytics, Machine Learning, Big Data, and IoT are preferable
  • Experience with agile methods such as Scrum is preferable
  • Good Communication skills, very good English language skills, German is preferable
  • Growth mindset, high self-motivation and passion, a visible culture of excellence
  • Ownership, self-direction and accountability in own work
  • Ability to work in self-organized and autonomous cross-functional teams
  • Desire for team cooperation, trustful and open communication
  • Adaptability, ability to handle ambiguity, excellent problem-solving skills, constant desire to learn
