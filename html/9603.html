Data Engineer Required 12 month contract Amsterdam !

A leading end customer is looking for a Data Engineer to work a 12 month contract in Amsterdam and Pernis !

The development team shift location to support the development at customer site - typical week arrangement may look like:

  • 2 days per week: Pernis;
  • 2 days per week: Amsterdam;
  • 1 day per week: Flexible (at home);
  • Travel cost will be covered by the project (depending on base location).
Functie-eisen

  • 3-5+ years Work experience in Python:
    • Knowledge about unit test frameworks;
    • Python coding standard;
    • CI/CD.
  • You are experienced in Machine Learning deployment:
    • Experience with productionizing the ML models would be a pre-requisite;
    • Experience with image classification would be a pre-requisite;
    • Knowledge about Keras.
  • You are familiar with Azure cloud environment:
    • Understand the differences of azure cloud services; understanding of the potential pitfall when choosing specific cloud service;
    • Have DevOps experience (optional);
    • Docker container.
  • Stakeholder management skills:
    • Can guide junior engineers both from technical aspect and project management aspect;
    • Communicate clearly and proactively.
  • Nice to have:
    • You are familiar with PySpark.
