Do you want to be part of a fast-growing team of data scientists working for our international clients from our Buenos Aires office?


We’re looking for data scientists willing to step up in the fields of machine learning and data engineering to provide our clients with top notch data solutions. You’ll work within the Wunderman global team and our clients based in North America. We offer you the opportunity to work on a wide range of data science problems, whether it be classification and regression techniques, covering up to NLP applications and computer vision. You’ll have the opportunity to build your expertise not only with traditional statistical models and algorithms, but also with more advanced deep learning techniques.


If you’re thrilled about the constant breakthroughs in the data science world, and love to share your knowledge about it, you’ll be excited to join our global data scientist team and contribute in the development of a strong data science culture within the company.


We’re willing to position ourselves as Google Cloud Platform experts when it comes to cloud products, and feel empowered with the fantastic open-source community to develop our own data science solutions. Technology stacks are extremely flexible, depending our clients preferences, so you’ll have the chance to learn a lot of different technologies.


WHAT YOU’LL DO

  • Design, develop and implement solutions using statistical techniques and machine learning to solve our clients needs

  • Manage & analyse large amounts of data & extract meaningful information for business stakeholders

  • Build & automate pipelines to acquire and transform data for quick model development


WHAT YOU’LL NEED

  • Advanced level in English (both written and communication)

  • Significant experience coding in Python (or other programming language, but willing to learn Python)

  • Knowledge of a variety of machine learning techniques (e.g. clustering, decision tree learning, neural networks)

  • Being familiar with common libraries (NumPy, pandas) and machine learning frameworks (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, Keras )

  • Relevant experience in applied mathematics, statistics, computer science, econometrics, or any other quantitative field

  • BONUS: Experience in distributed computing environments (Spark)

  • BONUS: Experience in machine learning productionalization


WHAT WE’LL OFFER

  • Great learning opportunities in the data science field and ability to work with industry-leading clients

  • Flexible working hours and possibility to work remotely

  • Competitive industry salary
