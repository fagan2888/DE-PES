pmOne Group Vienna, AT

Vor 1 Monat

Auf Firmenwebseite bewerben
At the earliest possible date, we are looking for motivated (Senior) Data Engineers (m/f).

Your Challenge

  • Integrate data from different kind of sources (Streaming, APIs, Sensor Data, Google Analytics, CRM, SQL DB, etc.)
  • Developing data models to store and analyze data in SQL, NoSQL or Hadoop/Data Lake platforms
  • Managing and curating the available data to assure its quality and availability
  • Support designing the data pipeline and the underlying data architecture
  • Participation in data preparation for Machine Learning and Data Science
  • Project Management
Your Profile

  • Strong interest in data engineering
  • At least 3 years of experience with SQL, Spark, Data Lakes, …
  • Experience with cloud platforms (Microsoft Azure, Amazon AWS, …)
  • Experience with agile project management methods such as Scrum
  • Ability to work in a team, management skills and very good communication skills
  • Excellent problem solving skills
  • Very good German and good English skills
  • Willingness to travel
Our offer

  • Great projects in the Advanced Analytics & Data Science space and exciting tasks
  • Working with a motivated and experienced team of experts
  • International team with a great spirit
  • Possibility to work with cutting edge technologies
  • Flexible working times and home office
  • Training opportunities and gaining knowledge
Did we spark your interest? We look forward to receiving your complete application documents including your earliest starting date and salary expectations by e-mail to hr@pmone.com or via our homepage.
