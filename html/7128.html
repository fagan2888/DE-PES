Data Engineer

Exact Match Recruitment Mississauga, CA

1 month ago

Apply on company website
Working as part of the Data Analytics team, you will support the existing big data Infrastructure on Google BigQuery and design and develop new processes and infrastructure to improve on the collection, consolidation and cleansing of data, ensuring it is readily available for analysis by the data scientists on your team.

Duties And Tasks Of This Position

Assist in the support and maintenance of existing big data infrastructure on Google BigQuery
Assist in development of new data assimilation and management platform
Develop systems to monitor health of the big data infrastructure (Java, C#)
Work with data scientists to understand data processing needs and develop infrastructure to support these initiatives
Create and maintain documentation
Support internal teams with data integration with newly developed big data platform

Experience/Skills Required

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or other related degree
Experience working with big data tools (preferably Google BigQuery)
Experience with Big Data Streaming (Apache Kafka)
Experience with message queueing systems (Pub/Sub, Kafka) at large scale
2+ years SQL experience
Strong programming skills ( Java or C#)or scripting experience (Python)
Experience with container orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes, or related.)
Experience designing and building data infrastructure solutions that are optimized for scale
Familiarity with machine learning concepts would be an asset
