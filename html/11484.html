Job Description

Summary/Objective

Shaw Systems Associates is looking for a passionate and highly motivated Enterprise Data Architect/Engineer, to act as a hands on Subject Matter Expert for all data projects, both internally and in support of our clients. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in Data Architecture, Data Movement, Data Management and Azure. The ideal candidate will provide leadership for the design, development and implementation of the overall data environment and provide solutions specific to their area of expertise. The Enterprise Data Architect/Engineer will specialize in Data Architecture, Data Warehouse design, ETL, Data Movement, Big Data, and Modeling, but will have hands on responsibilities. The Enterprise Data Architect/Engineer will ensure all architectures are compliant with enterprise architecture standards, provide leadership and guidance to the development teams and participate in the resolution of major project issues.

Responsibilities
  • Lead the data transformation, architecture and design for Shaw transactional applications for data, Data Wharehouse, Operational Data Stores and Data Movement.
  • Acts as the SME and develop data movement strategies using such technologies as ETL, Kafka and Spark.
  • Create and lead the Master Data Mangement and Test Data Mangement practices.
  • Acts as the SME around Microsoft Azure and Azure Data Factory.
  • Support existing teams in a technical, hands-on capacity, to deliver successful data projects.
  • Understanding of best practices to create documentation and recommendations for clients.
  • Present to small and large audiences though leadership of others and in lunch and learns to onsite and remote staff.
  • Develops data roadmaps for future Azure cloud implementations.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Requirements

  • A minimum of 5-7 years of experience as an Enterprise Data /Engineer.
  • A minimum of 5-7 years of experience in Data Modeling and Design.
  • A minimum of 5-7 years of experience in Data Wharehouses, Operational Data Stores and Data Movement using ETL, Spark, Kafka or other methods.
  • A minimum of 2 years of experience with SSIS, SSRS and PowerBI.
    • Test Data Management
  • Strong knowledge of how databases and ETL software function
    • Agile software development experience is a plus
  • Languages: T-SQL, C#
  • Experience with Big Data processing tools (Hadoop, Spark, Cassandra)
  • Service based architectures
    • Kafka; streaming; producer and consumer
    • Experience with Azure Data Factory a plus
    • Master Data Management
    • Data quality
    • Data integration
    • Data virtualization
    • Data migration and data movement
  • Experience with stream-processing systems such as Spark, Storm, etc

    • Education / Certifications
    • Four-Year College Degree (BA or BS) or Advanced Degree. A degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field is highly desirable.
    • Azure or AWS Cloud Certifications
    • One or more Certifications:
      • Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert (MCSE)
      • Microsoft Certified Solutions Developer (MCSD)
      • Microsoft Certified Azure Solutions Architect Expert
      • Microsoft Certified: Azure DevOps Engineer Expert

    Location:

    This position can be located in either Houston, TX, or Richmond, VA.

    Travel

    Limited travel (up to 25%) is expected for this position.
