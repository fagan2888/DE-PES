Marketing Data Engineer Advisor

Competitive salary
Bratislava, Slovakia

Dell provides the technology that transforms the way we all work and live. But we are more than a technology company — we are a people company. We inspire, challenge and respect every one of our over 100,000 employees. We also provide them with unparalleled growth and development opportunities. We can’t wait for you to discover this for yourself as a Data Engineer Advisor on our Marketing team in Bratislava .

Dell has been active in Slovakia since January 2003. We started as a centre of sales and customer support and transfer into a business centre supporting the whole EMEA region and the main financial hub of Dell, globally. We offer a broad palette of positions in IT, finance, sales, marketing, data science, technical support and business process support.

We are very honored and humbled to receive 3rd place in an annual 'best employer' survey conducted by Profesia amongst Shared Service Centers in Slovakia for the year 2018. Join our team and be part of life at Dell.

Reporting Product Specialist within a global Data & Platform Management team, supporting Integration needs with Marketing Automation Platforms, owning reporting development and performance evaluation of Campaign Selection Tools. Gather data & platform Integration requirements cross-region and cross-segment, analyze and define reporting and data ingestion needs, assist in developing appropriate solutions. Develop expertise on available configuration and integration levers. Implement required changes to a set of critical marketing tools, to meet customer data quality, transport or availability goals. Control and analyze integration processes, articulate recommendations to achieve better overall quality and improve reporting output.

Key Responsibilities
  • Reporting product expertise and support for a set of critical Customer Data Management tools and Marketing Automation Platforms
  • Analyze reporting and integration needs, identify gaps, formulate solutions, implement required configuration changes in back-end and front-end
  • Help creating and enforcing data and process governance around Customer Data platforms
  • Work with Marketing Product Owners to define and analyze problems as well as with program managers in meeting their data needs and understanding data requirements
  • In collaboration with others, interpret data and develop recommendations based on findings

Essential Requirements
  • Database Design and Data Analysis (SQL)
  • Product ownership or development experience with Marketing Automation platforms
  • Project / Program management – task oriented / self-organized
  • 3 to 5 years of proven experience in Data / Process Analytics, or Reporting Tools Management
  • Strong communication, written and verbal presentations in English

Desirable Requirements
  • Experience in global or remote Services/Business support type of environment
  • Marketing oriented acumen - SAS Marketing Automation experience, experience in CRM or Customer-related data management / analytics / process management / Marketing Automation Platforms

Salary starting from: 1900 EUR monthly gross

Benefits

We offer highly competitive salaries, bonus programs, world-class benefits, and unparalleled growth and development opportunities — all to create a compelling and rewarding work environment.

We are proud of the way we have embraced diversity at Dell and created an inclusive working environment. Read our Equal Employment Opportunity Policy.

Travel: 5-10%
