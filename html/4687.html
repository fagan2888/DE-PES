Senior System Administrator / DevOps (Cloud Platform)

Acronis Sofia, BG

2 weeks ago

Apply on company website
Acronis offers Professional Services for our Service Providers and Enterprise users to assist with the design, integration, implementation, and on-going operation of Acronis’ backup, disaster recovery, storage, file sync and share, monitoring, and other data protection solutions. We take pride in delivering high-quality services and deliverables, and we believe that our partners' success invariably depends on our ability to provide reliable and complete data protection and fast recovery services for virtual, physical, mobile and cloud environments.

We are looking for Senior System Administrator/DevOps Engineer in Cloud Platform to join our Sofia team, who have passion for scripting, coding, automation and doing everything as code. You need to have a proper understanding of the Software Development Lifecycle and possess good knowledge and expertise of various automation tools for developing digital pipelines (CI/CD pipelines) and improve the development cycle in general.

Responsibilities
  • Smooth running production systems and maintain system availability
  • Maintain infrastructure, configuration and monitoring in the way of "Infrastructure as Code"
  • Create, extend and improve delivery/development processed and systems
  • Participate in the architecture, design, deployment and maintenance phases of software
SKILLS & EXPERIENCE:
  • At least 5 years of experience as a Linux DevOps or Linux System Administrator
  • Excellent knowledge of Linux operating systems and Networking
  • Experience with virtualization and containers - OpenStack, VMware, KVM, Kubernetes, Swarm, Docker and etc
  • Experience with CI/CD implementation tools: Jenkins, Bamboo, GitLab and etc
  • Experience with configuration management and orchestration: Ansible, Chef, Puppet or Saltstack
  • Programming skills - Bash, Python, Perl or Ruby
  • DB administration: - Postgres, MySQL, Percona and etc.
  • NoSQL Databases: ElasticSearch, MongoDB or Cassandra
  • Monitoring: Zabbix, Nagios, Prometheus, Grafana and similar
  • Ability and desire to quickly ramp up on new concepts and technologies
