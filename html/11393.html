Data Engineer

33N Ltd London, England, United Kingdom

£40,000.00 - £60,000.00

4 days ago

33N was established in 2018 and is on a mission to improve patient care by combining clinical acumen and high quality data analysis. Empowering healthcare providers to make evidence-based decisions with their own data, gaining insights and understanding. This is achieved by a combination of clinical consultancy and training, alongside the power of data science and bespoke data tools and visualisations.

Over the last two years we have been successful in engaging and supporting organisations in the healthcare industry and are in the process of growing the team as the reach and impact of this work grows. As a Data Engineer at 33N, you will be joining a team of driven, entrepreneurial and intellectual analysts and NHS clinicians, that draw on a unique blend of skills and experiences to improve the healthcare sector. You will be involved in a variety of initiatives that solve complex problems in the healthcare sector such as the incorporation of concepts and ideas into vivid data visualisations, and augmentation of data pipelines.

Requirements

We welcome applications from anyone with a track-record of driving decisions with data analysis and a passion to improve the healthcare industry. We are looking for people with a creative and robust approach to solving problems.

So, who are we looking for? You will have a range of skills including:

• Analytical ability - bringing to bear brainpower to analyse complex scenarios and connecting dots in data and business problems

• Attention to detail - demanding the high standards of quality

• Technical creativity - applying intellectual dexterity to unusual problems

• Desire to learn - striving to learn and to teach others

• Communication - ability to communicate concrete technical ideas and abstract data concepts to a wide range of audiences

• Confidence - showing initiative with teams and clients

Essential

• Degree in STEM subject, Masters or PhD

• Demonstrable proficiency R or Python, and SQL

• Experience in writing clean, maintainable, tested, and well-documented code

• Experience configuring and developing BI tools and ETL processes

• Understanding of machine learning approaches

• Creativity, confidence, clear communication, etc

• Entrepreneurial mindset

Desirable

• Experience with data visualisation and data visualisation tools: Tableau, SiSense, Kibana and PowerBI

• Experience with AWS, Github and Jira

• Healthcare sector experience

Benefits

• Making a real different to the healthcare industry

• Quarterly training & conference budget

• Kindle lending library

• 25 days holiday, plus Bank Holidays

• Contributary pension scheme

• Frequent team socials and meals

• Working in a fast growing, exciting start-up
