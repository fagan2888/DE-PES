At PSL, we are looking for Data Engineers to work on the cleaning, storage, distributed processing, and analytics of huge amounts of data for the platforms of multiple North American clients. You’ll have the opportunity to solve challenges in streaming and real time analysis, batch processing of billions of records, the use of advanced NoSQL to support particular use cases and the integration of all of these elements with a cloud ecosystem.

At PSL, you’ll be able to:


  • Create multiple natural ETLs, in order to generate valuable information for the platforms on which we work.
  • Enhance and apply your knowledge to implement Fast Data in cloud platforms like AWS and GCP.
  • Interact with a series of modern tools that you may not know well, but have a desire to learn about, such as Notebooks in Jupyter or Zeppelin, AWS Glue, EMR, and others.
  • Work in an agile environment, implementing and following agile engineering practices in your projects.
  • Be an integral part of a community where you have the opportunity to constantly learn from very talented people, participate in courses and diverse talks to help you keep up with the latest in the world of technology and software development.
  • Positively impact the communities where we work. PSL offers a variety of social responsibility programs in which you can participate, including multiple teaching programs for the community and at universities and schools and programs aimed at promoting the role of women in technology.
  • Be part of a flat organization where all ideas are considered, no matter where they came from.


For this role, you should have:

  • Experience working with frameworks like Apache Spark, especially Processing, Batch or Stream modules.
  • Technical abilities in SQL and HDFS.
  • Clear understanding of distributed programming, concurrency, algorithms like map-reduce, and understand how to apply them in development.
  • Experience with AWS is ideal. You don’t need to understand all of the services, we will work with EMR, S3, ECS, Glue, among others, so if you have a solid foundation of cloud compuyting principles, it’s much better.
  • Foundational understanding of programming in Scala.
  • High level of conversational English. This role requires you to communicate with clients in North America.
  • Passion for coding.
  • An interest in constantly learning new technologies.
