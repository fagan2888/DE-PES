    The Rental Clients team is responsible for business applications that support the Airport Rental and Home City business to include web, tablet, and Kiosk solutions. For this reason, we welcome applicants that have previously worked in or are currently working in our Airport or Home City rental locations, who may be knowledgeable with our software applications.Holdings believes that our customers' experience should be first-class, so as a Test Engineer you will become our "Customer Advocates.” You will not only be responsible for ensuring high-quality software functionality, but also creating new ideas for better customer experience.As a Test Engineer, you will be responsible for assessing and evaluating the scope of our testing requirements. You will design, produce, execute, and maintain software test plans and test cases, contributing to the maintenance and enhancement of our products and test automation where needed. You will test applications used by the world's largest rental car company, while documenting, tracking, and reporting on defects. You will also analyze test results, leverage troubleshooting skills to assist with defining root cause, and conduct re-testing after defects are addressed.

    • Effectively manages time, with the ability to multi-task and work independently without close supervision
