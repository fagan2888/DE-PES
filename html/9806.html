COME JOIN OUR TEAM!


We’re a fast-growing SaaS B2B startup building a data integration cloud service that makes it exceedingly easy to integrate and process data in preparation for analytics. With Xplenty, our customers do not need to write code, install or maintain anything, it’s all taken care of by our platform. Serving the intersection of the hottest trends in technology today: Big Data & Cloud, we help customers unlock their data to run their business more efficiently and effectively.

If you like a fast paced, highly creative, International and dynamic environment to maximize your talents and expand your experience, then we might be the place for you.


OPPORTUNITY

We’re looking for a Software Engineer to join our awesome and fast growing Tokyo team. We're a high-growth company, headquartered in the US and have recently entered the Japanese market. This is an exciting opportunity to be a key part of our Japan team with huge growth opportunities.


RESPONSIBILITIES

As a Software Engineer, you will play a key technical leadership role, driving design, implementation and delivery. You will be able to communicate your ideas and provide thought leadership across the team. You will actively mentor others through code and design reviews, helping the team learn best practice.


We innovate using multiple languages, including Ruby, Python, Java, JavaScript and others.


REQUIREMENTS

  • 3+ years of professional experience in commercial or web-scale system software development and delivery
  • 3+ years of professional object-oriented programming and design experience
  • 2+ years of design and implementation experience of scalable computing systems, software architecture, data structures, and algorithms
  • 2+ years of professional exposure to big data, network, distributed, asynchronous, and concurrent programming techniques
  • Working knowledge of operating system fundamentals for Linux
  • Working knowledge of professional software development lifecycle best practices


PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

  • Passionate about technical leadership and mentoring
  • Passionate about perfecting the craft of software development
  • Strong verbal, written, and design communication skills
  • Experience in agile enterprise-scale software development
  • Experience with devops practices and operating production software
  • Experience with Amazon Web Services
  • Fluent English
