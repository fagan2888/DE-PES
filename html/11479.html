About the position

We are looking for a Community Hero to join our team and assist our players throughout their gaming experience, guiding them through features and functionalities and recommending solutions to possible issues.

If you’re an excellent communicator, familiar with helpdesk software and fluent in English and at least one of the following languages (Portuguese, Spanish, German, French, Italian, Dutch), we’d love to meet you!

Responsibilities

  • Respond to customer queries in a timely and accurate way.
  • Identify customer needs and help customers use specific features.
  • Analyze and report product malfunctions (for example, by testing different scenarios or impersonating users).
  • Keep track of technical issues and useful discussions with customers.
  • Monitor customer complaints on social media and reach out to provide assistance.
  • Share feature requests and effective workarounds with team members.
  • Inform customers about new features and functionalities.
  • Follow up with customers to ensure their technical issues are resolved.
  • Gather customer feedback and share with our Product team.

Requirements

  • Currently studying Business, Marketing, Information Technology or other relevant fields.
  • Excellent professional knowledge of English and at least one more of the following: Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Dutch. Knowledge of more languages is a plus.
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
  • Customer service orientation.
  • Multi-tasking abilities.
  • Excellent organizational and analytical skills.
  • Familiarity with our industry and social media is a plus.
  • Experience using help desk software and remote support tools.
  • Ideally, you have a love for mobile word and trivia games.

What we offer

  • Competitive remuneration.
  • Meal ticket.
  • Transpostation voucher.
  • Casual environment and dress code.
  • A space for resting moments, full of snacks, fruits and drinks.

About Fanatee

Fanatee is a mobile gaming startup founded in 2013, and since then we've been working so that people are able to play our games anytime, anywhere. We believe that quality is the key to success. Our creative processes are well planned and carefully studied, and our talent lies in making ideas turn to businesses: not for nothing, our apps Stop and CodyCross together have been downloaded over 50 million times around the world.

Our dream allows us to do what we love the most: be surrounded by talented, passionate people and we give them the chance to achieve goals as big as they wish.

  • If you want to share this experience with us, be ready to work in a light, fun and focused environment that breathes technology, innovation and game culture!
