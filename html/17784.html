MindGeek is the largest, privately held company in our industry developing unparalleled technologies for high traffic, high volume online properties. Our exceptional team consists of experts in every functional area of business and they are the key to our position as leaders in content delivery. We strive to innovate on a daily basis to ensure we continue to grow and create new revenue opportunities and cutting edge technology. If you are passionate about taking your career to the next level, then join the MindGeek team and be a part of something big.

The Data Analyst in the Business Analytics Department acts as an expert in leveraging data to identify, create, and communicate strategy with products, analysts, clients, and upper management.

Your Day-to-day Will Be
  • Client Management : Communicating and understanding client requests to help develop custom data solutions
  • Data Management : Exploring databases to leverage relevant information to optimize processes and build strategy
  • Project Management : Working with your teammates to prioritize and deliver important milestones and deliverables to clients
  • Analysis : Use data and build models to help solve problems using analytical and/or numerical methodologies
  • Reporting : Create custom reports for clients to help aid in their day-to-day and give them the power to make informed and data-driven decisions
  • Creating : Participate in weekly brainstorming meetings to identify pain points and build strategy to improve all facets of the company
  • Communication : Develop and maintain the ability to communicate complex data issues to various teams including product managers, engineers, and upper management


You Have
  • Bachelors : In Statistics, Finance, Computer Science, or other analytical field
  • Experience : At least 1 year in similar analytical role
  • Tools : Microsoft Excel, SQL
  • Skills : Data modeling, client management, analytics, business savviness
  • Languages : English (required), French (a plus)
  • Additional Tools (a plus) : R/Python, Power BI/Tableau
  • Additional Experience (a plus) : E-commerce


You Are
  • Analytical minded
  • Able to summarize complex solutions to any type of client
  • Comfortable manipulating large amounts of data
  • Organized and unable to miss a deadline
  • Curious and willing to constantly expand your knowledge
  • Willing to take an active role in a fast-growing team
  • Passionate about what you do and constantly motivated
  • Positive and excited to face new challenges


You’ll Receive
  • A competitive compensation
  • Flexible business hours
  • Summer schedule
  • Access to corporate Hackathons, Lunch & Learns, Leadership Seminars, Online Learning Resources
  • Daily free breakfast
  • Monthly lunches
  • Fitness allowance of up to 500$
  • Epic parties throughout the year
  • Opportunity to give back to the community through many programs (Pay It Forward, Ronal Macdonald House, Dans La Rue)
  • And more!

We thank all applicants for their interest, however only candidates under consideration will be contacted. No telephone calls please. No agency calls or emails.
