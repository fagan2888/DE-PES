Introduction
System Administrators are the gatekeepers to the many systems that run our company and our clients. As a System Admin with IBM, you will have the opportunity to provide high-value IT services and leverage our leading-edge technology portfolio in our global network. Your work has a direct impact on the day-to-day productivity of our business by ensuring integrity of and access to our most important resource: data.

Your Role and Responsibilities

Become a IBMer and continue your professional growth as a "WME Specialist Senior".

Discover what you can do at IBM, a company where you can be part of complex projects and you can improve all your technical skills in important challenges.

Required Technical and Professional Expertise

Blues Experience

Systems Engineer or related with experience in:
  • 2 years of experience in administration and troubleshooting IBM WebSphere Application Server.
  • 2 years of experience in administración y troubleshooting IBM WebSphere MQ.
  • 1 year of experience in administración y troubleshooting IBM WebSphere Message Broker / IBM Integration Bus.
JAZZ EXPERIENCE:
  • 3 years of experience in administration and troubleshooting IBM WebSphere Application Server.
  • 3 years of experience in administration and troubleshooting IBM WebSphere MQ.
  • 2 years of experience in administration and troubleshooting IBM WebSphere Message Broker / IBM Integration Bus.
  • 2 years of experience in administration and troubleshooting IBM WebSphere Portal.
  • 1 year of experience in administration and troubleshooting IBM WebSphere Process Server.
