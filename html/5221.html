ArcelorMittal is the world's leading steel and mining company, with a presence in 60 countries over the world. Guided by a philosophy to produce safe, sustainable steel, we are the world’s leading supplier of quality steel.

ArcelorMittal Construction is the leading global provider of steel building systems & solutions and sandwich panels for roofing, cladding, composite floors, light gauge structures, and modular housing. Supported by our technical expertise, we propose construction systems that respond to thermal performance and energy saving, acoustic, airtightness and fire requirements. Being established in 14 countries around Europe, our goal is to become the leading supplier in all the markets in which we operate, by creating innovative new steel solutions, bundling existing products with new ones.

We do this by listening to the needs of our direct stakeholders (architects, engineers, installers, final users, insurance companies). We can develop a competitive edge by working closely with these professionals and ensuring they directly influence the definition and design of our solutions.

In this overall context, we are currently looking for a Data Warehouse Manager / Business Intelligence Engineer who will participate to our data virtualization journey and support our Data-Driven strategy, by delivering expertise, in-depth technical knowledge, and world-class support to the business.

As Data Warehouse Manager / Business Intelligence Engineer inside the Data management & BI team composed with a Senior BI Manager who developed our Data-Warehouse solution – Infocentre – and a BI Specialist, you will participate to the maintenance and future implementations of our existing solution within an international context.

At the same time, you will understand and learn about the complexity of data integration implemented solutions and will become a technical reference to both Management and business users.


Based on complex business needs identification, you will be in charge of the analysis, conception, technical developments, and trainings of the users in order to provide a reliable solution for business decision.

Therefore, your expertise will be needed to:

  • Develop, implement and maintain sustainable, high-performance data-processing and data integration pipelines. This includes:
  • An operational knowgdege and expertise of administrating and modeling Microsoft SQL Server Database
  • Manage ETL tools in the Datawarehouse from various sources
  • Maintain SQL procedures and more globally Microsoft SQL server
  • Perform multidimensional modeling (Flocon / Etoile) (OLAP cubes and MDX language)
  • Consulting with team to build and continuously improve a variety of data products that create value for our business, finding ways to get optimal results from query performance
  • Maintain and fix bug problems, as well as providing user support
  • Be a sparring partner, and provide support and guidance to other local users to help develop their technical capabilities
  • Act as main point of contact with Management and sales, production, supply chain, finance teams

Education requirement


Master 2 Business Intelligence (Desired but not required if compensated by justified professional experience).


Experience & knowledge requirements


  • Strong experience and knowledge of concepts related to Architecture, design, implementation, and maintenance of data warehouses, Data Quality and BI
  • Strong knowledge of MS Database management and Tools such as SQL Server, T-SQL,SSIS,SSAS
  • VBS et C# Language.
  • Extensive experience in Data Warehousing using ETL tools (Microsoft SSIS)
  • Experience working with Reporting Tools such as SSRS or MS Power BI are a plus.
  • The ability to work with Analysts to design technically robust systems that meet the needs of business stakeholders


Personal skills requirement


  • Naturally curious and resourceful, you are not afraid to learn new technologies and new knowledge
  • Initiative way of thinking, strength of proposal, ability to understand the job, rigor and autonomy with a developed sense of service and contact
Your sense of service combined with your listening skills and a strong analytical mindset will be essential assets for success in this position
