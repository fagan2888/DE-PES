Our client a leading international enterprise in the retail industry, currently looking for Senior Infrastructure Specialist / System Engineer to join their IT team in Hong Kong.


We are now seeking a Senior Infrastructure Specialist / System Engineer


RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Provide technical support for server virtualization platform and all systems and infrastructure services ride on Windows and Linux servers
  • Conduct technical design & prepare hardware and software procurement specifications
  • Perform setup, installation, and configuration of development, UAT and production systems
  • Prepare and conduct system tests including load test, resilience test, backup and recovery test, disaster recovery drill, etc
  • Prepare and perform system rollout & system documentation
  • Perform system monitoring and performance tuning
  • Any other duties assigned by the supervisor

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Degree Holder in Information Technology or related disciplines
  • At least 3 years’ experience in IT application support and maintenance in Window & Linux
  • Hand-on experience in virtualization technologies: VMware/ Hyper-V
  • Advanced troubleshooting and multi-tasking skills
  • Familiarity with AWS/ MS O365/ MS Azure AD Administration
  • Good communication (English, Cantonese, and Mandarin) and interpersonal skills
  • Immediate available is highly preferable
  • Fresh graduate will also be considered
