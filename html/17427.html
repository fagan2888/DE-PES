Want to join the team as  experienced Electronic Engineer Hardware Orientation ?


Function:

A passionate hardware electronics designer who really enjoys developing hardware electronics,

  • Ability to be creative while developing for military applications in a structured

environment.

  • Willing to handle the complete development phase, starting from subsystem through design, until validation with possibilities to perform real field abroad.
  • Ability to Design For : Excellence, Cost, Manufacturability, Assembly, Quality and thereby having knowledge about design, manufacturing and assembly of circuits boards and willing to extend this knowledge.
  • Although the focus is set to hardware design, knowing to read, modify and write low embedded C software or some small C++ test applications on PC, is an asset
  • Interested in digital and analog design, microcontrollers, FPGA, power electronics communication interfaces and interconnections.
  • Interested in mastering EMC, power or thermal design considerations



Education:

  • Master or Bachelor with a strong passion for electronics design.
  • Real hands on experience in electronics design
  • PCB design with Circuit Studio or Altium is an asset.
  • C/C++ is an asset
  • French/English


What we offer :

An attractive remuneration package (permanent contract with advantages), we offer a human-centered and challenging environment to work in.
