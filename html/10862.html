DMO - Data Engineer - SWE II Bangalore

Who we are

The Data Management Organization is tasked with ownership and management of the shared services data environments. This function oversees the shared data services environment and ensures company-wide adherence to the data lifecycle, privacy, security, and ultimately the data strategy.

Who You Are

You want to be part of a community of the most talented, forward-thinking Data Scientists and Engineers in the industry. You are a strong Scala or Java developer. You demonstrate clear and concise communication and data-driven decision-making.

Experience And Skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
  • 5+ years experience building large scale, highly available, distributed computing systems and web systems
  • 3+ years of experience in Python, Hadoop, Mapreduce, SQL, and scala
  • Hands-on experience with ElasticSearch
  • Fundamental knowledge of data structures, data science, Machine learning algorithms
  • Ability to expertly design and produce high quality, high-performance code ready to ship
  • Understanding of software design principles and best practices

Bonus Points / Good to have
  • Data Governance experience
  • Understanding of Hadoop internals and metadata parsing is a huge plus
  • Familiar with the following tools: Git, Docker, Jenkins
  • Prior experience working for a consumer Internet company
  • Experience working with teams distributed across geographies and functions
