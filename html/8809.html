About The Role

The Data Engineer role at Billie will be responsible for building a reliable "single source of truth" DWH, which allows various departments including BI, Risk as well as Management to achieve conclusive information that enables them to work seamlessly and at their best. This will be a very hands-on role where you can make an impact and have the opportunity to work within functional teams on a daily basis.

You will be working on the following Technology stack: Google Cloud Platform, AWS, Snowflake, Periscope, Talend Open Studio.

The Role In More Detail
  • Develop and maintain data pipelines from heterogeneous sources to DWH;
  • Design and build multidimensional DWH;
  • Guarantee data integrity;
  • Define and document data sources, models and dictionaries;
  • Perform Ad hoc data analysis;
  • Build and maintain data archiving mechanisms.


Who We Are Looking For
  • B.Sc/M.Sc. in a quantitative field - with good understanding of Computer Science foundation (e.g. Data Structures, Complexity, Algorithms, etc.)
  • SQL Wizard
  • Solid ETL experience
  • Familiarity with Salesforce is a plus
  • Self-driven programmer, ideally enthusiastic about Python
  • Excellent English; German a plus!

  • A great office near Checkpoint Charlie with a free gym, healthy snacks and cereals, fruits, coffee and drinks.
