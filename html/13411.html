We are currently looking for a “Senior Microsoft Engineer” to support the business in achieving its objectives

Job description

The Microsoft Engineer will be part of the team responsible to deliver Microsoft projects to Dimension Data clients. He needs to have a good overview of the Microsoft architecture with a focus on Skype For Business, Teams both with enterprise voice and Exchange on prem and cloud based. Most of the projects involve a combination of some or all the following Microsoft technologies:

  • Active Directory
  • Azure AD: ADFS, AAD Connect, PTA, PHS, MFA, Graph API…
  • Skype for Business (with enterprise voice)
  • Exchange
  • Azure (virtual machines, networking…)
  • Office 365 (Exchange online, Skype for Business online/Teams, …)
  • Intune

This position will include the design of new infrastructures and defining migration paths, supporting the operational teams (f. e. Dimension Data Cloud teams) during implementation, migration and assisting the support team during troubleshooting and problem solving.

  • Understand customer requirements from a technical and business perspectives
  • Work with our clients to define projects designs and provide recommendations
  • Write implementation steps which will be used by the operation team
  • Support the operation team during migrations and configurations
  • Participate in solving technical issues in collaboration with the support team
  • Maintain and update customer documentation
  • Must have understanding of the ITIL principals

Here's what we are looking for in candidates

Technical

·     Degree in Engineering or equivalent training with professional experience

·     Strong analytical thinking and knowledge of procedures

·     Knowledge of routing and switching principles (minimum CCNA level)

·     SIP protocol knowledge

·     SBC routing architecture (Audiocodes, Sonus)

·     Good knowledge of Powershell scripting

·     Good knowledge of integrations with HTTPS service, SSL, SAML, REST API

Soft skills

·     Highly proactive and committed

·     Good verbal communications skills in French, Dutch and English

·     Responsive to the demand of working in a high pressure environment

·     Flexibility and willingness to work out of office hours

·     Motivated to work in a large infrastructure and the opportunity to develop different design perspectives

·     NATO clearance is a plus
