Job Description

A large general insurance company based in Centurion is looking for an experienced Data Scientist who has experience working with SAS and/or SQL

Requirements
  • Undergraduate or postgraduate degree in Data Mining, Data Analytics, Computer Science, Information Engineering, Statistics, Actuarial Science, Informatics or equivalent
  • 3-5 years’ relevant experience in Data Analysis
  • Must have SAS and/or SQL experience
If you would like to apply for this position, please send transcripts with applications.

Should you meet the requirements for this position, please email your updated CV attached to finance.jobs@tumaini.co.za, alternatively contact Monja on 011 462 3018 or visit our website, www.tumaini.co.za. Correspondence will only be conducted with short listed candidates. Should you not hear from us within 3 days, please consider your application unsuccessful.
