Senior Robotics Engineer

Delta Electronics Singapore, Singapore

2 days ago 37 applicants

Apply

Area:

Established in 2013, Delta Research Center (DRC) is devoted to Industry 4.0 – Cyber Physical Systems which enables the acceleration of enterprise transformation andupgrade. Meanwhile, DRC takes an open collaboration approach to actively collaborate with partners from industrial sectors, government agencies, academia, and research institutions for the mutual success of ecosystem.


Job Role:

You will be at the core team of Delta Research Centre leading disruptive technological advancement with strong business value that empower company’s new business in providing high quality products, services and solutions for Smart Manufacturing application. You will embrace and innovate for future research focus based on evidence and involved in open and collaborate teams. You will manage and execute research programs and projects that develop and deliver impactful research and business outcomes.


Job Responsibilities:

Perform and accomplish the research and development function to develop prototype to production

  • Apply latest technologies in new automation prototype and product development
  • Able to apply experience/knowledge in automation and control product development lifecycle
  • Responsible for specifying, developing, testing, analyzing and improving the important mechatronics system.
  • Adopting current industry standards / methodology to work (industrial standards, open source, common platform)
  • Contribute hands-on support for system integration, testing and troubleshooting including software and hardware


Job Qualifications:

  • Bachelor, Master degree in electrical and electronics engineering, mechanical engineering, mechatronics engineering, computer science or related field
  • Min 3 year experience in system integration or R&D project development
  • Familiar with Robotics Operating System Development or equivalent robotics development
  • Knowledge in embedded system, linux programming
  • Experience in automation system design and strong hands-on building capability with M&E knowledge will be a plus.
  • Able to travel for short periods (3 days – 2weeks)
  • Those with relevant experience will be considered for senior position.
