METRO Cash & Carry Romania is looking for an enthusiastic, customer oriented professional to join our team as a Data Scientist – CRM


Join us and you will:

  • Monitor  portfolio performance and customer segments based on specific KPI
  • Implement statistical models following customer lifecycle management
  • Support the development of  CRM infrastructure
  • Develop products based on our internal tool for campaign management
  • Manipulate and analyze large amount of data to discover trends/pattern and draw meaningful insights  – data analytics
  • Design, execute and analyze algorithmic experiments (machine learning) on various touchpoints of user behavior by consulting with business stakeholders to gain a successful personalization design
  • Find new opportunities to create and automate analysis on customer lifecycle
  • Develop escalation systems
  • Maintain a close communication with the IT supplier /other internal departments for any issues that may arise

To be successful in this role, you'll need:

  • Previous experience in a similar role
  • A graduate degree in Cybernetics, Statistics, Engineering, Science or any related field
  • JavaScript knowledge – junior level
  • SQL knowledge for the development of  basic  level requests
  • Knowledge of  terms like: tables, indexes, keys-values
  • Ability to present the results of the analysis/modelling
  • Expertise in data mining
  • Proficiency in English
  • Willingness to learn and apply technologies
  • Analytical skills
  • Project management skills and the ability to work with tight deadlines
  • To be team player, proactive
  • Creativity and inovation drive


What do you get in return?

  • You'll be supported by an enthusiastic and driven team
  • You will find a friendly environment & great opportunities
  • Friday is a half work day (ends at around 14:30)
