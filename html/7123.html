    Senior Associate, Talent Acquisition, Technology (EMEA) at IHS Markit

    • Valuations Services SRE is a global team that provides specialized technical builds/solutions across the suite of Valuations products. DevOps members work closely with the Development, Testing, Client Services teams to build and develop applications using the latest technologies to ensure the highest availability and resilience of all services.


    Our work helps ensure that Valuations continues to provide high quality service and maintain client satisfaction. Integration is key and we ensure we work with all parties to ensure Scalable and Cloud-native application suites.


    The team prides itself on its flexibility and technical diversity to maintain service availability and contribute improvements through design and development.

    IHS Markit is seeking a highly motivated person to join our DevOps team in Bucharest/London.

    DevOps is a rapidly growing team at the heart of ensuring the availability and correct operation of our valuations, market and trade data applications.


    Duties & accountabilities:


    The role of the DevOps Engineer is primarily focused on building functional systems that improve our customer experience. Responsibilities include:

    • Create infrastructure and environments to support our platforms and applications using On-Prem Infrastructure as various AWS Cloud services
    • Implement DevOps technologies and processes, e.g: containerisation, CI/CD, infrastructure as code, metrics, monitoring etc
    • Automate wherever sensible
    • Upgrade and patch our systems whilst maintaining service for end users
    • Take on challenges like migrating code repositories from different platforms to Git using Gitlab
    • Support, monitor, maintain and improve our infrastructure and the live running of our applications
    • Be part of the on-call and out-of-hours rota when necessary


    Your expertise

    • Deployment, maintenance and support of enterprise applications into AWS including (but not limited to) Route53, ELB, VPC, EC2, S3, ECS, SQS
    • Good understanding of CloudFormation/Terraform (or similar ‘Infrastructure as Code’ technologies)
    • Good understanding of modern CI/CD methods and approaches
    • Experience of enterprise systems deployed as micro-services through code pipelines utilizing containerisation (Docker)
    • Working knowledge, understanding and ability to write scripts using languages including Bash, Python and an ability to understand Java, JavaScript and PHP


    It’s a bonus if you also have :

    • Strong background in Linux/Unix Administration in IaaS / PaaS / SaaS models
    • Solid understanding of cloud security models and best practices
    • Strong experience with SQL and NoSQL databases such MySQL, PostgreSQL, DB/2, MongoDB, DynamoDB
    • Experience with automation/configuration management using toolsets such as Chef, Puppet or equivalent


    You are:

    • Demonstrated ability to work independently with minimal supervision

    • Ability to prioritise and multi-task balancing technical, business and other drivers

    • Takes ownership of issues and drives through the resolution.

    • Flexible and willing to adapt to changing situations in a fast moving environment

    • Able to build relationships with all teams, identifying and focusing on their needs

    • An effective team player and strong collaborator across technology and all relevant areas of the business. Enthusiastic with a drive to succeed.
