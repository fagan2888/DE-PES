    THE OPPORTUNITY

    You should be capable of understanding business requirement and extracting meaningful insights from varied, large & complex data sets.

    You should be flexible and enthusiastic in picking up new languages and technologies, as we continuously improve our tech stack to remain global leaders Analytics and Data Science.

    You should be well aligned with the team vision and work closely with your seniors to meet every milestone towards the company's goal

    KEY ACTIVITIES

    • You will perform analysis using transactional and loyalty card data to solve analytic problems related to our client’s business
    • Distil key insights from customer data that address the objectives of the analysis and prepare client-ready presentations that deliver these insights effectively
    • Provide actionable recommendations to improve and or/address the business question asked
    • Be able to build and use segmentations, summative analyses, and predictive modelling to deliver solutions to client challenges
    • Establish a close working relationship with the client team, production team and development team, collect essential information and ensure that methodologies and processes are shared and understood.
    • Strong organizational skills and workflow planning are key
    • Oversees the activities of the data analyst and associate data analyst personnel’s and ensures the efficient execution of their duties
    • Process improvement through automation or integrating best practices
    • Ensure quality driven execution of projects/ solutions
    • Create detailed process document and QA checklist for all projects with sign-off
    • Own the responsibility of processes/workstream between the onshore and offshore teams
    • Ability to work under strict deadlines with go getter attitude
    • High degree of self-motivation
    • Flexibility - within working hours, location, and also complexity of work

    RELEVANT EXPERIENCE & SKILLS

    • Data manipulation: used to analysing large volumes of data and a solid understanding of database principles and efficiencies required
    • Technical: Experience of using a statistical package (such as R or Python) essential, SQL must.
    • Project Planning: capable of planning and managing your own time to ensure your components of projects are delivered to deadlines.
    • Basic statistics: familiarity with central tendency, variations, correlation and regression is expected as a minimum
    • Communication Skills: Ability to explain analytical work in a logical way which can be understood by a non-analytical audience.
    • Written communication: creating of clear, concise and actionable materials for the client.
    • MS Office: proficient with Word, Excel, PowerPoint.
    • Relevant Experience: 2 – 4 years

    General Qualification & Skills

    • BS in Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, Information Management or Statistics
    • Self-starter, able to work independently with limited day-to-day supervision.
    • Familiarity with retail, and particularly supermarket retail data would be beneficial
    • Very strong analytical skills will demonstrate ability to generate actionable insight out of data.
    • Innovative and able to develop new analytical solutions to business problems.
    • Experience of communicating complex topics clearly and effectively to a non-analytical audience
    • Minor managerial role - where he/she oversees the activities of the data analyst and associate data analyst personnel’s and ensures the efficient execution of their dutie
