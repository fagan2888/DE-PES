Senior Site Reliability Engineer

ZALORA Group Singapore, Singapore

4 days ago

Apply on company website

ZALORA Engineering is looking for a DevOps Engineer/Site Reliability Engineer to work on its cutting edge infrastructure.

This opportunity will offer you a chance to work on a high traffic site serving millions of customers per month throughout Southeast Asia. You will be exposed to a polyglot architecture, modern development workflows and a forward-thinking, intelligent team dedicated to solving the big-picture problems in e-commerce.

We have built most of our systems on open source technology. Our stack features PHP, Go, Nix, Java, Javascript, Kotlin, Docker, Kafka, Solr, MySQL and more all in production. We believe in choosing the best tool for the job, and part of your responsibility will be to identify the tools to help us grow into the next five years at ZALORA.

You will be a part of a lean team, working alongside with our software engineers and product managers to build a reliable and highly scalable infrastructure.


Responsibilities


  • Participate in a team of smart and dedicated engineers
  • Design and implement systems for high-availability
  • Streamline development and deployment processes via automation
  • PagerDuty rotation a.k.a. owning what we build
  • Work alongside product managers and software engineers to build a robust infrastructure.


Requirements


  • BS/MS in Computer Science or related technical discipline.
  • 3+ years in a development, devops, or site reliability engineer role.
  • Working knowledge of both scripting languages (Bash, etc..) and high-level programming languages (such as PHP, Python), and very proficient in AWS/Cloud technologies (EC2, RDS, Terraform/CloudFormation, Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Experience working with Unix/Linux.
  • Understanding of web services, web application development, SQL.
  • Knowledge and understanding of network theory and concepts such as TCP/IP, UDP, DNS, and load balancing.
  • Experience conducting systematic troubleshooting of issues.
  • Knowledge of the whole web stack, understand how all the pieces fit together (front-end, web server, load balancer, database, network layer, etc.).

Should you need any further information, please contact us at tech-hiring@zalora.com

ZALORA Engineering


ZALORA is the leading e-commerce company providing fashion throughout South East Asia. ZALORA Engineering has teams in Singapore and Vietnam to serve millions of users in Malaysia, Singapore, Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, Indonesia, Philippines and Brunei. This regional diversity presents a lot of interesting challenges that you and your colleagues will face. You will learn a lot by tackling these challenges.


The ZALORA Story


ZALORA exists for the millions of fashion consumers in Asia seeking a shopping experience focused on their unique styles, trends and fit. As Asia’s leading online fashion destination, ZALORA was founded in 2012 and has a presence in Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia & Brunei, the Philippines, Hong Kong and Taiwan. ZALORA’s localised sites offer an extensive collection of top international and local brands as well as our own in-house labels across apparel, shoes and accessories for men and women. ZALORA is part of Global Fashion Group, the world's leader in online fashion for emerging markets.
