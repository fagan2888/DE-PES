    • Interested in making the world more resourceful, in a newly established R&D centre and in cloud based IoT energy management solutions?

    Itron is committed to make the world more resourceful by providing solutions that help manage energy , gas and water more efficiently while fostering the development of smart cities . In Hungary we are present with a manufacturing plant in Gödöllő and an R&D centre in Budapest at Deák Palota that was established in 2017 June and was awarded with the prestigious investor of the year in the R&D investment category.
    We are developing the next generation products of Itron portfolio, such as Temetra , Openway Riva, a massive scale IoT platform and a Street light vision management solution , whilst providing complex managed services oriented around business outcomes, respectively 2 nd and 3 rd level technical support across multiple Itron products to customers in every geographical area around the globe. Join us now as a

    Software Engineer

    We are setting up a new software engineering team in Budapest. In this team you will be an integral part of a globally distributed agile team with counterparts mainly in the USA. You would support a meter data collection platform for electrical & gas utilities and smart cities, using next-generation, cloud based scalable technologies.

    Now we are looking for a Software Engineer to extend our technology capabilities and leadership in enabling AMI and Internet of Things technologies. In this role, you will use your technical skills in the design and construction of our next-generation, scalable Distributed Intelligence and Advanced Metering Platform . Responsibilities will comprise of design, APIs, services, security, and operability of Itron’s metering and network technology, managing millions of nodes in Itron’s customer deployments.

    Responsibilities
    • Design for development/support of Itron software products
    • Contribute hands-on to new feature development and bug fixing
    • Review code and mentor junior engineers
    • Utilize Agile-based methodology (planning sessions, creating and reviewing user stories, estimating task effort, retrospectives, etc)
    • Participate in product maintenance

    Skills Required
    • Expertise in Java development and design, working on scalable, multi-tier products
    • Strong computer science fundamentals
    • Energetic, inspired, dedicated, passionate about software development.
    • A self-starter who has the ability and willingness to learn new material quickly and perform in a fast-paced environment
    • A great team player, sociable, with good communication skills
    • 3+ years of experience in the design and implementation of distributed highly available multi-tier systems in structured object-oriented programming languages
    • Solid understanding of Java programming language and standard Java libraries
    • Comfortable in a Unix environment , with an understanding of networking fundamentals
    • Experience with web service protocols such as SOAP and REST
    • Working knowledge of SQL and databases
    • Knowledge of version control and build systems, such as Maven and Git

    Skills Desired
    • Experience with product scalability/performance analysis and tuning
    • Any exposure to Python and/or Unix shell scripts
    • Experience with message broker technologies such as TibcoEMS or Kafka
    • Any exposure to JavaScript, CSS, and HTML
    • Knowledge of java development frameworks such as Spring, Ignite, and Ehcache
    • Comfortable with automated testing (unit/service-layer/UI)
    • Experience delivering products in a Continuous delivery / Continuous deployment pipeline
    • Knowledge of cloud services, microservice architecture and containers
    We offer you
    • Purpose : opportunity to create a more resourceful world by working on 21 st century's biggest challenges: water, energy and gas
    • Career : a newly established R&D center with immense growth in multinational environment
    • Personal development : English, soft skill and technical trainings
    • Community : Pizza parties, Yoga, Chill room, Xbox, foosball and other events
