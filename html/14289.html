ABOUT THE ROLE:
AI & Data Applications Center of Excellence is a new, rapidly growing department, rising to tackle this data challenge. We’re an all-in data team, spanning from Data Engineering to Data Science. Our key purpose is to manage the petabytes of data we have and turn them into value.

Currently, we’re looking for Big Data DevOps Engineers who will develop and operate our BigData solutions. Apply and be part of one of the most impactful teams in DHL.

YOUR WORK:
• Develop solution to integrate various data sources across DHL business
• Elaborate on the end-to-end quality of the implemented applications
• Actively participate on the practical operation of the applications
• Provide mentorship and feedback to more junior team members
• Seek for opportunities to simplify processes and routines
• Work in agile mode
• Be part of a highly international team around the globe

YOU SHOULD HAVE:
• 3+ years of experience of development in Java, Python, or both
• 2+ years of experience in Hadoop technologies: Hive, Spark, Kafka, HBase, HDFS
• Proficient in SQL
• Version control (Git), UML

NICE TO HAVE:
• Master degree in Computer Science or related field
• Experience with programming in Scala
• Experience with visualization tools: Tableau, PowerBI, Qlik
• Orientation in Machine Learning principles
