Duties Include The Following, But Not Limited To

Level 1-3: Platform & Administration

The Application Engineer is responsible for the administration of all company servers and services and platform installs. This includes:
  • Installing applications and operating systems.
  • Maintaining and updating applications and operating systems.
  • Monitoring all services and servers.
  • Maintaining and monitoring the current Company network.
  • Installing and third level support of Company Applications and services related to company solutions.
  • Provide technical support for team members.
  • Construct and maintain all required consulting documentation.
  • Provide a clear consulting communication channel.
  • Ensure all knowledge gained is documented and added to the company knowledge base.
  • Assist the Head of Customer Service with all client related issues, including onsite support.
  • Management of appropriate processes maintaining and supporting the above mentioned.
  • Consulting on the installation of the application, upgrades and patches.
  • Raising all new software bugs with Devco. This includes gathering all required information and applying the fixes to the appropriate platform.
  • Analysis of all system related issues and appropriately communicate the results.
  • Installation, Upgrading, Maintenance and Deployment of core Company Platforms, Operating Systems and Third party Applications/Software;
  • Provide Technical Consultancy on the company Application Platform and other Operating Systems (Unix/Linux/Microsoft) and Virtualization & Cloud Services to clients;
  • Perform System Troubleshooting and escalation of critical outages/downtime to direct line Manager/Team Leader;
  • Monitoring Systems and Infrastructure uptime and reporting any incidences during On-call duties;

  • Creating and maintaining documentation related to the Support processes, training, user manuals and wiki articles;
  • Identify, escalate and report on issues/bugs;
  • Notify clients (internal & external) on incidents detected;
  • Timely update clients (internal & external) on status of issues;
  • Generate and distribute incident reports;
  • Create trend reports;
  • Assist on deployed client issues (in accordance with both SLA and urgency);
  • Timely raise any software and hardware issues as reported by the monitoring tool.
  • Be on standby on nights and weekends as stipulated by the Infrastructure Manager. (7 contineous days including the weekend). The standby times are subject to change based on the business or departments requirements.
  • Log all problems and changes into the Internal Query Tracking system and responding to emails via the system.
  • Resolve problems and escalating to the correct technical resources if he /she cannot resolve the problem. Problems need to be resolved according to the Service Level Agreement held with each client.
  • Monitor system up time and morning infrastructure checks during his on-call shifts.
  • Notify clients in the correct manner & time of any incident detected that impact on the clients business.
  • Provide info, distribute and present incident reports to a pre-defined list of clients during his on-call shifts.
  • Create and maintain documentation related to the support process, training and user manuals.
  • Generate trend reports on delivery and infrastructure
  • Provide accurate feedback to management and clients. This can be done verbally or making use of a weekly status report that includes the following topics:
    • Incidents
    • Downtime
    • Trends
    • Types of queries
    • Training
    • Monthly report
    • Other


General Facility Functions Such As (but Not Limited To)
  • Data center administration (server room check, humidity, aircon check, clean, fire suppression system is functional)
  • Ensure that the generator is fully functional (diesel, water and oil check)
  • Camera, alarm and front door access systems check
  • Attend to the Support telephone and resolve any queries relating to deployed solutions, company Reader and Abuse queues
  • Participate in the Global Platform & Development Knowledge share

Linux/Unix/Windows System Management
  • Develop and maintain a multitude of Unix/Linux environments that encompasses several open-source applications i.e. Apache, MySQL deployed in off-site Data Centres and some with no physical access.
  • Installing, testing, monitoring, reporting, updating, hardening and auditing.
  • Ensure system availability and enough capacity of our critical data.


Requirements
  • Relevant IT qualification (NQF level 5 or 6)
  • 3-5 years relevant working experience on Linux systems administration
  • Working experience in network securities administration
  • Knowledge of DevOps best practices
  • Be fully proficient in a development language (preferably BASH/SHELL scripting)
  • Excellent verbal and written communications skills
  • Excellent listening skills
  • Excellent planning and organising skills
  • Excellent time management skills
  • Excellent client service skills
  • Fully proficient within a Linux environment
  • Familiar with Windows environment
  • Fully proficient on Virtualization & Cloud Services (advantageous)
  • Valid drivers license & own transport
