    • Mercer is seeking candidates for the following position based in the Warsaw Operation Centre:

    Data Scientist

    In This Role You’ll Be Responsible For

    Designing, developing and testing prototypes of advanced AI/ML algorithms to be used in various business applications (job matching, automated data corrections, data validation, advanced data analysis)

    Working with the Technology team to implement the algorithms in the technology solutions to ensure client/business needs are met and system performance is optimized

    Monitoring performance of the algorithms and suggest further ways of re-training and improving them

    Identifying opportunities for leveraging advanced analytics in transforming the business to the next level

    Closely cooperating with the Business, Technology and Operations teams to ensure that machine learning models operate correctly

    We Would Like You To Have

    1-2 years of professional experience in a previous data scientist position

    Very good command of English

    Excellent practical knowledge of

    Python

    (including version control, unit testing and documentation)

    Ability to wrangle datasets, performing operations such as splitting, joining, filtering and transforming datasets

    Exploratory data analysis. Working with datasets to extract basic statistical insight using models from machine learning, artificial intelligence, statistical learning or similar

    Good visualization skills. Ability to communicate findings through visualizations
