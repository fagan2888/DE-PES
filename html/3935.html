Data Engineer

City of Saratoga Southern Tigray Zone, ET

3 months ago

Apply on company website
The Data Engineer will be based in Stellenbosch, and will work with one of Saratoga's clients. You will be responsible for the implementation of individual applications on the Cognitive Platform according to industry standards and architecture guidance.

Key Responsibilities
  • Develop data pipelines from technical specifications as provided by Data Architects
  • Implement pipelines within agreed timelines
  • Perform unit testing from a pre-defined test plan
  • Provide implementation instructions for new development
  • Provide production support to production systems (stand-by)
  • Conduct investigations and solve system issues
  • Prepare production deployment packages
  • Ensure successful implementation
  • Provide coaching and mentoring to other team members
  • Participation and proactive problem-solving to meet team objectives


Company Information

We are a vibrant, innovative technology consulting company, and a preferred home for software solutions professionals. We have offices in Cape Town and Johannesburg.

The company culture is professional, yet people centric, and rewards excellence in all its forms.

Our people are some of the brightest in the industry, and many are leaders in their respective fields.

We invest in our people, and develop technology and business experts, by collaborating with clients on meaningful and challenging opportunities.
