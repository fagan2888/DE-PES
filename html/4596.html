Data Engineer

Tony's Chocolonely Amsterdam, North Holland, Netherlands

2 weken geleden 59 sollicitanten

Tony’s Chocolonely exists to make 100% slave free the norm in chocolate. To reach our mission, it is crucial to measure our own performance. Empowering Tony’s at all levels with not-to-miss data-driven business and impact insights in its own internal and external performance on our own plans, ambitions, and markets we operate in. BI’s mission is to leverage data for positive change.


As Tony’s Data Engineer, you are the product owner of the BI presentation tool and underlying Datawarehouse and data structuring. You are the key enabler to create data democracy within Tony’s, getting the opportunity to build and manage the technical side of the new BI capability within Tony’s that will elevate Tony’s to a data-driven organization.


In this role, you are the driving force behind the development, launch, roll-out, and management of Tony’s new BI infrastructure and tool that will be used across the company by different teams and roles. You are part of Tony’s new BI team reporting to Marijn, the head of BI and you work closely with Tony’s business analyst and team IT.


Creating one source of truth:

  • You map Tony’s many different structured data sources and how they overlap; you spot and untangle data inconsistencies.
  • You connect multiple data sources and create new cross-sectional information
  • You are comfortable with data ambiguity and are solution orientated in order to achieve a commonly shared view


Full control of the BI tool environment, end-to-end:

  • You will write the code for the data warehouse and BI front-end tooling, building the BI tooling from scratch, both front-end and data warehousing – keeping the data clean and well-structured
  • You create reports and dashboards and take them into production with an intuitive front-end architecture and ‘only Tony’ interface, keeping it simple and focus on core KPIs: less is more
  • You build up a basic level of business knowledge and have an analytical mindset to sanity check the correctness of the outcome of the reports
  • You make sure the system is robust with excellent (speed of) performance
  • You create and log an overview of the structured data sources and their business owners
  • Create a system where alarm bells go off when data is incorrect and identify the cause. You quickly detect bugs and, when applicable, you can fix the errors in the system
  • Can translate IT/BI requirements back to input requirements, aiming to keep data entry to a minimal level
  • You are the business partner of our external BI suppliers. You make sure they fully understand our needs and that they can fully attend to them.
  • You stay up-to-speed with the latest BI developments in the market and continuously seek to raise the bar on BI (features like predictive modelling)


the ideal candidate:

  • MSc in e.g. Computer Science, Software engineering or Applied Data Science
  • Minimum 3 years of work experience in BI and data infrastructure
  • You are experienced in coding in SQL and Python and have managed DWH in the cloud (e.g. Azure)
  • Experience designing the architectural structure of BI presentation tool industry leader (PowerBI, Sisense or Tableau)
  • You understand that technology doesn't come easily to everyone and that different teams have different needs and levels of knowledge. You know how to take that into consideration when choosing tools and adding features.
  • You always challenge yourself, have a positive attitude and are fun to work with :-)
  • You feel a clear connection with our core values: outspoken, willful, entrepreneurial & makes you smile and knows how to use these values in your role.
  • And lastly, you can make chocolate from anything and everything: maintain and support a can-do attitude, jump in wherever necessary, whenever necessary.


a peek into Tony’s BI team:

To change the chocolate industry and make all chocolate worldwide 100% slave free, it is crucial to measure our own performance. The BI team exists to leverage data for positive change; we want data to be available, insightful and fun for everyone in order to measure Tony’s own performance and progress in achieving our goal to make all chocolate 100% slave-free.

The team is new to Tony’s and will consist of a data engineer, business analyst, and reporting wizard, led by Marijn, our Head of BI and member of Tony’s management team.


what we offer

  • Full ownership, build something from scratch, lead the way and bringing into Tony’s data trends that will elevate our work and mission.
  • We want you to take a minimum of 28 annual leave days, that’s right minimum. More is fine too!
  • We take your development very seriously; we want you to dedicate up to 1 day a week on self-study and we offer annual personal training budgets.
  • A super tasty lunch & enough (healthy) snacks. Better than at home, we hear often..
  • Working-out during work hours; that keeps you sharp, so we are all in! And your sports outfit is on us, of course
  • An awesome office in the middle of Westerpark in Amsterdam.
  • We all work hard and have fun. Loads and loads of fun! Because we looooooooooooooove having fun at work. Don’t get us wrong, we know how to work our socks off too.
  • And of course, lotssss of chocolate.
