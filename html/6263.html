Position Description

Who are we? At CGI, we call our employees members because all who join CGI are building their own company - one that has grown to 70,000 professionals located in more than 40 countries. Founded in 1976, CGI is a leading IT and business process services company committed to helping clients succeed.
What we are building? We are building a next-generation module for anti-financial crime solution based on math, machine learning and AI, based on the data and our business knowledge.
With whom will you work? Currently, we are building a team around Jiří Palek . Jirka studied math, statistics, and machine learning at FNSPE, ČVUT. He loves solving real-based problems using math, he never stops in self-education and likes to share his knowledge. It is quite fun with him.
Who are you? It does not matter if you are a student, graduate, junior or senior. If you are Czech or not – foreigners are really welcome here! We need to cover the whole skillset for product development. We need you to be enthusiastic and motivated into this field, be opened and willing to learn new things, to share your part of knowledge and responsibility, to work for the team, to help to create a nice environment, to enjoy and create a funny and pleasant atmosphere within the team.

Your Future Duties And Responsibilities

  • We need your help to understand the clients and their needs and the data.
  • Extracting knowledge from the data. Building machine learning pipelines, validate them, validate results.
  • Discuss the cases with the clients on their site.

    • Technologies and tools we use
  • You can meet a big zoo of different tools here. From classical relational databases to big data platforms (Spark, Hadoop, ...), from local computations to cloud or server ones, GPUs.

    • Required Qualifications To Be Successful In This Role

  • Our main tool is , we are using jupyter notebook, jupyter lab, pycharm for our work.
  • You should know how to work with different data sources, from simple csv files to structured databases (SQL) and different data types (structured, unstructured, text, ...)
  • You will surely play & have fun with the data. A lot. For this, you should know something about visualization techniques.
  • You will need to put the data into the right shape – feature engineering, data transformation.
  • Of course machine learning is used here. Both supervised and unsupervised techniques we use here. You can meet deep learning here as well.
  • Machine learning pipeline validation and interpretation, quality metrics.

    • Nice to have
  • If you know something about big data storages, you can meet them here as well.
  • SW development skills – git, coding within a team, docker, cloud platform and computing.
  • Some knowledge of R could be used here as well.

    • Skills
    • Big Data
    • Python
