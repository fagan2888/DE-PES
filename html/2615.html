Job Description

Overall Purpose of the Job:

The Systems Engineer operates as a focal point for either customer configured projects or new product development projects. They are the coordinators of technical activity and this includes cooperating with mechanical, electrical, refrigeration and software teams within the business. The Systems engineer ensures a clear specification is communicated through the business and that project executions happens smoothly with quality assurance built into the process.

Key Responsibilities
  • Designs, defines and implements complex system requirements for customers and/or prepares studies and analyzes existing systems. Determines system specifications, input/output processes and working parameters for hardware/software compatibility.
  • Coordinates design of subsystems and integration of total system. Identifies, analyzes and resolves program support deficiencies.
  • Serves as the primary technical resource for design, manufacture and debug as the product move to completion.
  • Conducts experimental tests on equipment and evaluates results.
  • May also review vendors’ abilities to support development.
  • Depending on level, may function as a project engineer with the resident responsibilities of proposals, technical presentations, cross functional issue resolution, etc.
  • Expected to keep up to date on technical advances in the discipline and related fields.

Skills And Aptitude Profile
  • To be considered for this role you will ideally be qualified to degree level with at least 6 Years + relevant experience.
  • You will be proficient in developing design concepts, generating system specification & validation and use of computer aided design tools.
  • Experience in at least one of the following:- design for a low volume production environment, drafting and managing specifications, custom capital equipment, motion control, design calculations, acceptance testing, machine design and safety (eg FMEA).
  • Proficient in IT; Excel, Word, Outlook etc.
