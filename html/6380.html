PPM IT CoE

PPM IT CoE designs, implements , tests & supports all Jira common configurations & driving technology best practices evangelization across the BGs.

Main Responsibility Area

Overall responsibility of specified entities ( e.g. features). Plans, designs and executes items in own area. Coordinates activities within own responsibility area. Has developed specialist knowledge in one area. Coaches other team members in solving problems. Solves complex engineering problems of own technology area. Undertakes technical training responsibilities.

The candidate will be mainly responsible for creating reports and dashboards from the pipeline and occasionally handle various needs from different BGs of the company, regarding data visualization.

Your main responsibilities as a Data Visualization Engineer will be
  • Design PPM CoE related reports and dashboards
  • Design KPI/Metrics related reports mainly in PowerBI
  • Participate in design and build data pipeline
  • Work with the development teams and architects inside and outside PPM CoE team
  • Identify issues in dashboards and reports, and design future proof solutions
  • Help to improve the PPM CoE related dashboards continuously based on customer needs
  • Keep customer Demos and induction trainings
  • Coaches other team members

Position Requirements
  • BSc or MSc in computer science
  • Fluent in written and spoken English
  • Understanding of mathematical statistics
  • Experience in data visualization design and related software (Power BI or Tableau)
  • Understanding of web applications APIs
  • Linux scripting and/or SW developer skills (Python, TypeScript)
  • Advantage if knows Python Pandas and Django
  • RBDMS administration experience
  • Logical mindset, think in systems and have problem solving skills
  • Adapt changes and able to handle uncertainty
  • Understanding of DevOps
  • Understanding of SAFe
  • Basic experience with JIRA and Confluence
  • Proven to be quick learner and good team player
