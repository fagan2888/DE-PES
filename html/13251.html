IT Data Architect (Data Integration Developer and ETL Toolset Administrator)- Apply now!

Austin Energy Austin, TX, US

1 week ago

Apply on company website
Austin Energy seeks an IT Data Architect (data integration developer and ETL toolset administrator, including IBM Infosphere Datastage) for the Database Management and Data Services workgroup. This position develops ETL and data integration jobs, provides technical support, maintenance and administration of ETL toolsets. In addition, the position requires working with project teams in support of development and implementation of data integrations projects including requirements gathering, system design, troubleshooting, deployment, monitoring, system patching, upgrades, and performance enhancements to maintain optimal health of sandbox, development, test and production environments. The position will play a key role in Information Technology’s Database Management and Data Services group, responsible for development and administration of IBM Infosphere suite that enables the enterprise’s data integration needs.
