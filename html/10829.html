    Corporate IT Recruiter at JSC "Lithuanian Railways"

    • This is an opportunity to join the biggest Lithuanian transport company as Data Engineer. As a part of growing team you will be handling the design and construction of scalable management systems, ensure that all data systems meet company requirements, and also research new ways of usage for data acquisition. You should also know the ins and outs of the industry such as data mining practices, algorithms, and how data can be used.


    Responsibilities


    • Work on various data tables and form data models
    • Find hidden patterns using data
    • Prepare data for predictive and prescriptive modelling
    • Identify ways to improve data reliability, efficiency and quality
    • Prepare data catalog and data lineage


    Requirements


    • To have background in Mathematics/Statistics, Information Technology or similar
    • To be able to model and design modern data structures
    • To be able to describe data quality logical algorithms
    • Have experience with various data analysis and visualization software tools (SQL, Excel, BigQuery, etc.)
    • To have knowledge in DMBOK methodology
    • Experience with Master Data Management systems would be nice to see


    We offer


    • An opportunity to become a part of innovative environment in a rapidly changing company
    • An exciting opportunity to be a part of big organization and work with experienced professionals
    • High performing, driven and supportive work culture and outstanding development possibilities to increase your potential
    • Gross salary starting at 1895 EUR/month (salary offer depends on candidate‘s professional experience, knowledge and competencies)
