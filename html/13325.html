One Identity Budapest, HU

Overview

One Identity is an award-winning IT management software provider offering a broad selection of solutions that solve some of the most common and most challenging IT problems.

Our privileged access management solutions enable you to provide the full credential when necessary or limit access with granular delegation for least privileged access. All privileged activity is recorded and all keystrokes are logged with real-time analyzation of activity and data.

We are currently looking for a Senior Test Automation Engineer to join us.

Responsibilities
  • Leads complex technical analysis to design, implement and perform testing process for Privileged Access Management (PAM), including automation testing framework, blackbox/manual/exploratory testing and quality management tool selection/implementation
  • Solves technical problems in creative and effective ways
  • Troubleshoot, narrow, and determine the root cause of problems
  • As a member of QA team, represents the team and collaborates with the development in the design of features to ensure the testability of the software
  • Participate in test coverage analysis
  • Mentor junior QA colleagues
  • Write and perform acceptance tests for the Core team
Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s/Master’s Degree
  • Working knowledge of automated testing tools
  • Familiarity with Jenkins or similar CI/CD platform
  • Experience with Python language is an advantage
  • Good verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills in English
  • Teamplayer with good mentoring and supporting skills
We offer
  • Home office
  • Free choice of tools
  • Free use of Linux on your own notebook if you like
  • Flexible working hours
  • Personal learning/conferencing budget
  • English course
  • Table Tennis, c64, guitar, kicker, Xbox…
