    Martin Bøgelund Arvidsen

    • Are you a bright and talented software engineer with a keen interest in machine learning? Would you like to take part in our mission to bring PFA’s AI platform to the next level? Then come join our progressive team revolving around our newly established AI Center of Excellence and help deliver the next generation of high quality AI products in finance.

    In the AI CoE, we use scalable data science, machine learning and advanced analytics to create intelligent models which benefit PFA’s customers and employees. You will work on problems like recommender systems, NLP, personalization and process automation, and build frameworks and data pipelines for mature operationalization of machine learning systems.





    Your key responsibilities will be:




    • Assisting in the development and operationalization of machine learning products together with data scientists, analysts, business product owners and software engineers.

    • Maintenance and development of the application stack for developing machine learning and data science in PFA, in collaboration with our IT and operations department.

    • Maintenance and development of DevOps and ModelOps pipelines and infrastructure for automated deployment and testing of data science products, in collaboration with our DevOps team.

    • Managing daily tasks for the CoE, including evangelism, maintaining our web-site, documentation and governance.





    “PFA’s journey with AI is well underway, and now we need to expand and scale our platform and governance for delivering the next generation of products based on data science and machine learning. Come join a dynamic team where you’ll be challenged and have the opportunity to set your footprint on this strategically important business area for PFA.”, says Daniel Brevadt, head of AI & Analytics





    We expect, that you:




    • Have a master degree in a relevant field (technical, statistical, etc.).

    • Be an experienced linux user, including OS and application configuration and maintenance.

    • Know your way well around R or Python.

    • Have experience with or an interest in machine learning and data science.

    • Have experience in modern DevOps and IT Infrastructure, including cloud computing and containerization.

    • Have good and tidy coding and documentation practices.

    • Be equally efficient in your communication with technical and non-technical audiences

    • Be able to drive projects on your own and work independently.





    We will offer you:



    As an employee of PFA you get a more meaningful career. We believe that is important to have great colleagues - Monday also has to be a good day and we focus on development and health. If you are interested, we have a number of exciting offers that allow you to learn more about yourself and your own health. For us, it is important that our employees feel good and get energy from going to work.





    Do you want to join us?



    Send your application as soon as possible and no later than November 8 th 2019 to dhb@pfa.dk . If you want to know more about the position, you are welcome to contact Senior Data Scientist Johannes Waage at +45 39176433 .

    PFA is a pension company, but we are far more than that. In addition to pension and insurance, we offer savings in our bank for private customers and several solutions within health and housing. We are owned by our more than 1.2 million customers, and we were founded in 1917 to ensure the Danes freedom to live the life they want.
