  • 負責: design and debug of test interface HW, developing test plans, board issue debug, data analysis for device performance, HW/SW integration, characterization test, releasing cost effective production test solutions into mass production, test support in production, yield improvement and test time reduction activities.

    Role & Responsibilities

  • You will work closely with Multi-functional teams, circuit and logic design, verification, test engineering to accomplish tasks.
  • You will work in a cross-function Engineering team to help guide our GPU products from NPI to mass production. Along the way you will help to identify and solve a wide range of issues that are blocking the volume ramp.
  • You will help to define the product test methodology, characterization and debug.
  • Applicants should be experienced in SW and HW troubleshooting and have excellent communication skills. You must be able to work independently, methodically, and be results driven.
  • Ability to work with common bench top test equipment (oscilloscope, spectrum analyzer, logic analyzer, power supplies, etc).
  • Able to make data based decisions (data analysis driving to issue closure).
  • Able to work independently and with good initiative to overcome technical challenges.
  • Coordinate closely with peers & seniors to ensure timely and effective communication of all assigned work activities.
  • A self-starter required and able to deal with a high level of ambiguity.
  • The ideal candidate has worked at computer hardware company as a design or debug engineer.
