Data Engineer

Attraqt Sofia, Sofia City Province, Bulgaria

1 month ago

We are looking for a bright and experienced Data Engineer to join our team implementing our next generation data processing system components. In this challenging role you will analyse various structured and unstructured data sources, mine key information points and combine these into data feeds to our services.


Your quality-orientated yet pragmatic mindset will help you to quickly build maintainable large-scale data integrations. Our team of top engineers and ever-growing number of international customers provide a unique opportunity to learn fast and gain practical experience in e-Commerce and complex data processing.

MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES

«Design, develop and test data processing system components and features

«Participate in code reviews with other team members

«Document components and features developed to aid others

«Create unit tests for test automation

«Analyze, understand and implement solutions based on technical requirements

«Provide support and maintenance

«Balance pragmatic solutions with best practice and delivery

QUALIFICATIONS

«University degree in Computer Science or equivalent

«Excellent analytical, problem-solving and engineering skills

«Hands-on, practical skills in dealing with large amounts of data

«Experience working with ETL tools and databases

«Experience working with streaming data technologies

«Solid understanding of data integration concepts

QUALIFICATIONS CONSIDERED AN ADVANTAGE

«Familiar with scripting languages (JavaScript, Python)

«Experience in Machine Learning

«Experience working with cloud-based platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure)

«Experience with Agile project management

«Interest in e-Commerce

PERSONALITY TRAITS

«Self-thinking and having hands-on approach

«Good communication skills both written and verbal

«Good knowledge of English

«Self-starter with strong drive who mobilises people and gets things done
