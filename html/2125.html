Company profile

Serengeti is an international software development nearshoring and outsourcing consulting company founded in 2007 in Zagreb, Croatia.

We provide technology consulting and software development services to clients from Croatia and abroad, with a focus on the Western European market. Our core business is the development and maintenance of complex enterprise business applications. We build engineering teams with a strong focus on domain knowledge and technical expertise. Due to our distinctive model of outsourcing, we have become a valued partner for companies in the financial, logistics, energy, public administration, healthcare, and industrial manufacturing sectors.


We are continuously growing in revenue, employee count, and level of expertise. Our customers recognize us for our unique, custom-made customer approach as we aim to build strong, long term relationships that result in win-win-win outcome for all - our customers, our employees, and Serengeti.

Besides Croatia, we also have sales offices in Austria and the Netherlands.


Job Description


We are looking for a professional Embedded Software Engineer to execute complete embedded software development life cycle. The goal is to create scalable and optimized software systems. Our client is specialised in designing and manufacturing of robotized systems for extreme conditions and we would like that you join our team. If solving problems keeps you warm at night, flexible is your middle name, and you are looking for a long-term opportunity, please feel free to apply.


Tasks and responsibilities:

• Planning and analysis

• Design

• Implementation

• Testing and Maintenance of custom-made software modules

Requirements:

• Minimum 1 year of experience working in C language

• Experience in programming embedded systems (microcontrollers)

• Experience with Altium Designer application or similar tool for printed circuit board design

• Experience with communication interfaces like CANbus, RS485, RS232, SPI

• Familiarity with electromotor systems (Permanent Magnet Synchronous motors, inverters / motor controllers) is a plus

• Familiarity with battery management systems is a plus

• Familiarity with CAD applications (example Solidworks) is a plus

• Excellent knowledge of English - both written and spoken

• Ability to quickly adapt to different environments

• Eagerness to learn new things

• Constant desire to improve your skills and competencies

• Experience with software development for the automotive industry is a plus


What we offer:

• The opportunity to work in a fast-growing company, a proud winner of Deloitte’s “The Fast 50” award (which we won two times in a row!)

• An important role in a company of 150 employees

• Competitive salary

• Excellent work-life balance

• Employee recognition, awards, and gifts

• Personal and professional growth and development opportunities

• Various perks and benefits
