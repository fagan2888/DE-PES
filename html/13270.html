Data Engineer (m/f)


At ICIS we have several analytics teams dedicated to data-driven insight from data analysis and modelling related to key traded markets including carbon emissions trading, power and gas trading in Europe and for the global petrochemicals market.


We are looking for a Data Engineer to join our Data Team in our German office in Karlsruhe. As a developer, you will work closely together with our analysts, understand their data needs and support them with your automated data processing solutions. You will conduct analysis of the processed data and recommend data structures as well as techniques to address identified data problems.


You will also assist in improving our data infrastructure, to make data delivery more reliable and secure.

Main responsibilities
  • Understanding the data requirements of our business
  • Setting up and implementing data models for new data sets
  • Coding of automated download scripts
  • Code and data maintenance
Key skills
  • Passion for coding and problem solving
  • Experience in writing software, ideally Python
  • Basic knowledge in data modelling
  • Basic skills in SQL
  • Basic knowledge of web-technologies (FTP, REST, SOAP, XML, etc.)
  • Advanced proficiency in English or German (Basic English is a must)
  • Your particular strengths will form the focus of your job as part of a strong team that shares project responsibility.
We offer
  • Personal learning opportunities at a worldwide leading market information and insight provider
  • Flexibility and trust as part of our corporate culture
  • Opportunities to take over additional responsibilities to grow in your role
  • A vibrant environment working alongside a dynamic and motivated team
