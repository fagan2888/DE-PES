Data Scientist / ML Engineer (Mid-Level+) - Prague

Datamole Prague, CZ

Před 3 týdny

Zažádat na webu společnosti
We are an agile, data-driven team of 40 data scientists, data engineers, and software engineers. New technologies and the latest methods are part of our day-to-day work. Our business is to deliver innovative and high added value solutions by utilizing artificial intelligence techniques and data-driven methods. We analyze data from sensors, robots or high-tech devices and make them smarter. Our code and algorithms live through the devices and products manufactured by our business partners across the globe.

We are looking for an experienced data scientist/machine learning engineer who is keen on turning data into actionable insights and solutions. Are you up-to-date with the latest developments both in academia and in the industry? It is essential to know how it works, and it is even more important to know what to do to solve a problem.

Day-to-day Responsibilities
  • Data preprocessing, analysis, and exploration.
  • Research, implementation, and optimization of algorithms for challenging real-world problems.
  • Proof of concept prototyping.
  • Driving improvements to internal as well as customers’ ML products.
  • Supervising junior colleagues.
  • And a lot of playing with data.

Requirements
  • Passion for data science and machine learning.
  • Quantitative background (math, physics, engineering, data science) with additional exposure to practical machine learning.
  • Analytical thinking and results-oriented way of working.
  • 2+ years of experience with data analysis and data exploration.
  • 2+ years of experience applying math/machine learning algorithms in business or industry.
  • Proficiency in the Python data science stack: pandas, numpy, scipy, matplotlib, seaborn, scikit-learn.
  • Recent graduates (including PhD) are welcome to apply with a solid portfolio of personal/academic projects showing that you can translate models into code.

We offer
  • Friendly, family-like working ambiance of a small company.
  • A unique opportunity to participate in challenging, innovative projects in AI, Big Data, Robotic Systems or IoT. Therefore we can promise you will learn a lot.
  • Flexible working hours and competitive salary.
  • Office at Vitezne namesti, 1 minute to catch a tram, 2 minutes from metro Dejvicka.
