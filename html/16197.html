The Who, What, Why And Where

Robocalls are skyrocketing and 70% of consumers don’t answer a call if the caller’s number is anonymous. Twilio’s Trusted Communications team is on a mission to make sure that millions of consumers have the information needed to decide whether to answer the phone or not. Our product, Verified By Twilio, is just the first phase of a broader initiative to build greater trust in traditional communications.

Who?

Twilio is looking for exceptional backend software engineers who meet the following requirements.
  • 2+ years in designing, developing, delivering, and scaling modern backend systems
  • Demonstrated expertise in at least one server side programming language (Java, Go, Python, Ruby, Scala, ...)
  • Experience working with container-based applications, Docker specifically.
  • Hands on knowledge of AWS (Lambda, SQS, DynamoDB, ...)
  • Solid Linux command line skills.
  • Direct experience working with agile processes, continuous integration, and tests automation.
  • Commitment to sharing experiences and good development practices with the community.


Bonus Points For
  • In-depth knowledge of DNS (architecture, setup, operation)
  • Familiarity with public key cryptography (JWT, public key management)
  • Experience working with MongoDB and next generation distributed data stores.

What?

As a Backend Engineer at Twilio, you can expect to fill your days by:
  • Building APIs for the Verified by Twilio product
  • Ensuring a reliable system that matches our SLAs to maintain our customers’ trust in us while fighting security threats
  • Mentoring junior developers and the team in general.

Why?

At Twilio, fostering greater trust in communications is one of our top priorities. Our team plays an integral role in stopping unwanted calls and phone scams, while ensuring that consumers continue to receive wanted calls. For that purpose, we’re also creating a KYC repository to store verified information of businesses that will populate the phone screens as calls come in.

Twilio is a company that is empowering the world’s developers with modern communication in order to build better applications. Twilio is truly unique; we are a company committed to your growth, your learning, your development, and your entire employee experience. We only win when our employees succeed and we're dedicated to helping you develop your strengths. We invest in weeks dedicated to tackling hard problems and creating your own ideas. We have a cultural foundation built on diversity, inclusion, and innovation and we want you and your ideas to thrive at Twilio.

Where?

This position will be located in our beautiful office in Bogotá, Colombia . You will enjoy our office perks: catered meals, snacks, game room, ergonomic desks, massages, BBQs, gym stipend, bi-weekly All Hands and more. What you will also get to experience is a company that believes in small teams for maximum impact; seeks well-rounded talent to ensure a full perspective on our customers’ experience, understands that this is a marathon, not a sprint; that continuously and purposefully builds an inclusive culture where everyone is able to do and be the best version of themselves.

About Us

Millions of developers around the world have used Twilio to unlock the magic of communications to improve any human experience. Twilio has democratized communications channels like voice, text, chat, video and email by virtualizing the world’s communications infrastructure through APIs that are simple enough for any developer to use, yet robust enough to power the world’s most demanding applications. By making communications a part of every software developer’s toolkit, Twilio is enabling innovators across every industry — from emerging leaders to the world’s largest organizations — to reinvent how companies engage with their customers.
