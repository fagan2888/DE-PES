HOW IS WORKING IN THE GROUP IT TEAM SPECIAL?

Tesco Group Technology team is designing, building and maintaining technology systems for Tesco businesses in the UK, Europe and Asia. In the UK, we serve some 66 shoppers every second, so it's our goal to ensure every one of those customers experiences just a little better service on each visit. In the Data and Analytics team, we focus on solving complex business problems developing products that can be deployed at scale to our customers. Our work spans across multiple platforms that support business areas including Stores, Online, Fulfilment, Marketing and Clubcard.


KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Working with the Head of Programs and Planning to communicate and maintain the consistent set of programme, workstream and project management standards, guidance and templates to be used by the programme team
  • Working alongside colleagues in the Programme and Planning team to manage governance, reporting, planning and improvement of processes
  • Ensuring consistent approach to how teams record and monitor all workstream risks, issues and dependencies ensuring all are identified and managed appropriately across the portfolio in UK, Central Europe, India and Asia
  • Delivering high-quality management information for papers, reports and dashboards reviewed at a variety of governance forums


REQUIREMENTS:
  • Experience of working in large programmes or projects
  • Strong inter-personal skills
  • Strong Microsoft Office skills (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)
  • Passionate about making a difference for our colleagues and customers
  • Fluent written and spoken English language


Advantage would be:

  • Experience of using SharePoint, Jira & Aha! Tools and functionality
  • Holder of a recognised programme or project management accreditation
  • Experience of working for a major retail organisation
  • Experience of working within a Technology function
