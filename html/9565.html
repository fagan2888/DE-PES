    Who are we looking for?

    We are looking for an Software Engineer (Big Data) to join our Bangalore development center

    Requirements
    • You should have minimum 9 yrs of overall experience in IT industry with hands on experience in Cloudera Hadoop and Java .
    • You should have experience gained using the following technologies: Enterprise Java; Kafka; Hadoop; Hive; Hbase; HTTP APIs
    • You should be able to clearly demonstrate to us that you have played a role in successfully delivering a real time analytics solution using the technologies above, either in an academic or work based setting.
    • You should have the enthusiasm and energy to learn and contribute actively to solution design, product direction and our commitment to industry best practices.

    Other Skills Of Benefit
    • Experience of the following would be beneficial but is not essential: Google Cloud Platform, JavaScript/HTML5/CSS3 AngularJS; ReactJS Liferay Spring/Hibernate; MongoDB; P4/Git/Maven/Ant; SaaS deployment and maintenance; Agile methodologies.

    • Take pleasure in seeing smart, practical solutions put in front of customers;

    • Open to constantly refreshing and renewing your skills
