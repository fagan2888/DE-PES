Due to our tremendous growth we are looking for driven, smart account executives who can foster and develop existing client relationships with Media clients across France & Western Switzerland.

Akamai’s Account Executive is responsible for the sales cycle - from lead generation, renewals to deal closures by creating the demand within their territory across all business verticals and assigned accounts.

You will be expected to deliver against a quarterly Revenue & incremental new Bookings targets.

Responsibilities
  • Deliver against a revenue and incremental booking target through cross sell and upsell of Akamai’s extensive product portfolio.
  • Work with extended Akamai team to create and deliver customer business reviews.
  • Provide account planning to identify risks and opportunities across your install base.
  • Work with the lead generation team on closing new business.
  • Work with Marketing to create demand across install base.
  • Work with the extended Akamai team to monitor the service utilisation and value proposition for each of your customers.
  • Demonstrate sales forecasting excellence.

About The Team

As an industry leader with the world’s largest, most robust distributed computing platform, we offer sales teams world-class training and support, industry-leading lead generation and cultivation programs, significant growth opportunities, and dynamic, highly scalable compensation packages.

Our sales organization has nearly doubled in the past few years and is made up of highly, driven successful and bright individuals that thrive on innovation. We are proud that 90% of our sales management team has developed their careers with us, and look for those with similar ambitions and drive for success.

Required Education And Experience-

Applicants must meet one of the following education and experience requirements-
  • Relevant experience and a Bachelor’s degree or its equivalent

Desired Skills-
  • Proven Track Record in over-achieving quarterly and annual quotas through direct and indirect field sales.
  • Digital Media industry knowledge or experience
  • High ethics, integrity, and personal drive.
  • Strong knowledge of Internet and telecommunications concepts and demonstrate the ability to discuss them with intelligence and conviction.
  • Formal sales training and understanding of- Solution-Selling and/or Value Selling training or other metrologies.
  • Ideal candidates will bring with them a range of Internet industry-related contacts.
  • Strong background in Internet infrastructure and/or enterprise solution software required.
