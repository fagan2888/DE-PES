Successful candidate will be part of a growing Data Analytics Delivery Team located in Poland, focusing on various DB, ETL and Big Data technologies. Providing projects and services to international customers, clients and teams from France, UK, US, Germany, Philippines, India and other parts of the world. He/she will participate in end-to-end project implementations delivered in dynamic and young environment that encourages innovative solutions.


Currently we are looking for:

Data Governance Engineer

job location: Wolczanska street, Lodz, Poland


Main responsibilities:

  • Provide Architecture and Consulting services for processing, analyzing and storing of large amount of data
  • Provide support to projects, developing new or introducing changes to existing Information Governance applications
  • Large scale deployments, automation and configuration management
  • Performance analysis and tuning
  • Migrate applications or changes between environments
  • Proactive monitoring, deep investigations & trouble-shooting
  • Customer ordered audits and security analysis
  • Provide consultancy for ongoing Information Governance projects


Qualification background:

  • Bachelor or Master degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or equivalent
  • Familiarity with at least two of the following platforms: Oracle RDBMS, MS SQL Server, PostgreSQL , MySQL
  • Familiarity with Windows Server, Linux or Unix environments and filesystems
  • Familiarity with data import/export and data validation methods techniques across same or different databases
  • General knowledge on data visualization techniques and standards
  • Java, .NET development skills and/or PowerShell, Groovy scripting skills are highly appreciated
  • Database performance tuning experience
  • Solid problem solving and analytical skills
  • Autonomous, strong ownership and drive to get things done
  • Eagerness to learn and adaptability to changes
  • Strong English language skills
  • Strong interpersonal skills


Nice to have:

  • Data modelling and data design experience, knowledge of related standards (eg ERD)
  • Experience with transactional/ERP systems: PeopleSoft, Oracle EBS, SAP, Baan
  • German language skills


Why would you join us?

We offer a steady job in an international company in the heavily invested area, where you will encounter multiple opportunities to cooperate with inspiring people from across the world. A place where you can learn, work with experts, share your knowledge and enjoy many social activities and events prepared for our Employees.

Additionally, we offer:

  • Full-time employment contract
  • Numerous opportunities for personal and professional development and advancement within the company by taking part in interesting and challenging projects
  • Modern and friendly work environment with open door policy
  • Professional technical and soft skill training programs (internal DXC University, DXC Partners Network and certification program), opportunity to learn and evolve within a team of experienced colleagues
  • Private medical care, social benefits system, life insurance, Multisport+ card, Wellness Program (massages included), German language classes, relax corner (XBOX games and other), sport communities
  • Employee Assistance Program (providing help for employees in difficult life situations)
  • Competitive compensation package adjusted to candidate’s experience and qualifications
  • Flexibility in work arrangement (home-office culture & flexible working hours are in our DNA)
