Data Engineer / Scientist

Location: Warsaw, Poland



Are you looking to take your career to the next level?



We are looking for juniors and technical masters to join the Data and Analytics team.



Data Engineering is an acceleration factor behind the productivity and effectiveness of Data Science and Business Intelligence. Technical mastery and interpersonal /communication skills are therefore a must to thrive in a team. Your job is to design and build highly scalable analytical solutions including ML/AI pipelines and data warehouses, optimize ELT processes including machine learning models, design data models and code the logic into workflows, that will drive the analytics into insights. We’re looking for candidate to Junior, Mid and Senior positions.

Are you ready for the challenge?

“Do something that matters”.

With us you will:

  • Create (design/code) advanced ELT and analytic applications operating on high volume of data
  • Work on various cloud (Azure, AWS, Google) and big data platforms (Databricks, EMR, Cloudera, HDI)
  • Leverage most advanced data processing frameworks like Apache Spark, Apache Hive
  • Build interfaces to all sort of data sources like SAP HANA, Oracle, MySQL, Postgres, Azure Blob, ADLS, S3 and many more
  • Build orchestrated workflows with Oozie, Apache Airflow or Azure ADF
  • Create ML/AI solutions to support Data Engineering and operations team in self-healing systems, outage prevention or data quality
  • Optimize machine learning pipelines by understanding business logic behind prototyped models and coding them to handle high volumes of data in distributed computing
  • Design/build database/data warehouse solutions to support BI processes.
  • Design/build easy-to-use user interfaces (PowerBI, Spotfire, Tableau or custom JavaSript), which will enable the business to leverage complex, backend solutions at the ease of smartphone UX.
  • Build CI/CD pipelines using Gitlab or Jenkins


Apply if you have:

  • Passion for application design, development and/or creation of advanced analytical models
  • Programming skills in one of: Scala, Python, Java, R
  • Graduated with a BS or MS in Computer Science/Engineering, Programming/Software Development, Operations Research/Statistics, Mathematics or similar
  • Good understanding of databases (SQL) and data models
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • The following skills are desired but not obligatory:
    • Understanding of Apache Spark or MapReduce
    • Shell Scripting (linux/unix/Windows power shell)
    • AWS, Azure, Google clouds
    • Knowledge of Machine Learning and AI
    • BI Development in Power BI, Spotfire, Tableau
    • JavaScript
  • Demonstrated ability to handle multiple priorities
  • Demonstrated ability to fast learn new technologies
  • Fluency in English. Polish is desired but not essential




What can we offer you?



You’ll be at the core of breakthrough innovations, be given exciting assignments, lead initiatives, and take ownership and responsibility. Also, we provide creative work spaces where new ideas are valued. All the while, we will offer you world-class training and development to help you become a leader in your field.

And it’s not just about what you will do, but how we will make you feel: welcomed, valued, purposeful, challenged, heard, and encouraged.
