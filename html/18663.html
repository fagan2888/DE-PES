KBR have an exciting opportunity for a Senior Civil Engineer specialising in roads and infrastructure to join our Adelaide team. This is a key role in the business and a great opportunity for someone looking to join a global company and take the next big step in their career.


KBR are involved in some of the most exciting projects in Australia and joining us will enable you to work alongside and support the very best industry specialists.

Responsibilities

  • Plan, design and execute road and infrastructure projects to specified standards
  • Review designs prepared by others and verify their compliance to Project Deed, Australian Standards and relevant design guides.
  • Deliver within agreed budgets on or ahead of schedule
  • Provide direction, mentoring and support to the project staff and Civil Group
  • Assist on bids/ tender proposals for projects
  • Help develop business opportunities with key industry contacts
  • Prepare technically sound, well written proposals and reports
  • Maintain or exceed client satisfaction

QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE:

  • B.Engineering or equivalent
  • Minimum of 7 years’ experience
  • CPEng preferred
  • Strong highways / roads design and planning experience on DPTI projects
  • Experience on civil infrastructure, buildings civil and defence projects an advantage
  • Working knowledge of 12D design software and BIM integration for infrastructure projects an advantage
  • Working knowledge of commercial arrangements and relationships
  • Strong practical knowledge of and commitment to health and safety
  • Previous experience gained within an engineering consultancy environment required
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

Why KBR:

We are a proud professional services company at the forefront of Australian Infrastructure. KBR offers a competitive lifestyle and remuneration package that include family-friendly work hours and an optional rostered Friday off every four weeks.
