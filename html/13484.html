Microwave Planning Engineer

Rize Technology Corporation Düsseldorf, DE

Vor 1 Woche

Auf Firmenwebseite bewerben
Job Description

Main Responsibilities
  • Definition of standard external & internal procedures for microwave network
planning based on customer requirements, especially considering the
offshore delivery teams.
  • External and internal interface for project coordination.
  • Manage & review the results of all defined planning procedures.
  • Provide the detailed workflow for the processes, including every
input/output/analysis/template. etc
  • Knowledge transfer of all microwave planning processes to other colleagues.
  • Trouble-shooting - Investigate, isolate and drive solutions to problems
reported by customers to a closure
  • Transmission capacity analysis
  • Line of Sight Testing results
  • Link Engineering experience
  • BNetzA Frequency Application (documentation completion) & Hardware ordering
Requirements
  • Minimum 3 years practical experience in supporting or deploying microwave
planning project;
  • In-depth knowledge of microwave product technologies
  • Good knowledge in microwave planning principle – Background of fixed design
– LLD of Network topologies.
  • Familiarity with microwave planning tools such as IQ-LINK, Pathloss
  • Problem diagnosis and resolution skills are essential
  • Experience in other telecom vendor is priority – Huawei or SIAE
Top 3 things
  • Telefonica Germany experience
  • German speaking
  • MW Planning key words: Planning, design, Microwave, IQ-Link
