Job Description

Position description:

As the principal applications engineer for power semiconductor components designed for the emerging electric Vehicle (EV) market, you are expected to develop strong relationships with the technical staff at key accounts in the greater Shanghai area. You must be able to use your skills to keep customers fully engaged while using your technical skills to work closely with on-site engineers and solve problems. Engage the right skills from the extended support team to solve issues and win business. Managing customer expectations while reporting the issues back to the team for support and problem resolution is key to the success in this position.

Responsibilities

  • Design win for ON Products at key accounts
  • Develop customer relationships with key customer technical staff.
  • Hands-on ability to solve issues at customer site or engage the right support from team.
    • Effectively report issues back to team in US and follow up until resolution

    The successful candidate will have strong interpersonal and communication skills and a good understanding of power semiconductor devices and circuits.

  • Minimum BS or equivalent in Electrical Engineering
  • 8-10 yrs of FAE or applications experience in the power industry
  • Good understanding of IGBTs in various packages and their characteristics.
  • Good knowledge of power converter topologies and design
  • Familiarity with automotive standards for power converters like LV324
  • Familiarity with the use of lab equipment, like Digital oscilloscopes, waveform generators, etc.
  • Familiarity with the use Mathlab or other simulation software package
  • Good circuit trouble shooting skills and familiarity with EMC issues in circuits.
  • Prior customer facing experience or technical sales is a must.
  • Experience in automotive market is a big plus.
  • Fluent in Chinese Mandarin
