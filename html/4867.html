Reliability engineer

The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company Colmar, LU

il y a 2 semaines

Postuler sur le site de l’entreprise
Goodyear. More Driven.

Responsibilities
  • Implement and supervise preventive maintenance (electrical and mechanical) on production (tire building) machines
  • Review equipment downtime and implement countermeasure
  • Lead some CAPEX and OPEX project
  • Manage department/manufacturing unit budget
  • Coach maintenance technicians
  • Consider safety as 1st priority
  • Provide support during stand-by weekend
  • Link with engineering (support big capex project, keep machine documentation up to date, ...)
  • Organize/manage/support shutdown activities

Education

Master or Bachelor Degree in Mechanical and/or Electrical Engineering

Language
  • French and English are must, Luxemburgish and/or German an asset

Competencies Required
  • Strong practical knowledge in pneumatic, hydraulic and general mechanics: power transmission, mechanical assembly, mechanical guiding, drawings analysis
  • Knowledge in machine automation
  • Knowledge in industrial maintenance (preventive, predictive maintenance)
  • Ability to create technical drawings (Solidworks, Catia, Autodesk, …) is considered as astrong asset

Soft Skills
  • Coaching, leading small groups (shutdown/PM work organization, breakdown review with teams), good contact with supplier and other external company
  • Use of usual software: office, SAP, …
  • Strong analysis competencies, resistance to stress, willing to work during shutdown (vacation) and ensure stand-by weekend.

Goodyear is one of the world’s largest tire companies. It employs about 64,000 people and manufactures its products in 47 facilities in 21 countries around the world. Its two Innovation Centers in Akron, Ohio and Colmar-Berg, Luxembourg strive to develop state-of-the-art products and services that set the technology and performance standard for the industry. For more information about Goodyear and its products, go to www.goodyear.com/corporate.
