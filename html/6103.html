    • The company:

    Our client is part of Asia’s leading media broadcast and entertainment group, their purpose is to excite their customers with a range of exclusive and non-exclusive content, delivered in an ever-increasing mobile and digital life and where customer experience needs to be uncompromising.

    Work from an award-winning corporate office campus, including restaurants, bars, food courts, large outdoor space, weekly markets, cultural and corporate events, dry cleaner, convenience store, childcare, gym, doctors, phone shop, car share system, bus shuttle to and from Wynyard, Epping, Parramatta train Station, Macquarie Shopping center and more...

    The role:

    We are looking for a Systems Support Engineer who comes from a technical or developer background with experience working across mobile applications.

    This role may also suit a QA or Junior Developer who has experience of automation & scripting, most importantly your troubleshooting abilities, and you will have the determination to get to the bottom of an issue.

    You will be working within the mobile applications team where your main responsibility will be to get to the root cause of an issue.

    When an issue occurs, you will be looking at the end-to-end system and pinpointing what the problem is at a lower level.

    Requirements / Skills:

    • Solid experience as a System Support Analyst, or QA, or Junior Developers
    • Quality Assurance mindset
    • Curious and inquisitive
    • Knowledge around API
    • Automation or Scripting experience desirable
    • Troubleshooting skills
