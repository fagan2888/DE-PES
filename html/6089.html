Job Description

Cuerva is searching for a experienced cloud data engineer. You will be part of Cuerva´s revolution, developing its skills towards leadership in the world of smart grids. You will be responsible for working with massive data of electrical behaviors and creating new energy rules, giving rise to the digital and electrical transformation of the company.

Your Role // Responsibilities

  • Identify and understand the business needs to define a solution with solve them.
  • Your role will be to work with our massive data repositories to put together compelling trends and insights for the operations, marketing, finance, and sales teams.
  • You will use top-notch Business Intelligence and cloud technologies for analyzing various internal and external data feeds.
  • You love working with data, to create clear and effective reports and data visualizations, and to partner with customers to answer key business questions.
  • Develop, implement, and automate processes to collect and transform data from multiple sources
  • Define requirements for reporting sales data, customer behavior, and technical performance metrics, and work with technical teams and product managers to integrate into our development schedules

Experience & Skills

Required:

  • 4+ years (intermediate to advanced skill set) of experience working on data processing (structured and unstructured) and cloud architectures
  • Used to work with databases and advanced knowledge of SQL.
  • Experience in programming languages such us Java or C# to develop data integration mechanisms such us web services (REST, SOAP) or FTP.
  • Proven experience using cloud services and tools, preferably in the AZURE platform, (Data Lake Storage, Azure functions, Data Factory, Data Lake Analytics. Azure HDInsight, etc.)
  • Experience in manipulate and transform data with Python or R.
  • Research mindset and an ability to produce actionable results
  • Analytical mindset: know how to interpret the data to extract relevant insights and be able to translate business questions into analytical needs.

Nice to have:

  • Experience with batch and streaming data processing tools (Spark, Kafka, etc.).
  • Machine Learning /Deep Learning algorithms.
  • Data visualization with BI platforms (Power BI, Qlik, etc.).
  • Experience with Agile methodologies.
  • Scripting Bash / sh / Shell.

Basic Qualifications

  • Degree level qualification in IT/Engineering/Maths
  • Craft amazing stories using your impressive SQL capabilities and data intuition layered on top of our industry-leading data set
  • Experience with statistical modeling and analyzing large data sets
  • Demonstrated ability to coordinate projects across functional teams, including engineering, merchandising, design, marketing, finance, and operations
  • Strong interest in Information Technology, Big data and machine learning
  • Demonstrated leadership and collaboration skills.
  • A passion for transforming how the business runs is essential.

Cuerva is the answer if ….

You are looking for a stable professional career with a solid professional future.

You value the opportunity to be part of the world´s energy group.

You want to grow and develop within a dynamic company.

You believe that your commitment and dedication, together with the company´s, can and must have a positive impact on the environment.
