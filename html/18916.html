Job Description

Principal Duties and Responsibilities 工作职责 :

Be responsible for developing &maintaining Power BI Platform, to meet the defined OLA.
  • Manage PowerBI BI platform, be responsible PowerBI solution architecture & frameworks.
  • Be responsible for user access, security management
  • Be responsible for train end-users to regarding Dashboard reporting tools.
  • Be responsible for Data visualization with PowerBI solutions. Develop and maintain Dashboard & reports via PowerBI or any other related tools.
  • Perform administration tasks, such as coordination of software upgrades, patches & releases
  • Manage system load and estimate / propose scaling measures for new applications
  • Collaborate with service managers within Projects dependencies
  • Design data models necessary to support BI initiatives
  • Participate to gather and document reporting requirements to meet business needs
  • Communicate effectively with business, technical stakeholders as well as leadership
Job Requirements (Knowledge/Skills/Competencies) 录用条件
  • Strong communication, interpersonal and organization skills
  • Good communication (write and speak) in English
  • Able to understand &deliver what service manager requested on time with quality.
  • Expert knowledge on experience any BI/Reporting tools (e.g. Qlik, Tableau) PowerBI is preferred.
  • Experience with SQL, T-SQL
  • Experience in UI. To design the perfect of art BI solutions to customers.
  • Education in computer science or comparable course and proven experiences
  • Practical experience in implementation of BI /reporting, hands on experience in Dashboard reporting
  • Ability to plan and perform software releases, upgrades, patches etc. independently
  • Strong communication and presentation skills to work in a multicultural & international environment

About Manulife

Manulife Financial Corporation is a leading international financial services group that helps people make their decisions easier and lives better. We operate primarily as John Hancock in the United States and Manulife elsewhere. We provide financial advice, insurance, as well as wealth and asset management solutions for individuals, groups and institutions. At the end of 2018, we had more than 34,000 employees, over 82,000 agents, and thousands of distribution partners, serving almost 28 million customers. As of June 30, 2019, we had over $1.1 trillion (US$877 billion) in assets under management and administration, and in the previous 12 months we made $29.4 billion in payments to our customers. Our principal operations in Asia, Canada and the United States are where we have served customers for more than 100 years. With our global headquarters in Toronto, Canada, we trade as 'MFC' on the Toronto, New York, and the Philippine stock exchanges and under '945' in Hong Kong.
