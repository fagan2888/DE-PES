Sr. Data Scientist

Merck Group Shanghai, CN

5 天前

去公司网站申请


The Data Science group in the Bioinformatic Department, Merck KGaA extensively uses advanced analytics, machine learning, and deep learning models to create business and scientific values from data. We solve life-scientist-oriented business and scientific problems across Merck KGaA. We see our projects through the entire data science lifecycle, from problem definition, data exploration, to modeling, analysis, and deployment to production servers. We maintain a close partnership with IT to ensure that our models can be deployed and monitored in a quick, reliable and robust manner.



Responsibilities

As a Data Scientist, you will be a member of this diverse and highly skilled team. You will be exploring new datasets with business partners, learning the complexness of business process, building models, or coordinating the productionalization of the models. You will use a variety of tools, including SQL, Python, and shell scripting) in a variety of environments (e.g., various database, Linux servers, etc). You will work in a highly collaborative, team environment where you will quickly develop wide ranging exposure to new methods and best practices for performing data science. You will present data, models, software system, results, and conclusions to stakeholders.

Expected qualifications
  • Degree from a (985 / 211) university in Computer and Information Science, Statistics, Engineering or a mathematics-intensive discipline.
  • Bachelor's degree with 8 years of experience, or Master with 5 years of experience, or PhD with 2 years of experience.
  • Hand-on experiences with data management. Familiar with database schema, RDBMS (e.g., MySQL) and NO-SQL (e.g., MongoDB)
  • Hand-on knowledge with statistics, machine learning and deep learning models. Understanding the mathematical background of models.
  • Extensive experiences with programming. Familiar with Python, R, or other data-oriented programming languages (Python is preferred). Familiar with machine learning and deep learning packages (e.g., Keras, Tensorflow, Pytorch). Ability to automate data processing, modeling and service delivery.
  • Proven skills in documentation and presentation and excellent communication in English, comfortable with multiple-culture working environment
