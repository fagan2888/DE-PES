    • As our RF Product Quality Assurance Engineer, you will:

    • Conduct RF tests on Newtec devices to assure that requirements are respected
    • Create complex setups to conduct RF tests
    • Operate RF and data communications equipment such as a spectrum analyzer, power meter, …
    • Create and evaluate automate test procedures
    • Gather and summarize test results
    • Detect recurring patterns in identified issues and support processes
    • Assure issue reporting quality (troubleshooting flows, quality of information presented to the developers for further debugging)
    • Help resolve technical and on-going use case challenges
    • Assist our technical writers in the creation of comprehensive product documentation
    • Proactively engage with our engineering teams to exchange on specific product requirements and to Facilitate smooth integration of the components
    • Troubleshoot the integrated products where possible
    • Aspire towards a thorough understanding of satellite communication technologies and components

    As a prerequisite to become our Product Quality Assurance Engineer you:

    • Have Bachelor or master’s degree in Engineering (Telecommunication, Radio Frequency Communications, …)
    • Have Knowledge of Modulation and Coding Techniques
    • Master Linux and some scripting languages (bash, ruby, python, perl, ...)
    • Potentially have a first experience in test automation
    • Can demonstrate your knowledge of networking (in a Linux context)
    • Are conversational in English
    • Love to communicate with a broad range of technical experts and to share information
    • Are inquisitive and like to learn daily
    • Have a profound interest in satellite communication and new technologies

    Once on board you will:

    • Be part of our Product Quality Assurance team based in our headquarters in Sint-Niklaas (Belgium)
    • Travel sporadically towards our customers and work in close collaboration with team members based in Romania and Germany
    • Be working on various satellite communication applications and devices ranging from consumer satellite modems to high-end modulators and demodulators
    • Be involved in a wide range of technological domains, from software over network and system engineering to electronics
    • Be able to tailor our competitive compensation package to your specific transportation desires
    • Join an innovation and people driven high-tech industry leader
    • Be a member of a passionate global family that values openness and a can-do mentality
    • Take pride in enabling internet connectivity to the world (from schoolchildren in remote areas to in-flight Wi-Fi)
