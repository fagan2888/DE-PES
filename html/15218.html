Established in 1997, Libertex Group is the brand name for a group of companies that provides clients from over 120 countries with platforms and services for trading forex, CFDs and other online trading and educational products. We offer every client effective tools in training, analytics and education, as well as personal support where they want it.

Libertex Group has over 650 employees worldwide. At present the Company is on the stage of transformation – on the one hand, we have gained a great experience on the financial markets, but on the other, - we do need to change together with the world around us. New data driven methods replace traditional ways of doing business. Our main priority is providing to the clients

highly-automated, advantageous, personalized services based on machine learning and big data technologies as well as the keen insight of the client’s behavior and needs. The services which predict the client’s actions and enable the Company to offer him the products which he may need tomorrow, in advance – can be a good example of this priority. Another example of such services is the systems which control the risks of fraudulent operations online. You as part of the Data Science/Analytics team are going to participate in development and implementation of such

systems, to study the clients’ behavior and needs and support business with clear insights. But the most important thing is the opportunity to do independent researches, to find areas for improvement and implement your own models.

Our team is international and young, with flat hierarchy (the CEO is directly involved in setting and monitoring of the key-objectives of the team) which implies fast development and great opportunities. You also will participate in the most interesting international conferences regarding deep learning, data science, Python etc.

We are located in Montenegro – a South European country on the Adriatic coast. It offers the best ecology in Europe, the warm sea, activities like kitesurfing and mountain hiking in summer and mountain skiing and snowboarding in winter. Stunning nature, no traffic jams and nice food!

To summarize: a good quality of living.

Play. Learn. Trade.


Responsibilities:

  • Full-cycle development of machine learning pipelines: from research to implementation and support
  • Creation of scoring models, LTV models and recommendation systems
  • Automation and optimization of marketing campaigns
  • Risk analytics – research in anomaly and fraud detection
  • Pattern search and clustering of client behavior
  • Implementation of cloud based solutions (Amazon AWS) in cooperation with data engineers
  • Statistical analysis of business processes in order to improve and automate them
  • Support of data-driven culture


Requirements:

  • Good knowledge and experience with statistical, mathematical and machine learning approaches
  • At least Bachelor’s degree in Math, Computer Science, Statistics or Econometrics
  • Good level of Python and/or R
  • Proficiency in SQL (PostgreSQL, MS SQL) and NoSQL (ElasticSearch)
  • Experience with version control systems
  • Experience in practical data mining and working with large volumes of structured and unstructured data
  • Successful participation in Kaggle competitions
