    • About AYLIEN:


    Our AI-powered solutions make it easy for companies like Microsoft, Moody’s, and Sony to make informed decisions by extracting business insight and signals from the millions of pieces of content published online every day.


    About the role:


    Content is at the heart of our marketing strategy at AYLIEN and always has been. Creating innovative content that showcases the capabilities of our technology while telling a story using data and has been a pivotal part of our marketing activity. The data-driven stories we tell drive demand and educate our users while providing interesting insights into real-world events and topics of discussion.


    If you’re a data viz whiz looking for your next challenge and you thrive in a fast-paced environment where you’re given space to create and grow, we want to hear from you.


    Responsibilities:


    • Work with the content marketing and Data teams to ideate and create data-driven content ideas for the AYLIEN website and blog
    • Gather, validate and analyze data from our suite of intelligence products and third-party data sources
    • Visualize data in a meaningful and innovative way that enhances the impact and readability of our content while showcasing the power of our intelligence tools
    • Work with the wider marketing team to plan and create content (webinars, blogs, white papers, slide decks) according to a content calendar


    Requirements:


    • Strong research skills with the ability to spot a story and develop a narrative from scratch
    • Experience working with data analysis tools (Excel, Google Sheets, SQL)
    • Sufficient capabilities in one or more programming languages (Python, R, JavaScript)
    • Previous experience working with data visualization tools or libraries (Tableau, Flourish, Plotly, Charts.JS, Bokeh)
    • Technical ability to use and interact with APIs and data processing tools
    • Copywriting and editorial skills
    • Previous exposure to general Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing concepts
