Sr. Data Architect

Federated Co-operatives Limited Saskatoon, CA

Apply on company website

Federated Co-operatives Limited (FCL) is hiring a Senior Data Architect on a permanent basis at our Home Office in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan.

Who we are:

As a co-operative, we do business differently. We believe in working together to serve Western Canadians, delivering profits back to our communities and investing in sustainable growth. Our work is guided by our values of integrity, excellence and responsibility and driven by our vision of building sustainable communities together. To learn more about who we are, visit www.fcl.crs .

What you’ll do:

You will be responsible for the delivery of data products and delivering business value from those products in the wider business teams. You will engage with a wide range of internal and external parties and suppliers. You will negotiate with team leads and senior users to balance the demand on the Data Team and to ensure that all expectations are managed with transparency and reality.

  • You will successfully lead and manage all facets of agile data solutions design & delivery.
  • You will work with a high degree of autonomy and apply industry leading solution design and delivery knowledge, skills, tools and techniques to meet or exceed stakeholder needs and expectations.
  • You will establish and maintain enterprise data vision, strategy, roadmap, principles, standards, and governance for use by FCL and the CRS.
  • You will develop enterprise data architecture and data models (conceptual, logical and physical) to ensure that both current needs and future needs are met.

Why it matters:

We help local Co-ops grow and thrive by offering a range of professional services, including marketing, market research, information technology, human resources, accounting, risk management and business development.

Who you are:

You are looking for a career in Information Technology and:

  • You have a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science
  • You have a minimum of 8+ years relevant, progressively responsible experience and 4-6 years of experience in designing and implementing data architecture platforms (a combination of relevant education and experience may be considered)
  • If you are a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) it would be considered an asset.
  • You’re honest and trustworthy, are results oriented and strive to be the best in what you do.
  • You believe in collaboration, building relationships and value the perspectives of others.

Our Team Members receive competitive salaries, short-term incentives, a comprehensive benefits package and an employer-contributed pension plan. We encourage our Team Members to take advantage of learning opportunities, to grow and develop, and to foster a culture of teamwork and innovation.

FCL embraces diversity and inclusion and we’re working to create a workplace that is as diverse as the communities we serve. We support and provide an environment that allows every person to bring their whole self to work

If this opportunity speaks to you, we invite you to apply by October 30, 2019.

We thank all candidates for their interest, however only those selected to continue in the recruitment process will be contacted.

Please note you may be required to undergo a background and substance test in accordance with FCL policies.
