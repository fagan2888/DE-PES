Company Description

SwoopTalent offers Talent Data as a Service infrastructure to the Fortune 500 as a multi tenant, SaaS product. We use AI powered algorithms, machine learning and leading UX to automatically create and manage big data lakes for big companies. Our infrastructure processes terabytes of data daily and supports thousands of users. If you are excited about the future of data, AI and ML then we are the team for you.

Job Description

If you have experience in shipping SaaS software solutions working with large data and throughput volumes and you can understand data needs inside and across systems, as well as in analytics platforms, this role will give you a variety of challenges and opportunities. As a Data Engineer you will contribute to our entire software solution, supporting the development of in-app and standalone analytics.

You Will
  • Implement and manage robust data pipelines
  • Profile and analyze data to identify gaps and potential data quality issues; work with product managers to resolve these issues
  • Use Python, SQL, “R”, Snowflake, Looker, and other Big Data tools and technologies to code the data Engineering routines
  • Utilize automation to ensure high quality and fast delivery of data pipelines
  • Designs and jointly develops the data architecture with CTO and ensures security and maintenance
  • Assess suitable options, designs, and creates data pipeline (data lake / data warehouses) for our analytical solutions
  • Identify gaps and implement solutions for data security, quality and automation of processes
  • Support maintenance, bug fixing and performance analysis along data pipeline
  • Diagnose existing architecture and data maturity and identifies gaps
  • Gather requirements, assess gaps and build roadmaps and architectures to help the analytics driven organization achieve its goals

Qualifications

What will you need
  • 5+ years of data science or software engineering experience
  • Graduate degree in a quantitative or analytical field with material exposure to coding
  • Fluent English
And
  • Good communication skills
  • Detail-oriented, ability to multitask and work in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to work independently while also being a strong team player

Advantage
  • Machine Learning and AI experience

Technologies
  • Google Cloud / Public Cloud
  • Python/R (modelling)
  • SQL
  • Snowflake

Additional Information

As well as the chance to build award winning products at SwoopTalent, we offer you healthcare, paid vacation, team lunches, a fantastic office environment, paid family leave, stock options and a fantastic team of colleagues.
