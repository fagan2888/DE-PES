    • Our need:

    Are you creative and an innovative problem solver? Are you not happy with the status quo? If you answered yes to those questions, then we want you on our team. We are hiring a Technical Program Manager that is in the forefront of enabling the delivery of complex Omni Channel Information Security solutions at a Global scale.

    Your 10 + years of experience leading technical Agile projects is the key. Having Information Security and Infrastructure experience is definitely preferred and your overall PM / Scrum knowledge and experience is also just as important. The right resource will be a self-starter and take what we currently do from an Agile perspective and take it to the next level.


    Your experience in these areas is the difference maker:


    -Information Security Experience

    -Infrastructure Experience

    -Operational Experience

    -Technical Writing Experience

    -Customer Relationship Management

    -Agile/Scrum Experience (5+ years (recent))

    -Kanban Experience

    -End-to-end system deployment experience

    -Experience working in a large scale environments


    Minimum Qualifications:


    Bachelor of Science and 6 years' technical program management experience OR 10 years' technical program management experience.


    Would be even better if you have:


    -Advanced level Security knowledge of supported product space (ability to articulate and understand).

    -Intermediate Security knowledge and certifications acquired (Security+, SANS301, SANS401, CISA, CASP, CISSP, etc.) and Agile/Project Management certifications acquired (CSM, CSPO, PMP, CAPM, CompTIA Project +, CPMP, etc. )

    -Experience with strategic guidance and direction to portfolio team on usage of project manager tool-set (Jira, Confluence, Zoom, Tableau, etc.).

    -Advanced knowledge and responsible for product area budget and forecast budgetary needs to align with strategic direction Oversight of contractual processes with legal, VMO and business customer.

    -Establish business engagement relationships to formulate overall program strategy by driving both long-range and short-term planning activities with organizational functions and functional leaders. Has expert level domain experience and understanding of the programs, business and technical aspect, implications on the corporate strategy and whole end-to-end solution.

    -Advanced knowledge in project management methodologies and ability to develop/manage a longer term plan (3 to 5 years).

    -Experience with Implementing Agile maturity improvements and ability to showcase improvements via metrics.

    -Advanced knowledge of Agile principles and ability to influence the application throughout the organization.

    -Advanced knowledge in all Security projects for entire product space (ability to articulate and understand).

    -Experience providing oversight to scrum master and product team. Work with Portfolio leadership to assist with escalations, removing of roadblocks and managing priorities.

    -The ability to develop strong relationship with customer, technical SME's and leadership.

    -Experience with Governance and/or driving delivery of multiple medium to high complexity projects within a program or a program with cross-functional teams working on projects with multiple external dependencies and various methodologies.

    -Ability to support multiple product/scrum and/or development teams.

    -Ability to confidently present on product space at any time with little to no advance notice.
