    People at Cuebiq

    • Cuebiq is Italy's only true Big Data product company: we gather, analyze, engineer and visualize 4 billion GPS positions per day, with the mission of providing unique business insights and offline analytics to our clients through our SAAS Platform, Clara.


    We are an élite team of young, passionate, talented and friendly data professionals. As our Big Data Engineer you will design, implement and maintain processes and algorithms for ingesting, analyzing and serving large volumes of geolocation data.


    Hence, you (our ideal candidate) are an Engineer seeking new challenges: nowhere else in Italy will you be given the opportunity to solve problems on such a Big (Data) scale! On top of this, you must be delivery-oriented and with a strong technical background.


    Responsibilities:


    • Build and maintain pipelines and architectures for stream and batch processes;
    • Collaborate with data architects and data scientists to design and build analytic systems that are correct, scalable, reliable, and accurate;
    • Analyze large datasets to allow for data driven design;
    • Understand product and target use cases;


    Required skills:


    • 2-3 years (or more) of experience developing products in the data/analytics/graphing space
    • Deep understanding of data structures, algorithms, distributed systems, machine learning, and statistical methods
    • Expert-level knowledge of Java and the JVM platform (further languages running on JVM are welcome)
    • Experience with version control systems such as Git, SVN, Mercurial. We use Git.
    • At ease with industry standard software development tools (IDEs, etc.)


    We’re also looking for:


    • A hunger for coding
    • The ability to say no — and to explain why
    • Excellent English (we work a lot with the New York office)
    • Experience working in a distributed organization (again, New York!)
    • Excellent people skills, with a positive and collaborative approach
    • Results driven mentality


    The perks we offer:


    • If you’re like us, we know you’ll enjoy having a generous learning budget; smart working; a super casual office environment with a fully stocked kitchen, games, lounge area
    • You’ll get to travel with us: around Milan, with our free yearly ATM pass and, maybe, back and forth from our New York office
    • Plus the serious stuff your parents and your family will ask about: health insurance for yourself and next of kin.


    About Cuebiq:


    Cuebiq is a leading consumer insights and measurement company leveraging the largest database of accurate and precise location data in the U.S. Its data intelligence platform analyzes anonymous location patterns to allow businesses to glean actionable insights and better understand the offline consumer journey. Cuebiq’s SaaS platform provides clients offline location analytics, real-time audience optimization, cross-channel footfall attribution, as well as geo-behavioral audiences for cross-platform ad targeting. Cuebiq is headquartered in New York with offices in San Francisco, Chicago, Italy, and China.

    Cuebiq is also a leader in utilizing data and insights in the service of a variety of causes through its “Data for Good” initiative. The company shares location intelligence with researchers at top universities and nonprofit organizations to drive innovation for causes such as improving quality of life in underserved communities, natural disaster response, and smart city development. Visit Cuebiq at www.cuebiq.com.
