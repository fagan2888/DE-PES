ALTEN Polska Wrocław, PL

Aplikuj w witrynie firmy
We are member of French ALTEN Group present in 25 countries around the world and employing over 34,000 engineers and IT specialists. Since 1988 we deliver advanced IT systems for well-known brands, develop medicine and the renewable energy industry. ALTEN innovates aero and cosmonautics, trains, electric and autonomous vehicles, and even space rockets.

We are currently looking fora Data Engineer who will join our team.

Main tasks

Building and running Azure based Data Platform

Handling multiple Terabytes of data with focus on IoT data using Steam and Batch processing

Developing and maintaining analytical applications and services

Processing raw data and developing complex data models

Building scalable data pipelines using technologies from the Microsoft Azure Cloud

Implementing, running and deploying algorithms using the Hadoop ecosystem (e.g. Spark, Hive, HDFS) and the Microsoft Azure Stack

Working within a team of experienced data engineers and scientists

Requirements

Experience in building and maintaining Azure Big Data architectures and Enterprise Analytics Solutions

Experience with optimizing data pipelines, architectures and data sets

Advanced hands-on experience with SQL, TSQL, Python, pySpark, Hive, HDFS

Experience with Design and Architecture of relational SQL and NoSQL databases

Knowledge of DevOps Processes

Knowledge of Data Quality issues

Fluent in English or German language will be considered as a plus

We offer

Stable and long-term cooperation

Certification and training opportunities

Possibility to choose type of contract (B2B also)

Benefit package (MultiSport card, Medicover, MyBenefit)

Unlimited growth and development opportunity

Relocation support & relocation package

Do not hesitate and join our team!
