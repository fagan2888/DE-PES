Data Center Engineer

Huxley Singapore, SG

1 month ago

Apply on company website
Responsibilities Include But Will Not Be Limited To

The Data Center Engineer will be involved in overall infrastructure management of mission critical facilities.
  • Providing hardware support such as for power equipment (PDUs), multi-vendor transport and network equipment including devices from Cisco, F5, Juniper et al
  • Maintenance and support for all data center hardware and cabling as well as maintain accurate inventory of equipment and spares
  • Perform system changes during the maintenance window in a fast-paced and highly visible environment.
  • Execute technical troubleshooting to resolve issues within the data center such as for performance optimization, hardware failure, network issues, automation, et al


To Be Successful In This Role, One Must Have
  • At least 8 years of infrastructure experience and at least 5 years in a data center environment (large scale)
  • Knowledge of Linux and hardware systems support
  • Knowledge of data center functions and technologies
  • Exposure of enterprise level networking and storage equipment installs
