Senior Data Engineer We are currently seeking a Senior Data Engineer with experience in the design, development, and implementation of ETL solutions, data modeling, and construction of business intelligence and analytics solutions, including database design, core data architecture, and report/dashboard design and development. Candidates will have extensive interaction with front line business team members, and will be tasked with the analysis of business data and requirements, and the transformation of those requirements into data analytics solutions to support the business strategy of the Exchange Group. Strong written and oral communication skills are essential and candidates should be self-directed and motivated to work in a fast paced, team-oriented environment. Major responsibilities include:  Requirements gathering, design, development, and implementation of business intelligence solutions which can be leveraged by end-users  Hands-on development of ETL, internal/external data feeds and reporting solutions  Design and development of data models, reports and visualization and analytics solutions needed to support dynamic business intelligence capabilities  Support of critical production systems, including, when necessary, off-hours support Location: Princeton, NJ Organizational Structure: The Sr. Data Engineer will report directly to the Sr. VP of Business Systems Development. Qualifications:  Extensive experience with a business intelligence platform such as the Business Objects suite including Web Intelligence, Design Studio and Lumira, or other similar BI solutions  Experience working independently with users to define BI concepts, information needs and functional requirements, specifications and database models to determine appropriate data warehouse, ETL and BI report development solutions  Extraction and analysis of data, patterns, and related trends as needed, with the subsequent ability to synthesize the data into information consumable by the organization  Experience in database design and data modeling  Advanced knowledge of SQL scripting and working with relational database systems  Good knowledge of Linux/UNIX and shell scripting  Experience with very large data warehouse systems is a plus  Well versed in industry standard architecture design methodologies and processes  Strong written and oral communication skills  Self-directed and motivated to work in a fast paced team-oriented environment Education and Experience:  Minimum of a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Computer Science or Engineering  Five plus years of experience using business intelligence and ETL technologies to build largescale data analytics solutions  Financial industry experience is a plus
