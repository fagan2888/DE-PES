Data Engineer

Copenhagen, Denmark

500,000- 600,000 DKK

This role a Data Engineer gives you the opportunity to work for one of the fastest growing start-ups in Denmark. As the company are looking to be fully data driven they want to hire another Data Engineer to be responsible for the Data Infrastructure

The Company

This online retailer has experienced a huge growth the last 3 years and is predicting this to continue in the future. The company offers the opportunity to be part of a fast-paced company with a relaxed atmosphere where you will be given a lot of responsibility and freedom

THE ROLE

As a Data Engineer the role will include
  • Writing well- structured codes in Java, R, Scala or Python
  • Responsibility for the Data Infrastructure
  • Building real time batch Data pipelines
  • ETL and prepare data for future analysis by Data Scientists

Your Skills And Experience
  • Hand on commercial experience working with SQL
  • Strong experience with ETL tools
  • Preferably experience with Big Data tools (Hadoop, Spark etc.)
  • Experience with Cloud-based platform is a plus
  • Speak and write English, Danish not a requirement

How To Apply

Please register your interest by sending your CV to Chantelle Mynhardt via the Apply link on this page

KEYWORDS

Data Engineer/ Big Data/ Start-up/ Retail/ Scala/ Java/ Python/ R/ SQL/ ETL
