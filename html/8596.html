Plant Engineering Automation Specialist

UPS L'Hospitalet de Llobregat, ES

Hace 2 días

Solicitar en el sitio web de la empresa
#monpost

In our organization, the Plant Engineering Specialist will be reporting to the Plan Engineering Automation Manager. The Specialist will be responsible for building and sorting systems. With our team, we ensure the highest maintenance standards of the conveyor systems in order to keep the reliability at highest level, minimizing any downtimes and ensuring a safe work environment within the UPS buildings.



Please note that you will have to work the following shifts on a monthly based rotation:

4:00am – 13:00pm

12:30pm – 21:30pm

Your Daily Business
  • As a PE Specialist, you execute preventive and corrective maintenance actions (electro-mechanical as well as electrical) on the automated conveyor system.
  • You keep track of spare parts inventory and are capable of working closely with colleagues and subcontractors to ensure a high level of service to our internal customers.
  • For our new to be constructed, automated sorting facility in Barcelona (Plan opening date: April 2020), our department needs highly motivated, technically educated technicians who are willing to work in shifts (day, night, morning).
  • In order to be a good fit for your work, you will need to have knowledge of PLC’s and experience (at least 1 year) with maintenance and repairs of automated conveyor systems

The Requirements
  • Electromechanical - vocational degree. Electrical or Mechanical Engineering degree will be valued.
  • Computer skills and knowledge of the MS Office software package.
  • You should have good troubleshooting capabilities and be able to solve complex technical issues.
  • You need to be able to organize, plan and be able to be in the team and lead them.
  • Good communication skills in English
  • Driving licence
  • Good knowledge of the HMI/PLC systems
  • Willing to travel

What We Offer
  • Dynamic and diverse job
  • Options to develop and grow within the company.
  • Competitive salary
  • Health Insurance
  • The opportunity to participate in the construction of a new project
