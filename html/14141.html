Data Lake DevOps Engineer (AZURE)

We are looking for a DevOps Engineer in a Data Lake team. The role requires experience in Build, Deployment, and Configuration management.

Your Main Responsibilities Will Be
  • Build and test automation tools for infrastructure provisioning, data pipelines and machine learning deployment
  • Handle code deployments in all environments
  • Collaborate with the security and infrastructure teams to comply with the standards
  • Monitor metrics and develop ways to improve
  • Maintain day-to-day management and administration of projects
  • Improve performance and maximize compute resource utilization
  • Provide technical guidance and educate team members and coworkers on development and operations
  • Brainstorm for new ideas and ways to improvement development delivery
  • Working in a distributed Agile team

For Success You Need
  • Strong experience with implementation of CI/CD pipelines in Azure DevOps (or other similar tools).
  • Strong experience with Azure, git, PowerShell, Python.
  • Some experience with ETL pipelines, SQL and NoSQL databases, Azure services (Azure Data Factory, Azure Databricks, Azure ML, Storages, VMs)
  • Detail-oriented analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent verbal and written English communication skills
  • Open to occasional business travels
We offer you:
  • Opportunity to be part of a rapidly expanding global organization with irreproachable reputation
  • Opportunity to grow both professionally and personally
  • Learning opportunities (Pluralsight, learning projects)
  • Inspiring working atmosphere and many engaging activities
  • Clear career path and extra benefits
  • Attractive compensation package: salary (2000 - 4200 EUR gross/ monthly) depending on competences you have

We will be looking for Your CV!
