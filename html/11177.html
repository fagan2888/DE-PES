    • This key role will own and lead the strategy and execution of our Ultrasound IT offerings across Europe, in close collaboration with all Segments involved. This person will lead a team of U/S IT Specialists to drive sales of Ultrasound IT all over Europe through direct teams and indirect partners. Furthermore, this role will ensure a strong and effective marketing mix across the European region. The role reports directly to the General Manager GE Healthcare Ultrasound Europe.


    Your opportunity:


    The General Manager Ultrasound IT (U/S IT) Europe will interact frequently with a variety of sales, marketing service and upstream people inside and outside our market leading Ultrasound business (at Country, Europe and Global level). He/she will help accelerate market growth across the different customer segments in Europe to strengthen our position as market leader in U/S!


      • LEADERSHIP: Leading a 15MM USD business with a team of Sales, Application and Installation Specialists in a sales region across Europe. Select, develop, and motivate teams to optimize effectiveness. Remote leadership across Europe
      • Coaching direct teams across different countries as well as coaching indirect regional teams and stakeholders
      • Developing and implementing sales strategies to drive organic growth in the Ultrasound IT Sales market
      • Cultivating and leveraging customer relationships at the executive level
      • Development of long-term customer relationships through the ability to identify and capitalize on opportunities that immediately satisfy customer needs
      • VALUE PROPOSITION: Work with Regional, European and Global teams to formulate and cascade targeted value propositions by customer type/segment, adapted by country as needed, and in sync with the different U/S Segments and channels involved
      • DEEP MARKET UNDERSTANDING: Leverage market research to strengthen our understanding of the different markets and segments, and to identify market and adjacent opportunities
      • EVENTS: Proactively and actively support –and often lead/coordinate- key events such as congresses, educational seminars/courses, etc, while ensuring marketing consistency
      • INDIRECT DISTRIBUTION: Maintain a close relationship with existing distributors and actively help to build new distribution channels
      • TRAINING: active role in defining and driving internal field training initiatives
      • MARKET NEEDS: Consolidate & develop qualitative + quantitative insights about customers across segments and regions
      • COMPETITIVE: Consolidate, integrate and communicate competitive intelligence as appropriate
      • METRICS: analyze and monitor key marketing metrics and develop/execute specific actions where needed to improve performance
      • PROJECT Management and SUPPORT: Support the European regions to develop and drive U/S IT Enterprise projects


    Your profile:
    • Proven IT Sales Experience
    • Strong leadership skills
    • People Leader experience
    • Open mindset with experience in growth businesses
    • Prior experience working for a company in a matrix environment with multi-cultural teams
    • Demonstrated ability to energize, develop, and build rapport at all levels within an organization
    • Strong project management skills
    • Strong interpersonal and influencing skills
    • Ability to present content to all kinds of audiences with credibility and a strong commercial acumen
    • Significant travelling across Europe is required
    • Fluent English and German in speaking and writing

    What is highly desired but not mandatory is:

    • Experience of leading pan-European teams remotely
    • Third European language
