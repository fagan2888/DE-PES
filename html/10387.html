About Us

MediaMath’s mission is to help innovative marketers delight their customers and drive real business outcomes. Over 9,500 marketers in 42 countries use our enterprise solution to reach the right user with the right message for the right price, wherever they are. These are the leading brands and agencies in the world who rely on MediaMath to provide technology, services and leadership to help them continue to transform their marketing practices and compete in a quickly changing industry. Founded in 2007 as a pioneer in “programmatic” advertising, MediaMath has been recognized as a leader in the space in four independent evaluations over the last two years. MediaMath has offices in 16 cities worldwide and is headquartered in New York City.

Our digital advertising platform leverages massive amounts of data using AI and machine learning principles, enabling the quickest and most intelligent online ad space bidding in real time, 24/7. We leverage both public cloud and eight managed data centers around the world to maximize the return on every marketing dollar spent by our customers.

Key Responsibilities

This role sits within our Common Technology Services (CTS) team who work up and down the stack. This team uses best in class technology to develop low-latency, high-volume, secure, reliable, and scalable services. As a Senior Software Engineer, you will architect and own one or more of the CTS team’s core big data services utilized by other MediaMath engineering departments.

You Will
  • Design, develop, document, test, and debug new and existing software systems and/or applications in Java, Scala and/or Go
  • Act as technical lead for and own applications/services which are managed by our team
  • Participate in full development life cycle including requirements analysis and design
  • Write technical specifications based on conceptual design and stated business requirements
  • Develop and maintain thorough understanding of products and infrastructure; become subject matter expert
  • Collaborative on feature-based development, code reviews, and releases
  • Contribute to resource planning and continual process improvements
  • Mentor your peer software engineers
  • Ensure that applications/services adhere to quality, security, and resiliency standards, raising the bar when possible


You Have
  • BS/MS/PhD in computer science, or another STEM field paired with strong computer science fundamentals
  • 7+ years of experience in software engineering, or demonstrated equivalent experience and knowledge, writing bulletproof Java, Scala and/or Go
  • Experience with large databases, streaming data and streamline data processing, including Kafka, PostgreSQL, Flink, Impala, Kudu and/or Spark/Hadoop
  • Experience with on prem and cloud (AWS) computing
  • Prior experience as a technical lead of a SaaS system
  • Ability to scale, troubleshoot, and fix applications/services
  • Public GitHub repos with plenty of goodies that other software engineers will love


You Are
  • Someone who contributes to the open source software community
  • Passionate about technology and continuously hone your craft
  • A technologist, problem solver, and team player
  • A great communicator with and influencer
  • Hungry to learn and spread knowledge by teaching others
