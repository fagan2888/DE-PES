Description

Do you enjoy solving real-world problems with emerging technologies? Do you have a solid combination of theoretical (computational complexity, statistics and stochastic systems) and practical (web services, modern development frameworks) computer science skills? Are you driven to help customers solve their problems and take advantage of new opportunities. If you share our passion for the challenges of big scale, building cutting edge technologies, developing distributed applications that are easy to use and reliable, and forging the path on how serverless development is done in the cloud, then we may just have a role for you.

How do you know if you are a good fit for our team? You look at really hard problems as an exciting challenge, not a daunting task. You think about all the ways something could go wrong in solving these problems and see around the corners. Since the team shares your passion for building cool stuff, you will collaborate with your teammates closely and share data-driven perspectives you bring to all the problems we face. When our customers ask for the next big feature, you get jazzed because you know this means you’ll be able to truly dig into the solution and get it done right.

We are looking for a senior software engineer to drive feature development for a new AWS service. As a technical leader, you will set the technical direction for the team, drive design, coding and operational standards, mentor junior team members, and deliver designs and code for our most challenging projects. Partnering closely with the development manager and product manager, you will define the product roadmap and ensure it delights our customers.

Amazon has a fast-paced environment where we Work Hard, Have Fun, and Make History. You'll be surrounded by people who are smart, passionate about serving customers, and excited to come to work.


Basic Qualifications
  • Bachelors or Masters Degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 8+ years professional experience in software development
  • Proficiency in Java and Python or other OOP languages such as C#, Ruby, or C++
  • Experience in object-oriented design, data structures, algorithms, and problem solving
  • Experience designing and building sophisticated fault-tolerant distributed systems
  • Understanding of web services technologies such as SOAP, HTTP, WSDL, XSD, and REST
  • Strong understanding of system and application performance characteristics
  • Experience leading development life cycle process and best practices
  • Experience mentoring/training the engineering community on complex technical issues.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, sense of ownership, urgency and drive
Preferred Qualifications
  • MS or PhD in Computer Science
  • 10+ years software development experience
  • Experience developing on AWS
