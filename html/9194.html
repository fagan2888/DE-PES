ExxonMobil Curitiba, BR

Há 5 dias 45 candidaturas

Job Role Summary

The IT Support Analyst will provide technical support to end users on various technical issues and problems relating to both in-house and vendor software. Responsible for documenting and resolving service tickets in a timely manner, according to the Service Level Agreement (SLA).

Responsibilities
  • Monitoring Incidents/Work Orders queue and provide user problem resolution in a global environment
  • Identifying/proposing fixes and automation opportunities
  • Work with experienced team members, in order to troubleshoot, debug and upgrade existing software, identifying ideas to improve system performance and impact availability.
  • Providing proactive communication regarding system outages, issues or workarounds
  • Participating in developing/updating functional specifications for programming changes
  • Collaborate with other teams to develop solutions or complete changes/fixes
  • Developing/updating documentation (System and user)
  • Integrate software components and third-party programs. Independently install, customize and integrate commercial software packages.
Proficiencies

Key Skills Required
  • Troubleshooting
  • Communication with global team and business contacts
  • Flexibility
  • Some database (SQL) experience
  • Good English verbal & written communication skills
  • Ability to learn new technologies
Desired
  • Knowledge of modern technologies such as APIs and Cloud.
  • Developer experience (Java, C/C++, PowerShell, etc.)
  • Agile team experience / familiarity
