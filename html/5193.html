Senior Data Engineer- AWS, Python, Spark REQUIRED

Top Tier Technology Company Washington D.C. Metro Area

1 day ago

We are looking for a dynamic Senior Big Data/AWS Engineer to start onsite in DC asap. Strong Python and Spark required in Production, with AWS and Big Data.

REQUIRED:


• Over 5+ years of hands-on experience in a software development using Python, Spark and AWS

• Experienced in cloud data platforms tools and softwares

• Must have strong background & hands-on experience working on BI, Data warehouse, ETL projects as well as DWH appliances

• Experience with technologies like Hadoop, Python, Spark, Hive, Presto, NiFi, and Luigi

• Experience with the AWS ecosystem

• Data modeling experience preferred

• Scala or Java experience preferred

• 3+ years of experience using relational databases such as PostgreSQL, SQL, NoSQL databases

• Experience using ETL tools and designing large volume data processing

• Working understanding of CI/CD tools like Jenkins

• Ability to solution and use wide variety of open source technologies.

• Experience in implementing and consuming Web Service technologies/APIs (SOAP, REST, JSON). • Experience leading development teams and planning, directing and completing work without direct management
• A working understanding of scheduling and orchestration with tools such as: kubernetes, or airflow or equivalent.

• Experience working on a team operating under an Agile Scrum delivery methodology

• Excellent analytical skills and the ability to provide alternative solutions to technical problems

• Strong written and verbal communication skills • Possess strong leadership skills and a self-starter




For immediate consideration,email resume with home location, first and last name as well as contact information.
