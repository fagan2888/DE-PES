About the Role:
This is an exciting opportunity to work in Karratha as a residential employee enjoying the benefits of working in a growing & welcoming community highly supported by Woodside, you'll contribute significantly to the hub's campaign growth, work with innovative LNG technologies & contribute to the overall success of the community, hub & Woodside growth & success. The Senior Process Control Engineer will be responsible for developing and implementing process control strategies to improve operational efficiency, troubleshooting issues related to process control, undertake work planning and budgeting for specific work scopes & ensure modifications follow the management of change process. In addition the role will be responsible for preparing scopes of work for minor/medium CAPEX/OPEX work, justify current and future work programs as well as day to day duties you'll work with a close team & graduates & will have the opportunity to mentor & coach junior team members.


Duties & Responsibilities:
As the successful senior process control engineer you'll be expected to Develop and implement process control strategies to improve operational efficiency, troubleshoot issues related to Process Control, undertake rresponsibility for optimal performance of Base Layer Controllers, ensure all modifications follow the Management of Change process and undertake work planning and budgeting for specific work scopes. In addition to this the successful candidate will be responsible for preparing scopes of work for minor/medium CAPEX/OPEX work in sufficient detail to enable effective outsourcing of activities, manage service providers, manage expenditure of approved budget items within the discipline scope of work, lead incident investigations & act as a focal point junior colleagues & graduates.


Skills & Experience:
The successful candidate will ideally have a minimum of 10 years Process Control experience, preferably in the Oil and Gas industry, experience in loop tuning, experience in design, implementation & commissioning of base layer control schemes and experience in implementation and maintenance of Advanced Process Control (Model Based Control) technology - preferably with Honeywell Profit Suite software. In addition to the above the ideal candidate will have good programming skills, knowledge of Honeywell Control Systems (TPN as well as Experion) good knowledge of best practices, standards, systems and processes in their discipline. Furthermore the ideal candidate will be able to work autonomously particularly with investigations, deviation considerations, resolving plant issues & will have good communication skills & an ability to influence others, manage priorities effectively, provide advice and expertise to others. The ideal candidate will have a strong team based focus, assisting in peer checks and upskilling others as well as an adept ability to identify and assess engineering risk as well experience in work planning, execution and cost / budget management.
