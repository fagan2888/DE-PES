    Senior Recruiter at Oracle

    • The role is part of NetSuite organization – aquired by Oracle in 2016.


    At NetSuite , we believe the cloud is here to stay and so do our 40,000+ customers. We believe businesses should not be bogged down by the overhead of bulky data centers and expensive personnel to run it all. Businesses need to be lean, efficient and agile.


    NetSuite is literally transforming business around the globe by providing a cloud-based, unified system that delivers unprecedented capabilities to drive business forward. Founded in 1998 as THE cloud ERP pioneer, today NetSuite has transformed the business operations of our customers without the high costs and inefficiency of on premise systems.

    Now we are searching for a Senior Database Engineer to join our team in Prague.

    Job Description:

    • To analyze database performance and suggest solutions for particular problems of the NetSuite system
    • Close cooperation with database developers responsible for developing new code in database per propose of database analyst
    • Performance tuning
    • The job is for someone who does not want to write and own a ton of code. In this job it does not generally entail writing large features. The ability to pinpoint problems is more important than the ability to apply patterns to a business problem

    Qualification:

    • Strong knowledge of Oracle database and PL/SQL is a must
    • Experience with performance tuning
    • Analytical thinking. Person who is able to see problems in a broader way
    • English on a solid level (the team is spread around the world, you would have other colleagues in US and Australia and you would take part in regular update calls with them)


    What do we offer:

    • Very friendly company culture - no dress code, informal culture
    • Opportunity to be a part of new team with a startup atmosphere (NetSuite), backed by a strong enterprise (Oracle).
    • Time flexibility - no core hours when you have to be in the office. We do not mind you come at 7am or 11am.
    • Modern office - You will not regret a visit to our office. We have a fitness room, chill out zones with table football, ping/pong, play station, etc., a big terrace with a great view towards downtown, free fruit delivered twice a week, etc.
    • Great team, benefits, and a competitive salary - Oracle provides the benefits you would expect to get from an international company (extra holidays, paid sick leave, meal vouchers, flexi passes, English lessons within working hours and many others), but that goes without saying. We are searching for candidates who want to work on a great team. Competitive salary and nice benefits are a matter of course.
