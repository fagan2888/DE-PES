    Location : Mundhwa, Pune

    About Client :

    Our client delivers extreme automation to start-ups and agile enterprises through a combination of products, software, scripting, and workflow automation. They are headquartered in Pune and have offices in Singapore and the US.

    Job Overview:

    A basic to an expert level understanding of data visualization having worked on any such tools like Tableau, Data studio. A good amount of exposure to machine learning algorithms with implementation in practice is preferred.

    Requirements & Duties:

    • Solid background on building data pipelines from scratch to produce queryable datasets
    • Decent knowledge of any programming language like Python/Java or any other scripting language.
    • Excellent understanding of building data models for the target data warehouse
    • Must have an inherent talent of understanding data and its interpretation for end business users
    • A clear understanding of distributed computing, especially in databases, having worked on such databases extensively
    • Excellent hands-on in SQL with an in-depth understanding of query optimization
    • Good hands-on in any of the cloud platforms having a thorough understanding of the cloud concepts are required.
    • A good understanding of Big data technologies like Spark/Hadoop is a preferred
    • Decent communication skills to be able to express one’s point to client concisely

    Qualification:

    • Bachelor's / Master’s in computer science.

    • Bleeding Edge Technology- Most of their projects involve new tech as well. Elevate your skills by learning from seriously smart people and working with some of the hottest startups and largest companies in the world. P.S.- They even pay for you to go get certifications and training.
