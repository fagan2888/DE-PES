RPA/Automation Analyst

Honeywell Brno, CZ

Před 3 měsíci

Zažádat na webu společnosti
Join a team recognized for leadership, innovation and diversity.

Join a company that is transforming from a traditional industrial company to a contemporary digital industrial business, harnessing the power of cloud, big data, analytics, Internet of Things, AI/ML (Machine Learning), Automation and design thinking. You will support change that brings value to our customers, partners, and shareholders through the creation of innovative software and data-driven products and services. You will help engineer contemporary applications and services, constructing solutions that remain scalable, adaptable and replicable. Join our team where we will be transforming Honeywells IT organization through the delivery of technology products that will directly impact the companys growth.

As part of the Automation and Cognitive Services Technology group you will work on the design and implementation of various transformative and critical Digital Agents, AI Cognitive Services, ML/Deep Learning improved business use cases. This Analyst role will work with the Automation Delivery team, IT business partners, Functional consultants, and infrastructure/technology teams to meet customer requirements. This is a core developer role focused primarily on delivering Digital Agents (AI Chatbots), Process Automation with Cognitive Services, NLP/ML with an additional focus on innovation and new technologies implementation. You will be responsible for the end-end delivery of automation/AI use cases from pilot/design to release.

Design, Build and Deploy Digital Agents, Intelligent Process Automation, AI/ML use cases using leading Automation/AI delivery platforms.

Work on developing/training ML Models and API’s and enhancing existing process with cognitive capabilities.

Work in a startup-type environment to design and build innovative applications using Automation, AI Cognitive Service, ML from functional proofs-of-concepts to production ready scalable solutions.

You Have

  • Minimum 2 years of experience in software development using Python and Node.js for large corporates/organizations
  • Bachelors or Master’s Degree in Computer Science or Engineering fields
  • Minimum 1 year development experience in Chat Bot platforms/tools, preferably Microsoft bot framework
  • Minimum 1 year experience in building and deploying ML models using libraries such as scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch, MXNet, etc
  • Minimum 1 year experience in building IT use cases / solutions based on Cloud infrastructure and services, such as AWS
  • and/or Azure cloud platforms.
We Value

  • Development experience in RPA Tools & Platform & Implementations: Examples - UiPath, Automation Anywhere and other leading RPA platform vendors
  • 1 years’ experience in Web services/Restful APIs
  • Experience in ERP Integration and preferably with SAP platforms
  • Working Experience in an Agile/Scrum/Scaled Agile and DevOps based team environment
  • Certifications Process Automation Area / AI / ML
If you believe this is the right path for you, then wed love to hear from you.
