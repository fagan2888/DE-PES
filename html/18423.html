    • Matrax is one of the leading providers of sleep goods in Romania & Bulgaria with over 1000 points of sales, led by the belief that good sleep leads to a good life. We are a fast-growing company, driven by a modern approach to innovation, and guided by 15 years of experience and in-depth business know-how. Our philosophy is to offer the highest quality sleep products, do business with honesty and integrity, and deliver on our promises to customers & partners

    We promote & distribute two brands in Romania:

    • Magniflex : a leading global brand in luxury sleep and comfort goods with 60-year history and presence in 100 countries. Its strategy is product leadership: developing new and superior products through differentiation and innovation. Its mission is to give people better sleep and better life.
    • iSleep : Great products of uncompromising quality at an accessible price point achieved through best in class materials & technology, combined with smart production at leading Italian & Portuguese factories

    Our team is unmatched in product and market expertise, commitment and integrity and is constantly trying to break new ground and set higher standards. We are currently looking for a talented, dynamic and passionate professional to play a key role in our sales team and help us execute our vision of becoming the region’s leading sleep goods supplier.


    JUNIOR SALES RESEARCHER


    Your mission is to help Matrax become the absolute leader in sleep goods by achieving distribution in as many points of sales (shops) in Romania as possible. You will be investigating and uncovering new potential leads for our sales team building a pipeline of furniture shops, design showrooms, architects, online retailers, and others where we can showcase and sell our products. You will leave no stone unturned to search for new retail partners, in order for your colleagues to be able to successfully make the first contact with them and convince them to list our products.


    Daily responsibilities:

    • Dive into retailer websites, facebook pages, industry platforms & databases and similar sites to discover potential new customers
    • Track new potential clients and process data into the CRM system for your colleagues in the Sales team
    • Call retailers to find out whether they sell bedroom & sleep products
    • Proactively contribute to suggest/find potential new lead sources
    • Occasionally go on field trips to different cities to find offline leads
    • Trigger the potential new clients by sending out interesting emails
    • Contact the store owners via phone or email to introduce our collaboration to them
    • Convince them to schedule a meeting with one of your sales colleagues to hear more
    • Plan the route for your colleagues in the field
    • Join the Field Sales Representatives on some of their appointments to support and understand the entire Sales cycle
    • Keep your KPIs/sales targets in mind


    Your profile:

    This is the ideal job to kick-off your career! Are you a recent graduate or want to work full time next to your studies? Do you want to be flexible and work either full-time or part-time in this position? We are happy to hear from you!

    • Recent graduate or student

    • Native level of Romanian and good level of English
    • You know how to use MS Excel and Google Drive
    • Good research skills
    • Planning & organization skills
    • Attention to detail

    • Flexible working hours and informal work atmosphere with a young and highly motivated team
    • Competitive salary and benefits package
    • Chance to contribute to improving people’s lives through a product directly related to health and quality of life
    • An opportunity to work in a successful, high-growth environment with opportunities for professional and personal development
    • Possibility to work part-time or full-time in this position
