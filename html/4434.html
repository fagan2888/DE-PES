Job Summary

Wealth Management has become one of the fastest-growing areas in the global financial industry due to the increasing number of high net worth population and hence demands for the service. Phatra Securities is a leading onshore wealth manager with a total clients’ asset under advisory of over 500 billion baht. We serve our high net worth and institutional clients under PhatraWealth brand.

Data Engineer is part of the Data Analytics team in Investment Solutions Team. Data Analytics team provides expertise in data to better serve client needs (e.g. client segment, evaluation of service) and also provides tools and solutions to support business needs and growth. Responsibilities of Data Engineer include:

  • Create and maintain optimal data architecture
  • Work across departments to gather and understand data
  • Prepare data for predictive and prescriptive modeling
  • Identify, design and implement internal process to improve data reliability, efficiency, and quality (automating manual processes, optimizing data delivery, re-designing infrastructure for greater scalability, etc.)
  • Develop systems and tools to support new and fast-growing business
  • Extend new functionalities in the current systems to accommodate business needs

Qualifications:

  • High caliber Bachelor's/Master’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or other related fields
  • Knowledge of programming and experience working with data and software development
  • Working knowledge with SQL, C#, Java, Python, Javascript, or HTML will be advantageous
  • Demonstrate strong problem solving skills
  • Good team-player
  • Self-motivation
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
