Junior Process Development Engineer

The Lubrizol Corporation Montmeló, ES

Hace 3 días 31 solicitudes

Duties/Responsibilities
  • Plan, analyze, and safely define execution pilot plant extrusion line runs, including reactive TPU extrusion.
  • Foster productive communication with Pilot Plant operations staff and lead by example in a coaching/mentoring environment.
  • Foster professional relationships with Business partners including: Sales and Marketing, Product Management, Synthesis, Application, and Technology Services.
  • Be comfortable with routine customer and supplier interactions while reinforcing Lubrizol’s reputation and protecting Intellectual Property.
  • Support Estane UOI growth by practicing and advocating the use of business processes including: Pilot Plant Request (PPR) system, Manufacturing Authorization (MA) process, Product and Portfolio Management Process (PPMP), and other related processes.
  • Actively participate in and lead by example in plant and corporate HSE initiatives including: Department safety meetings, and environmental initiatives.
  • Participate in root cause analysis and ad-hoc incident investigations where necessary
  • Support pilot line reliability improvements and commitment to continuous improvement by supporting capital (CAPEX) improvements in Montmeló Pilot Plant.
  • Continuously improve processes and capabilities by enhancing work flow using statistical analysis (DOE) and implementation of improved control strategies.
  • Organize and lead process development projects and communicate/document outcomes.

Skills, Qualifications, Experience, Special Physical Requirements
  • Minimum of a Bachelor's degree in Chemistry and additional Master degree in Polymers.
  • 2 years of manufacturing operations experience, OR participation in the Lubrizol Co-Op program
  • Strong fundamental knowledge in chemical engineering principals with proven problem-solving abilities
Strong “hands-on” mechanical and chemical process engineering skills
