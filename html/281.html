Engineer, Network Systems
Luxembourg - Betzdorf

Internal Job Ad Description.
This position primarily supports the preparation of technical proposals in response to international tenders and contributes or lead the architecture, the design, specification, implementation and improvement of the communication, networking and ground segment system required for the satellite and hybrid communication systems.

Key activities include the collection of SES customer requirements, the engineering of communications systems, the preparation of technical studies, reports, specifications, contractual documentation and operational guidelines and the coordination of small multidisciplinary and multicultural project technical teams while working with SES internal and external customers and partners.

The incumbent is responsible for attracting new projects for SES in cooperation with the Sales team; he/she will contribute to or lead the preparation of tenders and proposals.

Primary Responsibilities / Key Result Areas
  • Deliver multi-disciplinary projects and services to international customers within the agreed budget, specification and schedule, meaning:
    • Design, engineer and scale netwrk environments that meet customer expectations for scalability and security and help SES expand its service offering by leveraging appropriate technologies and standards
    • Engineer, implement and maintain cmprehensive and consistent security solutions to mitigate identified risks to an acceptable level
    • Perfrm technical security assessments of systems and applications to identify vulnerabilities and non-compliance with established security standards and recommend effective mitigation strategies
    • Assess system and applicatin security requirements, threats, vulnerabilities and security risks in complex, heterogeneous systems and throughout their life cycle
    • Supprt the definition of policies, processes, procedures, configuration baselines and guidelines to ensure appropriate security risk management throughout the system life cycle
    • Design, specify, and implement netwrk solutions, comprised of VSAT, Earth stations, Base stations, communication systems and software systems
    • Perfrm transmission analysis, in particular link budget calculations, and studies in support of business development and customer services
    • Prepare the cmmercial documentation such as system requirements specifications, technical system specifications, operational procedure, cost estimations and contractual documents in coordination with other internal and external parties
    • Perfrm system design, handle budgets, handle procurements, manage installations and the operational handover of ground segment and communications systems
    • In the scpe of the project, coordinate with other functions (Project Control, Legal, Finance) for the transparent and effective realization of assigned project
    • In cordination with the Service Management team, prepare operational procedures and ensure the second Level Customer Support for the delivered system
    • Interface with external partners (e.g. custmers, NGO’s, Government bodies, investment partners, vendors/providers, shareholders, etc.)
  • Take an active part in SES Services’ proposal activities through the provision of Technical Specifications, Statement of Works (SOW’s) and Cost Estimations, in response to customer Requests For Information (RFI); customer Requests For Proposals (RFP), whereby the activities could include the set-up and supervision of small interdisciplinary proposal teams in function of the technical and commercial proposal preparation and the contract negotiation process
  • Proactively scopes the technical solution required to address customer requirements, assesses customers’ met and unmet needs, and recommends solutions that optimize value for both the customer and the firm
  • Awareness and evaluation of relevant technological developments, market trends, competitive factors, customer requirements, standardisation licensing and regulatory aspects

Competencies
  • Good understanding of project management (incl. budget management)
  • Ability to integrate into and/or lead cross-functional and/or virtual project teams
  • Proven written and oral communications skills
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • An innovative spirit and be business proactive
  • Proactive approach towards potential and/or current customers
  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Good conflict management skills in a multicultural and interdisciplinary set-up
  • Ability to work independently with minimal supervision and in a team

Qualification & Experience
  • Master Degree in Information and Communications Technology (ICT) and/or Telecommunications or equivalent
  • Minimum 5 years of applicable professional experience in network implementation and management
  • Systems engineering experience with communications from satellite-to-ground, air-to-ground and ground-to-ground
  • Sound knowledge in (satellite) communications, networks and systems, as well as data networking principles, and operational support systems
  • In-depth and practical knowledge of system security risk management, including system security risk assessment, specification of security requirements, definition of security concepts, design of secure systems, implementation of security controls, specification of secure configuration baselines, assessment of security controls and vulnerabilities
  • Experience in pre-sales engineering (e.g. technical proposal, cost estimation, facing customer) is considered as an asset
  • Proven project/budget and time management experience
  • Knowledge of performance, risk, cost, and schedule analysis
  • Sound knowledge with network design, deployment and operations, with emphasis on TCP/IP based networks running over different physical media
  • Familiar with UNIX/Linux Operating System, Virtualization platforms including VMware, Cloud Computing
  • Knowledge of IT practices and standards such as ITIL or DEVOPS frameworks
  • Solid knowledge of IT security threats, vulnerabilities, security technologies, controls and best practices
  • Fluent in English; proficiency in other languages is a plus (especially French)
  • Excellent written and oral communications skills
  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Willing to travel internationally

Key Requirements
  • NATO/EU SECRET clearances are considered a strong asset. Candidate must be willing to undergo a security clearance procedure as this position might require holding security clearance
