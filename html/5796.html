Are you a world person? Do you love the exotic and are you inspired by challenges?


If the answer is YES, welcome to Exoticca!!


At Exoticca we are looking for people with the desire to offer the best of themselves and to generate unforgettable experiences for our users.


We are a young, dynamic, multicultural team going through a period of international expansion, for which reason we are looking for new travel companions.


The Exoticca company was set up in the year 2013 by professionals specializing in the development and online distribution of travel.


Our mission is to provide our clients with the possibility of getting to know the most beautiful and exciting places on the planet, through the best travel offers on the market.


Features such as innovative design, easy usability, and intuitive navigation, together with values such as transparency, the pursuit of service, safety, respect and admiration for different world cultures, have turned Exoticca into the ideal showcase, thereby building customer loyalty to a high level.


What are we proposing?


Join us as a data engineer.


What will be your challenge?


Build the company’s next steps towards data maturity.


What do we ask of you?


Make decisions about data infrastructure and technologies needed.


Analyze business requirements and translating them into a good technical data design.


Partner with Tech Product Owners and other Data Engineers to refine business requirements.


Support, develop and maintain technical specification documentation.


Constantly strive to improve data accuracy and organization.


Experience required


Proven background of ETL development, including data warehousing and reporting architectures.


Experience working with document databases MySQL and MongoDB.


Working knowledge of Qlik (preferably) or other data visualization tools.


What do we offer you?


Competitive compensation depending on experience and skills


Flexible working schedule


Permanent contract


Exclusive location in the center of Barcelona


Package advantages: free fruit, flexible hours, continuous learning and the ability to travel at reduced prices!


And most importantly... being part of a successful multicultural team!
