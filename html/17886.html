Position

We are looking for a forward-thinking and hardworking Data Engineer who craves to learn and deliver every single day. As a Data Engineer, you will join KAYAK's Programmatic Marketing team in our Berlin, Cambridge and Kaunas offices. You will be taking care of data parsing, analysing, investigating, interpreting, modelling, and extracting knowledge from large volumes of data. One of your main goals will stepping ahead to process data in order to help drive better business decisions in our fast-paced environment. If you love travelling and want to work on a team of similarly talented, driven individuals, we'd love to hear from you.

Responsibilities

  • Mastering extraction of data from databases, writing scripts to parse, clean, combine, and process them
  • Your goal will be to build dashboards and visualisations of processed data, identify trends, anomalies
  • Investigation of data problems, identification of patterns will be your top game
  • Drawing inferences and conclusions, communicating results through reports, charts, or tables
  • Defining and implementing KPIs in conjunction with the business team


Basic Qualifications

  • University degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or Math/Physics
  • Expertise of SQL and databases is a requirement, with Hive/Hadoop a plus
  • Excellent programming skills with at least one high-level language such as Python, Java, or C/C++
  • Good knowledge of data structures, algorithms, and statistics
  • Knowledge of Unix shell scripting is valued
  • Knowledge of Machine Learning would be a plus
  • Superb interpersonal skills in English (both spoken and written), additional languages are a plus
  • Relevant experience in similar role


Soft Skills

  • You are an outstanding teammate who loves to engage with colleagues at a professional and personal level
  • You can handle a high volume of requests with frequent interruptions and prioritise their work while taking infrastructure and business needs into account
  • You have an extraordinary technical communication skills


About KAYAK

Our mission at KAYAK is to help people experience the world through travel, and provide the world’s favorite travel planning tools. With every query, KAYAK searches other sites to show travelers the information they need to find the right flights, hotels, rental cars and vacation packages.

And because our team is always on the lookout looking for ways to make travel planning and trip management even easier, we offer a variety of tools and features such as KAYAK Trips, Explore and Price Forecast and are constantly evolving our app, Facebook Messenger and Slack bots, Alexa skill and related A.I. innovations.

In addition to KAYAK, we manage a portfolio of metasearch brands including: momondo, Cheapflights, SWOODOO, checkfelix and Mundi that together process more than two billion consumer queries a year.

KAYAK is an independently managed subsidiary of Booking Holdings.

As an employee of KAYAK, you will be part of a global network that includes OpenTable and KAYAK's portfolio of metasearch brands including momondo, Cheapflights, Swoodoo, checkfelix and Mundi. Many employees are dedicated to one brand, but we all have the benefit of using each other’s strengths and insights. Together, we’re able to help people experience the world through dining and travel.
