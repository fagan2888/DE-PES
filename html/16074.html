Data Analyst, Greater China Service Management

Tesla is seeking a motivated, experienced, and knowledgeable Data Analyst to support Greater China Service Operations. You will work cross-functionally with Service Operation, Field Service, MRB, Customer Support, Technical Operation, Logistic, PDC, Finance to collect, measure, visualize and interpret data; to turn data into information, information into insight and insight into business decisions.

Responsibilities

  • Interpret data, analyze results and provide ongoing reports.
  • Develop and implement databases, data collection systems, data analytics and other strategies that optimize the Distribution Center’s operational effectiveness and efficiency.
  • Acquire data from primary or secondary data sources and maintain databases/data systems
  • Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets.
  • Uses data patterns and trends to root cause operational gaps and inefficiency, develop solutions in collaboration with operations teams.
  • Filter and “clean” data by reviewing computer reports, printouts, and performance indicators to locate and correct code problems.
  • Work with Service leadership to prioritize business and information needs.
  • Identify and define new process improvement opportunities.

    • Requirements

  • 3+ years working experience as a data analyst or business data analyst.
  • Technical expertise regarding data models, database design development, data mining and segmentation techniques.
  • Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages (Tableau, etc.), databases (SQL etc), programming (XML, Javascript, or ETL frameworks) and interactive dashboards.
  • Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets (Excel, SPSS, SAS etc)
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Initiative to gain and implement a broad knowledge of warehousing and supply chain best practices.
  • BS in Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, Information Management, Statistics or related field.
