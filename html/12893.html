JOB DESCRIPTION

ACN Japan is seeking a Data Integration Developer to develop and support data integrations of disparate systems, ETL processes, and manage data analysis initiatives to meet ACN’s business needs. This position will work with business stakeholders and IT developers to deploy data integration solutions and reporting analytics as well as provide instruction, training and guidance to team members to ensure effective use of system resources. This is a ground level opportunity in a maturing organization.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS

·

  • Work with analysts and business users to translate functional specifications into technical designs for implementation and deployment.

  • Gather and translate business requirements into logical physical data models.

  • Review development plans, quality assurance test plans and user documentation to ensure correct interpretation of original specifications.

  • Design and develop solutions after gathering business and technical requirements.

  • Develop ETL workflows, code for new data integrations, and maintain/enhance existing workflows.

  • Develop process and mechanisms to certify data accuracy.

  • Implement processes and logic to extract, transform, and distribute data across one or more data stores in a wide variety of formats.

  • Ensure proper flow of data to the appropriate systems in an accurate and timely manner.

  • Monitor and debug data import failure and address issues.

  • Ensure high quality and optimum performance of data integration systems.

  • Work with vendors on outsourced integration solutions and platforms.

  • Conduct review of existing or legacy integrations to ensure consistent methodologies are followed, and to make recommendations where necessary.

  • Conduct gap analysis between application and requirements and collaborate with stakeholders to estimate project impact.

  • Collaborate with users to help create business relevant reports to meet business requirements.

  • Support end user questions and provide high level training on data handling, tools, data flow, etc.


KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILIITIES REQUIRED

  • Bachelor's or Master's Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or other related field (or equivalent work experience).

  • 5 years of experience in Application Development, RDBMS, Database Management, or related fields.

  • Knowledge of and experience with data conversion, integration design, SQL (T-SQL, PL/SQL, PSQL), ETL.

  • Knowledge of and experience with handling data in various formats and character sets.

  • Knowledge of and experience with SDLC and utilizing various development methodologies.

  • Excellent organizational, and problem solving skills.

  • Excellent written and verbal communication and interpersonal skills.

  • Able to lead testing scenario planning and manage Test/QA execution.

  • Able to extract and analyze data for trends or issues.

  • Must be able to manage time, work under pressure and deliver on tight deadlines.

    PREFFERRED SKILLS

  • Familiarity with Front End / Web Development a plus.

  • Familiarity with MVC application development a plus.

  • Familiarity with SalesForce a plus.

  • Experience with creating system documentation (Wire Frames, Flow Charting, etc.).
