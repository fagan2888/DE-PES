Senior Computer Vision Data Scientist/Engineer

Sportradar Ljubljana, SI

4 months ago

Apply on company website
Overview

We are looking for an experienced Computer Vision Engineer to join our diverse, highly skilled and super enthusiastic innovation team to support our work on exploratory projects that will pave the path for Sportradar moving forward. You will work in a cross-functional team which is developing innovative computer vision systems. Sportradar is a global leader in understanding and leveraging the power of sports data for hundreds of business customers around the world, in turn entertaining millions of sports enthusiasts. Are you ready for this new challenge?

The Challenge
  • Research, design, implementation and optimization of state of the art CV/ML algorithms for our existing and upcoming products
  • Implement computer vision techniques for object classification, labelling, and prediction
  • Share knowledge and mentor a smaller team located across two or more locations
  • You will be working with a growing team of talented data scientists already dedicated to this mission
  • Support Sportradar's future computer vision and machine learning initiatives.
Your Profile
  • At least 4 years of experience with machine learning; of which some hands on experience with deep learning and its applications to computer vision (e.g. classification, object detection, recognition, segmentation)
  • 1+ year experience in developing or using deep learning frameworks (eg, Tensorflow, caffe, PyTorch, Keras)
  • Experience with setting up production deployment pipeline for CV/ML models
  • Comfortable with common data science toolkits (Python, R)
  • Must be comfortable with modern software development methodologies such as agile development, git, code review, etc.
  • C++, Spark and GPU programming is a plus
  • Experience in AWS environment is a plus
  • Fluent in English
  • Autonomous and rigorous, and a team player.
Our Offer
  • Competitive salary based on your experience
  • High degree of autonomy
  • Working with international colleagues (main language in the office is English)
  • Sportradar handles and provides sports data worldwide (video, data) in real-time: many opportunities to push forward innovative projects
The minimum monthly salary for this position is according to the Collective Bargaining Agreement, overpayment will be considered depending on qualifications and working experience

Sportradar

Sportradar is a global leader in understanding and leveraging the power of sports data and digital content for its clients around the world. We provide cutting-edge solutions and services to media companies, bookmakers, sports federations and state authorities.

We are a truly international business employing over 2,000 people in more than 30 locations around the world. Our rapid growth has been driven by technological innovation alongside an understanding of our clients' business needs. It is our commitment to excellent service, quality and reliability that makes us the trusted partner of more than 1,000 companies in over 100 countries.

We occupy a unique position at the intersection of the sports, media and betting industries. This creates an exciting and fast-moving working environment that rewards creative thinking and hard work. We are passionate about what we do, and we care passionately about our people.

If you think you're up to the challenge, come and help us to grow, innovate and develop!

Sportradar is an Equal Opportunity Employer. We are committed to encourage diversity within our teams. All qualified applicants will receive consideration without regard to among other things, your background, status or personal preferences.

Your differences help shape our future
