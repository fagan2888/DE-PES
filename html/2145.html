Solution Principal / Data Engineer – Big Data & Analytics

At DXC, we believe the power of insight comes from an intelligent, end-to end approach from Big Data/ Business Intelligence to information. DXC Analytics align the right people, processes, and technology to help our clients analyze and act on their information. Our consultants provide the advice and support clients need to transform and manage their Big Data initiative, and start unlocking the valuable business opportunities. Clients benefit from Big Data and enhanced analytics to improve customer engagement and satisfaction, create new business opportunities, and reduce costs.

DXC provides a foundation of information management and analytic capabilities to harness all value from data that matters, to deliver insights at the speed of business and improve business outcomes. We leverage our expertise in the new generation of analytics and big data to push our customers IT and business organizations to remain relevant.

The Solution Principal is part of the Build Organization within the Analytics Offering in North and Central Europe. You will act as a Solution Architect at different accounts/ customers and mainly works with Sales and Advisors on Big Data and Analytics opportunities to design and sell analytic solutions to our customers. Together with Sales and Advisory the Solution Principal is part of our Analytics go to market team.d enhanced analytics to improve customer engagement and satisfaction, create new business opportunities, and reduce costs.

The Solution Principal is part of the Build Organization within the Analytics Offering in North and Central Europe. acts as a Senior Solution Architect at different accounts/ customers and mainly works with Sales and Advisors on Big Data and Analytics opportunities to design and sell analytic solutions to our customers. Together with Sales and Advisory the Solution Principal is part of our Analytics go to market team.

Role and Responsibilities

Serve in client-facing role designing and delivering Analytics solutions and support Account and Sales pursuits to secure Consulting and Solution sales for Analytics

Responsible for providing a detailed technical design for enterprise solutions in the Big Data and Analytics space

Provides advanced technical consulting and advice to proposal efforts and solution design

Develops new methods of solution design and delivery to apply to situations

Acts as an advisor to customer senior Information Technology (IT) leadership and sets strategic direction for customers based on DXC’s solutions and products in the Big Data and Analytics space

Demonstrates ability to provide thought leadership around Big Data, data warehouse data and structures

Collaborate with Sales, Accounts and Delivery to identify growth opportunities and leads the Analytics solutions

Education and Competencies

Master degree required

English business fluent

Some experience in Business Intelligence solution design

Some experience in Big Data architecture and technologies

An understanding of Analytics and Data Management portfolio of services and solutions with focus on BI and Big Data. Cloud (AWS or Azure) experience or a desire to progress within cloud technologies is desirable.

Able to lead facilitated sessions with business and technology representatives in order to design and/or modify various Big Data/Analytics solutions

Good business and communication skills. Able to communicate in business terms and turn requirements into applicable solutions

Solid experience in creating data models in a large scale data warehousing environment

Must have solid understanding of relational database design fundamentals

Is aware of the capabilities and limitations of database technologies like (Hadoop, Vertica, SAP HANA, Greenplum, Netezza, , Exadata, MongoDB )

Experience integrating with enterprise BI platforms such as Spotfire, Tableau, QlikView, MicroStrategy, Business Objects, Cognos, etc…

Experience with ETL tools like Talend, Pentaho, Informatica

Experience either on Azure or AWS data warehousing or Big Data implementations

Demonstrated ability to develop clear understandings of customers’ needs and the ability to incorporate them into an Analytics solution

Highly motivated and strong performer who drives opportunities and solutions

Team player who is willing to act as a coach/mentor for junior staff
