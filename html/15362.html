About The Unit

Yara is the global leading provider of nutrition solutions for the most diverse crops by combining global and local knowledge of crops and farmer needs, along with a portfolio of high quality products and exceptional application competition. In recent years Yara has been growing and acquiring an important position in the Latin America fertilizer market and actively supporting the development of sustainable, productive and profitable agriculture for different crops.As a global organization we actively strive to reflect diversity in society.

Therefore, we motivate all qualified candidates from all backgrounds to submit their application. We are committed to creating a work environment that adapts to gender equality and allows us to combine professional development with the needs of a family or other personal circumstances. Currently we have the vacancy for the position Business Intelligence & Master Data Analyst will be responsible to provide high value information to support business decision making by translating business needs into technical requirements, designing and developing regional reports and dashboards, support and improve business intelligence governance in the region and contribute to the BI global initiatives.

Creation and modification of WHT maintenance (Argentina)
Finance income maintenance.

Restrictive lists validation.
Guarantee the application of authorization matrices and BULA policies.
