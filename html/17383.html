Make Your Mark – j oin our fast-growing and inspiring team of professionals using latest technology

Tieto Data Journey is a team consisting of recognized professionals in the fields of data science, data engineering and architecture, data transformation advisory, security and software development. We are a team of 80 talented, forward-looking and self-driven experts. With us you get to work with, for example Big Data, Machine Learning, Cloud technologies, Kubernetes, Databricks, Snowflake and Apache Spark. Being part of Tieto gives you muscles to truly have an impact – Make Your Mark.

We want you!

We are searching for Data Engineer for development of customer’s cloud data platform solutions. Your task with us is data pipeline development related, including design and development of, customer data pipelines and advanced analytics solutions. Your responsibilities include data engineer role for selected development activities and customer deliveries.

We expect you to have proven skills in the latest data platform technologies and passion to continuously learn new things and aim higher.

To Succeed In This Role, You Should Have
  • Experience in working with cloud environments, preferably Azure and AWS
  • SW Architecture development experience related to big data solutions, machine learning and/or advanced analytics
  • Hands-on experience working with data modelling and data integrations
  • Cloud-computing infrastructure experience
  • Knowledge of the major big data solutions like Hadoop, Hive, Presto, Databricks, Spark and Kafka is a plus
  • Several years of experience in working in technical roles in complex product development and/or other SW development projects
  • Good communication skills and ability to work independently with the customer representatives

We offer you
  • CAREER DEVELOPMENT: Being part of this team, we guarantee that you keep developing professionally and that you stay in the frontline of data business.
  • MEANINGFUL PROJECTS: Our projects make an impact. In one case we help Olympic athletes to enable them to reach their highest potential. In another case we use AI in cancer research.
  • HARDCORE PROS: We can proudly say that we have succeeded in building a team of outstanding professionals whose core competencies support each other.
  • FLEXIBILITY: We offer the flexibility to organise your work the way it suits you the best. We trust our people and feel no need to be checking timestamps or doing the headcounts in the morning.
  • LATEST TECH: We are not fixed to selected technologies rather we have the freedom to choose the best fit for the purpose.
  • OPEN CULTURE: We value trust and openness between our team members. With us you can be yourself: crack jokes, share your thoughts, ask questions and find your place.

Got interested? We are eager to hear from you, do not hesitate to contact us: Ville Aho ( ville.aho@ tieto.com , WhatsApp +358504630188)
