Description

Do you want to accelerate the creation of the Internet of Things and the dramatic impact it will have on the economy, science and society as a whole? Do you believe that it’s not connected devices themselves that are game-changing, but the services and experiences they deliver? Do you want to help define the future of healthcare by developing connected solutions that have a positive impact on people’s health? Are you interested in digging deep into “small data” as well as “big data” to deliver meaningful insights on consumers and medical professionals? Your responsibilities As Senior Data Software Engineer you are responsible for: Actively contributing and steering all aspects of agile software development: planning, tracking, architecture, development, configuration management, testing, integration, validation, and documentation estimating total required team effort, being closely involved in policy preparation and the program of the group leading improvement initiatives with regard to technological improvements, and performing future-oriented studies with other specialists being contact point for customer in the team, identifying leads and understanding market demand and customer requirements in the longer term Your team The company aims to improve the lives of 3 billion people by 2025. To accomplish this goal, the company is revolutionizing the way we look at healthcare systems by delivering innovation across the continuum of care. In this role, you will work on connected digital propositions that span all our businesses, driving programs that address healthy living, prevention, diagnosis, treatment and home care. You will collaborate closely with people from different cultural backgrounds, located all over the world, as if they were sitting next to you. Our user-centered connected propositions contain re-usable components, within an increasingly open ecosystem. They also use current connectivity standards and cloud services. In short, they empower people. The Connected Digital Propositions team of which you’ll be a part helps deliver best-in-class, end-to-end solutions. These include connected hardware, appliances, medical devices and wearables, and leading mobile apps across all major OS platforms. The team also develops various back-end cloud systems that integrate with these propositions. The team works according to Agile principles, following SAFe (Scaled Agile Framework) processes. You will work on highly innovative, first-of-their-kind propositions that are new within The company, so an entrepreneurial mindset is an advantage. Together with your team, you will strive for continuous improvement of deliverables, your way of working and yourself. The world doesn’t run from 9 to 5, so flexible working hours can be combined with a healthy work-life balance. Colleagues will challenge and coach you and you should willingly return the favor. Our offer We offer the opportunity to play a leading role in achieving our ambitions with digital connected products and to truly contribute to the way our innovations improve the health and well-being of our customers. You will work in close cooperation with global teams across the company, playing a key role in driving the transition process that will turn the company into a Digital Systems company. We invite you to be part of a challenging, innovative environment with great opportunities for personal development. We are looking for someone with a… Masters degree in SW Engineering, Informatics, or equivalent Minimum of 8 years of relevant work experience in software development, at least 4 years of experience in developing cloud based data solutions Proven track record in development methodologies like Agile/Scrum/V-Model Set of strong technical skills in Java in combination with big data solutions (e.g., Hadoop, Hive, MapReduce, Spark, Elastic Search, AWS,…) Hands-on experience with collecting, parsing, managing, analyzing and visualizing large sets of data to turn information into insights Strong analytical, conceptual and creative thinking skills A problem solving and pragmatic attitude This role will appeal to you if you… Enjoy working in cross-functional teams Are curious, open and feel at home in an international environment Have an affinity with innovative development projects and feel comfortable with relatively vague requirements
