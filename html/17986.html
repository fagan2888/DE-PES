Maison Moderne is building a new team of talented web engineers to amplify digital product innovation. As an early member of this team, you will play a leading role, crafting the team’s culture, systems architectures of existing and new platforms and processes.

Founded in 1994, Maison Moderne is the leading independent media company based in Luxembourg. A local outfit catering to international brands, we operate four complementary business units: a publishing house, content agency, advertising agency and business club. Our unique media ecosystem allows us to create value for clients across a vast media landscape in English or French, digital or print.

Our 100+ multi-lingual and diverse workforce is our biggest asset and at the heart of everything we do.
Profil Web : description du poste et du profil recherché Emplacement

Your Role

The Senior Full Stack Web Engineer will be responsible for keeping existing products reliable as well as assuring innovation and evolutions. As part of a cross-unit interdisciplinary team, you will lead the implementation of innovative new digital features, services and products. You will operate in back-end and front-end environments.

In close cooperation with senior management, you will design concepts and strategies to achieve continuous delivery pipelines in line with the DevOps culture. Together with the Data Engineer you will create and maintain high available data flows with different existing and new Applications and Platforms.

About You
  • Full Stack proficiency;
  • Java/Scala expertise with proven experience of at least 3 years. You have an interest in functional programming. Experience with the Play Framework is an asset;
  • Proficient knowledge of MongoDB or other similar document-oriented databases;
  • Professional experience with HTML5, CSS/Less, JavaScript/TypeScript. Knowledge of React is an asset;
  • Professional experience with cloud-based infrastructure, preferable Amazon AWS (S3, SQS, SNS, EC2, MongoDB Atlas);
  • Safe and familiar with agile/lean production methodologies (Scrum, DevOps). Professional Experience with continuous delivery workflows and with modern test, production and deployment tools, processes and platforms (Jenkins, Docker, GitHub, Bash, etc.);
  • Highly motivated to build and lead a team, to design production strategies, customized to Maison Moderne and with respect to existing processes;
  • Self-starter personality, ready to work autonomously;
  • Strong motivation to recognize quality as an essential part of the product (Technology, Design/UX, Process);
  • Passionate about technology and digital product development. You admire scalable, secure, maintainable and performant architectures. You like to create ergonomic and easy-to-use interfaces with web technologies.
  • Positive attitude;
  • Professional ability to estimate, assess and qualify project results;
  • Professional communication skills in English. Luxembourgish and French are an asset;
  • Experience with Salesforce, JIRA, PHP, MySQL and Drupal is an asset.
