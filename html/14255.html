Data Engineer, Financial Services Platform

Gojek Jakarta, ID

1 hari yang lalu

Lamar di website perusahaan
You will build data-driven solutions to improve our partners’ lives—including hundreds of thousands of drivers, merchants and millions of customers—by building data-driven product features, data analytics tools, data pipelines, and large, scalable data infrastructure. Your work will impact how users, drivers, talents, merchants, etc. in the Gojek ecosystem—and even more broadly, across Indonesia—access financial services each and every day.

Gojek has a continuously evolving data infrastructure, which includes Google Cloud Platform (especially Pub/Sub) and Kafka, and data processing tools such as BigQuery, Dataflow, Flink, and Spark. You will be exposed to tons of new data processing tools and we are VERY open if you want to explore or experiment with any new tool or architecture design. Gojek Financial Services Platform, in particular, is in the early stages regarding its data infrastructure, so this role will require close interaction with the technical PMs, engineers, CTO, and compliance.

Responsibilities
  • Design, build, launch, and maintain extremely efficient & reliable large-scale batch and real-time data pipelines with data processing frameworks like Dataflow, Flink, Spark, and Kafka, which will enable teams to consume and understand data faster
  • Run extensive and intensive POCs on data processing frameworks, technologies, and platforms
  • Maintain and improve data quality through testing, tooling and continuous evaluation of performance
  • Integrate and collate data siloes in a manner that is both scalable and compliant
  • Act as the bridge between our backend and analysts and work on data cataloging/management
Requirements
  • Strong understanding of data streaming concepts
  • Experience with building production-grade data processing tools such as BigQuery, Kafka, Pub/Sub, Dataflow, Flink, and SparkA
  • Experience in writing code in Python, Go, Scala, SQL, and Java
  • For you, test-driven development is a must
  • Passionate about crafting clean code and story-like documentation and interested in being the glue between engineering and analysis
  • Understand the value of collaboration within teams and be very customer-oriented
  • Knowledgeable about system design, data structures, and algorithms
  • Strong familiarity with data modeling, data access, and data storage techniques, like Data Mart, Data Lake, and Data Warehousing concepts
Gojek is a technology startup based in Jakarta, Indonesia. Specialising in ride-hailing and logistics, we are also the only company in Southeast Asia to be part of Fortune's 50 Companies That Changed the World (2017).

Gojek is a Super App: one app with over 20 services including food delivery, commuting, digital payments, shopping, hyper-local delivery, massages, and many more.

Gojek is Indonesia’s first and fastest growing unicorn building an on-demand empire. Our total of 2,000,000 driver-partners collectively travel 16.5 million KM daily – making us Indonesia’s de-facto transportation choice.

Gojek is a verb! Gojek is a way of life!
