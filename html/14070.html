    For our dedicated team in Zagreb we are looking for a

    SENIOR TEST ENGINEER (m/f)

    NICE FOR YOU:

    • A permanent employment contract
    • Attractive compensation including a 13th month salary
    • Good work-life balance with flexible working hours where no overtime is expected
    • Continuous education and guided professional development
    • A dedicated team, which is looking forward to new colleagues
    • Flat hierachies and short decision paths
    • A demanding and responsible job in a forward-looking sector
    • Modern office with good traffic connection close to the recreation area Jarun

    NICE TO DO:

    • You are responsible for the test management of new and existing products.
    • Within an agile environment you will help the team to refine user stories and make sure they are testable.
    • You will plan, design and develop features for the test automation framework.
    • Furthermore you will design and create automated test cases on different levels (module, subsystem, system).
    • You will identify potential risks and analyze test failure causes to ensure a high software quality standard.

    NICE TO HAVE:

    • University degree in computer engineering, software engineering or a related field
    • Profound knowledge of software testing (test case creation, test execution, methods, tools, etc.) at different levels (module, subsystem, system)
    • Knowledge of established methods of software testing (e.g. Certification as ISTQB Tester)
    • Accuracy, reliability and quality awareness
    • Practical experience in agile software development is a plus
    • Strong communication and business-fluent English required


    NICE TO MEET YOU! Become a part of ATRON.


    It doesnt work completely without formalities. When sending your online application to career@atron.com, please attach your CV and any references you may have (max. 6 MB).

    We look forward to learn more about you!
