Do you want to shape the future of AI and ML of the chemical industry?

Do you want to have a start-up flare within an established leading company – that has a generous budget for Advanced Analytics projects?

Do you want to be valued what you can and be pushed to get better at what you want to learn? This is your chance:


About your future employer:


This leading German Chemical company that is located in the Cologne Region is looking to grow its brand new team of Experts of Advanced Analytics. It is an international team working working closely with the Asia Pacific and North America Teams. They are looking for a Senior Data Engineer to support the team and be able to take crucial decisions alongside the Global Head of Advanced Analytics.

The tasks:


  • You will be responsible for identifying, defining, building and running data processing architectures and pipelines capable of handling and processing huge data sets from potentially heterogeneous data sources
  • You will be responsible for legal clarifications with regards to data protection classifications and their impact on the feasibility to use external cloud service providers as well as the security of the data processing platform
  • You will identify additional data sources to connect to the data pool for maximizing the value of the data pool in close collaboration with the Data Science Team
  • Furthermore, you will define strategies and roadmaps for building our data processing architecture as well as the required processes for most efficiently utilizing the data processing architecture
  • You will also ensure that data in the data pool is complete, available, valid, consistent, timely and secure in order to enable and speed up the work of the Data Science Team


Your profile:


  • M. Sc. in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics or similar
  • Experience  programming/scripting languages like bash, perl, ruby, python and
  • Good knowledge of one or more: Hadoop, Kafka, Spark, with SQL and noSQL databases as well as with edge computing  principles
  • Fluent in English or German

What my client company has to offer:


  • Become an expert in Team of Experts
  • Competitive salary
  • Stock options
  • Possibility to become Team Lead of a Data Team
  • Respectful international team

To learn more about this opportunity, please reach out to Nathalie Zetzmann with your CV.
