Data Engineer - Johannesburg

ASIE Personnel Johannesburg, ZA

2 weeks ago

Apply on company website
Our client in Johannesburg CBD is looking for a Data Engineer to join their company

Requirements
  • Minimum Qualification - B Degree Maths, Statistics, Engineering, Computer Science, Econometrics, Physics or Actuarial Science.
  • Must possess strong Business Intelligence architecture and development skills with SAS Business Intelligence tools such as SAS Enterprise guide.
  • Experience in SAS Data Integration Studio, SAS Visual Analytics, and SAS Management Console an advantage.
  • Experience in managing and monitoring a SAS batch environment.
  • Experience in UNIX, LINUX an advantage.
Duties & Responsibilities
  • Take ownership of data delivery end-to-end for specific business use cases, as well as set and manage SLAs.
  • Work with team and stakeholders to continually assess and redefine data technology stack to support changing data patterns and business use cases and to bridge the gaps between Data Engineering, Business, and Product by constantly collaborating with all parties to understand data needs.
  • Design, build, and evolve custom ETL processes for feeding consumer systems.
  • Design, implement, QA and deploy ETL transformations to enhance the Data Eco System and workflows to load, analyse and archive customer transactional data.
  • Ensure data quality is maintained throughout all managed systems Perform data quality analysis and introduce monitors and alerts to maintain it.
  • Build infrastructure to automate extremely high volumes of data delivery and creatively solve data volume and scaling challenges.
Please Note
  • Ensure you meet the minimum requirements as described above.
  • We will only be getting back to applicants that meet all of the above requirements.
  • Should you not receive a response from us within 2 weeks you can deem your application as unsuccessful for this specific position.
