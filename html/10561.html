Data Engineer

Department of Transport (DoT) London, GB

1 week ago

Apply on company website
Salary: £37,428 to £45,747 (Depending on Location, Skills and Experience)
Are you an analytical individual looking to establish yourself within the
public sector? Are you experienced in designing and implementing your own
data management systems? Do you enjoy working as part of a wider team of
highly skilled individuals? If so, we want to hear from you.
Job Description We are looking for a Data Engineer to join our Digital
Services Team.
You will be responsible for delivering smart and efficient ways for our
users to work as part of the Project Delivery function.
This will include cleaning up sizeable amounts of data, building usable,
easy-to-use data to whomever may require it.
This role will typically suit an analytical individual that is experienced
in assessing and converting large amounts of unstructured data sets.
You will be actively involved with the Development Manager and Data
Architects and have the chance to create positive working relationships
across the Department for Transport.
Role Responsibilities include but are not limited to;
  • Implementing data
    flows to connect operational systems.
    • Build accessible data for analysis.
    • Write ETL scripts and code to make sure the ETL process performs
    optimally.
    • Re-engineer manual data flows to enable scaling and repeatable use.
    About you The ideal person will hold a relevant academic background in
    computer science/mathematics/physics or related field.
    You will hold excellent knowledge of data landscapes, products & services
    and experience of working in a large-scale data development/production
    environment.
    This background will come with a demonstrable understanding of end-to-end
    data delivery techniques in Business Analysis, Data Modelling, ETL
    techniques, Data Exploitation, Quality Assurance and MI/BI solutions.
    You will have first class verbal and written communication skills to liaise

    Effectively With Data Architects And Other Experienced Professionals.

    Suitable individuals will have exceptional interpersonal skills, allowing
    you to engage effectively and develop strong, lasting working relationships
    with colleagues and stakeholders.
    About us We are part of the Department for Transport (DfT).
    DfT is a great place to work, and our ‘one team’ approach really makes a
    difference.
    We value our staff and that’s why we’re committed to nurturing an inclusive
    environment across the Department.
    You’ll be part of an innovative organisation that invests in all our
    people, ensuring we have the diverse skills to deliver transport that moves
    Britain ahead now and into the future.
