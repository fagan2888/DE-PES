    • Your new company

    An established organisation in its field

    Your new role

    As a co-lead, you will be responsible for working closely with business stakeholders and data teams to conceptualise and deliver data analytics and visualisation support to generate insights that will drive operational and management decision-making.

    • End-to-end data analytics of identification, collection, integration, usage and data visualisation
    • Provide required analytics and visualization support to support strategic decision-making
    • Driving insights to optimize clinical, operational and financial performance across the business
    • Be involved in discussions on overall business strategy, scorecards and KPIs
    • Proactively engage and collaborate with key stakeholders to establish and prioritize information and analytics needs

    What you’ll need to succeed

    The ideal candidate should already come with proven experiences delivering end-to-end data analytics projects from conceptualisation through to visualisation and be comfortable with clearly articulating insights derived and recommendations to stakeholders of all levels.

    • Master’s Degree in mathematics/ Analytics, Computer Science or equivalent
    • At least 5 years of proven experience delivering data analytics projects
    • Strong project management and delivery experience
    • Ability to deliver clear, concise reports and presentations/dashboards and effectively articulate observations and recommendations
    • Good interpersonal skills and able to work across different areas within the team and cross functionally outside the team
    • Good problem-solving ability, learning attitude and applying new knowledge

    What you need to do now

    If you’re interested in this role, click ‘apply now’ to forward an up-to-date copy of your CV.
