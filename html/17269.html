PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES

As the market leader of ERP software, SAP Digital Business Service provides a complete portfolio of maintenance offerings and a comprehensive range of services that enable customers to continually improve core business processes while leveraging their existing solutions. The SAP Solution Manager is a service and support platform, which is designed to assist customers to implementing and operating their IT solutions efficiently to increase the reliability and lower the cost of ownership. This product provides the tools, the integrated content, and the gateway to SAP that you need to implement, support, operate, and monitor your SAP solutions.

Today, SAP Solution Manager is the premier platform for the on-Premise customer installed base and partners, where we will deliver continuous innovation. For our customers there is no doubt that it is the SAP solution management platform for SAP centric solutions, especially during the transition phase to digital business. It will be the base of our hybrid solutions according to our company strategy. Continuous Innovation Services is responsible for the innovation and stabilization of the SAP Solution Manager. As a team we ensure that our large Customer Base receives continuous innovation and maintenance for SAP Solution Manager throughout their mature lifecycle. With that we safeguard customer’s investments and strive to retain our customers for life. Delight customers. Always.

EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS

You will be delivering world class continuous innovation in the areas of Process Management, IT Service Management and etc., which are main application solutions of SAP Solution Manager.
  • Responsible for continuous innovation and support of specific product features in a team.
  • Ability to analyze and solve functional and technical issues in existing and new code.
  • High level of quality, speed, reliability and efficiency
  • Works closely together with other team members to ensure the team success.
Tasks Include
  • Effective and efficient delivery of development support tasks in the assigned functional areas of responsibility
  • Analysis of complex software issues (on level Development Support)
  • Provide maintenance and support for the SAP standard functional scope in the assigned areas of responsibility
  • Ensure excellent quality and maintain high standards in message processing, customer notes, software corrections and software enhancements in the assigned areas of responsibility
  • Contribute on continuous improvement of software quality; design and implement improvements of software architecture and applications
  • Design and implement legal requirements and functional enhancements in areas of expertise
  • Provide technical expertise and advice to the team; foster team compliance with SAP’s product and process standards
Education And Qualifications / Skills And Competencies
  • Graduated with a Bachelor or Master degree in Computer Science, Engineering or other related fields
  • Strong programming background, ABAP, ABAP OO are the plus
  • Strong problem analysis and solving skill
  • High customer orientation with customer experience is a plus
  • Self-motivated, open minded and ability to learn new areas and technologies quickly
  • Strong constructive and open style communication skills
  • A good team player with good presentation and time management skills
  • Knowledge of other platforms and applications will be an added advantage
  • Fluency in German is a plus
