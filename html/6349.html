How You'll Make An Impact At Collibra

As a Cloud Engineer of Collibra’s Corporate IT team you will be focusing on expanding, enhancing and maintaining our several Cloud platforms and automate SaaS-based integrations. You will be responsible for enabling the different teams within Collibra, building out workflows, but also participating in the development of a cloud-only interactive learning and demo platform build on Kubernetes. Automation of recurring tasks and developing our platforms for a self-serving system is our goal. You will be able to focus on new technology and implement it where it can deliver operational or feature-driven enhancement.

A day in the life of a Cloud Engineer at Collibra:

Responsiblities

You’ll be reporting directly to the Manager of IT, and will be responsible for a wide range of tasks, including
  • Development and control of IT solutions by evaluating the current status and preparing alternative workflows for Collibra’s internal related processes
  • Management and optimization of SaaS and Cloud based infrastructure (AWS, GCP and Azure Cloud)
  • Technical management and optimization of internal IT Systems and Cloud Solutions in collaboration with our internal Service Desk team.
  • Check application and/or system related problems, give support or escalate.
  • Ensure quality of operations by technical coaching of the IT Service Desk team and people out of the business involved in a specific processes.
  • Stay up-to-date with current technologies and technical developments to incorporate any relevant applications to existing or new systems.


You Have
  • A passion for SaaS and Cloud based infrastructure
  • A deep understanding and experience with AWS or Google Cloud (mandatory) and Azure Cloud (preferably).
  • A very good understanding of Service Desk processes related to networking and security
  • Expertise in Linux, OSX, Windows, Unix Server
  • Good knowledge of scripting languages, namely Python and Bash
  • Knowledge of MDM like Jamf, Workspace One, Puppet, Munki, Docker, OKTA
  • Web Development and Jira knowledge and/or experience are an advantage
  • The motivation to work with other teams and to find the best solution
  • An analytical and methodical reasoning, great problem solving skills and an excellent eye for detail
  • Ownership of your job and you enjoy working in a fast-changing and deadline driven environment
  • Great team working skills and you are a believer is knowledge share and coaching


We Offer
  • A friendly and rapidly growing environment where your input will be valued and your growth fostered
  • Ongoing opportunities to collaborate with fellow Collibrians globally for team meetings, trainings, and conferences
  • Flexible hours- quality > quantity
  • Bi-monthly catered lunches, in addition to meal vouchers. Never go hungry again!
  • Did we mention happy hour?
  • A spacious office in Brussels and a company car (+ fuel card) to get you there
  • A state-of-the-art laptop
  • And of course, a competitive salary with a strong benefits package
