Experienced Systems Engineer, supporting a diverse data ecosystem. The successful candidate will work as part of an integrated team that includes administrators and engineers supporting Big data ecosystem, ETL, Data Quality and Visualization platforms.

Responsibilities:
• Contribute to the design, tuning and upkeep of Hadoop, ETL, Data Quality
• Contribute to tooling and automation – enhancing productivity of end users and developers in the ecosystem
• Collaborates with other departments on requirements, design, standards, and architecture of applications.
• Advise manager of platform risks, issues, and concerns.
• Contribute to the design, implementation, and enhancement of configurations, solutions deployed on big data platform for security, accessibility, auditability, compliance, data retention, usability, and performance.
• Work closely with infrastructure, network, database, business intelligence and application teams to ensure standardization/optimizations of solutions deployed on big data platform.
• Provide subject matter expertise on the capabilities and use of cluster components, big data technologies including but not limited data ingestion, data analysis, data governance, data management.

Skills:
• 5-8 years of data development experience utilizing the Hadoop platform
• Advanced coding experience/skills with the following:
o Linux shell scripting
o Python or R language or Java
o Apache Spark
• Demonstrated mastery in applied big data technologies and tools such as: Oozie, Airflow, Kafka, Hive, Pig, Impala, hive, HBase, ORC
• Experience with NoSQL databases
• Proficiency with data modeling and solution scaling
• Experience with AWS /Snowflake framework (preferred)
• Working knowledge of packaging/build tools such as sbt, Apache Maven or Ant (preferred)
• Working knowledge of packaging/build tools such as sbt, Apache Maven or Ant (preferred)
• 3-5 years of relevant experience working for large organizations (highly preferred)
• Understanding of Data Mining, Data Modeling, and Data Provisioning (acquisition, transformation and sharing)
• Strong written and verbal communication skills, leadership skills, and self-starter mentality
• Ability to handle multiple priorities and work collaboratively across functions
• Strong understanding of Agile Principles (Scrum)
• 2-3 years’ experience developing or supporting with one of the Hadoop distributions: Cloudera or Hortonworks
• 5 + years’ experience on ETL tools such as Datastage or Informatica
• 5 + years’ experience with OS administration around memory, CPU, system capacity planning, networks and troubleshooting skills.
• 3 + years’ experience with any of Java, Python or scripting languages (perl, shell etc)
• Experience working with RDBMS such as Oracle, SQL Server desired.
• Knowledge of Kerberos LDAP/AD integration and Encryption.
• Effective oral, written, and interpersonal communication skills.
