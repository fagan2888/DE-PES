Are you a Data Engineer looking for a new adventure, possibility within a Big Data environment? Are you a Data Engineer that either has experience with machine learning concepts or would like to get into an environment where this may be possible? Do you have extensive experience programming in SQL with exposure to Spark or Scala? Well then, this position might be just for you.

My client are looking to hire an experienced Data Engineer to come in and join their Customer Operations team based in South Dublin, and will be directly reporting into the Chief Data Scientist.

About The Company

My client are probably one of the most exciting start-ups in Europe at the moment and are heavily funded with backing from two globally recognised companies. As a company they handle data anonymization and analytics to help organizations meet the standards of personal data protection envisioned by the GDPR - they do this by a heavily backed Data Analytics function, which is at the forefront of what they do.

About The Role

As a Data Engineer you will be responsible for the following:
  • Gather requirements around data analytics and use of data
  • Build analytic tools/reports providing customer insights into their data
  • Development of tools to enable automated analysis of data risk reports.
  • Develop data validation & privacy test strategy including validation plans & test cases
  • Development of algorithms to implement required privacy enhancement controls for customer's use cases and applications


Minimum Requirements Needed For This Data Engineer Position
  • SQL - Extensive experience programming with SQL and excellent knowledge of core SQL functions
  • Spark/Scala - Experience working with Spark will be beneficial as they are moving it a more focused spark environment
  • Programming experience - Working knowledge of Python, Scala, Java or R - Software Engineering experience would be best suited for this role
  • Ability to present technical concepts and results clearly to different audiences and stakeholders.


Bonus Points
  • Experience with cloud infrastructure providers (AWS/Azure)
  • Working knowledge of Big Data technologies including Hadoop, Cassandra, Kafka, Redis, Hive, Impala
  • Experience designing and building Spark applications and data pipelines, monitoring and optimizing Spark job performance
  • Customer requirements gathering and KPI reporting / presentation of data science project outputs.


Reward For Working Here

Very competitive base salary, Bonus, Healthcare (Family), Dental, Life Insurance, Phone, Laptop, Car Parking, Tax Save Scheme, Active Sports and Social

Next Step

If the above information sounds like something you might be interested in, please do get in touch with Luke Sheehy (Head of AI & Data Recruitment) by applying below to hear more.
