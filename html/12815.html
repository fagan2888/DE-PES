SENIOR DATA ENGINEER
Berlin, Germany

€68,000 - €79,000

THE COMPANY


You can have the opportunity to work for a Berlin-based dynamically expanding e-commerce company.  With an international product, they are also international in the makeup of their teams both from an operational standpoint as well as having physical engineering teams in many of these.

THE ROLE


You will be part of the continuously improving Tech department have the responsibility of creating cutting-edge solutions which span across the entire user experience. As a Senior Data Engineer, you will be continuously working alongside Marketing professionals to attract new customers and create a unique and successful experience for an impressively large existing customer base. If you thrive in a creative environment where you are constantly problem solving, then this role will offer that.

As a Senior Data Engineer, you will support the Marketing Tech team by:


  • Developing top level data pipelines in a VMs and Cloud services environment using AWS and GCP
  • Utilise your existing experience in implementing scalable and secure API endpoints
  • Creatively improve and maintain new and existing infrastructure, whilst constantly adopting new tools
  • Whilst working in an agile environment you will share and adopt practices to increase marketing efficiency and automate as much as possible with regards to reporting
  • Tech stack – Python3, SQL, AWS and Google Cloud Services

YOUR SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE


As a Senior Data Engineer, you will need:


  • 4yrs + proven Software Engineering experience with at least 2 of those years being in Data Engineering
  • Advanced skills in both Python and SQL
  • Experienced in designing and implementing scalable data pipelines using either AWS or GCP
  • Knowledgeable on workflow management tools, preferably Airflow
  • Familiar with dealing with Linux servers and the command line
  • Experienced with continuous integration and deployment
  • A strong focus on engineering clean and well-developed solutions while maintaining simplicity and pragmatism
  • Strong English communication skills, with the ability to explain to various stakeholders’ complex technical projects and summarise these for non-technical stakeholders

BENEFITS

  • Meaningful work for a company who are directly their end users
  • Modern technical environment
  • Educational budget for personal development
  • Personal and professional development opportunities
  • Flexible working hours
