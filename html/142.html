About the role We are looking for a Big Data Engineer that will work on the collecting, storing, processing, and analyzing of huge sets of data. The primary focus will be on choosing optimal solutions to use for these purposes, then maintaining, implementing, and monitoring them. You will also be responsible for integrating them with the architecture used across the company.

How You Will Get The Job Done
  • Implementing Big Data solutions
  • Developing ETL processes
  • Improving data platform performance

Skills And Experience You Will Need
  • Hands on experience with Big Data tools such as Spark, Hive,
  • Experience with Machine Learning - Spark (mlib),Pandas, Scikit
  • Experience with NoSQL databases like HBase, Casandra,
  • Understanding of distributed computing principles
  • Knowledge of integration of multiple data sources
  • Experience with Container Solution, eg. Openshift, Docker, Kubernetes
  • Experience with AWS/EMR
  • Experience with messaging systems, such as Kafka
  • Good understanding of Lambda Architecture, along with its advantages and drawbacks
  • Programming skills wit at least one of following languages: Python, Scala

Nice to have
  • Knowledge of various ETL techniques and frameworks, such as Flume would be an asset
  • Experience with R and Tensorflow would be an asset
