Data Engineer

Unlimit Recruitment Bratislava, SK

6 months ago

Apply on company website
Náplň práce
You will be building a scalable data pipeline that provides real-time insights into the behavior of users on their movie streaming platform. The ideal candidate has good software development skills, experience with building data systems and is eager to keep on learning about the field.

Responsibilities
  • Deploy and maintain robust and scalable processing infrastructure, such as message queues, databases and data processing clusters in the cloud.
  • Write application logic that transforms web events into data views about different entities on our platform.
  • Implement web services that provide the resulting data views to analytics dashboards.
  • Setup monitoring of data pipelines to ensure high-availability.
Požiadavky

Requirements
  • At least 2-3 years experience on Data Engineer position or similar
  • Good knowledge of at least one high-level programming language: Java, Python, Scala, C++ etc.
  • Understanding of fundamental computer algorithms and data structures.
  • Experience building and maintaining data processing systems.
To Have Overview About/ Nice To Have
  • Experience with cloud computing environments, e.g. AWS or Google Cloud
  • Experience with log-based messaging systems such as Apache Kafka
  • Experience with stream processing frameworks such as Storm, Samza, Flink or Spark Streaming
  • Experience with batch processing systems such as Hadoop and Spark
  • Experience with workflow management tools such as Luigi or Airflow
  • Experience with infrastructure and configuration management tools such as Terraform or Ansible
O spoločnosti
Technological start-up focused on development of blockchain-enabled distribution platform created specifically for entertainment industry.
Výhody
  • International work environment in fast-growing company
  • Regular team building events
  • Office in the city center
  • Sick leave benefit
