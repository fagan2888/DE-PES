Your next destination: lux-Airport - Findel – Luxembourg

Do you know that lux-Airport is the operating company of Luxembourg airport, providing direct flight services to 81 destinations and employing more than 300 people?

In 2018 our airport welcomed 4,04 million passengers and we have the ambition to increase this number and to continue developing our activities!

To achieve this goal, we are looking to hire immediately a: Data Scientist (m/f)

Responsibilities
  • Identifying business needs and challenges;
  • Defining statistical models meeting industry-specific challenges;
  • Identifying and grouping all collection methods and all data sources required to conduct relevant analyses;
  • Developing analysis tools to collect the company’s data;
  • Designing and maintaining data warehouses;
  • Qualitative and quantitative organisation, study and summary of data sources in the form of usable outcomes;
  • Using collected data to identify applications, improve processes and develop products, programs and new uses;
  • Developing recommendations, proposals for solutions and improvement scenarios;
  • Technological and methodological watch, as well as standards watch to maintain skills up-to-date
Must Have Requirements
  • Studies : Bachelor’s degree in statistical analysis or computer programming and relevant experience in a similar position;
  • Experience : At least two years’ successful experience in the field;
  • Languages : French, English;
  • “B” category driving license;
