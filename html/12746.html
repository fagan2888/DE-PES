Data Engineer

Express Recruitment Nottingham, GB

2 months ago

Apply on company website
Express Recruitment are delighted to be supporting our industry leading technology client, who are experts in the Marketing technology sector. Our enviable client is looking for a capable Data Engineer, with demonstrable experience with SQL.

The successful candidate will be working as part of one of their established Development teams working to 6 week sprints. Ensuring a suite of products are shipped to the highest standard and that all things data adhere to that standard is of the utmost importance.

Working on the performance tuning of the current SQL code, and looking to the future with cloud based technologies, this is a fantastic role for an ambitious individual looking to take the next step in their career.

Duties
  • Designing and Developing effective and stable databases
  • Liaise with Testers and Developers to ensure product viability
  • Research and advise on new Database technologies including being a part of moving into the cloud
  • Performance Tuning of SQL code to improve the current systems
  • Provide in-depth internal database support
  • Take an active part in sprint meetings and apply your expert knowledge to help projects run within time frame
  • Share expertise with less experienced colleagues and stakeholders

Background
  • Competent user of Microsoft SQL Server versions 2008, 20014, 2016
  • Any familiarity with the .Net framework beneficial
  • Excellent communication skills and a willingness to learn new skills
  • Experience SQL Performance Tuning would be advantageous
This is a fantastic role that offers a wealth of benefits alongside the opportunity to be trained to an advanced level with Azure Databases. Alongside working for a cutting edge Software House this is an opportunity to be on the forefront of current technology.

Salary: c£35,000 – £45,000 DOE

Location: Nottingham City Centre

Hours: Monday to Friday, full time
