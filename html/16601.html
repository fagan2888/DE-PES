Intern - Software Engineer - Cloud Services (f/m/d)
Start: 01/10/2019 | Duration: 6 (in months) | Full-time

Deutsche Börse Group is one of the largest exchange organisations and market infrastructure providers worldwide and is far more than a pure marketplace organizer for the trading in shares. It is a trans- action services provider covering the full value chain of trading, clearing, settlement and market data. Our success is based on our highly integrated and automated IT systems. Therefore, Information Technology is the core of Deutsche Börse: With advanced technology, it affords companies and investors access to global capital markets. Information Technology designs, builds and operates the technological infrastructure of Deutsche Börse and 16 other exchanges around the world. More than 1,000 IT engineers implement the segments’ strategies through technological applications, thereby ensuring the competitiveness of the entire Group.

Your responsibilities:



  • Contribute to the transformation of our legacy applications into a modern cloud-based microservices architecture
  • Apply your abilities in Machine Learning and AI to high-scale financial technology applications
  • Apply your engineer skills in developing state of the art software to automate everything we do in our Corporate Processes
  • Design and build scalable, high-availability mission-critical systems and APIs
  • Solve problems using distributed, asynchronous messaging
  • Work closely with product, Google SRE and infrastructure teams

Our requirements:



  • Students must be enrolled in a relevant academic programme (e.g. BSc or MSc student in Computer Science or equivalent) recognised by the state during their internship and must have completed at least the first 2 years of their university studies OR have graduated for less than 2 years
  • Experience with one or more general purpose programming languages including Go or Python
  • Solid understanding of public clouds
  • Background knowledge of HTML5, CSS and Java script, R, Perl is an asset
  • Background in object-oriented programming, including good knowledge in algorithms and data structures
  • Pro-active in learning and highly self-motivated
  • Excellent written and spoken English skills alongside good communication and collaboration skills


Are you interested in working with a pleasant and very dedicated team? Convince us with an appealing application. Please use our online application portal.
