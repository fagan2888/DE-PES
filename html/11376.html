Software Development Engineer in Test Senior II Lead

Akamai Technologies Kraków, PL

4 tygodnie temu

Aplikuj w witrynie firmy
Akamai’s Web Performance Business Unit seeks a Senior Software Engineer in Test for the development of business-critical product capability. These positions are forming a new engineering team in Akamai’s Krakow office.

The SDET position is a challenging role as it requires skills from various areas of engineering including mobile. The core responsibilities include thorough testing of any features developed within the team and extensive automation that ensures the high quality of all the components and enables the team to frequently and smoothly release to production.

About The Team

We are creating a new team in Krakow which will drive a mobile application program as part of Akamai’s flagship Web Performance product line. These mobile services enhance Akamai’s performance and analytics capabilities for mobile applications. Combining mobile devices with the scale and advanced capabilities of the Akamai platform creates unique technical challenges. This team will work to understand customer needs and implement solutions in this environment. Domain of technology includes Android/iOS mobile, management plane with microservice architecture, and large-scale backend processing

Responsibilities
  • Mentor and augment existing team to address the dynamic nature of a rapidly growing company
  • Build the QA automation infrastructure, methodologies and the performance lab
  • Review functional requirements, design documents, and bug descriptions.
  • Create, maintain and execute manual and automated integrated product/system tests to replicate real-world scenarios by leveraging Use Cases and/or User Stories.
  • Work closely with developers to design test criteria and develop automated tests.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional team members within and across business units to leverage internal product knowledge and expertise for optimum efficiency.
  • Work with application designers to understand and model customer behavior.
  • Work in parallel on more than one QA project at a time.
  • Develop test coverage for mobile software applications
  • Ensure that system and software under test meet product design/specifications
  • Perform full SQA cycle for software releases in a fast-paced environment. This includes - but is not limited to - gathering specs, developing test plans, running automated and manual tests as needed, and analyzing test results
  • Continuously develop and enhance automated test suites to improve efficiency
  • Maintain our large-scale, distributed test environment
  • Work with engineering, product management, operations, network infrastructure and the business development team

Basic Qualifications

Desired Skills and Experience
  • Education- Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, a related field or equivalent experience
  • Experience managing and building QA teams.
  • Experience developing or testing mobile software applications
  • Experience working in Android, iOS or both
  • Experience using databases and SQL commands
  • Experience in software release testing on tight schedules with high quality
  • Experience in developing automated testing infrastructures
  • Experience with test automation frameworks and deploy/build tools (Jenkins)
  • Familiarity with Agile methodologies and best practices
  • Drive for product quality and attention to detail
  • Can-do, positive attitude

Desired Qualifications
  • Experience developing automation in Java, Python or Groovy
  • Experience with both Android and iOS
  • Experience with testing very large scale, distributed systems
  • Familiarity with test tools (such as Selenium) and continuous integration systems (such as Jenkins)
  • Familiarity with iOS languages (Objective -C and Swift with respect to XCode and RESTful APIs
  • Familiarity with Android Studio and Kotlin are a plus
  • Strong Knowledge of Networking protocols like HTTP, TCP, IP, DNS
  • Excellent English language written and verbal communication skills
