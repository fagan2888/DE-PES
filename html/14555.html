Data Engineer

Responsibilities :

  • Handle first stages of data lifecycle: Data cleansing, Data preparation, Data Integration
  • As part of the various missions we carry out, in all sectors of activity, the Data Engineer Consultant will ensure that he/she creates value for his/her clients: identification and qualification of uses, solution design, architecture and data governance
  • To design secure data pipelines handling large volumes of data, curious and eager to diversify into Data Analyst or Data Scientist roles.
  • Keen on learning and getting involved and taking responsibility quickly on high value-added missions
  • The high standards of the work and the concern for the quality of the deliverables will be particularly appreciated.

Requirements :

  • At least has over 2 years of experience in Data Engineer, Consultant or related business
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or relevant disciplines
  • Graduated from an Engineering School or holder of a Bachelor or master’s degree in Information Technology
  • Experienced in Data Engineering and/or Big Data
  • Master the main Big Data technologies (Big Query, ElasticSearch, Hadoop, Impala)
  • Specialist in structured languages (JavaScript; Java; Scala) or data languages (Python, R)
  • Knowledge of a cloud services environment (Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud Platform...) is highly appreciated
  • Experience with Agile management is appreciated
  • Autonomous, organized, good speaker, with interpersonal skills
  • With a strong taste for innovative technologies and digital trends
  • With international experience, used to work with multicultural environment
  • Fluent in English and Cantonese, French and Mandarin are a plus
