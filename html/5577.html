OYO is looking for data scientists to join us in Gurgaon for our International Business.


As a Data Scientist your responsibility will be to help team with building automated algorithms, the end to end data products and business insights generations. Role requires high technical aptitude, problem-solving abilities, motivation, intellectual curiosity, and a passion for data discovery.


RESPONSIBILITIES:


  • Closely working with the core team to design algorithms for dynamic pricing.
  • Conduct advanced statistical analysis to provide actionable insights, identify trends, and measure performance
  • Develop new technological ways for solving high-end problems with data science & analytics
  • Designs experiments, test hypotheses and build models
  • Works with different stakeholders to identify the business requirements and the expected outcome
  • Recommends ongoing improvements to methods and algorithms


REQUIRED SKILLS:


  • Extensive background in data mining and statistical analysis
  • Able to understand and various data structures and common methods in data transformation
  • Deep understanding of statistical modeling (Regression, Clustering, Decision trees), machine learning, algorithms, data mining concepts, and a track record of solving problems with these methods.
  • Experience with deep learning, SVM, k-nearest neighbors, random forests, ensemble methods, Logistic Regression, Probability Theory, Bayesian/Markov networks etc.
  • Demonstrated programming skills in relevant languages, including R, Python, SQL, etc.
  • Optional Skills: Spark, Hive, Pig, d3, Ruby
  • Graduate degree in computer science, operations research, statistics, physics, or mathematics is preferable
  • 3+ years of Data Scientist experience is mandatory
  • Great communication skills
