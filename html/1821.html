APPLIES EXCLUSIVELY TO THOSE WHO RESIDE IN BOGOTA OR SURROUNDING VICINITY


DESCRIPTION

As an IT Infrastructure Manager, you will oversee both the technical and managerial aspects of the organization's IT department. You will plan, direct and design Accedo IT infrastructure while simultaneously managing the team responsible for maintaining this infrastructure.

This position works exclusively in an office setting and will oversee/supervise and mentor lower-level IT employees and WAN/LAN engineers while reporting to the Chief Information Officer.


REQUIREMENTS:

  • Excellent verbal and written English communication
  • 5-7 years of experience in managing IT infrastructure personnel
  • 5-7 years of experience in managing Data Center class II and above
  • Degree in Computer Science or similar
  • 5-7 years of experience in data and voice communications and technologies
  • 10 years of expertise in PC hardware and peripherals
  • 10 years of expertise in structured cabling design and engineering
  • 10 years of expertise in LAN/WAN hardware
  • In-depth knowledge of ISO 27001, PCI DSS, ITIL, Privacy Act standards and regulations
  • In-depth knowledge of Information Security applicable to system management
  • Advanced knowledge in electrical engineering and IEEE standard
  • Advanced knowledge in electromechanics, focusing in backup power (ATUs, UPS, Generator) and HVAC systems.


CERTIFICATION/TRAINING REQUIREMENTS:

  • ANSI/TIA/EIA 568-A/B
  • BICSI RCDD
  • TIA-942-A
  • SSAE-18
  • ISO-27001
  • NFPA-70E
  • CDEP
  • Cisco Infrastructure/Data Center
  • Microsoft Server Core Infrastructure
  • SolarWinds
