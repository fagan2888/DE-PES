Key responsibilities:

  • Perform exploratory data analysis to find new insights that help Central Group make better decision on customer service, sales & promotion, and other new initiatives
  • Utilize machine learning techniques, such as logistic regression, decision tree, clustering, etc on complex data, and convert the results into business framework that helps enhance customer experience and business competitiveness
  • Effectively communicate insights to non-technical stakeholders, such as marketing team. This includes ability to design dashboard/tracking report Tableau, build PowerPoint deck/ Excel worksheet, and
  • Other ad-hoc analysis as requested from various parties within Central Group

Skills:

  • Understand huge and complex transactional database and its structure
  • Data crunching and preparation. Be able to write code to pull and manipulate data
  • Data visualization, dashboard and report design
  • Discuss data science within team while able to digest and simplify the concept and communicate them to non-technical persons

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor degree in mathematics / statistics / computer science / quantitative business or other related fields
  • Master degree in data science, quantitative business or quantitative fields (optional)
  • Experience in following fields are preferred: data mining, predictive model, statistical program, data analysis, business consulting
  • Experience with transactional data is also preferable
  • Knowledge of SQL are preferable
  • Knowledge of R, python, Tableau and other data science software are plus
  • New graduates are also welcome
