We Offer

Is your dream to have equal opportunities? Do you have a real passion for clients? Are you a dedicated team-mate who values integrity highly? Do you love to improve your analytical skills? You are an empathetic and trustworthy teammate. You will take care or handling client requests. You welcome diversity and like to work in passionate team this job is for YOU.

Join a high-performance team of data engineers to work on creative and innovative Big Data solutions. We have a Data Lake application based on Apache Hadoop stack (by Cloudera) and always think how to do things better!
Our main goal is to develop and fill with new features a Data Lake application for the bank by collecting data across all organisations. We cooperate with our sibling Data Analytics team to provide analytics and data visualization capabilities. Similarly we interact with engineers, product managers and architects to provide scalable, robust technical solutions consumed on different business level.

Our technology stack

  • Big Data technology stack Cloudera’s CDH with HDFS, Spark, Impala, Parquet, Flume and more
  • NoSQL databases – HBase, Neo4j
  • Messaging system RabbitMQ, Kafka is our next step
  • Programming languages C#, Python, Java and other if required
  • Scripting languages bash
  • Others SCRUM, JIRA, Bitbucket, Jenkins/TeamCity

Opportunities we provide

  • Space to extend skills in Big Data field and other data related areas (relational, graph and NoSQL databases)
  • Opportunity to face challenging and complex projects
  • Have a feeling that your work matters and clear visibility of its impact
  • Work with passionate and highly qualified people on creative projects

Key Responsibilities

  • You work on data pipelines to bring new data into our environment according to requirements captured by Business Analysts
  • You develop Big Data applications and additional capabilities for our platform by participating in projects or leading them
  • You influence technical decisions by bringing your ideas and expertise during our discussions
  • You will help with maintaining and supporting our application by participating in operation support.

Open to discussing flexible/agile working.

You Offer

  • You have solid software engineering skills and experience in this field.
  • You have an expertise in one of selected programming languages (preferable (in order) C#, Python, Java, Scala) and you are open to learn more
  • You have some knowledge/experience with Big Data technologies or are eager to learn them.
  • You like to work in a project where multiple technologies cooperate to deliver common solutions and you can introduce new ones when needed.

For more information visit Technology Careers.
