The Company

Leading electronics company.

You are responsible for design, simulation, implementation and verification of digital circuits in collaboration with other disciplines such as mechanics and Embedded software development.

The Candidate

You have some experience with the use of simulation tools and measuring equipment for measuring digital circuits and interfaces (standard interfaces such as SPC, I2C, and customer-specific).

You have experience with digital circuits that use FPGAs and CPLDs.

You preferably have experience with simulation tools to ensure signal integrity, in other words, optimum closure of signal lines, line length optimization, etc.

Some experience in writing VHDL code for hardware testing purposes is an advantage.
