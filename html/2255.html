    Market Job Description

    Department Overview

    Wells Fargo International Technology, in partnership with EDCM (Enterprise Data Center Management), provides support for in-region data centers and colocation facilities. Support focuses on planning, forecasting, day-to-day operations, and optimizing data center utilization.

    About The Role

    Work as a Data Center Operations Engineer supporting the team for planning, forecasting, day-to-day operations and optimization. You will interact with multiple functional groups. This contract position based in Hong Kong S.A.R.

    Responsibilities
    • Operational Management of Data Centers and Information Processing Critical Facilities within APAC region.
    • Provide support for problem root cause analysis, correction and prevention. Direct event recovery teams through expedited informed decisions to minimize production impact.
    • Standards, Policies and Guideline Enforcement- Ensure all activities taking place within the Data Center meet or exceed established Enterprise Standards.
    • Ensure all Availability Management Processes are followed
    • Assist in the creation and execution of the EMEA Data Centre Strategy in line with Enterprise strategy.
    • Critical Site Enhancements / Projects- Actively participate in all projects effecting Critical Spaces providing subject matter expertise and design of Communications rooms, cabling and racking.
    • Support all types of infrastructure projects including hardware, networks, virtual server and information security components.
    • Manage relationship and delivery for Third Party Vendors within Data Centers
    • Physical Access Management- strictly adhering to the site-specific IPF access policies and procedures.
    • Provide event management for outages and planned maintenance to 3rd party suppliers, including evenings, weekends and bank holiday coverage within the IPF sites.
    • Primary and Backup Environmental Systems Assurance- Ensure all site environmental systems conform to standards.
    • Act as SME for Technology during planning and execution of Power downs and critical facility works.
    • Develop and manage key relationships between internal domain partners, third party vendors / suppliers and Corporate Properties Group and Corporate Security Group stakeholders.
    • Proactive Component Monitoring- Ensure all critical environmental systems are proactively monitored.
    • Act timely on events to prevent service disruption.
    • Manage Data Centre Governance / Audit Readiness- assist in the completion of Monthly Audit Readiness and Governance procedures and reporting.
    • Manage BCP/Disaster recovery events and related simulation exercises.
    • Weekends and evening on site work will be required- including escorting or event management (site outages).
    • Some International Travel will be required.


    Market Skills and Certifications

    Requirements
    • University degree in Engineering or related discipline.
    • Certified Data Center Professional (CDCP) or equivalent qualification.
    • Experience of more than 5 years in data center operations / critical facility environment.
    • Detailed, in-depth knowledge of technology infrastructure including data center hardware, servers, networks and platform operations.
    • Hands on technical support and operational management of critical services in a high-pressure environment.
    • Strong knowledge of Networking within a Data Centre, including Networking design and protocols for switches, routers, hubs, servers, firewalls, wireless, etc.
    • Strong knowledge of Server / Storage environments for Windows and Unix .
    • Fluency in English. Strong communicator (both written and verbal)
    • Good documentation skills with the ability to articulate complex topics in a concise and articulate manner.
    • Formal Change, Incident and Problem management knowledge and experience.
    • Strong Knowledge of Microsoft Suite Visio, Office, Excel, Word, PowerPoint.
    • Experience with hardware activities (Commissioning and decommissioning of racks, equipment, cabling, trouble-shooting etc.)
    • Experience with some form of ticketing system (Remedy based systems preferred)
    • Some knowledge of formal Project Management.
