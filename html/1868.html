Hi, we're Workhuman!
We're currently looking to hire an experienced systems engineer. This is a production systems role, supporting all Workhuman's production environments (that in turn support millions of end users) with an equal focus on Data Centre and AWS Networking.
It’s a great opportunity to work with an experienced multidisciplinary team in a technically varied role, offering a mix of planning, maintenance and support of production systems, as well as hands on involvement in several systems projects.
Technology Stack;
Here's a small flavor of the technology you will be working with:
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • R ed Hat Enterprise Linux
  • Check Point Firewalls, Fortigate IPS appliances, f5 BIG-IP Appliances, Cisco Nexus Switches, Dell Force10 Switches
  • VMware 5.5 & 6.5, Veeam Backup and Replication
  • Nagios Monitoring and Asset Mgt. Tools
Responsibilities, Skills & Qualifications
Here's just some of what you will be doing:
  • Day to day operation, management and monitoring of
  • Check Point firewalls appliances and Fortigate IPS solution,
  • f5 BIG-IP load balancers,
  • Cisco Nexus and Dell Force10 switches,
  • AWS Direct Connect
  • Involvement in project work – for example;
  • Assist with our production data center migration.
  • Working with public private clouds integrations (AWS)
  • Working with the security team on various initiatives.
  • Supporting our software engineering teams and developing infrastructure solutions to meet their requirements.
  • Managing and monitoring of production backups and DR replication along with assisting teams with DR testing.
Skills And Experience
  • Experience implementing and managing highly available, on-line infrastructure services in an enterprise environment (Ideally SaaS).
  • Demonstrated proficiency with AWS Networking (Direct Connect, Transit Gateways etc.).
  • Experience managing and maintaining a multi-site Check Point firewall deployment.
  • Strong knowledge and experience with data-center networking – essential (CCNP or equivalent a plus).
  • Hands-on experience of web load balancers, f5 BIG-IP LTM, Citrix Netscaler etc.
  • Some experience of Linux systems and applications (RHEL ).
  • Excellent troubleshooting and technical problem resolution skills.
Highly Desirable:
AWS Certification - Architect or Operations Track
On Call/ Travel
  • In this role there will be a paid, on-call structure, rotating roughly every 4/5 weeks.
  • There may also be an occasional travel to facilitate project work, or the requirement for occasional Saturday work to support major releases for which there is an established TIL structure.
Benefits:
  • 25 days annual leave, increasing with years of service.
  • Company plan VHI Health Care & Dental cover for you, your partner and children.
  • A Pension commences after successful completion of your probation period.
  • Participation in Workhuman’s TAX FREE employee recognition program.
  • Discounted gift certificates.
  • Discounted TaxSaver Travel cards/free car parking (subject to availability).
  • Income Protection.
  • Life Assurance.
  • Access to our Tuition Reimbursement Scheme.
  • Access to Workhuman® University.
  • Additional ‘perks’- Free breakfast cereal, fruit, tea/coffee, fantastic catered events throughout the year etc.
The Company
Workhuman® is the world’s fastest-growing integrated Social Recognition® and continuous performance management platform. Our human applications are shaping the future of work by helping organizations connect culture to shared purpose. With a consistent stream of gratitude fueling unparalleled, provocative workplace data and human insights, Workhuman® Cloud is a critical software engine for global companies seeking to motivate and empower their people to do the best work of their lives. Workhuman® (formerly known as Globoforce) was founded in 1999 and is co-headquartered in Framingham, Mass., and Dublin, Ireland.
