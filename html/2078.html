We Offer

SMO - Enterprise Services are responsible for providing innovative approaches through maintaining knowledge of industry best practice, technology trends and product availability. Developing business and technical requirements through consultation with key client stakeholders and ensuring adherence to corporate standards for risk and security compliance.

The Analytics & Reporting team works as part of a globally distributed team that will be the primary provider of IT Operational Reports to the firm. At any given time you will partner with team members across all regions and time zones while reporting to a line manager in New York. You will collaborate with key clients to develop dashboards that will drive business decisions across the firm.

Key duties

  • You design/maintain/support Bigdata environment, including ETL
  • You work directly with key stakeholders to understand data requirements and deliver on timely manner
  • You analyze large data sets
  • You design very complex SQL statements, including Impala
  • You script automation solutions for Data and Reports
  • You adhere with Agile methodology and follow the process to maintain/support and to document the details
  • You have ability to easily communicate complex problem solving ideas to senior management and peers
  • You determine opportunities on automation for any manual process
In this function, you will be designing and implementing data solutions via Bigdata and Python. You also create automation scripts to help minimize manual involvement when building reports or dashboards. You support to migrate existing complex SQL tables into Bigdata, and collaborate with reporting team to integrate the migrated tables in Tableau. You should also be well versed with data design and have ETL process experience.

Open to discussing flexible/agile working.

You Offer

  • You hold Degree or higher education in Information Technology or equivalent work experience
  • You have at least 3-4 years of experience creating Bigdata environment
  • You have demonstrable experience with Data Engineering
  • You have deep knowledge in Bigdata impala/Hadoop
  • You are skilled in Python for crafting functions using Pyspark and other packages related to Python
  • Deep understanding of database architect for Bigdata environment on how the ETL process works would be an asset
  • You are highly motivated and dedicated self starter with good interpersonal skills as well as strong communication skills as you take care of the interaction with clients to understand or present the requirements
  • Experience in Service Now ITIL process would be a plus
