Product Configuration Engineer - Public Sector


Ivalua was founded in 2000 on the disruptive notion that Procurement can unlock significant strategic value not only in a company’s spend and supply chain, but throughout the organization.

We here at Ivalua believe that software should empower Procurement leaders, not constrain them. We’ve created a complete Source-to-Pay platform that delivers real innovation and drives today’s values, while also delivering best-in-class capabilities with the flexibility to meet the needs of tomorrow. Our team is driven by deep engineering skills and a love of cutting-edge projects. Now, over 18 years later, Ivalua has been recognized by top analysts, including Gartner and Forrester, as a global market leader. Our success is evidenced by our 98%+ customer retention rate within an active and engaged community of over 250 leading enterprises across 70 countries achieving a competitive advantage by strategically managing over USD 500 Billion in spend, powered by the Ivalua Solution.


The Role:


As a Product Configuration Engineer within our Public Sector team, working closely with our Public Sector Solution Manager you will be responsible for developing and supporting the vision for the Ivalua for Public Sector solution offering. This role will have you developing and delivering new offerings and features at a rapid pace. You will support developing product features and functionality to support the Public Sector product roadmap.


Responsibilities:


  • Developing and delivering key public sector features based on the public sector product roadmap for customers with solutions around their public procurement needs.
  • Support the Public Sector Solution Manager in developing product specifications, technical specifications, standard integrations and design documentation.
  • As required, engaging with prospects & customers in understanding their use cases and determining the value for the Public Sector.
  • Collaborate across teams. We win or lose as a team. Solution Leads play a critical role in creating alignment between product management, professional services and engineering to prioritize features for implementation.
  • Implement directly solutions and features requiring simple configuration and identify needs that required complex configuration and integrations


Requirements:


  • Experience with SQL development – writing advanced queries, stored procedures, and automating SQL queries
  • Experience with transfer protocols like SFTP, FTPS, AS2 as well as with the use of APIs
  • Experience with CSV, XML or any other type of document business standards
  • Hands-on-coding experience in web based technologies is a must
  • Hands on experience building integrations with SOAP and REST API's
  • Web Services
  • Participate in requirements discussions, solution sessions and standup meeting.
  • Strong background in product configuration and software (SaaS) development.
  • Creative thinker who thrives on solving challenging problems with analytical thinking skills
  • Ability to work autonomously and handle multiple competing priorities in a fast-paced environment
  • C#.NET and SQL Server experience is a plus
  • Experience building products for public sector markets is a plus
  • Experience with Cloud procurement technologies is a plus
  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience
  • We take care of our employees in every way we can with competitive compensation packages, comprehensive health benefits for employees and their families, a 401(k) match, PTO, a flexible work environment, catered lunch Fridays …and much more!
