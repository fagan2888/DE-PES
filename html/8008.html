Nexus is transforming how we use trusted identities, certificate management and protecting information. Nexus’ Smart ID solutions and IoT platforms are used by millions of workers around the world. We are on a crusade for a more secure tomorrow, where passwords and single authentication is a thing of the past. That crusade starts with you!. Join our team in Prague and be our next .Net Ninja!

What Will You Be Doing At Nexus

Nexus Group is an innovative identity and security company. We secure society by enabling trusted identities for people and things, in both the physical and digital world.

Part of what we do is called Nexus Personal Desktop App , an UWP (Universal Windows Platform) app enabling Virtual SmartCards on the laptop for employees accessing corporate IT systems, protecting data by secure encryption and integrity through reliable signing. This is typically used by large corporations.

The team responsible for building the App, is today composed of Developers and a Test Automation Engineer. That means close collaboration, possibility to work on multiple parts of the flow and a team end-to-end responsibility for the entire product. Agile methods, self-organization and continuous integration is part of the team DNA.

We are at this point looking for a strong developer that will work with the team on the UWP App. This means a daily problem solving and development as a team, focused on continuous improvements with the end-user in mind.

Example of things we use are
UWP App : C#/.Net, C++, XAML, REST
Tools : GIT, Jira, Confluence, Jenkins, SonarQube, Microsoft Teams

Who Are You

We are looking for someone with at least a couple of years experience of client software development for the Windows platform, ideally experienced with UWP. You enjoy working in a small team and you firmly believe that cooperation and team work is the way to success. Continuous improvement, working closely together and discussion is the way to learn from each other. If you also have experience in security, great, else we at least expect a strong interest and willingness to learn!
