Karoon Energy Brazil is hiring!


Karoon Energy is expanding its presence in Brazil and is looking for a Senior Development Geologist to join the Rio de Janeiro based subsurface team.

Candidate profile:

  • Development geologist with strong background in reservoir modeling
  • 10+ years of relevant experience preferred
  • Proactive and able to work in a dynamic environment
  • Proven ability to deliver through previous work experience.
  • Proficiency in industry standard modeling packages (Petrel etc)
  • Fluent in English
  • Valid work visa for Brazil

Responsibilities:

  • Working in a multidisciplinary team, lead the construction of static reservoir models, integrating geological, geophysical, petrophysical, and dynamic data
  • Analysis and evaluation of all geological data
  • Participate in well planning, support drilling activity and prepare post-well assessments
  • Contribute to reports and presentations (external, government, banks, etc)
  • Provide input to reserve certifications
  • Supervise third-party geological studies to be carried out on behalf of the company.

You can send your cv to rh@karoon.com.br
