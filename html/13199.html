Position Overview

As a Data Engineer of the Business Intelligence (BI) Team at Genesis, you will be responsible for expanding and optimizing our data pipeline and data warehouse to fulfill business/analysis requirements.

You will support our data analysts on data initiatives and will ensure data delivery and data veracity are qualified for ongoing projects and much more. The Data Engineer requires a strong data processing background coupled with an ability to ETL, data process automation and data collection, so we can operate data warehouse to support to make data-driven development decisions.

Responsibilities
  • Work with analysts and development teams to understand internal data sources, to create and maintain optimal data pipeline architecture.
  • Work with analysts to understand our business goal, to build up and maintain a data warehouse.
  • Work with analysts to crawl external data sources to enrich data variety.
  • Assemble complex data sources to meet functional / non-functional business requirements.
  • Identify, design and implement internal process improvements: automating manual processes, optimizing data delivery, re-design infrastructure for data velocity/veracity.
  • Build the infrastructure required for optimal ETL of data from a wide variety of data sources using SQL, Python and Google Cloud Platform.
  • Operate and maintain data visualization platforms: Tableau Server.
  • Work with data and analytics experts to strive for greater functionality in our data system.
Requirements
  • Experience building and optimizing data pipelines and data warehouses.
  • The ability to work on and manage multiple concurrent projects for multiple stakeholders is essential
  • We are looking for a candidate with 3-5 years of experience in a Data Engineer role, who has attained a Graduate degree in Computer Science, Information System or related field. They should also have experience using the following software/tools:Experience with RDB/NoSQL databases.Experience with data pipeline and workflow management tools, such as Airflow.Experience with Google Cloud Platform/AWS: BigQuery, Cloud Functions, EC2, RDS, RedshiftExperience with object-oriented/object function scripting languages: Python, C++, etc.Experience with working in Linux environments.Experience operating various third-party data visualization platforms, such as Tableau, Power BI or Qlikview.Experience with crawling is a plus.
  • Fluent in Mandarin Chinese and English.
