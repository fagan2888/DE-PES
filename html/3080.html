Solicitar en el sitio web de la empresa
Overview

We Innovate. We Create. We Collaborate

We are seeking a Software Engineer to join our Data Engineering team in Buenos Aires, who will collaborate with team members in our New York, Atlanta, London, and Geneva offices.

Candidates must have excellent development skills using a range of Relational, Hierarchical, Time-series or NoSQL databases. The ideal candidate will have experience developing and supporting data-intensive, Cloud-based applications.

This position offers an opportunity to do highly visible work that will significantly contribute to the growth of Axioma’s business. We need someone who is hands-on, detail-oriented, and obsessed with building world-class Cloud-based services and platform components.

Responsibilities

As a Software Engineer you will:
  • Influence design decisions by evaluating requirements from subject matter experts, translating business requirements into detailed designs.
  • Develop and enhance database access layer used by development teams across different database technologies.
  • Work to build and enhance high-performance, scalable systems.

Required

Qualifications
  • Understanding of database design and/or development using SQL or NoSQL databases.
  • Experience developing data intensive systems.
  • Experience with developing applications using C#, Python, Java or similar programming language.
  • Fluent English speaking and writing.
  • Prior employment experience must include experience with one of the following:
  • Design and implementation of complex/distributed IT systems.
  • Developing ETL systems.

    • Preferred
    • Degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Sciences or related field.
    • Experience with Object-Relational Modeling (ORM) concepts and technologies.
    • Knowledge of SQL.
    • Experience with bi-temporal data modeling.
