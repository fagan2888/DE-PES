A world reknowned organization who is known for their digital transformation and innovation is looking for very skilled Mid, Senior and Lead Data Scientists to join them on their new office in in Cape town CBD on a permanent basis.


You will be joining more than just a company, you will be making an impact in the world of technology and Logistics.


Salary up to R1.2 Million


Job Function:

Software Engineering and Data Engineering at a complex level in accordance with specifications and agreed timescales. A highly skilled data engineer who has the potential and or ability to act as a technical lead, able to take responsibility for developing and leading a team and be accountable for the quality of its technical deliverables. You will work closely with technical architects, management and other teams to deliver value to our customers.


Responsibilities:

  • Create and maintain optimal data pipeline architecture
  • Responsible for leading a team
  • Responsible for the quality of the team’s technical deliverables
  • Responsible for sustaining the team’s delivery pace
  • Establish the technical vision with the team
  • Resolve technical issues within the team
  • Work with technical architects and other stakeholders to define technical options
  • Structure work for the team and support the Scrum master in managing the team’s capacity
  • Help with planning priorities across team
  • Assemble large, complex data sets that meet functional / non-functional business requirements
  • Identify, design, and implement internal process improvements: automating manual processes, optimizing data delivery, re-designing infrastructure for greater scalability, etc.
  • Build the infrastructure required for optimal extraction, transformation, and loading of data from a wide variety of data sources using SQL and AWS ‘big data’ technologies
  • Build analytics tools that utilize the data pipeline to provide actionable insights into customer acquisition, operational efficiency and other key business performance metrics
  • Work with stakeholders including the Executive, Product, Data and Design teams to assist with data-related technical issues and support their data infrastructure needs
  • Create data tools for analytics and data scientist team members that assist them in building into an innovative industry leader
  • Work with data and analytics experts to strive for greater functionality in our data systems.

Experience Required:

  • Great experience working with large datasets and automated ETLs
  • Experience with Python
  • Experience in the design and implementation of data flows
  • Experience with Hadoop and Spark
  • Experience in the design and implementation of REST APIs
  • Exposure to Cloud technologies
  • Exposure to practical database design

Advantageous Competencies:

  • Experience working closely with machine learning or analytics teams
  • Experience with development process such as agile and scrum

Additional Requirements:

  • Analytical & problem-solving skills
  • Deadline driven, able to work in highly pressurised environment
  • Team player
  • Good communication skills
