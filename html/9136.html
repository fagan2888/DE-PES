    Náplň (druh) práce
    We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist who will support our product, sales, leadership and marketing teams with insights gained from analyzing company data.

    The role is responsible for producing basic business intelligence reporting like daily, weekly and monthly automated reports plus data mining, and finding patterns in customer behavior and product usage. Our ideal candidate needs to have a substantial amount of experience to be able to create a comprehensive and contextual basis of understanding business and customer behavior. In this position, you will report and work directly with the CEO and need to be self-motivated and self-sufficient.

    This is a lifetime opportunity to build a sophisticated and technologically advanced system of business intelligence practically on a greenfield in one of the fastest-growing technological start-ups! Join us and take our dynamic environment to the next level!

    Responsibilities

  • Undertaking data collection, pre-processing, data-mining and analysis
  • Automating and visualizing business reporting
  • Proactively searching for and finding business growth indicators
  • Building machine learning

    • Requirements

  • You have proven experience in a similar data role and a degree in a quantitative discipline
  • You have experience with using statistical computer languages (Python/R, SQL, etc.) to manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets
  • You have experience with statistical methods used in advanced analytics
  • You have a good understanding of machine-learning algorithms
  • You are able to design and deliver prediction models based on a variety of machine learning approaches, applied in different areas (propensity to buy models, churn modeling, customer segmentation, etc.)
  • You have experience with analyzing data from 3rd party providers: Google Analytics, ECRM Drip, etc.
  • You have an analytical mind and business acumen
  • You are self-motivated and have a strong analytical and problem-solving aptitude
  • Fluent in English
