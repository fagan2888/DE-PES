About The Team

We are devops team focusing on enabling all mobile delivery teams to understand the quality of their code. In past half a year we delivered multiple internal tools to increase quality of mobile applications as well as cloud infrastructure for mobile CI. We are working closely with multiple delivery teams across US, Germany and Prague.

About You
  • You are willing to learn. You are always looking for new technologies.
  • You’re creative. You have a good imagination and you’re always thinking of new and interesting ideas.
  • You can think critically, identify opportunities, and clearly articulate your point of view.

Qualification And Experience
  • Proficient in any/one programming language (e.g. Python, Java)
  • Hands-on experience with the whole SDLC: Requirement, Design, Code, Test, Deploy
  • Deep understanding of the computer science and its application
  • Good understanding public clouds, like AWS, Azure and/or GCP.
  • Experience with CI/CD tools and writing pipelines.

Bonus Points
  • Understanding of Mobile application development and testing
  • Knowledge of microservices architecture and container orchestration frameworks
  • Experience with configuration management tools such as Ansible, Puppet, or Chef
  • In-depth knowledge of UNIX/Linux: command-line utilities; familiarity with system administration tools and concepts.
  • Experience with describing infrastructure as a code, for example with CloudFormation, Terraform
