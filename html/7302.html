We are so proud to be the only retailer in the top 5 Best Places to Work in Australia for 2019, having now made the list for the sixth consecutive year!


At MECCA we provide a dynamic, creative, high energy atmosphere and seek individuals who are customer focused, ambitious and determined. Our most important asset is our people so we ensure that we have a supportive and exciting work environment with unlimited opportunities for growth and development.


THE ROLE YOU COULD PLAY

Do you love Beauty? Is data what makes you jump out of bed in the morning? Well, MECCA Tech are head down, immersed in some incredibly innovative and exciting projects and we are looking for an intelligent and dynamic Data Engineer to join us on this tech transformation. The Data Engineer is responsible for development, implementation and maintenance of data integration, processing, storage and reporting solutions and will partner with the business in identifying the right requirements in order for us to truly be the #1 Beauty Destination.


Your extensive experience in designing and developing reporting and BI solutions, broad knowledge of data structures and relational databases will be called upon on day one. A self-motivated and curious individual, you are methodical and sceptical when working with data and you have a strong understanding of data analysis and data science techniques but also passionate about working with new methods and technologies.


WE THINK YOU WOULD BE PERFECT IF…

At MECCA, we live and breathe our values every day, and we get excited about jumping out of bed to fulfil our purpose of enabling our customers to look and feel their best. Our culture is welcoming, supportive and energetic but we also have high expectations for all our MECCA-mazing team members. If you approach life with the attitude that nothing is a problem and there is a solution for everything, and you take pride in being exceptional at your job, then you will fit right in!


Our perfect candidate has:

  • 3+ years of relational database structures and administration of SQL server/Oracle/MySQL/PostgreSQL platforms
  • Strong programming skills in Python with focus on data manipulation, ETL/ELT and analysis.
  • Data integration (ETL/ELT) methods and techniques - flat files (CSV, JSON, XML), SQL (PostgreSQL, Oracle, MSSQL, MySQL) and APIs
  • Experience with Cloud Computing architecture and services, preferably AWS (RDS, Aurora, EC2, S3)
  • Experience working in both Windows & Linux environment
  • Expert knowledge of structured query languages: T-SQL, pg/SQL, PL/SQL.
  • Database design, query optimization, data migration.
  • Strong interpersonal skills
  • Strong analytic skills and commercial acumen
  • Passion and enthusiasm about problem-solving and new challenges
  • Has excellent attention to detail and organisational skills
  • Team player mentality who enjoys working closely with a small team
  • Ability to successfully operate in an environment of change and rapid growth of operation
  • Exposure to retail or the beauty sector

WHAT WE CAN OFFER YOU IF YOU JOIN US

We are in the business of delivering happiness to our team members, our customers and our broader community, and we love to acknowledge each team member’s contribution to our success. From quarterly product allowances, unlimited 40% discount and free products, through to reward and recognition programs, professional development programs, and health and well-being initiatives, we promise you that our unique company culture will constantly surprise and delight you - we will never be finished in making MECCA Brands the best place to work!


A LITTLE MORE ABOUT US

At MECCA Brands, we bring customers the best in global beauty across our 4 retail concepts: MECCA Cosmetica, MECCA Maxima, MECCA and mecca.com.au. We offer an exclusive edit of over 100 brands, paired with exceptional customer service and beauty expertise within each retail environment.


Working at MECCA Brands will never be just a job. Our unique and positive culture ensures team members are surrounded by other incredible and inspiring people, amazing opportunities, experiences and challenges, enabling them to be the best they can be in everything they choose to do.

To learn more about life at MECCA Brands and to apply for this position, please visit www.mecca.com.au/careers.
