Senior Electronic Engineer (IC Design)
My client is a Hong Kong based leading player in Technology Hardware sector with both internal R&D and manufacturing, definitely driving a revolution in IC manufacturing. They are now looking for a Senior Electrical Engineer to join their team.

Responsibilities
  • Design electrical panels and enclosures for industrial use
  • Responsible for electronic design from initial design to formal release collaborate with related parties
  • Participate in digital IP development/evaluation and SOC integration;
  • Protocol specification study, signal processing algorithm analysis, design prototyping and IC implementation;
  • Carry out micro-architecture design, RTL coding, logic synthesis, FPGA prototyping and IC implementation.
  • Carry out module level and top level verifications, and board level evaluation.
  • Interface with system design team and backend design team.

Requirements
  • Graduate in Electronic Engineering or equivalent.
  • 5+ years relevant experiences in semi-conductor industry is preferred
  • Familiar with CAD software, e.g. Pads power logic & PCB
  • Experience in digital circuit design
  • Knowledge in ASIC/FPGA design methodology;
  • Carry out micro-architecture design, RTL coding, logic synthesis, FPGA prototyping and IC implementation.
  • Carry out module level and top level verifications, and board level evaluation.
  • Interface with system design team and backend design team.
  • Experience in image, video or audio signal processing system implementation on DSP or RISC processors, especially with detailed level knowledge of video codecs, e.g. MPEG-4/H.264/VC-1 is an advance;
  • Able to code in 'C' and Verilog RTL;
