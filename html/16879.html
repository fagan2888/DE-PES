Onboarding Engineer

Schonfeld Singapore

1 day ago

Apply

Schonfeld is seeking a highly analytical engineer to join Schonfeld's Platform Onboarding team. The Platform Onboarding team is responsible for the end to end process of onboarding Portfolio Managers and brokers, including configuration, connectivity and FIX certifications.


The responsibilities of the role include:

- Performing FIX certifications

- Onboarding of Executing Broker DMA/SOR and Algo offerings into the Schonfeld EMS/OMS

- Configuration and onboarding of third party FIX drop copy sessions into proprietary risk solutions

- Onboarding of Portfolio Managers across all Schonfeld Front/Middle and Backoffice systems

- Documentation of onboarding procedures

Candidates must have:

- 5+ years of experience in financial services

- Bachelor’s degree

- Excellent communication skills

- Excellent problem-solving abilities and be highly analytical

- Experience performing FIX certifications

- Experience with programmatic use of REST APIs

- Expert level knowledge of the FIX protocol

- Strong knowledge of Linux

- Hands on experience with networking

- Strong knowledge of SQL

- Strong scripting skills (Python, Bash, Perl)

- Trading system support experience (OMS/EMS)


The ideal candidate has experience with:

  • Front Office - EMS/OMS solutions
  • Middle Office – Portfolio Management systems
  • Back Office - End of day clearing systems


Our Culture

We have built a culture that encourages people to share their ideas and perspectives. Team members come together in a highly collaborative environment where we emphasize hands on problem solving, not status meetings. Engineers are expected to self-organize the best way to solve problem and are collectively responsible for the entire team’s delivery. At Schonfeld, we value individuals with a growth mindset, who can work across many different skill sets and contribute in many ways. We look for talent who raise others’ skills through teaching and broaden their own skill sets through learning.
