    • Description

    Do you want to work with the next revolution in computing, and are you burning to leverage state-of-the-art data technology to develop new products and services? Then this is a unique opportunity for you to use your strong development skills and your passion for data. At Agnitio, we believe our single most important asset is our people, and we are committed to attracting top talent at every level, across the entire organization.


    The position is based in Copenhagen and requires Danish residency.

    Background

    Agnitio is a technology company that provides best in breed software solutions for the Life Sciences industry. We are among the best in delivering Digital communication solutions for our customers including some of the leading companies within their field.


    Key responsibilities for the Data Engineer include

    • Find new ways to make our data warehouse support increasing customer needs
    • Investigate new technologies to extend and improve performance and quality of our data
    • Integrate into external data sources to extend the dataset
    • Help build a data platform to support assisted decision making and advanced analytics
    • Ensure qualitative data for the BI team


    Formal requirements

    • Minimum master’s degree in computer science, engineering or similar
    • Minimum 3+ years development experience with data warehouse solutions
    • Good analytic mind and able to think out of the box
    • Knowledge within RedShift, MS SQL and other data warehouse
    • Good understanding of RDBS
    • Expert in C#, T-SQL, SSIS and SSAS
    • Excellent knowledge in MDX and MS SQL
    • MCSE: Data Management and Analytics certification or similar is a plus
    • MCSA: SQL 2016 BI Development certification or similar is a plus
    • Knowledge within statistical modelling, predictive models and machine learning is a plus


    Personal qualities expected

    • Structured, analytical and very detail oriented
    • Self-motivating and proactive
    • Professional and service minded
    • Strong English skills, both verbal and written


    You will be part of an informal and international organization, in a fast-growing company. We are looking to fill this position immediately.
