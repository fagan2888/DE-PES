    Talent scout and Culture chez Pictet Technologies SA

    • Ideally positioned at the heart of Europe, Pictet Technologies, the Pictet Group’s software factory, has developed a unique concept. We have established ourselves as a group of strongly rooted, agile professionals , with a long-term vision and a deep sense of commitment .

    In just 3 years, we have created an open , team-driven and innovative culture . Pictet Technologies has become a key player within the Pictet Group and the Luxembourg IT community, recognized by several awards:

    • Luxembourg Great Place to Work 2018 and 2019 in the medium-size company category,
    • 2019 Luxembourg Flagship by ASPI,
    • 2019 CIO of the year .


    To support our continued growth, we are now looking for senior .NET Software Engineers. As part of this newly created team, you will work on one of our key application, supporting Pictet’s Client Register Solutions Department. As such you will be involved in the platform evolution from risk mitigation to functional and technical evolutions.


    Our ideal candidate should have the following attributes:


    => Is mandatory, a strong experience in :

    • .NET 4.5 (or superior) development with an excellent grasp on C# , ASP.NET Web API , WinForms , Entity Framework
    • SQL-Server developement and Transact-SQL mastery


    => Is a really great to have :

    • Working experience with C++ , DevExpress WinForms
    • Some knowledge of security concepts, and database optimization and indexing


    => But also :

    • Advanced degree in software engineering
    • Sound experience of writing and testing quality software using object-oriented concepts
    • Some knowledge of industrialized software development tools and practices such as Git, Jira, Confluence, refactoring, continuous integration and Docker based deployment just to name a few
    • A keen interest in Open Source technologies
    • Some web design skills to complement your core programming skills


    => Without forgetting :

    • An agile mind-set . It’s not a buzz word: it’s about collaboration, growing together, supporting more junior team members, intellectual humility and strong desire to learn
    • Proven ability to deliver against deadlines
    • True team player as teamwork is at the heart of everything we do
    • strong French and/or English communication skills, both written and verbal


    In addition to solid technical knowledge and experience, we are looking for passionate individuals with a well-rounded personality and a good sense of humour: we don’t compromise on these.


    What we offer

    ·      A working culture fostering collaboration, professional development and learning opportunities

    ·      Top-notch technologies and premium hardware

    ·      An opportunity to work and grow alongside inspiring individuals

    ·      Space for open and meaningful discussions

    ·      A safe place to share and promote your ideas and vision

    ·      Lifestyle in line with the tech world: flexitime, state of the art facilities, flat organization

    ·      Non-negotiable values: excellence, integrity, respect, fun


    Limited travel required. Please note that an EU valid work permit is required. Thank you.


    If you are passionate about software development, get in touch and contact us at jobs@pictet-technologies.com
