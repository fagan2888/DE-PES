Solution Consultant (m/w/d)

Emarsys Berlin, Berlin, Germany

Vor 1 Tag

Auf Firmenwebseite bewerben

Due to high growth and ongoing demand across the business, Emarsys are looking for a Solution Consultant that will join the wider professional services team and ensure prospects and existing clients are matched with tailored solutions to fit their requirements.


Working collaboratively with Emarsys’ Sales, Client Success & Technical departments, you will act as the conduit between business and technology, gathering and analysing client requirements and providing professionally designed creative solutions to ensure a smooth contract signature process whilst exceeding expectations.


This client facing role will seamlessly combine creative thinking and commercial awareness to successfully agree with the prospect/client on services to be provided, while contributing to post-sales activities to provide a world class experience.


YOUR AREA OF RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Present Emarsys solutions in the pre-sales phase, providing technical consultation on multiple aspects
  • Develop customised presentations and demonstrations to prove the solutions business value to the client
  • Conducts necessary testing and validation
  • 3rd party integration review (e.g. with MS Dynamics, Salesforce, Magento)
  • Take ownership of the data integration architecture review
  • Liaise with the commercial team to ensure up to date product knowledge and clear collaboration with on-going projects


YOUR PROFILE

  • Experience in a technical role (e.g. Pre-Sales Consultant, Solution Engineer) – within a SaaS Provider, ideally in a Marketing Tech capacity
  • Experience with integration projects (e.g. API, Databases, MS Dynamics, Salesforce, Magento, Recommendation Engines)
  • Demonstrated ability and gravitas to explain complex technical topics to both technical and non-technical stakeholders alike (including c-level), blending genuine passion with assured competency
  • A track record of creating and leading customer facing demonstrations
  • Willingness to travel up to 40% (including international travel)
  • Technical competence across Relational Databases, HTML, JavaScript, MySQL
  • Fluency in German and English


ADDITIONALLY DESIRED SKILLS

  • Experience with Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) applications for Online Marketing, CRM, or Campaign Management
  • Knowledge of the online marketing space (specifically E-Mail marketing) is a definite advantage
  • Commercial utilisation of RFP’s and RFI’s
  • Additional technical knowledge of Google Tag Manager, SDK, CSS
  • High level of initiative with the ability to work without supervision
  • Track record of coordinating multiple projects at different states, concurrently
  • A preference for a fast-paced, agile environment


WHAT WE OFFER

  • Join a continuously growing company with a global presence, commitment to innovation and a leading market position
  • An inspiring and empowering company culture: working alongside talented, friendly and passionate people
  • A modern office in central Berlin, easily accessible via public transport
  • A well-designed onboarding plan: we set you up for success and support your career with on-going training and development
  • Great benefits such as regular company breakfast, free fruit and coffee, various sports- and team events, employee bonus card, company pension scheme, permanent contract, regular health initiatives, 2 corporate volunteering days per annum etc.


At Emarsys, we are committed to building diverse teams with a rich variety of perspectives and experiences. If your job profile closely aligns with our essential requirements and business need, we encourage you to apply. We are dedicated to assessing your competencies, learning agility and passion and not your age, disability, familial status, sexual orientation, gender, race, ethnicity, religion or nationality.
