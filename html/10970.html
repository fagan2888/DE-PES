How Your Typical Day Would Go
  • Works closely with the various FSS teams to identify areas for standardization and automation
  • Understand business matters and translate those into technical solutions
  • Provide support, advice & guidance through research of subject area, to support business development and improvement
  • Participate in projects both for self and those involving other FSS and System Solutions team members
  • Communicate best practices and process standardization to FSS and Locations across multiple operating units

  • Provide functional application/solution expertise to facilitate the set-up, implementation, development, upgrade and ongoing maintenance of those applications and solutions
