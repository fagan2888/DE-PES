HEOR Sr. Manager

Novartis Argentina, AR

Hace 1 día

Why consider Novartis? 927 million. That’s how many lives our products touched in 2018. And while we’re proud of that fact, in this world of digital and technological transformation, we must also ask ourselves this how can we continue to improve and extend even more people’s lives?We believe the answers are found when curious, courageous and collaborative people like you are brought together in an inspiring environment.Where you’re given opportunities to explore the power of digital and data. Where you’re empowered to risk failure by taking smart risks, and where you’re surrounded by people who share your determination to tackle the world’s toughest medical challenges.
Your responsabilities
  • Define and coordinate the implementation of HEOR strategy in order to facilitate/obtain access for core and new products
  • Active participation in the design of Market Access Strategy for core and new products along with rest of Market Access Strategic Team & Franchises
  • Create, maintain partnerships and work proactively with decision makers, payers, Gov Inst, Industry Chambers in order to shape the environment through health economics, HTA & innovative access to improve a receptive environment for our Novartis PH portfolio.
  • Collaborate with the strategic health policy plan, and programs for government
  • Utilize Health Economics studies as a tool to promote value-based and evidence based pricing.
  • Participate and represent Novartis PH in external and internal HE/HTA platforms, liaison for Regional /Global Initiatives.
  • Lead MA, enable & support local projects regarding HTA, data registry, health economics.
  • Build HTA, RWE and payer partnerships to shape the healthcare, policy and regulatory environments, through collaboration with different academic and scientific institutions.
  • Create and/or follow up MA strategies (value propositions, innovative contracting agreements) in order to influence and accelerate favourable decisions towards Novartis PH portfolio, aligned with defined objectives.
