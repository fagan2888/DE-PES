Introduction

    • Your Role and Responsibilities
    Business Intelligence Consultant / Big Data Engineer

    Our consultants in the business analytics optimization team work in an inspiring environment at the intersection of business and technology, at both strategic and operational level. With clients insisting on getting the best results that renders sustainable benefits, IBM consultants relentlessly work with them to find the right answer. The solution spectrum is broad and often highly complex combining the substantial strengths of IBM in analytic methods and techniques, the ability of our research teams to solve problems of unprecedented scale and complexity, and the industry, business knowledge and skills of our consulting teams. In their work our experts are unmatched in their ability to help clients use advanced analytics, business intelligence and information strategy for optimal results.

    The position is based in Bratislava, Banska Bystrica or Kosice.

    What would be your responsibilities?
    • Making available, integrating, and optimizing structured and/or unstructured data using database products, methods and technologies - including Big Data
    • Typical examples of the deliverables are database designs, information models (logical, physical, dimensional, etc.), data migration plans, and data warehouses
    • Applying technical skills in an internal or external customer billable services and implementation environment
    What do you need for your success?
    • At least 2 years experience in IT sector
    • At least 2 years experience in some of the following technologies: Cognos BI, Business Objects BI, MS SSRS, Microstrategy, Oracle Enterprise BI Server,
    Pentaho BI suite, Tableau, SAS, R or any BI tool +
    • At least 2 years experience in some of the following technologies: IBM InfoSphere DataStage, Informatica PowerCenter, MS SSIS, Talend, Hadoop Data Integration, Oracla Data Integrator, respectively understanding of Oracle PL/SQL and/or MS SQL T-script
    • Experience with Big Data tools (Apache Hadoop, Apache Cassandra, Apache Hive, Apache Spark, Hortonworks and others), Cloud experience, DevOps experience
    • Experience in development & maintenance in RDBMS (IBM DB2 or preferable Oracle DB or Microsoft SQLServer)
    • Experience in development & maintenance of document-oriented database
    • Experience in Data Modeling / Database Design / Information Governance / Data Quality Methodology
    • Experience in writing technical specifications
    • Fluent English
    Highly Preferred Skills
    • Experience in development & maintenance in RDBMS (IBM DB2 or preferable Oracle DB or Microsoft SQLServer)
    • Experience in development & maintenance of document-oriented database
    • Experience with Big Data technologies and Cloud solutions
    • Good command of German language
    What can you expect of us?
    • Salary starting from 1700 EUR gross, depending on relevant experience and language proficiency
    • Annual performance bonus payment
    • Performance evaluation on regular basis
    • Subsidized meal-tickets
    • Flexible working time and home office
    • Three additional vacation days
    • Up to three self-recovery days
    • 100% sick leave compensation
    • Life insurance and supplementary pension savings program
    • Private medical sanatorium
    • Long term career development and possibility of internal rotation
    • Classroom based trainings and 5000 e-learning courses – both internal and external
    • Recommend-a-Friend – get a bonus in the employee referral program
    • MultiSport card
    • Special IBM discounts
