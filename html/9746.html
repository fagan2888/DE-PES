Data Engineer (Java & Big Data Competences)

SEB Vilnius, LT

5 days ago

Apply on company website
Would you like to work in an international team of data engineering experts responsible for delivering large-scale Big Data solution for SEB? Do you want to play a central role in our exciting transformational journey to become the most data-driven bank in the world? How about doing that in a company with a strong belief that entrepreneurial minds and innovative companies are key in creating a better world? Now we are looking for a Data Engineer to join our team.

About The Position

As Data Engineer in Data Lake unit you will work in an environment with multiple Agile teams located in both Stockholm and Vilnius, consisting of experts in various Big Data technologies. Your main responsibilities will be to develop and maintain new technical capabilities within the Hadoop data lake and create data pipelines to make data available for various business users and applications.

Our technical environment consists but is not limited to Java, Scala, Python, Hadoop, Kafka, NiFi, Spark, Grafana and Elastic Search. Here you will have a chance to make a difference and work with challenging tasks. You will have great opportunities to both develop and take further steps in your career working within a highly collaborative environment.

Your profile

To succeed in your role with us, you should be a GEEK and you should LOVE solving ENGINEERING problems. In order to be successful you most probably have extensive experience in Java programming language. Having experience with Linux, Scala, Python, Hadoop, Kafka, NiFi, Spark, Grafana or Elastic Search would be considered as an advantage.

We expect you to have an analytical mindset and an ability to solve complex technical problems as well as have a passion for discoveries and challenges that drive your personal development and professional growth. You should enjoy working with new technologies, encourage and drive change and continuous improvement in teamwork, tools and processes you will be using. Being a team player open for growth and learning is thus crucially important for the role.

In our daily job, we work closely with our team-mates in Stockholm therefore traveling might be included in your role (3-5 days a month).

Working at SEB

You will work at a workplace characterised by entrepreneurship, openness and genuine commitment. Working with us will give you the possibility to grow your talent through everyday learning, extensive training and other development opportunities. We see the value of a flexible working life, wellness benefits and a healthy lifestyle for our employees. Also, since the start of our company, we have taken an active role in our society’s social development and all our employees have the opportunity to contribute to various social projects.
