We are looking for a Data Scientist with a product or marketing analytics background to join Standards Australia.
Who is Standards Australia?

We are an independent not-for-profit organisation, recognised by the Australian Government as the peak non-government Standards body. We develop internationally aligned Australian Standards in the national interest and work closely with government, industry, consumer and stakeholder groups.

We are a team of 150+ employees based in a great Sydney CBD location close to various forms of public transport. We have a collaborative, friendly and professional working environment where we encourage self-growth and career development.

About The Role

We are looking for a Data Scientist with a product or marketing analytics background to join Standards Australia.

As part of the product department, you will provide insights that will help us to deliver a quality experience to our customers. You will be responsible for structuring, analysing and interpreting information hidden in vast amounts of unstructured data.

This Role Will Be Centered On
  • Data discovery – to enable content enrichment which will, in turn, enable the business to deliver better products and services to its customers
  • Data experimentation – to help us acquire new and keep current customers engaged by providing utility
What you’ll do
  • Implement ways to enrich our content with metadata in order to make it more discoverable and useful to customers
  • Proactively perform data exploration to discover future opportunities
  • Drive innovation by developing new experimentation methodologies and statistical techniques that could sharpen our decision-making process.
  • Instrument tracking (e.g. via Heap, Google Analytics) for customer analysis
  • Lead the design, analysis, and interpretation of experiments that shape decision-making around acquisition and engagement marketing
  • Analyse marketing and product performance data through a variety of tools
  • Build performance dashboards (e.g. Tableau, Power BI)
  • Deploy Agile testing strategies (e.g. A/B testing) to quantify the impact of performance recommendations
  • Use advanced analytics and machine learning to detect significant variables/ features on an ongoing basis that detect emerging signals in customer behaviours
What we are looking for

We are looking for an effective communicator who is self-motivated and able to drive insights into actions that will improve business results. The successful candidate will have demonstrated leadership in applying analytic techniques to deliver actionable insights from data.

They Will Also
  • Have 5+ years’ experience within Data Science/Machine Learning/AI
  • Be able to use advanced segmentation techniques including statistical regression and machine learning
  • Be efficient in the use of statistical techniques, enabling easy interpretation and analysis of large amounts of data
  • Have an advanced level of working knowledge in SQL and Excel is essential
  • Be proficient with data / analytical packages, e.g. Tableau, PowerBI. R, Python
  • Have experience in working with complex datasets to deliver measurable impact
  • Be committed to delivering quality work with excellent attention to detail
  • Have excellent influencing skills and be able to gain buy-in and change mindsets to deliver long term sustainable and positive change
Working for Standards Australia

Integrity, Courage, Accountability, Respect and Excellence form the basis of all we do. We measure our success not only on the outcomes but on the path we took to get there.
We offer a flexible and positive environment where individual and team efforts are recognised and rewarded. The company is committed to a workplace which supports equal employment opportunities.

What now?

If this sounds like an environment that will bring out the best in you, please submit your resume and cover letter with your salary expectations. Eligible applicants must be a Permanent Resident or have legal rights to work in Australia.
