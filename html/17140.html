Mondi Group Vienna, AT

Auf Firmenwebseite bewerben
Are you passionate about using data to solve real world problems that arise in the paper and packaging industry? Do you feel passionate about using data of varied types and formats arising from multiple machines and processes? Does it excite you that you can extract impactful insights and decisions from this data?

If yes, let’s talk!

We are looking for high calibre Data Engineers to become a part of our digital transformation unit MONDI DX and work on challenging projects from all areas of paper and packaging industry. You will collaborate with our Data Scientists to map data fields to hypotheses and curate, wrangle, and prepare data for use in advanced analytical models and deliver our Business Units strategic advantages in their day-to-day operations.

You will work in an extremely agile and energetic environment with colleagues across Mondi. Your team members and stakeholders will include the business and process experts at Mondi, IT professionals from across the organizations, Data Scientists and Engineers.

Your Responsibilities-
  • help our Business Units model their data landscape, build high quality data transformation and extraction pipelines and define secure exchange approaches
  • work with multiple stakeholders to understand their data, and then design the ingestion process to store the data and prepare it for Data Analytics
  • handle vast amounts of data – work on database design and development as well as data integration and ingestion
  • work closely with data science colleagues and implement the best possible solutions for Mondi


Our Requirements-
  • strong knowledge of software development tools and methodologies
  • ability to develop creative and efficient solutions
  • tolerance in dealing with bad quality data
  • data security & governance expertise
  • strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • ability to work at an abstract level and gain consensus

Languages-
  • fluent English or German skills, knowledge of other languages is valued

Education-
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent
  • Master’s degree preferred


Experience-
  • 3+ years of experience in development of data platforms and pipelines
  • 3+ years of hands-on experience in one or more of the following- Python, Scala, Java and Unix/Linux systems
  • 3+ years of experience in SQL and designing and working with relational databases
  • 2+ years of experience with data modelling, design patterns, building highly scalable and secure solutions
  • 2+ years of experience in design and implementation of Big Data Solutions and distributed applications
  • experience in data warehouse or database architecture
  • experience with Big Data migrations or IT transformations, with architecting or operating solutions built on MS Azure, IBM Cloud, etc. is advantageous
  • experience working within a start-up or start-up like working environment is a plus


Other-
  • ability to work effectively with people at all levels in an organization
  • excellent interpersonal, leadership and communication skills
  • ability to communicate complex ideas effectively – both verbally and in writing
  • relevant clarification on big data stacks and platform (AWS Big Data, Cloudera, Apache Spark, etc.)

We offer-
  • challenging tasks and inspiring projects within a successful, dynamic and flexible environment
  • an international and encouraging market leader of the packaging & paper industry
  • an attractive working place in the Group office Vienna
  • engaging social benefits (e.g. health programs, Jobticket, special conditions, etc.)
  • individual development opportunities (e.g. The Mondi Academy)
  • the opportunity to unfold (y)our true potential within Mondi

Immediate start date
