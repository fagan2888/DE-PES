Sunnet Systems and Datacom Services Limited is an Information and Communications Technology company established to deliver world class information technology infrastructure, solution design and implementation, first class systems support and service delivery level that is constantly raised to exceed customers’ expectation and satisfaction.

We Are Recruiting To Fill The Position Below

Job Title: I.T Support Engineer

Location: Lagos

Key Responsibilities
  • Maintaining and administering computer networks and related computing environments including systems software, hardware and configurations.
  • Performing disaster recovery operations and data backups when required.
  • Troubleshooting, diagnosing and resolving hardware, software, and other network and systems problem
  • Replacing faulty network hardware components when required.
  • Coordinating computer network access and use.
  • Designing, configuring and testing networking software, computer hardware, and operating system software etc.


Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s Degree or Higher National Diploma (or Equivalent) in Computer Science/Engineer or a related discipline
  • 2+ years of experience in I.T industry.


Competencies
  • Strong understanding of network infrastructure and hardware
  • Ability to think through problems and visualize solutions
  • Ability to create accurate network diagrams and documentation for design and planning network communication systems.
  • Ability to quickly learn unfamiliar technology and products using documentation and internet resources.

30th August, 2019.
