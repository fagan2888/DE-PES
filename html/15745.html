Job Purpose

The role is critical in assisting with the visionary direction for technology solutions as well as facilitating, coordinating, and developing technical solutions for approved projects, system improvements, and ad-hoc or emergency requests. The Sr. Software Engineer should have proven responsibilities for strong understanding of coding and deploying the software development projects, enhancing and optimizing existing products, as well as strong knowledge of designing web/mobile applications understanding fundamentals of user experience in Korean Market.


Key Accountabilities

  • Ability to collaborate well with other peer developers and teams to carry out that the produced are well-constructed as aligned with product strategy

  • Ability to understand and identify business processes and link to customer research to improve services and experiences

  • Ability to resolve ambiguous requirements and have problem-solving skills

  • Establishing and guiding the website’s architecture using WordPress

  • Excellent communication skills with peers and cross-functional teams and adapt easily to changing priorities and business needs

  • Work with product team and software developers to improve existing product service and develop new platform

  • Passionate about product quality and willing to help improve standards technology

  • Experience in improving the design of mobile app/web application’s content to improve customer experience based on feedback analysis


Education: Bachelor of Computer Science/ Software Engineering or related technical field

Experiences, Skillsets & Personal Competencies

  • 5+ years of experience in software development of full product life-cycles

  • Strong experience on Install, configure and administrate WordPress sites for at least 2 to 3 years

  • Strong experience in front-end technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery

  • Good experience on JavaScript frameworks like Ember, React, or JS-based build tools like Grunt, Gulp, and Bower

  • Ability to build a web platform from scratch using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and TypeScript

  • Solid understanding of the relationship between HTML and search engine optimization (SEO)

  • Familiarity with differences between HTML5 and older versions of the standard

  • Experience designing and developing responsive design and adaptive design

  • Comfortable working with debugging tools like Firebug, Chrome inspector, etc.

  • Ability to understand CSS changes and their ramifications to ensure consistent style across platforms and browsers

  • Ability to convert comprehensive layout and wireframes into working HTML page

  • Knowledge of how to interact with RESTful APIs and formats (JSON, XML)

  • Proficient understanding of code versioning tools such as Git Hub, SVN, and Mercurial

  • Strong understanding of PHP back-end development

  • Strong experience in migrating the content from the other content management systems (CMS) to WordPress

  • Experience with web analytics reporting tools (Google Analytics, Moz, GWT, etc

  • Ability to perform duties in a very fast pace environment and ability to learn new technology quickly

  • Excellent technical analysis and problem-solving skills

  • Excellent communication skills. Ability to communicate effectively with an executive audience, and clearly communicate technical issues to a non-technical audience.
