Company Description

About the company:

Thinking Capital is the leader in the Canadian Alternative Lending space, using technology to be at the forefront of the FinTech industry. Since 2006, they have helped more than 10,000 small-to-medium sized Canadian businesses reach their full potential. By combining their people and technology, We are transforming the way business owners get the capital they need to grow. For more information, visit thinkingcapital.ca.

Job Description

Your role:

The DataOps Engineer applies Agile development, DevOps methods and statistical process controls to support data operations and analytics. DataOps orchestrates and automates the various data pipelines to make data consumption easier and more efficient while maintaining a high level of data quality.

Your Tasks
  • Supporting production data infrastructure (ETL, API, DB) to ensure data availability and accuracy.
  • Orchestrating data pipelines from source systems to data warehouses/stores and consumption endpoints
  • Automating data controls and data quality measures to support data governance initiatives
  • Tools/skills: AWS (S3, Redshift, Lambda, Glue ...), Python, shell scripting, Apache (Airflow, Presto ...)
Qualifications

Apply If You Have The Following Skillset
  • Strong knowledge of relational databases.
  • Good knowledge of AWS (S3, RDS, Redshift, etc.).
  • Experience development in Python, shell scripting.
  • Understanding of modern APIs (REST, JSON, etc.).
  • Understanding of open source data product (Airflow, Presto, etc.).
