Data Engineer

Hire Resolve Centurion, ZA

5 days ago

Apply on company website
Job Description

A well establish retailer is looking for a skilled Data Engineer to join their experience and talented team in the Centurion area. This could be the perfect opportunity for you!

Requirements
  • B.Sc. Computer Science or similar
  • 5+ years’ experience
  • Work closely with Data and Analytics Team to fulfil the department's quarterly objectives and ensure business deliver effective solutions
  • Design, build, and evolve custom ETL processes for feeding systems
  • Design, implement, QA and deploy ETL transformations to enhance the Data Eco System and workflows to load, analyses and archive customer transactional data
  • Ensure data quality is maintained throughout all managed systems
  • Perform data quality analysis and introduce monitors and alerts to maintain it
  • Help ensure best practices and technologies are used in the department by constantly ensuring data integrity is maintained across all data touch points
  • Build infrastructure to automate extremely high volumes of data delivery and creatively solve data volume and scaling challenges
  • No applications considered after 13th November 2019
Written applications to the following e-mail: it.jobs@hireresolve.co.za or fax to: 086 600 7977, you can also contact Daniella on 011 807 8064 or visit our website on www.hireresolve.co.za

Correspondence will only be conducted with short listed candidates. Should you not hear from us

within 3 days, please consider your application unsuccessful.
