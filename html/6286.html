    • Support the Head of Procurement in the digitalization transformation of the management and operation standards of the Group Procurement Team

    • Proposes organizational improvements of procurement functions which includes but not limited to refinement, redesign of existing and new SOPs, processes and procedures
    • Transform the food buying processes, bring the safety, quality and efficiency to the next level
    • Work with other Aden divisions such as BUs and Divisions to realize transparent and cost effective buying process
    • Champion the computerization of all buying processes and building up the logistics database of Aden for food and non-food product lines
    • Validates and ensure all existing sites are fully compliant with Aden’s buying policies and ensuring anti-corruption measures are implemented and in place
    • Develop and enhance the capabilities of all procurement personnel in being able to deliver effective and cost efficient purchases
    • Provide mentoring to junior procurement staff
    • Ensure all transition processes are set in place and can be implemented smoothly for all purchases


    Key Skills and Competencies:

    • Customer-oriented with good commercial sense and teamwork spirit;
    • English and Mandarin reading and writing skill,
    • Self-motivated, hard-working and good health;
    • Flexible to travel often in Mainland China;
    • Strong PC and information technology skills; with working knowledge of procurement processes and management of logistics databases;
    • Possess a service aptitude and a customer-service approach to problem resolution;
    • Strong skills in vendors management and contract negotiation;
    • Degree Qualified; Overseas Chinese or Expat preferred
    • At least 10 - 15 years’ total combined experience in procurement / logistics management
