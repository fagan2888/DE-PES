Reasons to work with us:

- Attractive salary package based on experience, skills and profile

- Being part of a dynamic and professional team

- Central office location

- Flexible, open and relaxed work environment

- Direct and open communication at all levels


What you need for this position:

-Expertise in developing Python applications for data processing

-Knowledge of GCP tools (PubSub, Dataflow, CloudComposer, DataProc)

-Knowledge of the web domain and mobile applications and their implications for data

-Experience with SQL and NoSQL technologies, as well as streaming tools

-Good interpersonal skills

-Strong cross team collaboration

-Good communication skills and fluent in both writing and speaking English


What you will be doing:

-The provision of data on GCP (in batch mode or in streaming mode as needed)

-Work in an agile environment and collaborate with data scientists, developers and product owners to identify the most relevant projects to contribute

-Industrialize all the work carried out by data scientists si model supervision

-Creation of a user data universe (datalake) that can be used by all the services of the group

- Understand the goals and strategy behind each project from conception through completion

- Work efficiently without close supervision

- Communicate effectively with project manager, technical leaders and other internal team members

- Identify opportunities for process improvement and make constructive suggestions for change

- Maintain a direct relationship with the client

- Adapt and solve exciting problems with new technologies

- Keep up-to-date with developing technologies and showcase them in your work

It is very rewarding to be part of a team that contributes to a client's growth and success. It's exciting to see innovative products I've worked on come to market.

- Senior Software Engineer, Gemini Solutions
