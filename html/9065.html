Job Description

Senior Civil Engineer
Golder Associates Africa is seeking a Registered Senior Civil Engineer to join the Mine Water Division in Midrand, South Africa. The selected candidate will be tasked to enhance the technical depth, experience profile and skills in mine water division.

Responsibilities Of The Role Will Include

Experience in civil engineering design of infrastructure in the industrial environment will be an advantage. Lead design engineer on projects that focus on the following in both in mines and industrial sites:

Civil engineering infrastructure including pollution control dams, clean and dirty water separation structure, river / channel diversions, feed water dams, stormwater drains, sewage and water reticulation and water treatment plants.
  • Lead engineer during the design process phases, i.e. concept, pre-feasibility, feasibility and detailed design phases of a project.
  • Provide technical advice and guidance on integrated infrastructure design issues to clients (internal & external);
  • As part of support to the divisional leader to develop business develop relationship with mining and industrial clients;
  • Negotiate and review engineering services contracts as and when required by the divisional leader;
  • Use advanced engineering techniques, tools and practices to integrate engineering designs for project decision making / recommendations;
  • Keep abreast of advancing engineering infrastructure technologies, methods and research & development;
  • Participate in training activities, mentoring and transferring knowledge to young design engineers;
  • Under the guidance of the divisional leader plan, develop schedules, coordinate and direct manpower on engineering projects to meet completion deadlines.
  • Supervise, coordinate and review / evaluate activities carried out by junior engineers;
  • Provide safety leadership in identifying and alleviating / eliminating health and safety hazards;
  • OH&S incident reporting;
  • Other duties as required;
  • Work with management to deliver on Golder engineering services strategic priorities;
  • Support and mentor junior engineers through the ECSA registration process.

The Successful Candidate Will Have a Background In
  • University Degree in the Civil Engineering
  • Professional Registration at ECSA (Min 10 years after professional registration)
  • At least 15 years of post-graduate experience
  • Member of FIDIC (International Federation of Consulting Engineers)
  • Experience in Legislation related to Infrastructure and Water Engineering / Environment
  • Experience applying engineering design standards, benchmarked against local and international standards
  • Engineering design experience at a consulting firm
  • Exposure to mining and industrial infrastructure and waste projects
  • Experience in handling and disposal of mining and industrial waste
  • Project Management of engineering design teams.
  • MS Word, MS Excel, MS Projects & MS PowerPoint
  • Drafting suite i.e. AutoCAD, Civil 3D, etc. (Basic knowledge)
  • Understanding of project management principles
  • Expertise in the design of:
  • Surface water infrastructure
  • Surface infrastructure
  • Mine related waste
  • Mine water reclamation plant waste
  • Mining products

Core Competencies
  • Be innovative in developing approaches and products in water engineering and digital transformation
  • Displays strong project management and report writing skills.
  • Takes direction from and supports intermediate and senior staff to achieve project and business goals.
  • Displays personal leadership in completion of work tasks.
  • Makes basic decisions with input from intermediate and senior staff.
  • Contributes to projects with a major focus on data collection and processing with an increasing role in analysis and interpretation.
  • Has an aptitude and interest in model development and application.
  • Contributes to sections of reports including description of investigations, results and analysis.
  • Employees focus on setting a positive example by identifying and reporting hazards, near misses and incidents.
  • Displays a 'safety first' focus and is setting a positive example by identifying and reporting hazards, near misses and incidents.
