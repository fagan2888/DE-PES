One of the major worldwide actors in the Software sector is now looking for its new DevOps Engineer .

You will be part of a multicultural environment counting 100 employees around the world giving you the opportunity to benefit from great growth opportunities .


You will be part of a team that supports its stakeholders in leveraging and interpreting data and make evidence-based decisions for their commercial strategy.


You will work on ambitious projects with major responsibilities which will allow you to keep on a successful path.


Your tasks:


  • You will work alongside with different teams to understand their business requirements and translate them into solutions

  • You will have responsibilities in all phases of the project


Your profile:


  • You have a bachelor degree or equivalent work experience
  • DevOps Engineer Kubernate, Puppet, Ansible
  • You speak fluently English, French or Dutch is a plus


Your benefits:


  • A salary up to 60 000 € depending on your experience
  • You will benefit from a full package including a car company, fuel card, net expenses, bonus, meal vouchers...
  • You will have the opportunity to take free training to develop your skills and enhance your career
  • You will join an international environment where you will have full of growth opportunities
  • You will have the chance to work for a well-known company where success is a one-way street !
  • You will be part of a prestigious group with an international network where you will be delighted every day
  • You will be part of a working family where you will feel appreciated for your hard work and praised as deserved
