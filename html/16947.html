Tumaini Consulting Johannesburg, ZA

1 year ago

Apply on company website
Job Description

Our client, within a well-established Insurance company is currently looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join their team. The ideal candidate is required to have Insurance or Financial Services experience and have excellent data experience.

Requirements
  • BSc Mathematics, Informatics & Computer Science, Quantitative Science, Statistics or Actuarial Science
  • Must have SAS, R, Phython
  • Machine learning experience is advantageous
  • Data Mining and Data Manipulation is advantageous
  • 3-5 years’ experience within an Insurance company and within a Data role
If you would like to apply for this position, please send transcripts with applications.

Should you meet the requirements for this position, please email your updated CV attached to finance.jobs@tumaini.co.za, alternatively contact Bianca Hauser on 031 350 4018 or visit our website, www.tumaini.co.za. Correspondence will only be conducted with short listed candidates. Should you not hear from us within 3 days, please consider your application unsuccessful.
