Data Analyst

Peoplebank Wan Chai, HK

4 days ago

Apply on company website


Successful candidates will be contracted to our client in public sector to perform the below duties:

Job Responsibilities

  • Perform data mining, interpret data, analyse results, like trends and patterns, in complex data sets using statistical / segmentation techniques;
  • Work with business users to identify the needs / requirements of data analytics;
  • Participate in data analytics projects for data modelling, solution design and development, data presentation and visualization;
  • Perform data integration, ETL and data mapping activities; and
  • Compile system documentation such as manuals and specifications.

Qualification Requirements

  • Bachelor degree or equivalent in Computer Science, Information Management, Mathematics, Statistics, or related disciplines;
  • At least 3 years of solid experience in the capacity of Data Analyst;
  • Good command of Python, R and SQL;
  • Proven experience with Tableau in building Tableau Prep workflows and dynamic dashboards;
  • Good experience in Data Warehouse and Relational Databases;
  • Experience with ETL tools - Informatica Powercenter / SSIS;
  • Experience in Cloud platform, such as AWS, Azure; and
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.

"All information provided by applicants will be treated as strictly confidential and will be used for recruitment purposes only."

Contact: Girly Yeung
