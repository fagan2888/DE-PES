About Us

Βeat is a mobile app that transforms the way people move in their cities. Tap a button on your smartphone and you get a ride in just a few minutes. Beat's mission is to become part of people's everyday life by making transportation more affordable, convenient, efficient and safe, while providing enormous economic opportunities to thousands of people who wish to earn extra income as drivers.

Beat is part of the FREE NOW group, the ride-hailing joint venture of BMW and Daimler. The app was founded in Athens, Greece, starting its life as "TaxiBeat". Within 3 years of operations in the Greek capital, Beat extended its service in Latin America, starting from Peru. Today, it's the fastest growing ride-hailing service in this region, serving millions of rides every day. Beat is currently available in Greece, Peru, Chile, Colombia and Mexico and has a Tech Innovation and Engineering Hub in the Netherlands.

About The Role

We are looking for a Senior DevOps Engineer with experience in Big Data Systems to join our fantastic highly skilled team and to help us manage, develop and extend our growing Big Data infrastructure. As a member of our engineering team, you will help build the next generation of our cloud-based Big Data stack.The position is remote, based in Europe.

What You'll Do Day In Day Out
  • Design architectures for real-time or near-real-time data management
  • Implement new and build upon existing systems and tools to handle ETL processes
  • Design and deploy pipelines that handle Machine Learning workflows from preprocessing and model training to production deployment
  • Work with Backend and Big Data developers to help build and deploy data-driven microservices
  • Work with our Machine Learning and Analytics teams and help design, implement and deploy the next generation of our Big Data and ML applications
  • Participate in on-call rotations to troubleshoot infrastructure and services issues
  • Monitor, maintain, automate and enhance our Hadoop based Big Data Infrastructure using modern tools and best practices
  • Diagnose, troubleshoot and resolve issues related to our Big Data stack. Preempt and respond to incidents


What You Need To Have Experience With
  • Administration of distributed systems (Hadoop would be a major plus)
  • UNIX-based systems administration and scripting
  • Major cloud providers (AWS, GCE or Azure)
  • Configuration management tools (Ansible, Chef or Puppet)
  • Infrastructure as Code tools (Terraform, Cloudformation)
  • CI/CD best practices and tools (Jenkins, Gitlab CI)
  • Python programming skills
  • SQL and NoSQL database systems (MySQL, MongoDB, Elasticsearch)


What's Nice To Have Experience/familiarity With
  • Containers and container orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)
  • ML frameworks (Kubeflow, MLflow)
  • Message Brokers (RabbitMQ, Kafka)
  • Scala, Java or Go programming languages


What's In It For You
  • Competitive full time salary
  • Private Health Coverage on the Company's group program.
  • Beat rides-travel in our city for free, at the tap of a button.
  • Breakfast, high quality daily lunch on a very low cost, fruit and snacks all day long
  • Flexible working hours, top Line tools, Spanish Lessons
  • Opportunities to participate in cutting edge trainings and conferences of your choice in Greece and abroad
  • A great opportunity to grow and work with the most amazing people in the industry.
  • Being part of an environment that offers challenging goals, autonomy and mentoring, which creates incredible opportunities, both for you and the company.
