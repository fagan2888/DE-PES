Independently named by FastCompany as one of the world’s most innovative companies in data science, Arimo’s Behavioral AI software is aimed at delivering predictive insights in commercial IoT applications.

Arimo's capabilities harness the behaviors of machines, devices, customers, and other entities to provide the most accurate predictions utilizing Deep Learning. With Arimo Behavioral AI™, leading companies are creating competitive advantage through new predictive insights, and delivering new services to their customers.

Our strong team includes executives with deep product and engineering experience. They have held senior positions at Google, Yahoo, Amazon, SAP, Microsoft, and VMware. Team members hold advanced degrees in computer science and business from leading universities. Arimo was previously funded by Andreessen Horowitz, Lightspeed Ventures, and Bloomberg Beta. To learn more, visit us at https://arimo.com.

Arimo is growing rapidly and now offering a position for Data Engineer . Passionate about building big data products and solving hard problems in creative ways? Join us and be a part of this exciting journey!

Responsibilities
  • Work side by side with our Data Science team to build and automate data pipeline, data ETL, etc. on distributed data processing platform such as Spark.
  • Process big data from Parquet/S3 to Tensorflow without CSV/TFRecords
  • Prepare data inputs for a generic blueprint "model builder"
  • Build model serving for Keras / PyTorch
  • Build production data pipeline for daily ETL and model retraining
  • End-to-end data processing, troubleshooting, and problem diagnosis.


Required Skills And Qualifications
  • 3+ years of experience working with large-scale datasets.
  • Solid experience in Spark and its ecosystems (Hadoop, Mesos, YARN, etc.)
  • Expertise in SQL scripting, data warehousing, and ETL development.
  • Proficiency in programming languages like Java, Scala, Python, etc.
  • Open-minded and excited to learn new things. Have strong opinions but willing to change with new data.
  • Minimum BS degree in CS or related discipline, MS/ Ph.D. preferred.

Arimo offers competitive compensation packages commensurate with your experience. We offer full benefits, company stock options, continual career & compensation growth, and many other perks.
