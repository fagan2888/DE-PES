Want to be part of our story?

Job Description

Responsibilities of a Business Intelligence Engineer within OpenBet/SG Digital:
  • Collaborate with technical and non-technical internal stakeholders to determine requirements, estimate the level of effort, and implement solutions for reporting that meets business needs.
  • Contribute to the development of transformation processes used to populate our BI infrastructure from multiple data sources.
  • Contribute to the design, development and implementation of BI solutions.
  • Design high-end reporting visualisations (Dashboards, interactive Graphs and Charts, Drill in reports etc.).
  • Support self-service reports for all customer teams, product teams and support teams.
  • Troubleshoot and analyze reporting and data input issues’ root causes.
  • Document the produced work.

Qualifications

If you are the right Business Intelligence Engineer for us you will be able to demonstrate:
  • University Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or any related degree.
  • Strong knowledge of SQL for designing, querying and managing relational databases.
  • Exposure to data warehouse design techniques and ETL tools (SSIS).
  • Experience in building OLAP Cubes and multidimensional/tabular data models.
  • Exposure to Reporting/Analytics tools (Power BI, SSRS etc).
  • Ability to quickly learn new tools and techniques.
  • Strong fundamental programming skills.
  • Strong analytical thinking.
  • Highly proficient in spoken and written English.
  • Great communication skills, team player mentality.
It would also be great but not essential to see these skills/experiences as well.
  • Knowledge of DAX language.
  • Experience in DW architecture design.
  • Basic DB administration tasks.
  • Working knowledge in API client development.

Additional Information

Why would you enjoy working with us at OpenBet?
  • Competitive benefits, an open and supportive environment as well as a modern and exciting workplace.
  • The opportunity to interact with global teams on a regular basis and the possibility to switch teams and projects as you and our business continues to develop and grow.
  • Tangible and genuine development - at OpenBet, you can take your career where you want it to go!
And if that’s not enough ; you will get to enjoy a stunning work location and flexible working whilst we provide you with the guidance and development skills you need to progress quickly and enhance your career.
