The Opportunity:

We are currently seeking for an experienced Data Engineer to take on a permanent position with our client based on the south side of Brisbane. If you enjoy working in a challenging, fast-paced environment with the ability to think outside the box, then this role if for you.

The Role:

Due to project demand and process restructure, this exciting opportunity has become available with our client to support the delivery of business intelligence reporting across the business, with a focus on designing and developing data models and solutions in an Azure environment. Strong communication and stakeholder management skills are required as you will liaise with various operational teams in the business.

Technical Capabilities:

Strong cloud-based data analytics experience and development of data pipelines for the collection and migration of operational data is required


Cloud based data warehousing experience with Azure Data Factory, Azure Data Lake Storage, Azure Blob Storage or Azure SQL Data Warehouse is required


Demonstrated experience with developing dashboards with PowerBI


Demonstrated experience with C#, C/C++ and .NET development is a plus
