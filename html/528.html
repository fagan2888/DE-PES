    Job Description

    About The Role Enable and inspire a multi-disciplinary team ('Squad') to pursue its mission, by guiding them on 'what' work should be completed by the squad. The Product Owner constantly prioritises tasks and the squad's backlog with the customer at the heart of their decision making. The Product Owner also ensures that the work is strategically aligned to the tribe mission and engages with other Product Owners to align and manage interdependencies. Whilst this role is not a people manager, it is a key leadership role, galvanising the squad around a vision and supporting them to successfully deliver customer value at pace. As a Product Owner you are a- "T-shaped individual" - Possess a breadth of knowledge in many fields, but also have depth in at least one area of expertise Voice of the customer - Must continually engage with the customer and stakeholders to ensure squad is working on the right things and represents what is valuable to the customer Storyteller - Go beyond the mechanics of chopping up a user story into the product backlog and tells the story that nicely ties together the why, the customer need and the vision Mobiliser - Ability to rally and mobilize informal groups of people around focused tasks and stories Knowledge broker - Enable collaboration and empower the Squad by finding the right people for them to talk to Conflict resolver - Have the courage and the capability to engage when things get difficult and collaborate / mediate to effectively resolve conflict Change Enabler - Passionate ambassador for our new way of working and culture Role Model - Truly embody ANZ's purpose and vales
    The Successful Applicant
    What You Bring To The Role Deep expertise on the relevant topic Ability to galvanise a diverse group of people around a common mission Ability to adapt and grasp new skills and content. Proven analytical skills and evidence based decision making Ability to ask for and give feedback Proven ability to innovate and adapt to latest development in area of expertise Demonstrates in-depth customer insights and acts as an advocate, enhancing the customer experience by challenging and encouraging others to put the customer first Maintains commitment to change; engages with others to gain buy-in and act as an advocate for continuous improvement. Proactively identifies, support and drive opportunities for change Extensive problem solving "toolkit". Broad range of visualisation techniques covering scope, progress, culture, goal, etc. Team culture, energy building and agile ceremonies Strong awareness of banking and finance industry trends and threats (technology, products, regulatory environment, competitors, innovations) Understanding of current and prospective customer needs and expectations Fosters all networks and leverages an understanding of others' behaviour to adapt influencing style and approach to effectively collaborate, build relationships & maximise impact
