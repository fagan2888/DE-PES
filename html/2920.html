The Role: Lead Applications Architect – Workplace Services

The Location: Buenos Aires

The Role: This position requires a diverse background in application architecture and development. It will support the Workplace Services Technology Enablement team in the development of custom applications and system integrations. Areas of focus include, but are not limited to, data lake architecture and reporting, API development, process automations, application integrations, and opportunities to learn/utilize machine learning techniques. The candidate’s workload will center around the definition, architecture, and development of applications focused on improving the way in which S&P’s global employees work.

You will assist with all major project initiatives, enhancements, analyses, and day-to-day support. This role will require a flexible, agile approach to meet our changing business needs. The technologies and skills employed will vary but, at a minimum, will include extensive use of AWS serverless architecture patterns (e.g., Lambda, Athena, and Fargate), Docker, Python, Terraform, and API development. Candidates will also need to contribute to the maturation of our CI/CD processes.

The Team: The team, through its deep expertise in various technologies, fosters knowledge sharing that results in a unified group strategy. Each of our team members is committed to making a genuine impact with the work that we do.

The Impact: S&P’s Workplace Services team is committed to providing S&Ps employees with a transformational working environment. As a member of the Workplace Services team you will have the opportunity to have an impact across the entirety of S&P global. This position will assist in the growth and evolution of S&Ps modern workplace.

What’s in it for you: We see this position as evolving over time with the candidate driving new opportunities to expand the services and capabilities of the team. The future is entirely up to us to define and create. In the near term, the candidate will work across a range of technologies in greenfield applications with the opportunity to pursue solutions using advanced serverless, automation, and machine learning technologies.

Responsibilities
  • Experience working as a senior member of a software engineering team
  • Experience working with a diverse suite of technologies
  • Experience providing sound/scalable architectural recommendations
  • Hands-on development experience using multiple languages/technologies – e.g., C#, Java, Node.js, Python, etc. (Your experience can vary from ours)
  • Strong knowledge in RDBMS development (e.g. MySQL, SQL Server)
  • Exposure to NoSQL development
  • Experience working with and designing RESTful APIs – in particular creating resilient applications dependent upon external APIs
  • Experience evaluating vendor solutions
  • Experience writing and evaluating technical and functional requirements.
  • Some experience designing serverless applications is useful

What We’re Looking For
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or Engineering is required, or in lieu, a demonstrated equivalence in work experience
  • 8-10 years’ experience in n-tiered application development.
  • Excellent knowledge of application design/architecture (e.g., design patterns, dependency injection, DevOps principals, elasticity, asynchronous programming, etc.)
  • Strong OOP foundation (any language)
  • Some experience to Python
  • 2+ years working in Unix environments
  • 2+ years’ experience working with AWS – e.g., EC2, Lambda, API Gateway, RDS, etc.
  • Experience piloting and driving new technologies – e.g., server-less architectures, NoSQL, bots, etc.
  • Experience working with continuous integration principals and technologies
  • Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to work effectively in a cross-functional team
  • Experience with software development lifecycle methodologies (Agile preferred)
  • ITSM, ITIL and/or ServiceNow certifications are a plus
