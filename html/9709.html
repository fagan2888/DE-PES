Who we are:


Open-minded intellectuals who embrace emerging technologies on our mission to create innovative Software Engineering Solutions that will impact millions of users around the world.


We are looking for:


A smart and passionate C++ Software Engineer with a good understanding of software architecture and design issues as well as solid knowledge of C, Linux scripting, computer networks. You will have the opportunity to work in a team of highly skilled engineers in charge of designing and implementing safety-critical software solutions for the next-generation of interlocking systems used in the railway field. Moreover, all activities are aligned with the latest approved Safety Standards.


What you will do:


    • Develop, maintain and verify new safety critical software products, recently ported on C++ & new OS and new HW.


What you must have:

        • Strong knowledge of C programming language;
        • Strong experience in C++ with STL (Standard Template Library);
        • Experience in Object Oriented Analysis and Design;
        • Unit Testing with CppUnit knowledge;
        • Experience in UML Diagrams;
        • Strong knowledge of C++ 11;
        • Previous working experience with Linux as Development environment;
        • Experience with DOORS for specification;
        • Experience with Jira for working on Scrum;
        • Experience with Git as repository.
