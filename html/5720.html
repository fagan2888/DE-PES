Developer, Digital Solutions

Brierley+Partners Tokyo, JP

4日前

企業のWebサイトから応募する
As a Developer of Digital Solutions in our Tokyo office, you’ll perform the integral role working collaboratively and cross-functionally with internal business partners. You’ll also be joining a cast of characters, all developers within multiple disciplines, under the dynamic Digital Solutions umbrella. This team specializes in loading and testing previously developed HTML emails and creating new HTML emails, often in multiple versions, languages, and testing methodologies. You will assist in problem-solving and solutioning, which are paramount to success in this evolving core team.

As a platform-agnostic loyalty and digital agency, we also work with multiple ESPs, including Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC), et.al. as well as a growing list of vendors that offer Personalization, Live Content, and Gamification in any medium, all managed within the Digital Campaign Service team. In addition, you’ll also set up promotion rules to provide some incentives such as bonus points and coupons via our core loyalty product.

Position Responsibilities
  • Participate in the strategy and planning of marketing campaigns to understand requirements and deliverables within a test/design framework.
  • Build Email, SMS, or PUSH notifications in HTML or with client provided content and images.
  • Maintain confidentiality while working closely with clients regarding all aspects of campaigns.
  • Follow + refine QA/QC steps with exceptional detail and continue to optimize practices for zero-defect reporting.
  • Plan and manage your workload and the scheduling + deployment of digital communications based on requested cadence, schedule, and platform.
  • Develop and deploy Email, SMS, and PUSH messaging for client programs.
  • Setup targeting rules to send Email, SMS, and PUSH message to appropriate members in client programs.
  • Setup promotion rules such as bonus point accruals for client programs.
  • Manage the change request process and ensure that management is notified in a timely manner of any potential changes in project scope/expected deliverables.
  • Monitor progress on projects according to the project plan and raise problems to management’s attention.


Position Requirements
  • Bachelor Degree.
  • Knowledge of database queries using SQL and/or PL/SQL.
  • HTML expertise required.
  • CRM and enterprise automation experience preferred.
  • Knowledge of Email Marketing processes and practices required.
  • Bonus , but not required - Experience using email service providers (ESPs) for the creation and deployment of email campaigns, preferably SFMC, et. al.
