Our Team
We have ex professional racers, trail runners, mountain climbers, craft beer enthusiasts, parents, dog and cat lovers, city dwellers, country folk and everyone in between. We solve problems together over coffee / tea / protein shakes in and outside of the office and everyones opinions matter. You will definitely find someone with similar interests at Latitude.


Excerpts of our stack
All things AWS (If we mention everything we use, you may get bored)
Python, Scala, Airflow and always open to considering new tech


Who are you?

You’re an experienced data software developer with advanced skills in a modern programming language for example python or scala. Experience in TDD / DevOps is essential. If you have a growth mindset and don't have mental blocks about learning new tech and new languages, we'd love to hear from you!

You're a people leader, grower, nurturer, you love a bit of solution design and engage stakeholders with ease.


The recruitment process
You’ll read this ad, perhaps do some research on us.
If it piques your interest, apply!
If you have amazing open source contributions, experience in high performing engineering teams and an impressive resume…

We’ll give you a call, chat about things and email a coding challenge.

We review it and cry (with happiness).

Then we bring you in to meet the team and go through a whiteboard session.
You verify us and the work we are doing.We chat some more and offer you a life changing job.
You tell everyone how awesome your life has become.

One last thing…
Don't dress up for interview. Prepare for a whiteboard session and a challenging chat.


Successful applicants will be required to complete a background check (including criminal history and bankruptcy check) prior to commencement of employment. Only open to Australian Permanent Residents or Citizens.
