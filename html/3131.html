Role Profile
Mediolanum is looking for a Data Engineer to join its expanding Analytical Intelligence Competency Centre (AICC). The candidate will be involved in the full life-cycle focusing on inception, requirements, design and testing of new Enterprise Datawarehouse deliveries.

This position will focus on designing and implementing new data models, ETL, and a range of Business Intelligence and Analytics outputs to support the needs of the business. The role will be a combination of delivering new requirements, in addition to providing support for existing functionality using the technology within the business. It is an exciting opportunity to join a growing team in the organisation.

The primary technologies in use are Microsoft SQL Server, Microsoft SQL Server Integration Services, Qlik Sense, Power BI.Duties
  • Design, build and test these data models, data imports and reports to meet business requirements, including supporting User Acceptance Testing
    • Use Microsoft SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS) and associated SQL tools to build, test, deploy and support a range of ETL processes
    • Support the administration of the underlying SQL Server platform
    • Solve data-related problems within the team and implement efficient data pipelines
    • Work closely with the business to understand their problems and operational needs.
    • Maintain data pipelines, measuring performance and ensuring good data quality by implementing adequate monitoring
    • Create structured and ad-hoc reports using Mediolanum’s business intelligence tools Qlik Sense & Power BI
    • Maintain required documents to support maintenance and knowledge building
    • Maintains data dictionary by revising and entering definitions
    • Determine changes in physical database by studying project requirements; identifying database characteristics, such as location, amount of space, and access method.
    • Maintains user reference by writing and rewriting database descriptions
    • Contribute in company data transformation and technological transformationSkills & Competencies
    • Proficient in SQL development and dealing with relational databases.
      • The Following, While Not Required, Are Considered An Advantage
      • Experience building APIs ( REST / SOAP )
      • Knowledge of Python or R
      • Knowledge of ML / AI environments and challenges – particularly as it relates to data management
      • Engineering and/or software development experience
      • Knowledge of Tableau or D3.jsQualifications & Experience
      • Degree+ (2.1 or higher) in Computer Science, Statistics, Engineering or Mathematics
      • 4+ years professional experience in Data Warehousing, Microsoft SQL Server Development, and Microsoft SSIS platforms
      • Experience with ETL tools such as Microsoft SSIS, Azure Data Factory, Talend or Informatica
      • Demonstrable architecture experience at enterprise scale
      • Data Warehouse and DataMart design and implementation
      • Data modeling of relational and dimensional databases
      • End to end project lifecycle experience from concept to delivery and support
      • History of working successfully with cross-functional teams
      • Visualization: Qlik Sense; Power BI
      • Continuous delivery and deployment using Agile Methodologies
