YOUR ROLE
  • Plan, schedule, and coordinate preventive & corrective maintenance activities to maximize use of maintenance technicians and minimize downtime
  • Liaise with Maintenance Supervisor to set priorities and amend weekly maintenance schedule as necessary
  • Maintain accurate Master Data and ensure timely and complete information is recorded in the CMMS
  • Support Spare Parts Storekeeper where job responsibilities permit (e.g. by managing inventory)
  • Review and update the maintenance management program related to amendments of the preventive or predictive maintenance schedule (e.g. repetitive problems)
  • Refer issues not related to preventive or predictive maintenance to the Project Engineer for resolution

Your Skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Mechanical or Electrical Engineering
  • Maximum 2 years of experience in a relevant industry
  • Experience in SAP system is preferred
  • Experience in an automotive or FMCG environment is preferred
  • Fluency in English
  • Knowledge of TPM and continuous improvement is preferred
  • Efficient user of the Microsoft Office Applications
