Who We Are

Loadsmart aims to move more with less. We combine great people and innovative technology to more efficiently move freight throughout North America. Our focus is on designing and building the best tools for our team and our customers by using machine learning algorithms to connect cargo with trucks. Through the better matching of supply and demand, we reduce wasted fuel and lost time, cutting out empty miles for motor carriers and providing instant booking for shippers.

Who You Are

You believe in game-changing innovations and are excited about reimaging a 700 billion dollar industry. You are an analytical person with a solid understanding of business and the impact that analysis plays in a companies growth.

The Role

We are looking for a Data Engineer to join our Business Intelligence Team, working from our Florianopolis office. We will also consider candidates who would work remotely in Brazil.

You will partner with leaders across the business to develop and maintain a data architecture that leverages the power of data to all users across the company (from data scientists to business users and leader board), continuously adding more data sources to it and helping your team to deliver the best actionable insights.

Key Responsibilities
  • Create SQL (Ad-hocs queries)
  • Build and maintain ETL
  • Build and maintain Integrations
  • Perform Data modeling (Data Warehouse, OLAP)
  • Maintain Data Integrity
  • Design and implement Data Automation (Exports, Pipeline)
  • Design and implement scalable processes

Qualifications
  • You’re fluent in English (both written and spoken)
  • Ability to face internal stakeholders and implement initiatives with little to no supervision.
  • Experience in data processing projects converting business needs to reporting requirements
  • Stay up to date on new and coming data engineering technologies
  • Minimum 3 years experience working with ETL and Advanced SQL
  • Very advanced skills in data processing and pipeline automation using Python
  • Advanced skills in ETL processes and concepts
  • Good skills on Version Control tools like Git and GitHub
  • Advanced skills in Excel, Google Sheets or similar platforms

Preferred But Not Required
  • Knowledge with Dimensional Modeling, Data Governance, Master Data Management, Security
  • Knowledge with Installation, Administration and Performance Tuning of Databases (OLTP, OLAP or No-SQL)
  • Experience with AWS ecosystem (Redshift, S3, EC2, Data Pipelines)
  • Experience with Linux OS, Cron jobs
  • Experience with Tableau, Power BI or Analysis Services
Because we are an international company, we only accept resumes in English.
