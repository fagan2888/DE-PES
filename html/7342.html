We're looking for a Business Intelligence Architect and Solution Engineer to join our great team!
Your Role
In our Business Intelligence (BI) department in Tallinn we are responsible for Overland Logistics BI and Data Warehouse (DWH) solutions. In a highly heterogeneous IT landscape we bring data together to provide profound reliable base for reporting and visibility for our customers. As a BI Architect and Solution Engineer your goal is to set up new scalable BI/DWH architectures and to design solutions that fulfill customer needs and bring our Business Unit Overland to a next level of data driven capabilities.
Your Responsibilities
  • As Business Intelligence Architect and Solution Engineer you take the responsibility to design and build our future overland BI/DWH architecture and solutions together with a team of data experts
  • In your role you acquire insight into the logistic business processes from the knowledge holders in the business, analyze and discuss requirements and transfer them into efficient BI solutions
  • You select the appropriate layered architecture for BI applications
  • You develop, manage, and update data models, including physical and logical models of the data warehouse, data mart, and staging area
  • Implementation, optimization and continuous improvement of complex and efficient Datamarts, SAP BusinessObjects Universes and Tableau Data Sources

Your Skills And Experiences
  • Bachelor or master degree in computer science or comparable professional experience
  • Deep understanding of Business Intelligence and Data Warehousing concepts and data modeling techniques
  • Strong conceptual, communications, and technical skills
  • Ability to translate business needs into technical solutions
  • Strong dimensional data modeling and database design skills
  • Profound experience in related technologies like Oracle Database, SAP Business Objects (Universe design) and Tableau Data Sources
  • Ability to work team-oriented, reliable and self-contained
  • High level of written and spoken English to work in an international team
Good Reasons to Join
Your work will have a direct influence on the future of logistics and IT. We value teamwork and learning, our working language is English. Your colleagues are experts who shape the IT industry in Estonia and worldwide. You can build your career here – every year 15% of our colleagues find interesting new challenges inside the company. We are located in Tallinn, Estonia – the most digitalized place on Earth. On top of that, we pay good money for a good job.
