Data Scientist / Data Warehouse Specialist

Globetom Pretoria, Gauteng, South Africa

2 weeks ago

Apply

Qualifications

BSc Computer Science or equivalent tertiary qualification

Experience

  • Minimum 3 years of working experience in a data warehouse environment with a good working knowledge of Data Warehouse principles and best practices.
  • Exposure or working experience with AWS Cloud Services will be a distinct advantage (in particular AWS Redshift).
  • Experience with test tools and proven regression test experience will be an added advantage.
  • SQL expertise is a mandatory requirement.

Job Description

Form part of a growing team taking our full-service stack for Information as a Service in the cloud to market internationally.

You will be developing ETL processes using Globetom’s Orcha platform (www.orcha.net).

Compensation

Negotiable based on experience and qualifications

Work Environment

  • You will be required to work from Globetom’s head office in South Africa in Centurion.
  • We are an avid Agile adopter and build our own platforms to compete in the platform economy.
  • We build our software and customer solutions on our own platforms and conform to Service-Oriented Computing principles (SOA and MSA)
  • We are an equal opportunity employer.


Benefits

  • Participation in Globetom’s employee share benefit schemes
  • Flexible working hours with core hours from 09h00 to 15h00 and freedom to arrange your working day around that
  • Leave starting at 20 days per year and increasing with 1 day per year employed up to 25 days after 5 years
  • A work environment where the quality of software tools and workstations is non-negotiable. We use the full Atlassian toolset for collaboration, source code control, issue management and regression testing.
  • Annual innovation award with a fully paid Mauritius holiday for the top innovator of the year and spouse and children.


Characteristics we are looking for

  • Talented, inner-motivated ICT professional looking for an environment where freedom to innovate is cherished.
  • Strong team player
  • We value individuals that can contribute to full-stack development projects in line with Agile and Microservices
  • Interest in cloud-native development in order to align with our strategy
