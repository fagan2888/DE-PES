Gemalto is now part of the Thales Group -

We are a global leader in digital security, bringing trust to an increasingly connected world. We design and deliver a wide range of products, software and services based on two core technologies: digital identification and data protection.

We’re excited about this new chapter for our organization and we are looking for talents to join us in accelerating our vision for next-generation digital security.


As a Software Engineer in the Identity & Biometric solution R&D you will contribute to fulfilling the marketing vision of governmental services based on secure electronic documents and electronic identity. You will be exposed to issuance and reading of secure documents (passports, ID cards), biometric devices, mobile handset devices, cryptography, online transactions and digital identity management. You will learn which principles are making the modern e-government a reality.

As a member of our team you will contribute to development of key products and components which are then used by other Thales Product Teams to deliver tailor-made solutions to governments and citizens all around the globe.

In our team we use Java EE and Spring Framework for development of backend applications over micro-service architecture, focused on production of identity documents and processing of biometric and cryptographic data. These are central components providing multiple interfaces to other system and web GUI for operators.

A week in the life of a Software Engineer (Java EE) :

  • You will work with a team of developers located at local & overseas site, cooperate closely with scrum master & product owner on task breakdown and timely project delivery
  • You will analyze the product needs and contribute to product specification
  • You will design UML architecture and contribute to implementation of reusable
  • software components and applications for mobile devices
  • You will work on proof-of-concepts of new technologies and innovative solutions.
  • You will give support to developers from other teams who are using your source
  • codes or binaries.
  • You will contribute to user documentation of your software.
  • You will technically support and develop with other team members for the solution (e.g. Mobile App Team, Deployment Team

To succeed at this job;

  • You should have very good knowledge of Java EE or Spring platform (EJB, JPA, JMS, CDI, Servlets), web technologies (HTTP/S, Angular, SOAP, REST), good understanding of relational databases (preferred Oracle), professional experience with application servers, UML and continuous integration.
  • It would be preferred if you have experience with scrum, testing & validation, networks, digital security, cryptography, PKI, smart cards, biometry, other programming platforms than Java (Android)
  • We would like someone to join our team who is interested in technology, enthusiastic about software development, team-player, autonomous, able to learn and has good communication skills.
  • We are looking for someone with at least 5 years of experience with development of enterprise applications in Java
