zeroG is dedicated to unlocking the intrinsic power of data to improve the world of aviation.

What are we looking for?

We’re looking for smart specialists to join our Data Engineering team.

Are you ...
  • A fearlessly curious truth seeker who wants to get to the root of hard problems?
  • A hands-on, practical nerd who likes to build things from scratch?
  • Comfortable with big data and knowledgeable about how to get the best out of it?
  • A quick learner who is open to new ideas and new technologies?
  • An insightful thinker with your own ideas about how we should transform our industry?


Why should you join us?
  • We thrive on a culture of diversity, mutual trust and a positive family spirit that sets us apart. Here’s what you’ll find if you join us:
  • A sense of ownership where you can take the initiative and see your work making a difference, and a place where we value creativity and open dialogue over rigid hierarchies.
  • A fascinating industry full of change and complexity, ripe with opportunities to transform the business with data science and automation.
  • Access to the latest cutting-edge cloud, streaming and DevOps technology.
  • Award-winning data scientists to collaborate with and amaze people with elegant solutions.
  • Smart colleagues from an incredible, diverse range of backgrounds – mathematics, nuclear physics, telecoms, government and even music!
  • A personalized training budget so you can decide how to improve your skills.
  • The best of a hacker start-up culture while working for a huge, world famous brand in Lufthansa Group, with travel benefits and much more.


At zeroG we put people at the heart of our work, therefore we’re interested in the story behind the CV. Thus, along with your diploma, transcript of records and if applicable letter of recommendation (Arbeitszeugnis) we also would like you to include a cover letter in which you share with us an example of how you brought a great idea to life and what your learned along the way.

Your profile

The Following Skills And Experience Are Essential
  • A Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in computer science, software engineering, or a related science subject – or equivalent relevant industry experience.
  • A proven track record in building data sets and pipelines for analytical use cases.
  • Expert-level knowledge in at least one of:
    • Building and optimising data warehouses and complex SQL queries. Useful technology skills include Teradata, Oracle and Microsoft SQL Server.
    • Building and optimising data lakes, distributed datasets and data pipelines. Useful technology skills include Hadoop, Hive, and Spark.
  • Expert fluency in SQL and/or at least one programming language: particularly Python, Scala or Java.
  • Great analytical skills and architectural know-how to select the right tool for the right use case.
  • Excellent technical understanding of how to define and meet non-functional requirements, including query performance, data quality and security.
  • Proficiency in English, a sense of humor, and a love for aviation are also important to us!


Knowledge of any of the following would also be an advantage:
  • NoSQL or graph databases
  • Message queueing and streaming systems: e.g. Kafka, Pulsar, JMS
  • Data pipeline and workflow management tools: e.g. Oozie, NiFi
  • ETL tools: e.g. Informatica, Talend
  • Stream processing: e.g. Storm, Spark Streaming, Flink
  • Other scripting and programming languages: e.g. JavaScript, Go
  • Data mining tools and language: e.g. SAS, SPSS, R


What work will you do?

We do both consulting and our own internal projects – so you’ll either be helping an airline customer learn how to transform their business, or helping to build exciting products and services of our own.

You Will
  • Build modern infrastructure that our data scientists can use for agile data exploration and research.
  • Automate the extraction, loading and transformation of data from a wide variety of sources.
  • Engineer large, distributed data sets that are built for a purpose – from extracting complex model features from streaming data, to aggregating years’ worth of data in a BI dashboard.
  • Turn data science prototypes into products with battle-tested, production ready pipelines.
  • Communicate openly with customers to understand their business, identify their most important metrics, and define their data requirements.
  • Advise customers on how to improve their data quality and governance.


About ZeroG GmbH

At zeroG we combine airline and data science know-how and work on the basis of distributed authority, collective intelligence and a high degree of self-organization. By unlocking the intrinsic power of data, we generate new insights, develop data-driven business models and innovative solutions that elevate aviation. Our team cultivates diversity, mutual trust and a positive family spirit – making zeroG unlike other organizations.
