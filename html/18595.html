Senior Full-stack Data Architect

The Adecco Group Sofia, Sofia City Province, Bulgaria

4 days ago

Apply on company website

Our trusted client The Coca Cola Company is expanding their new IT Development and Delivery Center based in Sofia. The team will develop across a wide spectrum of IT disciplines, including Information Security, Robotics, SAP, Infrastructure, Cloud and Business Intelligence. They are building a great team of technologists who will drive the digitization of their enterprise.


We are looking for a Senior Data Engineer to contribute to the development and management of BI and analytics solutions that turn data into knowledge. This role includes developing well designed, testable, efficient solutions by using best software development practices and leading and mentoring less experienced team developers. You should have an analytical thinking, background in data and business analysis and excellent communication skills. If you also have a business acumen and problem-solving aptitude, we’d like to meet you. Ultimately, you will enhance our business intelligence systems to help us make better decisions.

Key Responsibilities:
• Lead developers with expert knowledge.
• Translate business needs to technical specifications.
• Design, build and deploy BI solutions.
• Carry out database modeling and system performance tuning.
• Maintain and support data analytics platforms (e.g. Microsoft Azure BI Platforms & Tools).
• Conduct testing and troubleshooting.
• Evaluate and improve existing BI systems.
• Collaborate with teams to integrate systems.
• Create visualizations and reports for requested projects.
• Develop and update technical documentation.

Requirements:
• Bachelor’s Degree Required, BSc/BA in Computer Science, Engineering or relevant field.
• 3+ Years proven experience as a developer.
• Background in data engineering (data science experience is considered a plus).
• Experience with DBMS, ETL development and OLAP principles, NoSQL and Big Data technologies, preferably with Spark, Azure Databricks, Azure Cosmos DB.
• Proficiency in development with SQL, DAX, Python (C#, .NET, Power Query is considered a plus).
• Knowledge and experience with Azure data processing and computation services is considered a plus (e.g. Azure Storage, Azure Functions, Azure SQL DB, Azure App Service).
• Proven abilities to take initiative and be innovative.
• Analytical mind with a problem-solving aptitude.


If you recognize yourself in the description above, send us your CV in English today!

Only short-listed candidates will be contacted. All applications will be treated in strict confidentiality.
