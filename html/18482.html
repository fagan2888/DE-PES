    • Platform Software Tools Engineer

    NVIDIA's GPUs and SOCs are the world leaders in performance and efficiency, and we are continually innovating in creative and unique ways to improve our ability to deliver extraordinary solutions in a wide range of sectors. We are seeking Platform Software Tools Engineers who are passionate about what they do and are committed to making a difference in the world through their inventions.

    As part of the Platform Silicon Solution Team, we will co-work with different teams to spend effort for software support on GPU new feature validate and debug in system level. In this role, you would have great opportunity to learn knowledge about new GPU technology and get a lot of system debug experience. You need to research and develop techniques to applications in different GPU feature fields, such as power management, thermal control, PCIE to ensure the best possible performance on current- and next-generation GPU.

    What You Will Be Doing
    • Help design tools to automate product definitions, data collection, test case execution, and results analysis.
    • Partner with board design team/Firmware engineers on scripts/tools of different boards for different Firmware features validation.
    • Collaborate with silicon solution, board solution, application engineer teams to collect/discuss the tools requirement to cover the qualification to avoid the problem.
    • Work closely with SWQA team and driver team on GPU specific issues debugging. Would also need to develop scripts/tools for debug purpose.
    • Partner with different teams for customized images creation and scripts/tools development for automation test.

    What We Need To See
    • BS or MS in EE, Automatic Control, Systems Engineering
    • Strong knowledge of C/C++.
    • Strong knowledge of tools programming/debugging techniques.
    • Knowledgeable in MOSFETs, computer architecture.
    • Experience of script language such as perl/python.
    • Hands-on experience with lab tools (oscilloscopes, multimeters).
    • Experience with MCU/FPGA program/debug would be a plus.
    • Knowledge of windows/linux driver model would be a plus.
    • Good communication and organization skills, with a logical approach to problem solving, good time management, and task prioritization skills.
    NVIDIA is widely considered to be one of the technology world’s most desirable employers. We have some of the most brilliant and talented people in the world working with us. If you're creative and autonomous, we want to hear from you!
