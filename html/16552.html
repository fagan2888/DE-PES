Working Student – Data Scientist for Supply Chain (m/f/d)

Grohe AG Düsseldorf, DE

Vor 2 Tagen

Do you feel at home in Data Science and are you enthusiastic about digital transformation and data-related projects? Do you feel at ease in a fast-growing global company with open business communication and international colleagues? Then you are in the right place as Working Student - Data Scientist for Supply Chain for our Supply Chain Team!

Your Tasks And Responsibilities
  • Actively contribute to our projects and activities covering digital transformation by applying modern data analytics methods
  • Data collection, cleaning and preparing data for explorative analysis
  • Development of predictive statistical models based on efficient and effective methods
  • Visualizing and presenting the obtained results

Your Profile And Skills
  • Enrolled student in Mathematics/Statistics, Computer Science, Engineering or having a comparable qualification
  • Computational Thinking
  • Basic knowledge of Data Science and Machine Learning
  • Basic programming skills in languages like Python or R
  • Mathematical thinking, strong analytical and problem-solving skills, self-motivated
  • Very confident in using MS Office applications (Access, Visio, PowerPoint, Excel, Word)
  • Fluent in both written and spoken English and have strong communicational skills
  • Knowledge of German desirable

Welcomed Additional Skills
  • Programming skills in Java
  • Experience with databases and SQL
  • Knowledge in material flow simulation / production simulation
We offer
  • Flexible working time model
  • Individual further development through our internal further training programme GROHExcellence / GROHEfficiency
  • A very dynamic environment with global exposure
  • An international team of experts to inspire you and to learn from
  • Work location in an attractive, international city
  • Company restaurant and the free use of our in-house gym
Interested?

Then we are looking forward to your application including a certificate of enrolment.

For queries and further information, please do not hesitate to contact us.
