Description

Senior DC Infrastructure Engineering Manager
Amazon’s AWS Data Centers are a major component of worldwide cloud-computing infrastructure and are industry leading examples of mission critical facilities. Our data centers are industry leading examples of energy efficient, cost effective designs. The Data Center Engineering organization is ultimately responsible for all engineering discipline contribution required to deliver the most highly efficient and sophisticated electrical and mechanical systems in the world.
Engineers in AWS work to design resilient, cost effective data center facilities. We are responsible for achieving world class availability for our customers and drive to develop a fleet of buildings emphasizing security, safety, and efficiency, while finding new ways to meet AWS’s growing demand. We justify and communicate the technical decisions we make directly to AWS leadership and work hard to drive continuous advancements in our designs. As a Principal Engineer Manager at AWS you will have the opportunity to impact changes in our current design, as well as establish design procedures and protocols for future systems. AWS Engineering is responsible for the design of all MEP, CSA, controls, fire systems, and critical equipment within all of Amazon Data Centers world-wide. The team works directly with operations, security teams, field engineering, and construction management to implement designs that are functional, practical and innovative.
The team is seeking a Senior DC Infrastructure Principal Engineering Manager to lead the Principal Engineering organization as part of our global engineering leadership team. As a Senior Manager you will be part of a highly creative and effective leadership team comprised of Architects, Mechanical Engineers, Electrical Engineers, and Control Engineers, tasked with developing technical innovations for capacity delivery globally (both owned/leased and colo) and challenging the design status quo. You will be responsible for the delivery of projects impacting new designs and existing data centers, applying design quality controls, and providing technical guidance to team members. As a subject matter expert, you will be responsible for the design of prototypical DC facilities, localization of conceptual innovations, solve large scale implementation and capacity issues, and build a strong relationships with the regional industry.
Anticipated travel not to exceed 25%
Responsibilities of the Senior DC Infrastructure Principal Engineering Manager
The Senior Data Center Infrastructure Principal Engineering Manager will be responsible for the following:
  • Leading Data Center Infrastructure Principal Engineering staff globally including design innovation for new build and existing fleet improvements for owned, leased and colo facilities.
  • Develop strategic solutions which meet or exceed our quality requirements and fall within our budgetary and redundancy requirements.
  • Conceives, initiates, and leads projects with widespread impact on infrastructure design, innovation, and implementation.
  • Sets the standard for engineering, test, and best practices. Drives process improvements across the organization.
  • Decompose complex problems into straightforward and actionable solutions.
  • Be a leader within the group as well as within internal and external teams that support our data centers.
  • Provide technical oversight of the Global Electrical and Mechanical Basis of Design.
  • Serve as a technical advisor for AWS datacenter electrical and mechanical designs.
  • Work with internal teams to understand user requirements.
  • Work with local agencies having jurisdiction and certification agencies to ensure compliance with federal, state and municipal requirements and building codes.
  • Coordinate solutions and prepare content for executive interactions with financially quantified proposals and technical detail

Basic Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s degree in mechanical or electrical engineering.
  • 15+ years of experience with mission critical facilities, including construction or operations mission critical equipment/system design and implementation experience.
  • 3+ years managing engineering teams working on facilities outside the US.
  • 7+ years of experience with uninterruptible power sources, diesel generators, electrical switchgear, power distribution units, and automatic/static transfer switches.
  • 7+ years of experience with chillers, cooling towers, direct and indirect evaporative cooing, variable speed drives, and fan systems.
Preferred Qualifications
  • 10+ years of experience directly related to data center or mission critical facility design, construction, operation or maintenance of data centers.
  • Knowledge of building codes and regulations including Life Safety, IBC, NFPA, NEC, NESC and OSHA.
  • Ability to research new designs, technologies, and construction methods of data center equipment and facilities.
  • Ability to carry new design concepts through exploration, development, and into deployment/construction.
  • Ability and willingness to think outside of the box to find creative and innovative solutions to reduce costs with no impact on quality, reliability, or maintainability.
  • Ability to perform complex business case analysis to justify technical decisions and present the justification to management in a high level review.
  • Possess excellent communication skills, attention to detail, maintain high quality standards.
  • Professional Engineering License or Master Degree in Engineering
  • Direct experience with high voltage distribution systems design.
  • Direct experience with chiller systems, cooling towers and factory testing protocols
  • Managing experience with the design, construction, operation, or maintenance of data centers.
  • Previous consultant and jurisdiction negotiation skills associated with design, construction and project execution.
  • Experience with large scale technical operations or large-scale compute facilities.
  • Meets/exceeds Amazon’s leadership principles requirements for this role
  • Meets/exceeds Amazon’s functional/technical depth and complexity for this role
