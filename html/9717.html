SENIOR DATA SCIENTIST

Betting Connections Sliema, MT

1 day ago

Apply on company website
Our iGaming Client is looking to hire a Sr Data Scientist, you will be reporting to and working alongside the Head of Data Science to drive Data Science projects within the company. You will work closely with internal stakeholders, data stewards, project/program managers, and other IT teams to build statistical models whose output, through a manual or automated process can be used to identify customer behavior, deliver predictive forecast scenarios, scoring, identify opportunities & threats.

These will be your tasks
You will be at ease with manipulating large data set, you will be autonomous in data preparation for modeling using SQL, R and or Python and Knime. You will need to be a creative thinker and a strong sense of ownership and leadership to manage your projects to completion, propose innovative ways to look at problems by using statistical approaches on a number of available data sets and use modeling approaches which deployment or productionalization you will lead. You will also ensure proper documentation of the work and models to ensure reproducibility.

You will support the Data Science Head to share best practices on technology & infrastructure needed to achieve the DS department goal, as well as ensure that the company has the necessary skills to allow the development of data products or other implementation of the team models. This could be achieved through external hiring or by establishing a close collaboration with other departments. At ease with engaging with business partners and gather specifications, you will develop an understanding of the business and be able to present your insights in layman terms.

Objectives:
  • Deliver value to the company by building statistical models. Able to run some models independently once output has been assessed successfully, ensure its implementation/productionalization (data products).
  • Support business with creating models improving segmentation, VIP, early prediction, bonus abusers, free spins players….
  • Provide guidance on bonus and incentives associated risks to stakeholders.
  • Contribute to the creation of a data environment together with DW and Tech team to prepare the data for modelling needs.
  • Deliver small projects per quarter as interim steps of the bigger projects.
  • Documentation of the work and models to ensure reproducibility.
You're Offering These Qualifications

What we expect from you:
  • Experienced in conducting complex statistical analysis, preferably holding a Masters in a relevant discipline such as applied mathematics, statistics computer science, or engineering.
  • Experienced with methods including clustering, time series regression, decision trees, random forests, Bayesian inference, SVM’s, Markov models.
  • Familiarity with Jupyter notebooks or similar
  • Proficiency with GIT.
  • Experience in the gaming industry is a plus.
Preferred skills:
  • Extensive experience with a broad range of advanced analytics, data mining, statistical modeling, machine learning and data science techniques with an understanding of their applications and pros and cons.
  • Extended knowledge of SQL, Python or R or Knime.
  • Big Data technologies (e.g. Spark, Databricks).
  • Cloud services (e.g. AWS)
  • An effective communicator and networker, able to influence and explain complex topics to business stakeholders.
We're Offering These Benefits

Heath insurance, gym allowance, company's events and much more
