Chemicals Manufacturing Company, KZN Seeking a Mechanical Engineer to ensure OHSA legal compliance, critical maintenance tasks, optimisation projects and complete replacement Capex projects on site. REQUIREMENTS/QUALIFICATIONS (Skills and Experience)
  • BSC (Mechanical) Engineering
  • 5 Years experience in heavy industry environment
  • Chemical plant safety and technical knowledge
  • Computer literate in Microsoft office, projects and Computerised Maintenance Management Systems
  • GCC (factories), Maintenance- and project management recommended JOB DESCRIPTION (Tasks and Responsibilities) Adhere to all site PPE, safety requirements, safe operating procedures and work instructions
  • Attend safety training, safety committee meetings, induction and medical examinations
  • Ensure site mechanical compliance to the OHSA and Regulations
  • Ensure all ISO 18001 safety and ISO 14001 environmental requirements are met
  • All SAPGA legal responsibilities from the incomming isolation valve to the gas train isolation valve
  • Ensure all ESHA procedures and guidelines are implemented to comply with ISO 9001, 14001 and OSHAS 18001 stds
  • All lifting equipement and lifting machines maintained to Regulations and Standards refered to in the OHSA
  • Planned job observations completed
  • Assist with CPS projects including compliance to Maintenance Blue Print
  • Perform acting duties
  • Perform standby and overtime duties
  • Perform optimisation and replacement project management
  • Conduct trails to improve performance and/or reduced cost
  • Conduct breakdown investigations.
  • Compile service contracts for routine activities
  • Shutdown planning and control
  • Major breakdown repair supervision and control
  • Develop and update Operational and Maintenance procedures
  • Proposing and execution of plant modifications
  • Maintain availability of plant
  • Cost control
  • Optimise maintenance activities
  • Control over civil and corrotion protection activities
  • Control over site facility maintenance
  • All legal documentation, reports and certificates saved in specified locations on the server
