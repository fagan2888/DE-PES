Data Engineer Manchester
  • Up to £50k We have been pioneering the digital adoption space for the last decade. Through our years of experience, we discovered that software is only a small part of successful digital transformation, building our own Digital Adoption Platform (DAP) We have worked with world-leading organizations, throughout their digital transformation projects, and have first-hand experience across hundreds of digital adoption pain points. The Opportunity We are looking for a Data Engineer to be responsible for creating and maintain optimal performance across the data pipeline including the integration of machine learning models. Key Responsibilities
  • Create and maintain an optimal data pipeline architecture
  • Assemble large, complex data sets to fulfil requirements across Data Science and Analytics teams
  • Identify, design, and implement internal process improvements: optimizing data delivery, testing and enabling scalability
  • Build the infrastructure required for optimal extraction, transformation and loading of data from a wide variety of data sources using SQL/Python and AWS technologies
  • Build analytical tools to enable KPI monitoring of the data pipeline and machine learning model performance
  • Work with stakeholders including the Product, Development and Professional Services teams to assist with data-related technical issues and support their data infrastructure needs
  • Create data tools for the Data Science and Analytics team members that assist them in building and optimizing our product to further enhance our position as a leader in digital adoption analytics
  • Work with data and analytics experts to strive for greater functionality in our data systems
  • Produce high quality documentation of software design Required Skills/Experience
  • SQL
  • Cloud Technologies
  • AWS (Amazon Web Services) is ideal
  • Experience in any of the following
  • Python/Scala/R
  • Experience with any Data Transformation technologies (Kinesis/S3/Redshift)
  • PowerBI
