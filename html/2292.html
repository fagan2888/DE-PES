Senior Network Automation Engineer

Verizon Ashburn, VA, US

1 week ago

Apply on company website
Job # 530979

What You’ll Be Doing...

This position be part of the Edge Application Team and will be responsible for the engineering work on our national LTE EPC core network.

Responsibilities
  • Responsible for an edge application over cloud based architecture. This position will drive the evolution and performance of edge applications, with an emphasis on finding and developing better ways to automate recurring work effectively.
  • Demonstrate subject expertise to optimize applications and drive issue resolution. This involves working closely with all application owners and vendors to ensure end-to end service performance metrics are achieved.
  • Troubleshoot application issues and outages by working with cross functional teams and vendors.
  • Recommend system modifications or enhancements to improve application performance and quality.
  • Develop and refine application and infrastructure test plans.
  • Provide subject matter expertise to team.
  • Initiate and manage projects for network builds, modifying configurations, refining designs, and troubleshooting issues/analyzing problems.
  • Manage multiple projects at one time and ensure projects stay within budget.
  • Lead FOAs and support HQ proof of concept initiatives including new application turn-ups / instantiations.
  • Use independent judgment and discretion to make decisions in the best interest of the business.

You'll Need To Have

What we’re looking for...
  • Bachelor’s degree or four or more years of relevant work experience.
  • Four or more years of relevant work experience.

Even Better If You Have
  • A Degree.
  • Bachelor/Master’s Degree in science, engineering or related discipline.
  • Ability to troubleshoot network, servers, or application issues.
  • Knowledge of TCP/IP, LAN/WAN and IP routing protocols, familiar with Data Center architecture.
  • Knowledge of interdependent aspects of virtual applications including eNSE, VCP-E, Linux/Unix, application, and software skills (Python, Ansible, RESTful / NETCONF API and automation tools).
  • Working experience over Cloud Technologies such as Open stack, Kubernetes, Containers, etc.
  • Experience engineering, monitoring, and growing complex data networks preferred.
  • Experience in wireless switching environment.
  • Embrace change of a dynamic, evolving business environment, and contribute original thinking, openness, and alternative solutions for change initiatives.
  • Possess a proactive work style with the ability to set priorities, work with minimum supervision and with a diverse group of professionals.
  • Experience with professional development including technical and leadership training / certificates.
  • Strong work ethic, integrity and work extremely well in a team environment.
  • Excellent interpersonal skills.
  • Experience analyzing and resolving complex problems.
  • Willingness to work a flexible schedule, including nights and weekends as necessary.
