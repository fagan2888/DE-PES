Twinstar Industries Limited, a Leading Plastic Manufacturing Company Based In Sango Otta, Ogun State, Requires For Immediate Employment a Suitably Qualified Candidate To Fill The Vacant Position Below

Job Title: Production Supervisor

Location: Ogun
Reporting To: HOD Production

Job Description
  • Our manufacturing plant hopes to find a qualified and cost-conscious professional to fill the open position of Production Supervisor
  • The successful candidate will take the initiative to increase our production stats while reducing our total cost outlay. We’re looking for a creative thinker who does not mind sharing his or her ideas and implementing new strategies to benefit the business
  • If you are a hard worker with excellent managerial skills and superior time management skills, we want to talk to you.


Duties And Responsibilities
  • Create and oversee an employee training schedule that emphasizes productivity and resource conservation
  • Evaluate each station of the manufacturing process to ensure maximum efficiency
  • Enforce safety policies and procedures (both in-house and regulatory) to decrease the potential for employee injuries on the job
  • Inspect products prior to dispatch to ensure accurate labeling and record keeping
  • Maintain open lines of communication between employees, management and executives
  • Ensure all resources and assets are used efficiently and correctly
  • Supervise, co-ordinate and control production activities, operations, and workers in his shift.
  • Carry out daily production and material requirement plan
  • Ensure maximum utilization of the machines
  • Ensure quality output according to specification and guidelines.
  • Arrange proper workers allocation for efficient output and plan daily manpower requirement
  • Ensure set targets are met
  • Ensure proper records are kept
  • Ensure effective and proper handing over of the shift to the next shift supervisor
  • Carry out and ensure Data Analysis on materials and resources on the shop floor
  • Ensure elimination of waste and scrap in the shift
  • Maintain discipline in the shift and ensure compliance with rules and regulations in his shift
  • Arrange and collect materials from the stores and ensure efficient utilization.
  • To report faults on the machine to the Shift Engineer/HOD immediately
  • To submit daily production report/and other required reports to the HOD/Factory Manager
  • Should be computer literate and versatile in Microsoft Word and Excel
  • Demonstrable practical and hands –on experience
  • To perform any other tasks that may be assigned to you by the Management.


Qualification And Skills
  • HND/B.Sc. with 3 years experience or OND with at least 6 years experience in related field.
  • Should be computer literate, particularly in Microsoft Word and Excel. Knowledge of SAP accounting package will be an advantage
  • Previous experience in a supervisory capacity
  • Ability to create and follow schedules
  • Organization skills and willingness to assume accountability for plant practices required
  • Ability to stand for long periods of time
  • Age requirement: 30-35 years

Our Offer
  • We offer a competitive salary, a high-energy work environment, excellent benefits and the opportunity to build your resume.

Application Closing Date
18th October, 2019.
