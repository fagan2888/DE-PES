About The Opportunity

We are currently seeking a Digital Engineering (DE) lead for the Building, Infrastructure & Advanced Facilities (BIAF) team, based in our Singapore office. As the DE lead you will work closely with our project teams. You will play a key role in in the implementation, configuration and on-going support of our Building Information Modelling (BIM) systems and other digital technologies to ensure the successful delivery of projects. This is a key role in our Engineering/Project Services team. Applications are invited from candidates with a proven track record of practical leadership in the BIM and digital engineering fields. This role will initially support and advise one of the major programmes in our portfolio to help deliver 6D BIM.

Key responsibilities include
  • Develop, lead and manage the people, processes and systems for the business as well as key projects in the field of BIM and digital engineering.
  • Author and owner of key BIM documentation for projects such as Employer’s Information Requirements (EIR), BIM Execution Plans (BEPs) and Project Automation Plans (PAP).
  • Manage information for projects to ensure alignment of Common Data Environments (CDEs) with the governing standards such as BS1192, PAS1192, Singapore BIM Guide.
  • Ensure successful delivery of Project Information Model (PIM) including 4D (sequencing), 5D (quantity) and 6D (asset data).
  • Be the focal point of contact for client, modellers, engineers, project manager/s & design manager/s to deliver BIM and digital engineering.
  • Chair meetings, presenting at workshops and updating reports related to BIM deliverables of projects.
  • Define and draft tender and contract documentation on behalf of the client such as specifications, EIR and BIM toolkits.
  • Participate in tender reviews of BIM enabled contracts
  • Interface with Jacobs global design centres to coordinate BIM deliverables and ensure workflows are in place and followed in line with QA/QC procedures.
  • Ensure drawings, models and attributes are completed to the desired quality, programme & budget including audits to ensure they’re fit for purpose.
  • Assist with managing the resource and upskilling of existing personnel in matters of BIM and digital engineering based in various projects.
  • Attend and present at BIM and digital engineering industry events and workshops.

About You

The successful candidate will possess
  • Excellent communication skills in English in both written (procedures, reports) and verbal (presentations, meetings) mediums.
  • Demonstrable experience in the BIM delivery of large multidisciplinary projects. At least 5 years of relevant BIM Management experience.
  • Relevant Engineering, Information management or BIM qualifications.
  • Solid knowledge understanding and practical experience in BIM software (experience with Bentley products an advantage) including interoperability, data exchange and transfer, federation of models and clash detection.
  • Management and administration of a CDE (experience with ProjectWise setup and administration will be an advantage)
  • Set up and implementation of Information Management procedures in accordance with global BIM standards such as BS 1192, PAS 1192, ISO 19650.
  • Experience with developing BIM management processes and procedures for a project to deliver 6D capability, including drafting BEP, EIR and other relevant work instructions.
  • Experience in the practical application of 4D construction sequencing, 5D quantity take-off and COBie/Asset Management System (AMS) requirements.
  • Experience with software such as; AECOsim, OPPID, OPM, Navisworks and Bentley Navigator software will be an advantage.
  • Experience with enabling the use of BIM in field tools (i.e. Inspections, 3D scanning)
  • Have a good understanding of the local and international BIM standards / requirements
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Experience with managing BIM and engineering professionals including training and mentoring technical and non-technical staff.
  • Experience with developing a BIM and digital engineering strategy of an office or sub region will be an advantage.
