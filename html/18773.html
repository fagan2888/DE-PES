Data Engineer

IntaPeople - IT and Engineering Recruitment Oxford, GB

3 days ago

Apply on company website
We're helping to scale up a start-up based in Oxford. A Data Engineer is needed to work on their core platform systems. As the successful Data Engineer, you will be working closely with the Research and Systems teams, writing tools which use the data handling platform to enable the researchers to request top quality data for algorithmic development. Would you describe yourself as someone who is keen to try new things and curious about new technology - digging into areas that need fixing and improving?. As a start-up who are scaling you should be comfortable in a pretty fast-moving environment where priorities may change quickly. Experience in a similar role would be an obvious tick in the box however, we're more-so looking for Engineers who are up for a challenge. The current stack uses Python, C++, Rust, AWS lambada, MySQL, Parquet, Spark, Protobuf, Flask, Redshift and more…. To apply for the Data Engineer role you will need to demonstrate a core understanding of.
  • Python (including Pandas and Numpy).
  • Basic Linux competency with a desire to learn more.
  • Databases (e.g. MySQL, SQLite, NoSQL). We don't expect people to know 100% of the wish list though, so it would be great if you knew a bit about, or are keen to learn more about -.
  • Amazon RDS or Redshift.
  • PyTest.
  • Flask.
  • Docker.
  • Spark.
  • C++. Your role will involve closely working with other teams in order to keep data moving between customer sites and research team with vigorous attention to security, customer service and data permissions. Your role will be customer-centric, treating the researchers as your clients. IntaPeople is acting as an Employment Business in relation to this vacancy. By applying for this job you accept the privacy policy which can be found on our website. Your details will never be submitted to a client without your explicit consent.
