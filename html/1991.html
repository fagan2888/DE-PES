Function

Job Description

Hydro-Processing Catalyst Technical Services Rep. will be responsible for technical services for customers in territory.

Main Responsibilities
  • Execute technical services process to gain new refinery catalyst customers or maintain existing customers in line with Nippon Ketjen strategy in assigned territory.
  • Provide technical information to customers to promote sales ,to ensure success in use, and to assure safe handling of materials by using Nippon Ketjen / Albemarle’s global technical services tools and databases
  • Take a lead for winning target refinery catalyst opportunities in cooperation with Nippon Ketjen and Albemarle Asia-Pacific sales team.
    • Define technical needs (in cooperation with sales team) and commercials needs of target opportunity,
    • Prepare technical proposal
  • Resolve customer product performance complaints and issue
  • Developing relationships at various levels within the existing customer and potential clients in territory, in particular, the operation, technical service, R&D department and the procurement of refineries in assigned territory.
  • Providing input to Nippon Ketjen regarding strategy for maintaining existing business and obtaining new business. Participate in development of sales forecasts and operating plans.
  • Attending external seminars, meetings, and convention to help maintain Nippon Ketjen/Albemarle’s presence in the industry as well as stay on current industry developments
  • Collect market & customer information and contribute to the build-up of Nippon Ketjen/Albemarle’s overall market intelligence, to maximize Nippon Ketjen/Albemarle’s benefit within the global market

Qualifications
  • Refining Engineering degree or equivalent in Chemical Engineering or related discipline
  • Over 2-3 years of process engineer experience with refinerie s in assigned territory in the area of catalysts, process chemical, licensing/engineering services or equipment.
  • Working experience at technical or operation department of refinery (especially related to catalytic process) is desired but not a must.
  • Capability to learn technical aspects of Nippon Ketjen/ Albemarle’s refinery catalyst products line (hydro-processing catalyst) and use these aspects to enhance technical services process.
  • Good command of both Korean and English speaking/writing skills.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills related particularly to customer interaction
  • Strong computer skills with ability to use spreadsheet, database, presentation, and word processing software.
  • Teamwork sprit, self-motivated and honest.
