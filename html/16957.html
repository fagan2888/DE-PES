Introduction

voestalpine High Performance Metals is the global market leader in tool steel and also holds leading positions in high-speed steel, valve steel, special engineering steel and other special grades. Customers can rely on our well-established brands that have frequently set the standards for the special steel sector over many decades with their innovation and top quality.

You want to work in a team to leverage the power of AI by creating innovative data applications to serve data-driven models and engage complex active interaction?

Please find further information on our website: http://www.voestalpine.com/highperformancemetals/en/

Job Description

Design, develop, test, and deploy data-driven services, application and modules

Custom-develop data ingestion tools or modify and configure out-of-the-box solutions

Support data engineers and data scientists with code and algorithms where needed

Support developers in subsidiary locations with best practice examples

Maintain best practice code snippet database to build up an extensive code knowledge base

Act as developer of innovative new applications and provide 1st level support to end-users

Lead requirement engineering activities and propose state-of-the-art solution concepts

Work closely with various teams across the organization in order to enhance business outcomes

Execute complex and exciting end-to-end Data Engineering projects together with our Data Scientists

Contribute to process standards and project management of Data Use Cases

Qualifications

University degree in a quantitative field such as computer science, informatics, statistics, engineering or related discipline

3 years experience in a DevOps driven software development environment

Extensive knowledge of modern development paradigms (including object-oriented, functional programming …) and design patterns

Excellent knowledge in at least two modern programming languages, e.g. Scala and Python (incl. Django, Flask), Java, Shell scripting (Linux), preferably also C/C++

Understanding of distributed computing, code reviews and optimization

Basic knowledge of mathematical libraries and add-ons to perform scientific computation

Experience with (progressive) web apps, mobile, responsiveness, cross-platform technologies

Good understanding of ETL paradigms in traditional SQL and NoSQL environments

Experience in Lean Management and/or Six Sigma beneficial

Excellent written and verbal German and English skills

Enthusiastic about learning new tools and on track with the latest research and technology

Analytical and solution-oriented mind set, pro-active team player with profound communication skills

Willingness to travel (up to 30%)
