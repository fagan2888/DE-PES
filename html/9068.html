Job Description

As a data engineer you are responsible for the setup, deployment and production of data-intensive systems. You develop data pipelines that perform data transformation and pre-processing, and are able to deploy them at scale in a public cloud. You make sure that data is stored securely and safely using the technology that you see fit for the job.

With your knowledge of software, clusters, queues, containers, streaming, and graphs you work on business-critical systems with lots of data, or help build such systems from the ground up.

Qualifications
  • You like working with new data processing tools, like Airflow, Spark, Flink, and Druid.
  • You understand the importance of cloud and know your way around or want to learn that.
  • You like working for different clients and enjoy the excitement of regularly getting to learn new environments and know new people.
