Machine Learning Engineer

Vinted / Kleiderkreisel Vilnius, LT

1 month ago

Apply on company website
Vinted is the world’s biggest second-hand fashion marketplace. Our mission is green: we want to make second-hand the first choice globally. Recently, Vinted reached a major milestone – we became financially self-sustaining, and we’re still growing fast.

Currently, 35 million people use our platform, which is developed and supported by a team of 240+ people from our offices in Vilnius, Berlin, Prague, and Warsaw. We have a unique work culture that’s based on trust, honesty, and attention to self-improvement.

As part of Vinted’s Product Engineering teams, you’ll be developing new features for our members – all the way from concept to roll-out. We’re upgrading how we use machine learning to make our product better, so you’ll be part of a small, expanding team who focus on building models and applying deep learning to unstructured data.

You’ll be involved in decision-making, have clear task ownership, and participate in regular tech-lead meetups. And – last but not least – you’ll be working with a state-of-the-art tech stack: Python, Jupyter Notebook, TensorFlow with Keras API, PyTorch and Spark.

Your main responsibilities as our new Machine Learning Engineer will include:
  • Developing features that have a direct impact on our members' experience
  • Defining data requirements and engineer features for machine learning models
  • Selecting, training, validating, and optimising machine learning models
  • Collaborating closely with backend engineers to improve and automate model training, maintenance, deployment, and monitoring processes
  • Working with both structured and unstructured data
  • Using mainly neural networks and sometimes classical machine learning models
  • Applying Agile and Lean principles in your work

About You
  • You’ve mastered Python
  • You’ve built machine learning systems in production
  • You have experience working with deep learning models
  • You’ve worked with Keras or Tensorflow Keras API
  • You are happy working both collaboratively and individually
  • You are a smart, motivated problem-solver

Perks
  • Opportunity to join our stock options programme
  • Learning budget (13.2% from net salary)
  • 25 working days of holidays
  • All the tech you need to do your job
  • In-house restaurant
  • Monthly team-building events
  • In-house gym
  • A personal “Shop @ Vinted” budget
The salary range for this position is 3200-5800 Eur GROSS / month.

Please send us your CV or point us to your LinkedIn profile. We’ll be in touch once we’ve reviewed your application.
