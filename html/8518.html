Company Description

Solactive AG is a FinTech company operating globally and growing at a fast pace, headquartered in Frankfurt. Since 2007, we develop tailor-made and multi-asset class index solutions for ETFs and other index-linked investment products for the leading global investment banks and asset managers as our clients. Flexibility, efficiency and providing the highest quality are at the heart and soul of our business philosophy. We are now hiring!

Job Overview

Our Data Solutions team forms an essential link between our business and infrastructure teams: It defines and implements business processes in data pipelines, monitors existing services and thereby provides the best service to our customers. As a Senior Data Engineer in our Data Solutions team you will be working on a broad set of real-world problems of large scale that have direct and significant business impact. In close collaboration with our business analysts and software engineers you will create high-performance data processing solutions for scaling by using emerging technologies and approaches. Jointly we will define new best practices.



Your Tasks

  • Implementing data models and storage solutions for high and low volume data in an accessible and scalable manner
  • Building up a service-oriented financial market data platform including connectors, data storage systems and application interfaces
  • Optimizing queries executed in database systems by improving query plans and considering data partitioning and compression
  • Engineering ETL processes through the whole data pipeline using Python (Apache Airflow)
  • Being an in-house consultant for best track, store and access data


Your Profile

  • Academic degree in Computer Science or related field
  • At least 5 years of relevant work experience
  • Experience within the financial software domain with specific focus on data integration, data provisioning and data management
  • Advanced experience in implementing and optimizing data processing jobs in RDBM and distributed storage systems
  • Excellent SQL skills including experience with various database technologies such as MySQL / Postgres / MS SQL Server
  • Fluency in Python and/or Java
  • Intercultural competence, meticulousness and a passion for documentation
  • Very good communication skills in English (verbal and written)

Our Offer

  • Be part of the Solactive family, a motivated team with an international setting
  • Get to know the dynamics of an established, successful FinTech company
  • Experience a modern start-up culture with a flat hierarchy
  • Seize the opportunity to develop personally
  • Receive numerous benefits (e.g. gym, 30 paid vacation days per year, pension contribution, regular staff parties, access to Corporate Benefits, modern office space, employee lounge and free beverages)
