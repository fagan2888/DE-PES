Sr. Backend Engineer(Japan)


17 Media is a world leader in the live streaming industry and real-time content sharing mobile application for celebrities, entertainers, and influencers. We allow users to create and share their lives through live streams and photos, all while earning royalties.

We are a global company, with users in more than 50 countries. We offer a dynamic work environment, and an opportunity to collaborate with passionate individuals and leading-edge technologies. As we rapidly expand across the world, we are looking for dedicated professionals to be a part of our family, to be part of our vision to be the global champion in live media and entertainment.

We sincerely inviting top talents to join our top tier technical team based in Japan !

  • Location: SEIZAN building, 2-12-28, kita aoyama,minato-ku,Tokyo
  • Working Hour: From 10am to 7pm
  • Off day: Every Saturday and Sunday/ National Holidays/ Others (Offered by company)

We are currently hiring for Sr. Backend Engineer professionals that will:

  • Be versatile and passionate to tackle new problems as we continue to push the technology forward.
  • Design, develop, test, deploy, maintain, and enhance software solutions.
  • Design large scale, fault tolerant cloud based service infrastructure.
  • Analyze and improve efficiency, scalability, and stability of various system resources.
  • Deploy new product features and installations to facilitate our rapid iteration and constant growth.


You will be highly considered if you have the following experience:

  • At least 3 year experience in software development and system design
  • Go programming language.
  • Algorithms, Architecture, Cloud Computing, Database, Distributed Systems, Networking.
  • Previous experience with filesystems, concurrency, multithreading, server architectures, and distributed systems.
  • UNIX / Linux systems knowledge and/or systems administration background.
  • Good at large scale system design.


It will be great if you also have the following skills:

  • Strong analytical and coding skill.
  • Open minded, and creative thinking to create new ideas.
  • Ability to work independently with minimal guidance.


Languages

  • Chinese - Fluent
  • English - Intermediate


Benefits:

  • Annual Leave: All employees are entitled to 10 days of annual leave a year (upon the 6th month of commencement).
  • 17 Holiday: Additional 5 days paid leave to be applied continuously after 1 year of service.
  • 17 Holiday: Additional 13 days paid leave to be applied continuously after 2 year of services.
  • Anniversary Holiday: Upon joining, a day of leave per year will be granted during the month of commencement.
  • Team Building: Eg. Basketball club, Cartoon club and Morning yoga club.
  • Birthday Celebration: Every month there will be a birthday cake for those who are born in that month and the company will provide a fun and safe venue for celebrating
  • Insurance: Social insurance, Health Insurance.
  • Pre-onboard subsidies: Travel expense / Transportation cost / Hotel accommodation fee Support of ¥200,000.
  • Work VISA: We will help to process your documents after offer accepted.
