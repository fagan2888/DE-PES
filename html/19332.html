Senior Data Warehouse Engineer - Cape Town

Goldman Tech Cape Town, ZA

1 month ago

Apply on company website
Job Description

If you are a Senior Data Warehouse Engineer and ready to participate in the planning, maintenance and development of all aspects of the company's ETL; now would be the time to send your CV through.

Requirements

Relevant Qualifications

Minimum 5 Years’ Experience

Proficient in SQL and Database technologies (MySQL preferred)

Java

Experience with analytic databases and reporting/analytic tools

UNIX Scripting (shell, Perl, Python)

Duties

Design, implement, QA and deploy ETL transformations to enhance the Data Warehouse.

Design, implement, QA and deploy workflows to load, analyse and archive customer transactional data.

Respond to alerts, review error messages and fix reported bugs/quality issues in a timely manner.

Perform data quality analysis and introduce monitors and alerts to maintain it.

Work closely with Head of ETL and DW to fulfil the department's quarterly objectives.

Gain and maintain enough understanding of the business to deliver effective solutions.

Help ensure best practices and technologies are used in the department.
