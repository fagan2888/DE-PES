About Us

Liberty Global is the world’s largest international TV and broadband company, with headquarters in London, Amsterdam and Denver and operations in 10 European countries under the consumer brands Virgin Media, Unitymedia, Telenet and UPC. We invest in the infrastructure and digital platforms that empower our customers to make the most of the video, internet and communications revolution. Our substantial scale and commitment to innovation enable us to develop market-leading products delivered through next-generation networks that connect over 22 million customers subscribing to more than 46 million TV, broadband internet and telephony services. We also serve over 6 million mobile subscribers and offer WiFi service through 10 million access points across our footprint.

We’re always looking for great talent that wants to Grow With Us.

If you’re the kind of person who embraces change, challenges the status quo and has a “sky’s the limit” attitude, then our place is your place. Come and join us!

JOB PURPOSE

  • The PEGA lead system architect role is responsible for,acting as design authority for PEGA architecture.
  • Ensuresing alignment to Liberty Global IT delivery roadmaps. This involves providing technical guidance on architecture and designs for projects as well as acting as technical escalation point.
  • Ensuring systems design, business requirements, and architecture of solution is well understood by involved parties and responsible for overall delivery of software products.
  • Effective collaborator who builds working relationships with all related teams within the wider Liberty Global organisation with a focus on improving working practices and processes at all possible opportunities. She/he should have at least 8 years of progressive experience with at least one certification of PEGA Certified Senior System Architect or Lead Systems Architect.
  • Subject matter expert on the support of product sets adopted within the Agent Tools and AI team providing oversight and reviews of technical deliverables and solutions.

KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES

  • Experience in architecting and designing Pega based BPM/CRM solutions for large programs
  • Responsible for architecting and designing business applications using Pega PRPC and other Pega solution frameworks.
  • Lead the architecture, design and development/integration/test of new and upgraded communications platforms, services and/or features-capabilities.
  • Develop, document and maintain architectural guidelines/standards; being sure to consider network capabilities, product, security, compliance, operations, planning and engineering requirements as well as the impact on IT, System Integration and Test teams.
  • Provide thought-leadership to business and technical projects solving complex business requirements
  • Develops and demonstrates an advanced knowledge of the PRPC Architecture and all PRPC design and implementation features
  • Accountable for ensuring the business and technical architecture of the delivered solution is cost efficient and matches customer technical and functional requirements realizing business benefit.
  • Managing the integration requirements across different systems
  • Working closely with the business architect to verify that the outcomes and detailed requirements are aligned to the different benefits realization phase
  • Guiding architects to define the project or solution context and create strawmen/high level wireframe and integration
  • Guiding the team through the implementation (from design to deployment) of a solution or vendor specific technology

Knowledge & Experience

PREFERRED EDUCATION/ QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Third Level or Master Qualification in Software Engineering or IT relevant courses.
  • Having at least one certification of PEGA Certified Senior System Architect or Lead systems architect

Skills & Abilities

  • 8+ years of BPM and/or CRM systems implementation experience
  • Experience with PRPC v7.x (preferably V7.2 or above) with experience in upgrades and new Pega capabilities
  • Experience with Object Oriented programming, ideally Java
  • Experience with HTML, CSS and JavaScript as well as database experience
  • Experience of enforcing best practices in PEGA architecture / design / coding through reviews to ensure compliance with application quality, coding, UI and Corporate standards, including review alert logs, running pre-flight and PAL.
  • Expertise and good understanding of Relational Database Management Systems including architecting and designing for performance and scalability and working with Object to Relational Mapping schemes for distributed data access
  • Experience with the Agile delivery methodology
  • Experience working with Enterprise customers
  • Excellent communication skills
