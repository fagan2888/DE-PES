Digital Marketing Data Scientist

Vinted / Kleiderkreisel Vilnius, Vilniaus, Lithuania

2 weeks ago

Apply on company website

Vinted is the world’s biggest pre-loved fashion marketplace, with 22 million members in 12 countries. Our mission is green: we want to make second-hand the first choice globally.


A team of more than 300 people from Vinted’s offices in Vilnius, Berlin, Prague, and Warsaw develop and support our platform. We have a unique work culture that’s based on trust, honesty, and attention to self-development.


We’re looking for a Data Scientist who can help us take our digital marketing to the next level. You’ll be joining our vibrant and international marketing team, where we focus on expanding our core markets and conquering new ones in the most efficient way possible. Your focus will be on data integration, analysis, digital-marketing cost attribution, and reporting accuracy and automation.


You’ll be working hand-in-hand with the marketing data science team in Vilnius and will collaborate closely with the Berlin-based digital marketing team (so you’ll need to travel to Berlin from time to time). You’ll also be a part of the Analysts’ Guild – a team of smart and curious data geeks who’ll offer you all the support you need to excel in this position. If you enjoy playing around with data, creating models and automating manual tasks, doing a bit of coding on the side, and learning new skills – then read on.


In this position, you’ll:

  • Analyse and develop tracking/optimisation algorithms for digital marketing campaigns
  • Integrate external tools and databases (APIs, Google Adwords, etc.) with our custom in-house data warehouse platform
  • Develop and improve tools to automate campaign tracking, analysis, reporting, and optimisation
  • Communicate results to your team and top management
  • Help team members with analytical tasks or questions on an ongoing basis
  • Learn new programming and statistical languages
  • Author data pipelines via Spark- and Scala-based ETL framework
  • Create better processes, generate ideas, and lead change


About you:

  • Solid experience in data/BI analysis or data science
  • Strong business-result orientation and ability to work with iterative processes
  • At least 3 years experience working with R, Python, or another similar language
  • Able to extract and transform data with SQL queries
  • University education (BSc, MSc, or higher) focused on statistical analysis, big data, mathematics, physics, programming, or similar
  • Great teamwork skills
  • Excellent communication skills in English (written and spoken)
  • Prior experience with digital marketing or APIs would be a big bonus
  • Experience in management consulting or acquisition marketing would also be a bonus


Work perks:

  • Opportunity to join our stock options programme
  • Learning budget (13.2% from net yearly salary)
  • 25 working days of holiday
  • All the tech you need to do your job
  • Daily breakfast and lunch at Vinted Noise restaurant
  • Monthly team-building events
  • In-house gym
  • A personal “Shop @ Vinted” budget


The salary range for this position is 2100-3800 Eur GROSS / month


If this sounds like a challenge you’re up for, please send us your CV and a cover letter by November 8, 2019 . We’ll be in touch.
