We Offer

Trade Completeness (TRD COMP) Program is part of Group Operations Solution Delivery department within Group IT. TRD COMP uses a wide spectrum of technologies in the development of business solutions including languages such as Python and Bash, Data Analytics tool like Tableau and Big Data technologies (Hive, Impala, Hadoop and UNIX and SQL). There are positions in GOSD for a wide variety of skill-sets.

One of the most rewarding aspects of working in GOSD and in particular Trade Validation area is exposure to the huge variety of product types in a very dynamic market. This diversity builds lots of opportunity for internal movement, with teams in areas such as Research, Sales, Equities, Mortgage products; Bond trading, FX and Client Technology. It also means that the biggest challenge is to gain an in depth understanding of these products in order to better serve the needs of clients today, and whatever the market will bring tomorrow.

This position assumes that the vast majority of tasks will be entrusted with the expectation of full independence in their exercising as well as support the Scrum Master in leading Sprints and Releases.
  • Working in a fast-growing environment solving interesting problems with many opportunities for rapid advancement
  • Brainstorming and evaluating applications for new technologies and tools as they continue to evolve
  • Working in a pure agile environment with an experienced and seasoned Scrum Team
  • Partner with trade management, line business reps and IT business partners to perform assessment of current state trade validation processes including data analysis, documenting process flows and system diagrams
  • Gaining exposure to the senior management through various meetings and presentations
  • Working with the new technologies available in the bank relating to Big data (Cloudera Hadoop data lake, Alteryx, Tableau)

Open to discussing flexible working arrangements.
