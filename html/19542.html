Data Engineer

Moneysupermarket Group London, GB

5 days ago

Apply on company website
Are you seeking the chance to be part of a Data Engineering team who are leading the way in the use of cloud technologies across an industry leading digital business?

Do you want a role which will allow you to utilise your Python and SQL coding skills in GCP in a Data Engineering role?

Would you thrive working as part of a muti-disciplined centralised Data Team, taking ownership of the direct influence the direction of the company strategy through their technical expertise and use of data?

Then a Data Engineer role with Moneysupermarket Group is for you!

Our Product DataOps team focus on surfacing thousands of data points concerning our products and customers, placing these in the hands of our data science and product teams. We use a variety of cutting-edge cloud-based tools and technologies to foster a self-service data culture across the MoneySupermarket product and data space.

We embrace a culture of innovation in how we use data, from data ingestion, storage management, process management, and value creation to customer personalisation.

As part of the continuous improvement process facilitating our company-wide re:invent strategy, we are completely migrating from legacy in-house managed data platforms to cloud-based serverless data solutions and services using AWS & GCP technologies.

What will your daily life look like?

  • You will work closely with our cross-functional agile product teams to identify and deliver projects that will drive product development.
  • Maintaining and creating data pipelines in Talend and GCP.
  • Build the infrastructure required for optimal extraction, transformation, and loading of data from a wide variety of data sources including third party providers and our existing AWS platform.
  • Identify, design, and implement internal process improvements: automating manual processes, optimizing data delivery, re-designing infrastructure for greater scalability, etc.
  • Fostering a self-service data culture
What we need from you?

  • Strong SQL, BigQuery and Python skills
  • Experience with GCP, data pipeline and workflow management tools: Airflow, etc.
  • Familiarity with data modelling and relational databases.
  • Experience with one or more ETL tools (e.g. Talend/SSIS/Pentaho/Informatica).
  • Experience with code versioning systems (e.g. git) and CI/CD tools such as Jenkins and Google Cloud Build.
  • A passion for Data, cloud based services, web stacks and customer experience
We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity at our company. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, religion, color, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status, veteran status, or disability status.
