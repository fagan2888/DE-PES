Imagine a job where you…

  • Have a meaningful impact on the mission-critical products and services that clients rely on every single day
  • Enjoy a no-nonsense working environment where you collaborate with colleagues across business units
  • Have tons of room for career growth and opportunity

WHAT PROBLEMS WILL YOU BE SOLVING?

The Calabrio software suite generates large quantities of numerical, text, audio, and user-generated data. We are looking for an individual with a passion for Haskell programming and data to help our users extract the answers they need.

The position will involve development on our Haskell-based data engine using modern tools and libraries (such as Stack, Conduit, and STM) to add calculation features, optimize execution, and create reliable services within our platform. Interaction with other services (databases, messaging systems, caches, etc) is also important.

We take an agile approach to development, with an emphasis on creating good tests.

WHAT SKILLS WILL MAKE YOU SUCCESSFUL?

  • Experience working with databases (relational and/or No-SQL)
  • Ability to effectively communicate complex ideas to other members of the team

WHAT IS REQUIRED FOR YOU TO APPLY?

  • A degree in Computer Science, Computational Linguistics, Physics, Mathematics or related field
  • Proficiency with Haskell language, tools, and libraries
  • Experience working with databases (relational and/or No-SQL)
  • Cloud software development, deployment, and/or management
  • Ability to effectively communicate complex ideas to other members of the team

WHAT VALUES ARE IMPORTANT TO CALABRIO?

  • Collaboration amongst teams
  • Open communication across the company
  • Ambitious
  • Accountable
  • Customer Success
