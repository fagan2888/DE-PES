    Kelsey Dufresne, MBA

    Manager of Recruiting at Finance Professionals Inc.

    • We are recruiting on behalf of our client, a residential real estate developer in Toronto, to hire a Project Accountant for a 6 month contract. This position will assist the finance department with timely and accurate information reporting including responsibilities for job cost tracking, construction draw management, balance sheet reconciliations, processing bank draws, research, and communication with project managers on project status and payment approvals.


    Key Duties and Responsibilities:

    • Full cycle accounting for all project and operating companies
    • Produce draw certificates for projects, ensuring all necessary documentation is provided, signatures obtained, statutory declarations and WSIB certificates
    • Analyze, reconcile, balance and maintain accounting records - accounts receivable, accounts payable, completing cheque runs, looking into cash flows and preparing payroll for employees
    • Input and update project budgets, ensuring change orders and purchase orders are entered accurately and on a timely basis
    • Monthly reporting to project partners, including ensuring accuracy of financial information, and completeness of cheques and invoices
    • Remittance of taxes – monthly HST and WSIB
    • Visa Reconciliation on a monthly basis
    • Invoice and deposit Operating fee cheques on a monthly basis
    • Review and recommend relevant modifications to accounting systems and procedures


    Qualifications

    • Bachelor’s degree in accounting or related field
    • Experience with construction draws required
    • Knowledge of payroll legislation
    • Knowledge of Timberline preferred
    • A thorough knowledge of MS Excel, Outlook and Word
    • Attention to detail
    • Excellent organizational and time management skills



    Only those candidates legally eligible to work in Canada, need apply.

    For additional job postings, please visit https://www.fpinc.ca/opportunities/
