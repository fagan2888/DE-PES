afarax is looking for freelance consultants for a specific mission with a strong know-how in Data. We need your lights !

We are a Belgian based Team with a strong network of consultants and Companies active in various

business sectors and facing the challenges of the Digital Transformation. The concept is very simple: Once you enter the ecosystem, you can access to our projects and let us make your life a way easier.


The mission

In the context of Business Intelligence, our client is looking for a BI Data Engineer to perform

the following duties:

  • Understand, document and manage the expectations and needs of the business.
  • Convert the needs of the trade and the detailed analysis and respecting the standards.
  • Interact with other teams to establish the best possible solution.
  • Follow-up of the implementation with the developers or develop only the solution, under the
  • supervision of the architect / team leader
  • Write the test cases and execute them.
  • Investigation and creation of ad hoc queries and answers to business questions
  • Implementation and maintenance of the documentation technique


Is this you ?

  • Essential control and development of relational databases
  • Strong 2 years’ experience and writing complex SQL scripts
  • Datavault 2.0 expertise and modeling and dimensional modeling
  • Experience and DataWarehouse / BI
  • You are analytical, data-driven and you like the job and the team
  • You have the ability to work on multiple and simultaneous projects while respecting a good quality and delivery times


How we support you ?

  • Experience with SPARX Enterprise Architect
  • Experience with SAP BusinessObject Suite
  • Experience with JIRA, Confluence
  • Knowledge of Python
  • Experience with Talend
  • Experience with AWS (stepfunction, S3, ..)
