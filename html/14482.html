    About the Role

    We are looking for a Data Scientist that will work on analyzing large amounts of data to find patterns and produce insights that will improve our companies services. Our ideal team member will have the proper computer science, mathematical and statistical expertise, as well as natural curiosity and creative mind. You will operate in a high-end environment with state-of-the-art systems and approaches, joining a team of data specialists. We will rely on you to ask questions, connect the dots and uncover new exciting paths that will lead to business solutions.


    Key Responsibilities

    • Work as the lead data scientist and co-operate with the engineering team to strategize and execute the development of data products
    • Take full ownership of turning discoveries and ideas into machine/deep learning models and use models’ output to create and improve our SaaS services
    • Design and run reproducible experiments on data to test a hypothesis and present the results to key stakeholders
    • Follow best practises and stay plugged into emerging technologies/industry trends
    • Actively contribute to taking Data Science at Paloservices to the next level

    Requirements

    • Master’s degree in Computer science/Data science or a related field
    • A solid foundation in using the Python ecosystem (Numpy, Pandas, Jupyter, etc) for data analysis
    • Great understanding of core machine learning concepts & deep learning frameworks
    • At least 5 years of experience
    • Great understanding of core statistical concepts
    • Team spirit and willingness to learn from as well as mentor others
    • Good communication skills

    Desired Qualities

    Knowledge of the following will be an advantage:

    • Great programming skills and experience with software engineering principles
    • Good knowledge of Scala/Java and databases
    • Experience working on Linux
    • Experience with cloud services (AWS, GCP, Azure)
    • Experience with large-scale processing frameworks (Spark, Hadoop, etc.)
    • Experience with any BI tool (Tableau, Power BI, etc.)
