About The Company

At PVH we are transforming the fashion industry by breaking conventions every single day. We are always exploring ways to bring the industry forward and constantly accelerating the pace in which we do business. We believe that to succeed as a fashion lifestyle company we need to offer much more than high quality trendy collections. Our goal is to become the ultimate fashion tech brand.

Department Information

To achieve this, we are looking for an enthusiastic Infrastructure Engineer who will implement and support the technical infrastructure of our Digital Showroom across the world. Customer value and automation are key in deployment and configuration of our landscape. We are working towards enabling autonomous teams and ‘hands-off’ deployments. In this position you will be an ambassador to both our end-users and local field engineers or third parties so collaboration and maintaining a healthy relationship with adjacent teams is key.

In this role you will
  • Provide day-to-day delivery and tech support (3rd line) of Windows Desktop and Mobile applications;
  • Perform and improve the incident management process on the Digital Showroom Windows and Mobile environments and improve data distribution;
  • Pro-actively identify potential risks and providing solutions;
  • Implement and update the configuration management system;
  • Standardize a worldwide Digital Showroom hybrid-system (Windows and MacOS);
  • Inform local field engineers about new system updates or processes and build remote reporting lines;
  • Create new processes to measure service by all parties involved in supporting the Digital Showroom;
  • Actively participate in the change management process for Digital Showroom
  • Travel to several locations to fully support our growth and provide on-site training for the Digital Showroom environment.


You have
  • 5+ years proven experience with Windows Server 2016 and older, SQL Server, Windows 10, Azure, Office365, SCCM (cb), Intune (or other MDM solutions);
  • Extensive knowledge of PowerShell and PS automation,
  • Ability to architect the desired state configuration;
  • Knowledge of Ansible and MacOS;
  • JAMF certification is a plus;
  • Great communication skills in both spoken and written English


You Are
  • Passionate about technology and the impact it can have on organizations
  • Motivated by challenge
  • Driven, solution oriented and always improving
  • Highly collaborative and able to encourage the same in teams
  • Open minded individual and able to work with different cultures and mindsets.


We offer you

In the Digital Hub, we are agile and fast moving. We respect and grow our knowledge and skills, but we also have fun, we are supporting non-profitable efforts in PVH and we are challenging and inspiring each other every day to be even more creative, open minded and to explore new possibilities. Innovation and people are the key!


  • A unique opportunity to build industry transforming solutions;
  • Dedicated people managers to stimulate talent development;
  • Plenty of opportunity to make the role your own, to be entrepreneurial;
  • An environment where innovation is at the base of everything;
  • Competitive salary with benefits;
  • State-of-the-art campus facilities;
  • ‘All-you-can-eat’ healthy lunches and amazing events;
  • Wide range of courses at PVH University to satisfy your learning appetite;
  • Great colleagues (curious, ambitious, and fun);
  • International working environment;
