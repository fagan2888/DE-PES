Data Engineer

Job Description

Data Engineer at GoPomelo are working on some of the most interesting problems in a variety of different domains, working on both internal products and providing world-class consulting to clients.

You will join our team of Researchers and Data Engineer from around the world in doing research and tackling real-world problems for our clients, and for our Data Innovation Lab with strong data analytics infrastructure in Google Cloud

Requirements

Responsibilities Include
  • Extract huge volumes of data from multiple sources; including DWH, Big Data Hadoop, Social unstructured data, etc.
  • Employ sophisticated analytics programs and statistical methods to prepare data for use in predictive and prescriptive modeling
  • Formulate hypotheses for testing and designing experiments to capture new data
  • Explore and examine data from a variety of angles to determine hidden weaknesses, trends and/or opportunities
  • Develop model and conduct analyses using tools which may include Python, SQL, MapReduce, etc.
  • Develop Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and AI algorithm to solve respective business cases and pain points.
  • Communicate predictions and findings to management and other relevant teams through effective data visualizations, reports, and presentations
  • Identify practical approach to test and learn key hypotheses to extend analytic insight.

Qualifications
  • Bachelor / Master s’ degree in Business Administration, Economics, or Engineering with heavy concentration in statistics. Quantitative-oriented MBA is desired.
  • Passion for Data Science, Machine Learning in a research or consulting basis
  • A rigorous understanding of the fundamentals of statistics applied to business challenges and microeconomics is required
  • Strong proficiency in Excel/Sheets, Python, SQL, or other related coding languages and statistical, and data visualization tools
  • Familiarity with decision techniques including optimization, simulation, regression, decision trees, neural networks, cluster analysis, mixed models
  • Solid experience in traditional analytics, text analytics, voice analytics, Machine Learning, Deep learning, and AI
  • Good communication and presentation skills in English or Thai
