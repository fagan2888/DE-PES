Junior Data Scientist – you will analyze and crunch data to derive insights and help clients to uncover hidden potential driven by data and build first in a class analytical solutions.

  • University graduate – bachelor or master’s degree in fields such as Computer Science, Engineering, IT and other technical background, obtained not earlier than 2017
  • Quick deep-dive capability to tackle new challenges
  • Strong desire to raise skills in Data Science and Machine Learning
  • Strong knowledge in math and computer science
  • Knowledge in programming, Python is an advantage
  • Participation in hackathons and competitions (e.g. Kaggle) and other data science events is an advantage
  • Thorough analytical skills
  • Superior organizational, problem solving, and time management abilities
  • Communication and interpersonal skills
  • Upper Intermediate English (or higher) and Native Russian
  • 0,6 - 1 year of experience in Data Science and Machine Learning will be plus
