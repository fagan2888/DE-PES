Are you interested in working with education? Building high-impact consumer products that touch millions of students? Passei Direto is looking for a Data Engineer to help us making the best use of our large amount of data. You will work with our team of Data Science and leverage PD’s massive scale to build intelligence so we can deliver rich and relevant experience to our students.

Responsabilidades e atribuições
  • You will architect and build structures for BI, analytics and data science, integrating data from our various datasources;
  • You will build automated pipelines for processing and cleaning data;
  • You will become an expert in our datasets, understanding their strengths and weaknesses;
  • You will take care of PD's data and metrics ensuring their quality and visibility across the company;
  • You will work in collaboration with data scientists across advanced analytics to imagine and build creative solutions to challenging questions, most often with a clear line of sight from your work to real-world impact;
  • You will work on projects that cut across various facets of PD (from Product to Marketing to Content);
  • You will experiment with new analytical techniques and tools.

Requisitos e qualificações
  • You are proficient in SQL and have experience with some high level programming language (i.e. Python, R, Scala, Java);
  • You have experience with a range of data engineering techniques (i.e. data modeling, ETL, Data Warehouse, Data Lake) and tools (i.e. Hadoop, Spark);
  • You have earned a degree in mathematics, engineering, and/or computer science;
  • You are a creative problem-solver who is passionate about digging into complex problems and devising new approaches to reach results;
  • You are enthusiastic about learning new techniques and technologies.

Some Technologies We Use
  • AWS (EC2, S3, RDS, EMR, Glue, DMS, ECS);
  • R, Python;
  • MySQL;
  • Spark.
