Job Description

dunnhumby is the world’s leading customer science company. We analyse data
and apply insights from nearly one billion shoppers across the globe to
create personalised customer experiences in digital, mobile, and retail
environments. Our strategic process, proprietary insights, and multichannel
media capabilities build loyalty with customers to drive competitive
advantage and sustained growth for clients. dunnhumby employs over 2,000
experts in offices throughout Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas and
works with a prestigious group of companies including Tesco, Monoprix,
Raley’s, Macy’s, Coca-Cola, Procter & Gamble, and PepsiCo. For more
information, please visit us at www.dunnhumby.com.
dunnhumby is looking for a talented Lead Engineer!
You will, as an expert for one or more areas of engineering, deliver on the
development and deployment of highly complex technology products and/or
platforms and inspire others around it.

What You'll Be Doing
  • Develop and deploy complex applications, components, software, platforms
or services that are used at scale and contribute to significant
performance or business value (e.g. core components that are used across
teams, POCs,…).
  • Lead on new solutions. Gather input, feedback and requirements from key
stakeholders within Engineering and Product Management and define the
platform/ component roadmap for specific projects. Influence the team
software architecture as required.
  • Lead exciting and complex end-to-end projects and ensure Service Level
Agreements are delivered on. Define and develop solutions to complex
problems that arise ensuring standards are fully met and best practice is
adhered to.
  • Inspire engineers throughout the organisation, providing deep expertise,
thought leadership, expert advice and guidance to resolve specific
challenges.
  • Create clear standards, tools and technical guidelines in line with the
department’s strategy, policies and client obligations.
  • Manage internal and external technical stakeholders including product
owners, commercial and business stakeholders and product consumers.
  • Lead projects to improve engineering, test and operational excellence.
Qualifications

What you'll need:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Maths or Engineering or equivalent
  • Good experience in the relevant area of expertise
  • Software development (agile)
  • Software testing tools (e.g. Protractor, Selenium)
  • Product management (Agile)
  • Data Architecture & Modelling
  • Cloud infrastructure, management and hosting
  • Code management tools (Git)
  • Infrastructure as code / DevOps (Kubernetes)
  • Distributed Architecture
  • Asynchroneous computing
  • Proficiency in at least two of the below:
  • Scala
  • C# / .NET
  • JavaScript / React
  • Python
  • Hadoop / HDFS
  • Airflow
  • Linux
  • D / C++
  • PHP
  • Industry Trends / Thought Leadership
A Plus If You Also Have
  • Experience with projects of great technological complexity
  • ASP.NET experience
  • Experience with React, Redux, or a comparable JavaScript technology
(jQuery is not comparable)
Life at dunnhumby
dunnhumbians are passionate people who always start with the Customer, we
are ambitious and decisive, we listen and understand, innovate and explore,
and work together to grow one dunnhumby
  • Here you'll be part of a culture that welcomes participation and
discussion at all levels
  • You’ll get to work in an internationally collaborative environment
alongside teams of ambitious, inquisitive and forward-thinking individuals
  • We believe that learning and development never stops and provide
opportunities to feed your curiosity to understand, improve, and learn;
encouraging everyone to build on the best of what we do
  • You'll have the opportunity to work with ground-breaking technology
delivering extraordinary impact for our customers
  • We’re a great believer in work-life balance offering early finish Fridays
plus an extra day off on your birthday
  • dunnhumby's spirit of community and the diversity of our people's skills,
backgrounds and experiences makes us stronger. We continuously seek to
build an environment of inclusion, equality and respect because we know
that will make us the best business we can be
Discover where a career with us can take you. Meet our people on YouTube to
learn about the diversity in career paths across dunnhumby. For more about
our culture and to explore the benefits we offer visit our Careers site.
