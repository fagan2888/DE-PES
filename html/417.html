About This Job

Flexibility. Fun. Freedom to grow. It’s all happening at Nielsen, where analysts are working hard to drive decisions for top companies. We also gain global experience in an engaging, multi-cultural remote work environment.

Responsibilities
  • Working with product databases and responsibility for their correctness
  • Finding solutions to implement client requirements
  • Communication and cooperation with colleagues from foreign branches of the company, but not directly with the client.


a Little Bit About You

You’re looking for a career with room to grow and the training that’ll set you up for success. You can type quickly and accurately–even with your eyes closed, if you wanted. You have a love/hate relationship with charts and spreadsheets, but you wouldn’t have it any other way. You’re ready to break ground on what’s next.

Qualifications
  • Logical thinking
  • English on level B2
  • Knowledge of Excel - especially function - vlookup, exact & concatenate, filtration and working with tables.


About Nielsen

We’re in tune with what the world is watching, buying, and everything in between. If you can think of it, we’re measuring it. We sift through the small stuff and piece together big pictures to provide a comprehensive understanding of what’s happening now and what’s coming next for our clients. Today’s data is tomorrow’s marketplace revelation.

We like to be in the middle of the action. That’s why you can find us at work in over 100 countries. From global industry leaders to small businesses, consumer goods to media companies, we work with them all. We’re bringing in data 24/7 and the possibilities are endless. See what’s next with us at Nielsen: careers.nielsen.com
