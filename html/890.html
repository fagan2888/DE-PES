    To achieve this, we’ve brought together one of the largest teams of scientists, clinicians, mathematicians and engineers to focus on combining the ever-growing computing power of machines, with the best medical expertise of humans, to create a comprehensive, immediate and personalized health service and make it universally available.


    At Babylon our people aren’t just part of a team, they’re part of something bigger. We’re a vibrant community of creative thinkers and doers, forging the way for a new generation of healthcare. We’re only as good as our people. So, finding the best people is everything to us.


    We serve millions, but we choose our people one at a time…


    Purpose of Role

    Working as part of Babylon’s Data Tribe, you will join us to build a dynamic data platform, to help Babylon grow its capabilities in offering the most comprehensive view of consumer and population health. You will work as part of an end-to-end feature squad, which is working to build the large scale, high volume, high performance platform which provides insights to internal and external customers. It is these insights that will enable consumers to live smarter, and health care providers and payers to move from reactive sick-care to proactive optimisation of individual and population health. We are looking for Senior Data Engineers who want to add immense value to the business and help us tell the Babylon story.


    Key Responsibilities

    • Build robust production grade ETL pipelines and data models
    • Build and maintain the next generation of reporting and analytical solutions
    • Perform data modelling to streamline data by combining data from multiple sources
    • Use of relational, NoSQL, file system and graph databases for work
    • Work on Greenfield projects
    • Drive continuous improvements
    • Provide support and insights to data science and machine learning squads
    • Share you experience of data access practices and design patterns
    • Ensure coding standards are in place.


    Skills Required


    Essential

    • Experience in a data engineering role and with large-scale data environments
    • Proven experience of working with GCP, Google BigQuery, Hadoop and other SQL and NoSQL databases and are eager to learn even more
    • High ability to code in one flavour of Python or another
    • Demonstrate experience of using Docker for deployments in a micro-service architecture
    • Knowledge of at least one of the following: PowerBI, Tableau, Qlikview or similar


    Desirable

    • Ability to write unit, integration and behavioural tests

    Bonus

    • You have a pet named Protobuff


    Person Specification

    • You are enthusiastic and full of positive and infectious energy
    • You have been part of a distributed team and are a great communicator
    • You have the ability and don't mind changing direction with shifting priorities
    • You are a problem solver and can present tactical solutions and recommendations


    Qualifications and Experience

    • BA AND/OR MSc Degree in Computer Science or similar field OR substantial experience in developing robust, scalable and production grade data pipelines and models
