For one of our customers in Amsterdam we’re looking for a DevOps engineer – Cloud. The infrastructure is very diverse and the domains that you’ll work for are TV & Video.

You’ll work closely together with Solution Architects, Product owners, Test automation engineers and other DevOps engineers, to build and adept their ever evolving infrastructure.

This Makes You Happy
  • Ownership of your work and influence;
  • Working in Amsterdam, with all it’s benefits;
  • Working in a very diverse team, both cultural as in the team organisation & roles;
  • Travelling abroad once in a while to other international colleague’s;
Requirements:
  • Bachelor degree with a technical background;
  • 5 years of DevOps experience (Senior) in a Commercial environment as a DevOps engineer;
  • Strong experience on how to architect complex infrastructure;
  • Strong experience with their stack: AWS, Splunk, Atlassian Suite, PRTG, Linux;
  • Able to communicate with a diverse set of collaborators and stakeholders;
  • Excellent communication skills in the English language;
  • You are happy to take ownership on security and stability;
  • Willingness to travel occassionally abroad to near shore offices;
  • You are happy to work autonomously with a high level of responsibility and freedom;
