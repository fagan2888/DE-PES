Introduction

Do you want to join a team of talented professionals at a startup on the verge of explosive international growth? Our company has designed and developed an AI robotics platform that detects crop health in commercial greenhouses internationally, eliminating the need for mass chemical pesticide usage. With exciting products hitting the market in the coming months, we are looking for candidates who are passionate about high technology, food, innovation, and sustainability.


Company

We are the proud winner of the 2018 GreenTech Innovation and New Ventures BC 2017 Awards. In addition, we are members of the Climate Technology Center and Network and the SDG high forum of the United Nations. To find out more about our mission, our products and our progress please visit www.ecoation.com.

Position Overview

This exciting opportunity will include:


* Full-stack software development

* Creating delightful user experiences by designing and producing features that are simple, intuitive, performant and reliable.

* Building, testing and shipping new user-facing code in modern tools, including reusable components and front-end libraries.

* Translating designs, wireframes and business requirements into high-quality code.

* Owning entire features from specification through to shipping.

* Working effectively with a high degree of autonomy, within a collaboratively minded team.

* Collaborating cross-functionally with other teams, including engineering (internal and external), design, research, data science, product management, executive leadership, and recruitment.

* Commercializing the product and ensuring customer success and satisfaction.

Skill Requirements

* Expert proficiency with JavaScript (React, Redux, Node.js) or similar

* Minimum 5+ years of relevant experience in software engineering/ development

* Experience in the commercialization of a software platform, with exposure to the full product cycle from inception to launch to maintenance is an asset

* Ability to provide coaching and technical leadership to peers

* Ability to deliver well-tested, maintainable code

* Experience designing and shipping user-facing features in a modern, component-based UI framework

  • * Ability to learn and adapt to a rapidly evolving tech stack
