Data Analytics Engineer (Associate Director/Director)

Bank of Singapore, Asia's Global Private Bank Singapore, SG

2 months ago

Apply on company website
General Description

Candidate to support the transition of different functional teams within BOS Risk onto the Bank 4.0 data framework. This includes help building analytics and support re-engineering of critical business processes in the new data framework.

Role also involves supporting both the existing BAU processes and also working closely with business users to understand their needs and implement solutions within the existing data model

Responsibilities
  • Develop and manage Analytics and Dashboards based on the requirements provided by business users
  • Define business analytics requirements clearly and engage in discussions and solutions around them to get them successfully implemented
  • Understand the business context and deliver insights from data to drive outcome
  • Cross support the team in ad-hoc analytics, investigations and queries
  • Enjoy working with large data sets and utilize previous experience/tools to harness the data better
  • Automating existing reports and processes
  • Possess at least a Bachelor's degree and some years of experience in Data Science, Statistical Analysis, Computer Science, Quant Research or related field
  • At least 3+ years’ experience within banking or financial services
  • Strong programming skills in Python and SQL mandatory
  • Experience in visualization tools such as Qlik or Tableau
  • Structured and Logical thinker
  • Possess strong analytical skills and are comfortable dealing with numerical data
  • Experience in areas within risk (Operations/Credit/Market Risk) are an advantage

Reporting To

Team Head of Collateral Methodology, Risk Analytics & Reporting
