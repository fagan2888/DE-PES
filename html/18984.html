Key Responsibilities

The successful candidate would accomplish a wide variety of machine intelligence- related tasks such as:
  • performing research/investigation pertaining to the development and
evaluation of new (and existing) techniques, algorithms, and processes for

machine learning-based analytics, automated question answering using

parallel inputs (image and speech), and natural language processing/NLP

(including speech-enabled) applications
  • investigating use cases for these applications e.g. in the domains of medical
diagnosis and healthcare customer service
  • creating and testing production-level prototypes and/or experimental models
related to the use cases for the applications mentioned above
  • generating technical reports, charts, graphs, and other relevant documents to
support productization of the prototypes/models
  • advising senior research and business stakeholders on various aspects of
his/her work
  • identifying and applying problem-solving skills in addressing scientific and
business challenges of interest to the company

Education
  • MTech / PhD in Engineering/computer science from Tier-1 institutes.
Expertise
  • demonstrates strong hands-on experience in programming (Java, Python, C#,
C++)
  • Libraries: Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, Gensim, GATE for NLP.
  • Tools: Scikit-Learn, Keras;
  • - SAS – Visual Analytics; SAS - Visual Data Mining
  • demonstrates and applies a broad knowledge of machine learning and NLP through successful completion of moderately complex assignments
  • - Working experience with data from different sources for example, machine
Logs, Patient records.
  • familiar with fundamental concepts, practices, and principles of software
development, version control, and scalable infrastructure/platforms
  • provides track record of publications & presentations at top-tier
conferences/competitions
  • able to work well as individual contributor and within a multidisciplinary team
