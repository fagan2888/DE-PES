Senior Legal IT & Digital Counsel (m/f/d), Novartis, Prague, Czech Republic

2019 is an exciting year for Novartis as we accelerate our growth in the digital space. As Senior Legal IT & Digital Counsel you will provide proactive, integrated, legal and business oriented advice on all NBS IT & Digital matters.

Your Responsibilities:

  • Legal counsel for global NBS IT & Digital projects and contracts including: Leading multiple, complex IT & Digital projects and IT & Digital contract negotiations from the side of Legal (often several multimillion deals to manage at the same time); handling a manifold of internal and external constituencies (Novartis Business, IT, Sourcing, Data Privacy, Information Governance Management, Enterprise Architecture, external legal counsels, counterparties etc.)
  • Managing considerable global and cross divisional legal risks for the Novartis Group related to IT & Digital matters
  • Advising on and managing of various long term IT outsourcing arrangements (deals worth up to several hundred million)
  • Day to day legal coverage to NBS IT (facing up to 50 NBS IT Sourcing and NBS IT Supplier Management Office professionals in Switzerland, US, Czech Republic, India and other countries)
  • Team member and key contributor to Novartis Legal Best Practices (e.g. develop legal standards for IT & Digital transactions, ensure consistent legal advice global and cross-divisionally on IT & Digital related matters, sharing knowledge on IT & Digital related issues with other Novartis lawyers, create and update contract templates for IT & Digital transactions, develop trainings on legal matters for IT & Digital experts)
  • Team member and active contributor of the NBS Legal team at global level (for instance being part of the setup of governance structure and different processes for the NBS Legal team)

What you’ll bring to the role:

  • University Master’s degree in law; bar exam
  • Fluent English in writing and speaking
  • 10 years of experience over all as a lawyer in an international law firm or in a multinational corporation with proven experience in IT & Digital
  • Profound experience and expertise in managing global IT & Digital transactions

You’ll receive:

Monthly pension contribution matching your individual contribution up to max 3% of your gross monthly basic salary; Risk Life Insurance and insurance in case of long term sickness; 5-week holiday per year; (1 week above the Labour Law requirement) ; 4 paid sick days within one calendar year in case of absence due to sickness without a medical sickness report; Cafeteria employee benefit program – choice of benefits from Edenred CZ in the amount of 12,500 CZK per year; Meal vouchers in amount of 80 CZK for each working day (full tax covered by company); Company Car / Car Allowance; MultiSport Card; Stock Options. Find out more about Novartis Business Services: https://www.novartis.cz/

Why consider Novartis?

750 million. That’s how many lives our products touch. And while we’re proud of that fact, in this world of digital and technological transformation, we must also ask ourselves this: how can we continue to improve and extend even more people’s lives?

We believe the answers are found when curious, courageous and collaborative people like you are brought together in an inspiring environment. Where you’re given opportunities to explore the power of digital and data. Where you’re empowered to risk failure by taking smart risks, and where you’re surrounded by people who share your determination to tackle the world’s toughest medical challenges.

We are Novartis. Join us and help us reimagine medicine.
