  • Experience in ITM security constraints (fire detection, intrusion detection, etc)
  • Strong experience with Data Center operations, constraints, monitoring and improving

  • Open minded to innovative solutions
  • Strong communication skills, presenting highly technical issues verbally and in writing to both technical and non-technical personnel at all levels in the organization.
