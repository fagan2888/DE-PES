    • Solution Engineer Oracle Utilities Applications

    This position is available in our highly skilled Solution Engineering Hub in Bucharest, as part of a team dedicated to Oracle Utilities Global Business Unit.

    Utilities Global Business Unit in Oracle is focused on selling utility-specific solutions, the most important of which are:
    •   Oracle Utilities Customer to Meter
    •   Oracle Utilities Work and Asset Management
    •   Oracle Utilities Network Management System

    These are a set of industry leading solutions deployed globally by many of the major utilities companies.

    As a Solution Engineer in this team you will work very closely together with your presales field colleagues, with a main focus on:


    •   Respond to tenders
    •   Configure business scenarios in Oracle Utilities applications to be demonstrated to the client
    •   Create standard or customer specific demos
    •   Prepare product presentations
    •   Answer questions from customers and partners in preparing and delivering product demonstrations
    •   Interact with product development and product management about new releases, new functionalities

    •   Support other presales activities as they arise

    As a presales consultant you have the chance to develop in a very broad spectrum, ranging from product expertise, customer interaction, communication, and general IT market knowledge. Successful presales solution engineers are highly demanded individuals with the possibility to develop a career in many different IT (and non-IT) functions.

    PROFILE:
    To fulfill the role of Solution Engineer we are looking for individuals who match the following profile:
    •   Affinity with or experience in the utilities industry (Electric, Gas, Water)
    •   Very good understanding of utility business processes such as billing, metering and workforce management
    •   Experience (worked with) Utility specific software products; preferably Oracle, but others are acceptable as well.
    •   Worked in a business or IT role – preferably with exposure to utilities applications. Knowledge of one Oracle Utilities application and understanding of the data model is a plus
    •   Good understanding of Oracle or similar products
    •   Knowledge of the Oracle Utilities Application Framework is a plus
    •   Cloud knowledge will be an advantage

    •   Most important is an eagerness to learn! Oracle has an incredibly broad (and growing) portfolio of industry leading products. If you find it interesting and fun to pick up a new product and master it in a very short time frame, this is the place to be!

    Non Technical:
    •   Good communication skills, able to articulate key messages very clearly
    •   Openness to work with different languages & cultures
    •   Results orientation, you won’t be satisfied until the job is done with the right quality!
    •   Ability to work in (virtual) teams. To get a specific job done often requires working together with many colleagues spread out in different countries
    •   Self-motivated, you have the natural drive to learn and pick up new challenges
    •   Team-worker
    •   Ability to thrive in an environment that is fast-changing and often unpredictable
    •   Always looking to raise the bar of own skills/competencies development

    Other:

    •   Work experience in a similar role is an advantage

    •   Fluency (including writing) in English is a must. Fluency in another major European language is a plus

    WHAT WE OFFER:
    Oracle is a very successful, profitable and leading international IT provider. With close to 130.000 employees Oracle provides an environment that enables employees to learn, grow and be successful. Specifically related to the presales role in the service center we provide:
    •   An environment that is focused on continuous learning.
    •   Ample opportunity to train on new products and to develop new personal skills
    •   A combination of deploying technical knowledge and sales abilities
    •   A challenging and interesting work environment with the possibility for interaction with colleagues, customers and partners across the world
    •   Attractive salary and benefits
    •   Excellent possibilities to develop yourself and your career
