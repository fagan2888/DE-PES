Our opportunity

Being part of the Business Intelligence team under Claims Department, you will apply your professional knowledge and expertise in business analysis and transformation projects on customer care service, claims handling and also provide administrative support on day-to-day claims handling and customer enquiry. If you are interested in working in a dynamic, challenging environment, for a company that recognizes and rewards your creativity, initiative and contributions, just simply click "Apply online"

Your role

As Claims Data Analyst, Your Main Responsibilities Will Involve
  • Develop and implement end-to-end data pipeline from collection, analysis, reporting to visualization
  • Provide support for data issues, maintain best practices for data management and provide support where necessary for the data projects
  • Gather business requirements and translate this into report developments including change specifications
  • Handle phone calls tactfully
  • Register, assess, adjust and settle/decline claim cases
  • Work on ad-hoc projects when required


Your Skills And Experience

As Claims Data Analyst, your skills and qualifications will ideally include:
  • University graduate, preferably major in Statistics, Business Analysis, Mathematics or a related field
  • Minimum 2 years working experience in insurance / finance industry
  • Solid knowledge of VBA
  • Knowledge on SQL and R
  • Experience in BI reporting tools (e.g. Power BI, QlikView) is a strong advantage
  • Proficient in spoken and written English is necessary
  • Good understanding of data modelling and data management process
  • Good data analytics & programming skills
  • Self-motivated, independent and able to work under pressure
  • Candidates with less experience will be considered for Claims Service Officer position
