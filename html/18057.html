    We are looking for an intellectually curious Big Data Engineer with a significant set of technical skills and business acumen to join our team in Coca-Cola Hellenic Business Solutions & Systems Group Function (BSS) . You will have opportunity to automate generation of insights and integrate them with standard systems to support operational decisions at scale in an optimizing way.

    Tech savvy to innovate?


    ABOUT YOUR NEW JOB:

    A Data Engineer role involves different tasks as collecting, structuring and analyzing data to turn it into answers for the benefit of the business. You will ensure quality and consistency of data flows in the Big Data and Advanced Analytics (BDAA) process and projects .
    You will have the chance to work with the leading technologies & vendors and gain deep business understanding of FMCG industry on a group level. Using your skills, you will engage in development, testing and maintenance to keep data accessible and primed for analysis. Shape your role, challenge assumptions and introduce new ways of thinking and solving problems.


    YOUR NEW KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

    • Implementation and automation of Internal data extraction from BW on HANA using aDSO, InfoCubes and Queries as InfoProviders via OpenHub;
    • Implementing ABAP routines needed for transformations or extractor logic when extracting data from SAP source systems;
    • Implementation and automation of External data extraction from openly available internet data sources via REST APIs (e.g. OpenStreetMap, Google Maps, Flickr);
    • Data ingestion via Data Factory activities and store it Azure Data Lake Store;
    • Data cleaning, curation and enrichment by using Azure Databricks (SQL, Python, R, PySpark, SparkR);
    • Preparing consolidated data models for use by Data Scientists and populating SQL Databases for use by Web Application developers when a UI workbench is needed;
    • Exposing data via Azure SQL DW for consumption in BI tools, SAP BW and other central systems;
    • Data Catalogue maintenance/update describing the data in the data lake;
    • Converting complex business requirements into preliminary planned, organized and scaled data processing pipelines, following pre-agreed with the Data Architect standards and rules; contribute to keeping and improving those standards and rules.


    ARE THESE YOUR SECRET INGREDIENTS?

    • I have deep knowledge in manipulating, processing, and extracting value from datasets ;
    • + 5 years of experience in software development, data engineering, business intelligence, data science, or related field;
    • Proficiency with Programming Languages: SQL, Python, R to be used in Azure Databricks (Spark, PySpark, SparkR, SQL) for data processing;
    • I have strong project management skills and ability to plan and prioritize work in a fast-paced environment;
    • Experience with: MS Azure Data Factory, MS Azure Data Lake Store (WebHDFS), SQL Database (T-SQL), SQL DW (with PolyBase), PowerShell;
    • Ability to think creatively, highly-driven and self-motivated;
    • Knowledge of SAP BW for HANA (Extractors, Transformations, Modeling aDSOs, Queries, OpenHubs), ABAP (for extractor routines in other SAP source systems – ERP, CRM), SAP SLT;
    • Good understanding of established and emerging data technologies;
    • Experience with huge volumes of data (partitioned tables with billions of records, files with hundreds of GBs);
    • Fluent English speaker with capable writing skills;
    • Bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, mathematics, or a related technical discipline.


    ABOUT YOUR NEW TEAM:

    Coca-Cola Hellenic is a unique family, comprised of the Bottling Business, Accounting, Data Management, HR and IT centers , delivering services to the entire CCH Group.


    BSS is Business Solutions and Systems, a Central Function , meeting point where technology and processes are blended to create competitive advantage for our company - CCHBC. We are an international team of IT professionals , having 21 nationalities. We strive to be a strategic business partner, accelerating value creation and cost optimization, and sustaining CCHBC as a supplier of choice through the fast delivery of simple, innovative and cost-effective solutions and services.
