  • Creating automated ml-systems and constant performance tracking You will bring:
  • University degree in Mathematics, Computer science, Physics or similar

  • Experience with frameworks like MatplotLib, Scikit, Pandas, TensorFlow, Keras (Python Stack)
  • Proficiency in using query languages such as SQL
  • Knowledge of Natural Language Processing
  • Good applied statistics skills, such as distributions, statistical testing...
