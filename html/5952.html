This client is building a new team as they continue to progress their technological capabilities. They are a data-driven organisation, it guides their strategic decision-making and service/product development. As a result, they need a Data Engineer to support the integration of various products.

What You'll Be Doing

As a Data Engineer, you will support the integration of several products, requiring you to work with a combination of serverless technologies.

You will support the roll-out and execution of the data services across various business units. As a result, you'll collaborate with other engineering and data teams to ensure that the data is integrated and accessible.

This role requires a range of data engineering activities including; prototyping, ETL pipelines, process automation and more.

What Experience You'll Need To Apply
  • Minimum of 1 years' experience in data or software engineering
  • Python, Scala or Java development experience
  • Cloud experience (AWS, GCP, Azure or similar)
  • Pair programming experience preferred
  • Agile or similar methodology preferred


What You'll Get In Return For Your Experience

A base salary of up to £60,000 DOE with excellent bonuses and benefits package including flexi-time and WFH 1 day p/week.
