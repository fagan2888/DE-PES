Micron in Taiwan

Micron’s global footprint is designed to deliver comprehensive customer collaboration, support and quality around the world and throughout the product lifecycle. Taiwan plays a meaningful role in strengthening Micron’s position in leading-edge DRAM.

Micron’s Taiwan sites include sales and technical support in Taipei along with state-of-the-art fabrication facilities in Taichung and Taoyuan that together comprise the company’s DRAM Center of Perfection. The Center’s goal is to achieve the most efficient and effective technology transfer and manufacturing ramp by combining front- and back-end manufacturing, product engineering and technology development roles.

MTB ASM Process Engineering (ADT) Engineer Assistant

Job Description

As an Assembly Process Development Engineering Assistant, you will be responsible for evaluating and improving new process, equipment and material baseline, supporting in development of process, equipment and/or material capability and evaluating/qualifying new device, equipment and/or material. You will also be involved in supporting project handover and transfer to site as well as assisting in implementation of new process and process change to operation.

Responsibilities And Tasks

Evaluate and improve new Process, Equipment and Material baseline

Support Engineer to troubleshoot process problems and determine the best course of action

Make process adjustments to optimize quality

Support Engineer to troubleshoot deviations using systematic troubleshooting techniques

Ensure corrective action was taken to prevent reoccurrence of process development quality issue

Support in development of Process, Equipment and/or Material Capability

Assist Engineer in conducting experiments (DOE - Design of Experiments) and Engineering evaluation

Understand factors that impact process quality and propose solutions

Understand process limits and capabilities (CPK) and support process improvement evaluations

Assist Engineer in monitoring Process Capability for new baseline

Evaluate/Qualify New Device, Equipment and/or Material

Support new process, qualification builds

Support new equipment evaluation and qualification builds

Support new material (direct or indirect) evaluation and qualification builds

Assist in implementation of New Process and Process Changes to Operation

Assist Engineer in establishing new operation procedures/specification

Assist Engineer in updating Assembly On-Line Document System (AODS) and change communication procedures: ECN (Engineering Change Notice)

Familiarize with Assembly qualification procedures

Attain knowledge of material attributes (shelf life, storage requirements, etc.)

Coordinate and conduct training/briefing for operational personnel on product/process requirement, machine and operation procedures

Support Project handover and transfer to Site

Assist Engineer in preparing process documentation for handing over to Site

Assist data gathering (eg Yield, defect pareto, Quality Deviation Report incidence etc) for low volume production

Assist Engineer to gather feedback for Continuous Improvement after mass volume production
