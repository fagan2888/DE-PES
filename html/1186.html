    Recruiter - Central Functions at Primark

    • Role: Senior Data Engineer

    Purpose of role: A key focus of the BI & Analytics team is to provide a stable & robust enterprise analytics platform, that turns large amounts of complex data into a single version of the truth. You will be responsible for expanding and optimizing our data, as well as optimizing data flow and collection for cross functional teams. This position requires senior technical capabilities, an ability to recognise business problems and propose technical solutions from a BI & Analytics perspective that ultimately enable the organisation to achieve its goals.


    Key Responsibilities:

    • Database Solutions
      • Provision for & deliver on making large and/or complex data more accessible, understandable and usable.
      • Responsible for understanding design & maintenance of our existing Data Warehouse (Teradata) (whole solution / all layers).
      • Hands on work for the advancement of existing and new BI & Analytics projects including leading with design and build to appropriate standards.
      • Emphasis on ensuring solutions are robust and generic to enable scalability into the future.
      • Support of existing systems including management of support incidents as appropriate.
    • ETL / ELT
      • Design and implement ETL/ELT frameworks.

    • Standards and Best Practice
      • Establish and maintain standard processes and best practice procedures for use within the BI & Analytics environment.
      • Lead with technical advice and support to the BI & Analytics team and wider community in respect to good design practices and principles on development of our Datawarehouse platform.
    • Collaboration
      • Develop effective relationships and work collaboratively within the BI & Analytics team and across business and Technology functions to deliver data projects whilst taking a proactive approach to predicting the needs of the business.

    Knowledge, skills experience:

    • Educated to degree level (or equivalent). Bachelor’s Degree in computer science, Information Systems or Data Analytics preferred.
    • 10+ years’ experience in related field.
    • Experience with design and implementation of ETL/ELT framework for complex warehouses/marts.
    • Knowledge of large data sets and experience with performance tuning and troubleshooting
    • Core technical skills in Teradata including:
      • Strong knowledge of Teradata Architecture
      • Hands on experience in Teradata development using utilities and SQL
      • Good understanding of Data Warehouse Modelling concepts
      • Up to date on large scale data analytics and reporting technologies, trends and practices across a variety of industries.
      • Understanding of Backup and Recovery strategies
