Merlin is disrupting the employment industry. Our mission is to revolutionize the way hourly talent finds work and to enable employers with the ability to quickly connect to qualified candidates. We enable job seekers to find the best, most relevant jobs close to their homes while helping employers to find, screen and select the best candidates for every opening.

We are a group of operators with over 40 years of experience building online marketplaces, backed by top tier investors who have also funded Airbnb, Uber, Stripe, LinkedIn, Pinterest and many others.

We are looking for a data analyst with the drive to take our sales analytics initiative to the next level. You will use your knowledge, creativity and business acumen to distill optimization opportunities from Merlin’s data. Your work will have profound strategic weight for the company.

Your day-to-day
  • Develop reporting tools to share sales data in a digestible way with the rest of our organization.
  • Develop in-depth analysis to answer strategic performance questions.
  • Work closely with stakeholders in the sales team to define data solutions and identify opportunities to optimize Merlin’s sales metrics.
  • Define best practices and new processes to collect, analyze and report our metrics.
  • Build periodic analytics reports for board and investor meetings.
What you bring to the team
  • Strong knowledge of SQL
  • Knowledge of Python or R
  • Advanced excel skills including but not limited to pivot tables, vlookups, basic modeling
  • Knowledge of different analytics methods, including linear regression, logistic regression, CART, clustering, and data visualization
  • Independent thinking and curious about the nuances in the data
  • Excellent attention to detail and highly organized
  • Excellent oral and writing communication skills in english (c1)
  • Bachelor’s or master’s degree in a relevant field

Why Merlin

The true magic behind Merlin, is the people. We are a team of highly motivated rabble rousers! We are the movers and the shakers, the innovators and the storytellers. We are highly intelligent, forward thinkers with a growth mindset. We not only welcome, but initiate change. We take ownership of our personal and collective successes.

At Merlin, You Can Expect

  • An environment where ideas are heard and innovation is encouraged
  • Personal/professional growth and development opportunities
  • Comfortable office space, on the most important business area in Bogota (Chico)
  • Sharp, motivated co-workers from multiple nationalities
  • Coffee bar and snacks at the office
  • Stock option package
  • Executive level commitment to education, growth, and development
  • Healthcare plan
  • You can work from home a few days per week
  • Flexible hours + bike parking
