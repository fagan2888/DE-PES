Software Engineer: We are currently seeking to fill a full-time position as a Software Engineer in the Engineering Department at Medstrat, Inc. in Downers Grove, IL. The is a full stack developer role for the Joints product. The individual should have the ability to independently design and develop features for the product. This person must possess a solid understanding of Linux, be comfortable in a LAMP stack development environment and have a thorough understanding of software design concepts. The individual should stay informed on technology trends and product roadmaps to make strategic solution architecture recommendations. Duties will include debugging and fixing software issues in an existing product and creating enhancements in a new web app product. The successful candidate will be able to write software in an object-oriented paradigm, work within a Test-Driven Development framework and be detail-oriented. This position reports to the Engineering Manager. Description of the system: Our Joints product consists of a Picture Archiving and Communication System (PACS) server and web/native clients to access medical images. The client server architecture of our PACS creates a robust, scalable, distributed system for storage, search and retrieval of terabyte sized medical image data sets. The result is that our users have distributed access to MRI, CT, X ray and other medical image types from multiple types of devices which in turn improves clinical workflow. Our software provides image manipulation, image processing and pre operative surgical planning abilities to orthopedic doctors and clinicians at hundreds of orthopedic practices across the country. Our PACS is considered the best in class digital diagnostic and surgical planning solution in the orthopedic PACS market space. Essentials Duties:
  • Design and develop changes to PACS webapp and server in accordance with planning efforts.
  • Debug and fix issues that arise from customer feedback
  • Review proposed product changes developed by other Software Engineers.
  • Maintain test suites to automate unit and integration testing
  • Effectively work with multi-disciplinary personnel to achieve project goals Essentials Skills:
  • B.S. in Computer Science with 5+ years of experience in full stack software development
  • Significant experience in Python, Javascript
  • Experience in using web technologies for client side application development
  • Knowledge of SQL and database concepts
  • Experience with a LAMP stack
  • Demonstrated ability to be self-directed and to learn new concepts/tools required for the job
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate effectively in a multi-disciplinary team environment Related Skills:
  • Experience in e-commerce applications is a plus
  • Experience in cloud computing is a plus Benefits: We offer unprecedented benefits to our employees including a healthcare plan, 401(k), long -term and short- term disability, paid holidays and life insurance. About Medstrat: As the industry founder, Medstrat is the most experienced provider of orthopedic digital imaging solutions. Our expert engineering and digitally connected support staff are combined with proven, advanced, customizable software to optimize patient care, productivity and profitability within an orthopedic practice. - provided by Dice
