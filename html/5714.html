Job Description
  • Designs innovative UX web and visual communication that seamlessly interacts with brand, products and users
  • Pro-actively enhances and integrates designs and UX in our web platform according to the business direction
  • Connects the brand and products to people and represents value-centric thinking and excellence in aesthetic and benefit sensibilities, while simultaneously addresses the design challenges such as usability, marketing, and engineering constraints
  • This role will be highly hands-on, pragmatic, super creative, problem solver, and collaborative across multi-discipline colleagues from data scientist, marketing, brand, and engineering activities

Requirements
  • Bachelor or Master’s degree in User Interaction Design, Art, Engineer or related field
  • 2-3+ years direct experience in website design/UX development
  • Strong knowledge of Human Focus Design, Human Psychology, and Design Thinking process
  • Experience in designing for data-driven culture companies
  • Has a sense of practical engineering and understand ongoing business priorities
  • Ability to deal with a dynamic team and manage multiple projects while maintaining strict attention to details
  • Ability to communicate with both technical and business teams with right jargons and metrics
  • Good English communication skills

Benefits

Competitive salary, bonus, provident fund and be a fast-track employee with significant career growth opportunities in a fast-growing business.

Funand casual yet highly professional working atmosphere

Data-driven culture not by seniority or influencer

Flexible work hours with result oriented

Operate like a start-up under good governance company with great financial stability

Intensive professional training programs both in-house and external with global partner alliances.

Directly coached and supported by C-Level managements who are top notch visionary leaders in Thai IT industry.
