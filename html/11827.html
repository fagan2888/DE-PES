The Role
Our client, a large Telco is seeking a Data Engineer for a consulting engagement onsite in CBD precinct.
The position will suit a mid-level applicant with visibility and experience in a similar role.
The role will be responsible for;

Provide technical expertise to the team and other stakeholders on data initiatives and innovations
Structures and presents data that is ingested to users and to other developers using the Platform

To be successful in this role, you will ideally possess;

Requires experience with interfaces and implementing Data Quality management over complex enterprise data sets
Requires experience with

Big data processing - Python, Jupyter, Spark, PySpark, SQL, Splunk, AWS Glue, AWS Lambda, Serverless
Database and storage - AWS S3, Parquet, RDBMS, AWS Athena, Elastic/Kibana, Kafka

Requires 2-5 years' experience in a role for ingesting, structuring and presenting data for consumption
Excellent communication skills
Natural ability to analyze and solve problems, use initiative and multi-task
