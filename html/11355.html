    Hiring for Product Owner and Java Architect in Coupang！

    • Global Operations (GO) is the key department of Coupang that operates Supply Chain and Logistics to ensure a fast and high-quality delivery service to our customers. GO Data Science team applies data-driven techniques to transform business processes of fulfillment centers (FCs) and transportations. Our team provides ad-hoc statistical analyses to reveal operational problems and identify root causes in various operation processes. We provide decision support by building machine learning and optimization models/algorithms to solve business problems with complex rules and conflicting objectives, such as labor planning, task scheduling, FC picking/stowing optimization, operational anomaly detection, transportation routing, etc.

    Team Description (3-5 sentences):

    Fulfillment Center and Transportation Data Science is responsible for applying Data Science and Machine Learning to solve problems in Operational Cost, Last Mile Delivery, Inventory Management and Order Fulfillment Efficiency. The team faces a range of challenges in identifying opportunities to improve key cost, efficiency and customer experience metrics at large scale. The Manager of the Fulfillment Center and Transportation Data Science team will lead a team of Data Scientists to build new models and partnering with engineering teams to implement solutions into production environments.

    Key Responsibilities:

    • Lead Fulfillment Center and Transportation Data Science Team: Guide Data Scientists to solutions that solve business challenges in comprehensive and sustainable manner
    • Work with Product Owners to translate business challenges into problem statements
    • Own Cost and Efficiency KPIs while demonstrating improvement to top leadership
    • Develop vision and roadmap of future projects using cutting edge trends in Machine Learning and Data Science
    • Hiring and Development responsibility for Fulfillment and Transportation Data Science team

    Basic Qualifications:

    • Master’s degree in Statistics, Math, Computer Science or related Engineering field
    • 5+ years relevant working experience in Data Science or Machine Learning
    • Solid foundation in Operations Research with hands-on experience
    • Practical Machine Learning experience (Data Preprocessing, Feature Selection / Engineering, Training, Model Evaluation)
    • Experience leading a team of Data Scientists

    Preferred Qualifications:

    • Ph.D.
    • Experience building models with R or Python
    • Warehouse Management and/or Logistics domain experience
