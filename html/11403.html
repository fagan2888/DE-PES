    • Ugiat is a highly specialized company in video understanding solutions with expertise in the fields of computer vision, digital image/audio processing and artificial intelligence. It was born from the Technical University of Catalonia(UPC Barcelona Tech) as a bridge between the academic and the business world. The company main values are based on the continuous research and innovation offering cutting-edge solutions to the market.


    We are looking for a full-time associate/senior media engineer to develop professional applications based on streaming technologies. Besides, the candidate must be highly motivated to learn and give support to the rest of the team in scientific & engineering areas through the product development.


    Required Skills:

    • Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Telecommunications or equivalent.
    • Good understanding of the streaming ecosystem: media transport protocols (HLS, MPEG-DASH), video codecs, DRM, etc...
    • Strong C/C++ or Java coding skills, Python, OOP, and willingness to learn more languages.
    • Advanced knowledge about software design, algorithms, data structures, memory management, and concurrency.
    • Team Player.


    Additional skills:

    • Dominance of Javascript language.
    • Knowledge about artificial intelligence.
    • Excellent written skills in mother tongue and English.
