    • Engineering teams at Brainly are working on some of the most complex technical challenges in the ed-tech space and creating the product for almost 150 million students around the world. We focus on building the infrastructure and features that enable Brainly users to ask and answer millions of questions every day.


    With this size of monthly usage, we’re generating huge amounts of diverse types of data and are looking to leverage this better to improve our service. As Data Engineer at Brainly, you will have an opportunity to shape the data engineering function, being responsible for the choice of solutions and technologies in BI infrastructure area.


    What's in it for you?

    Data Engineering function at Brainly needs to address increasing business requirements as we start creating and implementing new processes for acquiring and processing data. This means

    Data Engineer is not only about working with cool technology, but also:

    • Working closely with various teams and business units to build the data processing flows reflecting their needs
    • Touching a lot of fields in the company (payments processing, data processing, supporting reporting systems)


    What you will do:

    • Work on data processing framework from gathering business requirements through designing and implementing them
    • Create and modify data flows for analytical and reporting purposes
    • Work with AWS Solutions related to ETL/ELT stack


    What will you need?

    Required

    • 2 years in a similar position or related field
    • Deep understanding of relational databases
    • Hands-on experience in SQL
    • Know-how of ETL stages

    Preferred

    • Experience in a data warehouse
    • Experience in cloud-based infrastructure
    • Reporting/visualisation tools (Tableau, Metabase, Google Data Studio)


    Additional details:

    • location: Kraków, Poland
    • start date: as soon as possible (however, we’re happy to wait for the right person)
    • some of our benefits: absolutely no dress code, flexible working hours, opportunity to work remotely from time to time, company lunches on Thursdays, private medical healthcare for you and your family, company events, and more.
    • 800$/ year of personal development budget + time off for conferences and workshops
