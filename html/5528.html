Software Engineer

GEW Technologies Cape Town, ZA

5 days ago

Apply on company website
Join our growing company specializing in Spectrum Monitoring Solutions (ITU) and be at the forefront of telecommunication monitoring technology.

General Areas Of Responsibilities
  • Software architect as well as team leader for the SMMS software engineering team.
  • Program and R&D management in collaboration with Department Manager
  • Software user requirements and functional specification drafting
  • Acceptance test formulation and verification
  • Data flow and data rule programming
  • Process modeling
  • Product and system software road mapping
  • Proposal preparation
  • Presentation and facilitation sessions with clients
  • Assist process to produce high quality software products according to defined time scales
  • Integration of software departments across continents: Processes/Tools/ Projects
  • New product, market and requirement analysis


Personal Attributes And Skills Required
  • Be a self-starter and take initiative on engineering requirements work
  • Good team player
  • High quality and results driven
  • Provide and commit to time estimates on intended scope of work
  • Willing and deadline driven on contracted GEW milestones
  • International Exposure at clients foreseen


Qualifications And Experience Required
  • Software systems engineering experience 2 5 years with management experience
  • B-Eng (Electronics) or BSC with a strong technical systems back-ground
  • Web applications, C#.NET, SQL Server, PHP, HTML5, JavaScript, Java, JBoss
  • Electronics or computer engineers with a systems engineering background will have an added advantage
  • Tools for Business Information Analysis i.e. Enterprise Architect
  • Have extensive best practice for software product engineering processes (non-financial systems focus)
  • Radio frequency and data transmission experience
