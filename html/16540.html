Collabro is a fast-growing company servicing the needs of major infrastructure and energy clients all over the world. With an outstanding history and key offices located in the UK, Australia and Malaysia, we develop, provide and support tailored personnel logistics and data integration products to clients on every continent. We are looking for a talented software engineer to join the Aberdeen office of our growing global team.



Responsibilities:

Duties include but are not limited to -

  • Developing Web Applications and APIs
  • Level 2 and Level 3 Service Desk support
  • Unit Testing software for quality and stability
  • Troubleshooting issues related to design or data
  • Generate and update technical documentation
  • Assist in installation and deployment of applications
  • Develop APEX reports
  • Administer and manage data modelling
  • Administer client change management requests


Desirable skills and experience:

  • Technical documentation, product documentation & technical frameworks
  • Service desk
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Effective communication to internal and external stakeholders on solution design
  • A desire and ability to learn and share knowledge with others
  • A strong team player
  • Work independently with minimal supervision
  • Show ownership of your deliverables
  • Work in a dynamic, fast-paced environment and meet deadlines under pressure
  • Willing to travel for client support both local and globally


Key technical requirements – more details & other requirements on request

  • Java
  • SQL
  • Oracle
  • JUnit
  • Maven


Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science/Information Technology
  • 2-3 years of relevant working experience in designing and implementing applications
