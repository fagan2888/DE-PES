Our Organization:

Within ArcelorMittal Flat Carbon Europe (FCE), our department “Digital & IT CMO”, is looking for a Data & Application Engineer .


The main missions of our department are:

  • Managing Information Systems (Writing Business specifications, coordinating projects, ensuring roll outs, etc.) based on the requirements formulated by the Business
  • Driving the evolution of existing Information Systems , again based on the Business requirements but also in concordance with the IS/IT Strategic Plan and IT Standards
  • Providing the necessary support to users of our Information Systems through our "Service Desk" Organization


Missions and responsibilities:

In the context of our ECMO Program, our Internal Data Scientist team is developing new pricing models/applications in Agile methodology.

Some of those models/applications are getting now industrialized and we need to prepare ourselves to switch them from project to more standardized maintenance mode. Within our Digital & IT CMO Pricing team, we’re looking for a Data & Application Engineer in order to:

  • Take responsibility from technical perspective on running pricing models/applications in maintenance mode including monitoring of performance
  • Structure/adapt the Databases in line with Business and/or Data Science team requirements
  • Interact with Data Science team, exchange ideas, help with new Model’s development (including Machine Learning components)
  • Setup and maintain new data flows which are required for models in development mode
  • Manage Master Data of models and perform mass uploads if required
  • Liaise with IT Infrastructure Team in the context of managed applications
  • Maintain and gain more experience with cloud services from Azure
  • Help in refactoring of code (mainly python) prior to industrialisation
  • Manage evolutions of different User Interfaces built around models (mass loads of operational data, rule engine improvements, functional changes etc)
  • Perform testing before deploying changes in production (non-regressions & new versions)
  • Be able to propose solutions / tools to fulfil Business requirements

The position is available immediately in Luxembourg.

  • Development and coding skills:

- Experienced cloud user ( A zure data factory v2, Azure cloud storage, azure SQL server)

Experience in SQL/database

  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
  • Good knowledge of English is mandatory
  • Proven success in contributing to a team-oriented environment
  • Business / Operational background (Sales, Supply Chain, etc. in an industrial world) is a plus
