    • Role


    We're currently recruiting for a regulatory specialist from the Medical Device industry to work in our clients European team based in Lyon whose main activity is to ensure that media documents are compliant: this includes both new media (product launch) and a systematic review of their European website.


    Skill Set


    • EU Regulatory knowledge and experience in regulatory compliance of class I - IIb medical device marketing communications (including Web, electronic and hardcopy mediums)
    • Knowledge (experience or training) in related MDR requirements
    • Able to work autonomously following on boarding training
    • English speaking
