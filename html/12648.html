Who are we?

We are a tech company that exists to help businesses succeed. Our team is continually growing, inventing and disrupting conventions. We believe business can do amazing things and it's our job to help them. We are dedicated in crafting an outstanding flexible employee experience for all team members and offer a culture that champions the real you. This is why your work matters. This is MYOB.

What Team Will You Be a Part Of

J oin our Operations and Architecture nation where you will immerse yourself within the central heart of how we empower and support our people through the technical solutions and tools that we possess. Be part of a collaborative group who cares about your career growth and development of work as well as be exposed to some complex problems that will challenge and extend your capability through new and developed systems.

The freedom to experiment and innovate drives the way we solve complex problems and is the central theme to everything we do here.

What's Involved

As an Application Security Engineer, you will be technically responsible for securing our products, remaining hands-on by being close to the tools. This role will see you collaborating with development and operational teams to ensure our products are highly secure in line with industry standards.

Engage with extraordinary engineers, product managers and members of multi-functional teams to drive the implementation of innovative security.

You will be finding ways of identifying and preventing security vulnerabilities, build tools and coach developers to help enable teams to deliver products securely into the market, as well as highlight risks and defects that may cause a threat to our products overall.

Bring your passion for security concepts, software development and automation. Iteration is fundamental, where you are adept at moving fast, and adapting to change quickly. The successful person will bring a passion for security and innovative change. We are looking for someone who is an inspirational security champion, with the ability to shape and build our security function by bringing customer security to the forefront of our development and operational processes.

What's Needed To Be Successful In This Role

The requirements listed below are guidelines, not hard and fast rules. You don’t have to satisfy every requirement or meet every qualification listed. If your skills are transferable and you are in the ballpark, apply. Applying gives you the opportunity to be considered.
  • 3+ years' experience in an application security or similar role, related to client facing cloud-based services, or similar large-scale complex enterprise solutions
  • Experience with and knowledge of security principles, techniques, and technologies.
  • Experience managing cloud based services preferably hosted in AWS and/or Microsoft Azure environments
  • Experience with threat modelling and vulnerability assessment
  • An understanding of OWASP Top 10 and CWE/SANS Top 25
  • Experience with manual and automated secure code and architecture reviews
  • Prior build and continuous integration/delivery experience utilising tools such as Buildkite and Jenkins
  • Ability to communicate technical concepts in an easy to understand format to peers and management
  • Security related certification is a bonus – e.g. OSCP, CEH, GWEB, GWAPT
