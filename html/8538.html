In short


AlpacaJapan (www.alpaca.ai) is Asia’s leading AI FinTech based in Tokyo and specialized in building Deep Learning and Big data technologies for financial markets. We are providing short-term and long-term forecasting signals for a wide range of assets.


We are currently hiring experienced an Data Engineer.



About Alpaca


Alpaca provides investment solutions for Bankers, Asset Managers, Traders and Market Makers worldwide. Our products include: AlpacaForecast, a platform that predicts market price movements; AlpacaRadar/Search, a new product that predicts market risk anomalies between cross assets; Alpaca AI Prediction Matrix, available in Bloomberg Apps Portal and launched in 2017.


In September 2018 AlpacaJapan raised $6M in a series A funding in order to expand globally and push the development of its product. Our groundbreaking technology has already attracted the attention of major banking and financial institutions within Japan and around the world and led to long-lasting partnerships.


Position & Responsibilities


We are looking for an experienced Data Engineer to join us in Tokyo, Japan. Our mission is to create a new collaboration platform between finance professionals and AI for the capital markets.


As a Data Engineer, you will -

collaborate with the data scientists to improve their productivity and create new products together, as well as build and maintain historical and live data pipelines with a focus on quality and performance. Your responsibilities will include:

  • contributing to build the robust, low-latency production environment handling high frequency streaming data
  • collaborating with the data scientists to improve their productivity: collect requirements, create roadmaps and build the data platform used for research
  • proposing solutions for meta data and data lineage management
  • building a scalable datalake and data pipelines with a focus on ease of use and clarity
  • instituting data quality monitoring and reports to alerting platform
  • driving innovation by evaluating new technologies, original data sources and recent research papers that add value to our products
  • collaborating with the team to iterate quickly on products and share knowledge


Qualifications

As a Data Engineer, we expect -

you to have strong general knowledge of systems and computer science, to be capable of designing scalable and data-intensive architectures and with practical experience working with data.


Minimum Qualifications:

  • A degree in Computer Science or related technical field
  • Strong structural knowledge of computer science from low to high layer
  • Strong understanding of data management, data quality monitoring and metadata management
  • Experience with scaling data pipelines and programmatic ETL
  • Exposure to continuous integration implementations that utilize DevOps style tools (such as CircleCI, Terraform, etc.)
  • Familiar with good engineering practices such as TDD, code review, git
  • Fluent with python stack: Numpy, Pandas


Preferred Qualifications:

  • 2 years of relevant experience
  • Knowledge of several programming languages: Golang, Rust, Java, etc
  • Experience handling streaming data and protocols
  • Previous experience in building data-centric products: Big Data, Search or Data Analytics platforms
  • Experience with containerized solutions: using docker-compose, kubernetes, mesos, etc
  • Experience with cloud services: EC2, S3, SQS, Batch, Lambda, EKS, etc
  • Experience handling financial data: Forex, Stocks, ETFs… is appreciated but not required
  • Experience with ML packages/frameworks (sklearn, PyTorch, Tensorflow, Chainer, etc)


Culture


Team Diversity:

We are a collaborative team of engineers, data scientists, quants, architects, designers, bankers and project managers from diverse backgrounds.


Community Engagement:

We benefit from the community and are happy to give back. We cherish connections from our external partners, investors and players from the start-up industry. We have recently open-sourced our high-performance time series database MarketStore (https://github.com/Alpacahq/marketstore) written in Go.


Work-life Balance:

We believe and honour the fact that beyond an employee’s working life, parenting, family and friends are all very important aspects of a person’s life. We allocate and encourage remote work time to allow for a fulfilling work-life balance.
