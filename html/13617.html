Data Analyst

Digital Transformation Agency Canberra, Australia

7 days ago

Apply on company website

We are looking for a talented Data Analyst to join gov.au Observatory, to help us achieve our mission to quantify user interactions with government services.


We want to understand how people use services and how that differs from how those services are designed. We will provide insights and context to government teams, equipping them with the knowledge to deliver better outcomes for their users.


You will be helping government do the hard work to radically improve government information and services. You will explore data to uncover new hypotheses and insights to uncover how Australians interact with the Government’s digital services.


Your skills will help the team identify conflicts between the design and use of government services, and then clearly communicate those conflicts to delivery teams across the APS.


Role specific tasks:

You will work with the Observatory team to combine quantitative and qualitative (research) data to deliver a comprehensive view of service delivery by:

  • Extract data from third party systems using tools to interact with APIs
  • Explore data to uncover interesting and important insights
  • Quantify where services are delivering poor user experiences
  • Contextualise quantitative and qualitative data within a larger landscape
  • Produce engaging visual experiences and artifacts that clearly convey key insights to a wide audience
  • Work with engineers to develop repeatable analysis and visualisation processes
  • Promote and foster a positive creative culture and approach to innovation, data analysis, sharing ideas, skills and knowledge to help build a strong reputation for DTA
  • Work at pace as part of an agile multi-disciplined team.


Skills and experience:

  • Explore data to uncover interesting and important insights
  • Contextualise quantitative data within a larger landscape
  • Produce engaging visual experiences and artifacts that clearly convey key insights to a wide audience
  • Work with engineers to develop repeatable analysis and visualisation processes
  • Experience with open source tools and systems similar to Python, R, Airflow, Tensorflow, NLTK, Sklearn, iGraph and Tidyverse.


Eligibility

This position is subject to the following eligibility requirements:

  • Australian citizenship
  • Baseline security clearance. The successful applicant must have or be willing and eligible to undergo and maintain an Australian Government security clearance at this level.


Opportunity type:

Non-ongoing (6 months) – Full Time
