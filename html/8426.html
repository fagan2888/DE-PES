The Institute for Security Studies (ISS) is seeking to appoint an Information Technology (IT) Officer. The IT Officer will support the Senior IT Specialist to provide end user assistance to around 100 ISS staff, and ensure implementation and maintenance of computer systems and networks.


Overview of duties

  • Provide technical support and training to end users in a multicultural environment
  • Install, configure, maintain, monitor and troubleshoot computer systems
  • Install, configure, maintain, monitor and troubleshoot networks
  • Maintain licence and asset registry
  • Assist with IT equipment procurement


Overview of requirements

  • Three-year tertiary qualification in IT, or an equivalent NQF skills certification
  • Six years experience in Apple/Windows server and Apple/Windows workstation support, maintenance, troubleshooting and training
  • Working knowledge of Virtual Box, anti-virus with management console and end point, ITIL framework, L1, L2 networking as well as network security and monitoring (firewall, web filter, load balancing, etc.), mail/webmail server, e-mail protection systems and Office 365 for business
  • Working knowledge of backup systems, scripts, automation and restoration, Windows backup and Time Machine
  • Basic HTML knowledge will be an advantage
  • Experience managing 3CX Based VoIP Systems will be an advantage


The contract will be for two years, and may be renewed subject to organisational requirements, funding and satisfactory performance. A competitive salary is offered.


Closing date: 1st November 2019


The ISS is committed to achieving employment equity in the workplace. Female applicants are encouraged to apply.
