About Lombard International

Lombard International is a leading life insurance-based wealth solutions provider with over 25 years’ experience. We have the strength and expertise to provide wealth structuring solutions using unit-linked life insurance to ultra-high net worth individuals, their families and institutions around the globe.

With head offices in Luxembourg and Philadelphia, and serving over 20 markets across the United States, Europe, Asia and Latin America, we are able to provide multi-jurisdictional wealth planning solutions on a global basis with a deep understanding of local cultures and attitudes.

Global assets under administration are in excess of €44.9bn ($51.0bn) as of 30 June 2019. Our global team of over 500 includes more than 60 technical experts and over 100 client services professionals specializing in multiple jurisdictions.

Funds managed by Blackstone own Lombard International. Blackstone is one of the world’s leading investment firms with $545 billion in assets under management as of 30 June 2019.

For further information about Lombard International visit: www.lombardinternational.com

At Lombard, it is in our nature to innovate!
We love what we do, quickly adapt, and we are agile, to bring out the very best in what we do.

The Senior System Engineer is responsible for day to day support of the IT infrastructure components, not limited to servers, storages, network and telecommunication solutions, monitoring, troubleshooting and ensuring resiliency of the business critical IT systems. In addition to that the role requires participation in projects and initiatives related improve current technical solutions and processes as well as transform existing IT environment into technology solutions which fit into business requirements and support growth of the organization.

Your role – Are you ready for a challenge?
  • Day to day management of the IT infrastructure components distributed into both on premise and cloud environments
  • Administration of virtualized servers environment
  • Network administration in cooperation with external vendors
  • Administration of firewalls and load balancers in cooperation with external vendors
  • Actively participate in IT automation initiatives which aim into Infrastructure as a Code approach
  • Contribute in initiatives related to improvement, cost optimization and consolidation of IT infrastructure solutions and services
  • Actively participate in modernizing, improving or replacing of the existing IT infrastructure solutions
  • Management of centralized monitoring tools - including alarms, thresholds, integration with other systems
  • Management of the backup solution
  • Administration of Citrix XenApp/XenMobile solutions
  • Ensure availability and resiliency of business critical applications / IT components
  • Contribute in improvement and governance of operational processes
  • Cooperation with internal IT teams in order to support existing and implement new business applications / IT infrastructure solutions
  • Manage incidents and requests within agreed SLAs
  • Technical ownership of IT infrastructure solutions


Your profile – Have you got what it takes to become our System Engineer- Infrastructure?
  • 4-5 years of experience in administering of Windows servers (Microsoft certification is an asset)
  • 2-3 years of experience in administering of Linux servers, e.g. RedHat, CentOS (Linux certification is an asset)
  • 4-5 years of experience in administering virtualized environments based on VMware, Hyper-V, etc.
  • Hands-on experience in network administration based on Cisco solutions
  • Knowledge of enterprise range storage administration, e.g. EMC VMAX, Dell Unity
  • Knowledge of network technologies including: routing, switching
  • Knowledge of firewall, load balancers and VPN solutions, e.g. F5, Cisco Asa, PaloAlto
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Exchange
  • Knowledge of Active Directory and GPO
  • Knowledge of second factor authentication solutions, e.g. Vasco
  • Knowledge and experience in public clouds, e.g. Azure, AWS (certification is an asset)
  • Knowledge of telecommunication solutions, e.g. CUCM
  • Experience with monitoring solutions, e.g. Nimsoft, Zabbix, etc.
  • Hands on experience in scripting preferably PowerShell and Python
  • Knowledge of automation solutions, e.g. Ansible, Terraform, Chef (nice to have)
  • Experience with enterprise backup solutions, e.g. CommVault, Veeam, NetBackup
  • Experience with processes automation tools - MS System Center Orchestrator, SMA OpCon (nice to have)
  • Experience with ITSM tools
  • Knowledge of Service Now (nice to have)
  • ITIL knowledge (certificate would be an asset)
  • Knowledge of security aspects
  • Very good communication skills in English
  • Ability to learn quickly
  • Flexible, motivated and a determined team player
