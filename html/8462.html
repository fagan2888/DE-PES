    Recruiter II at Planet Pharma

    • Client: Clinical Research Organisation

    Type: Permanent

    Availability: ASAP

    Location: Anywhere in UK

    Responsibilities

    • Performing the Lead Data Manager role with responsibility for the successful quality, planning, execution, monitoring, control and finalisation of all data management activities
    • Definition and maintenance of Data Management project plan, input into the overall project plan. Responsible for the execution of all data management aspects of the project plan and reporting of status across project team.
    • Implementation and maintenance of accurate, clear Data Management documentation. Responsible for the setup, maintenance and finalisation of the Data Management Study File
    • Oversight of all data review activities; measuring progress against the project plan.
    • Detailed knowledge and understanding of contracted Data Management obligations and associated budget; Ongoing assessment of budget, regular and accurate review of Data Management progress against financial milestones and monthly completion metrics.
    • Identifies, manages and communicates risk to timelines, quality and budget.
    • Effectively support regulatory and sponsor audits


    Qualifications

    • At least  4 years experience working as a Data Manager in Pharmaceutical industry
    • Experience leading international studies
    • CRO experience preffered but not essential


    Please contact Urszula Kaldi on +44 (0) 203 868 1009 or ukaldi@planet-pharma.co.uk for more details
