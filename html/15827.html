The Company

We are geeks, tech-lovers and inventive people working together to revolutionise the Data Centre world.

We bring Artificial Intelligence inside our buildings by developing innovative systems.

We are coming out the “start-up” phase, with now more than 60 people in 6 different locations, but still willing to keep the atmosphere of a small company.

The Job

We are looking for a convinced and passionate devOps engineer to join our team !

You will be in charge of managing our ever growing fleet of servers monitoring, configuration and maintenance , as well as being a key part of the installation of our Data Centers based all over the world .

You will also support development teams by improving our continuous integration and continuous delivery pipelines . You will analyse, develop and improve our existing workflows.

You will be facing some challenging time and tight deadlines but you will also have the opportunity to work on the latest technologies and make your own technical choices together with your Team.

The Perfect Match

You have at least a first experience in a similar position.

You master Linux , Git , Docker and provisioning tools such as Puppet and Ansible .

You have experience in networking (Firewall / IPSec).

If you are familiar with Jenkins , Consul, vSphere , it would be even better !

Same goes for any interest you may have for monitoring platforms (Prometheus / Sensu / …).

As an international company, you will work in an English-speaking environment : being English proficient is needed and any other language is a plus!

The Deal

Company shares in addition to your salary package

Complementary Health Insurance and Life Disability Insurance

Flexible schedule
