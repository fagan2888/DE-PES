This is an excellent career opportunity for a creative individual who strives to deliver superior technology solutions to external and internal clients. At Hatch you will work in a team setting around the globe and be based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We are in search of a task oriented, self-starter who is confident and has exceptional communication and analytical skills. You will be a person who thrives in an open world environment, but knows how to find a balance with guidelines. You are also a person that can work around flexible working hours as we are bound working with people in various time zones.

Hatch is all about challenge. We challenge our clients with innovative ideas that help them take their business to higher levels. We challenge our employees to take on responsibilities and take initiative regardless of position title or job description.

Responsibilities

Our Hatch Digital Project Delivery team offers a wide range of technology related services and solutions that support our global Hatch network and our client solutions. In this role, you will be a part of the Control, Automation and Electrical group responsible for developing and customizing of software used to deliver EPCM projects on a global scale. The development will incorporate all integration functionality to other utilized software for a complete and seamless environment for the project engineering team.

We are looking for a software specialist to add to our dynamic team. The responsibilities of the role include:
  • Responsible for the analysis of EPCM functional workflows and engineering requirements to develop and customize engineering tools (interface and back end systems and applications)
  • Develop and execute manual and automated testing of software applications to successfully and consistently enable delivery of high quality standards
  • Execute test plans using standard testing methods, tools and objectives. Document test plan results and report testing activity status to team lead
  • Identify opportunities to apply existing automation or other tools to meet testing needs
  • Research and remain abreast on quality standards and best practices relevant to current and future initiatives and development work.
  • Recommend and support development of implementation of process improvements to increase effectiveness and efficiency of Quality Assurance controls.
  • Be involved with all technical activities within the team such as estimating, planning, software development life cycle activities, testing, research, deployments and documentation creation
  • Emphasize the collaboration and communication of both the development team and project engineering teams to support ongoing projects and do fault finding.
  • Works well in a team environment with shared code; disciplined use of source control and process documentation.
  • Understand all company workflows and best practices and incorporate into the software development.
  • Collaborates with development to design and generate custom reports, by understanding the needs, priorities, alternatives necessary to support the project’s objectives.
  • Collects and understands requests for minor changes and major enhancements to the system, points out alternatives where possible
  • Investigates and, resolves issues identified by project users, engaging additional specialists where needed.
  • Feeds back progress to the users on their requests as well as new developments
  • Performs functional acceptance testing of bug fixes, minor enhancements and major enhancements and co-ordinates user acceptance testing.
  • Assists with knowledge capture and with the maintenance and update of user, marketing and training materials, manuals and tutorials
  • Prioritizes bugs and enhancements in conjunction with other functional support personnel and Global Discipline representatives.


Skills/Qualifications
  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent combination of education, training or work experience
  • Understanding of EPCM environment, workflows and deliverables
  • Understanding of EPCM type systems and tools
  • Demonstrated strong troubleshooting, analytical, technical problem-solving skills
  • High attention to details, motivation and commitment to quality and customer service
  • Ability to operate effectively and work under pressure in a fast-paced environment
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills including the ability to communicate with different levels of stakeholders from different parts of the world
  • Ability to report software problems (bugs, defects) in clear written form with reproducible steps
  • Demonstrated ability to learn and quickly comprehend new concepts and technologies
  • Understanding of all aspects of software development and implementation
  • Willing to work outside of normal business hours as part of a global team and to travel as required
  • Knowledge of Windows & Basic Networking
  • Knowledge of Databases and Data Structures
  • Knowledge of BIM principles, automation and data modelling


Experience
  • 3+ years’ experience working as a software specialist/administrator within the EPCM environment
  • 2 – 3 years of programming experience with object-oriented languages (VB Scripting, Visual Studio, C#, Java)
  • Engineering Project experience in the production of engineering deliverables within a team.
  • Experience in the following technologies desirable:
  • COMOS Design Software (Engineering and Operations)
  • Bentley Openplant and Raceway cable Management Software
    Other Experience
  • Working knowledge and experience in the following technologies an asset:
    • MS SharePoint online
    • MS SQL and database structures
    • Azure Storage
  • Information Technology and general computer systems.
  • Data integration, validation and verification Knowledge
