Software Solutions Engineer
£39,000-£40,000 & Benefits
Time Frame: Fixed term - initially 12 months

35 hrs per week, good benefits, on-site parking

A leading role with a leading organisation, Intapeople are seeking a Software Solutions Engineer / Architect who will be responsible for the effective selection, implementation, development and support of business critical organisation-wide IT systems at a leading organisation in Cardiff.

We're really looking for someone with a range of experience, any Dynamics CRM experience and TSQL, SSRS, SSIS skills would be easpecially of interest.

  • The development and maintenance of internally developed software solutions, observing industry coding standards
  • The continuous review and tuning of their bespoke web based systems
  • The continuous review and tuning of the content management systems
  • The development and integration of solutions into/with the CRM system as required
  • The development and integration of the website with key business systems.
  • Advise on purchase and implementation of relevant hardware and software, writing technical specifications as necessary
  • The development and maintenance of supporting documentation ensuring accuracy of information
  • Maintain knowledge and awareness of new technical developments and trends and maintain recommended industry best practice at all times
  • Supervisory responsibility for the Developer as required

    • This is a 1000 user organisation spread across 20 or so UK locations, it's a truly unique and friendly working environment and extremely stable. Please apply straight away if this sounds like you and get in touch with Puti if you'd like to know more.

    We feel this role should be commutable from Cardiff, Newport, Bristol, Cwmbran, Rhondda, Merthyr, Barry, Bridgend, Port Talbot and Swansea.
