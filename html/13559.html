Data Analyst and Automation Engineer (iptiQ)

About IptiQ

Become a part of a growing and dynamic entrepreneurial team within the established Swiss Re Group. iptiQ is a start-up unit, established to provide innovative protection solutions directly to consumers. Our team delivers the coordination, infrastructure and competences that are key to driving forward this new business; working closely with our partners and clients. Our mission is to reduce the protection gap by simplifying life insurance and making it more accessible to people. Our motto is "now life is easier for everyone". For us this means making life insurance easier to sell, to buy and to trust.

About The Role

The primary focus of this role is to support iptiQ's data integration processes to receive & validate our partners' data, to provide actionable data insights to iptiQ businesses and to develop process automation initiatives (e.g., RPA).

Your Tasks Will Be
  • Analyse and report business-critical data, identify and correct data quality issues
  • Develop relationships with sales, finance, HR, sales operations, and other functional teams to identify and address reporting issues
  • Oversee and assist with collection of data and reports as needed
  • Analyse operational data in order to provide trends and actionable insights
  • Create new tools and business processes that simplifies, standardizes and enables operational excellence
  • Participate in proof of concepts for new RPA opportunities
  • Develop and administrate bots to automate repeatable processes using tools such as UIPath, Automation Anywhere or Blue Prism
  • Strict adherence to both internal and external compliance policies
  • Some travel to Swiss Re locations may be required
About The Team

iptiQ EMEA is part of Swiss Re's Life Capital division which manages all our life & health primary insurance portfolios across Europe. iptiQ is a start-up, established to deliver creative protection solutions sold to consumers through Distribution Partners. Our team delivers the coordination, infrastructure and capabilities key to driving forward this new business, while working closely with the rest of the Swiss Re Group.

We are a dynamic, ambitious business unit who thrive on change and are passionate about high quality delivery to our customers and distribution partners.
We are establishing a data operation team within Bratislava which will support the iptiQ teams across Europe. iptiQ is a Luxembourg based insurer with Branches in UK, Ireland, Germany, Netherlands, France. iptiQ's data operation team supports the delivery of iptiQ's growth for these locations and other countries across Europe, currently Spain & Poland. We are looking for a creative self-starter to be part of this team with specific responsibility to deliver iptiQ's data management processes to ensure accurate, timely and business relevant reporting for iptiQ business steering.

About You
  • Experience in data management and/or data analysis
  • Strong analytics skills, ability to work with large data sets and extrapolate conclusions
  • Experience coding in SQL, R, Python or other data manipulation language
  • Understanding of data administration and management functions (collection, analysis, distribution etc.)
  • Real passion for and knowledge of technology and digitalisation
  • Experience with Robotic Process Automation (RPA) not required but would be a plus
  • You are committed to quality, attention to detail and are solution oriented
  • English is iptiQ's business language and therefore essential
