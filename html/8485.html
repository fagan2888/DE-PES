Job Description

Big Data Engineers with technical experience in optimizing of management and deriving insights from large, non-structured, non-relational data.
In this role, you will play as chief catalyst who can get on the ground floor and design, implement systems from scratch and that they work quickly (in real time), accurately, and at scale.
Design and implement distributed applications collecting event data while creating, analysing & reporting on different behaviour patterns for multiple ecommerce companies.
Research and prototype ideas for analytics and machine learning-based product features.
You will be developing practical, robust & high-performance distributed systems and play a innovator role in creating the next generation shopping experienceEducation
Graduation
Master of Computer Applications (M.C.A.)

Experience

Working experience of building applications on GNU/Linux.
Working experience in data modeling in non-relational databases, such as Cassandra, HBase, MongoDB, etc.
Proficiency in Map-Reduce concepts and experience with Hadoop /Spark or relative technologies.
Experience in distributed systems design and development using LISP/Scala/Java/C++.
Min 3 yrs of experience
