ABOUT THE JOB

​

This is an exciting time for our company! We are in full transition from a European leading service provider of airport equipment solutions to a global leader.


As a Technical Support Engineer you report to the Maintenance Manager. You are based at our headquarters in Steenokkerzeel but will travel more or less 20% of your time. Working closely with colleagues from the headquarter and from the different countries we operate in, you make sure our maintenance activities are at a high level of safety and quality, and in line with the OEM requirements.


For this position, we are looking for a highly motivated colleague who loves a fast-paced environment and international contacts. Next to this you are an analytical talent with a customer oriented mindset.

​

WHERE YOU’LL CREATE THE MOST IMPACT

​

You will be supporting our business by being responsible for:


  • Support of the maintenance management:
    • Setting up the maintenance schedule for the different types of vehicles;
    • Support with the standardization of the maintenance methods and instructions for the group;
    • Ensure the OEM technical documentation is up to date and available for the group;
    • Setting-up checklists for the maintenance of each vehicle;
    • Follow-up of technical issues with the supplier;
    • Etc.


  • Preparations of reports on maintenance topics


  • Technical support for various projects (innovation, business development,…)


  • Support the set-up of new entities worldwide in terms of maintenance management
    • Audits of fleet and maintenance activities
    • Provide training and support for all maintenance related topics.
    • Etc.

  • Actively participate in TCR’s OHS process by understanding and complying with the OHS policy, OHS procedures and requirements

​

THE IDEAL CANDIDATE

​

  • Is a young graduate or has 2-3 years of experience

  • Holds an engineering (electromechanics) degree or equivalent in experience

  • Has a good understanding on how a thermic, electrical and hydraulic engines work and is eager to learn more about it

  • Has proficient knowledge of Microsoft Office

  • Has strong analytical skills

  • Is customer oriented and solution driven

  • Is hands-on

  • Has a critical eye and works accurate

  • Communicates effectively with colleagues, suppliers, etc.

  • Has strong interpersonal skills and ability to collaborate with people from various backgrounds

  • Has a good understanding of the OHS

  • Is fluent in English (technical) – both spoken and written, any other language is a plus

  • Willing to travel world wide (up to 20% of his/her time)

  • Lives or willing to reallocate to Belgium, there is no expat contract possible
