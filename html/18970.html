What You’ll Be Doing...

Verizon is a Fortune 500 company providing Security and Network services to Enterprise and Government clients across the globe. Our Global Engineering Solutions (GES) is the technical design authority (TDA) for Verizon-managed customer networks. GES owns the customer’s network architecture and is responsible for the implementation of all design-impacting changes, architecture reviews, capacity management, problem management, etc. for the customer. The technical scope of GES includes LAN and WAN engineering, IP Services (DNS, DHCP) engineering, IP Telephony, Wi-Fi and various other network-based technologies. Within GES, a specific group is responsible for the engineering of customer networks that were completely outsourced to Verizon.

You will be designing, implementing and managing the deliverables for our customer networks (WAN, LAN, data center, IPT, etc.) and creating documentation for the implementation of the customer's network. As a Senior team member also support and coach other engineers in the team at a technical level to provide professional engineering support to enable VzB’s organization to provide high quality services/solutions to our customers.

As a Lead Engineer, your technical skills are top notch, and you are known for pro-actively driving the development of the engineering role together with the other Design Engineers, Implementation Engineers and digging into the details to find the best solutions. You know how to get to the bottom of your customers’ needs, and how to manage your stakeholders’ expectations. Talking to people comes very naturally to you and you have an ability to communicate complex information in a way that people understand.

You’ll Need To Have

What we’re looking for...
  • Bachelor’s degree or relevant work experience.
  • Experience in designing and implemented large scale customer networks.
  • Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) certification or relevant experience.
  • Fluency in English.

Even Better If You Have
  • A degree in IT and experience in large complex customer environment.
  • Cisco Certified Design Expert (CCDE) or Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert (CCIE) certification.
  • Experience with Cisco hardware (routers, switches), WAAS, Juniper routing and internal wireless products.
  • Project management experience.
  • Experience with contemporary technologies including MPLS, TCP/IP, IPSec, BGP, QoS, IP Telephony, Multicast, and/or other similar tools.
  • Software Defined Networking (SDN) knowledge and Virtualization Technologies.
  • Willingness to travel - 25% or less.
When you join Verizon...

You’ll be doing work that matters alongside other talented people, transforming the way people, businesses and things connect with each other. Beyond powering America’s fastest and most reliable network, we’re leading the way in broadband, cloud and security solutions, Internet of Things and innovating in areas such as, video entertainment. Of course, we will offer you great pay and benefits, but we’re about more than that. Verizon is a place where you can craft your own path to greatness. Whether you think in code, words, pictures or numbers, find your future at Verizon.
