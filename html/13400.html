Job Description

DXC Technology (NYSE: DXC) is the world’s leading independent, end-to-end IT services company, helping clients harness the power of innovation to thrive on change. Created by the merger of CSC and the Enterprise Services business of Hewlett Packard Enterprise, DXC Technology serves nearly 6,000 private and public sector clients across 70 countries. The company’s technology independence, global talent and extensive partner alliance combine to deliver powerful next-generation IT services and solutions. DXC Technology is recognized among the best corporate citizens globally.

We are looking for an Intelligence Data Scientist / Developer to join our Operation Engineering and Excellence team, a world wide organisation with representation in all regions. The role is part of the Intelligence Pillar team, who is responsible to develop different tools/applications to analyze data, implement proactive/predictive analysis, and implement various visualization components.

Job Specific Responsibilities
  • Attend daily Scrum meeting
  • Review requirements of tasks assigned, discuss them and translate them into technical design specifications
  • Develop the solution based on the specification, and using Agile methodology to constantly check with requestor
  • Do Unit Test / Develop auto-tests for Detect-to-Correct
  • Drive Move to Production via the release pipeline
  • Provide consulting/maintenance for those solutions
Soft Skills Required
  • Fluent English
  • Data analysis mindset, curious of business insight identification
  • Good communication skills
  • Good cross-cultural team player. Used to working in international environment
  • Well-organized, self-motivated, flexible, pro-active, able to work to tight deadlines
Technical Competencies Required
  • Expert data model, scripting tools (.net, python, angular.js, java, …)
  • Agile software development methodology awareness (GitHub, Jira, Jenkins, Terraform, …)
  • Machine Learning tools will be considered as an advantage
  • Good on SQL queries knowledge
  • Knowledge on Azure Cloud solutions
  • ITIL V3 foundation certification will be considered as an advantage
We Offer a Challenging Role Including
  • Continuous learning and technical training opportunities
  • Great opportunity for professional development in the IT field
  • Chance to work with large, enterprise-scale, high-tech environments
  • Competitive remuneration package
  • Medical insurance
  • 4 days additional paid leave (total:24 days)
  • Food vouchers
  • Life insurance
  • Access to multiple discounts and special offer for DXC employees.

We offer a challenging and very interesting role with definite opportunities for growth and personal development.
