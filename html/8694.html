Background

Our first and flagship product, TrashBot, is a "smart" trash can that combines next-generation robotics and sensor technology capable of differentiating recyclables from non-recyclable material. Simply place your garbage in a Trashbot and walk away. TrashBot automatically sorts recyclables such as metals, plastics, and glass from landfill waste, while simultaneously gathering information about the type of waste generated.

A single TrashBot has the potential to replace all of the conventional, spaceconsuming trash and recycling bins we currently use. From corporate workplaces, to stadiums and ball parks, to universities, schools, and airports, the practical applications for TrashBot are endless.

We're looking for an outstanding Software Engineer with a machine learning background who combines excellent technical skills and a demonstrated ability to get things done quickly and effectively. Applications from all skill levels and backgrounds are appreciated.

Preferred Skills And Qualifications
  • 3+ years of experience in a Software Engineering related field
  • Strong CS fundamentals
  • Experience with Computer Vision and Machine Learning
  • Willingness to learn aspects of other Engineering fields including robotics and hardware development
  • Proficiency in using query languages such as SQL, Hive, Pig
  • Excellent understanding of machine learning techniques and algorithms, such as k-NN, Naive Bayes, SVM, Decision Forests, etc.
  • Ability to clearly communicate across a variety of platforms
  • Experience working in a fast paced startup environment
  • A keen interest in robotics and sensors
  • Ability to speak confidently in front of large crowds
