Senior PHP Software Engineer

At eDreams ODIGEO​ we are looking for talented PHP Software Developers!

What do we do?


We are building the lodging platform, ODIGEO Connect,  that serves millions of requests a day in eDreams ODIGEO through separate and new applications and infrastructure. We are applying our experience in lodging systems and microservices architecture to build a state of the art bedbank with no legacy!


We look for developers that:

  • want to work in a totally new and independent platform

  • want to learn and apply what they have learned

  • can get closer to infrastructure , if they want to

  • want to play with new technology

  • are data driven

  • see the benefits of DDD and want to apply them

  • don’t want to maintain 10 years old applications (who does?!)

  • are interested in containers and cloud

  • are interested in microservices (and their challenges!)

  • see the benefits in infrastructure as code

  • want to work with high traffic!


We are constantly investing in infrastructure and software quality. We want our team to excel and to be a reference in our IT solutions.


Scalability and reliability are a must, which is why we promote automated tests, code reviews, continuous integration and delivery flows.


We use and love PHP7 *, but we are open to test and adopt other technologies if they fit better to solve a specific problem.


We train and innovate. You will have the opportunity to show us your talent and to help us to be ahead in the technology front. We give unlimited access to the entire O’Reilly online platform (books, courses, trainings, etc.) to each of our team members!


We are a team with a lot of passion about technology. If you join us we are sure that you will have fun and learn a lot from us (as us from you) and grow together as professionals.

What technology do we use?


At the moment we’re actively using (but the list is growing!):

  • PHP7.*, Node.js, Lua, [...]

  • MariaDB, Elasticsearch, InfluxDB, PostgreSQL, Redis, [...]

  • Docker, Vagrant, VirtualBox, AWS, Ansible, Prometheus, Alertmanager, Grafana, ELK, Kubernetes, RabbitMQ, Kong, NGINX, Consul, Trickster, GitLab, Jenkins, Portus, [...]


Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain applications to support the company business with special attention to code maintainability and application performance .

  • Contribute to our “all must be tested” motto and integrate your development to our CI.

  • Contribute to our continuous code review processes.

  • Work in our multidisciplinary team and stakeholders, helping to understand business requirements, formulate use cases and then translate that into a pragmatic and effective technical solution.

  • Define, analyse and integrate the right technologies that may suit the different cases.

  • Analyse and optimize persistence technology, access and design.

  • Help in defining, analysing and implementing any development tool that may help increase the team’s productivity.

  • Have fun coding! ;)


Required Skills and Qualifications:

  • Bachelors in Computer Science or other technical field.

  • 3+ years relevant work experience in PHP (OOP).

  • Experience with MySQL/MariaDB

  • Experience with Domain Driven Design

  • Experience with unit testing

  • Understanding and experience applying SOLID and Clean code.

  • Team and agile culture player.

  • Proficient English communication skills.

  • Personal interest for continuous learning and self ­improvement.

  • You find satisfaction in a job well done and want to solve head-scratching challenges.


Valuable skills (you have experience with or you would love to learn):

  • languages like Golang, Node.js…

  • Databases like Redis, Elasticsearch, MongoDB, Cassandra…

  • Asynchronous communication using events, Domain Events, CQRS, Event Sourcing…

  • Performance and Profiling

  • Frameworks like Slim, Silex, Symfony 2…

  • Docker, Ansible, Vagrant…


What do we offer?

  • Excellent environment for continuous growth and learning: with our Learning & Development programs, you will have access to tech talks, internal soft skills and technical skills trainings, language lessons, external events and industry conferences.

  • Free subscription of Safari Online Learning.

  • We support a phenomenal lifestyle and work-life-balance (e.g. half-day Fridays, team building activities, flexible hours, 24 days of holiday +2 extra days off, etc.).

  • Personal career path programs.

  • A free eDreams Prime subscription to benefit from significant discounts on your travels.

  • Competitive salaries & flexible benefits scheme: Ticket Restaurant, transport, private health insurance, etc.

  • Special discounts in local venues, such as gyms, restaurants, shops, etc.

  • New to the city? We also offer great relocation packages.

  • Amazing offices in the heart of Barcelona with a beautiful terrace to have a break and enjoy the views.

  • Annual awards for a variety of team and individual achievements.


… And the opportunity to work in a dynamic, dedicated, fun and passionate team of professionals. We journey together!


Come fly with us and join us now.
