The primary purpose of this position is to provide practical guidance and advice internally on Data Protection matters as they apply to COOK ®.

The primary purpose of this position is to provide practical guidance and advice internally on Data Protection matters as they apply to COOK ®.


Responsibilities:

* Provide practical guidance and advice internally on Data Protection queries and matters in association with Cook's Global and EMEA Data Privacy and Legal teams to ensure alignment.
* Maintain and develop Data Protection structures, policies, procedures and internal records/registers and implement updates where necessary in accordance with legal requirements and industry best practice.
* Deliver employee Data Protection training and continually review training programmes to ensure alignment with requirements.
* Work closely with the Information Security and IT departments to ensure that Data Protection requirements are incorporated into existing and new IT projects.
* Act as a liaison between functions and Information Security and IT in relation to explanation and resolution of Data Protection issues.
* Maintain the current Data Protection awareness programme and continue the development of data privacy framework.
* Work collaboratively with business functions to ensure that Data Protection considerations are incorporated at the outset of new projects.
* Support responses to data subject requests and supervisory authority queries (as appropriate).
* Proactively and collaboratively work with Data Protection coordinators to maintain compliance with current requirements and to develop and execute agreed solutions.
* Work with Data Protection coordinators to ensure that process efficiency and Data Protection requirements are complimentary.
* Assist with the investigation of data issues and resolution as may be required.
* Provide regular updates on Data Protection to Cook EMEA Senior management team
* Work collaboratively with Cook counterparts globally to achieve a one Cook approach to Data Protection requirements.
* As appropriate, continue involvement in external Data Protection community activities.
* Participate in Global Ethics & Compliance initiatives.
* Ensure that the COOK® brand and image is communicated in a positive and consistent manner to all customers, both external and internal.
* Ensure that all duties are carried out in compliance with the company's Quality Management System.
* Ensure that Cook's Code of Conduct is considered in all business matters carried out on Cook's behalf.


Requirements/Qualifications:

* Legal, IT or other relevant higher level qualification required.
* Good knowledge and understanding of European data protection and data security regulations.
* Professional experience working in a Data Protection related role in a commercial environment advantageous.
* Good understanding of IT technical requirements and challenges advantageous.
* Excellent analytical, reading/comprehension, writing skills and strong attention to detail.
* Excellent organisational skills with the ability to multi task prioritize a dynamic workload and manage conflicting deadlines.
* Must possess ability to exercise good judgment, communicate with clarity, diplomacy and discretion.
* Excellent leadership, problem-solving and interpersonal skills.
* Must be able to work independently under general supervision and occasionally work as part of a larger inter-disciplinary team.
* European language, other than English advantageous.
* Proven ability to communicate effectively on an individual and group basis.
* Availability and willingness to travel on company business.
