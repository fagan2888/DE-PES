Analyst, Innovation - 44601

Operations - Hong Kong Quarry Bay, Eastern District

Nielsen’s Innovation team (also known as BASES) helps clients develop and commercialize new products that generate profitable growth by identifying unmet consumer demand, optimize their innovations to deliver on demand, and support the acceleration of commercialization success with more precise launch plans and real-time optimization of in-market activities.

Through a blend of innovative market research technologies, such as evolutionary algorithms, predictive analytics, and client consulting, the Innovation team helps our clients improve their outcomes at every stage of the innovation lifecycle. We identify breakthrough product ideas, messaging that inspires action, fresh package designs and optimal launch strategies.

Working in the Innovation team, you will assist with the execution of marketing research studies by compiling, analyzing, and interpreting marketing research data, writing client reports and delivering ongoing complex client reporting providing valuable insights to clients

Responsibilities
  • Execute projects across a variety of clients, meeting agreed-upon milestones
  • Accountable for high-quality client deliverables and sales forecasts
  • Deliver work without data errors and inconsistencies
  • Partner with various analytical teams to provide cohesive insights and recommendations to our clients
  • Develop mastery of BASES products and services as well as the project process
  • Develop mastery of proprietary tools and metrics within the first 6 months of the job
  • Develop a strong understanding of successful innovation within the first 6 months of the job (Nielsen BASES Factors for Success, Demand Driven Innovation Theory, BASES Forecasting Models) and apply this understanding to client deliverables
  • Demonstrate a basic understanding of what drives in-market sales and forecasting theory
  • Analyze study results to draft emails, reports, and presentations
  • Support special projects and thought leadership initiatives
  • Handle project-related client correspondence as needed
  • Support the development of client training


The Ideal Candidate Should
  • Be a strong analytical thinker with strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Manage projects and deadlines independently
  • Have strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Understand market research results and use these results along with in-market data to pull together a story
  • Prioritize and meet deadlines
  • Demonstrate the ability to execute against projects for a variety of products, and develop a basic working knowledge of consumer and marketing drivers of sales volume
  • Work effectively in a team to execute projects


Education & Experience
  • Fresh graduates or recent graduates with a degree in Marketing, Economics or Business Administration
  • Strong analytical mind and excellent numerical skills
  • Very good interpersonal skills
  • Strong business acumen and financial knowledge
  • Awareness of the clients’ needs
  • Fluent in English and Cantonese

In this role, you would learn h ow to use data to answer clients’ business questions and optimize our clients’ new product launches . You will develop a strong sense of story-telling by using data.

By working in Nielsen, you will learn how to work with teams in other Asian markets and globally as well as understand the processes and project management of a multinational company.

About Nielsen

We’re in tune with what the world is watching, buying, and everything in between. If you can think of it, we’re measuring it. We sift through the small stuff and piece together big pictures to provide a comprehensive understanding of what’s happening now and what’s coming next for our clients. Today’s data is tomorrow’s marketplace revelation.

From global industry leaders to small businesses, consumer goods to media companies, we work with them all. We’re bringing in data 24/7 and the possibilities are endless.
